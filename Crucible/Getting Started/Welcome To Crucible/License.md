# License

Crucible is currently available exclusively to Foundry Virtual Tabletop users including our Patreon membership and select community members.

At this time, Crucible does not provide a license which can be used to create third party content or to modify the system or its rules.

It is our intent in the future to provide a favourable license which others may use both for creating addons and third-party content that they may share for both commercial and non-commercial purposes. The codebase for the system will be a separate license from the license for producing content using the system.
