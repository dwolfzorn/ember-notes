# Talents

[[Talents]] are unique abilities or skills that your character has gained over the course of their life, and as the game progresses are expanded upon as you gain levels.

In addition to the talents you gained when you selected an Ancestry and Background, each new character has 3 initial Talent points which can be used to select unique Skills and Abilities. Talents are a roadmap for your character's development, allowing you to invest into unique paths that provide powerful abilities.

After you have chosen your talents, the "Complete" button will appear in the top right of the character creation window. After you have completed creation, your character is complete and your selections are final, and your newly created Actor Sheet will open.
