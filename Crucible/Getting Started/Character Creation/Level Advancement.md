# Level Advancement

Each time you gain a character **Level** beyond 1 you can further advance your character's powers and capabilities. Each additional level grants you:

- 1 Ability Point which can increase one of your Ability scores by one.
- 3 Talent Points which can be used to purchase new character Talents.

After gaining a level you should take a moment to invest these points so that you are prepared to face more challenging and heroic tasks.

Leveling up occurs when your party has accomplished a certain number of **Milestones**. Milestones are structured around the completion of strategic objectives, the arrival of significant plot moments, or the vanquishing of notable foes.

Crucible facilitates the convenient marking of milestones through the Group Actor sheet, which allows the GM to add group members to the sheet by dragging them from the Actor sidebar. After a group has been formed, clicking the center of the hex for Milestones Earned raises an award prompt, allowing the GM to quickly add an identified milestone, amount of award, and reason for the milestone being awarded. When awarding a milestone through this method, the GM may also selectively grant the milestone only to particular group members.

## Milestone Progression

The number of milestone points required to advance in level is described in the following table:

| Character Level | Milestones Required | Cumulative Milestones |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | - | - |
| 2 | 2 | 2 |
| 3 | 3 | 5 |
| 4 | 4 | 9 |
| 5 | 4 | 13 |
| 6 | 5 | 18 |
| 7 | 5 | 23 |
| 8 | 5 | 28 |
| 9 | 6 | 34 |
| 10 | 6 | 40 |
| 11 | 6 | 46 |
| 12 | 6 | 52 |

## Leveling Up

Milestones earned apply to the entire party, even if certain characters or players were not present when the milestone was achieved. When reaching the number of milestones required to level-up, the entire party has the capability to advance in **Level**.

Advancing your Level happens during a **Rest** action, at which point each member of the party may increase their level by one, invest their granted ability point to progress their [[Ability Scores]], and spend additional [[Talents]] points.
