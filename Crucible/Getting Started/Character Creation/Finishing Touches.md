# Finishing Touches

While character creation is technically finished once the "Complete" button has been pressed, there are a few details you may wish to add to your character.

In the **User Configuration** menu you can set your configure your newly created Hero as your **Player Character**, which will allow the game to automatically choose that character for certain aspects of automation. For example, you can press the **C** key to quickly open your character sheet!

## Starting Equipment

Choosing starting equipment is an important part of beginning any adventure. We advise Gamemasters to work closely with their players to determine what basic equipment they feel is appropriate for each character by browsing the existing Equipment compendium (found on the compendium tab in the Items folder). Dragging items from this compendium onto your newly created Actor Sheet will assign the item to that Actor's Inventory.

A newly created character begins their journey with  **25** which is automatically added to their inventory. You may spend this initial budget on starting equipment. As you purchase initial equipment, deduct the full **Scaled Price** of each item from your initial gold allowance.

We anticipate that a guided experience to spending your starting gold budget on initial items may be added as a Character Creation step in the future!

It is important that when adding character equipment you mark anything that the character is wearing as equipped by clicking on the Equip button on the right hand side of the sheet next to the edit and delete buttons.

Gamemasters can create and use their own custom Items either through the sidebar tab (for items that will be used by many characters) or by clicking the + icon on the bottom right corner of any Actor's inventory tab for more unique, character-specific items.

## Appearance and Details

With character creation finished, it's a good idea to choose a picture to represent your character by clicking on the icon of the hooded figure on the upper left corner of the actor sheet. In addition, on the Biography tab, you can fill in some important details such as **Pronouns**, **Age**, **Height**, **Weight**, and provide a physical description to accompany your hero.

## Biographies

As a final piece of character creation, you might wish to provide a **Public Biography** that other players in your game will be able to see, containing some facts about your hero's reputation and history. Any details in the **Private Biography** section will only be shown to you and your gamemaster, making it a useful place to write your hero's complete backstory.
