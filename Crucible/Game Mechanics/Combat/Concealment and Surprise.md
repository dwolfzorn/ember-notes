# Concealment and Surprise

The [[Skills]] skill, paired with concealment from terrain are mechanics which allow creatures to be undetected by their enemies. Identification of hidden enemies is generally performed using an opposed [[Skills]] test or a passive Awareness check.

A character or adversary may be **Unaware**, a status condition which render them more vulnerable to attacks. Creatures that are Unaware act at Initiative 1 in Combat, last aside for Incapacitated creatures. Once a creature is no longer Unaware, they roll Initiative and act as normal for subsequent rounds of combat.

Complete mechanics, status conditions, and automation for stealth, concealment, unaware opponents, and surprise will be developed and added in future phases of development.
