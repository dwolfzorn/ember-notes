# Rest and Recovery

After expending resources in combat or via other types of challenges, characters will need to replenish their reserves. There are two special Actions that are used for this purpose:

**Recover**

A short rest that requires 10 minutes during which time resources of Health, Morale, Action, and Focus are fully replenished. Any existing Heroism is removed. Certain special Actions can be performed during a Recovery. Unless otherwise stated, only one such Action may be performed per character.

**Rest**

A long rest that requires 10 hours during which the characters have time to eat, sleep, recover, and perform other downtime actions. Rest delivers all the benefits of Recover, but also any accumulated Wounds or Madness are reduced by an amount equal to the character's level. Certain special Actions can be performed during a Rest. Unless otherwise stated, only one such Action may be performed per character.
