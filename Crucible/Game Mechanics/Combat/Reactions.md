# Reactions

**Reactions** are a special type of Action which can only be taken on some other creature's turn in combat. Reactions have specific triggering conditions like *you dodge a melee Strike attack* or *an ally you can see achieves a critical hit*. Reactions require spending Action or Focus points, and therefore cannot be performed if you do not have the required resources - even if the condition for the reaction is otherwise met.

A creature is unable to perform Reactions if they are **Unaware** or **Incapacitated**.
