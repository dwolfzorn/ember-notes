# Alchemy

**Alchemy** is the scientific practice of combining ingredients such as botanicals and minerals in order to produce consumable medicines, poisons, and explosives through zymurgy, distillation, or homogenization.

An alchemist can combine commonly available ingredients to produce consumable potions such as[[Healing Tonic]] or [[Poison Vial]], as well as throwable weapons such as [[Blast Flask]].
