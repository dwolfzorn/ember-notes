# Tradeskills Overview

**Crafting** in Crucible is performed using a set of **Tradeskills** which, similar to adventuring [[Skills]], are invested aspects of your character's identity obtained through [[Talents]] progression and through the accumulation of knowledge in the form of [[Schematics]].

The crafting system is under active development. Rules described here represent the intended design, but some details may not yet be fully automated.

It is not required for any character to invest into mastery of a tradeskill, however it is generally recommended to select at least one tradeskill to pursue in order to enjoy a well-rounded experience playing Crucible, particularly if your game is a long-form campaign.

The information in this journal describes each of the tradeskills, how schematics are defined and obtained, and how the crafting process functions when using a schematic to turn input ingredients into produced items.
