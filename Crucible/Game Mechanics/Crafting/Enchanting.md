# Enchanting

**Enchanting** is the art of combining existing Glyphs (created by [[Glyphweaving]]) with equipment created by other crafters (such as [[Smithing]], [[Tailoring]], and [[Jewelcraft]]) in order to imbue those items with permanent magical properties. The process of enchanting an item typically involves some additional sympathetic material components which are used in a ritual to bind the magical effect to a non-magical item, typically one of at least Fine quality.
