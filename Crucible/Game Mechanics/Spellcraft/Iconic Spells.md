# Iconic Spells

In addition to Composed Spells, investment into spellcraft talents unlocks Iconic Spell slots which provide a limited number of special spells which you know. These Iconic Spells have specialized effects which are not otherwise possible via the Composed Spell system and cover the breadth of magical capability that a system like Crucible requires.

## Learning Iconic Spells

Each character has a certain number of Iconic Spell slots — initially zero. When learning certain spellcraft talents, you will gain an additional Iconic Spell slot which can then be populated with an Iconic Spell provided you possess the prerequisite knowledge to learn it.

## Replacing Iconic Spells

When your character advances in level you may — if you wish — remove one known Iconic Spell from your repertoire, freeing that spell slot to be filled with an alternative Iconic Spell.
