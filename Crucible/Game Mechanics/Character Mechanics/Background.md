# Background

**Background** defines the cultural upbringing, professional training, career to date, and other aspects of a hero's past before the events of the game's story. These are intended to give depth to a character and some measure of explanation to the origins of their particular skillset and capabilities.

## Creating a Background

Crucible is not designed for a specific game setting. Gamemasters are encouraged to create or modify Backgrounds to fit the lore of their own worlds. To configure a Background in Crucible, create a new **Item** with the **Background** type. Fill out the Background configuration sheet to select four acquired Skills which begin at Novice tier and one acquired Talent.

Each Background includes:

- Novice Skills (typically 2)
- Knowledge Areas (typically 2)
- Known Languages
- Talents (typically at least one weapon training talent and one additional talent)
