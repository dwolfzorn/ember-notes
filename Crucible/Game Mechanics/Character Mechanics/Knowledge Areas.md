# Knowledge Areas

**Knowledge Areas** (or sometimes just called "Knowledges") are specific enhancements that reflect notable areas of a hero's understanding of some specialized training. While most knowledge areas come as a result of a [[Background]] selected during [[Character Creation]], it is possible to attain knowledge areas in other ways during character progression.

Knowledge areas are still being implemented and developed as a mechanical aspect of Crucible and may change over the course of the next several iterations.

While knowledge areas do not grant a specific mechanical bonus, they are often used in combination with skill checks:

- Sometimes, a **Knowledge** will substitute for a **Skill Check** that would otherwise be required.
- Sometimes, a **Knowledge** will provide certain **Boons** towards the attempted **Skill Check**.
- Sometimes, a **Knowledge** may be a prerequisite condition for even attempting a **Skill Check**.
