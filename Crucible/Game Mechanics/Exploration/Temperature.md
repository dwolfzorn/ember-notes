# Temperature

Environments in Crucible may be broadly classified into five different Temperature tiers from coldest to hottest.

**Gelid**

Freezing cold and inhospitable to any life that is not specifically adapted to conditions of extreme cold.

**Cool**

Cool or cold temperatures that may be uncomfortable for warm-bodied creatures, but are not generally dangerous.

**Neutral**

Typical environmental temperature, generally not noteworthy.

**Warm**

Warm or hot temperatures that may be uncomfortable for cool-bodied creatures, but are not generally dangerous.

**Boiling**

Burning hot and inhospitable to any life that is not specifically adapted to conditions of extreme heat.

These temperature tiers are also used to model the body temperature of creatures. Some mechanics depend on the temperature of the environment. Some mechanics depend on the temperature of a creature. Some mechanics involve the interaction of the two. In all cases, these temperature keywords will be used.
