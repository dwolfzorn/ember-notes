# Accessories

**Accessories** are wearable items that provide magical or practical benefits to the bearer. Accessories can be browsed in the **Crucible > Items > Equipment** compendium pack.

Characters have 3 accessory slots by default. Each accessory must be equipped to one of these slots to function. Most accessories also require [[Investment]], meaning they must be both equipped and invested before their magical effects become active.

Like weapons and armor, accessories have a physical **Quality** tier and a magical **Enchantment** tier. The enchantment tier is derived from [[Affixes]] applied to the item. Higher quality accessories can support more powerful affix combinations.

## Accessory Categories

Accessories are organized into the following categories:

**Clothing**

Wearable garments such as cloaks, capes, boots, and gloves. Clothing accessories are typically visible and may carry enchantments related to protection or mobility.

**Jewelry**

Rings, amulets, bracers, and other ornamental items. Jewelry is the most common vehicle for magical enchantment and frequently carries powerful affixes.

**Trinket**

Small objects such as charms, tokens, or talismans. Trinkets are often unique or unusual items with specialized effects.

**Other**

Accessories that do not fit neatly into other categories.

#### Investment

This accessory requires **Investment** to function. The item must be both equipped and invested before its magical effects become active. See [[Equipment Overview]] for more details.
