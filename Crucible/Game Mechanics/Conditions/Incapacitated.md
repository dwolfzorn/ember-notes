# Incapacitated

When a creature is entirely unable to act, it is **Incapacitated**. When a target is Incapacitated, its **Dodge, Block,** and **Parry** defenses become zero and it no longer benefits from **Ability Score** bonuses to **Fortitude, Willpower,** or Reflex defense. It cannot contribute to Flanking or otherwise aid allies.
