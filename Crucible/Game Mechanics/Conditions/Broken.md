# Broken

A creature that is **Broken** is vulnerable to psychological trauma. Heroes become Broken when they reach zero Morale. Any further damage to Morale taken while Broken increases Madness.

While a creature is Broken it cannot expend **Focus** and actions it takes suffer from **+2 Banes**.

Creatures which are Broken cannot contribute towards Flanking. Non-player Adversaries which become Broken will attempt to flee the battle unless they lack instincts of self-preservation or escape seems impossible.
