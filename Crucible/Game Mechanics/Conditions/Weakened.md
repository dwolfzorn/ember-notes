# Weakened

A creature that is **Weakened** is vulnerable to taking deadly wounds. Heroes become Weakened when they reach zero Health. Any further damage to Health taken while Weakened increases Wounds.

While a creature is Weakened, it suffers -2 to maximum Action and loses access to a free Movement action.
