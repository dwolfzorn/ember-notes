# Invisible

A creature which is **Invisible** cannot be detected using sight-based perception. Special detection modes such as **Thermal Vision**, **Sense Creature**, or **Echolocation** may still detect an Invisible creature.

### Expiration

Invisibility expires when its duration elapses, when the Invisible status is removed, or when the Invisible creature performs an action that reveals their presence.

An action reveals the actor's presence if any of the following are true:

- The action is a **Spell**.
- The action targets another creature (anything other than **Self**-scope or **None**-scope).

An action with the **Undetectable** tag is an exception: regardless of its target scope or spellcraft, it preserves Invisibility.
