# Flanked

When a creature is simultaneously **Engaged** by a number of creatures greater than its **Engagement** score, it has the **Flanked** condition. The Flanked condition has multiple integer stages, starting at Flanked 1, where each stage of Flanked contributes **+1 Boon** to melee attack rolls made against that creature.

While having more Engaged enemies increases your Flanked condition, having adjacent allies who are supporting you can nullify flanking. Allies contribute their own Engagement as a bonus to yours if you share enemies in common that you are both Engaged with.
