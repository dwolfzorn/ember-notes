# Flying

A creature which is aloft in the air with no supporting surface beneath it is **Flying**. A Flying creature does not fall, and Forced Movement cannot knock it from the air; a Flying creature which is pushed, pulled, or knocked back is displaced through the air without falling.

#### Automation

The Flying condition is applied automatically when a creature ends its own movement using the **Fly** movement action, and is removed automatically when that creature later ends a movement using any other movement action.
