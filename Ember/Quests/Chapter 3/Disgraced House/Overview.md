# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Main Quest occurs during Chapter 3 amidst the great city of [[Ordain]], and is intended to begin when the party receives [[A Cevher Summons]] a few days after the Events of the [[An Old Friend]] Main Quest.
>
> Unlike the events of Chapter 2, each of the Chapter 3 Main Quests can occur simultaneously, although the characters must manage their time accordingly for various encounters and events to proceed with certain measures of success.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> The "Disgraced House" Quest is designed for a party of Level 5 characters.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Main Quest that awards 3 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon completion of the [[Veiled Threats]], [[Set in Motion]], and [[Aftermath]] Events.
>
> #### Point of No Return: The Marlstone Gala
>
> The [[The Marlstone Gala]] Event serves as a point of no return for certain Chapter 3 Main Quests. Once the characters complete this Event, they can no longer continue or complete the Events of [[Smoldering Cinders]] and [[Spreading Sickness]].

Lyla Cevher asks for the party to join her at House Cevher headquarters, where she is reeling from the death of her uncle, Darius, and the disappearance of her brother, Funar. In his untimely absence, Funar is being blamed for the crime — and Lyla's first priority is to clear her sibling's name. She asks the party to investigate the scene of the crime and use a Locator Rod that is attuned to a pin she and her brother both wear to find his location. The characters discover evidence that the murder was carried out by a gang known as the Beacon Brigade, based in Lantern Roads; they also find out that Funar is being held in the neighboring Marlstone district, in the home of Hephiss Wandren, the head of House Wandren. Coincidence? Likely not.

After a tense visit from Hephiss, the party realizes that the Wandren matriarch alone holds the keys to Funar's prison — and that the upcoming Marlstone Gala is the perfect time to steal them. They'll have to recruit an alchemist who can copy the magically-warded keys by doing a dangerous favor, convince Lyla's old friend Juro Wandren to get them on the list, and scout both Lantern Roads and the Marlstone Manor to secure themselves a safe path in and out. On the night of the gala, the party must attempt to steal Hephiss Wandren's keys, have them copied, and find a way into her well-guarded lair where Funar is being held prisoner. As they go, they'll discover the real power behind the plot against House Cevher, and may even end up with an ally whose power can help them defeat it.
