# Revealed by Lantern

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration Event occurs when the party travels to [[Lantern Roads]] following the [[Veiled Threats]] Event. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Search for an alternate route onto the Marlstone Manor grounds.
> - Potentially uncover evidence that links the Beacon Brigade to Darius Cevher's murder.
>
> #### Area Walkthrough
>
> The party begins in the [[Lantern Roads]] Scene, where the central gameplay of this Event transpires. A complete description of the neighborhood and the gameplay that occurs there is detailed in the [[Lantern Roads]] Area Walkthrough.
>
> #### Traveling Through Lantern Roads
>
> There is a clear barrier between the northern and southern halves of Lantern Roads, and Marlstone adjoins the northern side. To get closer to Marlstone, they will have to find a way past this barrier and into the Northern half of the neighborhood. Three potential options are:
>
> - **Taking the Rooftops**: Parties can make their way up to the rooftops and across from the southern section of the neighborhood to the northern. This plays most to the strengths of **Strong** or **Athletic** parties, and leads them to the outskirts of the house of Beacon leader Vitt Wandren, who is ready for a fight.
> - **Going Through Shadowbox Alley:** Parties can make their way through the shops in Shadowbox Alley, most of which have been taken over by Beacon, to find a path through a nervous shopkeeper's house. This plays most to the strengths of **Stealthy** and **Investigative** parties. Once through, they can avoid combat with enough stealth.
> - **Playing the Part:** Parties can pretend to be a courier or prospective Beacon member and attempt to bluff their way in. This plays most to the strength of **Persuasive** and **Deceptive** parties. Once through, they will be generally accepted by Beacon and can make their way through with limited combat.
> - **Breaking Through the Gate:** While ill-advised, parties can take a direct run at the [[Locked Gate]]. All Beacon members in the area will respond, leading to a large combat. This plays most to the strengths of **Combat-Focused** parties, though it is not the only opportunity for combat.
>
> Whichever path parties take, once they make it into Beacon territory, they must search for a route through to the [[Marlstone Manor Entrance]] in the northeastern corner of the neighborhood.

### Following the Rooftop Route

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Discovering the Rooftop Path
>
> Parties can discover that there is a rooftop path by:
>
> - Talking to shopkeepers about the Beacon Brigade (at [[Just In Time]] or [[Shadowbox Souvenirs]]).
> - Noticing places where they could potentially climb up to a higher level (located near [[The Terrace Gardens]], [[Terracini's Restaurant]], and [[Shadowbox Souvenirs]]).
> - Seeing the rooftop path through the neighborhood while on another rooftop (like [[Lookout Point]] or [[The Terrace Gardens]]).
> - Tracing the path of a courier who disappears into [[Shadowbox Souvenirs]]) only to reappear later in the [[Second Light]] pawn shop.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Following the Courier
>
> When party members are in the center of the Plaza section of Lantern Roads, or at your discretion, they spot a familiar courier in the distance. Read or paraphrase the following:
>
> > As you make your way through the district streets, something catches your eye. Someone, actually. He's carrying a leather satchel now, and moving quickly through the streets, but you're fairly sure it's the courier who first brought you the message to meet with Lyla Cevher. He might know how to get to the other side of Lantern Roads, but he's already a ways away from you and moving fast.
>
> The courier is indeed **Hanslo Rey**, the courier who initially appeared in the [[A Cevher Summons]] Event. If the party wishes to see where he goes or follow him, they can perform any of the following group checks (if at least half of the characters succeed on their check, the entire party succeeds):
>
> - **Awareness (DC 17)** to keep an eye on him in the crowd of shoppers and watch him turn a corner toward [[Shadowbox Souvenirs]]. When the party reaches the same corner, he is not there.
> - **Diplomacy (DC 17)** to canvas the area for information about his location. They learn that the courier turned into that same alleyway.
>
> Any character who searches the alleyway and makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check notice footprints in the dirt, and that the barrels have been recently disturbed; they can recently conclude that the courier stood on it, likely to reach the roof.
>
> Whether or not the characters choose to follow Hanslo Rey, he appears again after the party first spots him, browsing in the pawn shop [[Second Light]].

> [!info] Social
> #### The Courier's Insights
>
> Hanslo is initially reluctant to say anything about how he managed to get his packages delivered, but greets the party.
>
> Any character who makes a successful `[[/check persuasion 15]]` check can convince Hanslo to share the following:
>
> - He can't reveal how to get into hidden places in the city — it would go against his principles as a courier — but he feels bad about what he's doing for Beacon.
> - He collects money from businesses that Beacon has deals with elsewhere in Ordain, and can see that it’s hurting them.
> - Darius Cevher's shop, Threadforge, wasn't one that Beacon was asking for money, but he did deliver a package there soon before the murder from the Lantern Roads courier station.
>
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Spoke to Hanslo:** The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check if they spoke to Hanslo at length during [[A Cevher Summons]]
> - **Critical Success**: The character also convinces Hanslo to reveal that the package he delivered to Threadforge had a powder in it, and that the rooftop path he uses terminates in a well-guarded compound; they only let couriers and initiates through, and there may be extra courier supplies in the Courier Stand in Shadowbox Alley.
>
> Hanslo is willing to give up his own [[Courier Bag]] for 25 gp.

#### Cora Attunement: Taking the Rooftops

Any character who takes the rooftops to reach the alternate entrance to Marlstone Manor advances their **Attunement: Cora (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Sneaking Through Shadowbox Alley

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### The Shadowbox Alley Route
>
> Parties can discover that there is a route through Shadowbox Alley into Brigade territory by:
>
> - Talking to shoppers who are aware of the area (at[[Just In Time]], [[Terracini's Restaurant]], and[[Shadowbox Souvenirs]]) or talking to shopkeeper Hettie at [[Hettie's Divinations]].
> - Exploring the Shadowbox Alley area by passing into it through [[Shadow Players Theater]].

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### The Beacon Brigade
>
> While making their way through this section of Lantern Roads, parties will have to watch out for members of the Beacon Brigade. If they are caught, they'll be placed in an impromptu jail ([[Impromptu Jail]]) and need to break or fight their way out.

#### Primordis Attunement: Going Through Shadowbox Alley

Any character who sneaks through Shadowbox Alley to reach the alternate entrance to Marlstone Manor advances their **Attunement: Primordis (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Bluffing Their Way In

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### The Bluff
>
> Parties can attempt to bluff their way into Brigade territory by:
>
> - Eavesdropping on conversations between Brigade members to learn how recruits get through at [[Terracini's Too]] or getting information about the process from [[Just In Time]].
> - Attempting to replace the current courier by stealing supplies from [[The Courier Stand]].
> - Learning and executing the proper procedure at the pawn shop Second Light for those in the know by observing at the [[Second Light]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Pawn Shop Procedure
>
> If the party decides to make it past the pawn shop, Second Light, they'll need to bluff their way through by:
>
> - Learning that they must bring a light, lamp, or lantern into Second Light to pawn. In addition to any light they have brought with them, the party can acquire lights in [[Shadowbox Souvenirs]] and the [[Shadow Players Theater]].
> - Using the phrase "Have a Brighter Day" to indicate to the shopkeeper that they are a member of the Brigade.
>
> This route results in the party obtaining a [[Brigade Key]], which can be used to open doors and avoid combat within Beacon territory.

#### Orbis Attunement: Playing the Part

Any character who bluffs their way past the Beacon Brigade to reach the alternate entrance to Marlstone Manor advances their **Attunement: Orbis (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Moving through Beacon Territory

Once in Beacon Brigade territory north of the gate, the party’s progress depends on whether they have snuck in or have procured a Brigade Key, which marks them as someone who belongs.

- Parties with a Courier Bag can move freely through Beacon territory.
- Parties with a Brigade Key can move freely through Beacon territory and can use the key to gain initial entry into **Vitt's House** and the **Weapons Cache**.
- Parties without a Brigade Key will need to continue to deal with Beacon Brigade members as noted in [[Area Overview]].

#### Ragen Attunement: Breaking Through the Gate

Any character who breaks through the gate and directly confronts the Beacon Brigade to reach the alternate entrance to Marlstone Manor advances their **Attunement: Ragen (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Evidence Against the Beacon Brigade

While moving through Lantern Roads, the party may encounter evidence heavily implicating the Beacon Brigade in the murder of Darius Cevher.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Evidence Against the Beacon Brigade
>
> Any of the following items can be used as evidence:
>
> - A Withering Dagger, which can be found in the [[The Barracks]] or the [[Weapons Cache]].
> - Plans for the killing, which can be found in [[Vitt's Office]] or [[Vitt's Back Room]].

Once evidence has been found by the party, mark the outcome below as completed.

`[[/outcome dariusEvidence]]`

### Concluding the Event

Once the party has successfully made their way to the **Marlstone Manor Entrance,** they know enough to pass their route and information on to Janix Mance, who will follow in their footsteps on the night of the Gala.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has discovered the secret passage, they can proceed with their other preparations (if they have not done so already):
>
> - Going to [[The Hallows]] to recruit Juro Wandren in the [[The Insider]] Event.
> - Going to [[Stonework Hollow]] to recruit Janix Mance in the [[To Copy a Key]] Event.
> - Going to [[Marlstone]] to scout Marlstone Manor in the [[Casing the Joint]] Event.
>
> If the party has finished their preparations, they should return to [[Lyla Cevher]] in the Tradeway, triggering the [[Set in Motion]] Event.
