# Aftermath

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Guide
>
> This Social Event occurs when the party returns from their adventure at [[The Marlstone Gala]]. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Debrief their allies on what transpired.
> - Learn what their allies are planning to do next.
>
> This Event is depicted using the "House Cevher HQ" Level of the [[Vista: Ordain Interiors]] Vista.

### The Crew's Next Steps

Lyla's primary focus is in bringing the evidence she has gathered to the Ordinate. Other members of the crew also have plans, if they survived the events of the night intact. The party may speak with them here to figure out their next steps.

> [!info] Social
> #### Lyla's Plans
>
> Lyla is focused on bringing the evidence of what House Wandren has become to the Ordinate, but is happy to bask in what the group has accomplished and will mention the following:
>
> - House Wandren is in a bit of disarray after the death of Hephiss Wandren, but is pretending that everything is okay. It is still unclear who will take over going forward.
> - Lyla is going through all the information you all found during the heist and its lead-up. She believes she has enough to get House Wandren removed from the Ordinate and House Cevher restored.
> - If Yllith, the captive Tyraphem prisoner found in Wandren HQ, survived (`[[/eventState otherhood2Wandren survived]]`), they have left Ember, but promised to return with information about what Pyix might be planning.
> - With Funar's help, Lyla will be taking her father's old position as one of the leaders of House Cevher. It isn't the position she sought, but she thinks it will give her the opportunity to do some real good.

> [!info] Social
> #### Funar's Plans
>
> Funar is both grateful and embarrassed by everything that happened — he had a lot of time to think while in the holding cell under Wandren HQ and though he has always wanted to lead House Cevher, believes that Lyla has shown she has what it takes to be the leader of the House. He plans to help her bridge the gap between the more traditionalist members of the House and the flexibility that the current times call for.

> [!info] Social
> #### Juro's Plans
>
> Whether or not the party returned Juro's ledger to him, Hephiss Wandren's death means that he is safe from any of her manipulations. He has no desire to be the new head of House Wandren, but will be sticking around Ordain to try to make the house a respectable one again, if that is even possible.
>
> - If the party did return Juro's ledger, he is especially grateful and promises to lend the group a hand in the future if needed.

> [!info] Social
> #### Janix's Plans
>
> Janix enjoyed the challenge of key copying on the fly and is interested to see what else he can do with the replicating mold he developed to help the party. He welcomes them to visit him in his shop anytime if they need anything going forward — he's always happy to help.

### Concluding the Event
