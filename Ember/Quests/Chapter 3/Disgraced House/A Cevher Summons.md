# A Cevher Summons

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Social Event occurs as the party moves through [[Ordain]] and encounters a courier bearing a message for them from [[Lyla Cevher]].
>
> In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Read Lyla's message and decide whether or not to meet her at [[House Cevher]] headquarters in [[Quarry Run]] to discuss the recent murder of her uncle, Darius.
> - Convince the messenger to share what they know about the current state of House Cevher.

### The Talk Around Town

After reading Lyla's letter, they can ask the messenger about the current situation with House Cevher.

> [!info] Social
> #### What the Messenger Knows
>
> The messenger, **Hanslo Rey** (Chaotic Neutral, Ordani Human, he/him), lingers for a moment after delivering the message; he clears his throat expectantly and holds out his hand. Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 12)** check knows that the messenger is waiting for an incentive to share more information.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 16)** check can convince the courier to speak further on the subject.
>
> - **A Bribe of 5 gp:** The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Politics**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Lyla's Current Situation
>
> **A:**
>
> > I'm sure you heard, but Lyla's brother is the one who killed Darius Cevher, the fabric maker down in Gossamer. Bloody business. The brother, Funar? He's run off, hiding from the authorities. I wonder if Lyla's gotten wind of where he is and is trying to sneak him out of town. She's been gone for years, I think — what a welcome home. I almost feel bad for her, Cevher name and all.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** House Cevher's Past
>
> **A:**
>
> > Should have known that a Cevher would be the one involved in something like this. They always claimed not to have been the cause of the Stonehollow Quake a while back, but it started in one of their sites and killed a whole lot of people. Since then, I've been waiting for the next disaster. This, though — this is a disaster of a whole 'nother sort.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Funar's Guilt
>
> **A:**
>
> > Of all the Cevhers who would kill someone, I never would have guessed it'd be the Funar kid. Didn't know him much, but delivered to him now and again. Very shy and quiet, but I guess it's always the quiet ones.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** House Cevher's Future
>
> **A:**
>
> > House Cevher may be in a bit of trouble. Everyone relies on them for stone and metal, but after the earthquake and this scandal — folks are remembering that they aren't the only merchants in town.

### Concluding the Event

With nothing more to say, the messenger heads off to his next job, leaving the party with Lyla's letter and invitation.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> If the party wants to meet with Lyla, they can proceed to the House Cevher Tradeway in Quarry Run to begin [[Family Business]].
