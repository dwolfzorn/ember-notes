# Finding Funar

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration and Social Event occurs after the party has used the Locator Rod to narrow down Funar Cevher's location to Marlstone Manor in [[Marlstone]]. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Investigate the manor grounds by speaking with the guards and staff.
> - Learn about the upcoming Marlstone Gala.

### Investigating the Manor

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster Note: Entering the Manor
>
> The party will have the opportunity to scout Marlstone Manor in later Events. As a result, it is important that players **do not enter** the manor now, as it will make the running of the Quest much more difficult. The obstacles below are designed to be a deterrent, but add to them as needed to deter a creative party.

If they would like, the party can do some initial scouting of the manor.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Do Not Enter
>
> The entrance to Marlstone Manor is blocked by a large gate and staffed with guards who are well-equipped to defend the grounds if needed.
>
> Any character who casts Detect Magic can note the presence of magic in and around the gate. With the Identify spell, characters know that the gate is warded with a spell that alarms if anyone comes too close and delivers a powerful burst of force energy.
>
> - Any attempts to sneak by the gates automatically fail.
> - If characters attempt to touch the gates or cross them without permission, they must succeed on a `[[/save dexterity 22]]` saving throw or take `[[/damage force 3d6]]`. Each time this defensive feature activates, the DC of the saving throw increases by 2 and the damage increases by 1d6 — the gate resets after 1 hour without being activated.

### A Chat with the Guards

> [!info] Social
> #### Talking to the Guards
>
> The guards will answer a few questions about the Manor, but are generally unwilling to engage in lengthy conversation or reveal sensitive information. However, any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 16)** or **Deception (DC 16)** check can get one of the guards talking; the points of potential conversation are presented below as Q&A blocks.
>
> - If the party accuses the guards of holding Funar, they are offended, throwing the party off of the grounds. They are unaware of Funar being held here and have no information to share on the matter.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Whose manor this is?
>
> **A:**
>
> > You must be new around these parts. No matter. Marlstone Manor has been one of the jewels of the Wandren Trading House for years. Whoever's running the house moves into the place and puts their own stamp on it, though it hasn't changed hands in a while. Hephiss Wandren has this house and the Trading House firmly in her grasp.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Speaking to Hephiss Wandren?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Hephiss isn't here. And she's not one to come out and greet random strangers at the front gates either. If you need something from her, go on and send a messenger. Or see her at the Marlstone Gala. If you're invited, that is.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Getting access to the grounds?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Sorry, no can do. I've heard of some of the previous Wandren heads doing open tours of the Manor, but Hephiss Wandren prefers her privacy. Only time she'll be opening up her house is for the gala. If you want a peek inside, you'll need an invite. Or to be one of the folks helping to prep the place. There's a call going out for cooks and gardeners and the like sometime soon.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** The upcoming event?
>
> **A:**
>
> > The Marlstone Gala's the biggest see and be seen event in town. I'm not always one for a fancy occasion, but I'm trying to get guard duty that night so I can walk the gardens, see what they look like when they're all lit up for a party. Good thing staff get first pick of assignments, right?

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Getting into the gala as staff?
>
> **A:**
>
> > No guarantees, but from what I hear, if you show up here two days before the gala and look halfway competent, you should be able to get the job. Good luck.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Funar Cevher is inside!
>
> **A:**
>
> > Excuse me, what? I'm not sure what you're talking about, but you need to go talk about it somewhere else. Get a move on, now. Go!

#### Luxarum Attunement: Befriend the Guards

Any character who befriends the guards to learn more about Funar's whereabouts advances their **Attunement: Luxarum (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Ragen Attunement: Offend the Guards

Any character who offends the guards while trying to learn more about Funar's whereabouts advances their **Attunement: Ragen (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.
