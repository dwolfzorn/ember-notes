# No Good Deed

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This social event happens some time after the party helped capture Ambassador Tezran, and involves the party being summoned to the Hallows to have a meeting with the Veiled Chain. In this event the party will learn:
>
> - The outcome of capturing the ambassador.
> - What the ambassador and Tayans did in response.
> - What the party should expect going forward.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> > So, the good news first: I secured you a payout for your work against the Ambassador's plot. It's not much, but it's something at least. I've also made sure to have the falsified records expunged and disconnected from your names.
>
> She places a heavy pouch on the table which jingles softly on impact. As you claim it she slides a dossier in front of herself.
>
> > Now, for the bad news.
>
> Yeela flips open a dossier, revealing a stamped document, reports, and an unmistakable portrait of Ambassador Loris Tezran.
>
> > Okay, so. Despite our collective efforts, Loris Tezran has managed to slip out of Hallows custody, and return to his embassy. Before you ask, no, nothing stuck. Instructions came down from the Ordinate directly that he be released immediately with no charges pressed. He didn't even get a reprimand for his actions.
>
> They close the file with deliberate calm.
>
> > Now, while insight into the Embassy is limited, we know for certain he’s not happy. You embarrassed him in front of Katerin, his underlings, the Ordinate, and more crucially: his Empress. You can expect him to hold a grudge, probably permanently, and eventually make you pay for it.
> >
> > I wanted to tell so you at least knew well enough to watch your back. I'd feel bad if he managed to blindside you.
>
> They pause, allowing the weight of that to sink in, and leave space for questions.

Yeela provides the party  **1000** as a reward for their efforts.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What about Toron’s fate?
>
> **A:**
>
> > That's a bit of good news, actually. He was moved to a secure off-site location where he could be handled properly. Not in the Tower, and not within Ordain’s public record. He’s cooperating, surprisingly. The Tayans won’t find him, not unless something goes catastrophically wrong.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** How did the Ambassador get free?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Political pressure, mostly. The Tayan Empire leaned hard on the Ordinate, and the Ordinate blinked. Political exchanges, trade threats, diplomatic gestures, the typical song and dance.
>
> She gestures to the dossier on the table.
>
> > The official report reads that the charges were ‘unsubstantiated and beyond the local jurisdiction of the Hallows.’
> >
> > In short, the man has friends in high places.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What kind of repercussions should we expect?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Anything, really. He’s resourceful and patient. Could send assassins or spies, could twist the paperwork and create red tape in the Ordinate to cause you headaches. He might even aim to poison your reputation with rumors, turn allies against you.
> >
> > The Redfiend’s grudge burns slow, but it burns until satisfied.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** And Katerin?
>
> **A:**
>
> > We know she was involved, but didn't come away with anything that’ll hold up in Hallows court.
> >
> > Every lead we dig into comes up clean, too clean, frankly. It's rather unnerving how good she is at covering her tracks, assuming it is her. It's possible she's got a world-class fixer on payroll, but that's just speculation.
> >
> > For now, she’s just under watch while we try to root out her allies in the Hallows.

### Wrapping Up

Once the party has asked all the questions they care to, the agent wraps up the meeting:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Yeela stands, signaling that the meeting is over.
>
> > Well, that’s the state of things. I'm sorry that the Chain can’t protect you from the games the Ambassador plays, but at least you've been warned. I felt you earned that much.
> >
> > I’ll be in touch if anything changes. In the meantime… keep your weapons oiled, check your locks often, and don't sit with your back to a window.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has finished receiving their update, they are free to go on their way, and seek adventure elsewhere in Ordain.
