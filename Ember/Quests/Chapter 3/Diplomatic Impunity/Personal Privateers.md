# Personal Privateers

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This social event has the party delivering Toron to Katerin Bastill after a successful escape from the Spellbreaker Tower. They will also:
>
> - Learn what Katerin will do with Toron.
> - Get advice about dealing with the ambassador after betraying him.
> - Receive a reward for their work with Katerin.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> After a short while you hear the thump of heavy footsteps leading up to the heavy doors swinging open. In strides Katerin, a grin broad enough to flash in the firelight aimed your way.
>
> > Well, my favorite band of agents finally returns, and with the man of the hour, no less!
>
> She scoops up a glass and bottle, filling one from the other as her keen eyes settle on Toron.
>
> > You look half-dead, friend. Let’s fix that.
>
> She sets the bottle down and slaps her hand on the table twice sharply, prompting two servants step through the doors at once.
>
> > See to this man properly: bath, clothes, meal, and a real bed, with company if he wants it. He’s done suffering the hospitality of the Ordinate and the Empire, let's show him how Bastilla does things.
>
> Toron hesitates for just a second, sizing up the servants and Katerin before allowing himself to be lead from the chamber. Katerin watches until he’s gone, then turns back to you, her voice dropping to a more personal tone.
>
> > You did good work. I imagine Tezran’s starting to realize what's happened by now. In a few days’ time your ‘criminal records’ will quietly vanish from the Hallows’ registry.
> >
> > Paperwork’s a delicate art, and I’ve paid for the best artists.
> >
> > Until then, stay here at the estate. The staff don't talk and my kitchens never close. You’ve earned a damned rest.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What should we do about Ambassador Tezran?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Don’t lose sleep over it, but definitely watch your back going forward. He went to all this trouble to get Toron out of the Spellbreaker for failing a mission, he might do the same or more to get his revenge on you for this.

### Katerin's Payment

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Katerin sits, the chair creaking under her bulk.
>
> > As for your the coin of the matter: You’ve earned a reward, and I always pay my debts.
>
> Then she gestures lazily toward a bronze coffer sitting on a nearby table, then begins counting off with her fingers.
>
> > You can take two thousand gold in the coffer and be on your way, or a thousand and a satchel of healing potions, half a dozen, all high quality. Or… I can pull a few strings and get you a magical trinket or two instead, something properly useful, or interesting, or both.
> >
> > The kind of thing House Bastilla doesn’t sell to just anyone.
>
> Katerin shrugs, leaning back once more, the decision clearly unimportant to her.
>
> > I don’t care which you pick, so go with what suits you best.

The party can take:

- **2000**
- **1000** and 6 x [[Potion of Healing (Greater)]]
- Up to two **Uncommon** magic items of the party's choice, subject to GM approval.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has finished receiving their reward, they are free to go on their way, and seek adventure elsewhere in Ordain. If they choose to remain in Katerin's estate they can stay up to three days as guests before being expected to leave.
