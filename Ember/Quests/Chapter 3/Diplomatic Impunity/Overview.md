# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> There are no specific requirements to begin this side quest, and it may begin at any time during Chapter 3 after the party reaches [[Ordain Gazetteer]].
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> "Diplomatic Immunity" is designed for a party of Level 4 characters.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest that awards 2 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 each upon completion of the [[Visiting Hours]] and [[Breaking the Tower]] Events.

Witness to a murder, and saddled with the responsibility of delivering a dead courier's final charge, the party finds itself embroiled in a series of increasingly dubious errands to appease the imperious Tayan Ambassador and avoid his ire.

The party will steal from the Cascillian Navy, help forge documents for the Hallows, and enter into fraught negotiation with unpredictable sea raiders. Why? All so they can infiltrate the uppermost levels of the daunting Spellbreaker Tower where a maximum security prison resides.

Once inside the party will have to concoct their escape plan, and convince a captured Tayan operative to escape alongside them. However, nothing in this quest is as it seems, and the party might not even be serving the interests of the Ambassador and the Tayans by the time they are starting their breakout.
