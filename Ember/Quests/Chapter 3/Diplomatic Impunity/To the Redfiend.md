# To the Redfiend

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This social event has the party delivering Toron to Ambassador Tezran after a successful escape from the Spellbreaker Tower. They will also:
>
> - Learn Toron's ultimate fate.
> - Receive a reward for their work.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> On the patio, at the garden’s edge, Ambassador Tezran stands with hands clasped behind his back, posture straight and still as the marble columns flanking him. Rakavi crouches nearby, restless claws brushing the gravel, leaving slow furrows as it watches you approach.
>
> Tezran turns at the sound of your steps. His expression holds neither warmth nor surprise, but finality.
>
> > The Empire’s lost son returned to our keeping. Very good.
>
> Toron sneers but says nothing. The ambassador gestures sharply, and two soldiers in Tayan crimson sweep out of the building to lay claim to Toron.
>
> The soldiers seize him, shackling his arms tighter as he curses his former commander in the clipped tongue of Imperial. A fist to the gut silences him before the soldier drive him forward, past the doors of the embassy.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What will happen to Toron?
>
> **A:**
>
> > The Empire does not leave liabilities unattended, and I do not suffer failure gracefully. He will be dealt with permanently.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Is it necessary to kill him?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Every act of loyalty carries blood somewhere on it. Whether that means killing, or dying, or both.

### Redfiend’s Favor

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> > Your task is complete, the Empire is satisfied. You’ve carried out a mission worthy of commendation, though none shall ever know it.
> >
> > As established prior, Katerin will see that your names are cleared of the unflattering accusations now cluttering the Hallows’ ledgers. I would remain unseen for a few days while she sees to the process, unless you want to be arrested again.
>
> He reaches to the side table, taking a small, leather pouch and holding it out.
>
> > Your recompense for services rendered, and a token of gratitude for your discretion. You are to consider your obligations to this embassy concluded.
>
> Rakavi’s talons click once against the stone as if punctuating the words.

Within the pouch is  **1000**.

The Ambassador has nothing more to say at this point, and the party is encouraged to leave.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has finished receiving their reward, they are free to go on their way, and seek adventure elsewhere in Ordain.
