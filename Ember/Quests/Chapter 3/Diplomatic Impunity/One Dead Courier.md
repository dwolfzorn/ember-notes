# One Dead Courier

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> In this combat event the party runs into a brazen attack on a courier belonging to the Tayan Empire. The party has the chance to do the following:
>
> - Intervene and capture or drive off the attackers.
> - Investigate the corpse of the courier.
> - Discover the courier's satchel of imperial documents.

#### Scene Under Development

The scene used for this event lacks walls, doors and lighting due to a technical limitation on how the scenes are configured. These limitations will be resolved in a future update.

### Dealing with the Murderers

The party can deal with the band of ruffians in several ways.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Fighting the Ruffians
>
> The group consists of 6 x [[Ordani Ruffian]] armed with small weapons. They have no qualms with killing, having just murdered someone, and will fight to kill. However, their morale will break if three or more ruffians are defeated, causing them to flee.
>
> #### Coercing the Killers
>
> It is possible to convince the ruffians to surrender, or flee if that is what the party wants to do. In either case, a successful **Intimidation (DC 15)** check is necessary. If the party causes them to surrender, they can be restrained and left for the authorities to pick up after the event concludes.

#### Ragen Attunement: Immediate Justice

Any character who joins in the fight to subdue the ruffians advances their **Attunement: Ragen (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

#### Primordis Attunement: Being Reasonable

Each character who uses their social skills to coerce the ruffians into surrender or flight advances their **Attunement: Primordis (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

### Dealing with the Courier

Once the killers are dealt with, the party is left with a dead body to deal with. They will likely want to begin with searching it.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Scene
>
> The scene consists of the deceased courier, and their dropped messenger bag which is full of documents. There are witnesses and bystanders, though many have already left the area, leaving only a few scattered folk trying to go about their business.
>
> **Knowledge: Crime** or **Knowledge: Politics** or **Society (DC 14)** know that this sort of crime is all but unheard of in Ordain. Most of the crime the city sees is either petty theft, smuggling, and some sort of corruption. Violent crime has always been rare, and something this brazen even more so.

The courier and their bag can be examined in closer detail.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Inspecting the Courier
>
> As one would predict, the courier is dead, and cannot be saved. Their body contains an opened envelope, and some personal effects, though none of it has any true value or relevance. Additionally, next to their body is a [[Damaged Tayan Satchel]] filled with scrolls, envelopes, and folders.
>
> **Knowledge: Intrigue** or **Knowledge: Crime** posits that the ruffians attacked just because the courier looked well off and was vulnerable. If they were after the documents specifically, they would have taken the bag and left straight away.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Politics** or **Culture: Tayan** or **Path: Imperial Conscript** recognize the uniform of a low-level functionary in the Tayan Empire. Their death could be a problem for the Ordinate, and if the documents don't get delivered, will likely hinder the local Tayan contingent as well.
>
> #### Courier's Orders
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Politics** or **Culture: Tayan** or **Path: Imperial Conscript** recognize that the opened envelope in the courier's coat bears the official crest of the Tayan Empire. Inside it is an official letter written in **Language: Imperial** and contains instructions for the courier. They were meant to deliver the satchel of missives and messages to Commander Loris Tezran in Embassy Rise in Ordain.
>
> Characters that don't understand Imperial will still recognize a proper name: the city district of Embassy Rise. It should be simple enough to assume the documents were meant for the Tayan Embassy there.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Using Necromancy
>
> If the courier is questioned via [[Speak with Dead]], they can provide a little information: they were sent from the Tayan Empire, they were delivering messages to the Tayan Ambassador in Ordain, Loris Tezran. They don't know what the documents contain. They don't know why they were attacked.
>
> If asked about the cipher used on some of the letters, they know that only high ranking officials have them.
>
> #### Resurrecting the Courier
>
> The party should not have access to the necessary spells when this quest is first encountered, and thus resurrection should not be a possibility. To that effect, there is no provision in the event text for reviving the courier and how that would change events.
>
> However, if the party waits to tackle this quest, they might have the necessary spells or items to do so. At a basic level, [[Unknown]] should always prove ineffective, as the courier will have been dead for over a minute by the time the ruffians have been dealt with. Any greater level of resurrection magic would be successful, however, the result of doing so is left to your discretion.
>
> Refer to [[Magic and Spellcraft]] and the [[Soul Cycle]] for guidance on how death and resurrection works in Ember.

The dropped bag beside the courier will also warrant examination.

#### Signara Attunement: Undeniable Curiosity

Any character who chooses to look through the bag of documents taken from the courier advances their **Attunement: Signara (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

#### Akon Attunement: Deniable Curiosity

Any character who resists the urge tothrough the bag of documents taken from the courier advances their **Attunement: Akon (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Bag
>
> The bag contains a large number of documents, mostly scrolls, but some in envelopes and sealed folders. The bag has been dropped and trampled, leaving many of the documents inside damaged as well. Many of the wax seals on the documents are broken already due to the abuse the pack suffered.
>
> #### Your Eyes Only
>
> The documents cover a wide range of topics, though the majority of them are terse acknowledgement of prior reports, or mundane letters that talk about weather and recent events. In a few cases there are diagrams and drawings of machines or floor plans for structures relating to some sort of "Highgate project."
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Intrigue** or making a successful **Awareness (DC 18)** check know that the mundane letters are encoded in some way, and hold secret messages, but without the cipher there's no way to determine what they actually say.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Politics** or making a successful **Society (DC 15)** check know that [[Highgate]]was badly damaged in a quake some years ago, and has been the subject of extensive reconstruction efforts. It seems that the Tayan Empire is somehow involved in that effort, at least partially.

Once the party has searched the scene there is not much for them to do, but they can wait for the authorities to arrive if they want. This takes a few minutes.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Holding Out for the Hallows
>
> If the party wants to wait for authorities to arrive, they can. Eventually a Hallows inspector will arrive to take reports and make sure the body (or bodies) and any captured culprits are dealt with.
>
> However, when it comes to the document bag, the party is put on the spot: they can either head to the Hallows to report the bag to the Veiled Chain, and let them handle it, or they can take the bag to the Tayan Embassy, to make sure the courier's work is seen through to the end.

#### Mayis Attunement: Waiting for Resolution

If the party chooses to wait for the authorities to arrive so they can report the events that transpired, or hand over surrendered ruffians, each character advances their **Attunement: Mayis (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has defeated the attackers and finished examining the dead courier, they can choose to visit the Tayan Embassy to hand over the wayward documents, or report the crime to the authorities in the Hallows.
>
> If the party chooses to report the attack, the [[Coming Clean]] event needs to be manually triggered to do so.
