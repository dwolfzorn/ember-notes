# Running the Gauntlet

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Combat and Exploration Event challenges the party to explore the [[Kalion Stadium Underworks]], which serves as the clandestine headquarters of the warlock [[Zira Hestidero]] and her surreptitious cult dedicated to the Shard God [[Ku'arta]]. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Explore the Kalion Stadium Underworks in search of The Undaunted hideout.
> - Either sneak past or subdue various members of The Undaunted and their minions.
> - Discover a lost temple to [[Ku'arta]].
> - Reveal the truth behind Zira Hestidero's terrible pursuit of eldritch knowledge and survive a deadly encounter with the loathsome [[Grim Assembly]].
> - Determine the fate of [[Jorey Swift]] alongside the other surviving Undaunted.
>
> #### Prerequisites
>
> [[Agraband Swift]] must be a member of the [[Party]] for this Event to occur.
>
> #### Area Walkthrough
>
> The party begins in the [[Kalion Stadium Underworks]] Scene, where the central gameplay of this Event transpires. A complete room-by-room description of the underworks environment and the gameplay that occurs there is detailed in the [[Kalion Stadium Underworks]] Area Walkthrough.
>
> In addition to the details provided in the Area Walkthrough, there are three key moments during exploration of the underworks where Event-specific gameplay should occur:
>
> 1. In the [[Chamber of Bargains]], the party may undertake [[Running the Gauntlet]].
> 2. In the [[Ritual Chamber]], the party may undertake [[Running the Gauntlet]].
> 3. After confronting Zira and her Undaunted allies in the [[Temple Sanctum]], the resolution to this story is realized in [[Running the Gauntlet]] and [[Running the Gauntlet]].

### Exploring the Underworks

In search of Jorey Swift, the party must explore the hidden headquarters of The Undaunted — a defunct and forgotten training facility deep beneath [[Arena Ridge]] known as the [[Kalion Stadium Underworks]]. Here, beyond monuments to fallen champions and a gauntlet designed to test the hopeful, a hidden temple to Ku'arta has gone moldering and forgotten for centuries. Until now.

If the party can survive the lair’s denizens and its handful of magical and mechanical gauntlets, they’ll arrive just in time to interrupt a profane ritual conducted by Zira in the temple's inner sanctum. A full description of the location can be found in the [[Kalion Stadium Underworks]] area walkthrough.

### A Bargain with Ku'arta

When exploring the [[Chamber of Bargains]], the party may have an opportunity to strike a bargain with [[Ku'arta]] directly, who has a vested interest in the resolution of this tale. If the party (or any member thereof) forms such a pact, mark the following outcome as completed and award attunement advancement.

`[[/outcome pactKuarta]]`

#### Luxarum Attunement: Ku'arta's Bargain

Any character who adopts Ku'arta as their warlock patron advances their **Attunement: Luxarum (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### A Bargain with Sha-Xotha

When exploring the [[Ritual Chamber]], the party may have an opportunity to undertake a profane communion with [[Sha-Xotha]]. Should the party (or any member thereof) establish such a pact, mark the following outcome as completed and award attunement advancement.

`[[/outcome pactAbyssal]]`

#### Abyss Attunement: Sha-Xotha's Bargain

Any character who adopts Sha-Xotha as their warlock patron advances their **Attunement: The Abyss (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Finishing the Business

Before concluding the Side Quest, record the final outcome that best corresponds to the fate of [[Jorey Swift]] and award attunement progression depending on which path was taken.

#### Heart Attunement: Jorey Redeemed

If the party convinces Jorey to turn his back on The Undaunted and leave his worship of Ku'arta behind, each character advances their **Attunement: Heart of Ember (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

`[[/outcome reconcile]]`

#### Cora Attunement: Jorey Undaunted

If the party fails to convince Jorey to turn his back on The Undaunted, each character advances their **Attunement: Heart of Ember (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

`[[/outcome separate]]`

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### The Death of Jorey Swift
>
> If Jorey Swift is slain during the course of the encounter with Zira Hestidero and her minions, Agraband's business is finished all the same — but the loss of Jorey is an unfortunate circumstance that may shadow the party for years to come.

`[[/outcome death]]`

### Eventful Goodbyes

With the existential cause of his Soulbound affliction resolved, Agraband is now free to relinquish his mortality and release the troubled spirit from his Soulmarked body. Naturally, he will want to share a few parting words with the characters (and his nephew Jorey, depending on the event's outcome).

> [!info] Social
> #### A Final Word with Agraband
>
> This moment of goodbyes is a somber one, with Agraband offering up a few bittersweet remembrances of significant times he shared with the party, good and bad alike.

After one final exchange between Agraband and the party, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> With a forlorn sigh, Agraband unslings his kithara and begins plucking a final tune, a bittersweet melody you'd swear you recognize from somewhere — but that you know in your heart to be novel to this fateful moment. Agraband's tenor is a strange but welcome sound here, where light and hope must dare to roam.
>
> > For foes and friends, a song begins, a stirring in my heart.
> > Down roads untold, our story folds, from epilogue to start.
>
> The bard offers you a small nod and turns away, meandering as he continues to play his tune throughout the dimly-lit chamber. The light of his wound swells to an intensity heretofore unseen, enveloping Agraband in a bright and otherworldly illumination.
>
> In a moment, he's gone, disappearing before your very eyes like the last rays of a dying sunset. But you can almost swear, if only for an instant, that the sound of that final song lingers on the wind. You wonder if it always will.
