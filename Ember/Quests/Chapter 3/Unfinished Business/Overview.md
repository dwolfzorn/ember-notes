# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Side Quest occurs during Chapter 3, and begins while the party traverses the [[Ordain Flats]] Biome of the great city of [[Ordain]]. There are no prerequisites for this Side Quest, but it must be undertaken within one week.
>
> This Side Quest also presumes the characters have maintained a friendly relationship with [[Agraband Swift]] of the Strayhearth Caravan, whom they last saw during the [[The Smoke Clears]] Event; the narrative relies on that amicable status to proceed.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> "Unfinished Business" is designed for a party of Level 4 characters.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest that awards 2 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon successful completion of the [[Cost of Living]] and [[Running the Gauntlet]] Events.

The party's trusted friend [[Agraband Swift]] approaches them with what seems to be a relatively simple task: help him track down his estranged nephew Jorey, who's rumored to have fallen in with a rough crowd in Ordain. However, the confrontation with Jorey's new associates doesn't go quite as planned, and Agraband is killed during a fateful scuffle … or is he? Endowed with supernatural life, Agraband becomes one of the mysterious [[Soulbound]]: newly dead individuals of Ember who must fulfill some great personal or cosmic duty before their souls can peacefully migrate to the City of [[Eternas]] in the Spiritlands. Thus, with young Jorey's fate hanging in the balance, the characters must help Agraband settle his own unfinished business — lest he remain Soulbound forever (and succumb to the condition's inevitable withering).

"Unfinished Business" will lead the party on a mystifying adventure through various parts of Ordain, starting with Agraband's fateful encounter with Zira and The Undaunted in the Smokerie. Following a brief period of recuperation at [[Traveler's Rest]] in [[Westgate]], the party must help Agraband learn more about his strange condition by speaking with local authorities — including the scatterbrained [[Conaris Haid]] in [[Temple Ward]], and the ever-elusive [[Sionia]]. Simultaneously, the party must help Agraband track down Jorey and learn more about the gang of dangerous misfits he's fallen in with. This course of action will take them to the Grand Kalion Stadium of Ordain's [[Arena Ridge]], where they'll initially watch The Undaunted in action on a field of simulated combat, before returning to confront Zira and her cronies in the Kalion Stadium Underworks — where a forgotten temple to Ku'arta conceals secrets untold.

Can the characters successfully uncover Zira's newfound allegiance to an outer god and unravel her malevolent plot to sacrifice her Undaunted cohorts in a rise to power? If so, they just might survive a final conflict with the warlock in her inner sanctum, alongside a flesh-warped creature of the Abyss known as the Grim Assembly (which threatens to wreak more havoc on the city than even Zira herself can imagine). And with any luck, the party can convince Jorey to turn his back on Zira's twisted schemes altogether — closing the book on Agraband's unfinished business in the process.
