# Mixed Media

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration Event occurs as the party enters the [[Flotsam]] district. By investigating its small community of crafters, artists, and merchants for information about an inflammatory mural, the characters can:
>
> - Meet and interview Varinna, an aspiring artist recently commissioned by [[House Salva]] and suspected of being responsible for the mural.
> - Meet and interview [[Falar]], an old adventurer and mage-turned-paint monger, the actual culprit behind the mural.
> - Interact with various locals who can provide further insight into the principal characters in this Event and their backgrounds.
>
> #### Area Walkthrough
>
> The party begins in the [[Flotsam Canal Market]] Scene, where the central gameplay of this Event transpires. A complete room-by-room description of the market environment and the gameplay that occurs there is detailed in the [[Flotsam Canal Market]] Area Walkthrough.

### Investigating Varinna

As the party investigates Varinna and [[Varinna's Studio (Lower)]], check the appropriate Outcome boxes below.

`[[/outcome metVarinna]]`

`[[/outcome confirmedAlibi]]`

`[[/outcome investigatedStudio]]`

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Varinna is Innocent
>
> Varinna is not the artist responsible for the mural and is being actively framed by another party. To learn this, the party needs to do the following:
>
> - Question her about her views on Salva and where she was recently.
> - Follow up on her alibi with Seylu Koine.
> - Examine her art to see if it matches the mural.
>
> A party that does this would reasonably conclude that Varinna lacks the motive and opportunity necessary to be the culprit. If the party [[Outside the Lines]] as part of [[Outside the Lines]], then they should arrive with the ability to invoke Mistress Caberi's name to force more ready compliance.

### Investigating Falar

As the party investigates Falar and [[Falar's Paints]], check the appropriate Outcome boxes below.

`[[/outcome metFalar]]`

`[[/outcome examinedPaint]]`

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Falar is the Culprit
>
> The artist responsible for the mural is [[Falar]], but this shouldn't be obvious to the party immediately. In fact, Falar does all that he can to pass himself off as a simple, disgruntled paint monger.
>
> A party that speaks to him during this event should be expected to find him hard to deal with, bitter, and highly critical of [[Ordain]], but not likely to be the source of the mural. His displayed art is neither good enough nor in the same style, and while he lacks an alibi, he isn't the only person in the market area who dislikes Salva.
>
> #### Falar's Motives
>
> Bitter that Varinna has begun to taste success, he has started painting murals in her art style to discredit her and harm her reputation. Deep down, he wants to hurt Salva more than anything and harbors no true ill will toward Varinna. In truth, she was the most convenient scapegoat, and if pressed later in [[Matters of Perspective]], Falar will even admit guilt over hurting her to get at Salva.
