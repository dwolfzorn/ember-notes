# Matters of Perspective

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Combat Event occurs when the party intercepts [[Falar]] at the [[Redwalk Ramble]], only to find he's already transformed a large section of the park into an illusory death trap almost guaranteed to badly harm anyone that wanders into it. By navigating these illusory hazards and battling Falar's minions, the characters can:
>
> - Confront Falar and his elemental minions in combat, including the [[Paint Globlin]] and the never-before-seen [[Chiaroscuran Beast]].
> - Decide the fate of Falar by either killing, capturing, or siding with him.
> - Navigate numerous illusory hazards placed on the map by Falar.
>
> #### Area Walkthrough
>
> The party begins in the [[Redwalk Ramble]] Scene, where the central gameplay of this Event transpires. A complete description of the park and the gameplay that occurs there is detailed in the [[Redwalk Ramble]] Area Walkthrough.

### Falar's Fate

Check the appropriate outcome below based on Falar's ultimate fate after the party confronts him in Redwalk Ramble. The outcome will affect the narrative in the following [[Commissioned Work]] Event. The possible outcomes for Falar are:

- **Falar is killed in the fighting**. He won't be able to face formal justice in the courts of the Hallows, but can no longer harm others.
- **Falar is defeated and captured**. It is possible to persuade Falar into surrendering himself to the party, though it isn't easy. In either case, he can be turned over to the authorities once captured. This would be Cherish's preferred outcome, based on the meeting the party had with her in [[Outside the Lines]].
- **Falar escapes**. This can happen during the encounter with him as part of combat, but could also be a result of the party deciding to let him go because they sympathize with him or agree with his cause.

Once the party has completed the encounter in Redwalk Ramble, mark the appropriate outcome below based on Falar's fate.

`[[/outcome killed]]`

#### Ragen Attunement: Falar Killed

Any character who commits to slaying Falar rather than capturing him advances their **Attunement: Ragen (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

`[[/outcome captured]]`

#### Cora Attunement: Falar Captured

Any character who commits to capturing Falar advances their **Attunement: Cora (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Primordis Attunement: Falar Convinced

Any character who convinces Falar to surrender advances their **Attunement: Primordis (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

`[[/outcome escaped]]`

#### Aura Attunement: Falar Released

Any character who refuses to pursue Falar and lets him escape advances their **Attunement: Aura (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.
