# A Sketchy Situation

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Combat Event occurs when the party enters [[Falar]] private studio. By investigating the studio, the characters can:
>
> - Fight a pack of [[Paint Globlin]] that were set in place to attack anyone who breaks into his studio.
> - Learn more about Falar's past and the difficulties he suffered that led him to his current state.
> - Discover his next move in the city and where he plans to carry it out.
>
> #### Area Walkthrough
>
> The party begins in the [[Flotsam Canal Market]] Scene, where the central gameplay of this Event transpires. A complete room-by-room description of the market environment and the gameplay that occurs there is detailed in the [[Flotsam Canal Market]] Area Walkthrough.

### Collecting Evidence

There are three major points of information the party needs to collect to complete their investigation into [[Falar's Studio (Upper)]] in the Flotsam Canal Market:

- The party spends time studying the art found in Falar's hidden studio. This should let them conclude that he is trying to copy Varinna's art to frame her.
- The party acquires either [[Falar's Studio Notes]] or [[Notes on Elemental Binding]] (or both). This is evidence that proves Falar developed a way to lure and capture elementals, including the paint globlins. The notes themselves aren't complete, however, and can't be used by the party in any mechanical way.
- The party finds [[Falar's Curious Note]]. This item is a direct hint for the party to head to [[Redwalk Ramble]] to intercept Falar before he seriously harms someone there. It can also be used as evidence against him later.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has fended off the Paint Globlin ambush and collected their evidence, they should have enough information to know that Redwalk Ramble is Falar's next target, and thus their next destination. There, they will confront Falar directly in the [[Matters of Perspective]] Event.
