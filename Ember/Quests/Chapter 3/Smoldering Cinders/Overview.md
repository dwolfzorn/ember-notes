# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This main quest occurs during Chapter 3 amidst the great city of [[Ordain]], and is intended to begin 3 days after the party has completed [[A Sage Welcome]], as the characters return to [[Cindarin Temple]] for [[Sin Marmot]]'s initiation ceremony.
>
> Unlike the events of Chapter 2, each of the Chapter 3 main quests can occur simultaneously, although the characters must manage their time accordingly for various encounters and events to proceed with certain measures of success.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> The "Smoldering Cinders" quest is designed for a party of Level 5 characters.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Main Quest that awards 3 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon completion of the [[The Secret Autopsy]], [[The Derelict Protector]], and [[Speaker Silenced]] Events.
>
> #### Point of No Return: Grave Assignments
>
> The [[Grave Assignments]] Event serves as a point of no return for certain Chapter 3 Main Quests. Once the characters complete this Event, they can no longer continue or complete the Events of [[Disgraced House]] and [[Spreading Sickness]].

The dead are restless in the city of Ordain. Amidst [[Sin Marmot]]'s initiation into the benevolent order of [[Cindaric Sages]], two separate incidents involving undead creatures will lead the party on a hunt for answers about how this macabre calamity came to be. "Smoldering Cinders" is a tale of faith, of succession, and of unseen horrors. In addition to its inherent mystery and adventure, this quest will allow the party to examine and question the philosophies behind Ember's [[Soul Cycle]], particularly as they interact with the people who represent it most within the setting — including the noble Cindaric Sages, along with the High Priest of [[Sockets]] (the Elder God of Death) and [[Sionia]] (the Shard Goddess of Necromancy).

After Sin's initiation, the party will help her solve a mystery in [[Westgate]] involving a curious cadaver, a corpse that turns out to be some kind of [[Vampyre Spawn]]. Meanwhile, [[Steros Kraver]] of the [[Burnished Hand]] is interested in courting the characters to join his cause as Protectors, a meeting that inadvertently leads them to another horrific encounter in [[Fallbrook]] with something known as a [[Harrower]]. After these two distinct occurrences, the party must look to the local experts for answers; and the Shard Goddess Sionia has one final task that will lead them into the subterranean heart of Ordain's [[Burial Grounds]] — where a deadly encounter with a monstrous [[Corpuleth]] hematophage and a horde of [[Skallith]] minions will test the very depths of their courage and capacity to survive. And all the while, severe changes in the Cindaric order await Sin and the party when they least expect it.
