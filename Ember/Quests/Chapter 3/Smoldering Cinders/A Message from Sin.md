# A Message from Sin

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Social Event the party receives a message from [[Sin Marmot]] to visit her at the Old City Library in the [[Scholar's Nook]] district. In this event, the characters can:
>
> - Respond to or ignore the invitation.
> - Gather information about Scholar's Nook prior to their visit.
>
> This event is designed to occur wherever the characters might be when the requisite amount of time has passed, rather than at a specific location. The readalouds have been constructed accordingly, but feel free to make any necessary adjustments to suit the party and the moment.

### A Curious Visitor

When the Tatterwing Moth arrives, the party has a brief moment to observe the creature directly and begin making some deductions about its peculiar nature and intent.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Identifying the Moth
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Wilderness (DC 11)** check is able to identify the tatterwing moth as native to the Arctus Plateau. Tatterwing moths are known to inhabit forest, grassland, swamp, and subterranean biomes, but are relatively scarce in urban environments.
>
> - `[[/knowledge beats]]`: The character gains **+2 Boons**.
>
> Characters with a **`[[/skill animalhandling 11 passive]]`** are able to notice that the behavior of the moth is peculiar and uncanny, as if it was being motivated by an outside influence.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 13)** check is able to recognize a magical gleam in the moth's eyes and traces of a latent supernatural aura, details that signify the moth's role as an arcane (or perhaps druidic) familiar.

### Sin's Message

The tatterwing moth carries a message from Sin Marmot: she's trapped within the library vaults beneath the Old City Library, and she needs the party's discreet help to escape. After all, she doesn't want to earn any demerits in her new role as an initiate of the Cindaric order.

After the characters have had a moment to survey the curious scene, continue with the following readaloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The moth speaks with a tiny whisper of a voice, but you can hear its words quite plainly if you focus your attention and lean in to listen.
>
> > Salutations, friends! Believe it or not, this is your old pal Sin Marmot speaking. But this isn't actually me, here and now. I'm still in Scholar's Nook, and I need your help.
> >
> > I'm stuck in the lower stacks of Old City Library, and I'm afraid to reach out to the other sages. I just started my training, and the last thing I need is a demerit for a bad job. If you can find it in your hearts, please come to Scholar's Nook and make your way to the lower stacks by any clandestine means necessary. That's all I can say for now. It's getting hectic over here. Please hurry!
>
> With that, the special gleam in the moth's eye disappears and the tiny creature flutters off and away with the wind.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Identifying the Moth's Magic
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 13)** check can determine the following details. The character is able to surmise that Sin is using some bespoke primal magic to communicate via the moth, rather than the shapechanging abilities she's also known for.
>
> - **Class is Druid**: The character gains **+2 Boons**.
>
> The [[Detect Magic]] spell reveals traces of Illusion and Conjuration magic, but the aura is vague.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Gathering Information: Scholar's Nook
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **`[[/skill charisma 13]]`** or **Awareness (DC 13)** check is able to round up some conversation about the Scholar's Nook district from a local Ordani citizen. Highlights include:
>
> - The Old City Library is also known as Tomehold, and is famed for the countless books that line its dusty shelves.
> - The five Coalescents who run the Old City Library are an eccentric group of Cindaric Sages, and rumor has it that they're all collectors of antiquity, with a friendly streak of competition among them.
> - If you're in need of a low-level spell scroll, the folks at The Secret Shelf can help.
>
> - **Path: Cindaric Initiate** : The character gains **+2 Boons**.

### Concluding the Event

This quest event is relatively brief, and the characters are Sin's only recourse if she hopes to escape her unfortunate predicament.
