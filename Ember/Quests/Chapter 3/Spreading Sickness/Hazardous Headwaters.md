# Hazardous Headwaters

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This combat event sees the party discovering and infiltrating a [[Mutagists]] laboratory hidden in ancient ruins under Ordain. In this event, the characters can:
>
> - Locate victims of a kidnapping ring, and greater machinations of the Mutagists and their allies.
> - Discover the macabre experiments of the mutagists and the results of them.
> - Stumble upon ancient ruins belonging to the long dead Sedyri people.
>
> #### Challenge Level
>
> This event is balanced for a level 5 party. A group of level 4 heroes can be successful but will need to employ careful strategy. If the party is not yet level four, it would be advised for them to seek other challenges and revisit this event later.
>
> #### Area Walkthrough
>
> The initial exposition of this event takes place on the region map until the party chooses their preferred approach to the hideout. Once that choice has been made, gameplay transitions to the "Forgotten Cistern" Scene where the central gameplay of this event transpires.
>
> Once inside the scene, a complete room-by-room description of its environment and the gameplay that occurs there is detailed in the [[Forgotten Cistern]] area walkthrough. This area has several special rules and gameplay expectations, so it is advisable to review the [[Gameplay Details]] page either before or during the party's exploration of the location.

After investigating the districts of Ordain for the source of the sickness, the party in concert with Ankarist now have enough information to make an educated guess on the origin of the contaminant: the Destine basin.

With Ankarist reassigned to another task, the party has been left to handle this leg of the investigation themselves. They've been asked to collect evidence, and try to find the source of the poison seeping into the water.

They are about to find more than that by quite a bit.

### Shutting Down the Lab

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Raiding the Lab
>
> The party will ultimately want to do the following to consider this event complete:
>
> - Defeat or capture the Mutagists and their Drakes.
> - Find and save any victims of the Mutagists in the area.
> - Confiscate or document the alchemical materials and machinery being used by the Mutagists.
> - Collect any evidence that would help shed light on the goals and operations of the Mutagists as a whole.

#### Luxarum Attunement: Mutagists Killed

If the party kills the Mutagists during their raid of the lab, each character advances their **Attunement: Luxarum (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

#### Cora Attunement: Mutagists Captured

If the party captures the Mutagists during their raid of the lab, each character advances their **Attunement: Cora (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

#### Heart Attunement: Victims Rescued

If the party manages to successfully rescue the kidnapped victims during their raid of the Mutagist lab, each character advances their **Attunement: Heart of Ember (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> After invading the Mutagist Lab and discovering the furtive origins of the toxicity, and evidence that there is something more afoot. The party must return to the Veiled Chain headquarters in the Hallows to share their findings.
