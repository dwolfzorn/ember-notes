# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This side quest occurs during Chapter 3, and can begin any time after the party arrives in Ordain.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> "Spreading Sickness" is designed for a party of Level 4 characters.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest that awards 3 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon successful completion of the [[Secondary Sources]], [[Missing Persons]], and [[Unhallowed]] Events.
>
> #### Point of No Return: The Marlstone Gala
>
> The [[Midnight Meeting]] Event serves as a point of no return for certain Chapter 3 Main Quests. Once the characters complete this Event, they can no longer continue or complete the Events of [[Disgraced House]] and [[Smoldering Cinders]].

Summoned to the Hallows to once more join forces with Ankarist to work on a investigation of critical importance. A new, dangerous sickness has appeared in Ordain, and already threatens the lives of dozens of citizens, and Veiled Chain now relies on the help of the party to make the difference. However, this problem is not as simple as it seems, and old enemies lurk in dark, forgotten places within Ordain.

Armed with leads across several city districts, the party searches for weaknesses in the city’s infrastructure, interviews healers about a new illness afflicting forge workers along the waterways, and eventually discovers a dangerous contaminant flowing through the mighty Destine River and threatening to poison all of Ordain.

The investigation takes them deep underground to ancient ruins that predate Ordain, where they uncover old foes — the Mutagists — at their usual tricks. They find human experimentation, deadly new Drake variants, and a harrowing plan to harvest Ember energy from human bodies.

Yet another layer of intrigue awaits when they return to the surface: someone is inciting Ordain’s common folk and refugees, turning them against the city. With a violent uprising brewing, the party must go undercover to find the movement’s core and shut it down before things spiral out of control.
