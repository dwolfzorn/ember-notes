# An Unexpected Requisition

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This social event sees the party receiving a request to meet from [[Ankarist]]. In short, the party:
>
> - Is contacted by a city courier and given a letter.
> - Receives instructions in the letter on how to reach Ankarist.
> - Can speak to the courier, though the courier knows little.

### Taking the Letter

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you take the letter, the courier pulls out a notepad and pencil, makes a note about the delivery, and wishes you a good day. Then they head off to their next delivery.

The party can stop the courier to ask questions, but the courier knows little and will leave promptly afterward.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Who hired you?
>
> **A:**
>
> > I work for the city. I was assigned this message along with a bunch of others. This one did come directly from a Veiled Chain source though, if you go by the stamp on the envelope. Hopefully you’re not in any trouble!

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** How did you know where to find us?
>
> **A:**
>
> The courier holds up a note for you to see, but not long enough to read the text on it.
>
> > I was given delivery instructions. The Chain seemed to know where to find you, which isn’t a shock. They watch everything and know who’s coming and going. Honestly, I doubt there’s much that happens in the city they don’t know about.

### Opening the Letter

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Envelope
>
> The envelope is stamped with both a Veiled Chain symbol, and a symbol of the Hallows, with a number scribbled on the corner.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Politics** or **Culture: Ordani** know that the Hallows handles post in the city free of charge. The ink stamps indicate several things: the official Veiled Chain and Hallows stamps show that both organizations handled the documents directly. The Hallows stamp is common on city post. The number is for record-keeping, as the city handles thousands of messages a day.
>
> #### Reading the Letter
>
> The message within is handwritten, from Ankarist:
>
> > I hope this message finds you well,
> >
> > I received word that you had arrived in the city, and your timing couldn't be better. There is a strange sickness affecting the people of Ordain, and I suspect it may be related to our previous cases I've worked.
> >
> > As the Veiled Chain is light on manpower, and I need help, I would like to hire you on to assist with the investigation. Come to the Hallows and go to the entrance to the Underfoot. Then follow the directions below to reach me.
> >
> > -Ankarist
>
> The attached directions are a series of turns and landmarks for traversing the Underfoot.
>
> Characters that have either **Knowledge: Intrigue** or **Culture: Ordani** or who make a successful **Society (DC 14, Passive)** know that Ordain was built over existing ruins and ancient pathways. The Hallows is home to the Underfoot, a network of tunnels and chambers rumored to hold secret meeting places and underground structures if one knows which turns to take.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has read the letter, they can head toward the Hallows to meet Ankarist.
