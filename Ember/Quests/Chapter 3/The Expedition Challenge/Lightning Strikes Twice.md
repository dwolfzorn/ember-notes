# Lightning Strikes Twice

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Social Event connects the party with the [[Fulgurite Blades]] during a chance meeting, as both groups traverse the biomes of [[Ordain]] in anticipation of the upcoming Expedition Challenge. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Happen upon the Fulgurite Blades, a group of [[Anachraenum]] adventurers who the party may have previously met during [[Curious Party]], as part of the [[Over The Moon]] Side Quest.
> - Learn about the forthcoming Expedition Challenge and the Fulgurite's intentions to win the contest.
> - Choose to either ally themselves with the Fulgurite Blades or become their steadfast rivals in the Expedition Challenge.
>
> This Event is depicted using the "Residential Corridor" Level of the [[Vista: Ordain Streets]] Vista.

### New Challengers

If the party has already met the Fulgurite Blades during [[Curious Party]], skip this section and refer to [[Lightning Strikes Twice]] below. Otherwise, read the following aloud as the characters meet the rival group of adventurers for the first time.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you approach the adventurers, a massive Cor'ak among them regards your group with a small measure of curiosity.
>
> > *Hey, you! Stop right there. What do you want?*
>
> Beside him, a Kivahr woman reads from a book that floats idly in her hand. She slowly looks up to scan your group with indifference before retreating back into her book. As she does, she calls out to a tall Nir'ae mage nearby.
>
> > *Sajor!*
>
> The Nir'ae mage — a bright-eyed woman with a curious expression — doesn't miss a beat, and makes her way towards you. Meanwhile, a fidgety Thornling nearby twirls a dagger as he watches the exchange. The Nir'ae caster speaks, and the tiniest measure of annoyance hides behind inquisitiveness in her voice.
>
> > *Well met, travelers … Can we help you?*

Led by Sajor, the adventuring party known as the Fulgurite Blades are junior members of the [[Anachraenum]], and are in the early stages of their preparation for the Expedition Challenge. Like many contestants in the legendary competition, the Fulgurites are wary of rivals and strangers during this exciting time. The characters can speak with Sajor and the Fulgurite Blades, but the quality of this exchange depends on how the party reacts to the other group's abrasiveness.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with the Fulgurites
>
> If the characters attempt to speak with the Fulgurite Blades, the quartet of Anachraenum adventurers are reluctantly willing to discuss the following information:
>
> - The Fulgurite Blades are a trainee adventuring party working for the Anachraenum. They are an established team, having been together for several years at this point, although some have been in the guild longer. During this time together, they have become a tight-knit and loyal group.
> - Leeph is the most recent member, having joined only a few months ago.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 16)** check is also able to learn the following:
>
> - Sajor is the leader of this group, and the other three members regard her with unflinching loyalty and dedication.
> - The group recently returned from a mission in the Forest of Stone, where they successfully uncovered a major find. Unfortunately, the guild didn't fully recognize their efforts, and the Fulgurites must compete in the Expedition Challenge to claim the accolades they deserve.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 13)** check can readily tell that Sajor has clearly earned the respect and admiration of her allies.
>
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character is assured by the relative transparency of the Fulgurite Blades and their goals as a party. Each of the members may have their own personal motivations, but they are unified as a team.
>
> #### Kazra's Faith: A Sect of Spectra
>
> If the characters speak to [[Kazra Steelshift]] about matters of religion or her particular faith, she explains that she is a member of the Sect of Arcvold — a rare faction of [[Spectra]] devotees who believe that the pursuit of magic and arcana leads to spiritual enlightenment. Characters who are followers or clerics of Spectra themselves will find Kazra to be more friendly with them.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 16)** check knows that the Sect of Arcvold was once a major faction within the [[Aedir]] Empire's religious groups. The Sect of Arcvold appears sporadically throughout ancient texts, and scholars maintain the mysterious sect established hidden temples across the world that remain lost to this very day. The sect is also associated with darker practices, including rituals of self-inflicted pain that are said to have enhanced their mental focus.
>
> - **Knowledge: Aedir**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Ancients**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

### Reunited Parties

If the party has already met the Fulgurite Blades during [[Curious Party]], read the following aloud as the characters reconnect with the rival group.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Sajor waves towards you with a smug expression:
>
> > Well, look who it is!
>
> Leeph offers an excited salutation.
>
> > Hi! You're looking no more worse for wear after the temple. That's good.
>
> Sajor remarks:
>
> > Yes, well, there wasn't much danger left. They were always going to be fine.
>
> To this Rorhim grunts, and adds:
>
> > Ought to give them something for their help, Sajor.
>
> Sajor scoffs, giving a dismissive wave.
>
> > They got their credit in the discovery filing.
>
> The normally quiet Kazra speaks up from the back of the group, drawing a few eyes from her cohorts.
>
> > Not what he means, Sajor, and he's right. We did make quite a bit of coin and they played a part in helping us achieve that."
>
> At this, there's a nod from Rorhim. A moment later several sets of expectant eyes settle on Sajor, who sighs a touch dramatically.
>
> > Fine…
>
> She pulls a coin purse off her belt and tosses it toward you.
>
> > Now, was there anything else?

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### A Friendly Reward
>
> Sajor throws a coin purse containing **`[[/award party 50gp]]`** to the nearest party member.

> [!info] Social
> #### Reading the Blades
>
> The Fulgurite Blades work together cohesively as a group. Nevertheless, each of them does harbor their own ambitions.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill ins 15 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Deception (DC 13)** check is able to intuit a few details about each Fulgurite Blade and their reaction to previous encounters with the party.
>
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: All four of the Blades want nothing more than to win the challenge and will likely do anything within their power to achieve that goal, even if it means opposing or fighting other teams.
>
> **Sajor**
>
> Sajor doesn't seem to regret the group's actions (not immediately at least). It's only as her team expresses misgivings that she'll start to second guess her leadership. Ultimately, she is not a bad person — she just prioritizes her team over everyone else.
>
> **Rorhim Ironcask**
>
> This veteran fighter doesn't feel any particular remorse for how things went, but does believe that the party deserves fair proper compensation for putting their necks on the line. He believes it isn't smart to make new enemies, especially if it can be avoided.
>
> **Kazra Steelshift**
>
> Kazra believes that things should be done to a respectable standard — people paid, credit given, and good relations maintained. She isn't about to start a fight with Sajor about it, but she demonstrably thinks it's her job to bring these standards to the forefront (and record the outcome in the process).
>
> **Leeph**
>
> This chatty little rogue is ultimately ambivalent toward the party and Sajor's actions. Leeph want what's best for Leeph (and the Fulgurite Blades), and generally follows Sajor's lead with ease.

### Potential Rivals

If the characters ask the Fulgurite Blades about their intentions to join the Expedition Challenge or for details about how to participate in the contest, read the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Sajor examines your group carefully before narrowing her eyes.
>
> > I see … Well, you'll need to tread carefully, friends. I can see that you've gained some experience since we last met, but I assure you: the Fulgurite Blades intend to be the champions of this year's Expedition Challenge.

> [!info] Social
> #### About the Expedition Challenge
>
> If the party continues on this topic of questioning, they can glean the following opinions from the Fulgurite Blades:
>
> - It's clear that the Blades have already signed up for Expedition Challenge and are waiting in Ordain for the event to begin.
> - Run by the [[Anachraenum]], the Expedition Challenge is the second competition of its kind. The first was held five years ago and was won by the famed adventurer [[Fernis Ossa]] and with her team, the Skywarders.
> - Those who win the Expedition Challenge are granted immediate membership in the Anachraenum, plus a pouch full of gold and the chance to borrow one of the guild's archival artifacts.
> - Contestants can compete alone or in teams, but you have a better chance of winning the challenge if you have a strong group.
> - Sajor isn't precisely sure when the challenge will occur, but she knows it could happen any day now. She is waiting impatiently with the rest of the Blades for news from All-Fable Keep.
>
> The Blades are trainee members of the Anachraenum and have been for a few years, except for Leeph — who joined recently and is not currently a trainee member. They generally see the guild of explorers and treasure hunters in a positive light, but often find themselves frustrated with the guild's slow and almost bureaucratic nature. Ultimately, the Fulgurite Blades feel as though they've been slightly overlooked of late.

If the party is friendly with the Fulgurite Blades during this conversation and in previous interactions, then the group will be less likely to attack them or be aggressive during the upcoming Expedition Challenge.

#### Cora Attunement: Friends with the Blades

If the party befriends the Fulgurite Blades, each character advances their **Attunement: Cora (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

`[[/outcome friends]]`

However, if the party is not friendly to the Blades during this conversation and in previous interactions, then the group will be more likely to attack them and be aggressive during the upcoming Expedition Challenge.

#### Aura Attunement: New Rivals

If the party makes enemies of the Fulgurite Blades, each character advances their **Attunement: Aura (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

`[[/outcome rivals]]`

Once the party is finished with their questioning, the Blades will continue to talk amongst themselves, making a deliberate effort to ignore the party.
