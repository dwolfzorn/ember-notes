# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Main Quest occurs during Chapter 3 amidst the great city of [[Ordain]], and is intended to begin when the party is approached by [[Fernis Ossa]] of the [[Anachraenum]] during [[An Auspicious Acquaintance]]. Alternately, the party may begin the Quest via the [[An Upcoming Challenge]] or [[Scholar in Need]] Events.
>
> Unlike the events of Chapter 2, each of the Chapter 3 Main Quests can occur simultaneously, although the characters must manage their time accordingly for various encounters and Events to proceed with certain measures of success.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> "The Expedition Challenge" is designed for a party of Level 5 characters.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Main Quest that awards 3 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon completion of the [[Reclaiming the Concourse]], [[The Wayfinder's Gauntlet]], and [[Closing Ceremonies]] Events.

Ten years ago, the [[Anachraenum]] hosted the first Expedition Challenge as a way to test the skills of regional adventurers and bring the city of Ordain together in the process. The intrepid [[Fernis Ossa]] emerged victorious alongside her party, the Skywarders, who became overnight celebrities. In the year that followed, the Skywarders achieved many other accolades — such as their delve into an ancient skyhold — and many began to view Fernis as the natural successor to then-guildmaster Veron Longspear. The Skywarders eventually disbanded, and Fernis Ossa's responsibilities for the guild became greater, with her reporting directly to Longspear in the days to follow. However, Veron Longspear was killed under mysterious circumstances a year ago while Fernis was visiting the [[Kithil]] city of [[Luen]]. Upon her return, Fernis was deeply concerned that something sinister was brewing in Ordain, and her trust of other members of the Anachraenum diminished. While the other members offered her the position of guildmaster, Fernis ultimately refused, and the untested [[Arcos Sarinland]] took over in her stead. Plans to host another Expedition Challenge are currently underway, and Fernis has started to formulate a plan to uncover the truth behind Veron's death and the growing troubles in the city.

This year's Expedition Challenge is expected to be a grand affair, testing the mettle of various new adventuring parties while finding new recruits for the ranks of the Anachraenum. The expedition begins with three smaller challenges (which can be completed in any order) that each award a Challenge Key, followed by one final trial that requires all three keys to access: the mysterious Chamber of Agaseros beneath All-Fable Keep, where a dangerous magical gauntlet awaits.

The party will be pitted against other teams of adventurers, who will appear periodically throughout the contest to apply pressure on the characters; however, these rivals are not intended to be present during the completion of the smaller trials themselves. The ultimate winners of the Expedition Challenge will be the party that completes all three smaller challenges to reach the end of the Chamber of Agaseros in the fastest time. All parties are magically tracked by guildmaster Arcos Sarinland and loremistress Adelyne Goss; and no matter how a party finishes in the challenge, all are given the option to join the Anachraenum at the end (unless they repeatedly demonstrate that they are not Anachraenum material). If the characters can emerge victorious, they'll take home a share of gold, a magic item from the Amaranthine Archives, and Fernis Ossa's respect in the mysterious days to come.
