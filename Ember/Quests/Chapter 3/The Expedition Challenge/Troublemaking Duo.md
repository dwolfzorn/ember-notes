# Troublemaking Duo

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> In this Social Event, the party encounters a mischievous pair of [[Waerd]] adventurers while traversing the [[Golden Flats]] — treasure hunters who are on their way to Ordain to join the [[Anachraenum]] Expedition Challenge. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Meet Mug & Cap, two foreign adventurers who are eager to join the Expedition Challenge in Ordain.
> - Learn a few details about the Anachraenum and the Expedition Challenge.
> - Navigate Mug and Cap's playful attempt to sabotage the party's participation in the Challenge with a bit of misinformation.
>
> This Event is depicted using the "Ridge Valley Road" Level of the [[Vista: Golden Flats]] Vista.

#### Portrait & Vista WIP

The Mug & Cap NPCs are intended to have Actors with associated stat blocks, bios, and portraits — the latter of which will be used to populate the Vista during gameplay.

### Meeting Mug & Cap

Before the party interacts with these two travelers, they can determine a few minor details from observation alone.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Scrutinizing the Duo
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Society (DC 12)**check can recognize that both of these travelers are [[Waerd]] in origin, and are covered in the "Waix" tattoos traditional to their people, markings that celebrate triumph in battle and hunting prowess. It's very likely that these two are experienced monster hunters (as corroborated by the ample weapons and armor they travel with).
>
> - **Culture: Waerd**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Path: Monster Hunter**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character has encountered Waix tattoos before, and knows that Waerds ritually tattoo themselves throughout their entire lives — recording important milestones, hunts, or battles they've taken part in via an array of intricate [[Lowland]] patterns and symbols.

If the party decides to interact with these two curious travelers, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The two travelers turn your way. Both are covered in striking tattoos and sport a number of curved, wicked-looking weapons strapped to scale mail made from what appears to be a creature of some kind.
>
> > Oh, who are you then?
>
> The Thornling speaks in a voice similar to a small child's and looks at you quizzically before tilting their head, awaiting an answer.
>
> > Well, clearly they are adventurers like us, Cap, I mean, look at them..
>
> The taller figure is also staring at you intensely, with his hands hovering near the sharp-looking short sword at his belt. The Thornling, however, seems more at ease and immediately perks up, hurriedly replying.
>
> > Oh, well, of course! Where are our manners? We are Mug and Cap! Fearsome adventurers from Oshclaw, are you heading to Ordain for the Expedition Challenge as well?

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with Mug & Cap
>
> These two Waerd monster hunters are on their way to Ordain to compete in the Anachraenum's Expedition Challenge, and while they aren't exactly keeping their plans a secret, they also aren't interested in advertising the contest to potential rivals. Their attitude towards the party is aloof, and full of civilized snark. Treat both Mug & Cap as [[Anachraenum Adventurer]].
>
> **Mug (Chaotic Neutral, Waerd Kiska-Wirrun, he/him)**
>
> Mug likes to play the "bad cop."
>
> **Cap (Chaotic Neutral, Waerd Thornling, they/them)**
>
> Cap likes to play the "good cop."
>
> Mug & Cap will sardonically engage in the following topics of conversation while casually attempting to dissuade the party from participating in the Expedition Challenge:
>
> - Their mission to compete in the Expedition Challenge in [[Ordain]], a legendary competition hosted by the [[Anachraenum]] to acknowledge and reward the most skilled treasure hunters in [[Aterica]].
> - A brief account of the Anachraenum, the region's scholastic guild of treasure hunters and explorers, who reside at [[All-Fable Keep]] in the [[Ordain Spires]] of Ordain.
> - Their origins as [[Waerd]] monster hunters from [[Oshclaw]] in the [[Lowlands]].
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)**check can intuit that Mug & Cap are friendly, but not entirely truthful, and can detect a glimmer of apprehension and suspicion behind the words they choose.
>
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: Mug & Cap are not hostile and are not interested in fighting the party, but they would very much prefer less competition in the Expedition Challenge and are trying to scare the party off.
>
> If the party accuses Mug & Cap of lying to them or trying to mislead them, the duo will sheepishly apologize. They don't possess any ill intent, and were simply trying to scare the party off — which they will openly admit as they attempt to smooth things over.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** The Expedition Challenge?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Oh! The Expedition Challenge is an event run by this guild in Ordain, you see, called the Anachraenum. You compete with other groups or teams in super deadly challenges and win gold and the location of some secret dungeon that the Anachraenum keeps hidden if you win. We heard about the last one, five years ago, and the winner ... what was her name ... Ferris, Ferna, Fernis ... something or other! She became rich and famous afterward, I think. Anyway, when we heard they were going to run another one this year, we got on the next boat to the Arctus Plateau!

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** The Anachraenum
>
> **A:**
>
> > The Anachraenum is simply the fancy name for the Lore Hunters guild! It's like this big organization of archaeologists, explorers, treasure seekers, and adventurers who travel around the world, exploring ruins and discovering treasure. Their home base is in Ordain, and they have this massive castle called the All-Fable Keep where they keep all the stuff they find!
> >
> > We've never been to Ordain before but have heard tales of the adventuring Anachraenum in our homeland. They are all about treasure seeking, monster slaying, and all-round exploration which suits us perfectly.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Your travel?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Didn't ya hear us before? We're from Oshclaw!
> >
> > We've been traveling across the Arctus Plateau for several weeks, left from Toraga. Its been pretty quiet, too quiet, too boring compared to the Lowlands. It's so peaceful here, ugh.
> >
> > We did get to kill a rampaging monster about two weeks ago, some kind of giant two-legged bird. Nothing fun like that recently, though.

As the party leaves Mug & Cap, read the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you turn away from Mug and Cap, the argument between the two begins again, each fervently trying to gain the upper hand. Gradually, however, it fades into the background behind you, and the sounds of the Golden Flats and the rustle of nearby grasses rises once more.
