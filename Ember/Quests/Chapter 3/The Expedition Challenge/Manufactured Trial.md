# Manufactured Trial

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Combat Event takes place northeast of [[Seawall]] at [[Jekeroka Villa]], where the party must endure the [[Anachraenum]] facility's numerous challenges. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Survive the villa's assorted traps and hazards, intended to test the party's alertness.
> - Encounter [[Anachraenum Aetherial]], constructed and programmed to test the party's combat skills.
> - Conquer a devious puzzle that intends to test the party's problem-solving acumen, and retrieve the [[Trial Challenge Key]] in the process.
>
> This Event is depicted using the [[Jekeroka Villa]] Area Map.

### Anachraenum Guidance

The party can continue their brief conversation with the loremistress, but the assistance she can provide for them is deliberately limited.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with Adelyne Goss
>
> [[Adelyne Goss]] is as terse and short-tempered as ever, and isn't particularly happy with her assignment here away from the amenities of [[All-Fable Keep]]. Nevertheless, Arcos tasked her with this duty, and she answered the call.
>
> Adelyne is reluctant but willing to discuss the following:
>
> - How to enter Jekeroka Villa by heading through the front doors, which currently stand open thanks to Adelyne (and the key in her inventory).
> - Scant details about the dig site itself, including the Aedir origin of the ruins.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 15)** check can convince Adelyne to let them know that the villa's interior is relatively small, but each room has a different challenge that they have to overcome.
>
> - **Path: Monster Hunter**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character convinces Adelyne to let them know one particular detail, revealing that another group of contestants began exploring the villa several hours ago — long enough to have either failed in their tasks, or to have activated the stasis fields of each character's [[Frozen Tear]].

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Can you help us?
>
> **A:**
>
> > No, I won't be giving you any real help whatsoever. I'm only here to observe, and to make sure no one dies. If I hear any signs of real danger, I'll intervene; but you won't get any information from me about what's inside. Rest assured that it's been calibrated in a way to test your dungeon-delving skills. For whatever that's worth.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About the villa?
>
> **A:**
>
> Adelyne's tone shifts slightly as her interest in the history of this location becomes clear.
>
> > After some careful study, we revealed the name of this place, which was once known as Jekeroka Villa to the ancient Aedir. Long before the Empire fell to ruin, this location served as the home workshop of a powerful Maejis. The place is magically fused with latent elemental energies, brought about from abstruse experimentation and mysteries lost to time.
> >
> > It should present a worthy challenge for you lot. I'll wager that.

### Exploring the Villa

The party is free to enter [[Jekeroka Villa]] whenever they're ready.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Completing the Challenge
>
> In order to retrieve the [[Trial Challenge Key]] from the hidden chest at the northern end of the [[Main Chamber]], the party must first defeat the four [[Anachraenum Aetherial]] who reside there.

#### Signara Attunement: Jekeroka's Ledger

If the party manages to find [[Jekeroka's Ledger]] while exploring the villa, each character advances their **Attunement: Signara (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> The party must gather all three Challenge Keys before they can return to [[All-Fable Keep]] for [[The Wayfinder's Gauntlet]], which occurs in the subterranean [[Chamber of Agaseros]]. If the party has yet to do so, they can find the other two keys at the following locations:
>
> - The [[Maze Challenge Key]] can be found at the [[Bramble Maze]] during [[Amazing Brambles]].
> - The [[Well Challenge Key]] can be found at [[Krator's Hall]] during [[Hidden Campsite]].
