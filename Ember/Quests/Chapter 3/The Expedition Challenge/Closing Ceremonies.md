# Closing Ceremonies

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Social Event occurs when the party returns to [[All-Fable Keep]] after surviving [[The Wayfinder's Gauntlet]], where they will finally meet with the [[Anachraenum]] Guildmaster [[Arcos Sarinland]] as they are duly rewarded for their participation in the Expedition Challenge. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Speak with Arcos about their performance in the Expedition Challenge, and earn a reward appropriate to the placement of their finish.
> - Accept their new standing as full members of the Anachraenum, with all the benefits that status entails.
> - Speak with [[Fernis Ossa]] about her lingering concerns of political upheaval in the Anachraenum, and plan the investigation to come.
>
> This Event is depicted using the [[The Arctus Plateau]] Region Map.

### Speaking with Arcos

The party can now speak with Arcos to receive their accolades — including full membership into the [[Anachraenum]] guild, a monetary reward, and their choice of a few select Magic Items from the guild archives.

> [!abstract] Arcos Sarinland
> **[[Arcos Sarinland]]**
>
> Level 1 · Unknown Unknown
>
> ![Arcos Sarinland](../../../assets/image137.webp)

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The guildmaster beams with joy as he steps forward, clasping your hands.
>
> > Well done, indeed! I trust the Chamber was a trial worthy of your talents, but not **too** dangerous in the end, I hope.
>
> He pauses for the briefest moment to take in your reaction before continuing.
>
> > First thing's first! You deserve the rewards you were promised. Here, take these."
>
> Arcos fetches a handful of shiny medallions from the pocket of his coat, and holds them out for you to take.
>
> > This medallion is not only a badge of honor … it signifies your status as full members of the Anachraenum, with all the benefits that entails. Indeed, you may even access the Amaranthine Archives here at the keep, where you can conduct your own studies — and borrow a relic or two, if you'd like. After all, we expect members to be well equipped!
>
> Arcos produces a small wooden chest from behind a desk nearby and opens it towards you, revealing a small collection of trinkets and baubles inside.
>
> > I've curated a few wondrous items for your consideration. But fear not — if none of these relics are to your liking, you may visit the Amaranthine Archives to trade it out with another, so to speak. With a small administration cost, of course.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### The Guildmaster's Rewards
>
> At this time, each character receives an [[Anachraenum Medallion]]. Arcos also hands the party a single leather [[Pouch]] containing **`[[/award party 300gp]]`**.
>
> Additionally, each of the characters can roll on the [[Amaranthine Archives Artifacts]] Table to receive a random Magic Item. At this time, the characters should only be able to view the "Unidentified" sections of these items.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with Arcos
>
> In the aftermath of the Expedition Challenge, Arcos is worried about many things, and while he is pleased with the party's success, he has also learned that they are working with Fernis in some capacity. This makes him suspicious of the party overall, and he doesn't fully trust them.
>
> Arcos is concerned about the results of the Challenge, and is willing to share the following information with the party:
>
> - Of all the groups participating , only the [[Fulgurite Blades]], the Salt Breakers, and the party themselves managed to conquer the [[Chamber of Agaseros]] (whether the party won the competition or not).
> - Mug & Cap went missing during the Keystone Challenge events.
> - The Lanterns all perished in the Chamber of Agaseros before [[Eveis Brightstone]] could save them.
> - Arcos can also explain the guild's expectations for the characters themselves, which are relatively simple and straightforward (if altogether negligible).
>
> If they enquire about the Amaranthine Archives Artifacts, any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 13)** check can tell that Arcos stifles a grin, amused by the notion of the characters receiving Magic Items with little-to-no knowledge of their specific abilities.
>
> - **Critical Success**: Arcos also seems to be preoccupied with some kind of distant concern. Perhaps this concern is guild-related, if not personal.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Expectations from the guild?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Nothing! You may go where you please, study, and explore at your own leisure. Of course, you do have access to the Amaranthine Archives, where you might help us further research the countless antiquities in our collections. Indeed, should your studies spur you on an excavation, we ask that you return to the keep with your finds — for which the guild will, of course, pay handsome rewards. Who knows, one day the archives might be stocked with your own discoveries!
> >
> > If you're looking for something more substantial, I may have a task for you to follow up on soon … I'm not sure if you've have any knowledge of the Crumbling Spire out in the Golden Flats to the southwest, but some new information has come my way that deserves scrutiny. More on this to come!

After the party has finished speaking with Arcos, he excuses himself to continue his duties elsewhere. Read the following aloud when the time is right:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> > Well then, I'm sure we'll be seeing each other again quite soon! Please come and see me if and when you have any further questions — but for now, I must get back to my duties as guildmaster. Congratulations, once again, on a superb first day in the Anachraenum.
>
> Arcos bows, and hurries out of the hall with a brisk, but enthusiastic gait, humming slightly to himself along the way.

### The Real Investigation Begins

Following the party's encounter with Arcos, Fernis Ossa will emerge from the shadows nearby to discuss the next steps of their ongoing investigation into the Anachraenum.

> [!abstract] Fernis Ossa
> **[[Fernis Ossa]]**
>
> Level 1 · Unknown Unknown
>
> ![Fernis Ossa](../../../assets/image135.webp)

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you begin to leave the hall, you notice a shape emerge from next to one of the wooden beams along the side of the main hall.
>
> > Well met friends, I trust the final challenge was not too arduous, hmm?
>
> Fernis winks at you and smiles broadly, before a shadow seems to flicker across her features for a moment.
>
> > Now that the challenge is over, we can begin our investigation. I’m sure that you will agree that something is happening both here within the Anachraenum and within the city at large. While I am focused on my guild, I’m not blind to the rising number of chaotic events, and I fear that Orodain is heading toward a climatic event that we are ill prepared to face. Together, I hope we can uncover the truth of things.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with Fernis
>
> Fernis is ecstatic with the party upon their completion of the Expulsion Challenge and welcomes them into the Anachraenum with open arms. She is also pleased that now she can proceed with her investigation into the death of the previous guildmaster, Veron Longspear.
>
> - Fernis will go on to explain that, while she has some ideas about where to start her investigation, she also needs the party to be patient.
> - She believes that Arcos will have no choice but to come to the party for help in the following days, as she has heard rumors that something massive has been uncovered.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Society (DC 15)** is able to convince Fernis to share one additional detail about her hunch: after thousands of years, people might have finally discovered a way to open the [[Crumbling Spire]] in the [[Golden Flats]]. This could mean that one of the largest and most important locations related to the [[Aedir]] outside the [[The Starshield]] will finally be able to be explored — offering up a treasure trove of knowledge and wealth (a prize that many in Ordain would likely kill for).

### Concluding the Event

The Expedition Challenge is now concluded, and the party has an undefined moment of relative rest and relaxation to enjoy before the next phase of Fernis Ossa's investigation begins. Furthermore, Arcos Sarinland has identified the characters as trustworthy allies, and will send them on a mission to the Crumbling Spire in the days and weeks to come.
