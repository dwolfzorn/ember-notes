# Reclaiming the Concourse

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### **Gamemaster's Summary**
>
> This Combat Event challenges the party to retake [[Toothbreaker Hideout]] in [[Ordain]] from the larcenous villain [[Raster Thorn]] and the dangerous [[Toothbreaker Thug]] who follow his rule. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Choose a method of approach once they locate Toothbreaker Hideout within Ordain's [[The Concourse]] district.
> - Explore the hideout and defeat its villainous inhabitants, a gang of malicious thieves and marauders known as the Toothbreakers, and their [[Scalemaw]] pets that inhabit the Concourse waterways.
> - Choose how to deal with the Toothbreaker leader Raster Thorn once they encounter him in [[Raster's Throne Room]] or elsewhere.
> - Report their findings back to [[Fernis Ossa]], who will provide the party with the recommendation they need to join the Expedition Challenge.
>
> This Event utilizes the [[The Arctus Plateau]] Region Map when it begins, and transitions to the [[Toothbreaker Hideout]] Area Map once the party chooses a method of approach (see [[Reclaiming the Concourse]] below).

### Planning an Approach

Once they arrive at the location's general vicinity in the [[The Concourse]], the party must choose their preferred method of entry to gain access to Toothbreaker Hideout, as detailed in [[An Auspicious Acquaintance]].

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Scene Transition
>
> The first part of this Event takes place on the [[The Arctus Plateau]] Region Map. Once the party chooses their method of approach and completes the accompanying gameplay, mark the corresponding outcome as complete, which will automatically transition the Event to take place on the [[Toothbreaker Hideout]] Area Map.

### The Front Entrance

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Frontal Assault
>
> The directions Fernis provided for the party are not difficult to follow, and lead the characters directly to the front entrance of Toothbreaker Hideout, where the characters can choose between the front door of the [[Canal Path]] or the [[Culvert Entrance]] in the water below.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 13)** check is able to gather some information about the route, and recognize the tactical disadvantages that a frontal assault would face. Narrow corridors, chokepoints, and unfamiliar terrain would all pose disadvantages to an attacking force, not to mention that the party will be outnumbered. However, it could be possible for the characters to talk their way in, if they can fabricate a business proposition that is sufficiently compelling to garner the gang's attention.
>
> - **Knowledge: Warfare**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Crime**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Fernis has cautioned that this front entrance is secure and well-guarded at all times. Trying to enter the hideout in this way will require social cunning or a direct assault against [[Raster Thorn]] and his [[Toothbreaker Thug]].

If the party chooses this method of entry, mark the following outcome to transition the scene from the [[The Arctus Plateau]] Region Map to the [[Toothbreaker Hideout]] Area Map. The characters will begin exploration of the hideout on the exterior [[Canal Path]], near the front door and the [[Culvert Entrance]].

`[[/outcome front]]`

#### Ragen Attunement: Frontal Assault

Each character who infiltrates the Toothbreaker Hideout via a direct frontal assault advances their **Attunement: Ragen (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### The Waterworks

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Canal Infiltration
>
> Fernis also provided instructions on how to reach the hideout via the waterworks — a labyrinthine network of subterranean canals and tunnels. The directions are complex, requiring concentration and a sharp memory to navigate the area, which takes the party precisely one hour to complete.
>
> The party must undertake a **`[[/skillCheck pathfinding 16 group]]`** check. On a failed result, the party loses their bearings and is forced to retreat to the surface, but they may re-attempt the challenge at the cost of one more hour of time.
>
> - **Knowledge: Subterranea**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> If the party fails this group check 3 times in a row, all members of the party gain the **Exhausted** condition.

If the party chooses this method of entry, mark the following outcome to transition the scene from the [[The Arctus Plateau]] Region Map to the [[Toothbreaker Hideout]] Area Map. The characters will begin exploration of the hideout at the interior [[Northern Canals]] located near the northwest corner of the map.

`[[/outcome canals]]`

#### Primordis Attunement: Canal Infiltration

Each character who infiltrates the Toothbreaker Hideout via the canal advances their **Attunement: Primordis (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Taken Captive

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Seeking Captivity
>
> Fernis acknowledged a third path for breaching the hideout, which is to become prisoners of the Toothbreakers to be used as fighters in their underground arena. To succeed at this gambit the party must perform the following steps, in order.
>
> **1. Find Some Toothbreakers**
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 15)** check is able to locate a group of [[Toothbreaker Thug]] somewhere in the [[The Concourse]] district. Each attempt at this check consumes one hour of time.
>
> - **Knowledge: Crime**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> **2. Start a Fight**
>
> Any character who makes either a successful **Deception (DC 13)** or **Intimidation (DC 14)** check is able to adequately provoke the Toothbreakers into starting a brawl. On a failure, the Toothbreaker Thugs are uninterested, and the characters must seek out a different group by repeating the "Find Some Toothbreakers" step above.
>
> - **Knowledge: Politics**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Attunement: Ragen**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Crime**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character can successfully drag one of their allies into the fight, regardless of that ally's result.
>
> **3. Lose the Fight**
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Performance (DC 13)** check is able to convincingly lose the fight, and is taken as a prisoner. On a failure, the character takes damage from the **Toothbreaker Blows (Hazard 8, Bludgeoning)** during the process of being subdued.
>
> - **Knowledge: Warfare**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Attunement: Mayis**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Once any involved characters manage to successfully complete all 3 of these steps, they are taken captive by the Toothbreaker Thugs and forcefully escorted to the hideout's [[Prison]].

If the party chooses this method of entry, mark the following outcome to transition the scene from the [[The Arctus Plateau]] Region Map to the [[Toothbreaker Hideout]] Area Map. The characters will begin exploration of the hideout as captives in the [[Prison]].

`[[/outcome captives]]`

#### Mayis Attunement: Taken Captive

Any character who successfully infiltrates the Toothbreaker Hideout by being taken captive advances their **Attunement: Mayis (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Eliminating Raster Thorn

Regardless of the specific approach the party chooses, their eventual objective within the Toothbreaker Hideout is to eliminate [[Raster Thorn]], the gang's villainous leader.

`[[/outcome eliminated]]`

#### Luxarum Attunement: Raster Thorn Killed

If the villainous Raster Thorn is slain in combat, each character advances their **Attunement: Luxarum (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Akon Attunement: Raster Thorn Spared

If the party chooses to spare the villainous Raster Thorn, each character advances their **Attunement: Akon (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Concluding the Event

The Concourse is officially reclaimed once Raster Thorn has been eliminated, and the party's mission for Fernis Ossa can be considered a success.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> Completing this Event earns the party 1 [[Milestone Progression]], potentially advancing them in Level.
>
> #### Next Steps
>
> With Fernis Ossa's sponsorship and official recommendation in-hand, the party must journety to [[All-Fable Keep]] to properly register for the Expedition Challenge during [[The Challenge Begins]].
>
> Additionally, now that the [[Toothbreaker Hideout]] has been rid of its villainous inhabitants and reclaimed, the party can start using the location as their own residence, which will subsequently be referred to as the "Concourse Hideout."
