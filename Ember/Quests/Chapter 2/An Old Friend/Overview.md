# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Main Quest occurs during Chapter 2.
>
> - If the party [[Leavetakings]] at the end of [[The Winding Trail]], they begin this Main Quest with the [[A Friendly Reunion]] Event.
> - If the party did not accompany Lyla, they might find her in Nain, in which case they begin this Main Quest with the [[Reuniting with Lyla]] Event.
> - If the party completes any of the other Chapter 2 Main Quests, this Main Quest becomes unavailable — Lyla completes her investigation and reaches Ordain independently.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> "An Old Friend" is designed for a party of Level 3 characters.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Main Quest that awards 3 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon successful completion of the [[Unwelcome Diversions]], [[Dash Away All]], and [[Fallen House]] Events.

The party begins this Main Quest immediately after deciding to accompany [[Lyla Cevher]] on her mission to uncover who is behind the bandit attacks at [[Helkas]]. With a few leads to follow up on — the shifting ink used in the bandits' notes, a maker's mark on the leather worn by the bandits, and an odd phrase on their tattoos — Lyla heads to Oldcraft Lodge to get information from her friend [[Sigil]], a Shard God known for his knowledge. There, he informs her that the entire Arctus Plateau has been disrupted, and [[Arcturel]], another settlement affiliated with [[House Cevher]], has recently suffered a string of disruptions. The issues may be related.

In Arcturel, a series of unfortunate incidents turns out to have a surprising cause. The [[Aburyx]], Kilner, a [[Tyraphem]] from the Inner Realm of [[Luxarum]], was sent to Arcturel along with several Celestial creatures to defame House Cevher. After an intense confrontation, the party uncovers Kilner's instructions. These instructions implicate another Trading House, [[House Wandren]], as well as the bandits that Lyla is already tracking down. After leaving Arcturel, Lyla tracks down an old childhood friend, [[Juro Wandren]], in the small village of [[Nain]], who suggests that the problems facing House Cevher are even greater than Lyla fears, urging Lyla to return to Ordain as soon as possible. Despite the advice, Lyla decides one more stop is needed, as the information gathered so far points to the nearby port of Seawall. Upon entering [[Seawall]], the truth becomes clear: Seawall has become a secret bastion for the [[Otherhood of Fortune]]. The Otherhood's leader [[Sticks]] brazenly blames House Cevher and the other Trading Houses of Ordain for many of the Arctus Plateau's recent troubles. The party is soon uncovered, and they must flee Seawall for Ordain by ship.

Once Lyla reaches [[The Ordinate]], her troubles are not over. makes a stunning public declaration. After a long investigation, [[Ordinate]] proclaims that [[House Cevher]] is responsible for the earthquake that many believe started the region's current troubles twenty years ago. The Ordinate removes House Cevher's seat, and Lyla narrowly avoids imprisonment while the Ordinate decides what punishment to inflict on her house. Worse still, her uncle has been murdered, and her [[Funar Cevher]] is missing, leaving Lyla and House Cevher's future uncertain.
