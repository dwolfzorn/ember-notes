# Locating Kel Kornan

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Social Event occurs in the [[Quarry Run]] district of Ordain on the Region Map, and steers the party towards the remote treetop village of [[Skybrush]] which has mysteriously fallen prey to an evil curse. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Meet Kadrick Pond of [[House Cevher]], who hires the party to investigate a disappearance in Skybrush.
> - Learn about the missing individual, Kel Kornan, and gather limited information about Skybrush.

### A Job for House Cevher

While traversing the [[Quarry Run]] district in Ordain, the party is approached by **Kadrick Pond** (Lawful Neutral, Ordani Human, he/him), a delegate of House Cehver, with a mission most important.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The gentleman coughs slightly and inclines his head in your direction, seemingly keen to discuss business.
>
> > Well met. I am Kadrick Pond, of House Cevher. Perhaps you've heard of me? Nevertheless, let's put the small talk aside …
> >
> > Your own reputations precede you, and Cevher is in need of some rather clandestine reconnaissance services. A modest reward and fine standing with the Trading House await you if both time and interest are available for this task.
> >
> > Shall we discuss the matter further, with proper discretion?
>
> Kadrick Pond crosses his arms in pragmatic anticipation, obviously awaiting your word.

> [!info] Social
> #### Speaking with Kadrick Pond
>
> After his initial speech, Kadrick Pond awaits a response from the party. And while he keeps House Cevher trade secrets rather close to the vest, he's happy to answer most questions the party might have about the job at hand. He makes the following details readily available:
>
> - The Trading House has lost contact with one of their most seasoned hires: an experienced stone and timber broker named **Kel Kornan**. Kornan has served House Cevher from her home in Skybrush for years.
> - House Cevher has a wide array of investors that depend on timely transactions and the prompt exchange of goods, and Kel's absence of communication has grown troubling for the Cevher bookkeepers.
> - Located several miles northwest of Ordain, Skybrush is well known for its precious stone quarries; but the frontier town also has a reputation for rare artifacts culled from the timeworn ruins of the [[Varún]] giants nearby.
> - House Cevher is willing to pay the party a modest  **10** if they're able to locate Kel Kornan and provide a report of her condition. If the characters take up this job and fail to deliver information back to House Cevher in Ordain, their reputation with the Trading House may be compromised.
> - The details of [[Locating Kel Kornan]] below — the job, the pay, and so on.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 12)** check can discern more about Kadrick Pond's intentions. He seems to trust the party's ability to carry out this recon job; and while there is a measure of importance to the mission, Pond doesn't seem to consider Kornan's disappearance a world-changing event.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The character can tell Kadrick Pond is used to keeping secrets from others, and there's no short supply of them here, but doesn't feel any unsettling intentions from him aside from a certain fear the unknown; and Kornan's disappearance seems more important than he leads on.
>
> #### The Kornan Contract
>
> Kadrick Pond is quite direct about the fact that House Cevher is willing to pay the party a modest  **10** if they're able to locate Kel Kornan and provide a report of her condition. If the characters take up this job and fail to deliver information back to House Cevher in Ordain, their reputation with the Trading House may be compromised.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 15)** check can convince Kadrick Pond to contribute an additional  **5 each** from the House Cevher coffers as payment.
>
> - **Knowledge: Trade**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.

The characters can ask around the city for information about Kel Kornan and Skybrush, but aside from basic details about the distant settlement, available information is scarce:

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Gathering Information
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 15)** check is able to gather additional basic details about the town of [[Skybrush]] and its most influential residents.

### Concluding the Event

Once the characters have gathered the desired amount of information from Kadrick Pond and Ordain at large, they are ready to venture forth. The party's reputation with House Cevher now depends on whether or not they eventually deliver information about Kel Kornan's whereabouts. They must travel to the settlement of Skybrush directly to learn more.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has finished their discussion with Kadrick Pond, they can begin their investigation by visiting Skybrush themselves, in [[The Situation in Skybrush]].
