# Delivered from Evil

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Social Event occurs in [[Skybrush]], and serves as the conclusion for "A Brush with Death." It chronicles the party's return to Skybrush with news of their triumph over [[Tethra Shùl]] and the dissipation of the terrible curse that afflicted the town. By speaking with the townsfolk, the characters can:
>
> - Raises issues of culpability surrounding Tethra Shùl's curse with the constable Mica Valaston.
> - Inform the surviving Erekos family and Revelry Wyst about what really happened to Dereth.
> - Interact with various citizens and businesses of Skybrush to put the townsfolk at ease and increase the standing of the party's reputation.
>
> This Event is depicted using the "Skybrush - Cheery" Level of the [[Vista: Skybrush]] Vista.
>
> #### One Ending or Another
>
> This Event encompasses the "good" ending to this Quest. If Tethra Shùl isn't defeated and her remains aren't cleansed, the state of Skybrush as described in the [[Loose Ends]] section of [[Loose Ends]] persists — eventually leading the town to terrible ruin.

### Counsel for the Constable

Once they've destroyed or consecrated Tethra Shùl's remains, the party must return to Skybrush with an account of the events they experienced — lest the treetop hamlet continue to live in fear of [[The Bewilderment]]. The most important point of contact for this effort is constable **Mica Valaston**, who is the town's only hope for order amidst the chaos (aside from the characters themselves).

> [!info] Social
> #### A Reward from Valaston
>
> Mica Valaston is eager to hear a full account of the party's excursion to [[The Bleak Archive]] and what transpired there.
>
> If the characters fail to present substantial evidence of their deeds, a successful **Diplomacy (DC 13)** check is required to convince the constable of the veracity of their claims.
>
> Should the party convince Mica — via evidence or persuasion — that they have relieved the innocent citizens of Skybrush from the curse, Mica offers them with a small donation of **5** from the town's public coffers.
>
> Mica Valaston can also help facilitate the sale of property within town, including the recently vacated homes of Qory Hult and Valence, the Harrowed.

### A Visit with Mr. & Mrs. Erekos

The party may wish to visit the grieving Erekos family to deliver news of their son's demise. If they do, **Zereth & Demetria Erekos** are eager to find some resolution to their open-ended sorrows.

Furthermore, **Revelry Wyst** has been spending more time with the Erekos family in recent days, and can be found grieving with them during this particular encounter.

> [!info] Social
> #### A Family Bereaved
>
> If the characters are delicate with the manner in which they share the news of Dereth's ultimate demise, the Erekos family is eager to offer them the following tokens of appreciation:
>
> - A dusty [[Bottle of Shadowcap Wine]]
> - A family heirloom. Roll once on the [[Arcturian Trinkets]] table.
>
> However, in the midst of credible rumors that have already started to circle about town, a successful **Deception (DC 14)** check is required to convince the Erekos family that Dereth had nothing to do with the curse that came to Skybrush.

#### Aura Attunement: Accept the Erekos Heirloom

If the party accepts the Erekos family's heirloom as part of their reward, each character advances their **Attunement: Aura (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Akon Attunement: Refuse the Erekos Reward

If the party refuses the Erekos family's heirloom as part of their reward, each character advances their **Attunement: Akon (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### A Village Indebted

The party may be able to put a large number of Skybrush townsfolk at ease by visiting The Roost to spread hasty word of their heroic exploits.

> [!info] Social
> #### News for The Roost
>
> As news of the party's victory at the Bleak Archive spreads throughout Skybrush, **Mattock** and the other patrons of The Roost are eager to celebrate with them. The characters drink and eat here for free for their first night back in town.
>
> From here on out (at the Gamemaster's discretion) members of the party have Advantage on Charisma (Performance) and Charisma (Influence) checks made at The Roost.

#### Luxarum Attunement: Local Notoriety

If the party members revel in their newfound notoriety with the locals of The Roost, each character who does so advances their **Attunement: Luxarum (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Orbis Attunement: Local Humility

Each character who humbly refuses the benefits of their newfound notoriety advances their **Attunement: Orbis (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

If the party managed to aid [[Liestra Grann]] during the course of [[The Abandoned Lute]], the characters may wish to visit her at The Long Haul for the barter and trade of errant artifacts.

> [!info] Social
> #### In the Grann Scheme of Things
>
> Liestra is extremely grateful for the party's assistance at the mountainside grotto, and is happy to offer them a 10% discount on goods purchased from her at The Long Haul. Additionally, she gifts them Their choice of [[Healing Elixir]] or [[Rallying Elixir]] during their first transaction of 5gp or more.
