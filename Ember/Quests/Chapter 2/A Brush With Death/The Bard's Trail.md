# The Bard's Trail

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This is an Exploration that occurs southwest of [[Skybrush]] on the Region Map, and leads the party northwest on a hunt for [[Dereth Erekos]], the young man wanted for the murder of his brother Branos. By investigating the clues left in this field, the characters can:
>
> - Encounter an interesting landmark along the way that points the party in the right direction — a scarecrow in an overgrown field that's been mangled by a haphazard blade.
> - Infer the mental state of the bard that they chase, through the wreckage he left behind.
>
> This Event is depicted using the "Derelict Farm" Level of the [[Rustvar Valleys]] Vista.

### The Mangled Scarecrow

In this small overgrown field located nearly two miles southwest of Skybrush, a scarecrow has recently been cut down by a large blade in what appears to be a random act of vandalism.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### What the Characters Find
>
> A closer inspection of the scarecrow indicates that it was haphazardly cut in half by a hefty blade of some kind. The straw innards are strewn about in disarray, and trinkets have been left behind — including a rusty iron helmet and a splintered wooden spear. Any character with **Knowledge: Forensics** or **Knowledge: Warfare** notices that the strikes seem undisciplined, as if driven by passion more than technique.
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 13, Passive)** spots boot tracks that match Dereth's boots.
>
> A further success on a **Wilderness (DC 14)** check is required to ascertain that these tracks weave beyond the scarecrow and head up into the foothills of the mountains to the northwest. This check can be repeated once per character per Rest.
>
> - **Knowledge: Tracking**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has gleaned what they can from the lonely field, they can:
>
> - Follow the trail northwest in order to locate Dereth's next stop in [[Trampled Florescence]].
> - Stumble upon Dereth himself in the [[The Abandoned Lute]] even further north, in the case that they miss the clue of Dereth's footsteps during this Event.
