# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Side Quest occurs during Chapter 2 and begins when the party discovers some clue about troubles in the town of [[Skybrush]]. There are no prerequisites for this Quest and it can be undertaken at any time, although it is certain to be encountered if traveling with [[Ankarist]] during the [[Ancient Paths]] Quest.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> The "A Brush With Death" Quest is designed for a party of Level 3 characters, although the final encounter of the Quest in [[Where Evil Lurks]] may prove difficult for a party that is not yet Level 4.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest quest that awards 2 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon completion of the [[Loose Ends]] and [[Where Evil Lurks]] Events.
>
> #### Content Warnings
>
> - **Domestic Violence:** In [[The Situation in Skybrush]], the party learns about Qory Hult having murdered his wife and child. This violence is the result of a curse known as [[The Bewilderment]] and perpetrated by an otherwise kind man, but information about the curse is not immediately made known to the party.
> - **Suicide:** In [[The House on Lonely Hill]], the party witnesses the artist Gedron Tath intentionally self-immolate, completing a suicide attempt before a curse can finish transforming him into a [[Horrendor]].

The remote village of Skybrush has fallen under hard times and grim omens. A mysterious undead curse called [[Unknown]] plagues its inhabitants, a mixture of stalwart mining folk and proud lumber workers who can't keep pace with The Bewilderment's malefic results. Those inevitably slain by the curse rise from the grave as ghoulish undead creatures known as [[Horrendor]].

Little do the people of Skybrush know, the Bewilderment originates from the shadows of a forgotten [[Shent]] vault buried deep within the hinterlands north of town. There, in a timeworn repository known as [[The Bleak Archive]], a wraithlike warlock named [[Tethra Shùl]] has remained trapped for centuries. From the confines of this ageless place, she utilizes the Bewilderment and its Horrendors to exert the malevolent will of her otherworldly patron, the Abyssal Horror [[Nethehepticas]]. Time grows short for the people of Skybrush, who Nethehepticas seeks to claim as foot soldiers in a forthcoming apocalypse.

Once they arrive in Skybrush, the party must unravel the mystery of the curse's origin and precisely how it came to town. They'll trace the ill-fated steps of a young bard named [[Dereth Erekos]], the curse's first victim, and learn how an assortment of five [[Varún]] relics bearing the curse were distributed to the unsuspecting citizens of Skybrush. As the curse spreads further and further, the characters will find themselves in a race against time to not only locate and unseal the Bleak Archive, but to also release the world from Tethra Shùl's evil ambitions by reckoning with her loathsome remains.
