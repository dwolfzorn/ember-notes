# Revealing the Mystery

## Recent History

*One week ago…*

An enterprising young bard named [[Dereth Erekos]], eager to win the heart of his beloved Revelry Wyst (and the approval of her family), ventured deep into the hills northwest of [[Skybrush]] in search of rare [[Shent]] relics that might turn his financial tides. Dereth's heedless quest for fortune and glory led him to plunder a forgotten Shent vault known as [[The Bleak Archive]], where the wraithlike apparition of an evil [[Varún]] warlock named [[Tethra Shùl]] has lurked for centuries untold. And curiously, the young bard was lured to this place by a recurrence of strange dreams and a trail of luminous blue flowers that lined his inconspicuous pathway into the lore-haunted hinterlands.

A frightened and astounded Dereth absconded with a handful of five relics from the tomb's antechamber, each of them cursed by the Varùn warlock's enduring betrayal. When he returned to Skybrush — newfound fortune and a bouquet of Moon Blossoms in hand — Dereth unwittingly brought doom and undeath along with him.

Any humanoid who claims and possesses one of these five stolen Varùn treasures becomes afflicted with a loathsome curse known as [[Unknown]], which drives them mad before killing them outright. After death, the unfortunate victim of the curse becomes an undead creature known as a [[Horrendor]], and serves as an immortal minion to Tethra Shùl and her otherworldly patron — the Abyssal Horror known as [[Nethehepticas]].

The citizens of Skybrush who've already come into contact with the accursed Varùn relics find themselves the targets of a woeful fate. Meanwhile, the strange moon blossoms bloom wherever the curse has taken hold. Perhaps a group of heroes will arrive in time to help the harrowed innocents of Skybrush survive …

## Tracking the Curse

The first time the party arrives in Skybrush, [[Unknown]] has already taken hold. There are four initial victims of the curse:

- [[Dereth Erekos]], who killed his brother Branos during a moment of magical confusion. Dereth fled the scene of the crime and currently presides over the mountainside grotto northwest of Skybrush where the events of [[Unknown]] take place. Dereth has fully transformed into a Horrendor.
- **Qory Hult**, who killed his wife during a moment of magical confusion before getting arrested by constable Mica Valaston. Qory is currently locked up in the Blockhouse Gaol in town, and is in the process of becoming a Horrendor. The party should encounter Qory sometime during [[Unknown]].
- **Gedron Tath**, who lives a solitary life at his remote home studio, located two miles southeast of Skybrush. Gedron is cursed but has yet to become involved in a violent accident. His death via self-immolation will occur just as the party arrives at his home to experience the events of [[Unknown]].
- **Kel Kornan**, who has retreated from her life of local commerce to hide away as The Bewilderment takes hold of her mind. The party won't encounter Kel Kornan until [[Unknown]] — their second visit to Skybrush (after they meet Gedron Tath).

Each time the party ventures out of the Skybrush city limits and returns, someone in town falls under the sway of The Bewilderment while they're gone — and has likewise accomplished either a confused murder or their own accidental suicide (perhaps by falling from one of the settlement's treetop catwalks).

No matter the cause of their demise, these cursed citizens become Horrendors all the same); and as long as the party fails to reach Tethra Shùl's inner chamber within the Bleak Archive and destroy or consecrate her worldly remains, the malevolence of her curse endures.

Qory Hult is the first citizen of Skybrush to perish while the party is away. Additional points of contact with the accursed artifacts include the following:

- [[Rune-Marked Band]]: The party likely found this during their encounter with Gedron Tath. If they don't take the ring, Mica Valaston will add it to the evidence locker in their office at the Blockhouse gaol.
- [[Rune-Marked Arrowhead]]: Qory Hult's murder weapon in the Blockhouse gaol is likely the first artifact to change hands.
- [[Rune-Marked Sash]]: Currently in the possession of Kel Kornan, who is also cursed by the item.
- [[Rune-Marked Vial]]: Located in [[Liestra Grann]] pawn shop The Long Haul, this item is a prime target for petty theft by an unscrupulous citizen of Skybrush.
- [[Rune-Marked Knife]]: The party will find this accursed weapon during their forthcoming encounter with Dereth Erekos in [[Unknown]].

The second time the party returns to Skybrush after a jaunt outside of town, there are at least two additional outbreaks of The Bewilderment to contend with. The rate at which the curse continues to proliferate should be determined by the party's relative advancement and associated class levels.

## Where the Moon Blossoms Grow

Beds of strange flowers blossom wherever someone has died after becoming afflicted by The Bewilderment. These [[Moon Blossom]] resemble small anemones and radiate a dim light at night. Few people have noticed the coincidence, but locals remain suspicious of the curious lambent buds nonetheless.

Once the party notices this concurrence, spotting the lambent flowers becomes easier for them, and is a definitive clue for locating the secluded tomb of Tethra Shùl in the Skybrush hinterlands.
