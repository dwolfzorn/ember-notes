# The Ballad of Dereth Erekos

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Social Event occurs in any settlement or town on the Region Map, and introduces the party to the remote treetop village of [[Skybrush]], which has mysteriously fallen prey to an evil curse. By listening to a traveling bard, the characters can:
>
> - Hear a mysterious song from Larka Hex, which tells the story of a star-crossed bard named [[Dereth Erekos]] and the doom that came to his village.
> - Gather limited information from Larka about Skybrush and its inhabitants.

### A Song of Skybrush

Here, the party first encounters the Signborn bard Larka Hex.

**Larka Hex (Neutral Good, Arcturian Signborn, she/her)**

This silver-tongued [[Arcturian]] bard knows a whole catalogue of tall tales from Signara, which have helped form the basis of countless ballads. Larka travels the [[Arctus Plateau]] looking for opportunities to exchange stories and compose new songs.

As the party watches here, the familiar tune that Larka plays is interrupted by a strange psychic event.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Larka begins singing with haunted detachment, as if the music she plays is not her own, and her despondent words weave a tale of sorrow …
>
> > In Skybrush, where the moon blossoms grow
> > and the wind in the mountains blows cold,
> > dreams a girl of her lover so 'lone,
> > of the bard who awaits to atone.
> >
> > No honeyed words save him this day,
> > whose brother himself did he slay;
> > and reason may ne’er find a way
> > for Skybrush to weather the fray.
> >
> > Oh Dereth, young Dereth, wherever you roam
> > Lurks the ghost of your brother,
> > where the moon blossoms grow.
>
> As the last chords of the sullen melody refrain, Larka appears bewildered and lost.

After singing absentmindedly of Skybrush and the doomed saga of Dereth Erekos, Larka is willing to engage with the characters, however befuddled she may be.

> [!info] Social
> #### Speaking with Larka Hex
>
> Larka has no memory of having played the new song, let alone having written it. It's as if she received the song directly from some unknown transmitter and manifested it into the physical world. After a few moments of rest, she's back to normal.
>
> Larka can offer the characters the following information:
>
> - She's never heard the name "Dereth," but is somewhat familiar with the "moon blossoms" that grow among the Skybrush hinterlands — though she's never actually been to Skybrush herself.
> - Charming ghost stories abound throughout the Arctus Plateau, but this tale of a bard who killed his own brother is new to her.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 13)** check gets a good read on Larka's reaction to this predicament. She seems a bit more confused than anything, having no recollection of the song or having played it. If the characters are already familiar to her, she is welcoming; on the other hand, she may be somewhat baffled by the concern of relative strangers.
>
> - **Critical Success**: You can sense a fear behind Larka's eyes that even she seems unaware of, as if strange and unseen forces are pulling the strings. This ignorance suggests Larka's involvement in this beguiling occurrence is quite innocent.

Larka's absent-minded song recurs once each day at a random time until the characters decide to investigate Skybrush for themselves.

### Concluding the Event

With the location of Skybrush newly indicated on their map, the party can travel there to investigate the sullen story of Dereth Erekos and whether or not it has any truthful merit.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has listened to Larka's song, they can begin the Quest in earnest by visiting Skybrush themselves in [[The Situation in Skybrush]].
