# Trampled Florescence

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This is an Exploration Event that occurs northwest of [[Skybrush]] on the Region Map, along the trail left by [[Dereth Erekos]]. By following this trail, the characters can:
>
> - Encounter a remarkable concentration of [[Moon Blossom]] that will point the way further north towards Dereth's current location.
>
> This Event is depicted using the "Moon Blossom Field" Level of the [[Rustvar Valleys]] Vista.

### Bed of Moonflowers

As the party follows the trail further north through the long valley west of Skybrush, they may notice themselves walking in tandem with wild growths of the luminous Moon Blossoms. The flowers are trampled in several sections, indicating movement across them.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### What the Characters Find
>
> Any character who makes a successful **`[[/skill wilderness 14]]`** check can trace trail of Dereth's booted tracks across the flower bed, leading deeper into the valley northeast. The deep impressions and staggering pattern of the footfalls suggest that the person who left them carried a heavy load.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a separate **Wilderness (DC 16)** recognizes that this extreme concentration of flowers has created a subtle pollen cloud and that the air is full of tiny spores.
>
> - **Knowledge: Alchemy**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Plants**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> There is something unusually serene about this location despite the grim troubles which underscore your current quest. The rugged pastoral beauty of the Rustvar Valleys is in full display here and the crisp air of these hinterlands coupled with the mazy smells of the wildflower meadow leave you wishing for time to rest and take in the majestic scene.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Dream a Little Dream
>
> If the characters spend at least 10 minutes in this location but do not voluntarily decide to take a Rest, they find themselves involuntarily setting down their packs and reclining amidst the wild meadow.
>
> Each character is so transfixed by the scene that they do not notice others in their company drifting off into a gentle sleep. Each party member faces a **Hazard 2 (Willpower, Morale, Psychic)** , and if they fail to resist, they fall asleep. Characters who succeed on fall into a daydream instead, unaware of the dozing of others. This torpor lasts for `[[/gmroll 1d4 # Hours of sleep]]` hours.
>
> At first the dreams are pleasant, but any sleeping character who has not yet experienced a nightmare of Nethehepticas eventually experiences one of the dreams described in the [[Night Terrors]] Event.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
