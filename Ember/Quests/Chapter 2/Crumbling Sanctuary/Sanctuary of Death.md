# Sanctuary of Death

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Combat Event finds the party returning to [[Corpin Sanctuary]] on the trail of the necromancer [[Evesso]], who leads a skeletal horde of undead minions on a rampage of death and destruction against the Cindarics. In this event, the party can:
>
> - Return to Corpin Sanctuary via the [[Sanctorum]] secret elevator.
> - Join in combat against Evesso's undead minions - including [[Skallith]], [[Crumbling Skallith]], and [[Skallith Warrior]] — while protecting the Cindaric Sages here from mortal peril.
> - Fight their way through a horde of skeletal undead to reach Evesso in the [[Entry Courtyard]] for a climactic showdown, which culminates in a wizardly stand-off at the [[Meditation Pavilion]].
> - Learn from [[Mira Wavehorn]] and the other sages about what transpired here in the party's absence.
>
> #### Orchestrating Combat
>
> This event returns the characters to the [[Corpin Sanctuary]] area walkthrough, and requires the party to move procedurally throughout the sanctuary, purifying it of undead and saving Cindaric Sages along the way.
>
> Although the entirety of Corpin Sanctuary is connected and relatively small, this event requires you to help set and maintain the pace of action as the characters make their way from the Sanctorum to the Entry Courtyard, and potentially further. The various "scenes" of this action sequence are presented below according to their location. When the party has finished one scene, a prompt will help you move them along to the next.
>
> The characters begin in the [[Sanctorum]] after riding the elevator back to the sanctuary, followed by a conflict in the [[Dormitories]], then onto the [[Refectory]] via the [[Winding Stairs]], before the first stage of their head-to-head combat with Evesso in the [[Entry Courtyard]]. This first stage of the encounter with Evesso will potentially lead them to a second that culminates at the [[Meditation Pavilion]], thanks to a handy Dimension Door spell from the necromancer's grimoire.
>
> #### Regarding Initiative
>
> Once initiative has been rolled, the characters will stay in initiative order throughout the entire event, until they have defeated or routed Evesso in combat. Enemies throughout the Sanctuary roll for initiative as soon as they're encountered in combat.
>
> The Cindaric Adherents and various forms of Skallith (Crumbling Skallith, standard Skallith, and Skallith Warriors) have been grouped together according to their respective types, and will act on the same initiative order as any creature that shares the same name. Three "dummy" tokens have been placed invisibly upon the area map to account for this feature.

### Sanctum Onslaught

Initiative is rolled immediately at the start of this combat event, after "Setting the Scene" has been read aloud. The characters begin combat from their places on the elevator at the center of the [[Sanctorum]]. Two types of undead creatures are currently present in this conflict, Skallith and their decrepit counterparts known as Crumbling Skallith.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Undead Tactics
>
> Seventeen [[Crumbling Skallith]] and one [[Skallith]] await the characters here in the Sanctorum. When the fight begins, these skeletal undead will rush the characters as soon as possible to meet them in melee combat.
>
> Crumbling Skallith who are close enough for melee attack will assault party members with their [[Shortsword]] attack. Meanwhile, Crumbling Skallith who are too far away for melee will attack ranged enemies and enemy spellcasters with their [[Shortbow]] attack.
>
> The lone Skallith will face off with the party member with the largest amount of hit points, using its [[Shortsword]].
>
> #### Dramatic Moments

Once this group of Skallith and Crumbling Skallith in the Sanctorum has been defeated in combat, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Just then, a voice calls out from the Dormitories to the north, where the sound of combat rings through the halls. It's the familiar voice of Mira Wavehorn, wrought with fury and distress.
>
> > Back! Back to the shadows, you loathsome creatures! You have no purchase here!

### Dormitory Danger

Spurred on by [[Mira Wavehorn]]'s outcry, the party must now head north to the Dormitories, where they'll encounter the sage herself and another wave of Skallith and Crumbling Skallith. When the characters arrive, Mira is fending off a siege of skeletal undead from the doorway to [[Mira's Chamber]].

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You arrive to see a corridor full of skeletal undead, who clatter over each other as they claw their way towards Mira Wavehorn's chamber. Near the end of the hall, the sage stands defiant in the threshold, fending off the attack with a measure of desperation. A light returns to her eyes when she sees you've joined the fray.
>
> > Help! We have wounded here! I can't hold them off forever!
>
> Mira looks over her shoulder into the chamber, surveying something — or someone — she's desperate to protect.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Undead Tactics
>
> 4 [[Skallith]] and 8 [[Crumbling Skallith]] line the hallway here on both sides, with 3 of the Skallith leading the charge into Mira's Chambers. When the party arrives, many Skallith and Crumbling Skallith will turn to face the characters in melee combat, while the Skallith closest to Mira will continue to attack her in the doorway.
>
> Most of the Skallith and Crumbling Skallith here will opt to use their [[Shortsword]] attacks in melee combat, resorting to [[Shortbow]] attacks if separated from enemies for a protracted amount of time.
>
> #### Mira's Tactics
>
> Mira spends her time blocking the doorway and making use of the Defend action, occasionally activating a **Aura of Life** to make it unpleasant for the Skallith to consider approaching her.
>
> If the characters attempt to talk to Mira during combat, she let's them know the following:
>
> - A wounded [[Cindaric Adherent]] is hanging on for dear life inside Mira's Chamber.
> - Evesso was seen retreating towards the Refectory with a mob of undead minions in tow.
>
> #### Dramatic Moments
>
> At the beginning of each combat round here in the Dormitories, excluding the first, one of several **Dramatic Moments** occurs. Each moment can only happen once. At the beginning of each round, roll on the [[Sanctuary of Death Moments]] table and narrate the result.

Once the group of Skallith and Crumbling Skallith here in the Dormitories has been defeated in combat, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Mira shouts out to you as anther set of bones clatters morbidly to the floor.
>
> > Evesso … Evesso has brought this upon us! We must stop him! Make haste to the Refectory. I have some dire healing to do, but I'm right behind you.
>
> You take note of another sage in Mira's chamber, their garments marked with a dark crimson stain from a rusty stab. As the life retreats from the sage's eyes, Mira rushes to heal them. She places her hands upon the wound as they begin to glow with holy light before regarding you once more.
>
> > Make haste! The fate of the Sanctuary hangs in the balance …

### Rumble in the Refectory

After the encounter with Mira in the Dormitories, the characters must make their way to the [[Refectory]] by way of the [[Winding Stairs]].

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Stairway Combat
>
> 4 [[Crumbling Skallith]] can be found traversing the Winding Stairs on their way to the Refectory. When the characters appear, these Crumbling Skallith immediately turn to face them in combat.
>
> The two Crumbling Skallith furthest to the south with start with ranged [[Shortbow]] attacks, while the two Crumbling Skallith to the north will rush forward with their [[Shortsword]].

After the brief encounter on the Winding Stairs, the characters can proceed to the Refectory, where a larger host of undead enemies wreak havoc — including a Skallith Warrior.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The once-inviting scents of the refectory have been replaced with a scene of carnage and the smell of death. Several skeletons ransack and pillage the refectory, upending furniture and hacking plates of meat with rusty swords. It seems you've become the target of this mindless havoc, as the malevolent creatures turn to face you. One of them appears quite threatening, adorned in corroded armor and armed with an ancient blade.

> [!abstract] Skallith Warrior

A rusty longsword at the creature's side belies its noteworthy strength, and a helmet crowns its cracked and decaying skull.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Refectory Combat
>
> 1 [[Skallith Warrior]] is flanked by 1 [[Skallith]] and 3 [[Crumbling Skallith]] here in the Refectory. When the party arrives, they immediately turn to face the characters in combat.
>
> Once this sequence of combat begins, the Skallith Warrior will target the character with the largest number of hit points or a spellcaster with its [[Frightful Glare]], whichever poses the greatest immediate threat. A ranged [[Hand Axe]] attack is at the ready for any spellcasters who maintain concentration throughout the battle, but the Warrior will quickly engage the party member that poses the greatest threat in melee combat, using [[Multiattack]] each round with its [[Longsword]].
>
> #### Dramatic Moments
>
> At the beginning of each combat round here in the Refectory, excluding the first, one of several **Dramatic Moments** occurs. Each moment can only happen once. At the beginning of each round, roll on the [[Sanctuary of Death Moments]] Table and narrate the result.

Once the group of skeletal undead here in the Refectory has been defeated in combat, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> With a sudden gust of wind full of pink leaves and ill portent, the doors to the entry courtyard blow wide open. You quickly become aware of another presence to the south, heralded by the swell of arcane light and the sound of esoteric chants.
>
> Evesso, the necromancer, awaits you in the courtyard, flanked by skeletal minions. He moves his arms in arcane gesticulation as a nimbus of purple vapor flows into his hands, bolstering the evil wizard with a necromantic facsimile of life.

### Confronting the Necromancer

> [!abstract] Evesso
> **[[Evesso]]**
>
> Level 6 (Elite) · Ashka Necromancer
>
> ![Evesso](../../../assets/image171.webp)
>
> This tall Ashka scholar is draped in the burgundy robes of a Cindaric Sage. His viridian-scaled form is lean and austere, and his countenance betrays an erudite contrition. Surely, this scholar is an accomplished practitioner of the Cindaric arts, if the age of his blue-green squama and the patina of his weathered staff are any indication.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Evesso's Tactics
>
> Evesso is accompanied here in the [[Entry Courtyard]] by a [[Skallith]] and two [[Crumbling Skallith]].
>
> Evesso has prepared for the eventuality that he may need to defend himself. His approach to combat at this stage involves doing everything in his power to delay or distract the party long enough to reposition himself. Ordering the Skallith to attack and engage the party to delay them. He has also prepared a magical ritual at this location to teleport himself to the top of the nearby [[Meditation Pavilion]], and will take the special action in the first round to do so.
>
> Once atop the Pavillion, Evesso makes steady use of **Ray of Death** and @spell[soul.arrow] to harry the party. If the Skallith are defeated and Evesso has not been directly engaged in combat atop the pavillion, he will attempt to use **Conjuration of Death** to raise two new Skallith to protect him and keep the party at bay.
>
> #### Dramatic Moments
>
> At the beginning of each combat round here in the Entry Courtyard, excluding the first, one of several **Dramatic Moments** occurs. Each moment can only happen once. At the beginning of each round, roll on the [[Sanctuary of Death Moments]] table and narrate the result.
>
> #### The Necromancer's Retreat
>
> If it looks as though Evesso may be killed or he is **Broken** he will attempt to flee by dramatically hurling himself from the roof of the Pavillion toward the valley below, using his [[Ring of Gliding]] to safely descend.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Event Outcome
>
> Following the encounter with Evesso, record the appropriate outcome noting whether the necromancer was killed or whether he escaped.

#### Cora Attunement: Evesso Killed

If the party manages to kill Evesso during the event, advance each character's **Attunement: Cora (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Abyss Attunement: Evesso Escaped

If the party allows Evesso to live or escape, advance each character's **Attunement: The Abyss (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Aftermath

After the battle has transpired and the smoke begins to clear, Sin urges the party to check in with [[Mira Wavehorn]], who aids the injured from the relative safety of the sanctuary's [[Kitchen]]. This scene provides the characters with a chance to debrief with Mira and discuss precisely what has transpired here, along with the opportunity to receive a reward for their help.

> [!info] Social
> #### Just Rewards
>
> To demonstrate her appreciation for the party's intervention, Mira rewards the characters with a small chest containing  **50** collected from the surviving Cindarics.
>
> #### Post-Mortem with Mira
>
> Although Mira is extremely thankful for the party's aid, she remains somewhat distracted. Her mind is elsewhere as she tends to the injured and surveys the damage that's come to Corpin Sanctuary.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill insight 15 passive format=long]]` or who succeeds on a **Deception (DC 13)** check can ascertain that something is bothering Mira, as she exudes an subtle air of anxiety and distant concern.
>
> - Characters with **Attunement: Heart of Ember** have **+2 Boons**.
>
> Any character who also succeeds on a **Intimidation (DC 16)** or **Diplomacy (DC 13)** check is able to convince Mira to share precisely what is bothering her: she's anxious about the imminent arrival of the [[Burnished Hand]], a military order whose aggressive oversight may upend the already tenuous situation at Corpin Sanctuary — particularly in regards to what the senior leaders of [[Cindarin Temple]] in Ordain have to say about it.
>
> - Characters with **Culture: Cascilian** or **Path: CindaricAdherent** have **+2 Boons**.
