# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This main quest occurs during Chapter 2, and begins if the party [[Leavetakings]] at the end of [[The Winding Trail]]. If the party chose not to, they can still rejoin Sin at a later time, in which case they start the "Crumbling Sanctuary" quest partway through, via the [[Reuniting with Sin]] event.
>
> Once any of the Chapter 2 main quests have been completed, it is no longer possible to access this quest (as Sin has independently reached Ordain without the party's assistance).
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> The "Crumbling Sanctuary" quest is designed for a party of Level 3 characters. It begins upon leaving [[Helkas]]. If the party did not thoroughly explore the Forest of Stone and its respective Chapter 1 side quests, they may still be Level 2. They can either press forward and expect to advance to Level 3 upon obtaining their next milestone point, or they could be encouraged to backtrack and explore further.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Main Quest quest that awards 3 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon completion of the [[Corpin Arrival]], [[Sanctuary of Death]], and [[A Sage Welcome]] Events.

Crumbling Sanctuary will take the party on a grand adventure as they discover the machinations of an evil necromantic plot, one that threatens to upheave the very Cindaric faith itself. After their alliance with Sin Marmot in Helkas, the characters will join the young druid on a westward pilgrimage to Corpin Sanctuary and the city of Ordain. However, this trip will prove far from idyllic.

An encounter at a pillaged farmstead known as Dradley Grange will reveal the presence of marauding forces within the Arctus Plateau, and serve as the party's first clues since Helkas that unrest is troubling to the region. Subsequently, the party comes face to face with a small mob of reanimated corpses while they attempt to ford the ruins of Keeper's Crossing on the River Elenain, followed by a scuffle with a group of raiders from the Otherhood of Fortune — suggesting that several forces are at work to bring ruin to the people of the Plateau.

Once Sin and the party make it to Corpin Sanctuary, they'll meet the respected Cindaric Sages and find themselves caught in the middle of an intriguing scenario. This plot involves a necromancer named Evesso, who has clandestinely infiltrated the Cindarics in an attempt to gain access to rare occult relics and the ancient tombs beneath Mial Mountain. If the party is unable to prevent Evesso from raising the dead, at least they can help stop him from destroying the Sanctuary … or can they?
