# Shadowed Depths

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> In this Exploration Event, the party emerges from the Corpin Sanctuary Elevator into the massive underground environment of the [[Mycelian Expanse]]. In this event, the characters can:
>
> - Search for clues of any necromantic activity.
> - Search for signs of passage by the missing sages, [[Evesso]] and [[Avwynn Taol]].

### Evidence of Passage

This should be a short event that serves as an introduction to the Pathways biome and its environment.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Area
>
> A successful **Wilderness (DC 14)**check after taking some time examining the location allows one to take stock of the biome and gather some info about it: This place is lively, filled with numerous species of fungi and plants that are bioluminescent, and more importantly have developed the ability to survive without sunlight. There is a large amount of fungus, mold, and slime growing alongside grasses, plants, and even a few oddly colored trees seemingly unaffected by the lack of natural light.
>
> A successful **Society (DC 14)** check while examining construction of the area yields some insights: The stone is properly ancient, bearing the hallmarks of ancient Shent architecture and stone shaping.
>
> - **Critical Success**: Closer examination of some stranger elements in the stone work appear to predate the Shent. If you are right, these structures were built by the ancestors of the Shent and then added to some time later. The original stonework may be over four thousand years old.
>
> #### Signs of Travel
>
> A successful **Wilderness (DC 12)** check reveals the presence of fresh tracks through the area. Kicked over rocks and some trampled grass reveals a path recently taken eastward, but the trail quickly dries up. Not much farther ahead the splattered, glowing residue of a couple trampled mushrooms betray a haphazard run through the underbrush, likely meant to throw off pursuers and save some time. Following the path left behind becomes almost trivial as a result.
>
> - **Critical Success**: Given the size and distance of the tracks and other markers alongside them, you suspect that the person responsible may have had a tail, or was dragging something along with them. Since the damage to the surrounding fauna isn't extensive enough to indicate something being dragged, it's more likely that this is a mark of Evesso's passage through the area.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Wilderness (DC 12)** is able to spot one particular oddity: a dead pyrefly, perched atop a bare spot of stone.
>
> - A further **Wilderness (DC 13)** skill check recognizes that these creatures wouldn't be down in the Pathways.
> - A further **Wilderness (DC 14)** leads one to deduce that the pyrefly likely got carried down here by one of the Sages, quite possible Evesso, who was recently overseeing the Cindaric ritual revolving around them in the sanctuary above.
