# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Side Quest occurs during Chapter 2 and begins once the party crosses paths with the aspiring agrimage, Edivel Sprout. There are no prerequisites for this Quest and it can be undertaken at any time, although it is likely to be encountered if traveling with Sin while undertaking the [[Crumbling Sanctuary]] Quest.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> The "Thorny Predicaments" Quest is designed for a party of Level 3 characters.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest quest that awards 2 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon completion of the [[Supplies and Demands]] and [[Aythorn's Blessings]] Events.

The party can begin this Quest in a few different ways — they may cross paths with [[Edivel Sprout]] as the aspiring agrimage attempts to get an errant spell under control, meet a pair of lost travelers who point them in the direction of the "seed settlement" Brevin, or stumble upon Brevin on their own. Each of these on-ramps eventually leads them to the pivotal event that kicks off the quest in earnest — Edivel's fall into the canyons as the result of an earth tremor.

If the party frees Edivel from where they are trapped at the bottom of the canyons, helping them escape the swiftly rising waters, the young wizard asks for help on their quest to learn the vineweaving spell they were trying to perfect when they fell. Edivel is convinced that the earthquakes have damaged Brevin's bridge and that this spell will help restore it.

To help Edivel, the party must venture through the creature-laden [[Bramble Gully]] to retrieve the right spell component, get Edivel's spellbook back from a would-be brigand, the [[Carmin Anther]], and find the originator of the spell that Edivel is trying to learn, famed agrimage [[Kali Andrella]]. Finding and talking to Kali is easier said than done, as she is barricaded in her home in the ruins of Steed's Point in the midst of traps, wandering jurtak, and aggressive gore birds. When not fighting the dangers of Steed's Point or the Canyons, characters have the chance to use persuasion and charm, first to set the Canyon Crawler on a more positive path, and then to help Kali Andrella decide if she should leave Steed's Point so that her mind is clear enough to help train Edivel.

With their primary mission with Edivel complete, the party returns with the agrimage to Brevin, where the bridge has indeed been compromised and is dragging the entire town into the canyon. With the vine weaving they've learned, Edivel is able to help, while the party provides support in dealing with varying crises caused by the rapid shifts of the town. Whether or not their combined efforts succeed in saving the Brevin bridge, their efforts catch the attention of the shard god Aythorn, who gives them a boon after blessing the town.
