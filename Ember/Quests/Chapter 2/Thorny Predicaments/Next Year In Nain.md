# Next Year In Nain

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Social Event the party find itself in the riverside settlement of [[Nain]] after some time has passed since the conclusion of Thorny Predicaments. The characters will be able to:
>
> - Catch up with [[Kali Andrella]] now that she has moved to the larger settlement.
> - Update Kali on recent events.

> [!abstract] Kali Andrella
> **[[Kali Andrella]]**
>
> Level 1 · Unknown Unknown
>
> ![Kali Andrella](../../../assets/image084.webp)

### Too Busy To Chat

If the party decides to leave before meeting up with Kali, she catches them as they leave:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you continue on your quest, focused on the task ahead of you, Kali calls out again to the crowd.
>
> > Look at them – so polite, not wanting to cut in front of the crowd. Probably off to do great deeds somewhere else on the plateau. Deeds we could use in these troubling times. Let’s send them off with a fond farewell.
>
> Kali leads the crowd in a rousing round of applause that seems to have a bit of her magic in it, raising your spirits as you head off on your next adventure.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### A Little Help From Your Friends
>
> Thanks to the gratitude of the crowd and Kali’s magic, the entire party has advantage on their next skill check, as if they had the benefits of the Help action.

### Catching Up With Kali

If the party goes up to see Kali, they find her at a makeshift stand in a small garden.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The sign announcing Kali’s presence hangs above a wooden stand that might seem plain if it wasn’t surrounded by plants and flowers, as if a garden sprung to life wherever Kali had decided to settle. On the stand sits a large jar filled with a combination of small trinkets, jewels, seeds, and coins. As you approach, she rises and steps away from the stand into the midst of the plants around it, where a small ring of tall reeds provides a small amount of privacy from the waiting line.
>
> > It’s wonderful to see you again, my friends. Here, come talk with me a moment before I get back to all my newly adoring fans.

> [!info] Social
> #### The Survivor of Steed's Point
>
> Kali is happy to tell the party what she's gotten up to since leaving Steed's Point. She shares the following:
>
> - Zenodora, who runs Zenodora's General Goods nearby, was thrilled to see her and just as thrilled about hyping up her story to drive people to her store nearby. Kali answers questions, helps those she can, and sends anyone who needs something simple to Zenodora's store.
> - She felt a little guilty at first for calling herself the Survivor of Steed's Point, but at least it's gotten people talking, and she's been collecting donations of goods for the revitalization of Steed's Point from the people she helps.
> - One day, Kali plans to return to Steed's Point and build it back again, but in the meantime it's nice to be out in society again and she's glad that her story can inspire.
>
> Full answers below.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Kali's New Store?
>
> **A:**
>
> > You like the shop? Zenodora's idea, really. She's been in Nain for years, has a General Goods store, so she usually knows the mood of folks in town. And the last few seasons, they've been jumpy. Rumors of bandits and living corpses and beasts running wild. Having someone like me here helps folks feel more secure - if I could survive Steed's Point, they can survive whatever life throws their way. Most folks just want advice, but I get to make a few magic items too, grow my garden. Not a bad life altogether.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Relationship to Steed's Point?
>
> **A:**
>
> > I haven't forgotten about Steed's Point. I'm not just a survivor of it, I'm part of its legacy. See that jar there? When folks come up to talk, if they want, they can leave something that'll help regrow Steed's Point. Some give money, or supplies, but I think my favorite are the seeds. Things we can replant there one day, after things settle down on the Plateau. I can't wait.

> [!info] Social
> #### What's New With You
>
> Kali is also thrilled to hear any news from the party. She reacts generally positively to anything they say, but has an especially positive reaction to any of the following:
>
> - If the party met Moriah, she is excited that her daughter is doing well and vows to get word out through those who travel through Nain of her new location. She's enthusiastic about potentially seeing Moriah soon.
> - If the party saved Brevin, she is pleased, though not surprised. She thought they'd be able to do it. She also shares that she has been in correspondence with Edivel, helping the young agrimage to continue to develop their craft.
> - If the party knows that Rattletrap is Bertron Steed, she is surprised, and worried that she left him alone in Steed's Point. She vows to increase her efforts to go back and make Steed's Point what it once was, or at least to go check on Rattletrap from time to time.
>
> Full answers below.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** If they've met Moriah:
>
> **A:**
>
> > My girl. I'm glad she's okay - thanks for letting me now. I hope you told her I moved here to Nain - not that I expect her to drop everything and come this way, but I hope one day she'll come and see her ma. We could be like oil and water sometimes, but after everything that's happened, it's a good time to have family around.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** If Brevin was Saved:
>
> **A:**
>
> > I knew you all would do it. That Edivel certainly was determined, and with your help keeping them out of trouble, they were bound to make sure that Brevin kept thriving. Good work! I've been sending them a few other tips and tricks over the last couple of months, where I can. Better to keep their hands busy learning agrimagic than running off causing trouble, especially these days, when there are more dangers on the Golden Flats than ever before.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Rattletrap is Bertron:
>
> **A:**
>
> > Really? You're not kidding with me? I guess that explains why Rattletrap stayed around Steed's Point for so long the way I did. Probably still there now. I should visit, see what he remembers. See if he's angry I left. See if he's angry I couldn't keep the jurtak from running us over and out of our homes. Better angry than dead, at least.
> >
> > The way he fell to the jurtak? It weighed on me, and now it weighs a little less.
> >
> > Thank you.

> [!info] Social
> #### Another Fond Farewell
>
> With their talk complete, Kali offers the party something to help their future adventures and travels. Generally, she gives a single portion of [[Growing Thorns]] to the party, but if the party brought her specifically joyful news, she gives them an additional item.
>
> - If the party met Moriah, she offers a pouch of Throwing Stones, which have the properties of [[Beads of Force]].
> - If the party saved Brevin, she adds an [[Herbalism Kit]] and an [[Everbloom]], which she has begun cultivating after getting a seed sent to her by Edivel.
> - If the party brought news of Rattletrap, she offers a [[Potion of Healing]] and a trinket she found while clearing out Steed's Point that reminds her of him. Roll on the [[Arcturian Trinkets]] table for the item at hand.

Kali wishes the party a final goodbye and good luck on their journey, acclaiming them to the crowd.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> > Farewell, my friends! Everyone, please, give these adventurers the well wishes they so deserve!
>
> As the crowd cheers you on, you head off to your next adventure with lighter feet, as if the crowd itself is lifting you up with a little sprinkling of magic.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> This ends the general quest line. The party is free to explore Nain or head elsewhere in search of new adventures. They can also buy alchemical or herbalist goods from Kali going forward, with a 20% "friends of Kali" discount.
