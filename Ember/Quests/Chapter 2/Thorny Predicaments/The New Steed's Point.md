# The New Steed's Point

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Guide
>
> In this Social Event the party has returned to [[Steed's Point]] some time after the events of Thorny Predicaments, and after convincing [[Kali Andrella]] to rebuild Steed's Point. Exploring the settlement, characters can:
>
> - See how things have changed since Kali began rebuilding.
> - Learn how things have been going for Kali and [[Rattletrap, the Rickety Man]].
> - Update Kali on recent events.

> [!abstract] Kali Andrella
> **[[Kali Andrella]]**
>
> Level 1 · Unknown Unknown
>
> ![Kali Andrella](../../../assets/image084.webp)

### Catching up with Kali

Kali is in the midst of working, but happy to see the party.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you step into the settlement, the thorns seem to melt away, and Kali steps out from behind a freshly-painted door, carrying a basket filled with goods.
>
> > Welcome to Steed's Point, we're so glad you came to vi—
>
> Kali stops talking as she sees you.
>
> > Oh, friends! I didn't see you there!
>
> Kali walks toward you, but somehow never stops working, her dancing motions somehow divided between pruning a thorny edge, casting a spell in its direction, and setting goods out on an impromptu table made of wood scraps to her side.
>
> > So glad you came back for a visit! How have you been?
>
> She gestures to the surrounding area.
>
> > It's got a lot more to do, but, it's coming along, right?

If the party completed [[The Rickety Man]] in the Book of Tales quest, and Rattletrap is still alive, the sound of metal clanging can be clearly heard near by.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A loud metal clang from in the distance brings a smile to Kali's face.
>
> > That’s Rattletrap, or Bertron. Both? He’s still the heart and soul of Steed’s Point, and he’s even gotten back into doing some smithing, though I don't let him do it unless he's been thoroughly soaked with water beforehand. Last thing I need is him catching fire.

If the party [[Grassland Artifice]] and Moriah is alive, she says:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> > You just missed Moriah. Still got the wanderlust in her feet. I’d almost blame her for it if I didn’t know I’m the one who probably gave it to her. But now Steed’s Point is on her travels, and she brings us things from near and far to help build this place back up. Feels like we’re mother and daughter again, ready to take on the world.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** How is the rebuild going?
>
> **A:**
>
> > We still have a lot to do to make Steed's Point anything like what it used to be. And in some ways, it can't be the same and we'd be fools to try. Can't bring back everyone lost, can't undo what time has done. What we can do is build back, make this a place folks feel safe visiting, trading, and sharing stories. With everything going on in these parts right now, travelers need a place they can think of as a safe resting place. And if those Otherhood or anyone else tries to come take it away again, they'll see just how strong we are.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What is the plan for Steed's Point?
>
> **A:**
>
> > As you can see, I'm doing my part to attract the right sort, putting together a few items that folks will like and trying to make the place hospitable.
> >
> > If you can tell those you meet that Steed's Point is a safe place to land, I'd appreciate it.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Have you been keeping up with Edivel?
>
> **A:**
>
> > I'm keeping an eye on them from afar as best I can. Last I heard, they'd had an unfortunate incident with some cave wasps and came back to Brevin lit up like a starry night, but they're getting better at agrimagic too. They'll be a force to reckon with one of these days. Eventually.

### Items For Sale

With their talk complete, Kali is open for business as a seller, with a few items for trade.

> [!info] Social
> #### First One's Free
>
> As a thank you for all they have done for her and for Steed's Point, Kali will allow the group to take one item for free from the following list. With a successful `[[/check persuasion 20]]`, the party can convince Kali to part with one additional item at no cost.
>
> - [[Growing Thorns]]
> - a pouch of Throwing Stones, which have the properties of [[Beads of Force]]
> - an [[Herbalism Kit]]
> - an [[Everbloom]]
> - A [[Potion of Healing]]
>
> In addition, Kali will throw in a bonus item for each of the following conditions met.
>
> - If the party saved Brevin, she gives them an additional [[Potion of Healing]] and a [[Potion of Climbing]].
> - If the party completed Grassland Artifice and she has been reunited with Moriah, she shares some of the knowledge that Moriah has brought from her recent travels, clearing the fog of war from an area TBD.
> - If the party helped Rattletrap, she gives them a new tool from his smithing shop, a [[Temporary Tool]], which they can use in an emergency if needed.

As the party departs, Kali wishes them well on their next adventures and offers an incentive to return.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you turn to exit Steed's Point, Kali waves you off while simultaneously checking over her remaining goods.
>
> > Thank you all for visiting! You're welcome back any time, and while I can't always give away my goods, you'll always get the Kali Andrella discount for Friends of Steed's Point. I hope you take advantage soon! And since you helped me, I hope you'll take a little help of your own.
>
> She smiles and waves in your direction as a warm breeze sweeps around you and it feels for a moment as if someone's steady hand is on your back, pushing you forward as you head into your next adventure.
