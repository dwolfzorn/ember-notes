# Revelers on the Road

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> In this Social Event, the party runs into a pair of travelers while themselves traveling across the [[Golden Flats]]. By speaking to Karao and Leini, who have gotten turned around trying to find [[Brevin]], the characters can:
>
> - Help the pair get their bearings and figure out the way to Brevin.
> - Learn a little bit about the travelers, and the local area.
> - Experience a tremor, and learn that they are becoming more common in the [[Arctus Plateau]].

### Speaking to the Travelers

**Karao (Kithil Kivahr, he/him)**

Calm, and reserved, Karao tends toward introspection and carefully considered action, contrasting with Leini's enthusiastic desire to act first and think never. He seems to have an infinite well of patience, and often serves as a moderating force for Leini's impulses.

**Leini (Kkithil Keth, she/her)**

Possessing a mind prone to wandering into often nonsensical tangents, Leini is eccentric to put it mildly. She makes no effort to explain how her thoughts flow from one to the next, but is happy to share them with any stranger she meets in her travels.

If a character is **Culture: Kithil**, Leini is especially excited to see a fellow Kithil adventurer. Her response varies based on time of day.

If it is night:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> > Of course! I should have known a fellow Kithil would be basking in the moonlight the way that we are.
> >
> > I know that part of going off Sunning is to experience other cultures that live under the harsh gaze of Lantyr before settling down, but we try to do as much traveling under the moons as we can, even now.
> >
> > You seem much more experienced, though — not just off sunning for a season like we are before heading back to your village, but adventuring full time. Any way you can help us?

#### If it is day:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> > A fellow Kithil!
> >
> > You look too experienced to just be Sunning the way we are, getting your time out under Lantyr's punishing light in order to learn from other cultures before returning to a sensible Kithil village to live under the moonlight.
> >
> > Perhaps you're better at keeping track of where you are under Lantyr's gaze, can help us find our way?

The pair of Kithil are sunning, a Kithil tradition of going out to explore the world during the daylight before returning to settle down for nocturnal life in a Kithil village.

> [!info] Social
> #### Reading the Kithil
>
> A character that makes a successful **Deception (DC 15)** check confirms that the two are simply lost. They bear no negative feelings, and pose no danger to the party. Speaking to the party, the pair can talk about the following:
>
> - They are heading to Brevin for the celebration of its new bridge. Leini will be helping a friend, Temi, with her shop, while Karao is hoping for a blessing from [[Aythorn]], who will be in attendance.
> - Shortly after crossing the [[Ordani Skywalk]], they were waylaid by a mysterious robber calling themselves the "Canyon Crawler," who demanded they leave most of their supplies and map behind.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About destination?
>
> **A:**
>
> Karao gestures vaguely in a direction, seeming unsure where to point.
>
> > We're trying to find Brevin.
> >
> > I don't know if you've heard of it, but it's one of those settlements grown from seeds, and they're getting ready to unveil a vine bridge that stretches all the way across the Splinter Canyons.
> >
> > Leini here is helping her old friend Temi with her shop during the event, but I'll be keeping my eye out for Aythorn - they're known to give out blessings at special occasions, and who wouldn't want to be blessed by a shard god?
> >
> > If we get there in time.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About getting lost?
>
> **A:**
>
> Leini explains:
>
> > It happened so fast. We weren’t more than a mile or two away from the Ordani Skywalk, on this side of the Canyons, when someone we didn't even see swooped up out of nowhere and attacked us.
> >
> > Lucky they didn’t try to kill us.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About getting attacked?
>
> **A:**
>
> Karao nods and says:
>
> > They didn’t seem too interested in doing real damage, honestly. Just threw out a bunch of threats and grabbed at our feet with vines until we agreed to leave our stuff behind. Including our map. They called themselves … what was it, Leini?
>
> Karao looks toward Leini who says:
>
> > The Canyon Crawler!
>
> Karao nods, looking back to you.
>
> > The Canyon Crawler. So, keep an eye out.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About the Canyon Crawler?
>
> **A:**
>
> Karao shrugs at you and says:
>
> > We've got no idea who or what they are. We never saw them, and weren't willing to risk them actually harming us. We weren't carrying anything worth dying for.

### Finding the Path

Without a map, Karao and Leini don't know where Brevin is exactly, but they know that it has a bridge that will be going across to [[Ordain]], so it must be near the [[Splinter Canyons]]. They just don't know how far north.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Way to Brevin
>
> A character can use any of the following checks to help the strangers figure out the direction of Brevin:
>
> - A successful `[[/check nature 16]]` check while surveying the land nearby would reckon that a seed city could viably grow in the northern region of the Splinter Canyons.
> - A successful `[[/check history 16]]` check would know a bit about the seed cities, including the presence of one in the northern section of the Splinter Canyons, built on the cliffside.
> - A successful `[[/check survival 16]]` check while looking at the dirt map that the travelers have drawn allows a character to compare it to the landmarks nearby and make an educated guess about Brevin's location.
>
> - [[Planting a Seed]]: A character that previously visited Brevin automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Trade**: A character familiar with trade automatically succeeds on this check.

If the party is able to help Leini and Karao, the two are grateful and begin gathering their stuff to head in that direction. Otherwise, if they are not able to help the pair, Leini and Karo thank them for stopping to speak with some wayward travelers.

### A Sudden Tremor

As characters are leaving, or at any point at the GM's discretion, a minor earthquake moves through the area.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Triggering the Tremor
>
> Mark the **Minor Tremor** outcome of this event as completed to trigger the earthquake tremor effect.

`[[/outcome tremor]]`

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The ground shakes slightly, as if it were being tugged back and forth and up and down, jolting the two travelers and shaking you from below. Wherever you try to place your feet, the ground seems to move away from them, and only your own balance and the things around you are there to try to keep you steady. The two travelers from Ordain look much worse for wear, jerking back and forth as they try to keep their footing.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### A Minor Shake
>
> Characters must succeed on a `[[/save dexterity 15]]` saving throw to stay upright during the tremors. On failure, the character is thrown to the ground and takes `[[/damage 1d4 bludgeoning]]` damage.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> In the aftermath of the shaking, Leini peers over at the map scrawled into the dirt and says:
>
> > At least our map is still readable! It's probably time for us to use it and get moving, before things start shaking again.
>
> Karao dusts off their legs and picks up their bag.
>
> > Agreed, these tremors always leave my legs wobbling. Feels like the last few years they've been getting more and more common around here. We should get moving before the ground opens up and swallows us, come on, Leini!
>
> Leini waves and begins walking off, Karao following after a moment later.
>
> > Thank you for your help, safe travels!

### Concluding the Event
