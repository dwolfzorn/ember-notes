# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Side Quest occurs during Chapter 2 as the party follows clues leading to a foreboding and fog-shrouded section of the subterranean Pathways. There are no prerequisites for this Quest and it can be undertaken at any time, although it is likely to be encountered if the party is traveling with Ankarist while undertaking the [[Ancient Paths]] Quest.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> The "To Fall and Fall Again" Quest is designed for a party of Level 3 characters, although the finale of the Quest, [[Shining Obelisk]], may prove difficult for a party that is not yet Level 4.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest that awards 2 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon completion of the [[Twisting Shadows]] and [[Shining Obelisk]] Events.

"To Fall and Fall Again" has its origins in the distant past, in the history of the giants — a chronicle that takes place over a vast duration of time, dating all the way back to the earliest days of the [[Age of Beasts]]. The first primeval giants encountered the malevolent influence of [[The Abyss]] long ago — ultimately leading to their downfall — and the survivors fled their Pathways homes to settle in the [[Shana Mountains]] that span the continent of [[Aterica]]. These people began to change, establishing a culture significantly different from their origins, and would eventually become known as the [[Shent]].

Unfortunately for the Shent, The Abyss and its rapacious denizens never forgot (or forgave) the Shent's cosmological meddling. When the [[Abyssal Shear]] that followed [[The Shattering]] ripped apart [[The Weave]] two and half thousand years ago, the Shent were ruthlessly targeted for destruction by servants of the [[Outer Gods]] and their Abyssal magic, who pursued them above all other mortals. However, the Shent — as seers and prophets of great power — had foreseen their own demise, and taken numerous steps to safeguard their knowledge in an effort to protect the world. One of the Shent, a giant named [[Mioroth]], built a machine that he imbued with his own mind and memories. Though Mioroth died along with his people, his resolve and ability to protect the world against The Abyss endures.

There are many routes that may bring the party into the [[Fogbound Caverns]] within the Pathways, but once there, they encounter not only the influence of The Abyss, but something far older and more terrifying than anything they've encountered before. The true scale of the threat is revealed at the end of the Quest, both within the [[Primordial Bastion]], and later, in Ordain, where the party will have the opportunity to speak with the Elder Goddess [[Spectra]] and have their worst fears confirmed. This Quest also continues the story of the character Mioroth, who the party may have encountered in [[Lunar Awakenings]] during the [[Over The Moon]] Quest, and [[Where Evil Lurks]] during the [[A Brush With Death]] Quest.
