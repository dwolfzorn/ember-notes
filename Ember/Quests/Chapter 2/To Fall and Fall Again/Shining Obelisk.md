# Shining Obelisk

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration and Combat Event takes place in the southwestern hex of the [[Primordial Bastion]]. In it, the party reaches the top of the bastion, where they find the guardian [[Luxaroth]] standing before the glimmering Orb of Lantyr — an ancient artifact that repels the Abyss. By ascending to the top, the characters can:
>
> - Defend and repair the Orb of Lantyr while a swarm of Abyssal monsters attempt to break through the barrier created by its power. They are likely aided by Luxaroth in this battle.
> - The party may find themselves fighting the Luxaroth instead, depending on their choices and how much Abyssal Attunement they have accumulated.
>
> In this event, the characters can:
>
> - Gain 1 point of Heart or Abyss Attunement — depending on whether they choose to repair or destroy the Orb of Lantyr (each character).
> - Gain 1 point of Cora or Luxarum Attunement — depending on whether they choose to defeat Luxaroth in combat or befriend him (each character).
>
> #### Area Walkthrough
>
> Exploration of this Event takes place on The Bleak Archive Area Map. A complete description of its contents can be found in the Area Walkthrough, [[Bastion Apex]].

### Climbing to the Apex

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Walkthrough
>
> Refer to [[Bastion Apex]] for a complete walkthrough of the area, which takes place on the [[Bastion Apex]] area map. This includes exploration of the area, the main combat encounter, investigation and skill checks, and other details not contained here.

While at the Apex of the Bastion, the party must choose whether to repair the Orb of Lantyr Relic or help destroy it. Depending on the party's decision, this may also mean confronting and defeating [[Luxaroth]]. Luxaroth may also be defeated independently of the decision to repair the orb.

### The Aftermath

Once the party has completed the encounter at the Apex of the Primordial Bastion, a number of different events may occur. While these mostly determine the way that the party leaves the Fogbound Caverns, they can also have repercussions in the future.

`[[/outcome repaired]]`

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Light Restored
>
> If the party repairs the Orb of Lantyr Relic and aids Luxaroth, the Avatar of the First Giants uses his renewed power to magically transport the party to the beginning of the Fogbound Caverns at ⬢ s.3205.2888. This is the shortest route out of the caverns, and the party may not wish to enter the area again. Thankfully, with the Orb of Lantyr shining brightly once more, Abyssal monsters and energies gradually begin to die back and fade over the next several weeks and months. If the party decides to leave the area this way, while it may be the quickest route, they may not learn of the diminishing Abyssal influence for some time.

#### Heart Attunement: Orb Repaired

If the party manages to successfully repair the Orb of Lantyr, each character advances their **Attunement: Heart of Ember (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

If Luxaroth survived the battle, mark this Event Outcome:

`[[/outcome survived]]`

If players defeated Luxaroth in combat, mark this Event Outcome:

`[[/outcome died]]`

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Luxaroth Gone
>
> With the Avatar seemingly gone, whether destroyed or recovering magically, the party will not be able to count on his aid again and have to make their way back through the Fogbound Caverns on foot.
>
> As the party makes their way back, any character that has **Knowledge: Abyssals** or succeeds on a `[[/check nature 14]]` check can confirm that the biome and area around them are slowly losing some of the Abyssal energies, and the omnipresent fog and feelings of wrongness are beginning to fade. Likely, this will take some weeks or months.

#### Cora Attunement: Luxaroth Defeated

If the party defeats Luxaroth in combat, each character advances their **Attunement: Cora (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Luxarum Attunement: Luxaroth Befriended

If the party chooses to ally themselves with Luxaroth, each character advances their **Attunement: Luxarum (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

`[[/outcome destroyed]]`

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Overwhelming Darkness
>
> With the Avatar destroyed alongside the Orb of Lantyr Relic, the light is now completely gone in the Fogbound Caverns, and darkness has fallen entirely. The party will not be able to count on the aid of Luxaroth ever again, as he is truly gone, and they must make their way back through the rising danger of the unbound Fogbound Caverns on foot.
>
> As the party makes their way back, any character that has **Knowledge: Abyssals** or succeeds on a `[[/check nature 13]]` or `[[/check arcana 13]]` check can confirm that the biome and area around them is becoming even more infused with Abyssal energies. The fog grows thicker, and attacks by monsters in every hex they pass through will also increase. In the long term, the darkness here is likely to spread even onto the surface of the Arctus Plateau.

#### Abyss Attunement: Orb Destroyed

If the party chooses to help destroy the Orb of Lantyr, each character advances their **Attunement: The Abyss (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Milestone
>
> Completing this Event earns the party 1 [[Milestone Progression]], potentially advancing them in Level.
>
> #### Next Steps
>
> The quest is effectively over after this event. However, if the party aided Luxaroth, he will have directed them to the "quiet nook" in the shining city above. There, in the event [[Magic Incarnate]], they will meet someone who may shed more light on the events here and assist them further in the coming struggles.
>
> If the party destroyed the orb, they will not have learned anything about the final event or where to find it, and thus will have to encounter [[Magic Incarnate]] naturally as they traverse the city of Ordain.
