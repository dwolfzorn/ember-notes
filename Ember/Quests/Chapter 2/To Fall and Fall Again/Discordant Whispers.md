# Discordant Whispers

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration Event has a low chance of triggering (and retriggering as often as every 48 hours) anywhere in the Arctus Plateau after the party begins Chapter 2 — with a much higher chance of happening at night. In it, members of the party experience malevolent and painful whispers, whose contents depends on decisions previously made.
>
> Each time the Event triggers:
>
> - Evaluate which whisper criteria one or more party members meets.
> - From that list of valid whispers, choose one that has no party member has heard before.
> - Have the party members to whom the criteria applies experience the whisper.

### About the Whispers

Unbeknownst to the party, these tempting whispers originate from [[Vhismara]] — one of the immensely powerful [[Outer Gods]] — who seeks to corrupt party members to serve the villainous purposes of [[The Abyss]]. Despite their offers of strength and power, these whispers are viscerally uncomfortable to experience, and bring with them painful headaches and migraines.

Potential whispers come in a variety of categories, each with related criteria:

**Quest-Related Whispers**

These whispers depend on Quest Events that the party has experienced, and should be heard by the party as a whole.

**Item-Related Whispers**

These whispers are based on items in a character's inventory, and should be heard only by the character currently carrying the item in question.

**Character Option Whispers**

These whispers are based on character options selected by party members, such as [[Attunement]] and [[Ancestry]], and should only be heard by the specific character they apply to.
