# Chessmen Homecoming

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This social event affords the party a final meeting with Zodi Trask and Varholomew Chess in Lower Arcturel, who eagerly await evidence of Silver Beam's illicit activity in the sinkhole city. In this event, the characters can:
>
> - Present the evidence they collected during the raid on the Silver Beam Headquarters, notably [[Larissa Toth's Memory Shard]], and receive the agreed-upon reward for their efforts.
> - Learn about the hopeful futures of the four Downsiders.
> - Learn about the future of Silver Beam operations in Arcturel.

### Mission Accomplished

When the characters reach [[Zodi Trask's Apartment]] in Lower Arcturel, they'll encounter [[Zodi Trask]] himself alongside [[Vartholomew Chess]], who are eager to discuss matters with the party.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The FInal Evidence
>
> In order to conclude their investigation for Zodi Trask, the party must present him with substantial evidence of Larissa Toth's machinations against Arcturel. This is accomplished via presentation of [[Larissa Toth's Memory Shard]], which details her devious plans.
>
> In addition to the Memory Shard, the characters may choose to present the following information taken from the raid:
>
> - [[Silver Beam Sentinel]] scraps.
> - [[Larissa Toth's Grimoire]], which details her esoteric ritual for The Diagram Transcendent.

Upon delivery of this final evidence, Zodi Trask awards the characters the agreed-upon payment of  **20**, and extends an invitation to stay in Arcturel as long as they'd like.

- If one of the characters managed to persuade Zodi to throw in some extra payment, he also awards them the [[Rallying Tonic]] and a [[Bottle of Shadowcap Wine]] as promised.
- Vartholomew Chess is overwhelmed with gratitude as well, and offers the characters a 25% discount on any future purchases of Chessmen automatons.

#### Heart Attunement: Downsiders Absolved

If the party successfully delivers enough evidence to Zodi Trask to implicate Silver Beam and absolve Hew and the Downsiders of their alleged crimes, each character advances their **Attunement: Heart of Ember (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

### Regarding Silver Beam

> [!info] Social
> #### Repercussions of the Raid
>
> With the matter of the investigation settled, Zodi has a few updates to share with the party regarding the future of Silver Beam's enterprises in Arcturel:
>
> > Arcturel's gonna need a couple days to react, but expect whoever is left after kicking around town after the raid to get rounded up and put on trial for their part in murder, sabotage, coverups and so forth. I don't expect Arcturel to be lenient with Silver Beam or its leadership.
> >
> > There may be jail time, exile from the town, seizing of assets and materials, loss of awarded work contracts… not to mention the complete erosion of any trust or good will they built up in the town.
> >
> > They'll be lucky if the consortium can even operate in Arcturel after this.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Politics** or **Knowledge: Intrigue** suspect that the Trading Houses will do anything they can to take out Silver Beam's reach in Ordain. Zodi agrees:
>
> > Oh yeah, House Cevher's absolutely going to drag this in front of the Ordinate. They won't take Silver Beam encroaching into their markets with grace, and I would expect them to push for a blanket ban on most Silver Beam products in Ordani markets. Extreme, but, don't mess with the Trading Houses.

### Regarding the Chessmen

Once the discussion of Silver Beam has concluded, Zodi shifts the conversation to the Chessmen and other constructs of Arcturel. Read or paraphrase the following section:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Zodi snaps his fingers and turns to Vartholomew.
>
> > Right, about the Silver Beam constructs, Chess. Expect the town council to ask you to look at all of the existing Silver Beam constructs, verify they are safe, and undo any sabotage you find. You up for that?
>
> Vartholomew faintly winces at the question.
>
> > That's a lot of work. Undoing the modifications is pretty straightforward, but there are quite a few of them to look at. Just tell me they aren't going to be given back to Silver Beam afterward.
>
> Zodi shrugs in response.
>
> > It's possible, but I doubt it. Will ultimately depend on higher powers than us, though. Once Arcturel draws up a formal response to all this there will be questions of design theft, ownership rights, and you might end up with a bunch of consortium constructs that need to be fixed in the near future.
>
> Chess grimaces.
>
> > I guess I should put up some help wanted flyers then...

> [!info] Social
> #### Second Chances for Shepp
>
> If the party mentions Ifton Shepp and his guilt and efforts, or has mentioned it, Vart will say:
>
> > Funny you'd mention him. He came by to formally apologize for what he did. I've been considering bringing him back into the shop to lend a hand. He knows more about what the Silver Beam constructs than I do, and he is a fine artisan.
> >
> > I might not be able to forgive him for the betrayal, but Arcturians always bond over a project, and whatever was broken can be fixed. So who knows…

### Regarding the Downsiders

Finally, Zodi and Vartholomew have some words to share about the fate of Hew and the Downsiders:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Zodi snaps his fingers and looks over at Chess.
>
> > That reminds me, I'm going to need to drag you before the town's merchant council and explain what happened to Chamberlain. I'm hoping we can get him full autonomy from Silver Beam and extend that to the rest of the Chessmen. I don't want any of them getting relegated to construct status if someone gets tired of paying them their fair share.
>
> Vartholomew nods:
>
> > You have my word I'll do anything I can in that regard, Zodi. They are more like the Hulg'run now than mere constructs, we have to recognize that and make sure they are treated fairly on that grounds.
>
> Zodi looks pleased by that and then turns his attention your way again.
>
> > But yes, about the Chessmen. We're working on getting them sorted out. Was there one you wanted to know about specifically?

> [!info] Social
> #### Chamberlain's Freedom
>
> Vartholomew speaks on the current state and future of Chamberlain:
>
> > For now, Chamberlain's been helping me at Arcturian Automatons and accompanying me around town. It's all under the pretenses of his repair and rehabilitation work to keep him from ending up back in Silver Beam hands. He's technically still their property, but he understandably doesn't want to go back.
>
> #### Hew's Promotion
>
> Zodi says the following about Hew:
>
> > Since Hew was still keen to mine, I got him a position within House Cevher's mining operations here in Arcturel. They will be starting next week, and I wouldn't be shocked if I have them running their own crew inside of a month.
>
> #### Rider's Alliance
>
> Zodi has the following update to share on Rider:
>
> > I talked to Hob Korell and got him to take Rider on as a new hire. It took some convincing, but since Rider is good with animals, and a bit harder to injure than the average person, he's going to be handling breaking and training some of the more unconventional steeds coming out of the Sinkhole Depths.
>
> #### Lucent's Return
>
> Zodi helped get Lucent their job back, and says:
>
> > Nodrick Stolas was happy to have Lucent come back, and made a deal to pick up the construct as Glimmer's security specialist. I'm not privy to the particulars of their agreement, but both parties seemed to be agreeable to whatever deal was struck.
