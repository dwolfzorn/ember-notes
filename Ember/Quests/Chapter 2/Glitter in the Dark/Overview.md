# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Side Quest occurs during Chapter 2 as the party is drawn by rumor or circumstance to the mining settlement of Arcturel. There are no prerequisites for this Quest and it can be undertaken at any time, although it is certain to be encountered if traveling with Lyla while undertaking the [[An Old Friend]] Quest.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> The "Glitter in the Dark" quest is designed for a party of Level 3 characters, although the finale of the quest, either [[Storming the Consortium]] or [[Checkmate for Chessmen]] may prove difficult for a party that is not yet Level 4.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest that awards 2 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point upon completion of the [[Junkyard Cogs]] Event, and a second upon completion of one of the mutually-exclusive [[Storming the Consortium]] or [[Checkmate for Chessmen]] Events.

Whether they reach [[Arcturel]] on the heels of rumors about the Renegade Construct or of their own accord, the party is promptly requisitioned upon arrival by [[Zodi Trask]] of [[House Cevher]] to investigate a local matter of utmost importance. A miner named Kellan Lorde was recently killed during a questionable incident with a [[Chessman]] construct during routine operations in The Dives district of lower Arcturel. The construct promptly disappeared, and escaped somewhere into the sprawling subterranean city or into a furtive corner of the [[Sinkhole Depths]], where it remains "dangerously" at-large.

The party must investigate the crime and track down the malfunctioning Chessman, known as the "Renegade Construct" to most of the citizens of Arcturel — but as [[Hew]] to the construct's compatriots. Along the way, they'll meet several of Arcturel's noteworthy denizens, including the famed artificer [[Vartholomew Chess]] (the [[Hulg'run]] responsible for making the Chessmen) and the [[Silver Beam Consortium]] executive [[Larissa Toth]] (a [[Railen]] transmuter who lurks at the heart of the Quest as its secret villain).

What the people of Arcturel don't know is that Hew's malfunction is actually a supernatural awakening caused by Larissa Toth's own arcane tampering — a detail the Railen executive is desperate to conceal from public understanding. In an attempt to sabotage House Cevher and Chessian operations in Arcturel, Toth has weaponized various Chessmen with a chaotic transmutation ritual known as The Diagram Transcendent. Indeed, a total of four awakened Chessmen lurk in the Sinkhole Depths away from prying eyes; and it's up to the party to determine whether the deeds of these constructs (nicknamed the Downsiders) merit their destruction or their autonomy.
