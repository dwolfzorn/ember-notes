# Storming the Consortium

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Against the Consortium
>
> This combat event challenges the party with the exploration and infiltration of the Silver Beam Headquarters in Upper Arcturel, where they must obtain evidence of Larissa Toth's insidious plan to undermine House Cevher, Vartholomew Chess, and other denizens of Arcturel. In this event, the characters can:
>
> - Join the Downsiders in the Underbelly nighclub at Glimmer, where they'll receive a report from Chamberlain about the Silver Beam facility and its security protocols.
> - Plan their precise method of infiltration.
> - Set out to infiltrate the Silver Beam headquarters and secure the evidence necessary to bring Larissa Toth to justice: [[Larissa Toth's Memory Shard]].

The party begins this event in the [[Glimmer Lounge]] of Upper Arcturel's Underbelly, alongside the Downsiders: [[Hew]], [[Rider]], [[Lucent]], and [[Chamberlain]] — who is particularly suited to provide a few clear details about the Silver Beam facility and its inhabitants.

> [!info] Social
> #### A Brief from Chamberlain
>
> Prior to his awakening, Chamberlain served the Silver Beam Consortium under the iron fist of Larissa Toth and her Railen engineers. Fortunately for the party, this time of servitude has provided the Chessman with an intimate understanding of the Silver Beam facility, its various hazards, and its personnel. Before the characters set off from the relative safety of the Glimmer Lounge, Chamberlain is able to share the following details:
>
> - Larissa Toth is assisted by 4 Silver Beam Engineers and 4 Silver Beam Guards. At any given point in time, one Guard patrols the upper floor, one Guard patrols the lower floor, and 2 Guards are stationed in the Security Room.
> - Silver Beam Servitors are stationed at the facility, but Chamberlain can't verify the total number, since he's been gone for nearly a month. There were at least two working models on the floor before Chamberlain escaped.
> - Alarm buttons are located throughout the facility that enable lockdown protocols: one in the Silver Beam Foyer, one in the Director's Office, one in the Security Room, and one in the Cubicles downstairs.
> - Multiple elevators at the rear of the facility provide freight access to the Dives below.
> - Although the party is unaware of the specific evidence they're looking for, Chamberlain is quick to explain that Larissa Toth keeps the most sensitive documents related to the Consortium's efforts in her executive office.
>
> #### Planning the Approach
>
> If they've yet to do so, the characters must now plan the method of ingress for their infiltration of the Silver Beam headquarters. We've detailed some possible infiltration methods below for the characters to consider, categorized as one of two main avenues of approach: **The** **Stealth Approach** and **The** **Direct Approach**.
>
> Gameplay for this event is intended to follow the motivations, proclivities, and instincts of the player characters, rather than rely on the guidance of the NPCs. If the party members struggle to devise a method of infiltration on their own, refer to the appropriate section below to offer them some guidance.

### The Stealth Approach

If the characters choose to sneak their way into the Silver Beam headquarters, the following methods of entry are potential options for them:

> [!info] Social
> #### Tradeway Deceptions
>
> Characters can attempt to bluff their way from the [[Silver Beam Foyer]] to the [[Director's Office]] via a series of successful Deception checks.
>
> A successful **Deception (DC 18)** check is required to deceive any [[Silver Beam Guard]], [[Silver Beam Engineer]], and [[Arcturian]] on duty in the facility, who are on high alert following Larissa's previous meeting with Zodi and the party.
>
> - Check once per question asked or lie told.
> - Characters with **Knowledge: Intrigue** have advantage on these checks.
> - Characters wearing [[Silver Beam Uniform]] also have **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> #### Underbelly Deceptions
>
> Some of the characters can don Silver Beam uniforms in an attempt to deceive their way through the facility. 2 extra Silver Beam Guard uniforms can be found in the [[Barracks]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Stealth via the Tradeway
>
> Spells or items that offer an illusory appearance or make the character invisible can help the characters infiltrate unseen or unnoticed.
>
> #### Stealth via the Underbelly
>
> Characters can use a [[Grapnel]] to span the platform from the Glimmer Lounge to the Silver Beam catwalks. A single Silver Beam Guard patrols this sector in 5-minute shifts. Each character must succeed on a **Athletics (DC 13)** check in order to cross quickly enough to avoid being seen.
>
> Additionally, characters can purchase a [[Potion of Jumping]] from [[Arvoda's Elixirs]] that can assist the effort when it comes to spanning the gap between Glimmer and Silver Beam.

#### Primordis Attunement: The Stealth Approach

If the party chooses the Stealth Approach for their infiltration of the Silver Beam HQ, each character advances their **Attunement: Primordis (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

### The Direct Approach

If the characters choose to physically force their way into the Silver Beam headquarters, the following methods of entry are potential options for them:

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Bravado via the Tradeway
>
> Characters can force their way into the Silver Beam facility via the Silver Beam Foyer by taking advantage of the element of surprise. The obsequious **Receptionist** (Lawful Evil, Railen Altyra, she/her) stationed here is poised to push the red security button, but can't act quickly enough to seal the front door if the party coordinates their movement. Treat the Receptionist as an [[Arcturian]].
>
> Once each of the PCs make their first moves, roll for initiative, including the foyer's Receptionist — who will immediately push the Red Button twice on her turn. See [[Gameplay Details]] for the effects of pressing the button.
>
> #### Bravado via the Underbelly
>
> If a Silver Beam Guard is alerted during the course of an unsuccessful "Stealth via the Underbelly" attempt (see above), or if the party brazenly attempts to span the gap from the Glimmer Lounge without attempting to sneak, the Silver Beam Guard on duty will engage as soon as the characters are spotted.
>
> Once the characters are spotted, roll for initiative. The Silver Beam Guard on patrol will call for help during the first round of combat, alerting the Silver Beam Guards at the Barracks. One of these Guards will join the encounter on the catwalk while the other Guard will move upstairs to alert the rest of the Silver Beam facility.

#### Ragen Attunement: The Direct Approach

If the party chooses the Direct Approach for their infiltration of the Silver Beam HQ, each character advances their **Attunement: Ragen (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

### Point of No Return

If the alarm is sounded at any point in time while the characters are infiltrating the Silver Beam headquarters, the entire facility is placed on alert, per the [[Arcturel Upper]] of the [[Arcturel Upper]] area walkthrough.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Silver Beam Security Protocol
>
> When the Silver Beam headquarters is placed on alert — whether via the Red Button alarm located in the Silver Beam Foyer or by word of mouth — all Silver Beam enemies in the area roll for initiative to join the initiative order. The various enemies will behave as follows:
>
> - The [[Silver Beam Engineer]] move to activate any inactive Silver Beam Servitors.
> - The [[Silver Beam Guard]] maintain a suppressing fire against the advancing party with their [[Heavy Crossbow]].
> - The Receptionist in the Silver Beam Foyer retreats from the scene into the Upper Arcturel Tradeway, never to be seen again. Any other arcturians in the area retreat as well via the Elevators into Lower Arcturel.
> - Once activated, the [[Silver Beam Servitor]] here will start off by targeting enemies at range with their [[Net]]. After a volley of nets, the Servitors will move into offensive positions, utilizing their [[Lightning Burst]] against any adjacent enemies before attacking with their [[Claws]] and [[Charged Claw]].
> - [[Larissa Toth]] will stand back to utilize her array of composed magic to reinforce the attacks of other Silver Beam forces from a distance, ordering the Silver Beam Servitors, Guards, and Engineers as she sees fit — notably leaning on the Control rune to target already engaged enemies and weaken their resolve, while reserving attention to any noticeable enemy spellcasters in order to lean heavily on Counterspells. She'll otherwise use **Arrow of Flame** and **Illuminated Ray** liberally throughout the battle. As soon as the party poses any kind of threat to Larissa, she'll retreat into the confines of the [[Director's Office]] and activate the 4 [[Silver Beam Servitor]] located therein.
>
> #### The Sentinel Arrives
>
> On the 3rd round of combat, an unseen [[Silver Beam Sentinel]] arrives via the Western Elevator to confront the players. Any Silver Beam Engineers nearby will move to support the Sentinel with [[Field Repairs]].
>
> A highly-aggressive technological nightmare, the Silver Beam Sentinel will attack enemy characters with no quarter, targeting the most powerful enemy in an attempt to incapacitate them quickly with its [[Flamethrower]] and [[Arcane Projectile Launcher]] actions.
>
> #### Dramatic Moments
>
> Once the Sentinel arrives, at the beginning of each combat round, one of six [[Silver Beam Sentinel Moments]] occurs. At the beginning of each round, roll on the table below and narrate the result. These moments can occur more than once, and continue until the Sentinel is destroyed or otherwise defeated.
>
> #### Reinforcements!
>
> If the characters happen to make short work of the Silver Beam forces and you find additional reinforcements are necessary for a balanced or challenging combat sequence, the two elevators here can deposit even more Silver Beam reinforcements to the battle. Mixed groups of `[[/roll 1d2]]` Silver Beam Servitors, a Silver Beam Engineer, and `[[/roll 1d2]]` Silver Beam Guards can arrive every other round.

### Confronting Larissa Toth

This event culminates when the characters encounter Larissa Toth, who is likely to behave in battle according to the following tactics.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Larissa Toth Tactics
>
> If and when combat in the main chamber of the Silver Beam Headquarters becomes too perilous for Larissa Toth, she'll retreat to the confines of the Director's Office for a final showdown with the party.
>
> When she first enters the chamber, Larissa will activate the 4 Silver Beam Servitors located on the pedestals here and take a defensive position behind the room's furniture. Any surviving Silver Beam forces in the facility at large will be commanded to fall back to the Waiting Room to flank Larissa's pursuers.
>
> If she has yet to do so, Larissa will activate her [[Tyraphic Transformation]] once she reaches the office. Once reinforcements arrive, Larissa will settle into a defensive position and prepare to hurl spells at the most vulnerable targets while the servitors act as a front line to distract the party.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Retrieving the Evidence
>
> The unidentified evidence the party seeks is contained in [[Larissa Toth's Memory Shard]], which can be found in Larissa Toth's personal inventory. Alternately, the characters can attempt to extract a "confession" from Larissa herself, but the Railen transmuter will not afford the party this information willingly.
>
> Larissa's desk also contains various accoutrements that reveal her occupation as a transmuter, including her research and rare components for the Luxarum incantation known as The Diagram Transcendent.

#### Signara Attunement: Transcendent Discoveries

The first character to adequately loot Larissa's desk and take possession of her research for The Diagram Transcendent advances their **Attunement: Signara (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.
