# In League with Silver Beam

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This social event allows the party to reach a conclusion of the quest's narrative by reporting their success at Aberin's Folly to Larissa Toth. In this event, the party can:
>
> - Journey to the Silver Beam Headquarters in Upper Arcturel for a meeting with [[Larissa Toth]] in her executive office.
> - Present evidence of their victory against the Chessmen at Aberin's Folly, including [[Hew]]'s physical remains — aka the [[Renegade Construct Scraps]] — if not the Renegade himself captured alive.
> - Receive a reward from Larissa Toth for their efforts.
> - Acclimate themselves to the Silver Beam Consortium's headquarters, which they can use as a resource going forward.

### A Warm Welcome

Once the characters have been welcomed into the Silver Beam antechamber, they have a few moments to examine their surroundings before [[Larissa Toth]] arrives. Refer to the Area Walkthrough for the precise details about this area and what information the characters can glean through observation.

After roughly 5 or 10 minutes, Larissa arrives to escort the party back to her office. Read the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The relative tranquility of this front room is interrupted by clangorous rumble of industry and machination, as the large door at the rear of the antechamber opens to reveal Larissa Toth, who emerges from the huge production warehouse of the Silver Beam facility. The comely bureaucrat steps forward with satisfaction in her stride as she regards your group:
>
> > I trust my champions were chosen well … Tell me, how did you fare against the Renegade and his Chessmen cohorts?
>
> Her eyes scan your belongings, eager to spot evidence of some triumph at the construct camp.

The party is expected to show some evidence at this time of their success at Aberin's Folly during [[Checkmate for Chessmen]]. If the characters didn't escort Hew in with them, they'll need to present the [[Renegade Construct Scraps]]. As long as the evidence is sincere, Larissa will thank the party and escort them further into the Silver Beam facility towards her office.

> [!info] Social
> #### Deceiving Larissa
>
> Alternately, the party could attempt to falsify evidence of a successful raid at the construct camp. If the characters attempt to present false evidence to Larissa (like a facsimile of the Renegade Construct Scraps), each character participating in the conversation must succeed on a **Deception (DC 18)** check in order to convince her of its legitimacy.
>
> - **Ancestry: Altyra** characters and characters with **Knowledge: Machines** have advantage on this check.
> - Alternately, any character who successfully charms Larissa through use of [[Rune: Control]] magic has **+2 Boons** on this check.

After a brief exchange in the Silver Beam antechamber, read the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The Railen executive escorts you northward, deeper into the Silver Beam facility, offering a glimpse at what the people of Arcturel can only imagine: a full-blown production facility for Silver Beam constructs, which are scattered throughout the area in various stages of completion.
>
> After a few moments, you find yourselves at the threshold of Larissa's executive office, which she enters without delay. She speaks to you without looking back, slowly making her way to an austere desk at the opposite end of the room:
>
> > Thanks to your efforts, the Consortium can sustain its endeavors here in Arcturel unhindered. As you can see, Chessmen will soon be a thing of the past, and our Aedir-inspired Silver Beam automatons will carry the load for the next phase of our expansions here in the Arctus Plateau. The Consortium is most grateful for your assistance with this matter, and I'm sure the people of Arcturel will be happy to know that justice has been done — justice in the name of progress. But I digress … A job well done deserves a just reward.
>
> Larissa reaches into a drawer beneath the edge of her desk, and produces a small pearl that gleans with a dull amber light, which she holds out towards you with an overt gesture of gratitude.
>
> > This is our latest innovation: a Focusing Pearl, which should prove useful to the spellcasters among you. I trust it will serve as a suitable gratuity for your accomplishments, and will serve you will in your ventures to come.
> >
> > I hope you've come to regard the Silver Beam Consortium as allies. We look forward to maintaining a profitable relationship with you in the seasons to come. Should you ever need my counsel, you know where to find me. Now, if you'll excuse me, I do have other matters to attend to. Oh, and you can leave the Shard of Sending with the receptionist on your way out. Good day, heroes.
>
> Larissa Toth dismisses you with a wry grin as she sits behind the desk, immediately absorbed in the accoutrements splayed out upon it. The door to the north slides open, where a Silver Beam Construct awaits, ready to escort your party back to the lobby.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Toth's Reward
>
> Larissa provides the party with their reward at this time: a [[Focusing Pearl]].

The party can attempt to continue their conversation with Larissa, but it goes nowhere fast, as she's eager to not only get back to her work — but to see the characters go about their own business as well. Larissa will entertain a few quick exchanges as the characters idle and leave, but a protracted conversation will be met with snide derision followed by an overt request to them to depart.

#### Luxarum Attunement: Silver Beam Allies

If the party accepts Larissa'a reward and embraces their new role as allies of the Silver Beam Consortium, each character advances their **Attunement: Luxarum (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

### Concluding the Event
