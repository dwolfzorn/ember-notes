# The Story So Far

## 8 Months Ago

- [[Pyix]] arranges for the purchase of land and mine holdings in/around [[Arcturel]], and instructs [[Larissa Toth]] to plan a new consortium branch the sinkhole city. The plan: establish dominance over [[Inkaro Pearl, White]] trade and destabilize [[House Cevher]], at the behest of [[Zerranyss]].

## 5 Months Ago

- The [[Silver Beam Consortium]] begins overt construction of their HQ in Arcturel, utilizing Chessmen purchased from their creator as well as a skilled labor force of Silver Beam immigrants from [[Railen]].
- Larissa Toth begins clandestinely deconstructing Chessmen in an effort to analyze the relationship of inkaro pearls to Chessman creation, and develop a rival construct that harnesses the power of inkaro pearls to a greater magnitude.
- Using various rare arcane materials sourced from the Arctus Plateau, Toth also begins working on a transmutation ritual that might alter a standard inkaro pearl with the magic of [[Luxarum]]. She hopes to develop "Argent Pearls" to power her new constructs and mark a new era for the Silver Beam’s prosperity and influence.

## 3 Months Ago

- Toth stumbles onto the accidental discovery of a transmutation ritual dubbed “the Diagram Transcendent” while working on her formula for Argent Pearls. Chaotic and somewhat inscrutable, the Diagram Transcendent seems to have a strange effect on inkaro pearls — and Chessmen powered by these tainted [[Inkaro Pearl, Entropic]] display erratic, often dangerous behavior.
- Thanks to the magic of an Entropic Pearl, a construct in Silver Beam’s service known as “[[Chamberlain]]” is the first Chessman to gain sentience. During the confusing incident, Chamberlain escapes and flees to the Sinkhole Depths — starting off with a brief time in Rock Bottom (a ramshackle tent-and-boardwalk town at the bottom of the sinkhole beneath Arcturel) before ending up at a remote spot called [[Aberin's Folly]] a few miles to the east.
- While in Rock Bottom, Chamberlain meets a grizzled loner named Ifton Shepp — the disgraced former assistant of Vartholomew Chess, who lost destroyed his reputation in Arcturel by taking a job with Silver Beam when they first arrived. Shepp’s search for redemption compels him to aid Chamberlain before the construct seeks more remote hiding.
- Manufacture of the [[Silver Beam Servitor]] line begins in earnest.

## 2 Months Ago

- Toth recognizes the unpredictable qualities of Entropic Pearls (and their negative effects on Chessmen) as a powerful tool in her effort to destabilize House Cevher.
- Entropic Pearls are seeded throughout Arcturel by Toth and her minions in an attempt to bring them into proximity with Chessmen. Toth is eager to inspire a duplicitous narrative: that Chessmen are unreliable and dangerous to the people of Arcturel.
- Entropic Pearls are secretly installed in a variety of Chessmen throughout town by Toth’s minions, including constructs located at: the Glimmer crystal shop in Upper Arcturel, the Mine in Lower Arcturel, and Hob Korrel’s stables (among others). The “onset time” of the chaotic transmutation magic stored in the Entropic Pearls is unknown.

## 1 Month Ago

- Various anomalies are slowly reported throughout town in regards to Chessmen and their behavior. The constructs seem to be malfunctioning, while in reality they’re being affected by the chaotic magic of the Diagram Transcendent via Entropic Pearls.
- The Chessman known as “Lucent” is awakened by the Diagram Transcendent. Originally in the employ of the Glimmer crystal shop of Upper Arcturel, Lucent is stirred to action and promptly escapes without major incident. Lucent is categorized as “missing” during the early stages of the investigation.
- The Chessman known as “Rider” is awakened. Originally in the employ of Hob Korell, Rider hijacks a riding lizard/frog during an incident and slinks off into the sinkhole depths.
- Both of these constructs find their way to Rock Bottom, where they Ifton Shepp. Shepp recognizes the unique qualities of these Chessmen and directs Lucent and Rider to find Chamberlain at his camp in the East.

## 2 Weeks Ago

- The Chessman known as “Hew” is awakened by the Diagram Transcendent, at a most inconvenient time. While working one day in the House Cevher mine, Hew malfunctions. Several witnesses see Hew fight with a House Cevher miner at the edge of the Lower Arcturel catwalk, and they both go over the edge during the struggle — falling to their supposed doom at the bottom of the sinkhole.
- While authorities were able to find scant evidence of the miner’s liquified corpse, there were no apparent signs of Hew the Construct — who is presumed to have survived the fall. With this so-called automaton killer on the loose, the people of Arcturel grow increasingly nervous.

## 2 Days Ago

- Another accident happens at the [[Inkaro Pools]] resulting in the death of a work overseer and destruction of the construct. This leads to the investigation during “[[Poolside Predicaments]].” What really happened: A Chessman working the Inkaro Pools was the latest construct to malfunction thanks to sabotage via entropic pearl.

## Current Day

- The party arrives and the characters begin their investigation in Arcturel.
