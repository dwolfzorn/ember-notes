# The Signborn's Secret

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration and Combat Event invites the party to explore [[Vortest Tower]], the remote dwelling of the [[Signborn]] mage [[Corvana Vortest]], in search of her story for [[Amalthea Stonecraft]]. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Reach Vortest Tower and uncover the otherworldly secrets of the strange facility, including a method of journeying to [[Signara]] via the tower's powerful [[Daemos]] enchantments.
> - Explore Vortest Tower for the magic user Corvana Vortest, along with clues about what might have happened to her.
> - Discover a group of fiendish [[Jahud Assassin]], who've been dispatched to Ember on a mission to slay Corvana and steal the mysterious [[Orphic Reliquary]] she protects.
> - Learn about Corvana's clandestine mission to protect the Orphic Reliquary for her liege, the Daemos known as [[Nalgomith]].
>
> This Event is depicted using the [[Vortest Tower]] Area Map.

### Exploring Vortest Tower

In pursuit of the completion of Amalthea Stonecraft's Book of Tales, the party has journeyed to Vortest Tower at the southeastern edge of the Golden Flats. Here, they'll discover a rather deserted [[Tower Antechamber]], where they must search for clues and the eponymous Signborn mage: the elusive Corvana Vortest. A full description of The Vortest Tower location can be found on the [[Vortest Tower]] area walkthrough.

### The Signborn's Tale

Once the party has successfully defeated the clandestine [[Jahud Assassin]] and saved [[Corvana Vortest]] from an untimely fate, they have a moment to explain their mission to the Signborn mage. As long as the characters are straightforward in the description of their task (and the details associated with it), Corvana takes no umbrage with relating her tale as a reward for the party's heroic efforts.

Read the following aloud when the characters ask Corvana to tell her tale:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The Signborn mage regards you with a moment of curious hesitation before her eyes drift to the arcane manuscript at your side, the volume of stories you've been collecting for Amalthea Stonecraft.
>
> > Your selflessness deserves a reward. And you clearly have a greater purpose, if that tome you bear is even half as enchanted as it seems.
>
> Corvana steadies herself and stows her implements while surveying the scene around you. A foul ichor seeps from the assassin corpses onto the glassy floor.
>
> > These jahud didn't arrive here on a whim. They came in search of a powerful Signaran artifact that resides here in the confines of the tower's interdimensional pocket, something I've been charged to protect since birth.
>
> The Signborn mage produces something from the folds of her robes: an octahedron made of alloyed silver and nacreous crystal, which shimmers with otherworldly iridescence. The artifact floats in the palm of her hand as she continues.
>
> > This is the Orphic Reliquary, a sacred vestige of my liege, the Daemos Nalgomith. It contains venerated knowledge acquired through countless eons of study and experimentation. Its secrets are unfathomable, and its possibilities endless. It holds the very history of my people.
> >
> > Like all Signarans and their kin, I am descended from one of the many Daemos who rule the realm of shadow from places unseen. Nalgomith is my sovereign and liege, and it is by Nalgomith's will that House Vortest has endured, with purpose. Like my parents, and a handful of generations before them, I am sworn to protect the Orphic Reliquary from those who might corrupt its knowledge for their unsavory purposes. Since birth, I have honed my sorcery and my studies to prepare for the inevitable: a day when the minions of Kerastes would arrive to take the Reliquary by force. Indeed, the jahud are foul creations of Kerastes, the Clandestine Arbiter, and were sent here to slay me and abscond with the Reliquary.
> >
> > Fortunately, you intervened. And the Orphic Reliquary continues to elude the grasp of the ill-intended. But we must remain vigilant, friends — the minions of Kerastes are as relentless as the Daemos themself.
> >
> > My life has been quite a solitary one, until now. I hope this account of mine is sufficient for your storytelling. Perhaps now that we've made an example of these assassins, other would-be interlopers will take heed and keep Vortest Tower out of their petty schemes. Or perhaps I'll find a new place to hide the reliquary.
> >
> > Time, and the fascinations of Nalgomith, will tell.

`[[/outcome collected]]`

#### Signara Attunement: Corvana Vortest's Tale

If the party manages to successfully record Corvana's Tale in Amalthea's Manuscript, each character advances their **Attunement: Signara (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Concluding the Event

Once Corvana Vortest's tale has been written into the Stonecraft Manuscript, the characters can hear the soft music of Amalthea's driftwood wind chimes stirring on the breeze.
