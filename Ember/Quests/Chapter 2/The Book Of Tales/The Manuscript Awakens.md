# The Manuscript Awakens

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration Event occurs once the party has collected the four stories assigned by [[Amalthea Stonecraft]] and transcribed them in the [[Stonecraft Manuscript]]. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Witness a story begin to write itself upon the blank pages of the magical Stonecraft Manuscript.
> - Follow the manuscript's instructions to meet Amalthea on the shores of an island in the northwest reaches of [[Lake Jinro]], a large body of water located west of [[Nain]]. Here, a mysterious [[Lake Jinro Lunar Shrine]] awaits discovery.
>
> This Event is depicted using the Current Scene of the previous Event.

### The Self-Writing Story

After the [[Stonecraft Manuscript]] comes to life, the party can take a moment to analyze its properties and precisely what this new passage means for their Next Steps.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Scrutinizing the Manuscript's Tale
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 15)** is able to confirm that the magical display of self-writing that just occurred is intrinsic to the manuscript's enchantments, and is not the result of some outside force or illusion (aside from Amalthea Stonecraft herself, perhaps).
>
> Any character who previously noticed the writing inscribed on a statue's stone book during [[The Storyteller of Nain]] can recognize the silver handwriting that just appeared in the Stonecraft Manuscript as being the very same.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Science (DC 14)** check can recall having seen this same handwriting on various tomes and parchments in Stonecraft Manor when the party first met Amalthea in Nain.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Society (DC 13)** check is familiar with [[Lake Jinro]] and its location on the [[The Arctus Plateau]] Region Map. Otherwise, the party must gather information about the lake's precise location (see [[The Manuscript Awakens]] below).
>
> - **Knowledge: Legends**: The character automatically succeeds on this check, and knows about the general history of the [[Lake Jinro Lunar Shrine]].
> - **Culture: Arcturian**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character has heard of an ancient shrine dedicated to Ember's moons that's said to adorn an island near the northwest corner of the lake.
>
> Any character who succeeds on this History check and also makes a successful **Society (DC 15)** check can accurately deduce that the phrase "isolated shores" mentioned in this new passage of the manuscript most likely refers to one of the two islands located among the lake's northwest reaches.

With this newfound information in hand, the party must now travel to the [[Lake Jinro Lunar Shrine]] to further their Quest.

### The Journey to Lake Jinro

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Finding Lake Jinro
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Diplomacy (DC 13)** check while interacting with the populace of an [[Arctus Plateau]] settlement is able to drum up information about the precise location of [[Lake Jinro]] and the two islands that dot its central reaches.
>
> - **Culture: Arcturian**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Legends**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

Once the characters have traveled to Lake Jinro and reached the large island within it, read the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You notice a familiar amethyst-plumed figure standing on the southeastern beach of this large Lake Jinro island. As you get closer, you're greeted by the warm smile of Amalthea Stonecraft, who seems to be waiting for you on this lonely shore.

### Concluding the Event

"The Manuscript Awakens" concludes when the party arrives on the shores of the [[Lake Jinro Lunar Shrine]] to meet Amalthea.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> [[The Moon Child of Lake Jinro]] begins as soon as this event is concluded, here at the southeastern shore of the [[Lake Jinro Lunar Shrine]], where the party is poised to accompany Amalthea for her mysterious ritual.
