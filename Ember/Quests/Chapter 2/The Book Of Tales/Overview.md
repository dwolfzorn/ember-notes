# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Side Quest occurs during Chapter 2 as the party arrives in [[Nain]] to witness the legendary storytelling of [[Amalthea Stonecraft]] firsthand. There are no prerequisites for this Quest and it can be undertaken at any time, although it is certain to be encountered if traveling with [[Lyla Cevher]] while undertaking the [[An Old Friend]] Quest.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> The "Book of Tales" Quest is designed for a party of Level 3 characters, although the finale of the Quest, [[The Moon Child of Lake Jinro]], may prove difficult for a party that is not yet Level 4.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest that awards 2 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon completion of [[The Signborn's Secret]] and [[The Turn of a Friendly Page]].

The most accomplished storyteller of the [[Arctus Plateau]], an [[Aster]] bard named Amalthea Stonecraft, is nearing the end of her years and needs help finishing her latest collection of local legends. Her job for the party seems rather simple: visit four different citizens of the [[Golden Flats]] and transcribe their unique stories. But the party will discover most of these tall tales to be remarkably true, and will eventually find themselves at the center of Amalthea's grand and magical narrative.

Presented somewhat as a collection of short stories, this Quest takes the party on a tour of the Golden Flats (and beyond) to meet various persons of interest; along the way, they’ll obtain detailed glimpses into Arcturian life and culture via these esoteric NPCs and their local folklore. Appropriately, "The Book of Tales" functions a bit like a collection of Standalone Events rather than a serial narrative, culminating in an epic moment of metatextual storytelling involving the party members and a magically-transmogrified version of the quest-giver.

After handing the party a hardbound manuscript full of blank pages, Amalthea sets them off to pursue the narratives as they see fit; and once they set out from Nain, the characters must gather the four stories on Amalthea's list before uncovering a fifth and final tale. During this finale, the party travels west to discover "the Moon Child of Lake Jinro" — a mysterious creature that turns out to be none other than Amalthea Stonecraft herself, reborn in a new adolescent body through some strange metaphysical process of Ember.
