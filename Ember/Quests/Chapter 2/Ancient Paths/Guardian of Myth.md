# Guardian of Myth

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration and Combat Event occurs when [[Ankarist]] and the party visit [[Mythspire Observatory]] in pursuit of a clandestine entrance to the [[Pathways]] below [[World of Ember]]. In this Event, the characters can:
>
> - Explore the aged ruins of Mythspire Observatory.
> - Survive a dangerous encounter with the ancient [[Mythspire Guardian]] in the observatory's [[Central Room]], whether via diplomacy or combat.
> - Discover the untimely fate of a another group of adventurers who previously came this way.
> - Locate the [[Ancient Lift]] on the observatory's lower level, which leads to the subterranean Pathways.
>
> This Event is depicted using the [[Mythspire Observatory - Upper]] and [[Mythspire Observatory - Lower]] Area Maps.

### Exploring the Observatory

When the Event begins, the party arrives at the [[Mythspire Approach]] outside the exterior doors of the ruined tower, and must explore the tower until they reach the [[Ancient Lift]] in the Lower Chambers. Along the way, the characters must survive a dangerous encounter with the enigmatic [[Mythspire Guardian]] in the observatory's [[Central Room]], after which they may proceed to the [[Pathways]] below.

Once the party is finished exploring the [[Area Overview]] Area Map, be sure to record the appropriate Event Outcomes and Attunement progression moments detailed below.

#### Aura Attunement: Mythspire Discovery

If the party manages to discover Mythspire Observatory without directions from Liestra Grann, each character advances their **Attunement: Aura (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Akon Attunement: Befriend the Mythspire Guardian

If the party chose to befriend the Mythspire Guardian, each character advances their **Attunement: Akon (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Abyss Attunement: Destroy the Mythspire Guardian

If the party chose to fight and kill the Mythspire Guardian, each character advances their **Attunement: The Abyss (+2)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### The Investigator's Mission

The party can choose to accompany Ankarist on his mission into the Pathways, or they can let the Drakon investigator forge ahead on his own. Mark the appropriate Event Outcome after the characters make their decision and Ankarist has had a moment to respond.

If the party decides to join Ankarist, he says:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Ankarist appears pleased that he'll have backup going into the Pathways.
>
> > Excellent. Glad to have you along. You've been worthy allies so far, and I'm grateful you'll be at my side on this little expedition of ours. From what I understand of the Pathways, it's a rather massive region, with more dangers than one can count. Not the place for lone adventure, by any means.
> >
> > Let's get moving as soon as you're ready.

`[[/outcome ankaristJoined]]`

If the party decides to leave Ankarist, he says:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Ankarist seems a touch disappointed that he won't have backup going into the Pathways, but he doesn't let it get the better of him.
>
> > I'll be honest with you, I was hoping that I wouldn't have to go into the Pathways on my own. Should you change your mind, you know where to look for me. But good luck getting it done … From what I understand, the Pathways are as expansive as the name suggests.
> >
> > Anyhow … safe and swift travels. Until we meet again.

`[[/outcome ankaristLeft]]`

Once the party has fully explored the ruin and activated the [[Ancient Lift]] to descend into the Pathways, be sure to mark the following Event Outcome and award the corresponding Attunement progression.

`[[/outcome unlocked]]`

#### Signara Attunement: Ancient Lift Activated

If the party manages to activate the Ancient Lift to the Pathways, each character advances their **Attunement: Signara (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Concluding the Event

Once the characters journey into the lightless depths of the Pathways using the Ancient Lift, they're well on their way to the astonishing discoveries that await them.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> Completing this Event earns the party a [[Milestone Progression]] Point, potentially advancing them in Level.
>
> #### Next Steps
>
> Whether the party chooses to accompany Ankarist or not, [[A Strange New World]] begins as soon as this event is concluded, and the Scene will automatically transition to the [[Pathways]] Level of the [[The Arctus Plateau]] Region Map.
