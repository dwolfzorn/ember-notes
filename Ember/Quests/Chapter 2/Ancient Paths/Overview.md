# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This main quest occurs during Chapter 2, and begins if the party [[Leavetakings]] at the end of [[The Winding Trail]]. If the party chose not to, they can still rejoin Ankarist at a later time, in which case they start the "Ancient Paths" quest partway through, via the [[Reuniting with Ankarist]] event.
>
> Once any of the Chapter 2 main quests have been completed, it is no longer possible to access this quest (as Ankarist has independently reached Ordain without the party's assistance).
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> The "Ancient Paths" quest is designed for a party of Level 3 characters. It begins upon leaving [[Helkas]]. If the party did not thoroughly explore the Forest of Stone and its respective Chapter 1 side quests, they may still be Level 2. They can either press forward and expect to advance to Level 3 upon obtaining their next milestone point, or they could be encouraged to backtrack and explore further.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Main Quest quest that awards 3 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon completion of the [[Guardian of Myth]], [[Empty Laboratory]], and [[Grim Findings]] Events.

Ankarist and the party set out from [[Helkas]] on a quest to uncover the origins of the drakes and investigate strange occurrences in the [[Pathways Gazetteer]] that might be connected. To achieve this, Ankarist must first consult his friend and local expert, Liestra Grann, who resides in the small hamlet of [[Skybrush]]. Here, Liestra reveals the presence of the [[Mythspire Observatory]], an ancient ruin that serves as an entrance to the regions deep below the surface. However, she warns Ankarist that he's not the only one looking to find the ancient ruin, and it is well protected by an ancient guardian.

Upon reaching the Mythspire Observatory, the party must navigate its crumbling ruins and overcome a formidable guardian to gain access to the Pathways beneath Ember. Success will lead them into this strange underworld full of dangers and secrets, many of which point them toward something lurking in the darkness. The party ultimately arrives at an abandoned quarry that has been transformed into a twisted laboratory and is actively being destroyed by its creators to hide their actions. With time running out, the party must quickly collect evidence before it's all destroyed and the lab's occupants escape.

Regardless of the outcome, Ankarist must eventually return to Ordain to report to the [[Veiled Chain]] in the [[Hallows]]. Whether the party gathers ample evidence or only firsthand experiences, the result remains the same: they reveal the existence of an ominous plot, and a conspiracy that may threaten all of the plateau.
