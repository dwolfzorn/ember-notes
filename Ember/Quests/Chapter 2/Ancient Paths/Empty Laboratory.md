# Empty Laboratory

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This exploration and combat event involves the party raiding a [[Repurposed Quarry]] that is serving as the home base for an arm of [[Mutagists]]. In this event the party will need to:
>
> - Discover who the people here are, and what they goals are, uncovering the existence of the Mutagists.
> - Defeat the Mutagists and Drakes found in the location, so they don't escape to cause harm elsewhere.
> - Collect useful information from the various facilities and labs which can be used to further Ankarist's investigation.
>
> #### Area Walkthrough
>
> The party begins in the [[Entry Tunnels]], which poses a hazard through its poisonous atmosphere. In addition, though the party has no way of knowing it, the Mutagists are in the process of destroying the lab to remove evidence of their actions.

### Ankarist's Plan

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Ankarist, eyes sharp and focused, looks toward you.
>
> > Seems the chaos has masked our arrival so far, but what could have them all riled up like this? We should take advantage of this while we can. If you've got any suggestions on what we do, now's the time to speak up.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What's your plan?
>
> **A:**
>
> Ankarist grimaces slightly.
>
> > I don't really have one, to be honest. We need to collect any evidence we can get out hands on, and ideally not die in the process. I'm hoping that the chaos will help cover us, but I suspect fighting's inevitable.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What should we collect?
>
> **A:**
>
> > We need to secure anything that's useful to the investigation: documents, materials, reports, records, log books, journals, anything that we can use to learn more about this operation.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Are we sneaking in?
>
> **A:**
>
> > We can try, but given how chaotic things sound, I don't know who long we'll be able to keep it up. Still, getting the jump on the people inside will give us better odds. However, be ready to start moving fast if things fall apart completely.

> [!info] Social
> #### Chaotic Approach
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Warfare**, **Path: Anchorite Marine**, **Path: Flameguard Militia**, or **Path: Imperial Conscript** know that the chaos ahead will mask their approach initially, and could let them get the jump on the first few encounters if they are careful about their approach. However, things could devolve quickly, leaving people out of position and exposed if they aren't careful to stick together.
>
> #### Dangerous Environment
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Alchemy** or **Path: Crown Apothecary** recognize the scent of dangerous chemicals in the air, and suspect deadly hazards could be in play. Covering one's face would help avoid breathing in deadly fumes. It is likely they have such protective equipment inside the party can acquire on the go.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Are we taking prisoners?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Generally speaking I try not to kill people unless I absolutely have to, so, yes if we can. However, I recognize we're not really set up to take many prisoners, I have two pair of manacles on me, so I can use those. I'm sure we can scavenge some rope in there, if we need.

### Entering The Lab

Refer to the Area Walkthrough for the [[Repurposed Quarry]] which contains this event's scene game play. After completing the area, return here.

### Kalasak's Fate

With the lab cleared of hostiles, mark the outcome based on the fate of Kalasak the Cutter:

`[[/outcome killed]]`

#### Ragen Attunement: Kalasak Killed

If they party manages to slay Kalasak in combat, each character advances their **Attunement: Ragen (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

`[[/outcome captured]]`

#### Cora Attunement: Kalasak Captured

If they party manages to capture Kalasak in combat, each character advances their **Attunement: Cora (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

`[[/outcome escaped]]`

#### Abyss Attunement: Kalasak Escapes

If Kalasak escapes the party's clutches, each character advances their **Attunement: The Abyss (+1)** at the conclusion of the event.

### Concluding the Event

With the lab scoured, its occupants defeated, captured, or routed, there is little more to do than wrap up, and head out.

> [!info] Social
> #### Ankarist's Next Step
>
> Ankarist is ready to return to Ordain to report to his superiors. What they've learned here in the lab complicates things substantially, but also gives them leads to follow. Before they can do that though, they need to find a way out of the Pathways, so that is his next plan of action.

In the interest of expediency, if the party has not already concluded that there must be a way back to the surface near the lab, Ankarist can point out:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> > We came a long way to get here, and by a route they occupants of this lab hadn't opened yet. That's a long way to bring supplies, and I didn't see any evidence of heavy traffic like we'd see from caravans or supply trains.
> >
> > Given how well supplied this place was, there has to be a way to the surface nearby. Rather than retracing our steps out of here, let's see if we can find the entrance to the pathways the locals were using.

All that is left is for the party to return to the surface, and a convenient exit from the pathways exists near the quarry lab.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> Completing this event (by shutting down the lab) awards a [[Milestone Progression]] point, potentially enabling the party to advance in level.
>
> #### Next Steps
>
> The party can return to the surface and continue the quest in the subsequent [[Emergence]] event.
>
> If, for some reason, the party insists on returning to Mythspire Observatory's base to travel to the surface instead, they risk missing the next event. Ankarist would advocate strongly for pressing on and heading directly to Ordain. If the party does backtrack, they may travel to Ordain via some other route, moving directly to [[Grim Findings]].
