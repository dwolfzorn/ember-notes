# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> There are no specific requirements to begin this Side Quest, and it is available to the party at the start of Chapter 1. Most groups will likely experience the full range of content after the party reaches [[Helkas]].
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> "Signal of Intent" is designed for a party of Level 2 characters. Its preliminary Events may be encountered by a Level 1 party, but the main content of this Side Quest will naturally occur after the characters have advanced to Level 2.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest that awards 1 [[Milestone Progression]] upon successful completion of the [[Traversing the Tower]] Event.

Long ago, two disparate cultures overlapped within this section of the Forest of Stone. The [[Shent]] were the predominant group, living and mining the area. The Shent were monitored and surveilled by the expansive and powerful [[Aedir]] Empire from a hidden Signalpost in the cliffs above. In the present day, while neither culture is currently active on Ember, evidence of their history and lives can be found throughout the region.

The Forest of Stone was once an outlying boundary of both the Aedir Empire and Shent civilization. People from both cultures cohabitated the area, forming an uneasy acceptance of each other. The Aedir were wary that the Shent could become enemies opposing their imperial expansion. The Shent were wary that the Aedir would attempt to conquer their peaceful and philosophical mountain realm.

An unlikely friendship emerged between a Shent student and an Aedir researcher, their mutual passion for inquiry and discovery breaking through the frosty barrier between the peoples. A preserved historical record of these ancient scholars holds the key to modern-day explorers being able to uncover and breach the halls of the [[Aedir Signalpost]], a military research and observation tower standing atop bluffs high above the Forest of Stone.

The party will follow this trail of clues, explore the sealed outpost, and discover how materials from its Underforge have escaped containment to threaten the safety of the town of [[Yakoshta]] in the ravine below.
