# Traversing the Tower

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> In this Explorationand Combat Event, the party enters and investigates the [[Aedir Signalpost]], for any number of possible reasons:
>
> - To investigate the rumors shared by Lorettia Ben-Wren in [[Helkas]].
> - To satisfy their own adventurous curiosity.
> - To find the source of an alchemical contamination seeping into [[Yakoshta Mine]], if the party has already begun the [[Ooze Control]] Side Quest and undertaken the investigation requested in [[A Miner Update]].
>
> While exploring the tower, the characters can:
>
> - Repair the central elevator that affords access to all four levels of the tower.
> - Learn more about [[Aedir]] ambitions and research during the reign of the Aedir Empire.
> - Discover the source of the alchemical contamination leaking out of the tower's Underforge, where Aedir constructs were manufactured.
> - Decide whether to cleanse that contamination or, if the characters are undertaking the [[Ooze Control]] Side Quest and have agreed to work with [[Jasper]] in [[A Miner Update]], collect samples for Jasper in [[Yakoshta]].
> - Defend themselves against the tower's remaining guardian, a [[Broken Aedir Sentinel]].
> - Choose whether to restore full power to the tower, preserving its invisibility field, or allow the tower's power source to fully wane, making it permanently visible.
>
> #### Area Walkthrough
>
> The party begins in the [[Aedir Signalpost]] Scene, where the central gameplay of this Event transpires. A complete description of the tower and the gameplay that occurs there is detailed in the [[Aedir Signalpost]] Area Walkthrough.

### Exploring the Tower

The Aedir Signalpost is the source of a leak of contaminated fluid that is poisoning the oozes in Yakoshta Mine below. The party has been asked to find and stop the leak. Once they are inside the Signalpost, the characters' objectives are generally the following:

1. Repair the [[Rotating Elevator]] or locate the hidden [[The Cliff Face]] passage in order to access the lower levels.
2. Defeat the Broken Aedir Sentinel that guards the [[Underforge]] and locate the source of contamination from within the tower. The contaminant is located in the [[Underforge]].
3. Cleanse, collect, or ignore the alchemical contamination, affecting downstream repercussions in the [[Ooze Control]] Quest.

### Restoring Power

While in the tower, the party must make a decision about what to do with the contaminant found in the Underforge and whether to restore and activate the tower's stealth capabilities — both are tracked via the outcomes in this Event.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Power to the Tower
>
> The party may choose to restore power to the tower by repowering and reactivating the generator in the [[Generator Room]]; to accomplish this task, they must recharge its power cells using the [[Underforge]] in the Underforge. However, this can only be done if the alchemical fluid has not yet been cleansed!

#### Luxarum Attunement: Restore Power

If the party manages to restore power to the tower, each character advances their **Attunement: Luxarum (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Resolving the Contamination

Once the construct in the Underforge of the tower has been defeated, the party must take some actions to determine the final fate of the tower and the alchemical fluid that seeps downward from it into the rock above Yakoshta Mine.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Cleansing the Contaminant
>
> The party may choose to decontaminate the [[Aedir Construct Fluid]] in the tower using the [[Decontamination Station]] in the Underforge, which is filled with a mixture of [[Alchemical Silver]] and water. When this mixture is further combined with the [[Aedir Construct Fluid]], the fluid loses all of its toxic properties and becomes harmless.
>
> If the party has completed this task, any character who makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 14)**check can confirm that the liquid remaining in the vats and on the floor of the Underforge has been rendered inert and no longer has any meaningful alchemical properties.
>
> - **Knowledge: Alchemy**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
>
> #### Claiming the Contaminant
>
> Alternatively, the party may use sample containers found in the Decontamination Station's supply cabinet to collect [[Aedir Construct Fluid]], either to give to Jasper during the [[Ooze Control]] Side Quest, or for the party's own use.

Depending on what choice the party makes with regard to the source of contamination, one of their attunements may advance:

#### Cora Attunement: Contaminant Cleansed

If the party chooses to cleanse or claim the contaminant, each character advances their **Attunement: Cora (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Luxarum Attunement: Contaminant Sampled

If the party collects samples of the contaminant for the purposes of further experimentation, each member advances their **Attunement: Luxarum (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

#### Abyss Attunement: Contaminant Ignored

If the party leaves the contaminant where they found it, each character advances their **Attunement: The Abyss (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.
