# Ooze Foes

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration Event occurs when the party returns to [[Yakoshta]] after siding with [[Jasper]] or ignoring the town's plight altogether. By exploring the ruined town, the party can:
>
> - Discover what happened to the Yakoshta settlement.
> - Find clues to the fate of [[Tauric]] and Squish.
>
> This Event will only trigger if at least 60 days pass after the party sided with Jasper during the [[Alchemical Decisions]] Event, or if the party does not intervene at all during the [[A Miner Update]] Event.
>
> This Event is depicted using the [[Vista: Yakoshta]] Vista.

### The Abandoned Settlement

Characters can search through the rubble of Yakoshta, looking for any clues as to what happened here. Broken furniture, dishes, and other implements are scattered across the ground.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### A Metallic Mess
>
> Bits of metallic substance can be found throughout Yakoshta on the ground and on the sides of buildings. Any character who makes a successful **Science (DC 12)** check while examining this substance confirms that it's identical to the substance left behind by the metallic oozes the party has previously faced in combat.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 12)** check while examining the trail of destruction can determine that the oozes came out of the mine and through the center of town.
>
> There is not enough metal in any of the piles to sell or gather.

There is no sign of survivors, but further inspecting the town for clues can turn up more evidence of what happened here:

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Squished
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check while investigating the town spots a drawing of an ooze that looks very similar to Squish, crumpled beneath a dull, slate-colored stone — the stone is stained a dark red. Appended to the drawing is a note:
>
> > They say the oozes are under control, that we don't need to leave this place, but I'm not sure. Sometimes I think I —
>
> The rest of the words have been torn away.

### The Overrun Mine

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### An Abundance of Oozes
>
> If the party is determined (or foolish enough) to enter [[Yakoshta Mine]], they find it entirely overrun with oozes, specifically:
>
> - [[Luminous Copper Ooze]]
> - [[Luminous Iron Ooze]]
> - [[Luminous Silver Ooze]]
> - [[Luminous Gold Ooze]]
> - [[Giant Luminous Ooze]]
>
> It is left to the Gamemaster to determine the number and location of these oozes. No sign of survivors exists within, nor are there any further clues about what might have happened.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> There are no further steps in this Side Quest — Yakoshta is lost and can no longer be visited as anything more than a memorial to the past.
