# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Side Quest can be undertaken by any party that explores the southern section of the Forest of Stone. This might happen during Chapter 1, but the Events of this Quest can also be experienced later in the game.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> "Ooze Control" is designed for a party of Level 1 characters.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest that awards 1 [[Milestone Progression]] upon successful completion of the [[Saving Jasper]] Event.

The Side Quest begins when the party encounters [[Tauric]], an enthusiastic local with a tame [[Oozeling]] and a strong affection for the creatures. Through Tauric, the party can learn more about the region before reaching [[Yakoshta]], where [[Sellen]] hires them to rescue trapped miners and investigate the crisis. Exploration of [[Ooze Island]], interaction with the people of Yakoshta, and the rescue mission within [[Yakoshta Mine]] gradually reveal that alchemical runoff leaking from the nearby [[Aedir Signalpost]] is driving the oozes into their altered state.

By the Quest's end, the party should understand that Yakoshta's problem is not simply monsters in a mine, but a collision between profit and ecological dependence. This conflict between Tauric's preservationist instincts and [[Jasper]] productivity-minded ambitions gives the Gamemaster room to emphasize local political and future consequences. If the party resolves the immediate danger, Yakoshta survives, but its long-term direction remains uncertain until they investigate the Aedir Signalpost and return with a sample of the alchemical contaminant.
