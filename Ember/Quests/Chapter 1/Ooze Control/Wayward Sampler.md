# Wayward Sampler

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Social Event occurs only if the [[Good Ooze, Bad Ooze]] Event did not happen; the party meets [[Tauric]], a young [[Hulg'run]]. By speaking with Tauric, the party can:
>
> - Learn about recent troubles in [[Yakoshta Mine]] and the surrounding region.
> - Learn about the local ooze ecology and how they have been recently changing.
> - Choose to follow Tauric back to the [[Yakoshta]] settlement.
>
> This Event is depicted using the "Cliffside Clearing" Level of the [[Vista: Dripstones]] Vista.

### Talking with Tauric

> [!abstract] Tauric
> **[[Tauric]]**
>
> Level 1 · Hulg'run Scout
>
> ![Tauric](../../../assets/image102.webp)
>
> The young man's gray body appears to be carved from rock, with lines of blue agate running through the stone like veins. His color is matched by the gelatinous body of the small blueish-green ooze that sits on his shoulder, nestled into a hollow that seems to have been carved for the purpose.

The Hulg'run excitedly introduces himself and his pet oozeling, [[Oozeling]], then asks the party for their help getting home. He's seen enemies in the area and is worried about safe passage. Tauric is happy to talk about anything and everything he knows as long as the party is there to keep him and Squish safe. He often includes Squish in the conversation as though the two speak a common language.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As Tauric speaks, the ooze changes position, but it is always somewhere on his body, whether draped around the neck like a scarf, wrapped around the arm like a long bracelet, or shifting from shape to shape in its shoulder perch.

> [!info] Social
> #### Talking with Tauric
>
> Tauric introduces himself, explaining:
>
> > I'm from Yakoshta, home of the famous Yakoshta Mine — one of the best up-and-coming mining operations in or around the Arctus Plateau. At least, that's what Sellen says. And since she's in charge, what she says goes.
>
> - Any character who makes a successful **Society (DC 13, Passive)** or **Deception (DC 13, Passive)** check realizes that Yakoshta and its eponymous mine are, in fact, not famous.
> - If asked, Tauric explains that [[Sellen]] is his older sister and the leader of the Yakoshta settlement and mining operation.
>
> Tauric requests the party to escort him to Yakoshta. If they do, Tauric will ensure the party is given a modest reward. If asked what he's doing wandering around outside of town, he'll explain.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> > I'm really more of a wanderer than a miner. I think that exploring the Forest of Stone is far more fun than being holed up in a cave. Besides, Squish likes to get out in the sun! Recently, though … well, I've been trying to figure out a bit of a mystery.

Tauric continues to explain in more detail if asked by the party.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What mystery?
>
> **A:**
>
> > For a long time, the local oozes were very relaxed and sometimes even downright friendly, like Squish! However, lately, local oozes have gone from friend to enemy in no time at all, and nobody is sure why.
> >
> > In Yakoshta, oozes are little buddies — they help in the mines and are more like pets than monsters. At least, that's how it used to be until these new glowing gold and silver and copper oozes started attacking.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Aggressive oozes?
>
> **A:**
>
> > They appeared a few weeks ago out of nowhere, driving the regular oozes away and attacking everyone they run into. They're bigger than the normal oozes and glow, and there's absolutely no way to reason with or tame them. I've tried feeding them, talking to them, singing to them, and it doesn't matter. They just want to fight.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Can we help?
>
> **A:**
>
> Tauric looks surprised for a moment as if the thought had not occurred to him, and then he beams at you with excitement.
>
> > Absolutely! I know that Sellen would want to meet you — she knows more about all this, and she would definitely want extra help solving this mystery. The mine's been having trouble running consistently because of the attacks, and that's only going to cause more problems for us down the line.
>
> The Hulg'run looks at you hopefully.
>
> > Would you come with me? It's several miles from here, but we'd be ever so appreciative!

> [!info] Social
> #### Invitation to Yakoshta
>
> For a more detailed conversation with Tauric, refer to the [[Good Ooze, Bad Ooze]] section of the [[Good Ooze, Bad Ooze]] Event. The answers he provides there are the same set of information he would share here.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has finished speaking with Tauric, they may move on; Tauric is happy to travel with the party to one of two places: Yakoshta (where Tauric lives), or Ooze Island.
>
> - If the party heads to Yakoshta directly, proceed to [[A Miner Matter]].
> - If the party heads to Ooze Island first, proceed to [[Stone Life]].
> - If the party chooses to go elsewhere to explore the Forest of Stone, Tauric decides to make his own way back — when the party reaches Yakoshta, he will be there.
