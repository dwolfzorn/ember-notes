# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This is the first Main Quest in *Ember* and serves as both a prologue and tutorial for the rest of the game.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> "The Winding Trail" is designed for a party of Level 1 characters, who will advance to Level 2 by the time the Quest is completed.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Main Quest that awards 3 [[Milestone Progression]]: 1 Milestone Point each upon completion of the Events [[The Collapsed Cairn]], [[Arrival at Helkas]], and [[Leavetakings]].
>
> #### Tutorial Experience
>
> Some of the Events at the beginning of this Quest are scripted and have been designed to incrementally teach players (and the Gamemaster) about *Ember's* various game systems. Early on, the scope of choices that players can make is somewhat limited, but this scope will gradually broaden until the players have full control over their open-world exploration.
>
> At the beginning of the game, the party is not allowed to control the location of the Strayhearth Caravan on the Region Map. Instead, the first three Events of this Quest follow a pre-established route until the party is given autonomy to begin exploration on their own.

The [[Arctus Plateau]] region has been a peaceful, rich, and welcoming land for hundreds of years, and large-scale conflicts or especially dangerous monsters have been rarely encountered. Recently, however, the region has begun experiencing upheavals and changes that have upset the status quo, and though life continues, a subtle unease and fear has begun taking root among communities and travelers. As the caravans that follow a long, winding, triangular route from the three major cities of Casla-Brava, Toraga, and Ordain continue their paths as they have always done, they have begun hiring capable adventurers and guards to protect them, just in case.

While the party is not yet aware of it, these caravans have every right to be worried as the grand machinations of the distant megalomaniac dragon Zerranyss advance and his decades-long plot to conquer the region and become its undisputed ruler unfolds. His followers and minions, some of whom are aware of deeper plans and some who are merely pawns, have become bolder and more eager to cause chaos and disruption.

A small group of the [[Otherhood of Fortune]] plots to attack the Dusktide Festival taking place in [[Helkas]], keen to steal the valuable goods and resources gathered for the event and use them to further their ends. Accompanied by sickly draconic experiments from one of Zerranyss's laboratories, the town is in great danger, and the clock has begun ticking.

Should the party become waylaid as they resolve or explore the side passages to the north and south of the main trail, there could be dire consequences for the town and a sign of the chaos that will inevitably unfold as the story continues.
