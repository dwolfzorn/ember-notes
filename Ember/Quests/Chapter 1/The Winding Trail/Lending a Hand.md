# Lending a Hand

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration Event occurs when the party returns to the Strayhearth Caravan after having completed one of the two Side Quests in the region. Upon returning, the party can:
>
> - Help clear the remaining rubble and rejoin the caravan.
> - Be rewarded by Agraband for their assistance.

While the party was away, Agraband and the rest of the Strayhearth Caravan spent their time methodically clearing the passage of rubble. They made good progress, but as predicted, the work was slow and difficult. Now that the party has returned, they can work together to clear the remainder of the blockage.

### The Path Reopens

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Blasting Free
>
> If the party has secured a [[Barrel of Blast Powder]] (either from Sellen in the [[A Miner Update]] Event or from the depths of [[Yakoshta Mine]]), then these explosives can be used to rapidly clear the remaining blockage. Advance time by **1 hour** and then proceed.
>
> #### Fulgurite Assistance
>
> If the party secured the assistance of the [[Fulgurite Blades]] during the [[Lunar Awakenings]] Event, they are present on the far side of the blockage where they have been assisting the caravan to clear the rubble. Between Sajor's potent spells and Rorhim's powerful strength, most of the remaining work has already been completed, allowing the party to rapidly clear the remaining rubble from this side. Advance time by **1 hour** and then proceed.
>
> #### Manual Labor
>
> In the absence of blast flasks or other methods of easily clearing the rubble, the required work involves several hours of hard labor, but no checks or challenges await the party. Instead, the work progresses rapidly and without any issues. Advance time by **4 hours** and then proceed.

### A Caravan Reunited

Regardless of which method was used to clear the remaining rubble, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you clear the last of the rubble and make contact with the work crew, there's a cry of excitement. The gap through the rubble opens enough to let in light and air; Agraband's face appears in the gap, covered in dust and sweat, but bearing a happy smile.
>
> > Good to see you all again!
>
> He turns back to yell their success to the rest of the workers, and a cheer echoes back. Before long, the path is widened enough for the wagons of the caravan to trundle through, and the remaining work of navigating them through the newly excavated pass proceeds with high spirits.
>
> Before long, you and the Strayhearth Caravan have embarked again on the winding trail toward Helkas — and the Dusktide Festival.

Mark the following outcome as complete to trigger a transition of the Region Map and remove the debris blocking the caravan's path.

`[[/outcome cleared]]`

> [!info] Social
> #### Payment as Promised
>
> If the party reminds Agraband about his promise of payment in exchange for assistance, he promptly offers the party  **10** as promised.
>
> If the party does not ask, Agraband approaches the party to provide payment when they next take an opportunity to rest.

If the Fulgurite Blades were involved in helping to clear the rubble, they bid the party farewell; they are returning to the Dripstones to engage in further exploration.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Sajor nods toward you curtly and says:
>
> > Well, that's our end of it. It's certainly been interesting. You've turned out to be more capable than I initially expected… but only just.
> >
> > Perhaps we will cross paths again if you make your way to Ordain. With a few more discoveries like these —
>
> She taps the satchel at her side containing the Shent Planisphere.
>
> > — the Fulgurite Blades will be the talk of the city!

### Concluding the Event

Depending on when the blockage is cleared, the caravan will take time to rest before pushing on toward their destination of Helkas.

**If it is 12:00am on Day 5 or earlier:**

Agraband is relieved and reminds the party that there is still plenty of time to reach Helkas for the festival. He proposes taking a **Rest** to recover from the strenuous work clearing the rubble and to get moving early the following morning.

**If it is later than 12:00am on Day 5:**

Agraband insists that there is no time to spare, and they must press on toward Helkas or risk missing the festival entirely. The caravanner spurs the caravan into action and they proceed onward to Helkas without resting.
