# Arrival at Helkas

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration Event occurs when the party emerges from the winding corridors of the Forest of Stone and sees their destination in the distance, the town of [[Helkas]]. In this Event, the party can:
>
> - Determine whether they arrived in time for the Dusktide Festival.
> - Learn about the key architectural and historical features of Helkas.
>
> This Event is depicted using the "Ravine Corridor" Level of the [[Vista: Verdant Paths]] Vista.
>
> #### Matters of Punctuality
>
> When this Event begins, you should mark in the **Event Outcomes** section whether the party arrived early, on time, or late; if the party reached Helkas alone, mark that as well. The recorded outcome (or outcomes, if the party arrived alone) determines which subsequent Event occurs when the party reaches the town proper.
>
> - MarkEarly Arrival if arriving before 4:00pm on Day 5.
> - MarkTimely Arrivalif arriving at or after 4:00pm on Day 5 but before 12:00am on Day 6.
> - Mark Late Arrival if the party arrives later than 12:00am on Day 6.
> - Mark Arrived Alone if the party arrives without the Strayhearth Caravan.

### Early or Timely Arrival

If the party has managed an Early or Timely Arrival, read or paraphrase the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you draw closer, you can see that the Dusktide Festival is already in full swing: the town is dressed in banners and streamers, a riot of color that well accompanies the happy bustle of festivalgoers. The air is rich with the scent of wood fire and roasting foods. The energy in the caravan begins to shift toward excitement, eager anticipation washing away the weariness of the road's travel.

Agraband is happy to answer some questions about the festival or the town of Helkas that the party may have.

> [!abstract] Agraband Swift
> **[[Agraband Swift]]**
>
> Level 3 · Human Caravaneer
>
> ![Agraband Swift](../../../assets/image095.webp)
>
> A lute is slung across the back of this aged human clad in an odd yet practical assortment of clothing and jewelry. The seasoned bard wears the salt-and-pepper locks of his long hair pulled back into a high half ponytail. His wide smile and calm demeanor lend him an air of experienced confidence, and a certain glint in his eye evokes the mirthful spirit of a beloved uncle. It's clear this man could spin a worthy tale at a moment’s notice.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About Helkas?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Well, Helkas is the last stop for anyone heading into the Forest of Stone, and the first for anyone leaving it. It's a combination mining town and caravan hub. The folks there are decent, hard-working, and just the right amount of weird.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Mining operations?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Ah! Well, that'd be the great, big skeleton of the leviathan the town is built in and around. Miners are carving out marrow from its bones. Bit gruesome, but valuable from what I understand.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** The Dusktide Festival?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Oh, I try to be here for it every cycle, if I can. There's games, gambling, music, and more. I've been to a lot of places, and attended plenty of Dusktide festivals, but not many places celebrate it as well. Dusktide is Helkas at its best, truly.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What Next?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Well, I'm going to spend a few days in Helkas, enjoy the festival, make some trades, get supplies, and see what goods are going where. After that? We'll be moving on to Ordain. You all should take some time to rest and relax, it's been a long road. Enjoy the festival! And if you want a ride to Ordain, you know who to ask!

The other NPCs in the caravan may also have opinions.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Ankarist about Helkas?
>
> **A:**
>
> Ankarist seems to have already collected his thoughts into a specific checklist.
>
> > First, I've got a friend in town I want to drop in on. Then, I'm going to have myself a bath and get all my clothing cleaned. After that? Ordain.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Lyla about Helkas?
>
> **A:**
>
> Lyla looks tired, but also relieved.
>
> > I am glad we're close. I've still have to get to Ordain, but it'll be nice to take a day or two to unwind after the Forest of Stone.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Sin about Helkas?
>
> **A:**
>
> Sin is ebullient and obviously giddy to be arriving.
>
> > I've never been to a Dusktide festival! Oh, this is exciting… I wonder if there are any Sanguinaries in attendance!

Clipper will want the loaned magic item back, if the party hasn't already returned it.

> [!info] Social
> #### The Merchant Messenger
>
> [[Clipper]] will approach the party and request that they return the [[Messenger Coin]] which was provided during their traversal of the Forest of Stone.
>
> > I'd like my messenger coin back now, since we're about to arrive in town. Don't want to lose track of it and have to hunt you down or something.
>
> They laugh heartily.
>
> > I'm kidding! I wouldn't hunt you down… I'd hire someone to do it for me. But seriously, magic coin back — please and thank you!
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 18)** check can convince Clipper of the merits of letting the party hold on to it.

Achieving an Early Arrival reinforces the Ragen Attunement for each character in the party.

#### Ragen Attunement: Early Arrival

If the party has an Early Arrival to Helkas, each character in the party advances their **Attunement: Ragen (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

Achieving a Timely Arrival reinforces the Akon Attunement for each character in the party.

#### Akon Attunement: Timely Arrival

If the party has a Timely Arrival to Helkas, each character in the party advances their **Attunement: Akon (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Late Arrival

If the party comes in with a **Late Arrival**, continue the description with this text:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you draw closer, you begin to realize something is amiss.
>
> Helkas is burning.
>
> The town is draped in smoke flowing from numerous large fires. The bright colors of banners and streamers meant to decorate the town are only barely visible through the haze. The air is thick with the acrid stench of burning wood and cloth, and you can see people running back and forth, some carrying buckets or helping others out of harm's way.
>
> The energy in the caravan sours instantly as fear and anxiety takes hold, wiping away the giddy anticipation there just moments ago.

The caravan members all have opinions on what they are seeing, and what they intend to do, but none are willing to delay the caravan getting to Helkas. They'll talk as the caravan rushes toward the town.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Agraband on Helkas?
>
> **A:**
>
> Agraband intends to rush the caravan into town.
>
> > Clearly, something horrible has happened. Likely the people there will need assistance — and supplies! The Strayhearth can offer both, and so help me, we will!

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Ankarist on Helkas?
>
> **A:**
>
> Ankarist's professional demeanor sets in, and he wants answers.
>
> > First thing's first, we need to find out what happened. Was this an attack? And if so, perpetrated by whom? We follow the facts and go from there.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Lyla on Helkas?
>
> **A:**
>
> Lyla looks troubled by this, but unsurprised.
>
> > I'd heard that there were sporadic attacks and raids happening in the area. I hope this isn't another one of them. We need to get in there, help people, and get answers if we can.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Sin on Helkas?
>
> **A:**
>
> Sin is concerned for the people, and wants to help.
>
> > We've got to help get those fires out and tend to the wounded. Every second matters here, so best not to waste any time.

Clipper will want the loaned magic item back, just to be safe, if the party hasn't already returned it.

> [!info] Social
> #### The Merchant Messenger
>
> [[Clipper]] requests that the party return the [[Messenger Coin]] which was provided during their traversal of the Forest of Stone.
>
> > Not sure what's waiting for us in Helkas, but I'd like that merchant messenger coin back, just in case I need it.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 18)** check can convince Clipper of the merits of letting the party hold on to it.

Experiencing a Late Arrival reinforces the Aura Attunement for each character in the party.

#### Aura Attunement: Late Arrival

If the party has a Late Arrival to Helkas, each character in the party advances their **Attunement: Aura (+1)** at the conclusion of the Event.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party's arrival time has been recorded, they may proceed into the town itself. The Event which occurs depends on the timeliness of their arrival:
>
> - If the party arrives early or on time, they experience the [[Dusktide Revelry]] Event.
> - If the party arrives late, they experience the [[Dusktide Destruction]] Event.
> - If the party journeys to Helkas independently and arrives without Agraband Swift and the Strayhearth Caravan, be sure to mark the "Arrived Alone" outcome as well.
>
> #### Milestone: Arrival at Helkas
>
> Completing this Event awards a [[Milestone Progression]]. If the party did not complete one of the other Chapter 1 Side Quests, this point is likely to advance them to Level 2.
