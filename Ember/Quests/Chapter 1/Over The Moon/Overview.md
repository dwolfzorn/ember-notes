# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Requirements
>
> This Side Quest can be undertaken by any party that explores the northern section of the Forest of Stone. This might happen during Chapter 1, but the Events of this Quest can also be experienced later in the game.
>
> #### Difficulty
>
> "Over the Moon" is designed for a party of Level 1 characters.
>
> #### Milestone Progression
>
> This is a Side Quest that awards 1 [[Milestone Progression]] upon successful completion of the [[Lunar Awakenings]] Event.

In the northern reaches of the [[Forest of Stone]] stands the [[Ancient Door]], a colossal sealed passage that has remained closed for longer than anyone can remember. Most regard it as little more than an obscure ruin, but that changes when the party encounters the [[Fulgurite Blades]], an adventuring team aligned with the [[Anachraenum]] of [[Ordain]]. The Fulgurite Blades are trying to open the gate and uncover what lies beyond, drawing the party into a newly accessible stretch of the [[Dripstones]] sealed away since the fall of the [[Shent]]. Beyond the door, the party moves through dangerous wilds, uncovering traces of [[The Shattering]] and the final days of the civilization that once held this region.

As the Side Quest unfolds, the search for lost history gives way to something far more urgent. At a [[Shent Moon Temple]], the party confronts hostile elemental forces and meets [[Mioroth]], a lingering projection of a Shent seer, who reveals that one of the surviving horrors of the Shattering still lies imprisoned nearby. Should the party choose to confront this lingering adversary in the [[Writhing Grave]], they'll have to choose whether to finish what the Shent started or give in to its enticing promises of power.
