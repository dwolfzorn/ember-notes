# Untouched Wilds

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Social Event occurs as the party passes through the [[Ancient Door]]. By entering this unexplored region, the characters can:
>
> - Meet the [[Fulgurite Blades]], if they did not already meet them in [[Lightning in a Bottle]].
> - Speak with the Fulgurite Blades and their leader, [[Sajor Velex]], for guidance.
>
> This Event is depicted using the "Waterfall Paths" Level of the [[Vista: Dripstones]] Vista.

### Fulgurite Blades Unmet

If the party did not previously encounter the Fulgurite Blades, read the following text aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A group of adventurers comes jogging up behind you, clearly drawn by the sight and sound of the great doors opening. The figure at their head, a Nir'ae woman, bears a mixture of excitement and annoyance, her eyes flipping between you, the great doors, and the land beyond.
>
> > I knew we shouldn't have waited to open the door.
>
> She looks to her cohort of adventurers and waves them forward.
>
> > Let's get moving. There's a lot of ground to cover, and we're not on our timetable anymore. On the double, Blades, this region won't explore itself!
>
> As they move forward, excitedly chattering with each other about what things they might find, and what goals they might have, a Kivahr woman wearing the symbols of the god Sigil speaks up:
>
> > Not to ruin anyone's fun, but we do need to catalog our findings before we carry on.
>
> This statement earns the Kivahr a couple annoyed groans from a Thornling rogue and Cor'ak fighter who clearly want nothing more than to go off exploring. The Nir'ae woman watches them in silence, not interfering until the pair look her way. Only then does she nod in agreement with the priestess and gestures for them to go help.
>
> Eventually she turns her attention back to you.
>
> > We haven't met yet. I'm Sajor, and these are my Fulgurite Blades.

> [!info] Social
> #### Meeting the Blades
>
> If this is the first time the party has met the Fulgurite Blades, refer to [[Unknown]] for the team's descriptions and some introductory conversation topics which can be explored.

### Fulgurite Blades Met

If the party previously met the Fulgurite Blades, they rejoin the party on the far side of the now-open passageway.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The Fulgurite Blades come jogging up behind you, clearly drawn by the sight and sound of the great doors opening. Sajor leads them, beaming a broad, impressed grin at you, the great doors, and the land beyond.
>
> > Impressive! I was wondering how long it'd take you to figure it out.
>
> She looks to her cohort of adventurers and waves them forward.
>
> > Let's get moving. There's a lot of ground to cover, and we can't let our new friends here find all the interesting things!
>
> As they move forward, excitedly chattering with each other about what things they might find, and what goals they might have, Kazra speaks up, cutting through the din:
>
> > Not to ruin anyone's fun, but we do need to catalog our findings before we carry on.
>
> This statement earns the priestess a groan from Leeph and Rorhim, who clearly want to go explore, but Sajor just watches them in silence, not interfering at first. Only when they look at her does she nod, silently agreeing with Kazra. Defeated, the rogue and fighter comply, and begin helping Kazra.

### Conferring with the Blades

This conversation occurs just inside the [[Dripstones]] Biome, and allows the party to get guidance and direction from the Fulgurite Blades and a sense of what they want to do.

> [!info] Social
> #### What Do They Know?
>
> Sajor and her band can only guess at what things might be in the area. It is an untouched region that's been closed off for longer than anyone has been alive. There could be all kinds of creatures and dangers lurking here, perhaps things nobody has ever seen. They are excited to see what they find and what valuables they can claim.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Knowledge about this area?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Shent have ruins all over, so there's a good chance of finding old ruins and sites here that have lain undisturbed for millennia!

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Your research?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Never you fear, we'll take extensive notes, but that's secondary to the process of actually finding something noteworthy!

> [!info] Social
> #### On Academic Integrity
>
> The Fulgurite Blades are more akin to treasure-hunting adventurers than dedicated historians, and that trait becomes much more obvious if the party asks about the attribution of discoveries, cataloging finds, and the like.
>
> Any character with **Deception (DC 15, Passive)** suspects that Sajor cares more about the treasure-hunting — or perhaps "fame-hunting" — aspect of her profession than she does about pure academic pursuits.
>
> Rorhim and Leeph don't seem to be any different. Kazra, however, seems to take their role as explorers and historians far, far more seriously. If anyone can be relied upon to carry out proper logging and organization of finds, it will be her.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Passage to Helkas?
>
> **A:**
>
> > This area could possibly extend to some southern route which might present a passage to Helkas or an area near it. There is no guarantee of such a passage though, otherwise we would have taken it ourselves.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Where are you headed?
>
> **A:**
>
> > We're going to start by heading north, towards the mountain ranges where the Shent were known to live. We plan to follow the canyon walls, looking for caves and ruins.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Traveling together?
>
> **A:**
>
> Sajor shakes her head at the topic.
>
> > Nope. You go your way, we go ours. We don't need extra people slowing us down, getting in our way, or interfering with our discoveries.
>
> After a pause she expands this thought:
>
> > In fact, I'd recommend you start heading south along the canyon walls, and see what you find while we head north. Shent carved and decorated many stones around here, so we're hoping to find something good.

Whatever direction the party takes, the Fulgurite Blades will take the opposite route to avoid traveling with them. Following the Fulgurite Blades is beyond the scope of this Event; they take their leave and vanish into the Biome. The Fulgurite Blades next appear at the [[Shent Moon Temple]] after the characters have already arrived.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Fulgurite Caravan
>
> At the end of this Event, a caravan Token representing the Fulgurite Blades will appear on the Region Map. This Token is a visual flourish, and is only visible to the party when it is adjacent to the party caravan Token; it should be moved at this time to represent the Fulgurite Blades leaving the area, then deleted.
