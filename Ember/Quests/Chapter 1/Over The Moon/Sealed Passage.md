# Sealed Passage

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration Event occurs as the party approaches the Shent gate in ⬢ s.3209.2869. By examining the gate, the characters can:
>
> - Discover a remnant of the ancient [[Shent]] culture.
> - Investigate its construction to glean insights into its purpose and makers.
>
> This Event is depicted using the "Ancient Shent Door" Level of the [[Vista: Verdant Paths]] Vista.

### No Entry

The Shent gateway is closed and impossible to open from this side. However, Events in a nearby Quest will lead the party to the other side of the door and provide them the means to open it. The doors are around 40 ft. tall and covered in vines and rocky protrusions that could allow the party to climb them for closer inspection.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Behind Closed Doors
>
> Any character who examines the door and makes a successful **Society (DC 14)** check concludes that, based on the door's size and markings, this structure was very likely built by the Shent, a culture of giants who inhabited this region long ago. If it was constructed by the Shent, it is likely close to three thousand years old.
>
> - **Knowledge: Shent**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Artifacts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character recalls that the Shent possessed a great reverence for Ember's moons, and fostered close connections to them. Perhaps a prayer, offering, or other invocation related to the lunar cycle might unseal the door.
>
> Any character who studies the runic markings and makes a successful **Arcana (DC 13)** check recognizes them as archaic symbols for transmutation, divination, abjuration, and evocation.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character notices that the inset gems are similarly inscribed and can deduce that the stone and crystals were likely enchanted to be magically sealed and warded. Without knowing the ancient spells used to perform the sealing, there is no way to unseal the door.

The crystals embedded in the door may tempt the party to pry them loose.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Gem Thieves
>
> The crystals embedded in the door are magically set and impossible to remove or damage without force far greater than the party can muster.
>
> The crystals were all sourced from this region's caves and contain latent magical effects. Attempting to interact with them up close triggers one of their [[Kaleidoscope Crystal Effects]], which could be especially dangerous for any character clinging to the stone wall dozens of feet off the ground.

### Concluding the Event

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Next Steps
>
> Once the party has finished examining the gate, they may continue traveling around the cliffs, eventually triggering the [[Curious Party]] Event.
