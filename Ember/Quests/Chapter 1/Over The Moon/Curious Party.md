# Curious Party

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration Event occurs as the party makes their way north through the [[Forest of Stone]]. By examining the scene, the party can:
>
> - See a notice left by a team within the [[Anachraenum]] known as the [[Fulgurite Blades]].
> - Survey the local area to determine where the group might be, with details pointing toward a camp located two miles due east.

### An Archaeological Claim

Examining the parchment below the banner yields a little more information.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The parchment bears a watermark of the same emblem sewn upon the banner above it. Its message reads:
>
> > Investigation of nearby archaeological site ongoing by the Fulgurite Blades under the auspices of Anachraenum operations. For questions or work regarding the opening of this sealed passage, see Sajor in base camp ahead.
>
> The final sentence has been struck through in black ink and replaced with an additional note that reads:
>
> > Do not disturb unless on official business.
>
> At the foot of the pole is a small pile of rocks that has been haphazardly splattered with dark paint or ink to form an arrow pointing east.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Official Notice
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 12, Passive)** notices that the note and banner are new, having been placed here somewhat recently.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The character deduces that the notice is less than two weeks old.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Society (DC 14, Passive)** check while examining the notice concludes that the note is legitimate and bears the proper watermarks and stamps.
>
> - **Culture: Ordani**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

The party may wish to consider this notice further and recall what they know about the groups mentioned on it.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### What Is the Anachraenum?
>
> Any character with **Society (DC 15, Passive)** recognizes that the banner belongs to the [[Anachraenum]] — an order of adventurer-historians based in [[Ordain]].
>
> Anachraenum adventurers, explorers, scouts, and scholars travel throughout the Arctus Plateau and beyond in search of hidden treasure, lost secrets, and remnants of the bygone civilizations. The guild is mostly comprised of teams that take on specialized jobs together, as well as freelance agents who handle Anachraenum business on their own.
>
> - **Culture: Arcturian**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Culture: Ordani**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character does not recognize the name "Fulgurite Blades," but recalls that individual adventuring groups within the Anachraenum often have their own self-styled monikers that reflect their methods or motivations.

If the party would like to get their bearings or try to track the Fulgurite Blades, they can do so by investigating the area around the banner.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Where Did They Go?
>
> The pile of stones forming an arrow at the base of the banner pole points east into the wide canyon.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 12)** check notices numerous old tracks in the mud. The tracks indicate that at least two people passed through here and headed east.
>
> - **Knowledge: Tracking**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character recognizes that one set of tracks was left by a Cor'ak, while the other set of tracks was left by a much smaller figure, probably a Thornling.
