# Milestone Progression

Ember uses a system called **Milestone Points** to determine how and when the player characters advance in Level. Instead of acquiring traditional XP by defeating monsters, advancement in Ember is based on completing specific [[Events]] within our many [[Quests]].

## Expectations

Generally, a **Main Quest** awards 2 Milestone Points while a **Side Quest** awards a 1 Milestone Point — though there are some exceptions to this rule. You can learn more about Main and Side Quests on the [[Quests and Events]] page of this guide.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Expected Progression
>
> It is expected that player characters in the full Ember campaign will reach Level 11 by the end of the campaign. Parties which are comprehensive in their exploration and completion of content may reach Level 12. A party which pursues only the main story will finish the game at around Level 9.

#### Current Progression

During the current scope of the game, players can expect to reach Level 5 if they complete the main quest through the end of Chapter 3 and explore to locate and pursue available side quests along the way.

## Leveling Up

Milestones earned apply to the entire party, even if certain characters or players were not present when the Milestone was achieved. When reaching the number of Milestone Points required to level-up, the entire party advances in Level. Only player characters advance in Level — non-player companions and allies either do not progress or have separate progression mechanics.

## Crucible Advancement

When the party advances in Level, each member of the party should increase their Level by one. Each player character may immediately advance their Ability Scores and Talents as normal and immediately benefit from that progression.
