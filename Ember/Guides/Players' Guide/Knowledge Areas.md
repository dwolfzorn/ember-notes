# Knowledge Areas

Your character begins with two **Knowledge Areas.** In the context of the Ember game, Knowledge Areas represent everything your character has learned from those who raised them, the Culture they grew up in, and the choices that they have made in their life to date. Knowledge Areas are determined by the character's [[Cultures]] and [[Path]], as noted in the list below, and are unrelated to their Ability Scores or Skills.

### Knowledge Areas In Gameplay

While playing the game, Knowledge Areas have several impacts and can be used for the following purposes:

- **A substitute for a Skill Check.** If your character has the necessary knowledge, they can gain related information without the need to roll.
- **A bonus to a Skill Check.** If your character has the necessary knowledge, they may receive **+2 Boons** to a related roll.
- **As a prerequisite to a Skill Check.** Some Skill Checks cannot be attempted unless your character has a base of knowledge about the subject in question.

> [!danger] Hazard
> Knowledge Areas are incorporated into the events that a Game Master uses to run the game. They have already been carefully configured, but ultimately, the GM has the final say on how a Knowledge Area may be used.

### Knowledge Area List

You may want to choose a specific Knowledge Area because it is a subject that you would like to gain **+2 Boons** with during gameplay, or you might be interested in that specific subject and would like to gain more information from Skill Checks or gameplay moments. The following list makes it clear which Knowledge Area is associated with certain Cultures and Paths.

#### Missing Knowledge Areas

At this point in development, there is no way to start with certain Knowledge Areas, as there is no Culture or Path associated with them. Eventually, more Cultures and Paths will be available that will close these gaps. You will eventually also be able to gain additional Knowledge Areas as you play.
