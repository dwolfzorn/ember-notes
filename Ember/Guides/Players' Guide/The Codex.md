# The Codex

The Codex is a compendium of knowledge that you have accumulated while playing Ember. The Codex tracks the Events that have happened to you, the Quests you are progressing, the Locations and Biomes in the world that you have discovered, the creatures you have encountered, and the characters you have met.

The Codex can be viewed at any time by clicking the ​​ icon in the Calendar interface element at the top of the screen.

## Journal

The Journal in the Codex — separate from the Journal entries in the Foundry sidebar — automatically tracks completed [[Events]] as the party moves across the [[Region Map]]. The Journal tracks the time and day a given Event was completed, and provides a small summary of what happened during the Event. This is a way to reflect on your journey and see when and where Events took place.

## Quest Log

The Quest Log records completed Events in an identical fashion to those logged in the Journal, but filtered to include only [[Quest Events]] (not Standalone Events), and displays these logs sorted by [[Quest]], rather than chronologically.

## Discoveries

When the party moves into a hex with a [[Location]] or [[Biome]] that they have not previously encountered, this new discovery will be noted automatically in the Codex. These Codex entries include further information about the newly encountered Location or Biome, and the day and time that Location or Biome was first encountered.

## Bestiary

The Bestiary automatically tracks encountered creatures and monsters that the party encounters in the game. This happens mostly inside [[Area Maps]]. Once discovered, a small summary will be recorded alongside an image and the day and time that creature was first encountered.

## Characters

When the party discovers a new character for the first time, that character is recorded here alongside a small summary, including their name, pronouns, [[Ancestries]], [[Cultures]], character portrait, the day and time that character was first encountered, and other details.
