# Attunement

Individuals in Ember can demonstrate an exceptional resonance to Ember's cosmological forces, a phenomenon called [[Attunement]]. Cosmological Attunements grant unique features depending on how closely your character align with important celestial forces in [[The Cosmos]] of Ember.

 You will select one of these Attunements as part of character creation (following your selection of Culture and Path), and you will begin your adventure at Rank 1 of your chosen Attunement with the abilities granted by that initial Attunement already active. During the course of your adventure, you will have abundant opportunities to further develop your chosen Attunement or to acquire Attunements with other forces via the [[Players' Guide]] system.

 Your Attunement progression and the ability to change which Attunement is active can be found on a special Ember-specific tab on the character sheet for player characters. . The various Attunements and their corresponding Ranks are listed below.

 ## Attunement Progression

 Attunement is progressed by acquiring Attunement Points, which occur as a result of various decisions you make during the course of play that are ideologically or philosophically aligned with the principal **Ideal** of a Cosmological Attunement. The table below describes the number of points needed to obtain or progress an Attunement Rank.

 | Attunement Rank | Points Required | Cumulative Points |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rank 1 | 3 | 3 |
| Rank 2 | 4 | 7 |
| Rank 3 | 5 | 12 |
| Rank 4 | 6 | 18 |
| Rank 5 | 7 | 25 |

 > [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster Judgement
>
>  There are a large number of designed Attunement progression moments scattered throughout the gameplay events of Ember. It is also possible for a Gamemaster to grant additional custom Attunement Points when a character takes some action that is strongly aligned with the Ideal of a specific Attunement. We encourage Gamemasters to consider Attunement progression as a way to reward exceptional roleplay or to add weight to climactic moments.
>
>  #### Expected Progression
>
>  You should expect the Attunement which is most closely aligned with your character's personality and ideology to reach Rank 5 by the end of the Ember campaign. You may also expect to progress several other Attunements to lesser Ranks, providing you with additional tactical options which may be situationally useful.

 ## Active Attunement

 Attunements provide various mechanical benefits, which may include both passive bonuses as well as usable actions, but only for the Attunement that is currently marked as Active.

 Your active attunement can be changed when you complete a **Rest** action. You can set an Attunement as active on the Attunements tab of your character sheet, using the Make Active button located to the far right of each Attunement's progress bar.

 When a new Attunement becomes active, other attunements become inactive. The talent granted by your new Attunement is automatically added to your character sheet while another prior talent is removed.
