# Geography

This game is set upon the eastern coast of Aterica, Ember's largest continent, where a great rocky shelf known as the Arctus Plateau meets Ember's singular great ocean. The crown jewel of the Arctus Plateau is the metropolis of Ordain, one of the eleven great cities of the world. This region is home to two cultures. The Arcturians are an innovative and hardworking people, with a long history and rich traditions. The Ordani are colorful, inquisitive, proud, and creative.

## Continents

The world of Ember has 8 major continents. The largest of these is called [[Aterica]], a supercontinent to the northwest of the [[The Starshield]] — a smaller continent upon which the Aedir Sun Tower was built that marks the center of the world and from which cardinal directions are defined.

## The Arctus Plateau

The windswept lands in eastern Aterica that border the ocean — and within them, the great city of Ordain — together make up the [[Arctus Plateau]]. Populated by a few isolated highland villages and towns, this highland plateau is rife with rocky outcrops and long winding roads between fields of dry grasses and isolated trees. The [[Arcturian]] people who inhabit this land are practical and innovative with a deep love of tradition, creativity, and community.

## Ordain

[[Ordain]] is one of the largest Great Cities of Ember, a glittering white and red jewel on the far eastern edge of the Arctus Plateau. Ordain is a vast and strategically located metropolis that is also one of the largest hubs of trade in the known world and the seat of several powerful organizations. [[Ordani]] culture and influence is felt worldwide and the Ordani language called "Coins", once used primarily for trade, is now considered the common tongue for many cultures.
