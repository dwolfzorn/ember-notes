## Significant Thanks

Our team wishes to express our sincere and significant thanks to the 3,808 backers on Kickstarter who committed their support towards seeing our vision for Ember come to life. Your support has been incredibly powerful in motivating and challenging us to make the game we have dreamed of for a community of fans who also believe in that vision. We hope you love it.

## No Generative AI

Our team is absolutely committed to creating Ember without any use of generative AI. Ember is a product that is lovingly crafted by talented creators who imbue the world you will explore with soul and humanity.
