# Ancestry

Ancestry describes your biological heritage, defines certain physical attributes of your character, and provides some signature features which are unique to characters of that Ancestry. There are 16 Ancestries available to choose from in Ember which are described in detail in the [[Ancestries]] entry of the Setting Compendium.

- [[Altyra]]
- [[Ashka]]
- [[Cor'ak]]
- [[Drakon]]
- [[Fej]]
- [[Hulg'run]]
- [[Human]]
- [[Keth]]
- [[Kiska]]
- [[Kivahr]]
- [[Nir'ae]]
- [[Signborn]]
- [[Thornling]]
- [[Vrjnhar]]
- [[Wirrun]]
- [[Zeph]]

As the first step of character creation you will select your Ancestry. Each Ancestry has its own unique origin story. Some ancestries are biologically compatible and it is not uncommon for characters in Ember to have a mixed ancestral heritage. You are welcome to describe your character as having mixed ancestry, however you must still select one Ancestry which defines the mechanical benefits and features that apply to your character.

## Aster

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Aster are individuals who were changed by the life force of Ember at their birth into something wholly unique. This change can be cosmetic — adding quills, feathers, tails, or other physical features not found in the Aster's ancestry — but it can also shift the individual's internal biology on the deepest level. Changes manifest differently in each Aster, and they can be created from any creature, regardless of its ancestry, culture, or sapience. The one commonality between Aster is that they're always changed at birth and that the change is always a permanent one.

During the Ancestry step of character creation, you may also select that your character has [[Aster]] heritage. This Aster trait is non-mechanical, but indicates that your character is different from others of your base Ancestry due to the particular qualities of their Aster alterations — you will still need to choose a base Ancestry to determine the relevant mechanical effects.

## Special Gameplay Notes

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Ashka Thermal Vision
>
> The Ashka ancestry [[Thermal Vision]] feature is implemented in Foundry Virtual Tabletop as a **Detection Mode**. Be sure to configure the **Prototype Token** for any Ashka character and assign them the **Thermal Vision** detection mode with 60 feet of range. This is not currently automated but may be in a future update.
