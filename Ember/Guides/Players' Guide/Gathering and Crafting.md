# Gathering and Crafting

The gathering and crafting system in Ember is a system designed to amplify and enhance the gameplay experience.

  #### Work In Progress

 The Gathering and Crafting system is not yet implemented and will be added during a later phase of game development.

  ### Gathering

 Through gathering, you can acquire materials from around the world through exploration, events, or defeating specific enemies.

 ### Crafting

 Through crafting, you may then take these gathered materials and craft items, objects, common weapons, and armor.
