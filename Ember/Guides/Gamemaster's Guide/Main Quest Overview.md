# Main Quest Overview

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Significant Spoilers
>
> This page contains **major spoilers** for the plot of Ember, *A Shard of Fear*. We recommend all Gamemasters read through it thoroughly, however, because it's important to have a clear understanding of the overarching storyline of the campaign that you'll be orchestrating for your players.
>
> On the other hand, some Gamemasters may strongly prefer to not arm themselves with full knowledge of the campaign's events. Proceed **only if you wish to have all of the information available to you** up-front instead of learning things as you play.

## A Shard of Fear

The title of Ember's main story is *A* *Shard of Fear*, a deliberate moniker that acts as a bit of a spoiler once certain narrative information becomes available to the players. What the player characters of this grand adventure will eventually learn is one of the world's best kept secrets: many of the events that have swept them into the chaos and fatefulness of an emergent conflict in the [[Arctus Plateau]] revolve around the [[Vile Dragons]] known as [[Zerranyss]] — a powerful spellcaster who has their insidious sights set upon godhood.

The machinations of Zerranyss are not readily apparent at first, and early events will serve to establish the lore of the Ember world (its [[Ancestries]], [[Cultures]], places, and paradigms), gently exposing players to this dazzling new setting and its associated gameplay. The first few chapters of the game focus on a slow reveal of early plot elements while foreshadowing the greater story. However, by the end of Chapter 2, it becomes clear that there is something very sinister behind the calamitous and destructive events that have ravaged the Arctus Plateau in recent years.

Zerranyss is first encountered during Chapter 3, although indirectly via the humanoid guise of [[Vinarith]]. Zerranyss' actions and ambitions directly cause the central plotline of the Ember campaign. The vile dragon is a cunning and strategic villain, and has orchestrated a complex plan to undermine and usurp the seats of power in [[Ordain]] — one of the great cities of the world and (most importantly) an access point to the [[Shard Caverns]], where Zerranyss believes a powerful [[Ascendancy]] may be found. Such an artifact would allow Zerranyss to undertake an ascension ritual which, if successful, would imbue the dragon with the power of a [[Shard Gods]].

## Chapter 1

The Chapter 1 Main Quest in Ember is called [[The Winding Trail]] and covers the party's journey to the frontier mining town of [[Helkas]], traveling with the eccentric caravanner [[Agraband Swift]] and the [[Strayhearth Caravan]]. The aftermath of this attack provides clues and ill omens that set the stage for the epic campaign that follows.

## Chapter 2

Following their fateful encounter in Helkas, the party now has a singular goal: to reach the great city of [[Ordain]] to the east. However, the characters have a choice of three paths corresponding to each of the three companion allies, who undertake separate journeys toward the metropolis.

**Traveling with Lyla**

If the characters depart Helkas with Lyla Cevher, they undertake the quest [[An Old Friend]]. Their journey will first lead them to a reunion and consultation with a powerful mentor to Lyla, the Shard God [[Sigil]]. From there, the path of clues leads to the industrial town of [[Arcturel]], the settlement of [[Nain]], and eventually the city of [[Seawall]]. Along the way, the party gathers clues towards the identity of the mysterious Otherhood of Fortune.

**Traveling with Ankarist**

If the characters depart Helkas with Ankarist, they experience the quest [[Ancient Paths]] and journey northward in search of a legendary Shent tower called [[Mythspire Observatory]] wherein an entrance to the vast subterranean [[Pathways]] may be found. As Ankarist and the characters explore the lightless depths, they'll uncover schemes of the [[Mutagists]] — a group of sinister alchemists whose twisted experiments are conducted within the secrecy of their underground laboratory.

**Traveling with Sin**

If the characters depart Helkas with Sin, they experience the quest [[Crumbling Sanctuary]]. The road ahead will initially take them to [[Corpin Sanctuary]] on the slopes of Mial Mountain, where Sin will make her first contact with the devoted [[Cindaric Sages]]. However, this introduction is waylaid by the presence of a villainous necromancer, whose unholy machinations will lead to a siege on the Sanctuary by a horde of skeletal undead.

Eventually, all three paths lead to the city of Ordain and the beginning of Chapter 3.

## Chapter 3

Having reached the great city of [[Ordain Gazetteer]], the party is now faced with three Main Quests associated with the key NPCs they met at Helkas (Ankarist, Lyla, and Sin). Throughout these adventures, the party will start to see the effects of Zerranyss' machinations on Ordani society. While exploring the city's bustling streets, the characters will begin learning about the insidious forces responsible for the problems at hand.

Although the party can initiate all three of these Main Quests, each quest has a "point of no return" event where the party must decide which of the three they'll continue to completion — once the characters finish such a Quest Event, they become locked out of the other two Quests for good.

Chapter 3 also introduces the player characters to a new key NPC, Fernis Ossa, whose associated Quest will become available as the party explores Ordain.

**Fernis and the Anachraenum**

Fernis Ossa approaches the party with a proposal to ally in response to recent concerns about the operations of the Anachraenum. In the [[The Expedition Challenge]] Quest, the heroes are sponsored by the fabled explorer as entrants in the grand Expedition Challenge — an extensive test of skill and capability that serves as a trial and competition for aspiring Anachraenum initiates. Fernis will also arrange for the party to reclaim possession of a secure and strategic hideout hidden within the derelict district of [[The Concourse]].

**Ankarist and the Veiled Chain**

If the characters choose to help Ankarist with his investigation into the clandestine Mutagists, they'll encounter the events of the [[Spreading Sickness]] Quest — a mystery that begins with some poisoned granaries in [[Lilifeld Stations]] before spanning the entire city of Ordain, eventually leading to a hidden laboratory in the Sedyri ruins of the [[Forgotten Cistern]]. And all the while, the party members are being manipulated by the machinations of [[Serethus]] — one of Zerranyss' devoted lieutenants — which culminate in a perilous ambush the characters must narrowly escape.

**Lyla and House Cevher**

Parties which reconnect with Lyla Cevher in Chapter 3 may assist her with an intricate maneuver in the [[Disgraced House]] Quest to expose and oust the enemies of House Cevher responsible for their downfall. They will investigate the death of Lyla's uncle Darius and become embroiled in the planning of an epic and dangerous heist against the rival [[House Wandren]] during the backdrop of the the prestigious [[Marlstone]].

**Sin and the Cindaric Sages**

If the characters choose to assist Sin Marmot with her initiation to the Cindaric Sages, they will experience the quest [[Smoldering Cinders]] and help the young druid to uncover further details of the insidious necromantic plot which was first revealed in the depths beneath Corpin Sanctuary. This investigation eventually leads to a climactic battle in Ordain's [[Burial Grounds]] where Zerranyss' ally and Blood Baron necromancer [[Amelia Naxan]] has unleashed a hematophage abomination to prey upon and wreak terror among the citizens of Ordain.

## Chapter 4 and Beyond

Chapters 4, 5, and 6 are not yet included, but will be added during a subsequent test phase. If you reach the end of Chapter 3 before this further content is added, we recommend taking a short hiatus from the campaign or spending time to explore the world and experience the many Side Quests which are available.

The events of Chapter 4 will compel the party to journey back out into various reaches of the Arctus Plateau, where certain people and places of Ember have been impacted by the deviant orchestrations of Zerranyss. Depending on which key NPC the characters have chosen to ally themselves with, these journeys could lead them to a variety of dangerous and intriguing locations in search of adventure.

**Lyla and House Cevher**

If the characters continue their adventures with [[Lyla Cevher]], they must respond urgently to the troubling disappearance of the shard god [[Sigil]], Lyla's mentor and a powerful ally of House Cevher. This quest will follow a bloody trail of clues from the Shard God's extradimensional lodge to the lawless streets of Seawall on a hunt for answers that culminates in a operation to take down the Otherhood of Fortune.

**Ankarist and the Mutagists**

The party faces a race against time to save [[Ankarist]] from the wasting effects of the mutagen inflicted upon him by the traitor Serethus. Their search for answers will lead the party to a remote island northeast of the Arctus Plateau and the seaside town of [[Caste]] in search of clues to the Mutagists' location. The clues they discover lead towards a terrible laboratory where blood-based experimentation has produced abominable revelations.

**Sin and the Cindaric Sages**

If the characters continue their adventures with [[Sin Marmot]], they will be charged to join the Lumek paladin [[Luna Karrowrath]] and her Flameguard warriors on a crusade into the northwestern [[Wedgelands]] where long-forgotten tombs of the [[Giant]] giants may hold answers about the looming undead threat. The discovery of an ancient evil from the time of [[The Shattering]] will challenge everything the characters think they know about undeath.

**Fernis and the Anachraenum**

Meanwhile, continued adventure with [[Fernis Ossa]] will compel the party to join her on a dangerous expedition in the southwest to explore the Crumbling Spire — an enormous fragment of the famed Broken Tower of the ancient [[Aedir]] Empire, sundered into countless pieces during the calamitous events of The Shattering. Puzzles, mystery, monsters, and more await the party beyond the gates of this massive dungeon.

### Towards Endgame

After completion of these Chapter 4 quests, the party will find themselves propelled towards the final arc of Ember's campaign (Chapters 5 and 6) via the revealed identity of Zerranyss and their terrible scheme to pursue ascension to godhood via communion with a Heart Crystal.

Faced with near impossible odds, the party must prepare to confront Zerranyss by completing what plotlines they can as well as by exploring lost and ancient places of the world to recover the knowledge and power to stand against a would-be deity.
