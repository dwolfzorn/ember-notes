# Providing Feedback

During this phase of development and testing, our team will be focused on continuing to develop and refine the major systems of Ember while actively reviewing and prioritizing feedback from our community of tester players. Your input is invaluable to us and every report, suggestion, and observation helps shape the game and ensures that the final product is as polished, engaging, and enjoyable as possible.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Where to Submit Feedback
>
> All feedback that you have about the game should be submitted through our official Discord server: [https://discord.gg/foundryvtt](https://discord.gg/foundryvtt) . We have set up a dedicated channel, **#ember-test-feedback**, specifically for this purpose. Within this channel are a number of individual posts where you can submit feedback. These are specific to systems, quests, or aspects of the game that we have highlighted as needing extra scrutiny. You can create a post yourself to highlight a topic; **however**, before you do so, please ensure that you have checked to see if there is already a post that discusses that topic.
>
> Using this channel enables our team to track feedback efficiently, discuss issues internally, and respond to your questions or requests for clarification more effectively.

## Submitting FeeDback

When providing feedback, please try to be as detailed and clear as possible. Useful feedback often includes:

- Steps to reproduce issues or bugs – This helps us understand exactly what happened and how to fix it.
- Screenshots or recordings – Visual examples can make it much easier for the team to identify and address issues.
- Your experience or suggestions – Share how something felt, what worked well, and what could be improved.
- Prioritization or severity – If something is a critical bug or a minor annoyance, let us know so we can address the most urgent matters first.

We encourage constructive, respectful, and thoughtful feedback. Remember, this is a collaborative process, and your input directly influences the development of Ember. While we may not be able to implement every suggestion immediately, we carefully review all feedback and use it to guide our updates.

Thank you for helping us make Ember the best it can be. Your participation, enthusiasm, and insights are what drive the project forward, and we deeply appreciate the time and effort you put into testing and sharing your thoughts with us.
