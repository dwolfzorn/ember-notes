# User Interface

Ember includes a number of unique interface elements at the top of the screen that may not be familiar, even to experienced Foundry users.

**Calendar**

In the middle of the interface element at the top of the screen, you can find the current [[time, date, season, and year]] as well as the position of important celestial bodies in the Ember [[Cosmology]]. You can expand and collapse detailed information about the calendar and cosmos by clicking the carat to the right of the current time of day.

**Maps**

At the top of the screen,to the left of the time, you can find the different Maps used to play Ember. You can access the [[World Map]] via the ​ icon, the [[Cosmos Map]] via the ​ icon, and the [[Region Exploration]] via the ​​ icon.

**Party Sheet**

At the top of the screen to the right of the time, you can find the Party Sheet via the ​ icon. This popup has information about the characters in your party, as well as your party's Level, Travel Pace, and banner.

**The Codex**

At the top of the screen to the right of the Party Sheet, you can find [[The Codex]] via the ​​ icon. The Codex contains information about Events you've completed, places you've discovered, and creatures and characters that you've met.
