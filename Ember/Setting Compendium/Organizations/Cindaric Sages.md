# Cindaric Sages

The Cindaric Sages are one of the three main political powers of Ordain, focused on the spiritual, emotional, and physical well-being of those who live within the city. Cindarics believe that all life in the cosmos has a destined role guided by the unconscious will of the Heart of Ember — the prime mover that resides at the center of the world. Often considered to be the living spirit of Ordain, the Cindaric Sages focus on tending to the city and its people; they view themselves as stewards and caretakers, driven by the Heart of Ember's virtuous and inscrutable intentions.

- **For the Heart**. Cindaric Sages believe that all life in the cosmos stems from (and is guided by) the unseen, unconscious will of the [[Heart of Ember]]. This peaceful organization is often seen as a priestly order, a comparison which has some element of truth to it — but rather than a specific deity, the thoughtful Cindarics are connected to the foundational life force of Ember itself.
- **For the People**. While the Sages spend a great deal of time meditating and communing with the Heart of Ember, they also play a pivotal role in the ongoing health and healing of [[Ordain]] and its people. They provide healing of the body (physical), mind (emotional), and spirit (magical) from the sanctuary of their various Healing Houses located around Ordain and the Arctus Plateau.
- **No Power Without Balance**. The Sages maintain a steady but sometimes uneasy political balance with Ordain's Trading Houses and the [[Hallows]]. Each of these organizations sends three representatives each to the nine-person [[Ordinate]], the bureaucratic decision-making body of Ordain.
- **For Cindaric Eyes Only**. A feeling of spiritual unrest has led the Cindaric Sages to become progressively withdrawn from the people and other immediate concerns. And while the Sages have always been protective of their secret lore and traditions, this inclination for privacy has only amplified in recent years, with no signs of stopping. As the Cindarics steadily turn their focus inward, they've begun to lose sight of some plights of the people.

## Operations

Traditionally, the Cindarics are led by the head of their order, known as the Holy Speaker, who presides over the Sages from Cindarin Temple in Ordain. A Steward of the Holy Speaker serves as their right hand, with a focus on more practical and administrative duties. Furthermore, a Keeper of the Sages is appointed to supervise all other sages in the organization, along with the resources needed to facilitate their wellbeing. Other Cindarics are assigned tasks according to their abilities and the needs of the people, and younger Sages are afforded training at one of the order's several healing houses. Many operations of the Cindaric Sages are supported by a militaristic organization known as the [[Burnished Hand]], whose legacy of monster-hunting and certifiable martial prowess serve the peaceful Cindarics well.

Their weighty responsibilities have led the Cindarics to occasionally overstep their bounds when it comes to deciding what is best for Ordain, especially regarding the privatization of knowledge. In recent years, the Cindaric Sages have become increasingly concerned with ritualistic communion with Ember and its divine core. This concern has eclipsed the order's relationship with Ordain and its people, leading many denizens of the Arctus Plateau to regard the modern Cindarics as distant and withdrawn. And while the Sages remain generally well-meaning and progressive, their historically-esteemed reputation and influence has started to wane. Indeed, many within the city of Ordain consider them ineffective at handling the bigger issues.

## Key Members

**[[Lilla Arien]] (LG, Ordani Signborn, she/her)**

Also known as the "Second Sage," the affable Signborn known as Lilla Arien serves as the "Keeper of Sages" at Cindarin Temple. This high-ranking position is tasked with overseeing the room, board, and wellbeing of all other sages in the order — duties which Lilla carries out with great respect and dedication, through good times and bad.

**[[Jon Vastil]] (LG, Ordani Human, he/him)**

Jon Vastil is the Holy Speaker of the Cindaric Sages, and has guided the order from a position of leadership for nearly 75 years. Ancient in age and infirm of health, the once commendable Vastil has faced growing criticism in recent years for a perceived lack of compassion towards the commonfolk and the city's mounting problems. An obsession with humanity's spiritual connection to the Heart of Ember is said to guide this aged leader, who battles a curious illness without cause or cure.

**[[Vinarith]] (NE, Ordani Human, he/him)**

Vinarith is the charismatic Steward of the Holy Speaker, and serves as the proverbial right hand to the highest office of the Cindaric Sages. Now that Jon Vastil has fallen ill, Vinarith has had to shoulder many of the Holy Speaker's responsibilities alongside Keeper Lilla Arien, which the enterprising Steward has accomplished with fervor and success.

**[[Mira Wavehorn]]**

Mira Wavehorn is the duly designated leader of the enclave of Cindaric Sages as Corpin Sanctuary, situated in the crags of Mial Mountain west of Ordain. An experienced sea captain from the Cascilian Republic, Mira manages to find ways to exercise her key learnings from those years on the high seas throughout her daily life as Corpin Sanctuary's chief steward.
