# Burnished Hand

The Burnished Hand is a military order of exceptional warriors clad in suits of thick bronze armor and furs who reside on the Rusted Fortress of Bura Island. The organization started out as a small group of Vrjnhar from the Oaken culture, but for the last several hundred years the order has consisted mostly of Ordani and Arcturian people (with a smaller number of other ancestries from across the world). The fortress itself was once the location of the largest concentration of Oaken within the Arctus Plateau, but the Vrjnhar who built it left many years ago; meanwhile, the trainees and other members who remained coalesced into a more official organization and eventually adopted their famed moniker.

For a long time, the Burnished Hand was the only organized military group in the region, and were known as a stalwart organization of monster hunters and justiciars when the Plateau was a much wilder and more primal place. Members of the order are not commonly seen inside the city limits of Ordain, but can often rather be found serving the needs of the Sages as protectors in more dangerous areas of the Plateau (and beyond).

- **Oaken Roots:** With deep cultural ties to the Oaken — who helped with the building and protection of Ordain during its early years — the Rusted Fortress on Bura Island was built to protect the bay and to serve as a fortified home for scores of Oaken warriors. Oaken Vrjnhar are extremely long-lived, however, they have a deep sense of restlessness and urge to explore dangerous places; so as the Arctus Plateau calmed, most of these warriors departed, leaving the fortress in the hands of Ember's younger Ancestries.
- **Cindaric Guardians:** Since the Cindaric Sages are not a militarized group, Oaken Warriors have historically protected and guarded the Sages on longer journeys, and a small elite group was often assigned to the Holy Speaker for protection. This close bond was aided by the connection many Oaken feel to the Cindaric belief system, and their legacy endures to this day via the Burnished Hand.
- **Ritualistic Techniques:** The Burnished Hand are a close-knit organization trained in ancient Oaken techniques, martial styles that involve ritualistic pre-combat preparations capable of enhancing their bodies and minds. Such focus gives these warriors the ability to briefly shrug off their wounds, increase their strength, or banish their fears and doubts.

## Operations

The Burnished Hand is helmed by the organization's Commander, the honorable warrior Steros Kraver, who presides over the order with a stern sense of rule and a keen realization of the organization's potential and limitations. A retinue of six Lieutenant Commanders serve beneath Steros, along with a battalion of 500 soldiers (including at least 100 initiates). These adherents are all learned in the ways of the Cindaric Sages, who they have solemnly sworn to protect.

While the Arctus Plateau has seen brief periods of unrest (such as the Mourning War), for the past several hundred years the organization diminished in prevalence, with most of the Rusted Fortress on Bura Island lying empty and abandoned. With the recent increase in threats, many are drawn to the fortress for a chance to fight back, drive away emerging monsters, and help return the region to its once peaceful state. As such they have become a more common sight in Ordain.

## Key Members

**[[Steros Kraver]]**

Steros is the stalwart Commander of the Burnished Hand. He is also a loyal adherent to the edicts of the [[Cindarin Temple]] in Ordain, where the Holy Speaker presides over the Cindaric Sages and their closest allies. Steros is a skilled warrior and a charismatic leader, and is regarded by many to be one of the most competent military leaders in Arcturian history.

**[[Aldath]]**

This ancient Vrjnhar warrior was a member of the Burnished Hand long ago, and has only recently returned to Ordain to investigate the once-proud organization. He resides in [[Nurk's Hollow]] near Bura Island, alongside a group of other exiled members he's gathered together for his cause. Aldath is a legendary warrior who is losing his edge to old age.
