# House Lilifeld

Among the five great Trading Houses, House Lilifeld is known as the living heartbeat of Ordain, the force that keeps the city fed and growing. Uniquely, Lilifeld is the only House whose seat of power lies outside the city proper, not even on the Arctus Plateau, but across the sea on the vine-wrapped Isle of Kortos. When it became clear that the Arcturian people nearby could only ever grow enough to sustain their own scattered communities, the rising urban sprawl of Ordain faced a crisis of hunger. In search of fertile lands beyond the plateau, interprising traders came across the lush, fertile Isle of Kortos, and among its rolling fields roamed the Terracant: massive, vine-horned beasts with mossy coats and eyes like slow-burning amber.

These ancient creatures, part beast and part natural force, seemed to be the perfect solution to the growing cities food problem. Through a special bond with the Terracants and their innate abilities to boost crop yields, House Lilifeld emerged not only as a merchant power but as the cornerstone of Ordain’s survival.

- **Bread and Wine:** Among House Lilifeld’s sprawling estates on the Isle of Kortos, colossal golden terraces of wheat ripple gently alongside endless rows of grapevines. The terraced fields climb the hillsides in graceful steps, each plot tended to by Lilifeld workers and faithful, well-cared-for Terracent herds. Harvested wheat becomes the bread that fills the tables in Ordain, a humble yet vital counterpart to Lilifeld’s famed vintages for delicious wine.
- **Decadent Dishes:** House Lilifeld is famous not only for what it grows but also for what it creates, with many legendary chefs among their ranks. The culinary arts are taught to all Lilifeld members, trained to coax extraordinary flavor from humble crops, vegetables, meats, and fruits sourced from across the known world. Within Ordain, Lilifeld has taken over nearly the entire Restaurant Row and owns stakes in many cafes, taverns, inns, and restaurants throughout the city. Their partnership with House Bastilla brings in exotic spices, seafood, and arcane delicacies from distant shores.
- **Foundation of Fashion:** The lush, mossy fleece of the Terracant is spun into fine wool with a subtle shimmer and remarkable durability, and within Ordain it forms the foundation of everything from everyday cloaks to enchanted finery. House Lilifeld does not personally make these clothing items but instead has a close partnership with House Salva, the premier weavers and designers of the city.

## Operations

House Lilifeld has a subtle but far-reaching influence. Its members are resourceful, hardy folk who spend most of their time in Ordain, distributing food throughout the city's markets, running restaurants, and cooking. On Kortos, they oversee the cultivation of wheat, grapes, and the care of their prized Terracants. Every harvest is carefully timed with the seasons, while wool shearing, food preparation, and trade coordination are handled with near-ritual precision. Lilifeld’s operations blend curated and carefully crafted magic with generational know-how, ensuring that every barrel, bushel, and bale is ready for market, whether bound for local tables or the grand halls of Ordain.

Unfortunately, while House Lilifeld has close relationships with every other house and is generally beloved by the people, despite being a commercial enterprise, their relationship with Arcturians and the Agrimages Circle is far from glowing. Agrimages dislike House Lilifeld's commercialization and industrialization, finding their techniques to grow food using the Terracant to be widely disruptive to natural ecosystems. As a result, Lilifeld is one of the only major houses that struggles to gain stable footing within the Arctus Plateau, often facing resistance from local Arcturian communities who view them as outsiders disrupting the old ways.

## Key Members

**[[Krafton Lilifeld]] (Lawful Good, Ordani Human, he/him)**

The current head of House Lilifeld, Krafton is a shrewd, hard-working statesman known as much for his relentless work ethic as for his legendary obsession with alcoholic drink. Not merely a connoisseur, Krafton treats winemaking and distillation as high art, personally overseeing new blends, commissioning experimental brews, and hosting long, boisterous tastings that double as political negotiations. Beneath his easy charm and wine-stained grin lies a calculating mind, one constantly working to expand Lilifeld’s influence across Ordain. He is often found in the Lockstop Winery in Lastgate, overseeing production.

**Torin Lilifeld (Neutral Good, Ordani Keth, he/him)**

Overseer of Lilifeld’s estates on the Isle of Kortos, Torin is a commanding presence who walks the fields in finely dyed Terracant wool, equal parts farmer, strategist, and businessman. He keeps Lilifeld’s harvests flowing smoothly, even in drought or political unrest, and is firmly at work in the background of the house.

**Serenna Ryes (Chaotic Good, Ordani Drakon, she/her)**

Serenna is a brilliant and celebrated chef, the visionary behind Lilifeld’s most renowned recipes. She is currently cooking in the lavish Odd Bites restaurant, which has a waiting list of nearly two weeks to get a table. Her dishes blend rare spices from the Lumek Firelands, exotic meats from the Lowlands, and vegetables from the Kithil Highlands. Serenna also personally trains many new Lilifeld members and is whispered to possess a recipe book sealed with a spell that only opens under the moon of Orbis.
