# House Salva

House Salva presents itself as a patron of high fashion, fine art, and rising entertainers, curating culture and taste for the entire city. But behind this refined image lies a more practical and far-reaching operation. Salva is the primary supplier of textiles and clothing across Ordain, ranging from everyday garments to elite gowns and shimmering, magically enhanced fabrics. Additionally, thanks to their access to canvas and a wide-reaching network of talent scouts, Salva is also a major, although somewhat secretive, producer and trader of maps. House Salva agents gather more than just artistic talent; they collect geographic and social intelligence used by the House to guide both fashion trends and strategic ventures.

- **Reputation is Everything:** With so much of House Salva's public face focused on areas like art and style that are more about subjective judgment than objective measure, reputation is seen as a tangible representation of trustworthiness.
- **Image First:** The appearance of things is important within Salva - this doesn't necessarily mean that Salva members are flashy, but they often make sure to have a touch of glamour about them in some way shape or form to mark themselves as influencers and tastemakers.
- **Proving Ground:** Within Ordain, Salva is considered one of the most important houses due to its cultural fascination with clothing and styles. While this serves the house as a whole, different House Salva members deal with this differently, with some trying extra hard to prove themselves at all times and others getting a chip on their shoulders about their worth being constantly questioned. As such, members of House Salva are often treated to a higher standard than other houses, but consequently are judged far more harshly for their missteps amongst their fellow members and the people of Ordain.

## Operations

Within House Salva, reputation and trustworthiness are essential and are measured through the use of a magical thread, given to prospective members of the house by their first mentor. The more a person has been vouched for by others in the house, the brighter the glow and the longer the thread, with newer members of the house trying their hardest to “shine” within the house both literally and figuratively. The thread is used differently by each member of the house, with some weaving it into clothing, others using it in the signage in their shop, and still others displaying the spool as is on their person. Any method is acceptable as long as the thread can be clearly seen.

## Key Members

**[[Nix Salvar]] (Neutral Good, Ordani Kiska, she/her)**

Nix Salvar is the current leader of House Salva, a name she adopted when she became its head. She was once a renowned actress who impressed the city’s residents with her emotional depth and played a wide range of roles in theater performances throughout the city. Using her considerable charm and business acumen, she has secured several major deals for House Salva, including a partnership with Bastilla to place agents on several of their ships so they can produce more accurate sea charts.

**[[Mistress Caberi]] (Lawful Good, Ordani Human, she/her)**

Mistress Caberi is a warm, friendly seamstress often surrounded by bolts of luxurious cloth, textiles in vibrant colors, and a large following of adoring fans. Her fame is so widespread that she has become a member of the Ordanite after being elected by the Hallows and through her connections with House Salva, and although she is not technically a member of the house, she is so closely connected that she might as well be. She welcomes all who enter her quaint shop in Gossamar with a radiant smile, where the aroma of fresh clean fabric mingles with soft laughter and cheerful conversations about the latest trends in Ordain. With her nimble fingers, she weaves magical threads of various hues, crafting exquisite and unique pieces of clothing for the city's most passionate fashionistas.

**[[Cherish Ellerie]] (Chaotic Good, Ordani Human, she/her)**

An up-and-comer who doesn't want to wait and earn her reputation piece by piece. Instead, she wants to grab it. Cherish befriends new artists in the city in unexpected locations, from the clubs of Kencleft to the altars at the Numinous Shrines, and pushes them to do big things, with her as their sponsor.

**Vujin Barriq (Lawful Neutral, Ordani Fej, he/him)**

Vujin ascended through House Salva because of his reputation as a scout and mapmaker, and has become the person within that house who almost exclusively deals with the [[Veiled Chain]] and adventures from the [[Anachraenum]].
