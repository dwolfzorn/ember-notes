# Anachraenum

Headquartered in Ordain, the Anachraenum is a relatively young organization compared to some of the city's other groups. For the most part, they see Ember as a chaotic but wondrous world where "treasure" means much more than material wealth. The carefully curated discoveries of the Anachraenum are numerous, and for nearly two hundred years, this storied group has successfully explored some of Ember's most perilous landscapes in search of legendary ruins from bygone ages. The vaults of the Anachraenum's All-Fable Keep and its archives are filled with all manner of arcane relics, artifacts of long-dead cultures, and curious unearthed oddities.

- **Enterprising Excavations:** Archaeology is perhaps what the Anachraenum is best known for, and the organization's unique approach to nontraditional dungeon diving stems from their tendency to search extensively for the strangest and most fascinating historical oddities with an almost complete disregard for rules or oversight.
- **Archive Operations:** The Anachraenum maintains the extensive Amaranthine Archives within the All-Fable Keep, a sprawling museum of relics, artifacts, and curated displays that is available for viewing year-round. Admission is offered at the cost of a small donation thanks to the generous sponsorship of wealthy elite patrons, typically members of the various Trading Houses.
- **Political Distance:** Both the leaders and members of the Anachraenum stay as far away from political matters in the city as possible. While All-Fable Keep is their home and headquarters, they tend to think of themselves as a more global organization and act independently in many aspects of their work.However, they do sometimes work with the Trading Houses, particularly House Cevher.
- **Wayfinders:** Most members abide by the same tenet: that cultural and intellectual enlightenment await those with enough bravery and wits to survive the most devious dungeons of Ember's ancient civilizations. As such, while the focus of the Anachraenum is officially scholarly, a great many adventuring types are drawn to join for the fame, glory, and prospect of uncovering treasure. These types are typically distinguished by the term "Wayfinders".

## Operations

Members of the Anachraenum rarely work alone and tend to form groups and parties within the guild itself to pursue their own interests, whether it’s archaeology, anthropology, or treasure hunting. These teams often conduct fieldwork in some of the most dangerous areas in the world, often facing logistical challenges, treacherous terrain, deadly monsters, creatures, and ancient traps. It is not uncommon for communication with groups in the field to be lost for many months, and some disappear entirely, presumed killed in action. Despite the danger and the drive to explore ancient sites in search of relics and artifacts, most Anachraenum members maintain a strong desire to preserve whatever artifacts they uncover, carefully documenting them.

Once back at the All-Fable Keep, the work continues as members categorize and analyze their discoveries. This involves meticulous cataloging within the Amaranthine Archives, where each artifact is studied for its historical, cultural, and magical properties. Some members spend their days restoring damaged relics or conducting research to uncover the history and events behind them.

The guild traditionally allows anyone to join, though membership is earned through years of dedication and proof of skill. Some members devote their lives to scholarly pursuits within the safety of the All-Fable Keep, studying ancient cultures and forgotten lore, while others take to the field, braving ruins, dungeons, and untamed lands in search of relics.

## Key Members

**[[Arcos Sarinland]] (NG, Ordani Keth, he/him)**

Arcos Sarinland was once a promising young ranger who joined the Anachraenum forty years ago and became a close friend of Darion Bastilla. He was widely celebrated for his participation in the Battle of the Throat, where a combined fleet led by Darion destroyed the Blood Barons' assembling ships in Golden Bay; however, he retired from active adventuring not long after. For thirty years, he taught and guided new members in Ordain, always maintaining a friendly yet troubled demeanor. Recently thrust into the guild's leadership after Fernis Ossa declined the nomination, he faces a number of increasing challenges as Ordain slips further into unrest.

**[[Fernis Ossa]] (LG, Ordani Ashka, she/her)**

Fernis Ossa is one of the most well-known and admired Anachraenum members living in Ordain. She led a group of adventurers called the Skywarders many years ago before effectively becoming the envoy and deputy to the previous guildmaster, Veron Longspear. She spent many years among the people of Ordain, and her adventurous spirit is matched only by her dedication to her friends and comrades. Fernis often organizes gatherings and events in Ordain and travels to many nearby friendly cities like Luen, Wick, and the Lumek city of Hearth. She has a genuine interest in the stories and lives of the people she meets.

**[[Adelyne Goss]] (NG, Waerd Human, she/her)**

Loremistress Adelyne Goss is a sarcastic and sharp character, and despite her extensive physical injuries sustained after battling a dragon deep in the Duskflare Jungles many years ago, she remains an intensely insightful and productive scholar and librarian. She is also extremely proud of her Waerd heritage and wears a combination of traditional Waerd clothing interwoven with ancient Sedyri trinkets.

**[[Haruspex]] (LG, Ordani Signborn, they/them)**

One of the most famed Wayfinders of the Anachraenum currently operating in the Arctus Plateau is the ranger known as Haruspex, a ruthless solo adventurer whose unparalleled scouting skills and prowess with a longbow have earned them the reputation as one of Ember’s most successful treasure hunters.
