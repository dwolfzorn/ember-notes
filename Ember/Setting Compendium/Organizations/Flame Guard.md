# Flame Guard

## Operations

Within the Flame Guard, which is the principal organization responsible for the protection of the Lumek, there are a number of ranks depending on experience and skill. The Flame Guard is not composed solely of warriors. It includes healers, engineers, fire-mages, scouts, and even farmers trained to defend their settlements with torches and Lumarin steel blades. It embodies the unified front of Lumek culture: everyone contributes, and all roles are vital. In the eyes of Lumé, there is no "lesser" sacrifice.

| High Command | Pyre Lord | Commander of the Flame, the supreme leader of the Flame Guard. Oversees all crusades against the undead. Wields ancient relics of fire and is a close personal friend with Lumé. |
| --- | --- | --- |
| High Command | Marshal | Responsible for strategy, alliances, and the coordination of Flame Guard in the Lumek Homelands. |
| Senior Officers | Flamebearer | An elite rank given to seasoned warriors or mages. Blessed directly by Lumé. Often sent on solo missions to destroy undead strongholds or targets deep within the Moiran Lands. |
| Senior Officers | Captain | Commands multiple Fireteams. Leads major offensives against vampire strongholds and undead hordes within the Moiran Lands. |
| Junior Officers | Cinder Warden | Leaders of the various smaller forts, keeps, or defensive placements in the Ash Peaks. |
| Junior Officers | Redcloak | Leads small units in battles or missions. Usually oversees a Fireteam. |
| Soldiers | Crusader | Veteran soldier or spellcaster. Trusted in battle and often assigned special tasks or front-line leadership. |
| Soldiers | Torchbearer | Newly promoted soldier. Carries the literal and symbolic torch into battle. Often assigned to defensive duties or patrols. |
| Soldiers | Firebrand | Fanatical, untrained members who often throw themselves into the front lines. |
| Trainees | Ashrunner | Scouts and soldiers in training that are often used for carrying messages in battle. |
