# Sanguinaries

The Sanguinaries are far smaller in number than the Cindaric Sages, with little influence beyond a handful of scattered pockets across the known world. They lack organized defenders or formal security, relying instead on improvised measures and the vigilance of individual members. Their hierarchy is loose, centered on a symbolic leader whose role is more to guide than to command. What strengthens them, however, is their deep connection to magic. Many of their number are skilled sorcerers and druids, and the order as a whole maintains a profound tie to the ancient and powerful Casia, and the realm of Primordis, drawing on that bond to further their studies and fuel their cause.

- **Heart of the World:** Like the Cindaric Sages, the Sanguinaries believe all life in the cosmos exists as part of the unconscious will of the Heart of Ember—the creationary force at the center of the world.
- **The Burden of Truth:** The Sanguinaries differ from the Sages in one crucial way: they believe the Heart of Ember is dying. This conviction is the cornerstone of their faith, shaping their worldview and driving their actions. Each member must grapple with this shared burden and the responsibility to act against such an end. How best to achieve this varies widely, but all pursue it with relentless determination.
- **Defiant Independence:** Born from suppression by the Cindaric Sages, the Sanguinaries place great value on freedom of belief and expression. Their order is highly dispersed, with members scattered across the known world—even as far as the distant Tayan Empire. Leadership provides little formal doctrine, leaving individuals and groups to interpret their cause as they see fit.
- **Radical Elements:** A small faction within the Sanguinaries believes awareness of Ember’s decline must be spread at all costs. These zealots push for decisive, often disruptive action, and pursue it whenever they can. Among their own, they are tolerated as fanatics; to outsiders, they are little more than doomsayers and madmen.

## Operations

Though small and scattered, the Sanguinaries take an active role in defending Ember from threats they see as symptoms of its decline. Their efforts vary widely from cell to cell, but certain patterns are common. Many devote themselves to the destruction of the undead, whom they regard as abominations that gnaw at the strength of the living world. Others turn their focus to the encroaching corruption of monsters and threats from beyond the cosmos. Some Sanguinaries speak of combating the Great Darkness or one of its forms in many fragmented terms: the Hollow, the Famine, the Blight, but what they mean by this is unclear.

Most do not set out to become Sanguinaries. Members are usually drawn in after uncovering fragments of truth about the Heart of Ember or being approached by a wandering believer. Initiation is informal, like an invitation to a ritual, a trusted secret, or a test to destroy an undead or a monster. Each path is personal, with commitment born from conviction rather than oath, leaving the order diverse but united by a shared burden.

## Key Members

**[[Avwynn Taol]] (Neutral Good, Maziran Human/Nir'ae, she/her)**

Avwynn Taol is the iron heart of the Sanguinaries, a mystic-scholar whose power and presence command attention wherever she goes. Exceptionally well-read and insatiably curious, she has spent her life chasing the truths buried in Ember’s histories, cultures, and legacies, piecing together patterns others overlook.
