## Driftborn Nomads

Far in the frozen reaches of the north dwell the Fikost, the merciless hunters of the deep and masters of the icebound seas. Made up almost entirely of the Wirrun, few other Ancestries can endure the killing cold this far north, and even the hardy [[Vrjnhar]] must rely on thick furs and enchanted warming stones to survive. The Fikost, however, are a culture shaped entirely by a hostile world; their culture, faith, and very blood honed by centuries of enduring the icy wastes and an endless winter.

The Fikost way of life is built upon the hunt. They stalk the colossal beasts that roam the Hoarfrost Sea and the frozen plains that surround them on the continent of [[Aterica]], from massive schools of razor-toothed fish to the great Whalufa and even [[Leviathans]] and darker horrors that dwell in the deep. Their skill at drawing such creatures to the surface is unmatched, blending powerful sea-magic that can lure or bind what no ordinary line or net could hold.

Unlike most peoples of Aterica, the Fikost do not have any true settlements upon the mainland. Instead, they live aboard vast living ships, semi-sentient vessels grown from enchanted ice, bones, and Deepstrand. These ships are tended like kin, fed and maintained over generations, their hulls singing in low, mournful tones as they drift through the polar mists. Each has its own temperament, slow and cold-minded, yet fiercely loyal to its crew. A ship may choose its captain, bond with its family, and even refuse to sail if dishonored or wronged. To lose a ship is to lose a treasured friend and Fikost mourn such losses as they would the death of a family member.

The Fikost often hunt the tundra and the frigid depths alike, bringing their kills back to their floating ships anchored along the ice-sheathed coast. The mainland itself is too perilous as packs of monstrous Trolls, Abyssal monsters, and Mur’kaal roam the wastes, slaughtering all they find. The Fikost despise the Trolls, yet they are too pragmatic to fight them openly. They know that large-scale slaughter of Trolls can trigger explosive breeding cycles an old, grim truth passed down through generations. Thus, the Fikost avoid conflict when they can, vanish into blizzards and the icy seas.
