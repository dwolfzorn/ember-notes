# Languages

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Lore Summary
>
> The people of Ember speak many languages.
>
> 1. The most common language in Ember is literally called Common, though among its creators, the Ordani, it is actually known as Coins. This language is used because it is both a derivative of the older human language of Arcden, which is still spoken in many places, and incorporates many words from the ancient Aedir, making it understandable at a basic level to a wide range of cultures.
> 2. Most people speak the language of their specific culture and community. This is because 'Common' is considered the trading language, and most cultures in Ember are multicultural. This means there are very few languages tied to ancestries or creatures, although these do exist. Notably, dragons speak their own secret language.

It's said that the Inner Realm of Signara was the first place in Ember where words were both spoken and understood for the first time. Over time, this knowledge extended to other Inner Realms and eventually to the entire World of Ember. Today’s dialects are echoes of earlier forms, shaped either by the intentions of gods or fractured by the violent tremors of world-altering cataclysms such as the Shattering. In some areas, a single word can carry the melodic resonance of a celestial blessing, inherited from Luxarum, while in others, the language is rough and sparse, born from the desperate necessity of survival and the need for faster communication.
