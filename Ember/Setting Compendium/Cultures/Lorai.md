## Legacy of the Sun

Beneath the jagged peaks of northern [[Oakengarde]], the Lorai dwell in deep, hidden enclaves, remnants of the ancient Aedir civilization and the last true heirs of the Sunfire Empire. Their cities are carved from stone and lit by glowing crystals and carefully tended conduits fueled by the Lifeforce of Ember. Outsiders rarely gain entry, and those Lorai who venture into the world above are bound by oaths of blood and magic to conceal the location of their homes.

The Lorai are a people burdened with shame and responsibility. They carry the legacy of [[The Shattering]], the cataclysm that fractured their once world-spanning empire and scarred the cosmos. Unlike simple isolationists, the Lorai seek to understand the disaster and, where possible, repair the damage it caused. Their efforts are focused on restoring the moon of [[Akon]], which was shattered by the cataclysm. While the task itself is daunting, they remain undeterred. For the Lorai, this task borders on a spiritual redemption as they pursue the knowledge and practical means to mend the cosmos and redeem the overwhelming failures of their ancestors.

Lorai society is defined by study, ritual, and the careful preservation of knowledge. Scholars of the Shattering dedicate their lives to understanding history, magic, cosmology, and, in particular, the [[Heart of Ember]]. They meticulously record both arcane and mundane observations, seeking patterns that might illuminate the path to Akon’s restoration. Libraries, observatories, and underground laboratories form the heart of every enclave, while art and craftsmanship, particularly metalwork, sun-forged glass, and stone-grown architecture, honor the aesthetic legacy of the Sunfire Empire.

Complementing the Lorai’s scholarly pursuits are the Shattered Blades, a warrior order forged from guilt and determination. Named in memory of Aedir’s failure during the Shattering, they are tasked with defending the enclaves, confronting Abyssal threats, and safeguarding knowledge vital to the restoration of Akon. Shattered Blades train in both martial skill and celestial-imbued techniques. Their weapons, often fractured and reforged from relics of the empire, symbolize the world’s current broken state and the hope that it can be remade. Many rituals accompany their combat: ceremonial duels, the lighting of sunfire beacons, and meditative practices designed to harmonize body, mind, and the cosmic forces they strive to restore. While fearsome in battle, their actions are guided by a code of restraint, humility, and devotion to the penance of their people.
