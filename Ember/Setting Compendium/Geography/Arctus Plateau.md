# Arctus Plateau

#### Under Construction

This page is currently being developed and subject to change in future updates.

- **Cultures**: The primary culture group in the region is [[Arcturian]], but pockets of [[Kithil]] and [[Bejak]]. In the city of Ordain the primary culture group is [[Ordani]], a metropolitan offshoot of Arcturian culture influenced by the steady foreign trade the city sees.
- **Settlements**: Numerous small communities, towns, and villages dot the region. Major population centers include Ordaine, Seawall, Redrak, and Arcturel.
- **Economy**: Agriculture and Livestock are primary industries in the region, textiles, stone, and gems make up secondary industries, all of which see regular export from major ports such as Ordain and Seawall. Ordain stands as the largest economic power in the region.
- **Politics**: While numerous smaller political organizations and ideologies exist, the dominant political power in the plateau is the city-state of [[Ordain]], which exerts considerable pressure on surrounding communities by way of the organizations that operate under its auspices, including the Trading Houses, and [[Cindaric Sages]]. While smaller organizations exist and wield considerable power in their respective realms, they are considered secondary to the influence of Ordain.

## Environment

The Arctus Plateau features extraordinary diversity of terrains including mountains and highlands, windswept grasslands and forbidding forests, down to lush coastal wetlands and rocky oceanside cliffs. Despite this variance, the region shares some unifying qualities: The land is often rugged, and rocky, with plateaus and cliffs constantly breaking the skyline, forcing inhabitants to take winding routes as they travel. The region itself is even surrounded by jagged, inhospitable terrain that makes entry and exit difficult outside of specific locations.

Canyons and valleys carved by ancient events both geological and mythical cut across the plateau, while sprawling plains and river systems define the region’s more habitable expanses. Across these varied landscapes environmental hazards such as flooding, wildfires, and rockfalls are common, creating countless challenges for the locals to overcome.

The land’s climate is predominantly shaped by its elevation and ruggedness, giving rise to distinct dry summers in the majority of biomes, with pockets of microclimates influenced by nearby bodies of water, sudden elevation changes, and the residual effects of mystic upheaval.

## Ecology

The flora and fauna of the Arctus Plateau are marked by resilience, adaptability, and, magical traits that grant them unique edges in their respective environments. The vast environmental diversity of the plateau demands the beings that inhabit it to be equally as diverse or perish when meeting some predator that has. This makes for an unpredictable, and often perilous region for the beings that call it home.

Plant life across the region is dominated by hardy grasses and shrubs adapted to their localized biomes, but capable of spreading far and wide no matter the conditions. Here, tall grasses thrive in fire-scorched savannas, and crowd out weaker plants in magically tainted forests, while the verdant river valleys, deltas, and lakes become overgrown with wild plants taking advantage of the rich soil and ample water.

Animal life is equally diverse and competitive, with each biome hosting species adapted to its specific challenges and unique dangers under threat of eradication. Predators are prominent and specialized here, with the boundaries between biomes only serving to create new opportunities for these creatures to adapt their hunting methods to the expanded prey on offer.

## Lore

The Arctus Plateau was settled in pieces and waves across the centuries. Numerous societies have risen and fallen in this time, including the ancient Aediri, Shent, and Sedyri people who all had settlements in this region at one point or another before their respective collapses.

Records, ruins, and burial sites still persist in far-flung corners of the Plateau and speak of settlers that arrived thousands of years ago on the heels of mystical cataclysm and upheaval to claim pieces of the plateau for themselves.
