# Human

Humans are direct descendants and inheritors of the [[Jarn]] and thus take on many of their physical characteristics. They are, however, incredibly varied across the surface world, with many regional differences depending on environments, history, or close connections to other ancestries with which they may have integrated or mixed. In many ways, a "standard" human does not exist. Skin, hair, and eye color have a truly massive range of tones with every type and combination possible. That said, some things set Humans apart; compared to many other ancestries, humans are the most adaptable to the world's hardships and have excellent stamina and endurance.

At a basic level, Humans follow the same shape and form as their Jarn predecessors, with two arms and legs, five fingers on their hands, and five toes on their feet. The only actual difference between the Jarn and Humans outwardly is the reduction of hair across the body, but apart from that, they are remarkably similar. Because Humans don't tend to have any distinguishing characteristics like other ancestries, such as sweeping horns, intricate scales, or thick fur, they are often considered tame and uninteresting by some. However, due to their drive to expand and explore, Humans frequently encounter other ancestries and incorporate them into their cultures and nations, increasing diversity and becoming even more powerful as a result. This may explain the remarkable expansion and growth of human kingdoms since the events of the Shattering, and if they continue, they will one day surpass the [[Aedir]].

### Biology

Human biology is not very different from their predecessors the Jarn; their inner organs, such as a single heart, lungs, and digestive system, are the same. Human skin is thin and easy to cut or damage, and their bones are strong but generally not as strong as those of other ancestors. Humans, however, have a distinct lack of body hair, which comes with the ability to sweat profusely during exertion. This means that Humans can regulate their body temperatures much more efficiently than other ancestries and are capable of incredible feats of stamina and endurance. In fact, Humans have been known to outrun almost all other creatures and are the last ancestries to remain fighting during battles.

However, another key difference from other ancestries on Ember is the Human soul, which is often described as "Radiant" by anyone who studies such things. A Human soul is curiously potent, and Humans seem to have a much closer connection to the [[Heart of Ember]]; this often manifests as an intense passion for life, strong willpower, and a desire to experience everything possible in their lives. This, combined with an extremely tough and resolute mental fortitude, is one of the Human's greatest strengths.

In rare instances, and somehow linked to a Human's radiant soul, an individual can experience a supercharged boost to their bodies, known as a "Blaze". This ability is very difficult to control and not at all common but it empowers a Human with vastly increased strength, speed, and magical abilities for a short duration in dangerous or life-threatening situations. Humans call this Super-Adrenaline, and it is responsible for some of the most legendary feats imaginable, such as the slaying of [[Dragons]], [[Shard Gods]] and monsters.

### Cultural Connections

Human history began in the Aran Peninsula around three thousand years ago. Since then, they have spread across most of the continent of [[Aterica]] and a large part of [[Kessia]] as well. The ancient city-states of the [[Kelmezian]] are the oldest known settlements. Many cultures now claim a distant origin back to those surviving Humans who stood against the darkness during the [[The Shattering]]. Today, the aggressive and constantly expanding [[Tayan]] is a prominent example of just how overwhelming the Human ancestry has become. The gleaming metropolis of [[Ordain]] is another more peaceful example, but it is equally remarkable that such a great city has risen in such a short period of time. It also seems to be a cultural successor to the Aedir in ages past, a vast melting pot of ancestries and cultures all living together and linking the known world.

Many other existing cultures and ancestries of the world are astounded by Human expansion, and most also view them as possibly the greatest force for good in the world, potentially capable of conquering the countless threats and challenges of the Ember Cosmos. Many such cultures are enthusiastic and eager to work with Humans; the [[Oaken]] especially are more than happy to teach Humans survival tricks and help them in their struggles, viewing them as a younger version of themselves but with greater drive.
