# Signborn

The Signborn were created by the Daemos from Nir’ae for purposes that are as of yet unknown, but their fiendish influence can be seen in the Signborn’s mix of the ethereal and the dangerous. Signborn all possess at least one additional set of limbs, which may be incorporeal, ethereal, or both. Their thin pointed horns and long whip-like tails can appear ghostlike and hazy at their edges, a contrast to their vibrant hair of cyan and purple. Outside of their extra limbs, their skin is muted, often in varying shades of gray

Signborn limbs may be ethereal, but they are also effective – though some are incorporeal, most are able to be used either physically or magically, if not both. They are also capable fighters – many have delicately thin but dangerously sharp claws instead of nails, or fanged teeth that are easily wielded as weapons. These, in addition to the angularity of their faces, can give them a harsh and foreboding appearance despite the hazy ghostlike nature of their extremities.

### Biology

Signborn's internal organs are comparable to their ancestors, the Nir'ae, and other descendants of the ancient Jarn. Any internal systems that connect to their ethereal limbs simply fade away at the connection point with the rest of the body, with no loss of functionality. In fact, the ethereal limbs of Signborn are generally immune to physical harm, though magical damage to these same limbs can affect them drastically, causing excruciating pain.

Some Signborn bear markings that echo those on their original Daemos creators, showing up as vivid patterns or symbols, while others gain additional ethereal limbs, including limbs that seem almost disconnected from the main body of the Signborn in question. These are usually arms, but can manifest as tails, legs, or even heads. These connections to the original Daemos are not passed on through family lines, instead appearing randomly on Signborn at birth. A few scholars have attempted to determine a pattern to the manifestations, but they have yet to find any definitive connection other than general Signborn ancestry.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Signborn Heritage
>
> Sexual reproduction between different ancestries is very possible and can result in children with mixed ancestry. Players can play as characters with mixed ancestry, but this requires consultation with the GM and may not be ideal for first-time players or GMs. See [[Biology]] for more information.
>
> Signborn are Humanoid and thus able to reproduce with other Humanoid ancestries. Though not all with Signborn ancestry have ethereal limbs, it is generally thought wise to have a magically-trained healer on hand if another ancestry has a child with a Signborn, as the ethereal nature of their limbs can make birthing complex.

### Cultural Connections

Signborn's visible ties to their Daemos creators can make others view them with apprehension or suspicion, and their numbers are small and widespread enough that they have never created a culture of their own, instead generally choosing to adapt to the cultures around them. Signborn can generally be found anywhere on Ember, and while some have made a name for themselves as adventurers and warriors, others disguise themselves with magic or clothing so as not to invite questions about their appearance or intentions from strangers.
