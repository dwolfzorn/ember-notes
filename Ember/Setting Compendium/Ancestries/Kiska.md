# Kiska

Though it has been eons since the Kiska left their Casia ancestors in the Inner Realm of Primordis and came to Ember, they still retain many connections to their ancestral roots. Physically, they have several cat-like features, including furred ears and shoulders, claw-like fingers, long thick tails, and mane-like hair. This hair is often worn long to showcase both its glowing roots and dramatic length, and is generally found in shades of red, yellow, and orange that match the colors of Kiska eyes.

Beyond their appearance, all Kiska retain some connection to the shadow magic common on Primordis, giving them the ability to fade into the shadows or blend into a crowd without being noticed, despite their striking looks. Many are drawn to spellcasting, feeling a sense of familiarity when they practice magic, especially with spells that utilize darkness, shadow, or illusion. Even those Kiska who do not use magic have a general sense of when it is being used around them and an instinct for when they might be under threat, though this is rarely specific or targeted enough for them to pinpoint its exact source.

### Biology

Kiska are humanoid, though they have several adaptations that make them both tough and nimble. Their hyperflexible joints allow them to turn and bend in ways that many others cannot, and they are capable of short bursts of speed when needed, though this does not often translate into long-term endurance. This, like their cat-like physical features, is thought to be a result of their Casia ancestors, who are known ambush predators. Kiska also have light-sensitive eyes, which give them the ability to see well in the darkness and dim light, and, for some, see the resonance of magic in the world when it is intense enough, as happens during moon zeniths or in the presence of powerful artifacts.

Though Kiska often stand out on Ember, thanks to their relative rarity, boldly colored hair, and long tails, they have the ability to obscure their physical form when they wish to, blurring the edges of their bodies and hair to blend into the shadows and becoming almost invisible to those who are not fully focusing on them.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Kiska Heritage
>
> Sexual reproduction between different ancestries is very possible and can result in children with mixed ancestry. Players can play as characters with mixed ancestry, but this requires consultation with the GM and may not be ideal for first-time players or GMs. See [[Biology]] for more information.
>
> Kiska are compatible with other Humanoids on Ember and can reproduce with any other Humanoid ancestry. Their relative rarity and occasional standoffishness mean that little is known about the impact of Kiska heritage on their descendants.

### Cultural Connections

Kiska have been on Ember for generations, since the days of the Shattering, but have a small enough population that they have always been more likely to blend into other cultures than create a set culture of their own. Their connection to the magic of the Inner Planes, which few others share, can also give Kiska the sense that they are more evolved than other ancestries and should observe them rather than truly integrating with them, though this attitude differs widely from one Kiska to the next.
