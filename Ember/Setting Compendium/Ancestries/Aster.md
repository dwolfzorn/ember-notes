# Aster

At the moment of their birth, some individuals are altered by the life force of Ember, changing at a fundamental and biological level into a completely new form. Though these individuals are collectively known as Aster, each change is different, and can occur to any living thing on Ember, whether humanoid or not, and whether sapient or not. Some Aster are changed cosmetically, while others are altered internally, but all have been fundamentally transformed in some way.

Other than the fact of their transformation at birth and the sapience held by all Aster regardless of their origin, there is no rule or observable pattern in how an Aster has been transformed or why. Speculation about either is considered to be a questioning of Ember itself and thus verboten in nearly all polite society.

### Biology

The biology of Aster varies widely, and can contain biological variations and alterations found nowhere else on Ember, or simply not found in the original biology of the Aster in question. While some Aster share biological traits, no two are ever exactly alike. Even in the extremely rare case of Aster twins, each is affected differently by the lifeforce of Ember and changed in their own unique way.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Aster Heritage
>
> Aster reproduction differs from one individual to the next, but Aster are generally sexually compatible with humanoids. Being Aster is not an inherited or inheritable trait — the children of an Aster inherit the biological traits of that Aster's parents.

### Cultural Connections

Aster can be found across cultures, ancestries, and locations on Ember. Reaction to them varies widely. Some cultures view being Aster as a blessing, and others a curse. Some welcome Aster among them, while others view them with suspicion. Some Aster remain with their communities of birth, while others leave to find and create new definitions of family.

As Aster are rare, they are usually found with those of other ancestries, backgrounds, and cultures, though there are occasional small settlements and neighborhoods within larger cities that predominately contain Aster from across Ember, drawn together by the shared experience of having been made fundamentally different than those around them. In  AS2512A massive earthquake hits the Arctus Plateau and the city of [[Ordain]], centred below the city in the Shard Caverns, the quake topples many buildings and kills roughly a thousand citizens across the city. New building codes are enforced and magical wards are employed across the city to protect against such events in the future. Building repairs are still ongoing for some of the larger structures damaged by the event. After the quake, Aster children are born more frequently in the following decade. This event comes to be known by a few names within Ordain, including the Stonehollow Quake.
