# Vrjnhar

Oakengarde is a region filled with deadly creatures and plants, many of which prowl beneath the region's icy mountain slopes waiting for prey. To survive in this harsh land, the Vrjnhar body has adapted over time, becoming stocky and well-muscled, with redundant organs, thick fur and beards that are resistant to physical and magical damage, and a spine with an exaggerated spinal curve that puts the vrjnhar's shoulders in the path of danger instead of their more vulnerable necks. This resilience, as well as their stamina, make Vrjnhar some of the toughest humanoids in all of Ember. Even their beards, which they usually keep meticulously trimmed, cannot be cut with normal barbering tools, requiring gemstones or specially designed tools to maintain.

### Biology

Vrjnhar have many biological adaptations for surviving the extremely harsh environment of Oakengard. Their thick leathery skin is difficult to pierce even with sharpened steel and their bones are denser than average, with greater amounts of iron, making them tough and very durable. Within their bodies Vrjnhar possess twin hearts, three lungs and a fully encased rib cage and stomach, protected by small bone plates that allow for a full range of athletic movements but acting almost like chainmail under the skin.

Their digestion is equally strong, with an additional sulfuric gall bladder that produces powerful acids, breaking down even the foulest or most inedible materials. As a result, Vrjnhar are able to eat foods that others might find unpalatable, dangerous, or deadly, and often take a heavy hand with spices when preparing food. There are varieties of hot pepper, for example, that have such a powerful effect on other ancestries that only vrjnhar are able to truly appreciate their taste.

A Vrjnhar beard is usually thick and full in appearance, and has the same tensile strength as steel. It, like their fur, also has anti-magical effects, with spells sometimes (but not always) dissipating on contact.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Vrjnhar Heritage
>
> Sexual reproduction between different ancestries is very possible and can result in children with mixed ancestry. Players can play as characters with mixed ancestry, but this requires consultation with the GM and may not be ideal for first-time players or GMs. See [[Biology]] for more information.
>
> Vrjnhar are compatible with other Humanoids on Ember and can reproduce with any other Humanoid ancestry. The hardiness of the vrjnhar manifests differently in each offspring, but many have either redundant lungs or a larger than average heart.

### Cultural Connections

Most Vrjnhar in Ember belong to the Oaken culture, which is centered in the continent Oakengard. Much as Oakengard has shaped the Vrjnhar ancestry, it is also shaped the Oaken culture, which reveres survival, adventure, monster hunting, and martial prowess. Beyond Oakengarde, many Vrjnhar leave Oakengarde and travel, secure in their physical hardiness to keep them safe as they travel. While many Vrjnhar eventually return to Oakengard after their adventuring, craving the challenge of this dangerous land, some settle in small groups in other places and among other cultures. One of the most long-standing groups of this type is an enclave in Ordain, which has maintained a strong trading relationship with the Oaken for hundreds of years.
