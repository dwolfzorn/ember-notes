# Zeph

Zeph are magical winds that have coalesced into human form, made from the primeval gales of the hollow Wind Moon, Aura. Their semi-corporeal and semi-translucent form can vary, but is generally soft and light, with the clothing a Zeph wears and the items they carry creating much of their physical silhouette. A Zeph's core body is generally storm-cloud gray, but their extremities can be a wider range of colors and illuminations. Often, the brightest color is seen in their hair, which extends from behind their mask in elaborate (and sometimes gravity-defying) configurations.

Each Zeph is born with a mask, which changes as the Zeph themself does. The masks may appear to others to hover in the air in front of a Zeph, but it is in fact the core of who each Zeph is, both biologically and spiritually. While Zeph are corporeal enough to take damage from direct physical contact, they are particularly vulnerable if their mask is damaged in any way, and do their best to deflect or avoid any blows that would harm it, often using the wind magic that comes from their connection with Aura. Zeph can also use their masks to express emotion, shifting them at will to reflect the Zeph's current personality.

### Biology

A Zeph’s mask is their source of physical constitution and spiritual potency. The more damaged or exhausted a Zeph becomes, the more insubstantial their physical form generally becomes, but the mask endures. The mask is believed to help Zeph recover physically from injury, as Zeph are especially vulnerable if their masks are damaged in any way, and their masks remain behind when they die, unlike the rest of their body, which dissipates over time.

The form behind a Zeph's masks is partially translucent and partially non-corporeal — a reflection of the wind magic that they possess from their link with Aura. While they have enough physical form to wear clothing, carry items, and take damage, Zeph's ability to shift the edges of their swirling form can make them difficult targets to hit during combat scenarios.

Unlike many of Ember’s humanoid ancestries, Zeph do not reproduce naturally. New Zeph generally generate spontaneously from the winds of Aura, finding their way to the city of Conundrum by instinct. Zeph propagation on Ember has been noted to have happened on Ember, but is not well known, other than the fact that their emergence generally corresponds with the Wind Moon's Zenith.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Zeph Heritage
>
> Sexual reproduction between different ancestries is very possible and can result in children with mixed ancestry. Players can play as characters with mixed ancestry, but this requires consultation with the GM and may not be ideal for first-time players or GMs. See [[Biology]] for more.
>
> Zeph are not corporeal in a traditional sense, and thus not able to reproduce with any other ancestries, though some cultures do have legends that revolve around the star-crossed romance between a Zeph and a more corporeal ancestry. Depicting the offspring of a Zeph would likely be best expressed via an [[Aster]] ancestry.

### Cultural Connections

The majority of Zeph occupy the city-state of Conundrum, located at the hollow core of the Wind Moon, but in the modern age, some have sought their purpose elsewhere, with some exploring the depths of the Inner Realm of Primordis and others traveling across the face of Ember itself. As they occur on Ember fairly rarely, and often come to Ember in pursuit of something that they could not find in Conundrum or on Aura more broadly, Zeph on Ember generally either live solitary lives or focus on making connections with those of other ancestries and cultural backgrounds on Ember, using their personal connections as the measure of how they should live their lives and interact with the world around them.
