# The Knight & The Dragon

- Origin: Between 500 & 1000 AS. Human
- Knowledge: Well Known by [[Tayan]], [[Kelmezian]], [[Cascilian]], [[Lumek]], & [[Ordani]].

In the legend, Isota, a Knight of Verador kills a [[Cruel Dragons]] in the forests of Vanthar. The story goes that the Dragon inhabited the mountains nearby and would regularly fly across the area, attacking the villages and peoples indiscriminately. The villagers had begun to leave offerings to the Dragon hoping that it would be satisfied with livestock and whatever meager treasures they could find. Alas the Dragon was only emboldened and landed with a great roar in front of the village of Kayhurn demanding that once a month the villagers bring it young adolescents as a sacrifice. One day a young knight named Isota, rode north and heard the horrific stories of the dragon’s atrocities and proclaimed that they would slay the beast.

As Isota entered the Forests of Vanthar they came across a large glade where one of the young villagers lay bound awaiting the coming of the dragon. The villager asked Isota to flee and leave them to their fate but Isota refused and hid between the trees. When the dragon landed in the clearing, savouring the sacrifice in front of them, Isota charged from the side and managed to piece the thick dragon scales with a long spear of iron. This wounded the dragon but did not kill it and it roared in fury, spinning its wing and long tail around like a whip. A great battle ensued and though wounded, Isota emerged triumphant, cutting off the head of the Dragon and securing it to their mounts saddle* They returned to Kaythun in victory and were given a lavish celebration.**

**In some versions of the legend Isota removes the head but it bursts into flame, igniting the body of the dragon which burns down to blackened bone.*

***Some early versions of the story claim that Isota marries the young villager they rescued, which caused some dispute in the region, as the villager was the village chieftain's son or daughter and gave Kaythun a considerable boost in prestige which other cities removed/ignored in later versions.*
