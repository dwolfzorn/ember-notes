# Auria Fisher

- Occupation: Adventurer, Scholar
- Period: (2494- Present)
- Affiliation: [[Kirargy Knights]]
- Ancestry: Human
- Famous Works: Shards of Power, The Ancients of Ember.

Auria Fisher is an independent scholar and adventurer from the [[Tayan]] Empire. She is an associate member of the [[Kirargy Knights]] Order of the Spear. She is accompanied on her adventures by her companion, Jerius Steel, a senior member of the Knights. She is an extraordinarily charismatic and dynamic researcher passionate about discovering lost information and gathering spoken tales and sagas from local inhabitants. She has been active for only twelve years but possesses a skill or lucky knack for tracking down obscure and fascinating subjects, revelations, and lost relics. She is also a powerful sorceress who follows the edicts of well.. [[Laeora]].

### Personality

Auria is quickly becoming a celebrated hero of the Tayan Empire, and her daring exploits are the stuff of rowdy tavern songs and spoken of among the ranks of various Tayan Armies for her determined and unrelenting spirit in the face of danger. She was assigned male at birth but transitioned with the support of her parents sometime in her adolescent years, embracing a vibrant, joyful demeanor, and she is happy to indulge her fans with exuberant expressions and catchphrases.

### Connections

*I first became aware of Auira not long after her nineteenth birthday. Her prayers and requests for knowledge intrigued me, especially considering her station in the Tayan Empire. Few would have dared to reach out to me as she did. Our bond has become closer over the years, and I do my best to protect our relationship. If The [[Calyn Kariset]] were to discover that Auria is one of my followers, I'm sure she would be put to death as a traitor. Still, she seems unconcerned by the danger and continues her exploits with my aid occasionally. Jerius may be aware of our connection, but so far keeps this information to himself.*
