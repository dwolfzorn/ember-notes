# Mayis

- Keywords: Water, Survial, Storms
- Ideal: Patience
- Age: Countless Ages
- Other Names: The Tempest Moon, The Fathomless, The Eternal Tide

Eternal grey, white, and blue storms shroud Mayis in a permanent cloak of howling, unstoppable hurricanes. Yet beneath this turbulent atmosphere lies a dark, roiling ocean, broken by shattered floating islands that rise and fall with the moon’s heaving waves. Immense and untamed, Mayis is a perilous, elemental expanse where otherworldly water elementals swim through the currents, and enormous behemoths lie in wait behind floating underwater islands of ice.

Mayis is a world defined by a singular element: water. Its single vast ocean is a wild and lethal expanse where survival requires not only contending with crushing pressures and unpredictable currents but also with dangerous denizens of the deep. Though the moon has remained untamed throughout its existence, it has long been home to the Typhic, smaller cousins of the moon's other legendary inhabitants, the Behemonths. While most of the Typhic population resides within the monumental city of Tohr, anchored to the back of the Elemental Sovereign [[Areyter]], others survive in nomadic floating villages that drift through the dark waters. These outposts are often tucked away in the jagged cracks and ice-chasms of the moon’s wandering frozen islands, hidden from the larger predators.

To mortals on Ember, Mayis embodies both awe and caution. Its relentless storms and vast, unpredictable oceans are seen as symbols of the undying strength and power of the waves. Sailors, travelers, and seers speak of the moon as a place that tests the mind and spirit alike, offering insight and reward only to those willing to wait, observe, and respect its rhythms.
