# Primordis

- Keywords: Shadow, Illusion, Predatory
- Ideal: Cunning
- Age: Countless Years
- Other Names:Realm of Capricious Shadows, Shadowlands, The First Forest

Primordis is a violently dangerous realm for mortals. Ancient entities, deadly creatures and the realm itself treat all visitors as prey, and you are just as likely to be hunted as you are to become a toy in some twisted game. Like the other Inner Realms of [[Luxarum]] and[[Signara]], Primordis is the pinnacle of an otherworldly realm. It feels utterly alien for any mortals who dare tread its paths. Fortunately, it lies so far from the [[World of Ember]] that it has remained isolated and mysterious throughout every age of the Cosmos, and mortal visitors or explorers are practically unheard of.

Mortals connected Primorids and the Blooming season long ago as Primordis slowly drifted above the surface world and Luxarum waned. Primordis is also visible in the night sky, glimmering with a soft red glow roughly the same size as one of the Moons of Ember, highlighting its importance to ordinary people. In truth, all the Inner Realms are much larger than most mortals know and are comparable in size to the World of Ember itself, in addition to having even more powerful effects on the surface world than the Elemental Moons.

Very little is known of the realm itself among mortals beyond whispered tales and ancient stories of its strange, predatory inhabitants. Like the other Inner Realms, for most, it simply forms part of the tapestry of the night sky and the seasonal and yearly change. Arcane spellcasters tend to revere and study the distant secrets of Primordis during their lifelong quests for esoteric knowledge; otherwise, the realm simply forms the basis of a wide variety of cultural celebrations and stories surrounding the passing of the [[Seeding]] and the [[Blooming]].
