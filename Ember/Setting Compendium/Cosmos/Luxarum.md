# Luxarum

- Keywords: Zeal, Faith, Light
- Ideal: Ambition
- Age: Countless Ages
- Other Names: The Realm of Endless War, Golden Realm, Land of Light

Luxarum is a realm of unbelievable contradictions, where golden, warming light is used to end lives and cause untold devastation. Luxarum has fewer cosmological dangers than [[Signara]] and [[Primordis]], and its magic is straightforward in its raw destructive potential but the real dangers of the Golden Realm lie with its inhabitants, the unwavering and relentless [[Tyraphem]] who view mortals as mere tools and objects to further their personal war efforts. The most fascinating aspect of Luxarum is that for the vast majority of mortals on Ember, the realm is considered to be a warming, hopeful place that brings life and light after the darkness of winter. This is partly due to the connection between light and the energy given off by Lantyr. Throughout countless myths and legends, the Tyraphem are often viewed as guiding forces of goodness and help guide mortals but nothing could be further from the truth.

Luxarum is a deeply disturbing and alien realm. Indeed, it lies so far from the [[World of Ember]] that it has remained isolated and mysterious throughout every age of the Cosmos, and mortal visitors or explorers here are practically unheard of. Mortals do, however, have a clear understanding of the role that Luxarum plays in the yearly calendar, associating its passage in the sky as a marker for the [[Steading]] and [[Gleaning]]. Luxarum is also known as the Golden Realm, because (unlike the furtive shadows of Signara or the soft red glimmer of Primordis) Luxarum glows with an intense golden light in the night sky, and is quite visible with the naked eye even when obscured by clouds. Mortals generally have little knowledge of Luxarum itself other than the realm's importance in the cosmological order.
