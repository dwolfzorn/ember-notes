# Metaphysics

## Gravitational Line

The World of Ember is a thick, flat disk, and its primary force of attraction is governed by the Gravitational Line: an invisible, plane running horizontally through the center of the disk. Gravity on Ember operates by pulling all things perpendicularly toward this central plane, establishing a consistent sense of down" for inhabitants on both the upper and lower surfaces. The Gravitational Line's unique characteristic is the deliberate weakening of its field strength as one approaches the edges of the world. As you approach the edges, vast sections or entire regions experience lower gravity, and structures can be built higher into the sky, and creatures may grow taller and more slender. You can "fall" if you step out into the [[Sea of Stars]], but slowly, and eventually, you will be pulled down and into the [[Pathways]] just like the water from the world's oceans. This also explains the perpetual maintenance of the world's hydrological and material balance, as any water that falls from the oceans inevitably reemerges further within the oceans at deeper levels.

## Teleportation

Within the [[Cosmos]], cosmic travel is achieved exclusively through the mastery of teleportation magic. This method of travel is not simple, and those who try to misuse it or lack a certain level of understanding find that moving themselves or others over vast distances in Ember is both difficult and dangerous. Moving between distant points, such as the [[World of Ember]] and the Elemental Moons, demands not only immense magical power but also flawless spatial calculations and knowledge. Miscalculation results in instant, often fatal consequences. The secrets to generating stable and long-range portals are tightly guarded, primarily held by deities or the [[Rhivan]] [[Star Mages]].

## Strength of Conviction

The esoteric magical nature of the Ember cosmos results in various mysterious facets that currently lie beyond scholarly understanding. One particularly curious element is the observable truth that an individual's strength of conviction, or willpower, can enable powerful magical feats and allow individuals to connect with the same magical systems as those that have learned, studied, and understood magic at a deeper level. This suggests an underlying metaphysical fabric, or perhaps a deep connection to the [[Heart of Ember]], that links the power of the [[Soul Cycle]] directly to reality.

Determined and dedicated individuals, often those with heroic levels of willpower, can manifest power through belief alone, bypassing traditional arcane training. This feat is exceptionally rare and extremely difficult to achieve, but such individuals are capable of incredible feats of strength. Consequently, most inhabitants of Ember pursue faster and more reliable routes to power, such as the direct worship of deities or the channeling of the tangible cosmological forces and beings that are found throughout the cosmos.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Atheists in Ember
>
> Outright denial of deities or major cosmological forces is virtually nonexistent, given the overwhelming abundance of historical and everyday evidence. The various deities of Ember have a profound and undeniable influence, and many walk among mortals. However, this tangible presence does not eliminate debate; indeed, given the immense scale and fractured history of the Cosmos, a great deal of conflict and discussion exists among groups and cultures over the true nature, domains, and motives of specific deities or forces.
