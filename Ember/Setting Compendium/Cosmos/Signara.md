# Signara

- Keywords: Science, Technology, Darkness
- Ideal: Curiosity
- Age: Countless Ages
- Other Names: Realm of Ferocious Curiosity, The Endless Dark

Signara is an incredibly unpredictable place for a mortal from the [[World of Ember]]. It is the realm of ancient fundamental ideas and concepts made manifest by only a suggestion of tangible reality. A mortal's mind can quickly become shredded if they interact with something they shouldn't have. Like the other Inner Realms of [[Luxarum]] and [[Primordis]], Signara is the pinnacle of an otherworldly realm. Fortunately, it lies so far from the World of Ember that it has remained isolated and mysterious throughout every age of the Cosmos, and mortal visitors or explorers are practically unheard of. Mortals have a clear understanding of the role that Signara plays in the yearly calendar, long ago associating its passage in the sky as the marker for the colder months of the year. Signara is often called the Dark Realm as, unlike Luxarum, which shines brightly, and Primordis, which glimmers softly in the skies above, Signara is frequently lost amid the darkness of the Sea of Stars. Even during the months of[[Withering]] and the[[Stilling]], its light is barely visible to the naked eye.

Mortals have little knowledge of Signara itself other than its importance in the cosmological order. They do, however understand and are aware of Fiends and their nature to entrap and sign pacts with mortals in exchange for their souls. As a result, Signara has the worst reputation among the Inner Realms, seen as both a dark and evil place and a place where evil creatures come from. Signara and Fiends are often the villains in countless tales and myths, even though many Fiends have noble — or at least neutral — intentions and simply adhere to the ideas of the [[Soul Cycle]].
