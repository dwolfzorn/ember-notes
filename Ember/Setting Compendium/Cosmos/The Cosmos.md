# The Cosmos

The [[World of Ember]] is known as Eiru and sits at the center of the [[Sea of Stars]], a vast expanse that stretches to the edges of the [[Cosmos]]. At the core of Eiru rests the Heart of Ember. Eiru is a vast world, but it is not spherical; instead, it appears like a thick disc, with a surface on one side, an under-surface on the other, and twisting underground Pathways that are home to myriad environments within. Each day, Eiru is bathed in the radiant light of [[Lantyr]], a celestial body and Elder Goddess also called “the Sun."

## The Weave and The Abyss

The [[Sea of Stars]] is the name for the space within the [[The Weave]] , enveloping Eiru, the Moons, and Realms contained within. The Weave is a mystical barrier that glimmers with blue, purple, and pink light in the darkness and can even be seen from the surface of Eiru on clear nights. This strange barrier marks the perimeter of the Ember Cosmos and is the bubble that shields everything within from the eldritch madness from beyond.

Beyond the barrier of the Weave is a place called [[The Abyss]], a word that is itself barely spoken or well understood by those living in Ember. It is the terrible and unimaginable outside, so alien that few can comprehend its secrets or even attempt to understand it. The Abyss is believed to be an infinite realm of impossible, mind-warping corruption. Thankfully, the Weave's constant protection prevents these maddening energies from flooding into the Sea of Stars to cause untold destruction.

## The Moons of Ember

As the Weave formed around Eiru during Creation, so too did six moons — each of which encapsulates the essence of certain elemental and primeval forces: fire, water, earth, air, order, and chaos. These moons are massive spherical objects with distinct orbital patterns. Each moon also gave life to its own denizens, beings of energy and matter, from the tiniest elemental motes to the mighty Elemental Sovereigns.

Each moon serves as the physical, spiritual, and cosmological origin of its own elemental energy; the dispersal of these energies throughout the Sea of Stars has a major influence on the world of Ember and its denizens. As a particular moon reaches its zenith, directly overhead Eiru, the world is bathed in elemental energy.

## The Inner Realms

Alongside Eiru and the moons are a trio of realms that float just barely upon the surface of The Weave itself. Each was influenced differently during Creation by their proximity to The Abyss, meaning that, while the Inner Realm is part of the Cosmos, it is among the most alien and otherworldly places imaginable. Their influence cannot be overstated, and the effect they have upon Eiru is even more intense than that of the Elemental Moons.

Each Realm has its own distinct magical force that plays a vital role in maintaining the cosmic balance and forms a mysterious link to the inhabitants of the World of Ember. These forces are Light tied to the realm of [[Luxarum]], Darkness tied to the realm of [[Signara]], and Shadow to the realm of [[Primordis]], and, like Eiru and the moons, these realms are inhabited by their own unique beings and creatures.
