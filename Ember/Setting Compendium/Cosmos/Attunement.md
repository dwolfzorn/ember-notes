# Attunement

Different souls within the Ember [[Cosmos]] occasionally exhibit varying degrees of attunement, and only a rare few are capable of resonating with more than one cosmological force at a time. Those who achieve such alignment may occasionally manifest extraordinary capabilities, their thoughts, senses, and actions subtly influenced by the essence of the forces they reflect.

The exact process by which a soul becomes attuned is not known, but leading scholars theorize that such attunement develops when a person lives their life according to a fundamental **Ideal**:an underlying philosophy or tenet which guides the soul to harmonize with the metaphysical nature of the cosmological force.

Careful observation of attuned souls has allowed Ember’s sages to discern recurring patterns of thought, behavior, and ideology. These patterns have been codified into the currently accepted classification of Ideals, each of which represents a distinct philosophical or metaphysical path through which the soul may resonate with one part or aspect of the cosmos. Some Ideals encourage discipline, cunning, or patience, while others call for spontaneity, ambition, or the preservation of life itself.

Though these classifications are widely accepted among the learned, attunement remains a deeply personal and often unpredictable phenomenon. The strength, expression, and effects of a soul’s resonance can wax and wane across a lifetime, and even the most careful study cannot fully predict the consequences of deep or prolonged attunement. The eleven accessible attunements in Ember and their Ideals are presented below.

## Akon

Souls attuned to [[Akon]] resonate with a harmony to The Shattered Moon and generally exhibit adherence to the Ideal of **Discipline**.

#### The Ideal of Discipline

To be attuned to Akon is to bind oneself to the remnants of a broken perfection. Though the moon lies in ruin, its ideal of order and discipline endures, a quiet purpose that calls to those who live by code and conviction. People on Ember consider Akon to be most associated with discipline, but this is not mere obedience to rules but the mastery of one’s will: the strength to act with purpose, duty, and to remain steadfast in the face of chaos.

## Aura

Souls attuned to [[Aura]] resonate with harmony to The Hollow Moon and generally exhibit adherence to the Ideal of **Individualism**.

#### The Ideal of Individualism

To be attuned to Aura is to embrace one’s own path. Those aligned with the Hollow Moon act according to their own desires, guided by personal insight and the urge to seek new horizons or experiences. Their minds are restless, alive with ideas, reflections, and creativity that often diverge from tradition. Yet such independence carries the risk of obsession or relentless ambition. True attunement to Aura lies in balancing freedom with responsibility, knowing when to soar and when to anchor oneself amidst the shifting winds.

## Cora

Souls attuned to [[Cora]] resonate with harmony to The Blasted Moon and generally exhibit adherence to the Ideal of **Pragmatism**.

#### The Ideal of Pragmatism

To be attuned to Cora is to see the world through the lens of what must be done. Those who follow her ideal embrace practicality over perfection, acting not from hope or despair, but from a clear understanding that the world just is, and a person must ultimately carry on with firm resolve in the face of every challenge. They are steadfast and unsentimental, grounded by experience. Yet such pragmatism can harden the heart, for the path of endurance often leaves little room for gentleness. True attunement to Cora lies in knowing when to stand firm and when to yield.

To be attuned to Mayis is to embrace patience, understanding that not all storms may be faced at once, and it's often better to let the currents swirl around you. Their actions are deliberate, measured, and they persevere where rashness would lead to ruin. True attunement lies not in controlling the waters, but in moving with them, accepting the cycles of turmoil and calm, and finding clarity while waiting for the perils to pass, or waiting until the right moment to act.

Those guided by the Spirit Moon’s ideal of Spontaneity embrace chaos, knowing that meaning is found not in what may happen but in what happens now. They are the dancers of chaos, the voices of chance, the souls who refuse stillness.

To be attuned to Luxarum is to burn with unyielding purpose. Its burning light calls to those who seek greatness, driving them toward perfection through willpower and ambition. Such individuals see every obstacle as fuel, every failure as proof that they must shine brighter still, often at the expense of others, driving them towards selfish actions. The light of Luxarum is not gentle. It consumes as much as it empowers, and those who chase its brilliance too far often lose themselves to the same zeal that once lifted them.

To be attuned to Primordis is to think five steps ahead, to move unseen, and to act with subtlety, precision, and deception. Primordis favors those who are both careful and clever, turning obstacles into opportunities. Yet the shadows of Primordis are never static; the unobservant are easily ensnared, and even the clever can be misled by the realm’s ever-shifting tricks. Its lessons are gained in stealth and observation, and the rewards belong only to those who can outsmart others.

## Heart

Souls attuned to [[Heart]] resonate with harmony to The Heart of Ember and generally exhibit adherence to the Ideal of **Vitalism**.

#### The Ideal of Vitalism

To be attuned to the Heart of Ember and its Lifeforce is to feel the pulse of existence, the quiet rhythm that binds every living thing across the cosmos. Such individuals often act as unseen anchors in times of despair, their presence is a calm barrier of hope that steadies the living world against entropy and decay.

Those driven by an instinctive need to protect, to nurture, and to preserve are those most likely to be attuned to the Heart of Ember. Yet this primordial devotion is not without burden, for to feel life so keenly is also to feel its loss in equal measure. Being attuned to Ember itself means carrying the weight of every dying breath and every fading spirit, their souls burning with the unending duty to keep the flame of existence from guttering out.

## Abyss

Souls attuned to [[Abyss]] resonate with harmony to The Abyss and generally exhibit adherence to the Ideal of **Nihilism**.

#### The Ideal of Nihilism

To be attuned to the Abyss is to surrender meaning itself. Those who find resonance with its whispers do not seek power, only release from purpose, from the unbearable weight of order, consequence, and life itself. The Abyss does not offer guidance, only the reflection of a truth so vast and indifferent that it unmakes all others.

Such attunement is not chosen; it *seeps in*. It begins as a quiet erosion of conviction, a thought that nothing truly matters, that Creation itself is an error, that the Cosmos is but a momentary flicker in an eternal dark. A mortal may see the value in thoughtless destruction or the corruption of life into undeath, and those who walk too long with these thoughts find themselves untethered from reality, their dreams invaded by monstrous ideas and beings that drive them to madness. And yet, there is a strange peace in the greater darkness: the stillness of surrender, the quiet knowledge that all things, great and small, are equally meaningless.
