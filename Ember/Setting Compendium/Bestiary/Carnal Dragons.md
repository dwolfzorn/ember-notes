# Carnal Dragons

Carnal Dragons are the scaled [[Bestiary Overview]] of fang and claw, the apex predators of large wilderness territories. They are four-legged and wingless, with thick scales and powerful muscles that allow them to leap, run, and hunt at high speeds. They are unnerving in their movement, with strange otherworldly abilities and innate magic. Some can slip between shadows to ambush their prey, others can blend entirely into their environments, while others can remain motionless for months, waiting for the perfect moment to strike.
