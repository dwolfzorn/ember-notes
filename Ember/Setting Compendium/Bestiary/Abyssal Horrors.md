# Abyssal Horrors

Most mortals on Ember do not consider Abyssal Horrors to be real. Only a few scholars believe they may have once existed due to the prevalence of information and consistent tales of individual Abyssal Horrors from various places in the World. However, to most, they are simply part of the myths and legends surrounding the Shattering and the collapse of civilizations long ago.
