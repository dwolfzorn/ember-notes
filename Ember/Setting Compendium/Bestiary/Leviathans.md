# Leviathans

- Keywords: Primordial, Colossal, Strong
- Ember Type: Creature
- Type: Beasts
- Other Names: Ancient Beasts

Leviathans are some of the most ancient creatures to ever exist, or so people believe. In many tales and stories, they are guardians of ancient places and creatures of the world and of nature. This is very far from the truth, however, and some groups and mortals in the world today know the truth. Leviathans are natural creatures, yes, but they are often ruinous, deadly, and destructive forces that kill, eat, and lay waste to the environments around them. They were once prevented from destroying the world by primordial heroes like the Shard God [[Janar]], who hunted them and protected mortals from their destructive wrath.

A few extremely rare Leviathans seem to have transcended their bestial nature and ascended as [[Shard Gods]] like the great bone serpent, [[Obrisire]]. However, this is incredibly rare, and few examples of such creatures exist despite the time periods involved.

Today, Leviathans can be found across the whole world of Ember; they are rare but not entirely uncommon either, and if you explore the wild places of the world, it's likely that you may find one hidden in the deepest of forests or striding across the boundless plains or tundra. They are best avoided, however, and have fearsome reputations, and many nearby cultures will even protect them or at least make sure no one enters their territories and disturbs them.

Leviathans have no true basic appearance; they take the form of huge, often gargantuan, or even colossal creatures and animals that are wilder, fiercer, and absolutely deadly. Some Leviathans are said to be many miles in length and size and are the greatest forces in the region simply because no other forces can destroy them. They are known to kill Dragons and even [[Shard Gods]] and there are mythological tales of Shard Gods falling in battle to them in the case of [[Godkiller Orinoth]].
