# Cruel Dragons

No other monsters on the World of Ember are as justifiably feared and reviled as Cruel Dragons. Among mortals, they are infamous for destructive, malicious rampages, and they are so well known that their eradication is the sole objective for entire kingdoms. With four main limbs, two massive wings, and maws of sword-like teeth, Cruel Dragons are fire-breathing, monstrous entities that grow to colossal sizes. Living in remote mountainous regions and the far north among the ice-capped northern reaches, they sometimes sleep within their lairs for centuries before emerging to cause chaos.
