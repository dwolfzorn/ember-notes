# Dragons

- Keywords: Destruction, Fire, Scaled
- Ember Type: Monster
- Type: Dragon [See [[Carnal Dragons]], [[Cruel Dragons]], [[Vile Dragons]]]

It is said that Dragons are malice and hatred in physical form. Indeed, the very word is known to strike fear into the hearts of mortals, most of whom have been told countless tales of these monstrous creatures since birth — stories filled with terror, and mystery, and doom. A key history lesson that almost everyone learns from these tales is that Dragons of Ember are all unique creatures; no two Dragons are the same in terms of their exact physiology and motivations, but three main subtypes of categorization do exist. Dragons are also relatively new to Ember (the most ancient of their kin having arrived nearly twenty-five hundred years ago during [[The Shattering]]).

Many of Ember's denizens believe Dragons to be a punishment wrought by the folly and hubris of the [[Aedir]] after the Shattering, and pray the whole draconic scourge will be banished from the world if and when the Sun Tower is rebuilt. Others know the true origin of these powerful creatures to be a thing of divine inspiration, for the first Dragons were the progeny of the Elder Goddess [[Alar]], who brought the [[Cruel Dragons]] into being alongside the Abyssal Shear as descendants of her ill-fated Wyrms. But while Alar sought to create a Wyrm-like creature that could resist the influential taint of the Abyss, abyssal corruption would unfortunately become a hallmark of draconic nature.

Collectively and colloquially known as "True Dragons" to help distinguish them from drakes and lesser dragonkin, three specific subtypes of Dragon are known to populate Ember and the Cosmos. The aforementioned Cruel Dragons were the first Dragons brought into the world, and are known to be massive creatures of winged destruction — with breath of dragonfyre so hot, it can melt metal and stone with ease. [[Carnal Dragons]] are smaller and more feral than their elder siblings, and are known to be some of the world's most aggressively powerful hunters and predators, dominating their local biomes with primeval lethality. [[Vile Dragons]], on the other hand, are creatures of inscrutable cunning and otherworldly wit, and are known to be some of the most powerful spellcasters in the Cosmos (verily, the mere idea of this sorcerous offshoot is considered a farce by Emberites who refuse to reckon with the awful possibilities of their being).
