# Solaru

The Solaru began with a single member, Keia, creator of the [[Nir'ae]] ancestry. After creating the Nir'ae on the [[Aran]], she magically transported them to [[The Starshield]] and ruled her people directly for many years. Following her marriage to the charismatic warrior Oryn, she later helped him to ascend, and he became the first [[Aedir]]. Over time, they had many children, some of whom also ascended, like Aursia and Valen. These children married and had children of their own, continuing the lineage and forming a complex family tree of deities. Eventually, the Solaru pantheon ceased direct rule over the Aedir and delegated governance to the Imperial family, the mortal descendants of Keia and Oryn and their children, but the gods continued to guide events from behind the scenes. Over time, the Solura pantheon expanded, becoming the largest in the [[Cosmos]]. The strength of the Aedir people grew with their gods, and when the Aedir Empire was established and expanded across the surface world, the deities spread with them.

Eventually, as the Sun Tower reached its final heights and the Aedir rejoiced, the Solaru pantheon gathered, and the events of [[The Shattering]] unfolded. In a single instant, the vast family of gods was ended with a finality that shocked the Cosmos. Very few of the [[Shard Gods]]and [[Wild Gods]] that made up the Solura survived the Cataclysm, but among them was Aursia, who founded the Sunalin pantheon upon the [[Kessia]] continent.
