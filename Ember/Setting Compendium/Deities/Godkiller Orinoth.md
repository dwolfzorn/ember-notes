# Godkiller Orinoth

- Keywords: Serpent, Maw, Hunger
- Category: God-Like Being (Leviathan)
- Areas of Concern: Knowledge, Power, Hunger
- Sacred Weapon: Fanged Blade
- Other Names: Godkiller, Hunger in the Deep
- Pantheons: None

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> **Edict of Godkiller Orinoth**
>
> > Come to me, so that I may feed.

Orinoth is an unusually intelligent and beguiling [[Leviathans]] that has become one of the most feared and dangerous of its kind in the known world. He primarily resides within the Yawning Sea, an area that covers essential trade routes between the [[Lumek]], [[Cascilian]], and the Lowlands and the primary shipping lane to the great city of Ordain from the west. Orinoth's lair is said to exist within a deep trench, and he can sense when brave and foolhardy ships pass by overhead. He greatly enjoys sinking such vessels on a whim, consuming the reckless sailors flailing in the dark water. However, he can also breathe upon land and drag himself along coastlines to attack villages and anyone slow enough to remain on their path. He is also known to bask in the sun upon rocky coastlines or beaches, especially when nearby creatures or mortals arrive to offer him food or sacrifices.

Orinoth is well known for his eternal hunger, not only to satisfy their unquenchable desire for nourishment, but he also greatly desires knowledge and information about the world. He is rumored to absorb memories after slowly digesting whatever he consumes over many weeks. For creatures like animals and beasts, this is instinctual, animalistic knowledge. For sapient creatures like mortal humanoids, or especially cunning creatures like monsters or other Leviathans, he can access their deepest thoughts and desires. Orinoth often uses this information to reach out to deceive the consumed ones' relatives or loved ones and convince them to serve him.

He is often described as utterly malicious in his actions; he is greedy and extremely possessive of his knowledge, but mortals can speak with him over vast distances if they have a connection. He may be willing to gift power to such mortals if they serve him and further his agenda, but his only true goal is to further his understanding of the world by consuming and eating everything he can. He is only limited by his fear of being caught by several [[Shard Gods]] at once upon land, and he is well aware that he needs to moderate his actions just enough so that powerful entities do not seek him out above other concerns.

## Appearance

Orinoth is a [[Leviathans]], but they are an extremely unusual and mysterious example of their kind. He is terrifyingly intelligent and manipulative and has amassed vast knowledge throughout his long life thanks to his innate ability to absorb memories of those he consumes. Their form is akin to a colossal eel or serpent, albeit one with a face that is strangely forward-facing. They possess many rows of extremely sharp teeth and two eyes of burning blue light and glittering fin rays that glow blue, deep red, and pink. In the darkness of the seas, countless shimmering bioluminescence strands flow behind their main body, glittering in pulsing patterns that are said to captivate and enthrall anyone who stares too long at them. Orinoth also bears strong arms along his massive length with powerful dexterous hands that they greatly enjoy using to accentuate their speech.

## Influence and Followers

Few beings on Ember are as depraved or as cunning as Orinoth. In pursuit of their eternal hunger, Orinoth spends their time scheming and plotting in decades-long efforts to enthrall mortals and trick them into venturing over his lair. This may mean becoming a helpful, almost kindly source of information for scholars or taking on a more dominant and overpowering persona to force weak-minded fools into betraying their friends and people. Regardless, Orinoth is insidious and is capable of speaking into the minds of beings many hundreds or even thousands of miles from their physical location in the same way a deity might. Many mortal beings can become tricked into serving them, and Orinoth uses his vast knowledge as bait to mass even more over time.

Some follow Orinoth willingly, usually aquatic monsters such as [[Skum]] or [[Nepkela]], who build shrines and crude temples underwater or along coastlines to venerate Orinoth like a deity. Blood sacrifices to Orinoth by such creatures are common, and they will attack, raid, and pillage the lands around them to satisfy their master's needs.

Various [[Shard Gods]] have hunted Godkiller Orinoth since his emergence, and he is currently a target for many more, though often lower down on the list of current threats. Most fail in their attempts to kill him, as he always finds a way to escape or kill anyone who ventures into his lair. However, he is hated beyond measure by the Shard God [[Malae]], who wants nothing more than to see him slain. She was close friends, and some say even lovers, with the Shard God [[Janar]] who Orinoth slew. Followers of Malae, otherwise known as Witches, will often take it upon themselves to hunt down and destroy followers of Orinoth.
