# Ravela

**Areas of Concern:** Fire, Healing

**Typical Followers**: Pyrrhen, Fire Elementals, Healers

**Domains:** Fire

**Pantheons:** None

**Symbol:** A serene face raised to the sky with both palms raised, surrounded by swirling flames.

Ravela is one of the three Elemental Sovereigns of Fire, a [[Wild Gods]] who embodies fire as a tool of restoration, nature, and healing. Following the cataclysmic events of [[The Shattering]], she single-handedly prevented the Fire Moon of [[Ragen]] from descending into a total, lifeless wasteland. During the Shattering itself, her sister [[Fiyara]] turned on Ravela in a fit of corrupted rage. Even though she was also reportedly killed outright in the conflict, Ravela somehow survived. Because of this, she is often considered a goddess of rebirth through this miraculous, self-directed resurrection, yet she remains one of the few deities in Ember who visibly appears crippled by her wounds.

Throughout the [[Cosmos]], Ravela is now well known as a powerful healer who wields a unique Healing Flame, a shimmering, golden-white flame that lacks the heat to burn but possesses the power to knit flesh, purge blight, and coax life from the earth. While this Healing Flame is powerful, ironically, it seems to be unable to fully heal Ravela herself, as she is by far the weakest of Elemental Sovereigns. Despite her weakened state, she is deeply revered by those who live in the harshest environments of Ember and on the Charred Moon itserlf.

## Appearance

Ravela's pale body bears extensive scars from blackened, charred skin, with deep fissures that emit a soft white light. She appears visibly crippled, with thin, unused legs that prevent her from walking. While some in her situation might radiate agony or resentment, Ravela is calm and serene. Her face, though scarred, remains composed, and her eyes hold a steady, patient warmth. Dominating her broken silhouette are two magnificent wings of pure, golden-white flame. These wings ripple with the fluid grace of silk in the wind, casting a gentle, restorative light around her. They are the only parts of her that seem untouched by her conflict with her sister. These wings give rise to her most well-known moniker as the Firebird of Ragen.

## Influence and Followers

Ravela is deeply loved by the remaining Pyrrhen and Fire elementals on Ragen; she is seen as both a saviour and a motherly figure. Following the [[Abyssal Shear]], much of the moon remains a wasteland full of lingering monsters and terrors. In this brutal environment, Ravela and her dedicated followers act as both guardians and gardeners. They maintain hearth-sites, massive bastions of warmth and light where the healing fire of Ravela keeps the monsters at bay while tirelessly working to purge the abyssal rot from the moon’s crust. It is said that the Phoenix, the legendary bird that resurrects itself in flame, was created by Ravela, and anyone who speaks with one is able to speak to Ravela as well.

On the surface of Ember, her followers are far fewer but no less devoted. They are typically found in the most scarred landscapes or places that desperately need healing, such as among the [[Lumek]] attempting to resist the undead plague of the [[Ossarchate]].

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Using Ravela as a Deity
>
> Using Ravela as a deity for a Cleric, Paladin, or Warlock Patron is an act of embracing resilience over power. Ravela provides a fundamentally nurturing source of power, and her magic doesn't just mend flesh; it targets trauma and corruption, making her the premier choice for those combating Abyssal monsters. A devotee of Ravela often plays the role of the traditional healer, someone who wants to fix the world and those around her. Thankfully for the mortals on the surface world, despite Ravela never leaving the moon of Ragen, she can connect with mortals through creatures known as Phoenixes.
