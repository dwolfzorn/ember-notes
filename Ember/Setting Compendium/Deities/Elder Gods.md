# Elder Gods

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Lore Summary
>
> - Origin: Created at the birth of the Cosmos.
> - Cosmic Aspects: They are cosmically empowered but have physical bodies.
> - Worship: They can hear and grant distant prayers, intervening from anywhere in the cosmos.
> - Soul Anchors: They *can* safeguard their followers' souls, taking them to the Inner Realms.
> - Destruction: Strangely, they can be killed. Or perhaps not; what exactly happens is unclear.

As most mortals on the [[World of Ember]] don't see much difference between [[Shard Gods]] and Elder Gods, they are not treated any differently. Elder Gods can gift power to their followers and answer prayers, and some Elders are just as visible and active on the surface as Shard Gods. [[Spectra]], the Elder Goddess of Magic, is often found in academies and libraries across the surface world, and she often speaks with mortals on a daily basis; she is also considered one of the principal deities of the [[Star Mages]] of [[Rhivan]], who are among the few mortals who know her true age and nature.

Typically most elders are not actively worshipped today due to their more esoteric nature and tend to govern some of the more obscure aspects of reality that mortals do not pay attention to in their daily lives. [[Sockets]], for example, the Elder God of the Soul is also considered the God of Death and would be the most popular and well-known of the Elder Gods, consistently across most cultures in Aterica. But even he is not as actively worshipped or revered as much as local Shard Gods who impact people's lives in more direct ways. The idea of an afterlife comes from mortals' understanding of [[Soul Cycle]] which is technically the domain of Sockets, but many mortals understand this concept in the context of their local pantheons and deities with distinct beliefs across cultures surrounding death, rituals for burial, offerings, and prayer.
