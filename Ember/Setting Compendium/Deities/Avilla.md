# Avilla

- **Keywords:** Lost, Untamed, Ordani
- **Areas of Concern:** Anti-Magic, Protection, Healing
- **Sacred Weapon:** Sling
- **Other Names:** The Spellbreaker
- **Pantheons:** None

Avilla is a [[Shard Gods]], who has no presence in the present-day age of the world, disappearing from all records roughly six hundred years ago. Her story is told by many in Ordain as the story of [[The Spellbreaker]]

## Appearance

Avilla is currently a lost deity and has no known present-day appearance. Before she left Ordain, however, she was said to be unremarkable in almost every aspect. She had unkempt, loose, dark brown hair and a slightly slumped figure, preferring to wear simple Cindaric Sage robes. On her way out of Ordain, Avilla was said to carry an ethereal staff, carved from ancient wood but somehow removed from the physical world. At its tip rested a void of nothingness that shone softly with pale blue and orange light.

## Influence and Followers

No one in Ordain actively worships Avilla as a deity, and she has no shrines or temples, but the Ordani venerate and remember her in stories and tales as part of their history. In some regards, she is a cautionary tale, and the Ordani as a people have a lingering apathy towards magic as a result. They do not fear or distrust magic users in any way, but they tend to use magic in small ways and as part of daily life rather than studying elemental or destructive paths as a result.

Strangely, Avilla has a large following among the lands of the Cascilian Republic. Although she has no clerics or priests, many view her in a much more positive light, using her story as further justification for their anti-magic stance.

> [!danger] Hazard
> *Using Avilla as a deity for a Cleric, Paladin, or general devotion is not possible. The most likely reason is that Avilla is long dead, or perhaps she still lives but refuses to recognize any true followers.*
