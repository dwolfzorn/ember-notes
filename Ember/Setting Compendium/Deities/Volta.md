# Volta

**Areas of Concern:** Change, the Dawn, Secrets

**Typical Followers:** Travelers, Anyone going through a change

**Domains:** Twilight, Light

**Pantheons:** [[The Nineteen]]

**Symbol:** The horizon line with the rays of dawn above and the dusk colors below.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> **Edict of Volta**
>
> > Moving at the speed of change.
