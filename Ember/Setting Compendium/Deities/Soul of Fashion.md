# Soul of Fashion

- **Keywords:** Clothing, Styles, Secrets
- **Areas of Concern:** Clothing, Fashion, Ordain
- **Sacred Weapon:** Rapier
- **Other Names:** Prisma
- **Pantheons:** None

Little is understood about the "Soul of Fashion." The people of Ordain often refer to this entity similarly to how one might speak of a guiding concept or principle, but they do not actively worship it. Instead, the term is frequently used to describe the Ordani's unique sense of style and their pursuit of clothing trends.

A few fervent believers assert that its influence is a real force, felt throughout the city, where dressing in beautiful garments becomes an expression of the soul itself. The vast majority in Ordain believe is is a collective cultural expression of thier culture. Regardless of whether the Soul of Fashion is a genuine force, it undeniably plays a crucial role in shaping the identity of Ordain, as its people prioritize fashion and stylistic expression in their clothing above many other concerns.

## Appearance

No appearance is associated with the "Soul of Fashion".

## Influence and Followers

Most Ordani, and even those outside the city, have heard of the "Soul of Fashion," and all Ordani have some connection to it in some way. Though it is not considered a person or entity, it is part of daily life. Many speak of the Soul as part of themselves or others, especially when they are particularly active in social fashion scenes.

> [!danger] Hazard
> *The Soul of Fashion cannot be worshipped as a deity by a Cleric, Paladin, or for general devotion; it is not considered a deity.*
