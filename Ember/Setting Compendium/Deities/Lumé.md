# Lumé

**Areas of Concern:** War, Fire, Murder

**Typical Followers:** Paladins, Soldiers, Fanatics

**Domains:** Fire, Other

**Pantheons:** None

Symbol: A crown of fire, pierced by multiple swords

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> **Edict of Lumé**
>
> > Only fire reveals a person's true nature

Lumé is the radiant fire goddess queen of the [[Lumek]], a steadfast, uncompromising deity of compassion and strength, regarded by many as one of the most skilled warriors in the world. While she now rules over the Lumek and most of the [[Fej]] ancestry in Ember, she is not the original creator of the Fej; that was her mother, Naor, an ancient deity, who crafted them long ago during the early days of the [[Age of the Tower]]. Controversy surrounds Lumé regarding whether she deliberately murdered her mother after the events of the Evernight. Some believe it was accidental, others think she had no choice. During the Evernight, when undead swarmed the Lumek, Lumé was among the first to act, leading her people in battle after battle. She succeeded in pushing back the emerging [[Ossarchate]] at great cost but appeared frustrated with her mother's inaction and focus. What transpired between the two deities remains uncertain, but Naor died shortly afterward, and Lumé took over leadership of the Lumek. The people, frightened and angry over the ongoing undead assaults, rallied around Lumé despite the rumors and renamed their realm the [[Lumek Firelands]].

## Appearance

Lumé is a remarkably beautiful deity who adopts two primary forms. During the recovery period after undead assaults, she takes on a regal form, resembling a tall Fej woman with coal-black skin and an inner fire that emanates from her shoulders and head, wearing a plain golden crown. Despite her formidable appearance, her eyes remain calm and compassionate. Her attire blends intricate regalia with practical, form-fitting clothing suitable for rebuilding and aiding her people.

When the undead swarm into Moriran's Lament, targeting her children and nation, her appearance changes dramatically. The once gentle and thoughtful ruler becomes a fierce and deadly warrior. Her crown transforms into an elaborate helm, and her body is encased in blackened armor. Her form becomes more elemental as her skin ignites into blazing flames, and her eyes turn into white-hot pits within her fiery face. Lumé wields a long, slender obsidian sword, which glows with intense white-hot heat as she fights the undead.

## Influence and Followers

Nearly the entire culture of the Lumek follows and worships Lumé to some degree. Even among the [[Shard Gods]] who are accessible and approachable, Lumé is especially present in her people's lives. As a ruler, she makes time for her subjects and is often seen walking the streets of her cities and towns, helping where she can. In many cases, she offers blessings and gives people gifts. She seems well aware that the cycles of war that swept across the region are a constant pressure and tries hard to relieve her people's burdens. This makes her especially beloved and revered on a personal level, as her people can see firsthand how hard she works to keep them safe.

Direct followers of Lumé are peerless warriors and among the most powerful opponents of the undead in the world, imbued with power from Lumé personally. Many are based in the city of Ash on the western edge of the Firelands and guard the mountains to the north against any encroaching undead.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Using Lumé as a Deity
>
> Using Lumé as a deity for a Cleric or Paladin, or as a Warlock Patron, is a path of fierce, unrelenting resilience and a desire to live up to greater ideals. Lumé is a deeply passionate and present deity who cares greatly for her people. She never seems to show doubt but often expresses her grief and sadness over the burden her people carry. Because of her empathy, she rarely pushes anyone, except herself, to extremes, which often makes her followers try even harder to match her. She rarely leaves the Lumek Firelands but communes with her followers in other lands when they reach out to her, offering guidance and soft words of encouragement.

> [!info] Social
> #### Relationships
>
> Lumé is a steadfast friend of the Shard God [[Thayloc]], as both are passionately opposed to the undead, and they even employ similar methods, directly opposing threats head-on. Their bond is strengthened by past trauma and sadness, and they are often seen reminiscing and sharing memories together.
>
> Rumors suggest Lumé is in a relationship with [[Raineka]], the Shard God, which has shocked many of her followers. It is an intriguing pairing since the two deities are radically different: Lumé is associated with war, battle and command, while Raineka is a goddess of peace and healing. Reports indicate they have spent considerable time together over the past few years, with Raineka increasingly seen in Hearth, the capital city of the Lumek, granting blessings to its citizens.
>
> Lumé detests Ku'arta and it's rumoured she even tried to kill him in the past, long before the Evernight. However, she does begrudingly accept that he is aligned with her against the undead.
>
> Lumé has a complex and often strained relationship with [[Lorgi]], who shares many of her concerns. The two align on so many things that, at first glance, they would seem to be steadfast allies. Instead, the two have conflicting personalities and clash not over what needs to be done but over the manner in which it is achieved.
