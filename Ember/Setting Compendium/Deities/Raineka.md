# Raineka

**Areas of Concern:** Rain, Healing

**Typical Followers:** Healers, Diplomats

**Domains:** Life, Peace

**Pantheons:** [[The Nineteen]]

**Symbol:** Raindrops in the shape of a rainbow

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> **Edict of Raineka**
>
> > Breathe slowly and know peace.

Raineka is a reserved and reclusive [[Shard Gods]], known for her healing abilities and deep empathy. She prefers one-on-one conversations over the attention of crowds. Despite her social anxiety around mortals, she has been known to be fearless in the face of danger, appearing during conflicts between the [[Waerd]] factions in the nearby [[Lowlands]] to aid the fallen. She also actively confronts monsters and seems to hold a tightly reined contempt for the undead. For this reason, to the Waerds she is a highly respected deity, with many shrines and followers across the Lowlands. Many warriors carry charms or talismans believed to invoke her divine protection, especially when they are injured.

Many believe she is a young Shard God who arose relatively recently, but evidence suggests she is older than her followers know. She is part of the 'Family of Storms' and is considered the daughter of [[Katu]] and [[Vekalla]], both of whom adore her. She spends much of her time on the southeastern shores of the [[Arctus Plateau]], among the scattered villages and tribes of [[Ashka]], at her home alongside Katu and Vekalla on the floating Isle of Storms, or within the Lowlands. She rarely visits the city of [[Ordain]], finding the confines of a bustling city too much to bear.

Raineka is also worshipped by many across the region as a bringer of rain for the harvest, a role she greatly enjoys. This is even more prominent among the [[Kithil]] villages, who worship her ability to bring the calm, nurturing rains much more than her healing abilities. For the Kithil, however, Raineka is a distant force, and she is rarely found on their shores.

## Appearance

Raineka is a very curious deity in many ways, appearing most often as a watery, elemental woman with dark blue skin and strange lighter patterns and color blends crisscrossing her form. She tends to hide most of this and wears a vibrant blue cloak with a deep hood, its patterns shimmering like raindrops across the surface. Under her hood, she often hides swirling, glowing blue eyes and a wry, playful smile. She can be very nondescript when she chooses to blend in among crowds and prefers not to be noticed. When healing the sick or performing a larger blessing, she reveals much more of her divinity and lifts her hood, revealing a shining, tumultuous mass of watery hair that seems to flow and twist like falling water in hues of blue, pink, and white.

## Influence and Followers

Despite being part of the Nineteen for as long as it has existed, she has no formal priesthood or any major temple built to venerate her. Raineka has many followers who revere her peaceful healing abilities and her desire to help others. They follow her example by tending to the sick regardless of circumstances. Many can be found within Healing Houses or as local doctors, settling in villages, towns, or cities and spending their lives caring for the well-being of ordinary people. Some take a different path and wade into battles instead, risking their own lives to spare and aid those at the brink of death. She tends not to form close relationships with most of her followers, possibly influenced by her anxieties, but she does occasionally find a particularly worthy follower with whom she forms a deeper bond.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Using Raineka as a Deity
>
> Using Raineka as a deity for a Cleric or Paladin, or as a Warlock Patron, is a path rooted in deep empathy, reserved for those who genuinely seek to help others. Raineka highly values self-sacrifice and shares her power with anyone committed to healing. She also embodies hope, attracting positive influences that aim to preserve it even in the toughest environments. When communicating with Raineka, she tends not to speak much, but her voice is soft and calming, making her intentions easier to understand through her reassuring presence.

> [!info] Social
> #### Relationships
>
> Raineka is the daughter of Katu and Vekalla and the sister of [[Sokali]]. She has a very close relationship with her parents and lives with them on the Isle of Storms. She sees her sister Sokali less frequently, but they adore each other and try to meet at least once a year during Ember's winter seasons.
>
> Rumors suggest Raineka is in a relationship with [[Lumé]]. It is an intriguing pairing since the two deities are radically different: Lumé is associated with war, battle, and command, while Raineka is a goddess of healing. Reports indicate they have spent considerable time together over the past few years, with Raineka increasingly seen in Hearth, the capital city of the [[Lumek]], granting blessings to its citizens.
>
> Among the other Nineteen, she regards [[Sigil]] as a close friend and jokingly refers to him as 'Uncle Beardface' and sometimes visits him at his home in [[Oldcraft Lodge]]. She calls [[Remira]] her aunt, and despite their differences, Remira often tries to involve Raineka in public events and encourages her to have fun.
>
> Though it's unclear why, she does not seem to like [[Sionia]] or [[Volta]].
