# Nymbohr

**Areas of Concern:** Wind, Peace

**Typical Followers:** Zeph, Air Elementals

**Domains:** Air

**Pantheons:** None

**Symbol:** A soft, hazy mist with almost invisible clasped hands within.

Nymbohr was once a being of immense strength and calm who ruled over the Hollow Moon of [[Aura]]. However, he has long since faded into seemingly nothingness. He now seems to exist as a ghost in the breath of the world, lacking the will to speak or perhaps the agency to intervene. To those who know of his existence, he is thought to be present in every pocket of air and a being that exists in every corner of the [[Cosmos]]. For those sensitive to the elemental force of Air, Nymbohr can be felt as a lingering pressure in the air around them rather than a divine voice. These individuals do not receive visions nor can they hear a voice that helps or guides them; instead, they experience the Sovereign's presence as a literal pressure, as if a faint hand were pressing down on their shoulder.

Once a primordial [[Wild Gods]] of immense power, Nymbohr no longer appears to have a domain or a place of power, yet he has somehow kept his sanity. This perplexes many scholars, with some believing he has transcended the typical capabilities of a Wild God, while others argue that he has indeed descended into madness, with his insanity manifesting as a cold indifference to life.

## Appearance

Nymbohr lacks a tangible body; he manifests as a shimmering distortion in the atmosphere, spread across the entire Cosmos. This lack of body means that he cannot be injured and may as well not exist for any practical purpose.

## Influence and Followers

Nymbohr has very few followers on the surface of Ember. But the few who are rare typically find comfort in silence and isolation. Rather than priests seeking divine favor or intervention, his devotees are often master navigators, stoic philosophers, or hermits who interpret Nymbohr's state of mind through the rigorous study of weather patterns and shifts in the surrounding air's pressure. Because Nymbohr no longer has a domain or seat of power, his followers are often nomadic or dwell in isolated mountain caves.

On Aura, Nymbohr is venerated by various sects and groups among the creatures and air elementals of the moon. Among the [[Zeph]] in the city of Condundrum, he remains highly respected and worshipped to this day. As Nymbohr has no temples and no physical presence, potential followers often attempt to bind themselves to his power by ritually blindfolding themselves in isolated places of the world. Once blindfolded, the initiate shifts their perception and reaches out to the air and wind, attempting to achieve a perfect equilibrium. The process is difficult and may take many years, but followers often speak of a true connection when they first feel something akin to a hidden hand on their shoulder, a literal, physical pressure that anchors the follower to Nymbohr’s elemental power and allows them to read the air's intent.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Using Nymbohr as a Deity.
>
> Using Nymbohr as a deity for a Cleric, Paladin, or Warlock Patron is to enter a pact with an entity that does not seem to exist in the traditional sense. Any followers walk the path of complete independence, as their deity will give them no answers and has little care for their goals or dreams. Mortals are granted power in a very abstract sense, and they must find their own way through the chaos of life, knowing that the Sovereign of Air is always watching but will never act on their behalf.
