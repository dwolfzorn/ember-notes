# Sunalin

The Sunalin pantheon is the radiant heartbeat of [[Kessia]], and while [[Lantyr]] remains the most prominent entity of worship, the pantheon was forged by Aursia. Originally part of the [[Solaru]], Aursia abandoned her peers after witnessing them descend into tyranny that failed to uphold the true tenets of righteousness and faith. After [[The Shattering]], Aursia’s zeal and opposition to monsters and evil gathered many scattered [[Shard Gods]] and unified them to protect the remaining ancestries on the northern shore of the [[Kessia]] continent. Today, this faith is so deeply integrated into [[Kessian]] identity that it is no longer merely a religion but a total social and military ideology.

The relationship among the gods of the Sunalin is based on necessity and military commitment rather than any true family ties or deeper bonds. Aursia resides in [[Kasarain]] and helps lead the Kessian people, while many of the other Shard Gods live in nearby cities, such as Pyriou'sasa, who resides in the city of Iktae. Korvak resides with the Bjordans to the east, while Vin'osul rules over the great city of [[Tempest]]. In the years after the Shattering, the Shard God Etarra was ripped apart by his own people, who had turned into undead ghouls, and now presides over a nation of undead and is reviled by the other gods.
