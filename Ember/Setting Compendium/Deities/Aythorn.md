# Aythorn

**Moniker:** The Trickster Thorn

**Areas of Concern:** Endurance, Plants, Trickery

**Typical Followers:**

**Domains:** Trickery

**Pantheons:** [[The Nineteen]]

**Symbol:** A flower made of thorns

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> **Edict of Aythorn**
>
> > Joy is best when it is shared — and unexpected.

Aythorn is a young, energetic, and friendly [[Shard Gods]] who moves throughout the world, meeting new people and playing small pranks to lift their spirits and forge personal connections. They are a very young Shard God, having ascended only three hundred years ago in the Tayan hinterlands, in what is now the [[Tayan]]. Since arriving in the [[Arctus Plateau]] twenty-six years ago, they have become an overnight celebrity, quickly charming the population of Toraga by hosting parties, events, and helping to solve local problems. Their incredibly captivating and charming persona is deeply attractive to many mortals, who have become enamored with a highly empathic and personal deity who seems to genuinely care about their lives. They have quickly become recognized as a member of the [[The Nineteen]], with many starting to refer to the Pantheon of the Arctus Plateau and surrounding regions as "The Twenty."

While Aythorn is happy to talk and tell all kinds of stories, their identity and origin remain a mystery. They are quick to call themselves an ascended [[Thornling]]. They are proudly non-gendered and chaotic, and they don't really understand gender or other social or cultural norms that most humans abide by. However, Aythorn also has some very curious characteristics that run counter to this idea. To begin with, they don’t look like other Thornlings and their skin is a greenish organic material rather than rough bark, and unlike other Thornlings, they greatly enjoy food rather than simply needing liquids to survive.

As they have settled more into the Arctus Plateau, Aythorn has become good friends with the Shard God [[Volta]]. Aythorn usually invites them to parties and gatherings, and the two get along famously both enjoying celebrations, music, and good food. Aythorn calls Volta "Boba," a Thornling expression of close family. However, this is generally not seen as a true familial relation but rather a measure of respect and friendliness. With many other deities, Aythorn has a very positive relationship and spent several years living with [[Katu]] and [[Vekalla]] family on the Isle of Storms.

Aythorn is a Shard God of increasingly great renown; their fame is spreading through their eagerness to help people's daily lives and bring them joy, in addition to throwing some legendary parties that last for days. Unlike other [[Shard Gods]] or notable heroes, Aythorn steers clear of conflict or battles. One of the main reasons they left the Tayan hinterlands was that the area was volatile and dangerous, and the culture was increasingly focused on militaristic goals. Aythorn prefers charm, trickery, or even stealth when confronted with a hostile entity, and most of their stories revolve around them doing just that to all kinds of powerful foes.

## Appearance

A vibrant, colorful deity, Aythorn appears to be some kind of mixed being between a [[Thornling]] and a [[Human]], or possibly some other ancestry, proudly commenting that they are a "big" Thornling. They are generally very tall for a Thornling, standing at nearly five and a half feet, and they can increase or decrease this even further when the situation calls for it. They have large, glowing, colorful flowering petals as hair and a wide, charming smile. They easily attract attention with their expressive gestures and strong, soft voice. Unlike most gods, Aythorn doesn't shy away from their divinity or try to disguise themselves and happily wanders among adoring crowds, giving out little blessings, playing pranks, and dispensing advice.

## Influence and Followers

Aythorn has a large and growing number of followers, especially in the [[Tayan]], who remember Aythorn fondly from the many years they spent traveling from settlement to settlement. In the Arctus Plateau, their followers are also growing in number, but they are concentrated around settlements where Aythorn has previously spent time. Most of Aythorn's followers might not be considered priests or clerics; however, they certainly pray and ask their god for guidance, especially if they have met Aythorn personally, although they prefer to think of themselves as just good friends instead.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Using Aythorn As A Deity
>
> Using Aythorn as a deity for a Cleric, Paladin, or general devotion is common in specific regions of the World of Ember, such as the Tayan Empire and the Arctus Plateau. Outside these areas, worship or knowledge of Aythorn is virtually nonexistent, though not entirely out of the question. Aythorn's attention for their followers or friends is almost immediate, and they answer calls personally almost every time. They are unlikely to help their followers directly in battle by intervening personally, but they might assist them in escaping or finding another path to victory.
