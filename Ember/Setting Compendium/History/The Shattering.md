# The Shattering

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Lore Summary
>
> The Sun Tower of the Aedir explodes with cataclysmic force, wiping out the entire continent of the Starshield and ending thousands of years of Aedir supremacy over the surface world. A section of the Sun Tower rockets outwards and shatters the Elemental Moon of Akon, breaking it apart.

In a single moment of unimaginable destruction, the apex of the [[The Broken Tower]] of the [[Aedir]] exploded with such force and power that it appeared to shine with the full force of the sun, blinding those facing it directly. The explosion shattered the upper sections, expelling colossal chunks of stone and rock into the cosmos. For the Aedir living on the Starshield, close to nearly the entire population of millions, was annihilated instantly as the force of the explosion and magical energy radiated across the continent. Across the oceans, around the Starshield, the blast struck with less force, but depending on the distance, it was still powerful enough to smash aside buildings and lay waste to entire cities. The skies of Ember became choked by fiery red and orange storm clouds as fire seemed to engulf the world, casting the surface into glimmering darkness for several months. In the chaos and destruction that followed, the terrifying emergence of [[Bestiary Overview]] completed the devastation in many places, seemingly materializing out of nothingness to kill, consume, and destroy everything around them.
