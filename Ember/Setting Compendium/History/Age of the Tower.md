# Age of the Tower

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Lore Summary
>
> The Age of the Tower, also known as the Age of Sunlight, was an era in Ember's history that lasted tens of thousands of years and saw the rise of the first global empire, the Aedir. The Aedir explored most of the surface world and even traveled into the Sea of Stars, eventually building the colossal Sun Tower at the center of the Starshield continent. It was also the period when cultures, peoples, and ancestries began to interact with one another in more meaningful ways, resulting in collaboration, alliances, but also major conflicts and wars.
>
> - **Mortal Ancestries:** The Creation of the Aedir, Fej, Thornlings, Hulg'run, and Humans.
> - **Global Conflict:** The first global conflict occurred between the Aedir and the Theroch. This conflict spread and involved many deities, cultures, and peoples, and even involved forces from the Cosmos, such as the Tyraphem and the Wyrms of Akon.

The Age of Tower was the period in which the Sunfire Empire of the [[Aedir]] reigned over nearly all of Ember and wrought great wonders across the world. They established a near-universal dominant culture and a dynastic empire of scholarship, arcane supremacy, and technological mastery.

This age of the world is shrouded in mystery, myth, and legend. Still, many of the details and specific events were once meticulously recorded and documented by the Aedir and some other cultures active at the time. This means that ancient scrolls and tomes found in crumbling ruins may speak in detail about the Age of Tower and the majesty and power of the Aedir; such treasures are priceless to collectors and historians.
