# The First Dawn

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Lore Summary
>
> The First Dawn is the first and longest eon of the Ember Cosmos; the period is sometimes referred to as the Creation or the Longest Age, and it includes not only the beginning of the Cosmos but also the formation and creation of many of the first life forms across the Inner Realms, Moons, and the surface world.
>
> - **Cosmic Creation:** Formation of the Inner Realms, the Elemental Moons, the World of Ember, and the protective barrier of the Weave.
> - **Elders:** The Emergence of the Elder Gods, Alar, Spectra, Ven'avé, Sockets, and Lantyr.
> - **The First Life:** The arrival of elemental spirits, primitive plants, and terrestrial animals.
> - **Early Sapience:** The first sapient species, the Xaxin on one side and the Theroch on the other side of the World of Ember.

The First Dawn is a period of the world that is almost entirely unknown and rarely considered or discussed among the mortals of Ember. Anyone discussing or attempting to describe events during Creation should be treated with the highest degree of doubt and scrutiny, as their statements are likely fiction. A few older, more established cultures are aware of certain pivotal events, yet there may be contradictory information, with numerous creationary myths they believe are part of their religious or spiritual beliefs.

Some of the only real facts of the period include the idea that the First Dawn is when the [[Cosmos]] itself coalesced, giving birth to the Inner Realms and forming the Elemental Moons, and eventually the [[World of Ember]]. It was during this expansion that the protective Barrier of [[The Weave]] first rippled outward, acting as a cosmic shield to protect the newborn reality from the hostile, alien energies beyond. During this period, the [[Elder Gods]], [[Alar]], [[Spectra]], [[Ven'avé]], [[Sockets]], and [[Lantyr]], manifested, establishing the metaphysical laws that would govern existence.

As the Cosmos stabilized, the first flickers of life began to stir. Primitive [[Elementals]] spirits and strange lunar creatures appeared first, followed by the emergence of the [[Tyraphem]] and their celestial kin on [[Luxarum]], the [[Daemos]] and [[Fiend]] on [[Signara]], and the [[Casia]] and Fey within the wild forests of [[Primordis]]. On the surface of Ember, life blossomed from simple flora into a complex ecosystem, with simple creatures and animals. Eventually, the long silence of the world was broken by the rise of the first, sentient and sapient mortal ancestries, the [[Xaxin]] and the [[Theroch]], who emerged on opposite sides of the world.
