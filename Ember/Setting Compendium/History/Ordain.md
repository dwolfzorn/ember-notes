# Ordain

- Keywords: Regional History, Present Day
- Length: 807 Years
- Other Names: The Foundation of Ordain.

Eight hundred and seven years ago, the citizens of the [[Kelmezian]] city-state of Yarino were invaded from the north by the Kingdom Reysta, originating from the Tayan Heathlands. The people of Yarino at the time were led by a timid and ineffectual prince who had wasted his people's riches on extravagant palaces and lavish feasts for other nobles. As the war escalated and the poorly funded army of Yarino retreated to the city walls, a largely overlooked group of mystics who had begun calling themselves the [[Cindaric Sages]] began criticizing the prince, attributing the city's plight to his reckless greed.

When the Reysta armies reached the city, the terrified populace spiraled into a frenzy as the prince attempted to escape on ships chartered with the last of the city's wealth. The Speaker of the Sages, Adonia, had garnered immense respect for her impassioned speeches and sermons. She claimed that salvation for the people lay far to the east. Enraged and inspired, the common people assaulted the fleeing prince before he could board his ship. By all accounts, the prince was torn apart by a mob at the harbor and his name was lost to the annals of history. Adonia then rallied as many people as possible onto the waiting ships and set sail eastward. Miraculously, their journey into the Silken Sea and northward along the coast lasted many weeks, yet not one ship from the fleet was lost. Upon spotting the towering cliffs and blood-red fields of grass north of the [[Arctus Plateau]]'s shores, the fleet anchored and began establishing a new settlement.

Soon after, the fledgling town was named [[Ordain]], an ancient Yarino term that means "heart-born," and its inhabitants began calling themselves the [[Ordani]]. The Cindaric Sages emerged as a significant political force in the newly founded city, with many, if not all, citizens viewing Adonia as a holy saint sent by the heart of the world to guide them toward a greater destiny.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> After the vast majority of the population fled Yarino, it was sacked and abandoned for several generations. However, in 1817 AS, Verena sent an expedition to reclaim the ruined city and begin rebuilding. The modern city of Yarino is therefore only related to the Ordani people in a vague historical context.

For the large majority of its history, Ordain has remained exceptionally stable and peaceful, with very few major events affecting its rise as one of the greatest cities in the known world. It has grown steadily in both population and scale. Due in part to the cooperation and shared governance across multiple organizations, no one group ever gained enough control over elements of the city to cripple progress. In addition, Ordain's position in the world was secure and entirely unthreatened by its neighbors, who largely supported the city's successes.

Regrettably, Ordain's recent history over the last twenty years has begun to turn the dial in the opposite direction and has been filled with increasingly turbulent events. The once peaceful and thriving city, is now confronting numerous issues that threaten and or place extreme pressures on the systems that many residents depend on to secure their futures.
