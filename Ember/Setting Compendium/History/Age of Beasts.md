# Age of Beasts

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Lore Summary
>
> The Age of Beasts was an era of Ember'sHistroy that lasted for hundreds of thousands of years and saw the rise of some of the largest, most primal beasts and animals, in addition to some of the older mortal ancestries.
>
> - **Primordial Creatures:** Emergence of the Leviathans as the dominant lifeform on the surface of Ember.
> - **Mortal Ancestries:** The Creation of the Co'ark, Jarn, Giants, Varak, Vrjnhar, Saurvek, Ashka, Carrow, Leafia, Zeph, and Wirrun.
> - **Ascendants:** The first Shard Gods arise after communing with Heart Crystals buried in the world.

The Age of Beasts is so far removed from the memories of mortal ancestries alive today that it may as well be fiction. So much of the history is entirely unknown, and while some of the earliest cultures arose during this time, almost nothing is known about their struggles. These cultures and corresponding ancestries are thought to have created and maintained vast kingdoms and nations of their own. Still, this period was so extraordinarily long that almost everything has been lost, buried, or crumbled into nothing. There remains a few ancient stories, sagas, creationary myths, and legends that are told of this age, handed down through countless generations, but most cannot be verified for accuracy or truth.
