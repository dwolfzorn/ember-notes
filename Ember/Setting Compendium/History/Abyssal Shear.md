# Abyssal Shear

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### WARNING
>
> References to the Abyss, the Abyssal Shear, Abyssal Horrors, Outer Gods, etc, are not things that the player characters would typically learn about before gameplay begins, unless under very specific circumstances for background purposes. A more in-universe name for the Abyss among mortals is "The Shroud", with Abyssal monsters often called the Malvorn.
