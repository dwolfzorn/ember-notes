# Ranger

The world of Ember is a primal place, full of constant change and unknowable dangers. One of the most capable self-defense mechanisms the living planet has at its disposal are the disparate heroes known as Rangers, who appear like diamonds in the rough — and emerge as some of the world's most noble and selfless protectors. Whatever calls a Ranger to duty, they persist as some of the realm's most prized slayers of monsters and tyrants.

## Beast Master

Rangers of this type specialize in a deep and lasting bond with a Primal Companion, a magical beast that appears as a physical manifestation of a legendary animal from a bygone age of the living planet. Many of Ember's Rangers strive to become Beast Masters, and these beast-bonded warriors can be found among nearly all of Ember's societies — from the agrarian [[Arcturian]] of the eponymous Plateau, to the imperialist [[Tayan]] of western Canasia. Most notably, symbiotic enclaves of Beast Master Rangers and their allies can be found frequently among the [[Bejak]] peoples northeast of Aterica, where a demanding nomadic lifestyle benefits from the perpetual comforts and security of primal companionship.

## Fey Wanderer

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> The term "Feywild" does not exist in Ember. However, certain concepts of the Feywild persist via the Inner Realm known as [[Primordis]] — also known as the Realm of Capricious Magic, or the Shadowlands.

For one reason or another, these enigmatic Rangers possess a strong connection to the arcane wilds of [[Primordis]], where the shadowy glamour of fey magic is most potent. Fey Wanderers can find their way to this subclass through any number of methods — be it a lineage that stems from The First Forest, a fateful encounter with a powerful fey artifact, or via the whims of an ambitious or influential [[Casia]]. Like most Rangers, Fey Wanderers tend to be outcasts, but their capacity for illusion magic often makes up for their societal shortcomings. And these masters of glamourous subterfuge are capable of utilizing their Fey Wanderer Spells and "Primordial Gifts" to astonishing effect.

## Gloom Stalker

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> The term "Shadowfell" does not exist in Ember. However, certain concepts of the Shadowfell persist via the Inner Realm known as [[Signara]] — also known as the Realm of Ferocious Curiosity, or The Endless Dark.

Gloom Stalkers of Ember are more commonly known as the "Somber Guard," and draw upon the ethereal, otherworldly magic of [[Signara]] to fell their enemies and traverse the tenebrous wilds of the living planet. Whether a Ranger has encountered a technological wonder of The Endless Dark, has reasoned with a [[Daemos]], or is the product of a fiendish lineage, something mysterious has drawn them to the natural and supernatural darkness of the cosmos. Members of the Somber Guard tend to be at home among the darker recesses of Ember, such as the subterranean [[Pathways]] and the lightless canopies of places like the [[Duskflare Jungles]] or the [[Moiran Bonelands]].

## Hunter

Hunters are perhaps the most common type of Ranger to be found on Ember, but their combat and survival acumen remain unmatched — particularly when set against a foe that's been marked by their supernatural tracking skills. According to legends passed down throughout the ages, the esoteric techniques of Ember's Hunters originated long ago among the [[Waerd]] of the [[Duskflare Jungles]], who've had to maintain a stalwart vigil against the ceaseless assault of monstrous creatures and preternatural villains for centuries. These days, Rangers who excel in battlefield tactics and the lore of the wilderness make the strongest candidates for effective Hunters of the living planet.

## Swarmkeeper

The swarm of nature spirits that empower these esoteric Rangers stem from a deep and plentiful connection to the Heart of Ember itself, which is connected to all things that inhabit the metaphysical ecosystem of the living planet. Swarmkeeper Rangers can hail from any walk of life, but the [[Sanguinaries]] of the Arcturian [[Bloodwoods]] and the [[Strider]] of the [[Corebright Forest]] tend to actively cultivate these skilled weavers of spiritual current. When it comes to the physical manifestation a Swarmkeeper's swarm takes when it appears, it's not uncommon for the Swarming Insects common to Rangers of this type to resemble Ember's benevolent Pyreflies — which are coincidentally revered by the druidic [[Cindaric Sages]] and Sanguinaries in many of their rituals.

## Winter Walker

Winter Walkers are Rangers who excel in the frigid, unforgiving temperatures of Ember's coldest biomes — such as the hoary expanse of the [[Vaznor]], the glittering domains of the [[Rhivan]] southwest of Kessia, the jagged peaks of the Spine of Ember, or even the haunted unknown wilderness of far-off [[Jekaros]]. Notably, the [[Fikost]] people of [[Rimedrift]] and the [[Wardcall]] people of the Wintec, Katic, and Pertta kingdoms are known to venerate Winter Walkers among their ranks. Winter Walkers of Ember are also known to be familiar with the strange arcana related to these glacial areas as well, like the powerful Wardscale armor of the Wardcall and the portal technology of the Rhivan [[Star Mages]].
