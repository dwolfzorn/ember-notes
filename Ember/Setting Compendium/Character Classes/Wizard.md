# Wizard

Ember is a world steeped in magic, so it's no wonder that Wizards occupy a rather important place among the various occupations that player characters can take up. Whether their mystical knowledge comes from the study of Ember's most venerated libraries or the exploration of long-forgotten places, these arcane spellcasters tend to achieve remarkable positions of power throughout the cosmos. Perhaps more than anything, a Wizard's subclass helps them find their way towards this cosmic destiny, whatever it might be.

## Abjurer

Protection and banishment spells are the tools of the Abjurer, who primarily uses their magic to defend and redirect. Abjurers of Ember learn their most potent spells from the Abjuration school of magic, which is transparently one of the world's more ubiquitous arcane traditions. Although they can be found all throughout Ember, Abjurers are remarkably common among the war-torn [[Kelmezian]] city states, the Signara-savvy [[Waerd]] of the [[Duskflare Jungles]], and the moon-worshipping [[Kithil]] villages of the Kaustic Hinterlands.

## Bladesinger

The history of Bladesingers on Ember is an enigmatic one, with roots stretching far back to the ancient days of the long-forgotten [[Aedir]] Empire. And while the originators of this esoteric school of magic are long gone, the arcane tradition endures through the teachings of the Aedir's descendants (like the [[Lorai]] and the [[Nalvani]] peoples) as well as ancient texts, manuals, and illusory diagrams that have survived the ages. Bladesinging is one of the rarest arcane subclasses on Ember, and is only learned by a select few Wizards — whether they've inherited the traditions's esoteric techniques or sought them out.

## Diviner

Diviners utilize their magic to perceive the hidden and see the unseen, utilizing the so-called "sixth sense" of legend to detect that which has been obscured. Diviners of Ember are a somewhat rare lot, and many of the spells that comprise their school of study are relatively hard to find in the wild. The knowledge of Divination magic is rather guarded throughout many of Ember's realms, and the perceptive specialists who practice this tradition tend to go by names specific to their order or origin. The [[Rhivan]] Star Mages of the [[Star Academy]] are among the most celebrated of Diviners in the cosmos, and their arcane predictions — known for their keen precision and uncanny foresight — are held in high regard by the hopeful and the hidden alike (for equal but opposite reasons, of course).

## Evoker

Also known as Elementalists throughout most of Ember, Evokers are Wizards whose arcane spellcasting harnesses the metaphysical power of the elements. Naturally, Evokers of Ember have a strong relationship, reverence, or fascination with one or more of Ember's six moons, and weave spells that literally evoke the elemental magic of the living planet's lunar bodies. Furthermore, Elementalists are often combat-focused, finding opportunity in realms where their aggressive spells can see practical use. Some of Ember's most powerful Elementalists have emerged from places like the Kaustic Hinterlands, where the moon-worshipping [[Kithil]] live among the ruins of the ancient giants; or the ports of coastal cities like [[Caren'ac]] and [[Kalvard]].

## Illusionist

Illusionists are Wizards who specialize in magic that obscures the senses and warps the mind. And since spells from the school of Illusion are cosmically rooted in the tenebrous magic of [[Primordis]], Illusionists of Ember are often colloquially known as "Shade Mages." One of the more ubiquitous of Ember's arcane traditions, Illusionists are often dismissed as too playful or unnecessary (much like the Inner Realm of Primordis itself); but learned adventurers and sages alike know that Illusionists can become just as powerful as any Wizard, if not more so. After all, the mind is perhaps the most important of one's faculties; and Ember's Illusionists are masters of mind control. And wherever civilization or intrigue can be found — from the industrial corners of [[Caren'ac]] to the bustling districts of [[Ordain]] — so too can a competent Illusionist.

## Order of Scribes

Ember's Order of Scribes was founded by one of the world's oldest champions of [[Spectra]], the Elder Goddess of Magic. This champion — a powerful Kiska Wizard named Breña Vhast — established the Order's first sanctuary long ago in the [[Lowlands]] during the [[Age of the Tower]]. In the millenia since, casters of all stripes have sought out the Order's curious and esoteric traditions, so that they too might unravel the immortal cosmic mysteries of Ember's arcane Weave; thus, these arcane spellcasters end up serving the inscrutable whims of the elder goddess, whether they know it or not. Indeed, by merely perpetuating the Order and its teachings, each Order of Scribe Wizard on Ember becomes a historical part of Spectra's legacy. And in the years since the Order's inception, other strongholds have been established throughout Ember — such as the remote Palimpsest Tower of the [[Cascaal Peninsula]], and the venerated [[Scholar's Nook]] in Ordain.
