# Dangerous Descent

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Exploration Event can occur while traveling into the [[Splinter Canyons]] from the [[Golden Flats]]. In this Event, the party can:
>
> - Demonstrate their wilderness survival skills by preparing for and charting a course down the perilous cliff face.
> - Potentially overcome one or more hazards.
>
> #### Stages of Descent
>
> The canyon wall is 360 feet tall. The party must complete three stages of descent in order to reach the canyon floor; each stage represents approximately 120 feet of movement and takes approximately 1 hour. The stages of descent are:
>
> - [[Dangerous Descent]]
> - [[Dangerous Descent]]
> - [[Dangerous Descent]]

Each stage represents a Group Check — if at least half of the characters succeed, they proceed to the next stage of descent without incident. If over half of the characters fail, however, roll on the [[Dangerous Descent]] table. After resolving the hazard, the party proceeds to the next stage.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Making Preparations
>
> Before embarking on the journey, the party may describe any preparations they're making for the climb. Appropriate tools, equipment, magic, or other precautions may grant **+2 Boons** or automatic success on a relevant roll, per the Gamemaster's discretion.
>
> Such preparations may include: rope, pitons, climbing gear, anchored vines, magical flight, or any other sensible method of descent.

### Stage 1: Charting a Course

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Stage 1: Chart a Course
>
> The characters must first identify a safe path down the canyon wall with a group skill challenge. Every member of the party must make one of the following tests:
>
> - A successful **Wilderness (DC 14, Group)** check to identify a navigable way down the thick vines cascading down the cliff face.
> - A successful **Awareness (DC 14, Group)** check to determine the best course carefully edging across a series of narrow ledges and shallow footholds that pockmark the cliff face.
>
> If the group check is failed, roll on the [[Climbing Hazards]] table.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Stage 1: Falling
>
> Characters that fall from the effects of a Climbing Hazard fall 360 feet, suffering a **Terminal Fall (Hazard 360, Fortitude, Severe)** and landing **Prone**.

### Stage 2: Making the Descent

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Stage 2: Making the Descent
>
> The characters must next traverse the most dangerous portion of the climb with a group skill challenge. Every member of the party must make one of the following tests:
>
> - A successful **Athletics (DC 16, Group)** check to use their strength and leverage, performing the heavy lifting across difficult stretches and securing handholds for their companions.
> - A successful **Athletics (DC 16, Group)** check to use their agility and balance to trailblaze nimbly across narrow ledges, ensuring a steady footing for their companions.
>
> If the group check is failed, roll on the [[Climbing Hazards]] table.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Stage 2: Falling
>
> Characters that fall from the effects of a [[Dangerous Descent]] fall 240 feet, suffering **Critical Fall (Hazard 240, Fortitude, Severe)** and landing **Prone**.

### Stage 3: Reaching the Canyon Floor

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Stage 3: Reaching the Canyon Floor
>
> The party must succeed on a final **Wilderness (DC 16, Group)** group check to withstand the grueling demands of the extended descent to safely reach the canyon floor below.
>
> If the group check is failed, roll on the [[Climbing Hazards]] table.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Stage 3: Falling
>
> Characters that fall from the effects of a [[Dangerous Descent]] fall 120 feet, suffering **Deadly Fall (Hazard 120, Fortitude, Severe)** and landing **Prone**.

### Concluding the Event

Once the party has reached the floor of the Splinter Canyons, they're free to continue on their journey.
