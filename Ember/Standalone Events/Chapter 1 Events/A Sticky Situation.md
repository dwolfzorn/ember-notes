# A Sticky Situation

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This standalone event set in the [[Verdant Paths]] places the characters face-to-face with two monstrous arachnids. In this standalone event, the party must:
>
> - Avoid damage and disorientation as they tumble down into a overgrown gulley during a sudden landslide.
> - Survive an untimely attack from two [[Sitherian]], who lair in a small cave nearby.
> - Search the cave for assorted loot from previous victims of the predatory arachnids.
>
> This event takes place on the [[Verdant Paths]] area map. This event is more likely to occur if the party is moving at a Fast or Reckless travel pace. This event may repeat, but will not do so for at least 24 hours.

The unstable earth beneath the party's feet gives away causing the group to tumble down a small embankment into a gully.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Tumble in the Rough
>
> Each character must face a **Minor Rockslide (Hazard 4, Reflex, Bludgeoning)** from the sudden but sloped fall. Any character who fails this save by more than 10 also gains the **Prone** condition.
>
> While they've already spied one of the creatures, there are a total of two [[Sitherian]] here: the initial assailant in the cliffside cave to the southeast, and another hidden Sitherian among the foliage to the northwest.
>
> #### Arachnid Attack!
>
> Characters with **Awareness (DC 16, Passive)** are able to spot the hidden Sitherian to the northwest, who will attempt to engage the party unseen. While the Sitherian remains hidden, it has advantage on attacks against enemy characters who fail to detect it.
>
> #### Sitherian Tactics
>
> The two arachnids are hungry and eager to attack the party.
>
> They will attack the character deemed the most vulnerable or who poses the greatest threat, rapidly converging to use their [[Venomous Bite]], working together to gain the benefits of [[Pack Hunter]].
>
> If additional challenge is desired, the Gamemaster can rule that the Sitherian have created some sections of webbing using [[Web Spinner]] prior to the beginning of combat.
>
> If either Sitherian becomes **Broken** or if their companion is killed, they will attempt to retreat from combat and preserve their lives. They will utilize the rugged terrain and their ability to quickly climb to escape from harm.

If the characters think to search the small cliffside cave during the battle, they may find a few accoutrements that could prove useful to them during combat. Otherwise, the dank lair of these Sitherian awaits scrutiny after the fight.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Dank and strewn with aged webbing, this small cliffside cave appears to be the private lair of the large arachnid beasts who just attacked you. The fragmented skeletons of several smaller creatures hang suspended in the webs, and there is a pervasive odor of musky decay here. Your skin crawls with apprehension during each moment you spend amidst this dismal shelter.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Investigating the Cavern
>
> Any character who takes the time to search the cave will spot some [[Spider-cap Mushroom]] growing from rotted webbing and decaying molted husks of the Sitherian who dwelt here. A successful **Wilderness (DC 14)** check allows a character to harvest `[[/roll 1d3+1 #Mushroom Samples]]` usable samples of the rare ingredient.
>
> The first character to succeed on a **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the cave will also locate the desiccated corpse of an ill-fated humanoid in the webbing, with the following items to be found upon it:
>
> - A mold-covered but still effective [[Healing Tonic]].
> - `[[/roll 1d2]]` random results on the [[Corpse Loot]] Table.
