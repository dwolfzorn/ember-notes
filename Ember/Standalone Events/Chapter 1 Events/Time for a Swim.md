# Time for a Swim

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This standalone event occurs while the characters are traveling the aqueous expanse of the [[Dripstones]] at a fast or reckless pace, and presents them with an immediate set of skill challenges that will determine whether or not they become hurt and/or lost. In this event, the party can:
>
> - Attempt to avoid bludgeoning damage wrought by the rough, preternatural currents of the biome's magical "quickwater."
> - Try to stave off a level of Exhaustion that might occur from being swept up in this untimely slipstream.
> - Determine if they've been thrown off-course by this untimely encounter, and precisely how much.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Slipstream Hazard
>
> The characters must immediately struggle to avoid being pummeled by the preternatural currents of the quickwater's slipstream. Each character must withstand the **Raging Slipstream (Hazard 4, Fortitude, Bludgeoning)**}. Any character which fails to resist this hazard also suffers the **Exhausted** condition.
>
> - Characters with **Knowledge: Seafaring** avoid exhaustion even if they are affected by the hazard.
>
> #### Swept Off Course
>
> The untimely deluge of the quickwater may have impeded the party's journey, forcing them to spend time recovering from the torrent. The extent of this inconvenience is determined based on how effectively the group withstood the hazard of the chaotic slipstream.
>
> - If less than half the party was affected, the characters spend 1 hour recovering from the maelstrom.
> - If more than half, but not all of the party was affected, the characters spend 2 hours recovering.
> - If the entire party was affected by the maelstrom, they lose 4 hours recovering from the ordeal and the party is forcibly relocated to a hex adjacent to the current one in a direction approximately opposite their intended path of movement.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Gathering Quickwater
>
> Characters who manage to withstand the torrential Quickwater and who have the wherewithal to collect a sample of the arcane deluge can attempt to do so with a successful **Athletics (DC 15)**check and a suitable container for capturing the elusive liquid. On a success, the character harvests `[[/roll 1d2]]` vials or doses of [[Quickwater]].
