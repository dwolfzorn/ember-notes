# Shimmering Dark

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This **Exploration** event allows the party to encounter the mind-altering magical effects prevalent in the Kaleidoscope Caverns. In this event the party can:
>
> - Discover a treacherous new biome, the [[Kaleidoscope Caverns]].
> - Collect unique crystals that form here which could be interesting, useful, or dangerous.

### Cavern Exploration

The crystals of the kaleidoscope cavern are mesmerizing, but spending too long in their presence can have a mind-altering effect on those who travel nearby. As the cavern's magical effects take hold, read or paraphrase the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Each time you blink or turn your head, the light from within the violet crystals seems to shift, creating a hypnotic effect that makes it difficult to concentrate on anything else. As your vision focuses in on the shimmering swirl of light you feel increasingly woozy as countless dots of light infiltrate your field of view and can't be shaken off.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Crystal Calamity
>
> Characters encountering this event will find the crystal caverns have begun to influence their perceptions. Characters with **Knowledge: Subterranea** immediately suspect that the crystals formed in this place are not entirely natural.
>
> Any character who can see the light emitted by a cluster of kaleidoscope crystals must face **Kaleidoscopic Disorientation (Hazard 4, Willpower, Psychic, Morale)**. If this hazard is not resisted, the character additionally suffers from one of the following effects which persist until the successful completion of a Rest.
>
> |  | #### Kaleidoscope Crystal Effects |
> | --- | --- |
> | 1 | #### Drained  The light from the crystal saps all of your energy. You are overtaken by an unnatural wave of lethargy and you gain the **Exhausted** condition. |
> | 2 | #### Intoxicated  You feel unsteady on your feet, your limbs are sluggish, and your mind is dulled. You are affected by the **Exposed** condition. |
> | 3 | #### Confused  You are befuddled, your thoughts clouded, and words jumbled. You have trouble speaking coherently. You are affected by the **Disoriented**condition. |
> | 4 | #### Haunted  You hear ominous whispers in the shadows all around you. These voices are all you can hear and you suffer the effects of the **Deafened** condition. |
> | 5 | #### Light Sensitive  All light becomes painful and overwhelming for your eyes. You suffer the effects of the **Blinded** condition. |
> | 6 | #### Focused Mind  Your mind becomes sharp and resilient against distractions. You gain the benefits of the **Resolute** condition. |
>
> After facing this test, the characters are immune to the effects of the kaleidoscope crystals for 24 hours.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Taking a Kaleidoscope Crystal
>
> If characters want to take a [[Kaleidoscope Crystal]], they must break it off of the cavern wall or floor. Liberating a crystal from where it is embedded in the cavern wall requires a successful **Athletics (DC 16)** check to extract it from the cave wall.
>
> Attempting this check and failing causes the crystal to shatter, generating an immediate release of magical energy that immediately inflicts a [[Kaleidoscope Crystal Effects]] to anyone who is not already immune to the effects.
