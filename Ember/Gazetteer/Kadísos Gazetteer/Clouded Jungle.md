# Clouded Jungle

**Work In Progress!** This biome and its accompanying entry are not in the scope of Alpha One Gameplay. All text provided here is currently **in development** and **incomplete**. This entry will be revised and expanded on in future updates.

The lush, thick and verdant jungle of Kadisos is the largest biome on the island, and teems with life.

### Environment

Travel through the jungle is slow and perilous as there are no paths or roads to follow, requiring expeditions to hack their way through the undergrowth to make any sort of progress. Here and there one can find crumbling ruins and markers of an ancient people that once called this island home. Even these ruins are treacherous, threatening to topple or collapse if not treated with care.

### Flora

The jungles of Kadisos are diverse, and choked with plants. Large tropical foxglove trees, and palms blot out the sun as exotic plants and thick undergrowth make all but the most well trod paths a pain to travel. Scattered amongst the dense foliage are a number of ancient volcanic stone ruins, encrusted with moss, leaves or fungi, they seem small and broken against the ceaseless growth of nature. Numerous jungle plants grow in this biome that are hazardous, either through their toxic nature, or by their ability to strike the unwary without warning.

Many powerful creatures and monsters thrive in the humidity and heat of the jungle basin, travelling through its depths and making prey of anything in their path. Given the extreme density of ground cover, threats of ambush are high. To make matters worse, many of the island's natural predators and creatures are particularly adept at stalking through the fog that frequently settles over the island.

### Lore

The locals at Graven's Rest openly recommend against going too deep into the jungles as that's where the greatest dangers reside. This is especially true when the fog rolls in and makes it impossible to travel with purpose. During this time a terrible monster, the "Mist Stalker" some call it, is said to hunt anyone who doesn't belong. Rational thinking would lead one to believe that anyone who vanishes into the jungles likely just got lost and died of exposure or wildlife, but this is an island steeped in old magic… so perhaps it's not entirely far fetched.

For all the danger and difficulty it presents, the jungles are also a great source of useful materials for anyone with the skills to extract them. There are numerous types of wood, plant material and vines which can be used for construction, as well as edible plants, medicinal herbs to sustain oneself with, not to mention countless wild animals to make use of.

Many of the creatures in this biome and on Kadisos as a whole are not found elsewhere, making knowledge of them rather uncommon. Given how generally unexplored the region is, even seasoned botanists and naturalists are likely to discover plants they have never seen before.
