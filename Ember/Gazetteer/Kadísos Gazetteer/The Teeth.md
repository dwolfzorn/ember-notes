# The Teeth

**Work In Progress!** This biome and its accompanying entry are not in the scope of Alpha One Gameplay. All text provided here is currently **in development** and **incomplete**. This entry will be revised and expanded on in future updates.

The Teeth of Kadisos is a treacherous and desolate region, characterized by its sharp, unforgiving cliffs and jagged rock formations. Located in the northwestern part of the island, this area is littered with the wreckage of unfortunate ships that have fallen victim to the powerful winds that constantly sweep along its length.

### Environment

These broken remnants serve as a haunting reminder of the dangers that await those who venture too close to this cursed place. The beaches are muddy and inhospitable, offering no respite or shelter to survivors. Even those who manage to reach the safety of the rocky shores are not safe from danger, as the remote nature of this region presents an additional challenge for anyone attempting to escape.

### Ecology

The treacherous cliffs and jagged rock formations offer little purchase for vegetation to take root, and is largely a gray, desolate wasteland of jagged stone. However, there are a few resilient plant species that have managed to adapt to this harsh terrain such as mosses and lichens which cling to the crevices of the rocks, their pale green hues contrasting against the rugged landscape. These hardy organisms are able to survive in the salt-laden air and withstand the strong winds that constantly buffet the area. Beyond this, some small shrubs and grasses can be found scattered sparingly along the coastline.

The Teeth make it difficult for most creatures to survive, with the treacherous rocks and limited presence of plant life further restricting the availability of resources for animals. As a result, the fauna in the Teeth biome is sparse and specialized, with mostly small lizards and rodents living between rocks and preying on each other and insects.

The only large predators of note are the crag raptor and kadhana lizard. These agile and stealthy predators make their nests among the cliffs, taking advantage of their superior mobility to hunt each other and their nests.

In addition to these predators, there is another macabre presence in the Teeth biome known as gore bird murders. These scavenging birds feast on carcasses littered along the beaches from shipwrecks, taking advantage of any unfortunate souls who meet their demise in this treacherous place.

### Lore

Remnants of the great cataclysm that destroyed the ancient people of Kadisos the first time, the teeth are magically hardened shards of stone forced out of the earth. Though they are not themselves malevolent, the winds, tides, and teeth seemingly work together to drag ships into the stony maw. No story of sailing around Kadisos is complete without a tale of a close call or brush with the teeth and certain death they promise.
