# Reaver Ocean

**Work In Progress!** This biome and its accompanying entry are not in the scope of Alpha One Gameplay. All text provided here is currently **in development** and **incomplete**. This entry will be revised and expanded on in future updates.

The Reaver Ocean, surrounding Kadisos entirely, is a vast expanse of water that connects the island with numerous nearby regions.

### Environment

The Reaver Ocean is known for its tumultuous nature, especially around Kadisos, with frequent natural and unnatural storms making navigation around the island treacherous at best, and deadly at worst. To seek refuge from these dangers, many vessels anchor in the relative safety of Graven's Rest sea cave. Here, they can find repairs offered by locals and gather necessary supplies for their journeys.

While there are shallow areas around the island, with sharp rocks meeting the water's edge, there are also rocky and sandy beaches scattered along the coast. However, as one ventures further from shore, the depth of the water plunges suddenly into an abyssal ocean plateau.

### Ecology

The Reaver Ocean is a vast and diverse ecosystem, teeming with unique and resilient marine flora. Seaweed forests sway with the ebb and flow of the currents, providing shelter and food for a variety of sea creatures, and colorful coral reefs dot the ocean floor, creating vibrant undersea landscapes teeming with life.

Floating plants known as sea drifters can also be found in the Reaver Ocean. These plants have adapted to life on the open water, utilizing air-filled bladders to remain buoyant as they drift along with the ocean currents, often serving as perches for migratory birds or cover for lurking sea life.

The Reaver Ocean supports a rich diversity of marine life, ranging from small creatures to magnificent sea giants. Schools of shimmering fish dart through the water, their scales reflecting sunlight in a dazzling display, chased by nearly invisible slips of mercury-skinned sharks.

### Lore

In addition to these familiar creatures, there are also mysterious and elusive beings that dwell in the depths of the Reaver Ocean. Deep-sea bioluminescent organisms light up the darkness with their ethereal glow, and much worse which no mortal has ever seen before.

Overall, the Reaver Ocean is a realm of wonder and danger where a diverse array of plants and animals coexist beneath its turbulent surface.
