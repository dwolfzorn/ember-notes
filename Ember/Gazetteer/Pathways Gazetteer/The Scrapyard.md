# The Scrapyard

The Scrapyard is a cruel and uninviting place where constructs go to die, and Silver Beam can conduct their most unsavory operations in secrecy. As with any Silver Beam operation, visitors are unwelcome, and workers will use force if necessary to drive off interlopers.

## Environment

This open-air forge and junkyard is nestled in a narrow stretch of high, rocky walls covered in the Sinkhole's natural flora. Navigating the area requires significant climbing to avoid being funneled by natural stone pathways and barriers of old, rusting metal.

## Ecology

Rocky and overgrown with local flora, the scrapyard is home to all manner of little critters and creatures that make nests in the rocks and heaps of scrap. Even the rock burrowing Crevvet can be found here, the skinny little rat-like beings having invaded the depths long ago. Here, these creatures can hide out safely, as larger predators are unlikely to approach a site actively occupied by humanoids and constructs.

## Lore

This site was established by the Silver Beam Consortium out in the Sinkhole Depths to hide the untoward activities of those assigned to work the forge and scrap heaps. The work here is all done in support of the larger operation being undertaken in the Silver Beam headquarters found in Arcturel.

Here a team of Silver Beam engineers and constructs take apart Chessian constructs, repurposing their parts and melting down their scrap to be re-forged into material for creating new Silver Beam constructs. The internal corpses of the doomed constructs are also altered before being shipped back to Arcturel for final assembly, programming, and activation.
