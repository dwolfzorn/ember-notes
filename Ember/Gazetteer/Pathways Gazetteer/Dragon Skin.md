# Dragon Skin

Left in a somewhat remote part of the Pathways, the skin of an ancient dragon molders in the dark corner of the mycelian expanse, though its presence is all but impossibly to miss if one travels through the massive tunnel where it rests.

### Environment

The ground around it was torn up and dug out as the dragon struggled to pull itself free of its skin. However, the area is not remarkable otherwise.

### Ecology

There is a stark absence of both plant and animal life around the skin, suggesting an unwelcoming aura, ensuring no living things dare grow or linger here.

### Lore

This massive skin once belonged to the dragon Zeranyss, and was abruptly deserted after the massive being shed it. Over the years, the lair has become a site of interest for Mutagist scientists seeking unique resources for their research. Simultaneously, it has attracted at least one lunatic who pays homage to Zeranyss, worshiping the dragon with a shrine resting beneath the dead of the discarded skin.
