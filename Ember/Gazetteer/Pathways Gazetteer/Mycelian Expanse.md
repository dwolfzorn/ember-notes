# Mycelian Expanse

The Mycelian Expanse is mostly flat ground with few changes in elevation of note save for a few plateaus and winding tunnels. However, the area is home to several rivers that flow toward a massive central chasm which descends abruptly into the core of Ember and presents the main navigational obstacle of the region.

### Environment

Travel through the fungal groves of the expanse is challenging and disorienting, as the region has no discernible sense of night or day, leaving travelers in a perpetual twilight. Despite the name, this area of the Pathways has little in the way of useful landmarks or unique terrain to aid travel, making it very easy to become lost.

### Ecology

This region is overgrown with life of all shapes, kinds, and dispositions. The most abundant life in the expanse is fungal in nature, though many plants live in these lightless caves without any difficulty, seemingly surviving despite lack of sunlight. The fauna of the region is equally varied and in many ways utterly alien to anything seen on the surface.

### Lore

Once, the burning blood of Ember flowed through these massive caverns, bringing with it the powerful life magic that shaped the whole of the world. Over time the currents of magically charged magma shifted, and diverted to other regions, leaving behind massive tubes and chambers of stone.

Over time the stone cooled and the lingering magic began to affect the region. Ancient remnants of matter and life left behind when the expanse pulsed with life began to grow and warp, changed by the ambient magic swirling in the dark spaces. Before long glowing mushrooms forms, fields of strange grasses sprouted up, and gnarled trees with glowing leaves became home to an ever growing menagerie of strange life forms.
