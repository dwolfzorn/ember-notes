# Voidspan Bridge

This massive bridge crosses an underground chasm that plunges into the dark depths of Ember. Without it, crossing the gap would take much longer.

### Environment

Slung across a monumentally large chasm in the center of the vast mushroom caverns in the Pathways, the Voidspan Bridge stands out with its glowing chains and levitating stone. The stone itself is clean but worn, the ancient stone preserved by the powerful magic flowing through it. The air is filled with a powerful hum and periodic rumbling that stem from the energy flowing through the chains and the occasional grinding of the stone sections moving against each other.

### Ecology

The bridge itself remains devoid of typical plant or animal life using it as a habitat, and the magical hum of this space almost certainly wards off any creatures that might consider crossing it.

### Lore

The Voidspan Bridge is one of the many great mysteries found in the Pathways beneath Ember. Built by Giants that predate even the Shent, the size and enduring nature of the construction hint at advanced magical technology that has withstood eons of weathering.
