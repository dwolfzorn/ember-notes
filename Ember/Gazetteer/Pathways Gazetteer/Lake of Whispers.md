# Lake of Whispers

This large lake lies deep underground within the Pathways of Ember. Very little light illuminates its dark waters, and a soft, almost imperceptible whisper lingers over its surface.

### Environment

The lake itself is calm, and a low fog constantly clings to the surface of the water. Aside from the rare swirl or eddy from below, the lake is utterly devoid of life. No fish swim in the water, no insects can be seen hovering above, and the water is so clear that even smaller creatures and flora are absent. It is an eerie and chilling place, and while it seems safe to cross at first glance, its depth could conceal any number of aquatic dangers.

### Ecology

There are no fish living in the lake and no natural life forms of any kind. However, during certain times of the year, abyssal entities like the Abyssal Eels swarm above the waters in large numbers.

### Lore

The Lake of Whispers is at one end of a much larger pathway that is completely filled with water. It runs deep into the depths of the world, and while at some point the twists and turns of the sections below curve and split in such a way that prevents water from flowing in large amounts into the Fogbound Caverns, just enough reaches the lake to prevent stagnation. At some point, this lake was likely used by the first Giants, but any trace of their presence has long since disappeared.
