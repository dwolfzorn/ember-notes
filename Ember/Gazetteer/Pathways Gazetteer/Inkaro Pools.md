# Inkaro Pools

This particular acreage of pools is owned and operated by the Silver Beam Consortium. Like other Silver Beam holdings, the Inkaro Pools are strictly off-limits to interlopers and uninvited guests. A small team of Silver Beam guards patrol the perimeter surrounding the Pools and drive off trespassers without a second thought.

## Environment

The large pools are deep and naturally roiling thanks to the gasses bubbling up from the rocks that cover the bottom of them. While some of the pools here are accessible by wooden decks and walkways, many of them are lined by wet stones and nothing more.

## Ecology

This collection of subterranean water basins serves as natural habitats for Pathways creatures known as Mootaps — monstrous, crustacean-like predators who produce the luminous spheres known as Inkaro Pearls. Beyond this the pools are host to all manner of much smaller, much less dangerous aquatic and amphibious life such as Puddle Scuttlers, Acid Dart Eels, and schools of diminutive of Bubble Fish.

## Lore

These pools have been worked by Arcturel for decades, first by locals, then by House Cevher, and most recently by the Silver Beam Consortium. While they are not the only pools in this region that host Mootaps, these are the closest to Arcturel, and the most productive that have been found thus far. As a result, whenever anyone from Arcturel talks about "the Inarko Pools" they almost certainly mean this location specifically.

The work of harvesting Inkaro pearls from the gullets of Mootaps is dangerous work at the best of times and has claimed numerous lives over the decades. The task requires the Mootap be restrained and stunned before a worker can dig out the large pearls. Any misstep in this process can prove fatal, and that danger has led to the rise of automation as a means of making it safer. Now, teams of constructs work the pools, overseen by flesh and blood supervisors, with minimal risk to mortal life.

The magically infused Inkaro pearls are Arcturel’s most prominent export, as they have many uses and are utilized across the plateau as sources of power for constructs, reagents for spells, and powerful light sources to beat back the dark.
