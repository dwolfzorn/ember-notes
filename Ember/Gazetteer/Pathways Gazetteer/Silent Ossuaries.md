# Silent Ossuaries

Nestled in the caves are a collection of man-made spaces where the remains of ancient humans are stored.

### Environment

While not inherently difference from the rest of the Myceliean Expanse, this secluded area is extensively populated with artifacts, creations, and decorations of a long-lost society that once ventured into these depths.

### Ecology

This area is densely overgrown with the natural flora of the expanse, and no signs of curation are visible anywhere. This site has sat abandoned and forgotten for centuries at least, allowing nature to reclaim the space.

### Lore

Long ago, before the great cities were built, ancient Arcturians built tombs in natural caves all over the plateau. This silent ossuary is nearly identical to those ancient tombs, though its placement is unexpected.

It's unknown if these tombs were established by the ancient Arcturian peoples the ventured into the pathways using the mechanisms of an even older people, or if they were built by an offshoot that resided down in the dark below ember Surface. In either case, they found a secluded space where they could lay their dead to rest, and generations of bones now are interred there.
