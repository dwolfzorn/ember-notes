# Primordial Bastion

The bastion is undoubtedly a masterful construction from a people that have long since vanished from Ember. One of the most interesting aspects of this structure is that it seems to have been carefully and meticulously preserved, left in a deliberate state to repel intruders. Traps are carefully laid out; puzzles and the interpretation of runic script require access to inner areas. However, there are hints that the structure was once used for a different purpose, such as small empty holes in some of the walls and areas where the design of the floor’s patterns has clearly been cut away to accommodate defensive features.

### The Lightless Halls

Within the bastion, there are a series of large chambers and halls, connected by enormous, soaring hallways that rise nearly 50 feet to the ceilings. This colossal edifice has endured countless years without any noticeable faults or cracks; when carefully observed, it seems to shimmer slightly from magical reinforcement. Only a thick layer of dark gray dust covers the floors of the chambers. Throughout the dim halls, some rooms are filled with ancient runic carvings, machines, traps, softly glimmering golden adornments, and a deep unsettling silence.

### Ecology

There is little, if anything, growing within or around the bastion. Even the sickly pale plants and mushrooms of the surrounding biome of the Fogbound caverns seem to avoid encroaching too closely to the smooth stone walls.

### Lore

The bastion was constructed by the first Giants of Ember long ago during the Age of Beasts. This ancient era of the world remains nearly entirely unknown, but it’s evident that at some point, the Giants who built this structure abandoned it. One particularly curious aspect of the bastion is especially mysterious, given what is known about the Giants of this period. They had little, if any, contact with or knowledge of the surface and were believed to have stayed underground until they emerged to see the skies for the first time after the Unveiling. So how then could they have possessed knowledge of Lantyr to build or create an artifact that harnesses some of her power?
