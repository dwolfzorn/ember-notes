# Sinkhole Depths

Home to the sprawling multilevel settlement of Arcturel, the Sinkhole Depths is the most easily accessed part of the Pathways in the Arctus Plateau, and as a result is the most well known and well studied of the vast underground regions in the world. It is a landscape rife with natural and unnatural resources, not to mention untold dangers lurking in the shadows.

### Environment

Though downright treacherous in places, much of the terrain is easy enough to travel, and can even support the use of drawn wagons if routes are chosen carefully. Unlike other locations beneath the surface of Ember, the depths are mostly open, without winding tunnels to make navigation confusing. However, the rocky, uneven ground is not entirely yielding, and the presence of long fissures in the earth means that traveling aimlessly through the depths can quickly become fatal if one is not paying attention.

### Ecology

Mammoth broadleaf trees with shimmering leaves tower over patches of rust-colored mushrooms, creating a unique ecosystem that thrives in the absence of reliable sunlight. The denizens of the depths are numerous, and dangerous, with countless minor Inkaro pools dotting the landscape, many of them home to lurking Mootaps, while packs of savage Jurtak prowl the lightless spaces, looking for unwary prey that have wandered too far from the light.

Scattered throughout the rocky terrain are low places that have formed natural basins where mineral rich water can collect and feed local plant life and sustain the various creatures that roam the area. Though not every pool is host to them, Mootaps can frequently be found within, preying on any creatures that get too complacent in the water.

Though the dense spore content in the air below the depths is harmless to humanoids, it does tend to coat everything, and can't be easily avoided. These spores can spoil unprotected food and water supplies, cause rot and mold to take root very easily, and even cause difficulty breathing when especially dense. This only adds to the dangers and difficulties of long-term survival in the region.

### Lore

The Sinkhole Depths have long been a source of fascination and mystery. It is the shallowest point of the Pathways in the Arctus Plateau, and thus was able to be accessed when a massive sinkhole opened up overhead. Over the years it has been home to mining operations, hunting expeditions, failed attempts to establish camps and settlements, and any number of surveys. This makes it the most well mapped and well traveled section of Ember's subterranean expanses.

However, the depths are no less dangerous than any other section of Pathways, and many expeditions and ill-planned endeavors have come to swift, tragic ends as a result of the Sinkhole's many hazards. To date, only Arcturel and its nearby camps and operations have withstood the trials of the Sinkhole Depths. These settlements serve as the starting point for any new ventures into the spore coated gloom around it.
