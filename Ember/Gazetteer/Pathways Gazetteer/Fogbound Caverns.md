# Fogbound Caverns

The Fogbound caverns are a small biome deep within the pathways, lying below the [[Mycelian Expanse]] by several miles. It is an eerie, deeply unpleasant, and ultimately horrifying place that few dare to tread, even with the promise of extensive rewards. It is a place where soft, unsettling words or screams creep into the mind, and some believe the whispers are echoes of long-forgotten secrets, carried on tendrils of entangled darkness. Though few have dared to explore deeply, the caverns hold a prominent place in some circles as a realm best left undisturbed except for the truly brave, with rich rewards somewhere hidden within.

### Environment

Navigating the Fogbound Caverns is a challenging endeavor. The slick, damp rock from the heavy fog makes navigation treacherous, and the prevailing darkness is made all the worse by the thick fog itself, which limits visibility and masks terrain features like sudden drops and narrow paths. Dripping stalactites far above quickly add to the damp and wet environment, creating additional perils by coating surfaces with a slick, hazardous layer of gelatinous ooze. The ever-shifting shadows in this darkened landscape contribute to the feeling of intense disquiet and foreboding. While the caverns are eerily silent and seemingly free of immediate dangers, the occasional horrifying soft scream or snarl can be heard in the distance.

### Ecology

There are no natural creatures or plants living in the Fogbound Caverns; everything that exists in the gloom is a twisted form of something else or an entity from beyond Ember entirely. Misshapen fungi cling to dark rocks, their pale, ghostly glow offering minimal light. The blighted earth supports little else, and the fog creates a damp, moldy environment that is both cold and inhospitable.

Some creatures venture into the caverns from elsewhere, becoming corrupted by the twisting forces and energies present and beginning to blend into the fog and stone. These creatures, while elusive, pose a constant threat, as stealth and surprise are their favored tactics. One of the most common entities encountered in the biome are Abyssal Eels that roam in large swarms and devour whatever life they encounter.

### Lore

This is a place rife with Abyssal corruption and energy which has seeped into the stone, poisoned the wildlife and plants here, and turned a once vibrant stretch of the Mycelian Expanse into a withered and foreboding hole that no wise being should ever venture into. It was once home to a shining outpost of the First Giants, and the lake that now exists in the center of the Fogbound Caverns was once a traversable tunnel leading to other First Giant settlements. Unfortunately, during the Unveiling, this part of the Pathways suffered greatly, and the Orb of Lantyr became a crucial relic in stopping the spread of corruption.
