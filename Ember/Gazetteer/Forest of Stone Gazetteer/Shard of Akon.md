# Shard of Akon

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>

>
> - This location is depicted by the "Shard of Akon" composition of [[Vista: Verdant Paths]].
> - This location is featured in the [[Sheltered Campsite]] event.

### Environment

Scattered throughout the area are large, metallic-looking ruins that cast long shadows and hang at odd angles, blocking the sky. This cool, damp, sheltered space has been heavily used by travelers as a resting spot, with many newer bolts, hooks, and furnishings affixed to the metal ruins. Campsites have been cleared of underbrush, and old and discarded items are hidden among plants, such as rufuse, broken tools, and broken pottery. It's evident from a cursory glance that travelers often camp here, and there are small collections of fallen logs assembled around soot-stained fire pits among the ruins as well.

### Ecology

The air beneath the Akon ruins is damp and still, with the structures providing ample shade from the overhead sun. Numerous insects scuttle around in the shadows and across the moist soil. Many plants, like bushes and vines, appear to have gradually crept over the metal ruins over centuries, accumulating and contributing to their decay.

### Lore

During the Shattering two and a half thousand years ago, the moon of Akon was shattered, and numerous pieces, both large and small, fell onto the surface world. It is not entirely clear which part of the moon these shards originated from, but their design is extremely unusual, featuring twisting shapes and intricate markings. These shards are also heavily weathered, with rusted and dulled surfaces that make them appear more like twisted rocks than metal. However, scraping away the outer layer can reveal the truth. Very few people know the true nature of these ruins, and that they are a part of the moon of Akon. But as many caravans pass through the Forest of Stone each year, this sheltered campsite has become a key point of interest on the route to and from [[Helkas]] in the east.
