# Crystal Carving Cavern

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists within the [[Kaleidoscope Caverns]] in ⬢ s.3198.2867, and was once used by the Shent to carve crystals for experimentation.
>
> - This location is explored in the "Arcane Machine" composition of the [[Kaleidoscope Caverns]] area map.
> - This location is featured in the [[Multifaceted]] event.

### Environment

Throughout the cavern, signs of ancient industry are evident everywhere. Many of the larger crystals have small chips and cracks, likely from mining activities or samples taken for study. Some crystals are wrapped with bands of bronze metal for reinforcement or containment. Scattered, brittle tools, fading and crumbling, are found in piles or corners. The same can be said for bones and remains of ancient Giants; there are several scattered around the cavern, all of which seem to have met violent and terrible ends with twisted limbs, bones lying at odd angles, and skulls smashed by tremendous force.

### Ecology

Initially, the cavern appears lifeless, but after a few moments, several small creatures become visible. Tiny, strange-looking lizards with crystal-like scales can be seen, and tiny nearly invisible, eels with transparent skin drift through the air, shimmering and vanishing in the faint light.

### Lore

Long abandoned, there is no indication of how long the Shent visited and utilized the cavern to gather the crystals, though it's likely they only utilized the crystal technology around the time of the Shattering, roughly two and a half thousand years ago. In the center of the cave stands a now badly damaged brass machine. Originally created by the sage Mioroth, it was intended to protect the region from the confusing magic of the kaleidoscope crystals. The Shent used this cavern to carve runic symbols into crystals, transforming them into personal wards in their upcoming fight against the Abyss.
