# Verdant Paths

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This is the predominant biome of the Forest of Stone, located on the western side of the Arctus Plateau.
>
> - Travel through this biome is generally [[Region Exploration]].
> - This biome is visually depicted by [[Vista: Verdant Paths]] and the [[Verdant Paths]] area map.
> - The Pollen Storm [[Weather]] event can originate in this biome.

The Verdant Paths are an expanse of stone cliffs and canyons whose rocky surfaces are coated with signs of life – crawling vines, shocks of color from local mosses, and trees with twisting branches covered in bright foliage. Ample shade from the overgrown cliffs creates numerous stretches of dim, dappled space to rest and hide. The paths are filled with wildlife as a result, with countless safe havens and habitats for all manner of creatures to flourish in.

### Environment

Traveling through the winding passages is easy and comfortable in most cases, with plenty of shade, water, and well-worn terrain with minimal obstacles beyond the infrequent landslide or collapsed archway. Shade from dense overhead foliage creates a cool, dim environment that is often damp with condensation, but gives easy escape from the sun.

It is only vertical traversal that becomes slow and hazardous, as the mossy crags and overgrown cliffs make it hard to judge perches and handholds, many of which are slippery or unstable.

### Ecology

The Verdant Paths abound in botanical and mineral wealth, and teems with curious animals suitable for hunting and trapping, and perhaps even domestication. Despite the richness of the region, there is only one active mine in the paths, as the remote and sometimes difficult to access nature of the paths makes accessing its treasures a trial.

### Lore

The Verdant Paths, like the rest of the Forest of Stone. was created in a battle between two giant creatures – a Leviathan and a wurm – who tore through what was once one of the large foothills at the base of the Hersa Mountains, opening its caverns to the sky and leaving broad passageways through the newly-created Forest of Stone in their wake.

Over time, these passageways changed dramatically, their original forms being lost to the march of time. Through weathering of wind and rain, the movement of animals, the growth of local plants, and the cycling of the seasons the region was transformed into the Verdant Paths seen today.

In the past this region also fell under the frontier regions of the Shent culture, and was home to at least two mines and a temple, if not more. As a border region of their nation and the ever expanding Aedir empire, it was also home to a spy post used to monitor the area.
