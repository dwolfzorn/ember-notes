# Overgrown Sunwell

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists within the [[Verdant Paths]] in ⬢ s.3211.2872, three miles south of [[Helkas]].
>
> - There is no area map or vista composition that visually depicts this location.
> - This location is featured in the [[Well Enough Alone]] event.
> - Its appearance on the region map changes depending on the [[Well Enough Alone]] outcome of that event.

This particular Sunwell is significantly overgrown by the lush flora of the Verdant Paths and missing its Wellstone, a central orb that attracts solar energy and allows it to gather within the Sunwell's shallow basin. Consequently, this Sunwell is inoperable unless the overgrowth is cleared and a replacement Wellstone is procured.

### Environment

The Overgrown Sunwell exists in a broad dale with expansive views of the sky above. The perimeter of this dale is surrounded by rocky spires and chimneys that are typical of the Forest of Stone, but this location provides a rare clearing that is less claustrophobic than typical for the biome. It is likely because of this comparatively spacious setting that the Aedir constructed the Sunwell here.

### Ecology

The clearing around the Sunwell is free of large obstacles, but the dale is still thick with low-lying trees, shrubs, vines, bushes, flowers, and other foliage. Many creatures who typically lurk in the rocky recesses of the Verdant Paths avoid such expansive sunlit areas, lest they fall prey to regional predators like Suarrok or Crag Raptors.

### Lore

Shortly before the Shattering, this region of eastern Aterica marked the outlying boundary of the Aedir empire. Advanced scouts and imperial regiments were tasked with building a network of functional and defensive structures across this perimeter to help secure the Empire's foothold upon the continent. This Sunwell was one of thousands constructed across the continent. Most such wells have been destroyed, while a rare few function still to this day.
