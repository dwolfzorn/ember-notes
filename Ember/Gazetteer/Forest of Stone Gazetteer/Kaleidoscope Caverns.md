# Kaleidoscope Caverns

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This biome is located in the Forest of Stone, providing cavernous connections between the adjacent [[Verdant Paths]] and [[Dripstones]].
>
> - Travel through this biome is generally [[Region Exploration]].
> - This biome is visually depicted by the [[Kaleidoscope Caverns]] area map.
> - On the region map, the Kaleidoscope Caverns are concealed by canopy tops which become hidden when hovering over them.

The Kaleidoscope Caverns are a system of meandering and dimly-lit caves interspersed throughout the Forest of Stone. The predominant feature of these caverns are the ubiquitous clusters of translucent crystals that reflect and refract any light they are exposed to, creating a bewildering landscape of shifting light and shadow.

### Terrain and Environment

The ever-changing illumination makes it difficult to tell how large the caverns are or how far they extend. No matter how much light is shone within the caverns, they always appear to be dimly lit.

Travelers who move through these caverns often feel a sense of unease that they can't quite place — especially if they bear light with them which heightens the crystals’ effects. Those impacted by this bewilderment grow more deeply unsettled the longer they remain in the area, with effects ranging from hallucination of images that disappear when looked at directly, whispers that can only be heard when right at the edge of sleep, sudden moments of clarity about what to do with their lives, and a fascination with the light emitted by the crystals that can lead to them neglecting their own needs to stare into the crystals' depth. Occasionally, these deleterious effects linger even after departing the caverns.

### Resources

The most notable resources present in this biome are fragments of [[Kaleidoscope Crystal]] which are plentiful within the area. Other resources include rare minerals, gemstone deposits, ooze formations, and a variety of mushrooms and fungal growth. It is not known whether any creatures are native to this habitat, and the stories told by travelers tend to vary wildly on this account.

### Lore and History

Various legends speculate about the origins of the mysterious magic inherent in the Kaleidoscope Caverns; experimentation of sorcerous Nir’ae, an ancient war between the Shent and the Abyss, or other stranger tales. Sages continue to study the caverns and some ambitious scholars believe spending time within them is worth the risk in exchange for potential clarity offered by the crystals’ arcane influence. This research is dangerous, and slow to progress.
