# Yakoshta

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> Yakoshta exists within the [[Dripstones]] biome in ⬢ s.3217.2866 on the south side of the Forest of Stone.
>
> - This settlement is visually depicted by [[Vista: Yakoshta]].
> - This settlement is featured in [[A Miner Matter]] and subsequent events.

> [!info] Social
> #### At a Glance
>
> - **Settlement Type:** Mining Encampment
> - **Size:** Miniscule (30-60)
> - **Primary Cultures:** **Culture: Arcturian**
> - **Predominant Ancestries:** **Ancestry: Hulg'run**
> - **Languages:** **Language: Common**, **Language: Arcden**
> - **Governance:** Founder ([[Sellen]]) and mine supervisor ([[Jasper]])
> - **Architecture**: Stone-hewn cliffside dwellings
> - **Economy:** Mining and trade of precious metal ores

### Landmarks

**Patch's General Store**

An armor and clothing repair shop run by Patch, a hulg'run whose body and clothing are both a patchwork of colors.

**Mine Mixtures Alchemist**

A potion shop run by Simar, a Thornling from Brevin (who sells about as many placebos as they do real potions - so far no one has noticed).

**Day's End Lodging**

A gathering point for the miners, where they come together to talk about the day, hang out, and occasionally hold social events.

### Locals

**[[Sellen]] Neutral, Hulg'run, Arcturian, she/her**

The leader of the Yakoshta mining community. Straightforward but friendly, she is excited about the future of the mine, but takes a slow and steady approach to progress. While she would never admit it to anyone, she sometimes feels isolated by her position, and brought her brother Tauric with her to Yakoshta in part because his love for her and lack of interest in mining keeps her grounded.

**[[Jasper]] Chaotic Neutral, Hulg'run, Arcturian, he/him**

The mine supervisor, working underneath Sellen to ensure that the mine is as productive as possible. Jasper is aware that Sellen mostly gave him this position because he has been agitating about ways to make the mine more efficient, but sees his role as being a voice for progress (and making enough money to add more jewels as ornamentation).

**[[Tauric]] Neutral Good, Hulg'run, Arcturian, he/him**

Sellen's younger brother, who accompanied her to support her and get his chance to "explore the world." He found his passion when he discovered the friendly oozes in the area and he has become an ooze enthusiast, championing them as helpers of the miners and as a way to learn more about nature. He is convinced he can communicate with his ooze, Squish, and takes it everywhere.

**Garil Neutral, Human, Ordani, they/them**

A human trader who is loosely affiliated with the Ordani Trading Houses and the main source of income and supplies to Yakoshta. They provide the wagon and take requests for goods from Sellen in return for a higher than average fee and the opportunity to gossip freely about everything going on in the Arctus Plateau.

**### Culture

Nearly everyone in the encampment has a primary job within the mine, but there are a few friends, romantic partners, and family members who accompanied the group. Most of these supporters of the project primarily work to keep up the community by building new structures, harvesting the nearby area, or logging and tracking goods that come out of the mine. Others function as traders, meeting caravans that come through the Forest of Stone or transporting wagons of ore to the nearby town of [[Helkas]].

An outlier resident of the town is [[Tauric]], who is focused on learning more about the oozes. While his early enthusiasm for this project led to the miners discovering the helpful properties of the metallic oozes, there are some who grumble that he is allowed to be a wanderer instead of contributing to the encampment because he is Sellen's brother. The mine supervisor, [[Jasper]], is one of the main grumblers, as he wants the mine to make as much profit as possible.

### Governance

The leader of the Yakoshta encampment and mining operation, [[Sellen]], brought the miners to Yakoshta to take advantage of the Shent ruins, mine as much as they can, and earn enough money to create a long-term mining community elsewhere.

Sellen is supported in her work by [[Jasper]], the supervisor of the mine who handles most logistical operations related to mining activity.**
