# Dry Outpost

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists in the [[Verdant Paths]] in hex ⬢ s.3205.2861 and is used by various caravans and travelers who journey through the Forest of Stone.
>
> - This location is depicted by the "Dry Outpost" composition of [[Vista: Verdant Paths]].
> - This location is featured in the [[Skies Above]] event.

### Environment

While the surroundings are filled with the lush plants and foliage typical of the Forest of Stone, some of the decaying buildings seem to be in various states of repair, and the area around them has been cleared. Numerous signs of refurbishment are evident: windows are boarded up, sagging roofs have been reinforced, and several new wooden planks have been installed in place of older ones.

### Ecology

When travelers pass through, the area serves as a refuge for smaller creatures like rodents, birds, and lizards that live nearby and inside the structures. However, when caravans arrive, the area becomes somewhat quieter, and the only visible life consists of a swarm of flying and scurrying insects that seem to infest the area.

### Lore

Once a bustling rest stop with a tavern and general store, this small homestead was established and operated by the Joyton family for nearly thirty years. After Varon Joyton’s death, his wife, now in her eighties, chose to relocate to Helkas, leaving the site abandoned. Over the past decade, neglect has taken a toll, evident through visible damage and deterioration. In another ten years, it’s possible that no structures will remain.
