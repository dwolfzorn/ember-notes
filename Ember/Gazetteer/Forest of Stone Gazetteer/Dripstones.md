# Dripstones

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This biome is located in the Forest of Stone, on the western side of the Arctus Plateau.
>
> - Travel through this biome is generally [[Region Exploration]].
> - This biome is visually depicted by [[Vista: Dripstones]] and the [[Dripstones]] area map.

The meandering cliffs of the Forest of Stone occasionally conceal and support a mossy biome of glistening and mineral-rich lowland known as the Dripstones. These humid and vaporous areas are notable for precarious paths, cascading waterfalls, slick rock, cenotes, and gleaming pools bounded by sudoriferous erosions and teeming with semi-aquatic flora and other life.

### Environment

The pond-dappled lowlands of the Dripstones are challenging and perilous to traverse, this biome is difficult terrain unless otherwise noted. Rain or fog is more common in this environment, resulting in an intermittent haze that can occasionally stifle long-range visibility of the area.

### Ecology

There are abundant sources of potable water in the Dripstones, along with a variety of other resources such as oozes, arcane vegetation, and rare mineral deposits. Creatures native to this habitat are frequently amphibious. Water elementals are also more likely to be observed in this environment.

### Lore

The Dripstones were shaped in part by the cataclysmic events of The Shattering, like much of Ember. Prior to that pivotal moment, the Forest of Stone played host to a handful of Shent landmarks and remote Aedir strongholds. Most notably, the Shent Moon Temple in the northern reaches of the Dripstones is noteworthy for its powerful attunement to Ember’s moons, and Mayis in particular. In the war following the Abyssal Shear, however, the Shent in this area were driven back, leaving behind only ruins and fallen guardians.
