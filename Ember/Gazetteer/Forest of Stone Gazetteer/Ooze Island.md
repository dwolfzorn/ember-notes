# Ooze Island

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists within the [[Dripstones]] in ⬢ s.3215.2864, as an ancient Shent breeding ground for small friendly Oozes.
>
> - This location is depicted by the "Ooze Pool" composition of [[Vista: Dripstones]].
> - This location is featured in the [[Stone Life]] event.

### Environment

This location is known for its natural pools of viscous ooze. Several large ground depressions are filled with these thick pools. While evidence hints at past humanoid activity, nothing recent is apparent.

### Ecology

The small island here once served as a natural gathering place for [[Oozeling]], who would mix and mingle throughout its shallow waters. Over the years, hostile oozes have caused the more docile ooze population to dwindle significantly.

### Lore

Miners from the nearby [[Yakoshta]] encampment once exploited this natural resource, transforming it into an ooze farm. They extracted oozes and fed them metallic dust to alter their physical characteristics for mining purposes.

However, new species of luminous oozes rose up from the primordial goo and attacked, decimating or absorbing most of the original docile oozes and scattering the survivors. The "farm" here has since been abandoned, leaving behind naught but empty pools and the walkways between them.
