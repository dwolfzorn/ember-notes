# Redwalk Ramble

- **District Type:** Recreational
- **Population:** 312 (Sparse population of artists, merchants, and nature lovers)
- **Lifestyle:** Observational (Performance artists and paid fashion models supplement the natural beauty of the park)
- **Services:** Performance (Impromptu fashion shows and performance art)

### Culture

The Redwalk Ramble is a place of natural beauty, but despite its well-tended grassy knolls and abundant flowers, it is most well known as a place to see the latest and greatest in both Ordani fashion and entertainment, thanks to the influence of Trading House Salva. House Salva, which focuses on clothing, fashion, and the arts, encourages the designers from the nearby Gossamer District to use the Ramble as a display case of sorts, sending their work out on the backs or in the hands of trusted ramblers, whose job is to meet people, mingle, and encourage anyone who admires the goods they are wearing to visit the appropriate store. This arrangement gives the Ramble a lively atmosphere, as do the many performance troupes in the area, who often perform small pieces from larger shows in the hopes that they might catch the eye of a House Salva scout.

Despite the generally festive feeling to the Ramble, the meadows and other plant life within it are quite serene. The Hallows uses magically treated stone to make some areas of the Ramble, like the large open-air amphitheater at the heart of the district, more resonant, while others remain quiet corners for conversation or contemplation where loud performances and ramblers selling goods are strictly forbidden. There is also a certain order to even the busier sections of the Ramble, as locals in the district pride themselves on being able to display some of the best that Ordain has to offer without overwhelming those around them and are not hesitant to report any overenthusiastic performer or rambler to House Salva leadership for a dressing down.

### Landmarks

**Ettie's Exhibitions**

This indoor space run by Ettie Salvari hosts events for House Salva that range from fine art gallery exhibitions to living fashion shows, often using its proximity to the open space of the Ramble to let the events spill out into public spaces and bring them additional recognition. Ettie is known for her eye for talent, and many wait with bated breath for her declaration at the end of exhibition of who to watch going forward.

**The Ramble Amphitheater**

This large tree-shaded performance space is occasional used for major events like fashion shows or performance showcases, but is open to foot traffic and public performance when not in active use. Performers looking to catch the eye of a new audience member, supporter, or patron perform small shows, while decadently attired ramblers serve as both walking displays of the goods they have been hired to sell and welcomers meant to put newcomers at ease.

**80 Steps to Glory**

This set of 83 steep steps would be fairly unremarkable if not for the encouragement of House Salva to make it a showcase for art and entertainment. Collaboratives of artists and designers across disciplines often work together on projects that utilize the steps and showcase their talents, with recent exhibitions ranging from a history of Ordani fashion expressed through dance to a mural made entirely of flowers that changed as they bloomed, grew, and died. House Salva scouts are known to spend time in the area, looking for those who might be worthy of funding for more permanent exhibitions.

**Umber's Pass**

A wooded arch that leads to one of the more secluded sections of Redwalk Ramble, as its opening is perpetually overgrown with vines. The vines have been cleared more than once, but always return, usually thicker than before. As it is popular with lovers looking for spots to meet in secret, it is believed that they replant the arch with vines anytime it is cleared to maintain their secret hideaway.

### Locals

**Arvin Spoole (True Neutral, Arcturian Human, he/him)**

Arvin Spoole arrived in Ordain from the Arctus Plateau as a talented fabric repairer, but has reinvented himself with his ability to upgrade the outfits of those around him, setting up shop in the Amphitheater and making small tweaks to the clothes of passersby in return for generous tips. His only rule is that he will not “refashion” the clothes of any Arcturians, and he seems to actively avoid them, along with any questions about his past.

**Ettie Salvari (Lawful Evil, Arcturian Human, he/him)**

The head of Ettie's Exhibitions, she may have inherited her shameless pursuit of glory from her father Palo, who changed his last name to Salvari just in the hopes that it would make his ascension in House Salva faster. Unlike Palo, who never made it particularly far up in the House, Ettie is an excellent talent scout, and has a knack for spotting the one or two artists in her exhibitions who will ascend to great heights. Unfortunately for those she discovers, her stamp of approval comes with a price, and she collects favors from those she put on the map for the rest of their lives. Those who don't approve receive a visit from Palo Salvari, whose true talent lies in intimidation.

**Kantella (Neutral Good, Ordani Signborn, she/her)**

Retired rambler Kantella's weekly pamphlets highlighting the best fashion of the past week are well-loved in the Ramble, in part because she tends to weave them in with local news, gossip, and even the occasional matchmaking – she has a surprising ability to match people up based on their fashion sense that has led to several friendships and at least one romantic pairing.

**Zanzi Felten (Lawful Good, Ordani Human, she/her)**

Zani Felten grew up at a time when the greenery of the Ramble dominated most of the district, and has watched as pieces of it have been given away to House Salva shops over the years, something that she deeply opposes. While her many many letters to both Hollows and House Salva are considered a bit of an annoyance, both value the care she brings to the remaining parkland, which she unofficially oversees maintenance of with the help of the Redwalk Rangers, a group of Ordani youth that she has recruited and are trying to teach to love nature's beauty over "artificial glamour."

### Lore

Some claim that Redwalk Ramble’s name comes from an era when the color red became a fashion must-have in Ordain, causing every storefront to display its own version of the color, but in truth, it is named because of its proximity to Redrak Fields.
