# Coinwealth Heights

- **District Type**: Residential with some Commercial.
- **Population**: 2,332 (Low. Expansive manors take up most of the space, foot traffic is light.)
- **Lifestyle**: Exclusive. (The district hosts numerous wealthy families and their retinues in expansive homes..)
- **Services:** High-end retail, dining, clubs, and private warehouses.
- **Influences**: As a stronghold for House Bastilla, members and associates of the house are common here.

Coinwealth Heights occupies a secluded corner of the south-western slopes of the Ordain Spires, a buffer of elevation separating it from the city’s bustle. It enjoys access to private docks in the south, has its own lighthouse, and extends its influence into the surrounding districts with impunity. It is also the historic stronghold of House Bastilla, whose influence is often felt but rarely seen by the locals here.

### Culture

Coinwealth Heights is ruled by money and legacy. Most residents are old merchant blood or those whose fortunes have purchased entrance and place. Everything that happens in Coinwealth Heights is done behind layers of luxurious privacy.

House Bastilla governs the district with a firm yet invisible hand, as this districts is the seat of its power and home to its leaders. An expensive and loyal security force of private guards preserve the district’s impeccable calm, turning back the uninvited and tending to everything from garden parties to the subtle affairs of house politics.

### Landmarks

**The South Gate**

One of the many ways into the city, this heavy gate is guarded day and night by house Bastilla’s elite retainers. Unlike other entrances to Ordain, here access is tightly restricted, with admission requiring invitation or favor with house Bastilla. The gate is an evergreen point of friction as city officials contest Bastilla’s claim to sole control of these lucrative southbound arrivals, but cannot break their hold over it.

**Gilded Stems**

Hidden behind opaque glass and black marble façades, the Gilded Stems is an exclusive wine bar and tasting house serving vintages from private cellars around the plateau and even beyond. Patrons are hand-selected and may be admitted only by the direct sponsorship of another patron. Inside, crystal sconces shimmer above deep velvet seating, and private alcoves allow for discreet discussions of business or pleasure.

**The Risers**

A massive, two-tiered warehouse complex that hangs off the bluff’s edge with huge pillars rising up to hold it aloft. This warehouse unites the manors above with the private harbor below. Here, a collection of elevators move cargo up and down the bluffs.

**Scintella Lighthouse**

Set atop the lower bluffs near the private stone docks, this elegant lighthouse of marbled white bricks serves as the city's beacon. Its glass lantern burns ever-bright with magical flames, guiding ships of all kinds toward the mouth of the Destine.

### Locals

**Matienne Bastilla (Lawful Neutral, Ordani Human, she/her)**

A stoic figurehead of House Bastilla and the Heights itself, Matienne is not what most expect of the normally bombastic Bastilla members. She rarely appears in public, preferring to run district affairs from her estate or the private lounges of the Gilded Stem. She is known for cultivating cordial ties with those useful to her house and enforcing district order with an iron hand slipped into a velvet glove.

**Dalbin Grell (Lawful Evil, Ordani Human, he/him)**

Head of Bastilla’s private security force at the South Gate, Dalbin is a precise, hawk-eyed man renowned for his utter professionalism and stubborn resistance to interference from outside authorities. He maintains detailed records on every entrant and adheres strictly to House Bastilla’s command, though he and his men can be bribed if one is willing to part with enough coin.

**Forella Meycor (Neutral Good, Cascillian Human, she/her)**

The master steward of the The Risers, Forella oversees every shipment and staff rotation. Though not a member of House Bastilla, she is well paid to ensure that house reputations and client privacy are preserved. She is generally pleasant to staff, but ruthless toward incompetence, and unfailingly discreet in all transactions.

**Brennet Vossin (Chaotic Neutral, Ordani Human, he/him)**

Owner and sommelier of the Gilded Stem, Brennet is as theatrical as he is discerning. With an encyclopedic memory for faces and wine labels alike, he delights in reciting the histories of rare bottles if only to justify the exorbitant prices they command. Though he'd never admit it, he detests House Bastilla and its countless sea captains that think wealth can buy culture and standing.

**Galen Scrit (Chaotic Neutral, Arcturian Signborn, he/him)**

A witty tailor recently granted a permanent atelier in Coinwealth Heights, Galen’s developed a scandalous reputation for incendiary gossip and relationships alike. Despite this, he is beloved for his clever conversation and revolutionary designs.

### Lore

Coinwealth Heights began as a humble extension of early Ordain back when it was known as Exile's Landing. Over the years it was built up, serving as a point for hunters to travel out into the grasslands, or a location where lumber could be brought down to the city.

Eventually house Bastilla selected it as the future enclave of its influential trading captains seeking respite from the city’s bustle. As the house's fortunes swelled, so too did their estates here, and the district’s character forever shifted toward exclusivity, wealth, and secrecty.

House Bastilla’s command over the district was historically tenuous, but was cemented a century ago when they brokered and enforced agreements to create an extensive estate management network. This network now sets standards and expectations of residents, provides private security, and controls who can be hired to work in the district. Since then, entry has remained tightly controlled, and all services within the Heights are either sanctioned or run by associates of Bastilla.
