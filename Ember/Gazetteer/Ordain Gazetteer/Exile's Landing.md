# Exile's Landing

- **District Type**: Commercial with some Residential.
- **Population**: 19,000 (High, busiest during daylight, remains lively in the evenings due to taverns and inns.)
- **Lifestyle**: Eclectic (Waves of visiting merchants and tourists mingle with the numerous locals .)
- **Services**: Docking, cargo exchange, river shipping, inns, historical harbor, taverns, and docks.

Exile’s Landing hugs the western riverbank of the Destine, and the district’s working harbor remains vital, alive with cargo ships and fishing boats alike, their arrivals counted as they have been for centuries.

At the district’s core, preservation and progress mingle: historic facades and brickwork stand beside new shops and warehouses, as centuries of rebuilding and renewal slowly overtake the oldest pieces of Ordain's history.

### Culture

Exile’s Landing is the city’s soul laid bare: a place where centuries of history, and historians fight against the needs of industry and commerce demanding expansion and renovation. Hanging in the balance is the first pieces of Ordain's founding.

While the district committees work closely with the Anachraenum to preserve old buildings, document the history of the place, the immense prosperity from the affluent Marlstone and Coinwealth Heights districts has inevitably flowed downhill and brought an ongoing wave of gentrification with it.

Facing the loss of the district and city's history, locals and historians have begun to resist, if only to buy themselves enough time to preserve some of Ordain's oldest structures, though some view it as a lost cause.

### Landmarks

**Ten Step Tavern**

A sleek and modern structure of smooth, white stone, this tavern rests in the middle of two roads leading up to more affluent districts. A placard declares it to be the district's oldest pub, but it looks anything but old. The original structure was torn down a few years back, and replaced with a much more modern building, and reworked to cater to richer clientele.

**Old Refuge Inn**

Among the city’s oldest operating inns, albeit rebuilt numerous times, this old structure is weathered, and still bears smudges of smoke damage in places. Inside, its floors are paved with some of the original stones that lined the district's streets, and it reportedly has timbers taken from the first ships that landed, though nobody knows if that's true.

**Wid Street Market**

A small but vibrant marketplace where vendors hawk wares and locals acquire their staples. Once the district's central market, it has since become geared to tourists and visitors. Now, much of the market has become overpriced and choked with souvenirs and baubles exalting the historic district

**Yarin Hall**

A stately, columned structure standing at the top of a rise, overlooking a good portion of the district. Once made of recovered ship timber, and local wood, it was rebuilt in gleaming white stone after a devastating fire claimed the original structure. It is now maintained as a small museum and event space. Yarin Hall is a center for district meetings, civic celebrations, the First Landing Historical Society.

### Locals

**Darik Volst (Lawful Evil, Ordani Human, he/him)**

An enterprising owner of the Ten Step Tavern, Darik bought the old structure, tore it down, and built new. He heads the local business association, and actively champions the modernization of Exile's Landing to benefit local merchants and noble.

**Navir Chari (Lawful Neutral, Lumek Fej, he/him)**

Navir is the wizened keeper of the Old Refuge Inn, and has been running the inn since he was a young man. He dislikes the rich and wealthy injecting themselves into the area, and intends to run the inn until he keels over just to spite the people trying to buy it.

**Salla Embral (Neutral, Ordani Ashka, she/her)**

An Anachraenum preservation architect overseeing restoration projects in the district, Salla has strong ideas about what must be saved — and what can be adapted. She is equally at home in archival records and negotiating with wealthy merchants.

**Paddock Vale (Chaotic Good, Ordani Human, they/them)**

A local street performer, storyteller, and veteran of the Anachraenum. Paddock is the head of the First Landing Historical Society, and spends all his time bringing to life the saga of the city’s founding for anyone willing to listed.

### Lore

Eight centuries ago refugees made landfall at the mouth of the Destine river on the western shores. The settlers broke up their vessels for timber, using the planks and spars to raise the first crude shelters, piers, and watch-posts that would anchor their new home. The settlement was known as Exile’s Landing — a place where hope could be kindled, and a new beginning found.

This district is there the great collaborative dream of Ordain was born. For decades it was the center of all commerce and government, and it grew outward in rings, not unlike the trunk of a tree. As it did, Exile's Landing went from a sparse village to a town, to a city. Eventually the district's authority was shifted elsewhere, but the history remained.

Four centuries ago a fire swept through the dock front, consuming much of the historic wharves and crowding riverfront. The fire’s scars remain today, with a few buildings still bearing smoke damage, and a handful of fire-blackened pilings sunk deep beneath the modern piers.
