# Ordain Flats

The Ordain Flats are comprised of numerous districts that tend to share a common social philosophy in addition to their relative geographical features: one of comfort and prosperity. The Flats contain several neighborhoods defined by their local businesses or the very people who live there, with a regular emphasis on safe and affordable residential housing — even in the most industrial of areas.

### Environment

While the wide cobblestone streets of the Flats are considered standard terrain in most instances, they tend to be so full of urban traffic (from local citizens on foot, to the occasional hand-cart or beast of burden) that the avenues are justifiably difficult to navigate during peak hours of commerce and trade. Still, navigating the streets of the Flats remains a simple enough task; the city planners took this traffic into account, and the broad whitestone plazas between each district allow the tides of foot traffic to flow remarkably unhindered. Ordain is a city that's been built to be lived in, and the environment is a constant reflection of that intention.

### Ecology

There are several places throughout the Ordain Flats where flora and fauna have been dutifully cultivated. Ample gardens and parks dot the landscape here, with an impressive array of supplemental efforts by locals; potted plants line countless storefronts and windows, carefully maintained courtyards are everywhere, and even a few entire districts are dedicated to the preservation of nearby flora and fauna from the Arctus Plateau. Nature is never far from the Flats, where birds are known to perch on the city's ornate ledges to sing their songs, and beloved pets accompany their owners during daily business.

### Lore

The Ordain Flats are sprawling enough to be rather disconnected when it comes to the history of their development. However, the Cindaric Sages and the Ordinate were directly involved in the zoning of these districts as they were settled; a key interest throughout these historical expansions, and well on into the future, is the comfortable habitation of Ordain's citizens alongside their means for survival.

Because of the relative quietude and safety of the various districts that comprise this urban biome, the Ordain Flats are a place where one can potentially get away with anything. A few neighborhoods actually play host to clandestine locations, with denizens of unsavory or illicit aims. Yet, as small as these organizations tend to be, they rarely pose a severe threat to the people of Ordain at large (for now).
