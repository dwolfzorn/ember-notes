# Trader's Corner

- **District Type**: Commercial with some Residential.
- **Population**: 12, 130. (High. Fine townhomes house the wealthy on main streets while middle and low income housing can be found on side streets in historic neighborhoods off the main avenues.)
- **Lifestyle**: Mixed. (Locals are wealthy merchants and nobility, but outside foot and wagon traffic is constant throughout the day.)
- **Services**: High-end retail, fine dining, exclusive goods.

Trader’s Corner is an old, historic district that has undergone expansion, reconstruction, and revitalization many times throughout its existence. In the shadows of new construction and shining white stone are older, stoic structures of rock and wood that hearken back to earlier days of the district. Though even these markers of history are vanishing behind new facades of stone, or face outright replacement.

### Culture

Trader's Corner is a place of affluence, commerce, and leisure, at least for those who visit it as customers. Operating around the throngs of shoppers, entourages of elite hangers-on, and visiting elite from beyond the city is an army of shopkeepers, tailors, jewelers, painters, and Trading House funded guards that keep things running.

Trader's Corner relies on the sweat, skill, and dedication of the common folk, but its clientele prefer not to be reminded of it. As a result, this divide is a clear one, but deliberately painted over with the finery crisp uniforms, and mercantile etiquette.

### Landmarks

**Crossway Plaza**

A broad public plaza connecting major avenues between other districts, this plaza sees intense traffic at almost all hours of the day. Capitalizing on the constant traffic, the plaza holds most of the district's most popular shops, offering art, food, drink, and fashion at a high premium. Whether the quality is truly better remains a matter of opinion, but the sellers here swear their offerings are second to none.

**Goldwire Avenue**

This cluster of jewelry shops and metalsmiths sit tucked into a single, winding side street, and is considered the single best place in all the city to acquire accents, accessories and statement pieces for one's wardrobe. Inside each storefront are secured cases of expertly made and perilously overpriced pieces that can be had by any with the coin.

**Silver Street Market**

Located just a few minutes away from Crossway Plaza, this small market is where the various servants and house stewards working for the elites of Marlstone come to refill pantries. The good here are overpriced to take advantage of the clientele, like much of Trader's Corner, leaving locals to look elsewhere for their needs.

**The Laurel Table**

An elegant restaurant set within a historic townhouse, famous for its seasonal menus and rooftop garden. Meals are extravagant and expensive at the invite only establishment, and its head chef is notorious for turning away guests for improper conduct.

**Sarmell Wharf**

Named for someone history has long forgotten, the tiny wharf of Trader's Corner practically disappears into the greater waterfront area of Ordain, but the few docks and warehouses there almost exclusively serve this district and its businesses. The wharf maintains a private stevedore team.

### Locals

**Lirma Grell (Lawful Neutral, Ordani Human, she/her)**

Lirma runs the tiny wharf on the south end of the district. She is exacting and composed, adept at managing the expectations of wealthy patrons, local merchants, and her crew. She mostly handles private deliveries from other locations on the waterfront, and knows not to ask questions.

**Yerig Fenmarch (Neutral, Ordani Nir'ae, he/him)**

A local healer that travels between businesses and homes taking care of folks who may have become injured or fallen ill. He is in charge of the local noticerie and has close ties with the Cindarics, of which he is a devout follower. Yerig is a trusted and beloved figure throughout the district.

**Shevra Tassel (Lawful Good, Ordani Kivahr, she/her)**

Captain of the local House Bastilla funded guard contingent, Shevra coordinates patrols across the affluent parts of the the district with vigilance. Though she knows her guards are only there to protect the commercial interests of her House, she makes sure that her guards look after the shopkeepers and workers as best they can, if only to keep things running smoothly.

**Hestyl Drein (Chaotic Neutral, Ordani Signborn, he/him)**

The brilliant and often tempestuous head chef at The Laurel Table. Hestyl mixes tradition with innovation, drawing patrons from every corner of the city with dishes and flavors found nowhere else. He is notoriously hard to work with, firing cooks and servers often for whatever failure he cannot stand that week.

### Lore

While the public face of Trader's Corner is new and elite, a smaller, lesser known community exists behind it, in the shadows. The few historic streets that remain have been relegated to housing for middle and lower income families that work in the district. The businesses in these historic areas are smaller, much more modest, and serve just the locals. Most that visit the district will never see them.
