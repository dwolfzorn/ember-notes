# Lower Ashvale

- **District Type:** Residential with Institutional and Commercial.
- **Population:** 26,259 (High. Homes, apartments, and barracks with heavy foot traffic).
- **Lifestyle:** Modest-to-Wealthy (Career Cindarics, Burnished Hand, other professionals).
- **Services:** Healing, lodging, housing, food & drink, commercial trade, leisure.
- **Influences:** Heavy Cindaric and Burnished Hand presence.

Lower Ashvale sits at the foundation of Cindarin Temple, and its people do their best to live up to the principles the benevolent sages preach. Home to the largest Healing House in all of Ordain, Lower Ashvale also contains several smaller homes and organizations that do their best to help those in need — whether they are providing free housing, low-cost subsidized goods, or access to jobs. Citizens who need any or all of these three elements tend to settle in Lower Ashvale, often taking advantage of these services before providing them or subsidizing them in return.

One of Lower Ashvale's main attractions is Highroad Sanatorium, a venerated Cindaric healing house whose exceptionally learned Sages cater to a wide variety of ailments. This is a welcome boon for the district's elderly citizens, which are ample (and lively, despite their age). Lower Ashvale also hosts a fortified edifice known as Veneration Hall, the defensible headquarters of the Burnished Hand.

### Landmarks

**Veneration Hall**

This squat fortified structure serves as the headquarters of the [[Burnished Hand]] in Ordain. Ample barracks here provide duly-designated housing for the Burnished Hand's local forces, and vaults within contain a modest arsenal stocked with powerful arms and armor (both mundane and enchanted alike). While the true seat of the military organization's power is the [[Burnished Hand Fortress]] of Bura Island, Veneration Hall plays a pivotal role in the ongoing security of Ordain, and is known city-wide as a bastion of law, order, and peacefulness.

**Ashvale Sanatorium**

This large tower structure is one of the most esteemed of healing houses maintained by the Cindaric Sages in Ordain. The upstanding reputation of the Sanatorium is not only the result of its extremely accommodating facilities (which are modern, spacious, and well-equipped), but of the timeless expertise of the Sages who work here — veritable masters of their respective healing trades.

**Valenhome**

A bustling inn and tavern owned by **Midnight** **Max** (CG, Ordani Kiska, he/him), a retired wayfinder of the Anachraenum. Known for its lively front room and its unflinching comfort in times of trouble, Valenhome offers some of the most affordable and welcoming rooms in town.

****### Locals

[[Steros Kraver]] (LN, Ordani Human, he/him)

Steros Kraver is the stalwart commander of the Burnished Hand, the order sworn to protect the Cindaric Sages. Steros is a skilled warrior and a charismatic leader, and is quickly becoming regarded by many to be one of the most competent young military leaders in Ordain's history.

****Hathnan Tholos** (LG, Ordani Kivahr, he/him)**

An [[Aster]] druid with a metaphysical connection to Ember's water moon, this considerate healer serves the people of east Ordain as the Head Sage of Highroad Sanatorium. Hathnan's prolific abilities as an unflappable sage are humorously matched by his remarkably handsome countenance and striking physique, qualities that have endeared him with many starstruck patients throughout the years.

****Gabi Greenbriar** (CN, Arcturian Human, she/her)**

This 15-year-old streetwise urchin has made a name for herself busking throughout Lower Ashvale, playing her Arcturian and Ordani folk tunes for coin. A skilled player of the vielle, Gabi is known for her stirring renditions of rural dirges, and for emerging talent as a songwriter in her own right. Fortunately, the charity of Lower Ashvale affords her a modest lifestyle as a transient; but her ambitions are far brighter than her checkered past, and the young bard aims to raise enough money to open a venue of her own one day soon.

**### Lore

A unique arcane construct of huge proportions and indomitable power lurks in the dungeons beneath Veneration Hall, crafted from wood and crystal and stone by the skilled Cindaric Sages of Highroad Sanatorium and the celebrated blacksmith **Delos Travertine** (LN, Arcturian Hulg'run, he/him). Not many know of the existence of this enormous sentinel, which the Burnished Hand has promised to only call forth in the most desperate of hours. Some remain skeptical about the massive construct and its clandestine nature, fearful of the potential threats it could pose to the city and its people if control of the automaton fell into the wrong hands (or, worse yet, if the Burnished Hand itself fell under the influence of less-scrupulous leadership).******
