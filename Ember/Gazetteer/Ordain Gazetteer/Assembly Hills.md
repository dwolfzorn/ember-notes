# Assembly Hills

- **District Type:** Institutional with some Commercial
- **Population:** 1,098 (Low. Civil workers and bureaucrats, with moderate foot traffic).
- **Lifestyle:** Comfortable-to-Wealthy (Civil servants and bureaucrats).
- **Services:** Accounting and exchanges, deeds and titles, public records, legal services.

Built among the spires east of Spellbreaker Tower, Assembly Hills is a testament to classical Ordani architecture, and its majestic stone edifices should stand as enduring monuments for countless ages to come. These large, exalted buildings include public places like Commerce Hall and the Spiretop Commons, along with more restricted locations including Precept Hall and the various Trading House offices.

Ultimately, Assembly Hills serves the people of Ordain as the go-to district for all matters of a legally commercial nature. Lawmaking is not really proposed, passed, or carried out here in Assembly Hills, but this is certainly where much of the legislature of institutions like the [[Ordinate]] and the [[Hallows]] begins. As they say: "a word-on-the-street in Assembly Hills is worth a hundred words elsewhere."

### Landmarks

**Commerce Hall**

This large edifice serves as the central meeting place for Ordain's Trading Houses, and is dominated by a large meeting chamber surrounded by a variety of smaller offices (collectively known as The Stations). Trading House representatives congregate within Commerce Hall at least once a week to discuss region-wide issues and initiatives. Meanwhile, the Stations cater to the specialized needs and endeavors each of Ordain’s esteemed Trading Houses. Station Bastilla caters to the commerce of sea trade and arcane objects, Station Cevher focuses on the trade of ores and metals, Station Wandren maintains a catalogue of their luxury goods and services, Station Lilifeld is a source for all things agriculture in Ordain, and Station Salva facilitates the commerce of Ordani arts and entertainment.

**Chamber of Records**

This secure repository contains countless ledgers and tomes that detail an extensive history of Ordain’s civil and municipal information. Records here range from business registrations and trade deals to marriages, birth records, and an elaborate scholastic account of Ordain’s storied past. A network of secret passages below the Hall of Records is connected to similar chambers beneath Spiretop Commons, but only the Hall's attendants and a select few know about these covert tunnels.

**Precept Hall**

This municipal building hosts an elaborate archive of Ordain’s historical and modern legal codes, along with a small garrison of bailiffs and magistrates employed by the [[Ordinate]]. Precept Hall is regarded by most Ordani citizens as a rather stodgy place, rife with the petty-yet-necessary evils of civic administration and sociopolitical red tape.

**Spiretop Commons**

The edifice known as Spiretop Commons serves Ordain as a public assembly house, with multiple meeting spaces and a large central plaza for special events and high-profile arbitrations. Every kind of public activity from legal hearings to secular marriages has been carried out on the steps of Spiretop Commons — a place truly deserving of its moniker.

### Locals

****Breccia Cobblestone** (NG, Ordani Hulg'run, she/they)**

Bookish and obsequious by nature, Breccia Cobblestone works as the primary front desk clerk at the Hall of Records, a job she takes quite seriously. A trashy romance novel is the way to Breccia's heart, which beats with hidden passion and wanderlust. Treat Breccia Cobblestone as a [[Commoner]].

****Calahad Prinn** (LN, Ordani Human, he/him)**

This aged Ordani bookeeper is the stern but fatherly administrator of Precept Hall. The various staff members employed at Precept Hall regard the sixty-something widower with a great measure of genuine respect, and Calahad holds that esteem in high regard. But he also carries a troubling secret: his brother-in-law is a high-ranking member of the [[Otherhood of Fortune]], a fact that privately haunts him to this very day. Treat Calahad Prinn as a [[Noble]].

****Evela Farfeld** (CG, Arcturian Human, she/her)**

A duly-initiated member of [[House Lilifeld]], the sagacious and comely Evala Farfeld serves as the Head Trading House Liaison at The Stations, where she assists each of Ordain's Trading Houses with their respective mercantile needs. Before she settled down in Ordain in her early forties, Evela adventured throughout the Plateau as an agent of fortune — and is known by many both far and wide as a reliable information broker. Treat Evela Farfeld as a [[Spy]].

### Lore

The overt municipal nature of Assembly Hills means it is a place of commerce more than cultural activity, so narratives that surround the district tend to reflect the practical problems of the people of Ordain. Assembly Hills is less rich in its own local history, particularly given its relatively recent construction near the western perimeter of the city, but it is most certainly a place where rumors can often carry more weight than gold. When gathering rumors in Assembly Hills, it's not altogether uncommon to uncover a lot more about the city than one's expecting, along with poignant details about the latest trade disputes or transcontinental gossip.
