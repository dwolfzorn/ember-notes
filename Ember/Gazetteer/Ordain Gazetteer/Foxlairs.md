# Foxlairs

- **District Type:** Commercial and Residential
- **Population:** 7,330 (Medium. Comfortable town homes with lawns and stables).
- **Lifestyle:** Affluent (animal handlers, home owners, etc).
- **Services:** Lodging, housing, domesticated animals, livestock, stables, gardens.
- **Influences:** Expertise in animal handling via early Kithil settlers.

A great deal of the commercial real estate in Foxlairs is dedicated to the lawns, gardens, aviaries, and stables that comprise the district's beast trade. Aside from the ever-ubiquitous house cat, if there's a domesticated animal to be found on the surface of the Arctus Plateau, it can probably be located here — bred and raised by some of the best animal handlers in the business. Beasts ranging from horses, rask, and nothrin to hawks, serpents, and monkeys can be found here in ample supply (along with the district's titular foxes, which have been cultivated in the city of Ordain since its settling).

And while much of the residential sector of this neighborhood is defined by its "comfortable" cost of living, several town houses in the district belong to more aristocratic members of Ordani society (and this affluence is immediately recognizable in the architecture and size of the estate). An influx of Kithil immigrants during the neighborhood's formative days have also resulted in a sizable population of transplants from places like Luen and Hillhome, who are known for their deft skills when it comes to handling avians and various tree-climbing species found in Foxlairs.

### Landmarks

**Jessos Station**

This assortment of stables, aviaries, and animal pens is one of the oldest establishments in Foxlairs, comprised of over a dozen enclosures for the breeding, growth, and training of an impressive variety of domesticated animals. Thanks to the venerated historical nature of the Station and its remarkably comprehensive facilities, prices here can range a little high compared to other areas of the Arctus Plateau (or even Ordain). But, as the spry **Nathira Jessos** (Neutral Good, Human Ordani, she/her) loves to say, "when you hand your gold over to Jessos Station, you're paying for the pedigree," a phrase that has assured customers quite adequately for over four generations.

**Elefield Training & Sales Stables**

Owned and operated by the stolid and dependable animal handler **Cosmo** **Elefield** (Lawful Good, Human Ordani, he/him), this assortment of a half-dozen stables is famed for the quality of the rask and horses that are bred and raised here. The animals of Elefield Stables aren't simply known for their tenacity, they tend to be the noblest of domesticated creatures — traits passed down by the ever-considerate Cosmo and his loyal staff of trainers and stablehands. And whether it's true or not, there are some Ordani that swear by the so-called fact that rask raised by Cosmo Elefield are the fastest on the Plateau.

**Cloverleaf Bed & Breakfast Inn**

This rustic bed & breakfast hostelry is known city-wide for the unparalleled deliciousness of their savory local cuisine, lovingly cooked by its chatty proprietor **Bramba Cloverleaf** (Neutral Good, Keth Ordani, she/her) and her socially-awkward husband **Dorin** (Neutral Good, Human Arcturian, he/him). The prices here are surprisingly moderate, given the excellent quality of the food and amenities; and no one has ever left a bad review. Patrons are invited to write anonymous well-wishes, intimate hopes, and fond remembrances on small slips of parchment before hanging them on a tree in the inn's foyer — a sort of ritualistic and asynchronous communication between the kind souls who stay here.

### Locals

****Anika Rouge** (Lawful Good, Human Ordani, she/they)**

This aged local actor and storyteller is known to wander the district with pockets full of seeds, nuts, dried fruits, and grains, which she lovingly distributes to the animals of Foxlairs (wild and domesticated alike) while she ambles through the district each midday and dusk. Anika has long been known as one of the finest thespians to grace an Ordani stage, capable of remarkable scene studies, moving orations, and soul-stirring musical performances. Anika's many decades of storytelling experience and countless fans provide an unparalleled network of knowledge, which she rarely uses aside from the dissemination of hot gossip.

****Aezra "Racer" Sissos** (Chaotic Good, Ashka Ordani, he/him)**

This lithe rask-jockey is considered to be one of the Arctus Plateau's most skilled professional riders, capable of mounting and handling any saddled beast known to Ember. When he isn't busy earning coin or accolades in some prestigious competition, he lends his services to Jessos Station — whose patrons are most likely able to afford his handsome training fees.

****Talion Graves** (Neutral Evil, Human Tayan, he/him)**

This retired cavalier works as a stablehand for Elefield Training & Sales Stables, where he's (so far) been able to fade into an obscurity he so desperately desires. While Talion maintains the guise of a genial laborer, he's secretly a murderer on the lam from Imperial authorities, and a deserter of the famed Kriagry Knights of the Dragon Empress. Talion keeps the cause for his treason close at all times — a gemstone artifact known as the Vermilion Tear of Caren’ac, which allows its owner to read the minds of others.

### Lore

Foxlairs was initially developed by a consortium of independent investors led by the famed animal trainer Bastian Jessos, who came into the possession of a large inheritance following the untimely death of a family member. With newfound funds in-hand, the headstrong handler established the first stables that would eventually become Jessos Station, an institution that is largely responsible for the overall conservancy of domesticated animal life in Ordain.
