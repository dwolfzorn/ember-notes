# Dustforge

- **District Type:** Industrial with some Commercial
- **Population:** 1,029 (Low. Forge workers, metalsmiths, and customers, with low foot traffic).
- **Lifestyle:** Comfortable-to-Wealthy (The Dustforge provides a steady influx of wealth).
- **Services:** Processing and refinement of ore, minerals, and raw arcane materials.

Dustforge serves as Ordain's primary foundry and processing plant for the refinement of ores, minerals, stones, and a wide assortment of arcane materials. A few town homes and apartment buildings allow the workers of Dustforge and associated endeavors to live close by, but more than a few of these places are occupied with locals who simply like the moody inspiration or noisy escape the industrial district so readily provides. Indeed, a small community of avant-garde Ordani artists has taken shape here over the past year; some of these dreamers claim that the mechanical clamor of the neighborhood is part of their creative process (along with similar musings), and their general lust for life is a welcome addition to the neighborhood.

House Cevher's commercial interests in ore and arcane materials necessitates their heavy involvement with the district's business concerns, but the Dustforge is ultimately operated by an group of independent smithies under House Cevher's employ. The Ordinate provides minor oversight, particularly when it comes to the distribution and regulation of the materials that come and go through this massive foundry complex.

### Landmarks

**The Dustforge**

The district's namesake is a massive forge and an adjoining refinery complex maintained by [[House Cevher]], where most of the stone, ore, and crystals that are mined from beneath Ordain are brought for processing and refinement. A tireless guild of laborers known as the Dustforgers work the refinery, comprised of a diverse group of talented blacksmiths and geologists from all walks of life, including several Vjrnhar, Kivahr, and humans among them. For an added element of security, the Dustforge is manned by a pair of [[Chessman]] at all times.

**Cevher Station**

Nestled below the eaves of the Dustforge, this office complex serves as a base of business operations for agents and allies of House Cevher, whose vested interest in the Dustforge stems from their designated purview over ores, minerals, and arcane materials. Most of the facility is built to house various meeting rooms, workshops, and private offices. A small barracks on site affords over a dozen employees and guests of House Cevher enough accommodations for week-long stays when necessary, while other long-term housing is available from the company nearby.

**Discount Minerals**

This cluttered crystal emporium is supplied directly from Dustforge surplus, and offers traditional retail opportunities for regular individuals in addition to affordable bulk prices for licensed local businesses. Managed by the laconic appraiser **Broonan Pebblesmith** (NG, Ordani Hulg'run, he/him), Discount Minerals is a great place for consolidating coin into precious gemstones, for crystal artisans to find superior raw materials, and for arcane casters to procure distinct spell components.

### Locals

****Fineas Stonecrow** (NG, Ordani Kivahr, he/him)**

This slag-scarred blacksmith serves as Forgemaster General of the Dustforge, duly appointed by House Cevher and a coalition of senior Dustforger blacksmiths. Known for his assuring baritone voice and a fierce hometown pride in Ordani culture, Stonecrow is one of the city's most respected leaders, and is frequently called upon by the [[Ordinate]] as an arbiter for local disputes. Naturally, the tall Forgemaster's expertise precedes him, and his ruggedly handsome silhouette is unmistakable.

****Elora Pholthos** (LN, Arcturian Human, she/her)**

Prodigiously talented and advanced well beyond her years, this cheeky young artificer is the Lead Engineer responsible for analyzing the various raw materials that are delivered to the Dustforge for processing. Supported by a small team of other professionals (many of which are jealous of her talents), Elora is quickly becoming one of the Arctus Plateau's preeminent specialists on arcane elements and precious stones.

****Moira Madrigale** (CG, Ordani Zeph, she/her)**

This would-be wayfinder is a cantankerous administrator for House Cevher, relegated to serving the esteemed Trading House as one of their key liaisons at Cevher Station. Anyone who wishes to tour the Dustforge refinery and surrounding facilities is beholden to Moira's permissions, which can often be purchased with a small bribe or a favor in kind. An amateur archaeologist, Moira is fond of wiling away her free time amidst the public museums of [[All-Fable Keep]], daydreaming of her own adventurous escapades.

### Lore

The foundry of Dustforge is actually inhabited by a huge **Magma Golem**, a construct of molten ore that helps power the massive refinery — and that the Forgemaster can command when necessary. The Forgemaster's enchanted hammer is actually the control rod for the construct; and the dutiful Fineas Stonecrow is known to sleep with the hammer at his bedside (when it isn't beneath his pillow).
