# Orchard Lanes

- **District Type:** Residential and Commercial (food, taverns, shops)
- **Population:** 13,242 (Medium. Homes, apartments, and businesses with heavy foot traffic).
- **Lifestyle:** Modest-to-Wealthy (Culinary professionals, craftspeople, service professionals, laborers, merchants).
- **Services:** Lodging, housing, food & drink (fruit, tea, wine), leisure.

This quaint residential neighborhood’s eponymous orchards are rife with fruits culled from all corners of the Arctus Plateau, and the local streets are lined with verdant flower beds full of luscious vine fruits such as grapes, kiwis, and berries. This greenery is duly tended to by sages from Cindarin Temple along with local volunteers; and — as the seasons allow — the resultant produce serves as a complimentary food source for locals and the city at large.

The houses and apartments of Orchard Lanes are generally occupied by a wide array of Ordani citizens whose middle-class incomes afford them a relatively comfortable quality of life. Here and there, an inviting market or curiosity shop dots the arboreal landscape. A gentle breeze often sweeps through these whimsical lanes, where life tends to slow down just long enough to enjoy it.

### Landmarks

**Sweet Reliefs**

A favorite haven for many citizens of Ordain, this local tavern is well known for its potent wines and refreshing ciders. Sweet Reliefs is a place where good times are paramount above all else, a hazy den of conversation, gameplay, and unwinding. Indeed, many teenagers and young adults of Orchard Lanes and neighboring districts set their sights on Sweet Reliefs as a place to start their careers (although the small confines of the tavern allow relatively few of these aspiring youths to claim a coveted job).

**Briarhearth**

This sagging, four-story edifice offers affordable apartment housing for locals of modest-to-comfortable means. Briarhearth is owned and operated by a former House Cevher executive of trustworthy repute named **Wanel Loag** (LG, Ordani Human, he/him), alongside his kindhearted agrimage wife **Marinela** (NG, Arcturian Human, she/her). Short- and long-term lodging is usually available for those in need, and the Loags are known to offer temporary clemency for those in desperate need.

**Mystical Musings**

A cluttered curio shop stocked with spell components and rare alchemical ingredients, Mystical Musings is maintained by the aged wizard **Rory Fairweather** (NG, Arcturian Human, he/him), who absent-mindedly offers his spellcasting services for remarkably fair prices.

**The Hazard House**

This secret clubhouse serves as the base of operations for The Undaunted, an underground league of daredevil exhibitionists led by the tenacious [[Zira Hestidero]]. The Hazard House's precise location is unknown to nearly everyone, but its reputation among Ordain's sordid underworld is slowly on the rise.

### Locals

****Rory Fairweather** (NG, Arcturian Human, he/him)**

A native of the remote mountainside village of Skybrush, this courteous and elderly wizard operates the cozy arcane emporium known as Mystical Musings. An absent-minded adherent to the traditions of enchantment magic, Rory is happy to offer his low-level spellcasting services to anyone in need (unless, of course, their agenda is measurably distasteful). Treat Rory as a [[Mage]] with a more comprehensive spell list for spellcasting services.

****Jonda Ven'dara** (CG, Waerd Human, she/her)**

Tall tales and local legends surround this wizened Cindaric Sage who lives in hermetic seclusion amidst the confines of her aged Orchard Lanes townhouse. Neighborhood children weave endless tales about what she does inside the spooky old house, and actual visitors to the estate are few and far-between; but despite the wild rumors and accusations that surround her lonely lifestyle, she's a remarkable mind.

****Brando Grath** (CN, Ordani Human, he/him)**

This gregarious thirty-something piker is an opportunistic clerk employed by the Ordinate who utilizes duplicitous methods to settle his numerous gambling debts. As a result of his activities (both illicit and otherwise), Brando is connected to more than a few interesting and unsavory characters in Ordain.

**[[Zira Hestidero]] (NE, Kelmezoan Human, she/her)**

Zira Hestidero is a Kalaxan warlock of the undying shard god Kuarta who leads a ragtag enclave of scrappy Kuartan adherents known as The Undaunted.

### Lore

Orchard Lanes has a warm reputation among most of Ordain's citizens, and tends to be the quiet kind of place where one can escape the hustle-and-bustle of the rest of the city with some measure of regularity. The eponymous lanes and their endless orchards are discreet places, perfect for afternoon reading or a romantic stroll. Naturally, this also provides a furtive haven for more clandestine activity, and nurtures fertile ground for tall tales and local legends. Several ghost stories are connected to the fledgling history of Orchard Lanes, and are celebrated with pride by its local denizens; such tales include "The Tale of Lost Darrisa" and "Steppler's Cat."
