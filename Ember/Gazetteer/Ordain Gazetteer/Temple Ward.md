# Temple Ward

- **District Type:** Institutional
- **Population:** 1,012 (Low. Grand temples and religious institutions with heavy foot traffic).
- **Lifestyle:** Respectable (Priests, acolytes, and followers of various faiths).
- **Services:** Spiritual guidance, healing, religious education and practice.

The primary feature of this monumental district is a temple to the Elder God [[Sockets]]. Also known as the Graveflame Sanctuary, this edifice is recognized by its impressive gothic architecture and its eponymous flames — magical fires that burn with an eerie, spectral light. The grand front entrance of this primary temple is a sight to behold as well, yawning upon the city like some tenebrous maw. Meanwhile, dormitories for local priests and smaller temples that service other gods can be found lining the streets that surround the Sanctuary; and all are invited to participate in the Deathless god's ceremonies, regardless of their religion or past.

A temple to the sun goddess [[Lantyr]] dominates the eastern corner of the district, a regal building marked with elaborate gilded runes that tell stories of "the Energy Moon" and her many triumphs via a palimpsest of ancient languages. And to the south, a large temple that honors the triumvirate of [[Katu]], [[Vekalla]], and [[Raineka]] stands in defiant simplicity compared to the rest of the district; this obelisk-shaped structure allows adherents of all three faiths to worship in proximity to one another.

The inkaro lanterns that light this hallowed neighborhood are distinguished by their pale purple light, which casts a gloomy and surrealistic tint upon the district's lurid stone buildings. Temple Ward is a solemn place, accompanied by a small citizenship of faithful priests and curates, along with the ambient sounds of sullen Ordani bards performing the Arctus Plateau’s most inspired dirges.

### Landmarks

**Temple of Sockets**

This bastion stands proudly as Temple Ward's largest sanctum, marked by an impressive shrine to [[Sockets]] known as Graveflame Sanctuary — where braziers of magical flame burn eternal with a pale white light, and a magnificent statue of Sockets himself overlooks the solemn central chamber. The absent-minded [[Conaris Haid]] serves as the Temple's High Priest, and is attended during most daylight hours by a small envoy of priests and acolytes who are eager to learn from him.

**The Keeper's Ossuary**

This hallowed crypt beneath the Temple of Sockets houses the worldly remains of some of the most venerated of the Elder God’s priests and champions. Protection and maintenance of the Ossuary has become a rite of passage for fledgling acolytes of the Temple, who must learn their rotes beneath the timeless gaze of their forerunners. Meanwhile, a series of secret passages connect various chambers and corridors of the crypt to unseen places, unbeknownst to all but Conaris Haid and his most trusted acolytes.

**Remira's Bower**

This huge public gazebo is crafted from the same pale white stone that defines most of Temple Ward, but is crowded in a notable overgrowth of budding vines and flowering brambles tinged with otherworldly glamor. Beneath this kaleidoscopic bloom, the stonework is adorned with romantic depictions of Primordis. Blessed by the obscure shard god Remira, this restful place is known to bestow divine boons on those who are willing to pay homage to the love goddess beneath its stony eaves.

### Locals

**[[Conaris Haid]](Neutral Good, Human Ordani, he/him)**

This brilliant and eccentric philosopher serves as the High Priest of the Temple of Sockets in Ordain; and though he wears the guise of an affable, absent-minded codger in public, this expertly studied and accomplished cleric is one of the Arctus Plateau’s most trusted sources of information on Ember’s mysterious cycle of souls.

****Yasmora Zaban** (Neutral Good, Human Maziran, she/her)**

Yasmora is the wry-witted keeper of the titular fires of Graveflame Sanctuary inside the Temple of Sockets. She takes her duties seriously, but is always ready to mete out a certain measure of gallows humor and good nature. Treat Yasmora as a [[Priest]].

****Merida Helestian** (Chaotic Good, Human + Kivahr Cascilian, she/her)**

A cleric of the wind goddess, this humorless priest serves as the keeper of Vekalla's sanctuary alongside allied adherents of Katu and Raineke. Although she can be uncomfortably earnest, Merida nevertheless remains a trusted advisor for the restless hearts and wayward sailors that are known to seek haven here. Treat Merida Helestian as a [[Priest]].

### Lore

Tellers of tall tales speak of how Sockets himself raised the stone towers of Graveflame Sanctuary from the cobbled streets of Ordain with his own divine inspiration — and these tales are mostly true. However, the godly will of Sockets was actually summoned to this corner of the city by his faithful worshippers, who originally erected a smaller, humbler shrine where the Temple of Sockets now stands. One of Conaris Haid's predecessors — an Arcturian Aster named Dorina Mariu — put part of her very soul into the creation of this shrine, and her selflessness was rewarded. To this day, some say they can hear the wise voice of the incorporeal priestess as they wander the amethyst light of Temple Ward's hallowed streets.
