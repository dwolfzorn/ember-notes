# Splinter Canyons

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Biome is located within a long canyon corridor that runs the length of the central region of the [[Arctus Plateau]].
>
> - Travel through this Biome is generally [[Region Exploration]].
> - This Biome is visually depicted by the [[Vista: Splinter Canyons]] Vista.
> - This Biome can be explored within the [[Splinter Canyons]] Area Map.

The Splinter Canyons plunge from the Arctus Plateau in a near-vertical drop, traversable only via narrow switchbacks and occasional shelves and plateaus. Their depths are far enough from the surface of the Golden Flats to have their own unique mix of flora and fauna, which are either adapted to survival on the canyon walls or near the river on the canyon floor.

### Environment

The Splinter Canyons combine three types of terrain: sheer rocky walls, the river that snakes through them, and the narrow strips of land between water and stone (the route most travelers follow once inside). The canyon walls conceal occasional caves that hold both hidden dangers and valuable resources; several of these caves have been transformed into mining operations by [[House Cevher]], who have invested heavily to clear natural predators and protect the entrances from flooding.

### Ecology

The Splinter Canyons are prone to rockfalls, and water frequently upwells from subterranean springs, triggering sudden flash floods. As a result, the flora and fauna of the Splinter Canyons are well adapted to clinging to, burrowing through, or scaling the steep walls. The ubiquitous [[Gore Bird]] also plunges into the canyons in search of easy prey.

One example of such adapted life is the Vale Vine, a deeply rooted, flowering plant that grows vertically, forming a thick netting along the rock face; in places it is sturdy enough to aid ascent or descent, though its fronds often conceal caverns and lurking predators. The [[Gumtoad]] uses powerful hind legs, sticky forelimbs, and a prehensile tongue to leap great distances and adhere to canyon walls during flooding. Finally, the Stone Lilly is a broad, floating plant that resembles a simple stone, but is actually a carnivorous plant that latches onto prey and sinks to the river floor. When dead, however, Stone Lillies form safe floating platforms.

### Lore

While there are some communities in the [[Arctus Plateau]] that use canyons and tunnels as places to live, like [[Arcturel]], the Splinter Canyons are widely considered off-limits, as they were created during [[The Shattering]] and are perceived as somewhat unnatural by Arcturians. The rising waters also became much more sporadic following the major earthquake that rocked the region twenty years ago. Some superstitious travelers and locals believe that their depths hold secret horrors, and point to difficulties at some of the mining concerns operated by House Cevher in shallower parts of the Canyons. In truth, the main threats to anyone who passes through are simply those of the natural environment: rockfalls, flash floods, steep paths, and predators used to using all three to their advantage.
