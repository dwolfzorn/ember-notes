# Lake Orial

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists at ⬢ s.3198.2916 in [[Redrak Fields]], northeast of [[Ordain]].

Bookended by the Bloodwoods to the northwest and Redrak Fields to the southeast, Lake Orial is something of an oddity for the people who frequent its glittering purple waters. Although it sits at the edge of civilized territory, Orial is a place of copious natural riches maintained by a variety of Arcturians. While dredgers haul rich mud and silt from the lake, farmers work the fertile land, and fisherfolk cast their plentiful nets.

### Environment

Lake Orial is fed by the waters of the Orial Tributary to the north and the Tarca River to the west, which are naturally enriched by the mineral-laden soil of the nearby Bloodwoods biome. Much like Lake Yrthain to the west, Lake Orial is the result of a synthesis between the primal growth of the Bloodwoods and the subtle sanctitude of Ember's waterways. These two woodland rivers constantly supply the lake with rich resources, making it a prosperous area for local settlers — and the key to the great productivity and prosperity of the entire [[Redrak Fields]] biome.

### Ecology

Life around Lake Orial is not without its perils, rare though they may be. The proximity of the lake to the Bloodwoods means the occasional unplanned encounters with its strange lifeforms are a definite possibility for the otherwise peaceful settlers here. While incursions from Bloodwoods fauna like amerasps and bloodrunners are too frequent for comfort on the northwestern shores, domesticated animals like rask and yarnac also populate Orial's southern and eastern coasts. Meanwhile, Bloodwoods flora like redgrass and black aspens are abundant along the lake's northern perimeter, and give way to Redrak grassland the closer one gets to the southeast. Thanks to the industry here, fishing is not as abundant as it once was; but chordates like eels and mudfish can be found in constant supply.

### Lore

The shores of Lake Orial are primarily inhabited by communities of hard-working Bejaki settlers. Daily life around the rural expanse of Lake Orial is difficult, but also cherished by the Kivahr, Keth, and others that call the land home. As such, the legends and lore of Lake Orial are infused with those of the Bejaki people. More than one sordid tale involving Trolls of the far north has been shared among storytellers who journey here, along with legends of odd cyptids that lurk among black aspen in the neighboring woods.
