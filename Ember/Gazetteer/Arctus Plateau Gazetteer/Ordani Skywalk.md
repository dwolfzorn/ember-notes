# Ordani Skywalk

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists at ⬢ s.3208.2901, spanning the [[Splinter Canyons]] west of [[Ordain Gazetteer]].
>
> - This location is featured in [[The Blockaded Bridge]].

The great Skywalk of Ordain a centuries old pathway bridging the two sides of the plateau, and is one of the most iconic and enduring sights in the region. Almost everyone traveling to or from Ordain will cross the Skywalk, and take in its magnificent view of the canyon below.

### Environment

The bridge is made entirely of pale stone quarried from somewhere far beyond this region, as it's white color is unlike any of the stone found in the area. Underfoot the bridge is made of thousands of unevenly sized slabs pieced together and decorated in an vast array of styles. This extends up into the walls and watch towers of the bridge, each one unique in its own way.

At the midpoint of the bridge travelers gather to rest, take in the view and socialize. Here one can find impromptu swap meets, maker shows, concerts, and the like, though they never happen with any kind of predictable schedule.

### Ecology

The bridge sees constant traffic at all hours, and their make consist of a varied sort. Locals walk the bridges at night, enjoying the glow, while trader caravans trundle across it, bound for one of Ordain's many markets or warehouses. The towers, manned by members of the city's Hallows keep watch over the bridge, helping travelers, and looking out for trouble.

### Lore

The stone this bridge is made from was quarried from deep beneath Ordain, and was brought to the surface at great cost to the city, but the end result is nothing short of magnificent. The entire structure is centuries old, and embodies the meeting of Arcturian and Ordani cultures. The bridge features art, inscriptions, reliefs, makers marks of the countless artisans that contributed to its construction. Even today artists and crafters petition in the Hallows for the right to put their mark on the Skywalk, or performance maintenance in the hopes of putting their name alongside the others there.
