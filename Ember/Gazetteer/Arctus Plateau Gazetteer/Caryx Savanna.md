# Caryx Savanna

The Caryx Savanna stretches its vast and untamed land south and west, from the coasts of the Reaper Ocean to the lands beyond the bluffs of the Golden Flats. This expanse of unyielding grassland is shaped by the elemental seasons of water and fire — life and death — dictated by the rise and fall of [[Mayis]] and [[Ragen]], respectively.

### Environment

The Elenain River defines the Savanna's northern boundary, highlighting stark contrasts — lush floodplains near the river give way to increasingly dry, harsh stretches that test even the most experienced travelers and resilient inhabitants. Known for its extreme heat and intense rainfall, the area frequently experiences brush fires, which serve as both hazards and signs of change. These flames, seen as Ragen reaches its peak, often spread uncontrollably, forcing wildlife to migrate toward the Elenain River. This migration marks the start of movements across the plateau and sparks the emergence of thousands of vibrant Prisma Beetles from the soil. When the fires threaten to consume the Savanna in an endless blaze, Mayis reaches its zenith, bringing a powerful monsoon season. The rains flood the low plains, creating large mud lakes that nourish migrating animals. With renewed vitality, the land flourishes again — herds grow, hunters thrive, and the Savanna reverts to a chaotic, wild landscape, at least until Ragen waxes again.

### Ecology

The Caryx Savanna is dominated by vast fields of resilient, golden grass that sway in unison under the relentless sun. Monumental Boarbark Trees serve as natural landmarks, and in some cases, as hollowed-out shelters. Along the riverbanks, more vibrant vegetation claims the floodplains and thrives unchecked, supported by the life-giving waters of the Elenain.

Life in the Caryx Savanna is a vivid tapestry of adaptation and survival. Grass Runners, Tiller Birds, and the annual migration of colorful insects represent the constant churn of migration and change in the region. Several predators stalk these migratory creatures, the most dangerous of which is the towering Caryxidar.

### Lore

The Caryx Savanna has long been a wild area of the Arctus Plateau, home to many predators and ancient creatures from the [[Age of Beasts]]. After [[The Shattering]], a few scattered families of [[Ashka]] settled on the eastern and southern shores, establishing hidden villages and trying to tame the land around them. They brought with them deep cultural beliefs in the harmony between fire and water. However, despite their efforts, their ritual practices have disrupted the area's natural balance, causing extreme weather phenomena and moon zeniths that bring chaos, destruction, and intense intermittent magic to the region. The violent two-season system eventually led most of these villages to be abandoned, except for [[Tajar]], a settlement on the Savanna's southern edge, which still clings on despite the dangers.
