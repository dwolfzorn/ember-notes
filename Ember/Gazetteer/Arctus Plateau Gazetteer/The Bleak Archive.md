# The Bleak Archive

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists at ⬢ s.3194.2884 in the [[Wedgelands]], north of [[Skybrush]].
>
> - This location can be explored within the [[The Bleak Archive]] Area Map.
> - This location is featured in [[Where Evil Lurks]].

This uninviting edifice carved out of the mountainous Rustvar terrain is rumored to be an esoteric repository built by the ancient Shent giants. Few people of the region even know about the Bleak Archives or its whereabouts; thus, the remote timeworn vault sits here lonely and forgotten, its archaic contents unseen.

### Environment

The Bleak Archive is carved into and out of ashen gray slate, which also comprises the craggy precipice that leads up to the vault's mist-shrouded entrance. The threshold is flanked by statues of two celestial hounds; and scattered cerulean moon blossoms decorate the ground here, lambent beneath the light of Ember's moons.

The interior consists of a false antechamber, a main gallery, a true inner sanctum, and several corridors and smaller chambers that surround them. The gallery in particular was designed to serve as a "false tomb" to dissuade robbers and thieves who might plunder the location's infamous hoard, which actually lays locked behind a complex mechanism powered by revolving mirrors and arcane light. The Archive is also laden with a handful of traps and hazards installed by the [[Shent]] to punish would-be tomb robbers; meanwhile, the facility's true valuables lurk timelessly in the well-hidden true vault, which serves as the Archive's most powerful containment area.

### Ecology

The Bleak Archive was designed to act as a spiritual trap of sorts for the various artifacts, treasures, and trinkets contained therein — all of which have been polluted by Abyssal energies that predate the Shattering. It is quite difficult for living creatures to call this remote and forsaken place home.

The vault's main resident is a loathsome [[Varún]] wraith named [[Tethra Shùl]], who served in life as a devout warlock for an Abyssal Horror named [[Nethehepticas]]. Thanks to the power of an artifact known as the Stone Hand of Uroth, Tethra Shùl has also summoned undead minions to this place, quadrupedal creatures made of shadow known as [[Corrupted Cadrithor]]. Furthermore, an insidious [[Horrendor]] lurks within the northeastern study, eager to wreak havoc on unsuspecting interlopers; and a [[Sitherian]] has made its home in the southeastern refectory, which is secretly exposed to the outside world via a small hole in the vault's exterior wall.

### Lore

Known as Ebbok Zhùr by the ancient Shent artificers who built it prior to the Shattering, this trap-laden vault was originally constructed to contain a volatile repository of Abyssal artifacts and eldritch curiosities. Today, it covertly serves as the sepulchral lair of Tethra Shùl, who has made foul use of some of the Archive's most dreadful relics.

Tethra Shùl first came to the Bleak Archive in  AS5, at the behest of otherworldly visions of Nethehepticas transmitted to her in fevered nightmares. A loyal cadre of five Varùn renegades came alongside her, but would perish in the Archive's trapped antechamber when the warlock sacrificed them to gain forbidden entry. Eager to solve the vault's riddle, Tethra Shùl was subsequently trapped alive within the Archive's innermost vault, a prisoner of her own ambition.

In the face of death, the warlock made a bargain with her Abyssal master: she would serve Nethehepticas in perpetuity as an undying wraith, who would one day execute a clandestine and insidious plot. Meanwhile, the corpses of her minions spawned the eldritch curse known as the Bewilderment, which eventually spread to the residents of Skybrush after the fateful intrusion of Dereth Erekos. Centuries later, Tethra Shùl continues to weave her elaborate plans of chaos and murder from the confines of this ersatz tomb; and the remote settlement of Skybrush is the closest target of these malevolent schemes.
