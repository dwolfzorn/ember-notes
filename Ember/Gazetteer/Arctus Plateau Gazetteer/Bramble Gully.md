# Bramble Gully

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists at ⬢ s.3204.2898 within the [[Splinter Canyons]], south of [[Brevin]].
>
> - This location is featured in [[Growing Pains]].

This small outcropping within the Splinter Canyons once held the only settlement within the biome — a small community of Thornlings known for their elaborate floral and plant displays — but it has since been abandoned, left behind after tremors in the area cracked the well that fed the community. In the years since, that water has run directly into the ground of the gully and the plants have grown lush and wild, providing food and shelter for a number of creatures in the area.

### Environment

Bramble Gully is a small outcropping that forms a mini-plateau within the Splinter Canyons — while it, like much of the land against the side walls of the canyons, is somewhat shadowed, it is high up enough that it does receive some direct sunlight and rain from above, helping the plants in the area to thrive. Still, most of the water that feeds the area comes from the ruins of the well created by the Thornlings who once lived in the area, who are believed to have created channels that lead all the way down to the water source beneath the canyon's floor.

### Ecology

Bramble Gully has one of the broadest varieties of plants in the Splinter Canyons, as many of the plants that were collected and tended by the Thornling community who lived in this area have continued to thrive and even intermix, creating new varieties not seen anywhere else. While the wild beauty of this gully is often admired from a distance, dangers abound for anyone who ventures close, as many creatures, and even some of the plants themselves, wait to prey on anyone who ventures within the tangle of plant life in the area, including gumtoads, sarracenias, and amerasp swarms. Despite these dangers, some ne'er-do-wells enjoy the area, as the cross-breeding of some of the flowers has led to the natural development of new potions, intoxicants, and poisons.

### Lore

A small community of Thornlings, known for collecting and displaying colorful plants collected from across the Arctus Plateau and beyond, once lived in this small outcropping within the Splinter Canyons, using a hand-dug well to supply themselves and their crops with an abundant supply of water. When shifts in the land due to earthquakes cracked the well, the community similarly fractured and eventually abandoned the land. Many members resettled in nearby Brevin, but others scattered, joining communities from Helkas to Ordain. In their absence, the area has become overgrown with the far-flung plants favored by the community before their departure and remains one of the most colorful sections of the Splinter Canyons.
