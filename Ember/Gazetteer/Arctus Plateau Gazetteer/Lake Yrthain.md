# Lake Yrthain

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists at ⬢ s.3196.2908 in the [[Bloodwoods]], north of [[Ordain]].

Lake Yrthain comprises most of the expanse of the Tarca River, which flows from the Wedgeland mountains in the northwestern expanse of the Bloodwoods, and feeds a larger Lake Orial to the east. Yrthain is a cloistered place, choked by the oppressive growth of the biome’s tallest redgrass and oldest black aspens.

### Environment

The environs of Lake Yrthain are rather unforgiving compared to the relative reachability of nearby Lake Orial. Here, deep within the Bloodwoods’ savage expanse, the soil is heartier and the stone is mightier. Weather in the local area is also subject to the primal whims of the biome, embodied by occasional fits of strong mountain gales from the north and the turbulent river rapids that result from them.

### Ecology

Thanks to its relative sequestration, Lake Yrthain and the surrounding areas are a veritable crucible of preternatural Bloodwoods flora and fauna. Creatures like amerasps and bloodrunners frequent the lake’s overgrown shores, and the waters of the lake are inhabited by an array of freshwater fish and squamous amphibians — from benign catfish and carp to pernicious species of eels and daggerfish.

### Lore

The legends that stem from Lake Yrthain are steeped in speculation and suspicion, and speak of dark supernatural forces that permeate the Bloodwoods themselves. One such tale is the legend of Ardan Vail, the story of a hunter who was drawn to Lake Yrthain by the alluring song of a Primordal enchantress. As the story goes, this otherworldly spellcaster fell to earth after her heart was broken by a powerful Casia, and seeks her revenge in the arms of countless unwitting lovers — who inevitably find their dooms at the center of the primal lake (like poor Ardan himself).
