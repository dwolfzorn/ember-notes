# Lake Mithra

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists at ⬢ s.3205.2890 in the [[Golden Flats]], adjacent to [[Storsa's Strand]].

The waters of this lowland lake sway with the constant southern flow of the River Elenain, fed by two source tributaries in the mountains to the north. Lake Mithra stands as a bounteous signifier of Ember's preternatural abundance, and is regarded by some as one of the Arctus Plateau's most treasured natural resources.

### Environment

While Lake Mithra embodies the dry summer climate of the rest of the Arctus Plateau, it also exhibits a range of unique environmental qualities that stem from the enchanted nature of the Lake's waters, which are infused with primordial energies that even the residents of nearby Storsa's Strand struggle to understand. Due to the humanoid population in the area, the environs here are relatively well kept, with less overgrowth than one might expect. The lake's perimeter is marked by a varying stretches of low sandy beaches and a surfeit of steep craggy overlooks.

### Ecology

Lake Mithra is known far and wide for the extravagant freshwater cuisine harvested from its depths, and the intermittent arrangements of fruit trees that line its steep rocky shores. Despite the lake's relative compact size, it's an ecological cornucopia, one that provides ample resources for the region. Chief among these is the wide array of freshwater fish that populates the lake, from carp and trout to catfish and eels. Furthermore, avians and mammals can be seen lurking about Mithra's grassy circumference in steady supply, drawn here by the lake's generous natural food cycle.

### Lore

Lake Mithra is celebrated as one of the Arctus Plateau's most curious sites of interest, and is known far and wide for the lightly enchanted nature of its waters — although the precise magical qualities of the lake are a source of contention. On top of Mithra's ecological abundance, locals have the odd tendency to drag curious treasures from the bottom of the lake. Sometimes, these treasures carry with them a small stroke of bad luck (a chance many residents and visitors of Lake Mithra are willing to take). No one quite knows how the magic of Lake Mithra functions or from whence it comes, but all are thankful for its confounding arcane bounties.
