# Krator's Hall

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists at ⬢ s.3197.2902 in the [[Wedgelands]], northwest of [[Ordain]].
>
> - This location is featured in [[Hidden Campsite]]

The Anachraenum came across this site several centuries ago, and it serves as a retreat and training location for Wayfinders within the guild. Traversing the depths of the Bloodwoods themselves can be extremely dangerous due to the many miles of twisting forest paths and the presence of overwhelming magical anomalies and effects. However, in this gorge, the Anachraenum found a secluded, self-contained area to train in an outdoor environment that was perfect for thier needs. In addition to the location itself, the Anachraenum also chose this site because of a strange object on the gorge's floor — a well of water with extremely mysterious properties they called the Well of Orbis.

The Well of Orbis is a striking feature just beyond the campsite, its surface shimmering with soft, white magical energy. Glowing stones encircle the well, casting light throughout the dark, shadow-filled gorge. The Anachraenum has studied it for many years, and interestingly, anyone trying to examine the well often encounters riddles and strange, puzzle-like challenges that appear in their minds at random. Sometimes, these provide new insights into their research or lead them to explore new places in the world. Most of the time, however, the riddles and puzzles are playful, nonsensical, or offer intriguing insights into the world.

### Environment

Krators Hall is hidden deep within a mountain gorge, not far from the Bloodwoods, north of Ordain. It is exceptionally well concealed, and very few would be able to find it without being directed their or without a map or chart provided by the Anachraenum. For most of the year, the site is carefully locked up and left to the mercy of nature. Only during the Withering season do the Anachraenum members travel there as a kind of retreat and annual celebration event. It was also used previously during the first Expedition Challenge as a site that tested the mental resolve and problem-solving skills of the participants. It is likely that it will be used again in the upcoming second Expedition Challenge as well.

### Ecology

The area of the gorge is very small, but it contains many of the same plants and trees present in the nearby Bloodwoods. Thankfully, however, apart from the Well of Orbis, there are no magical effects present within the environment here. There are also very few large creatures, and throughout the years, the Anachraenum has documented that most monsters and creatures tend to deliberately avoid the location.

### Lore

The Anachraenum has studied the Well of Oribs for nearly two hundred years, but after all this time, they have little understanding of its true purpose or how it was created. It may not be connected to the moon of Orbis at all and is just a name that stuck.
