# Vortest Tower

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists at ⬢ s.3220.2891 in the [[Golden Flats]], west of [[Seawall]].
>
> - This location can be explored within the [[Vortest Tower]] Area Map.
> - This location is featured in [[The Signborn's Secret]].

This lone tower is the remote dwelling and arcane workshop of the Signborn mage Corvana Vortest, an erstwhile purveyor of spells and oddities who lives a hermetic lifestyle near the eastern edge of the Golden Flats. The Vortest Homestead is open to visitors "by invitation only," and it is a rare occasion that lady Corvana issues such invitations willingly.

### Environment

At a glance, Vortest Tower appears to be an 80-foot tall, 50-foot wide circular stone edifice with a single chamber located at its floor level. In reality, this circular antechamber serves as a method of otherworldly conveyance to a larger complex of eight separate chambers — all of which are located in a pocket of the shadow-haunted Inner Realm of Signara, and connected by a series of arcane bridges powered by esoteric magic.

The bone scarecrows that decorate the tower's exterior grounds on Ember are little more than slapdash wards meant to inspire fear and suggest the presence of some bleak entity or force of nature here. Simple illusion magics enhance this air of doom by supplementing Corvana Vortest's crude visuals with evocative sounds of softly-wailing despair.

### Ecology

The gruesome effigies and illusion magics that persist here serve to keep visitors and wildlife away, but flora persists in abundance, as no one maintains the grounds with any regularity whatsoever; this neglect is so apparent, it borders on abandonment. Thus, Golden Flats vegetation like dawngrass and feverbloom are copious; and the only creatures to frequent the area include the occasional grassland rodents and avian raptors.

### Lore

Vortest Tower was created by the Daemos Nalgomith many moons ago to serve as a dwelling and workshop for Corvana Vortest's great grandmother Eda Vortest, who was among the Daemos' most trusted of attachés. In the final days of the Forsaken War, House Vortest was charged with the protection of one of Nalgomith's most hallowed artifacts: a matrix of arcane knowledge known as the Orphic Reliquary. The creation of Vortest Tower has ultimately allowed House Vortest to hide the Reliquary far away from their enemies, while keeping it perpetually tethered to Nalgomith's otherworldly magic. And little do the citizens of the Golden Flats know, Corvana Vortest's isolated lifestyle is part of a clandestine effort to obscure her mysterious occupation: the ongoing protection of the Orphic Reliquary and the curation of its Signaran secrets.

Those bold enough to seek Corvana Vortest's spellcasting services have been met with silence in recent years, and it seems the Signborn mage isn't currently willing to offer her arts to the public. However, this silence is merely a prelude to the events of The Book of Tales, wherein characters may actually meet and aid Corvana Vortest in her cosmic task.
