# Shrouded Borehole

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists at ⬢ s.3204.2888 in the [[Wedgelands]], outside of [[Storsa's Strand]].
>
> - This location is featured in [[Savage Descent]].

The borehole is a secret and well-hidden tunnel that winds its way down into the Pathways from the surface; very few are even aware of its existence. Its walls are jagged and sharp — passing through its tight gaps would require careful and slow maneuvering even under the best of circumstances, and traveling through the borehole is never the best of circumstances. Those who make their way through its narrow pathways find the passageway filled with a deep and unsettling darkness that begins to assault their mind. The horrifying sensation forces most to turn back. Those who continue experience greater and greater stress and intensity as they proceed through its depths, with voices, whispers, and snarls slowly flooding into their minds.

### Environment

The sharp rocks of dark stone twist and appear almost carved as they snake around confusing bends and sudden drops in elevation. There are no life forms of any kind in the borehole, and it appears completely devoid of even dust and dirt. An intense and often screeching wind rushes through the tunnel, but it stops and starts in bursts, sounding to most like someone screaming in pain.

### Ecology

There are no life forms, whether plant, animal, creature, or monster in the borehole. However, it could be said that the darkness within the borehole is alive, whether it is pooling unnaturally in recesses or converging into swirling, raging whirlwinds as the air moves swiftly through the passageway. The concentrated darkness is best avoided if possible, but often the tight space means that those who travel through the borehole's passageways have no choice but to pass directly through it. Those who do experience flashes of great pain, and deep gashes often appear on their bodies.

### Lore

The borehole is so unusual in its shapes and path through Ember that it's hard to imagine that it was created by natural forces. Its origin may remain a mystery, but some believe hat it was created when an Abyssal Horror managed to claw its way out of the Pathways.
