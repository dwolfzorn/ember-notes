# Rustvar Valleys

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Biome is located in the northeastern region of the [[Arctus Plateau]].
>
> - Travel through this Biome is generally [[Region Exploration]].
> - This Biome is visually depicted by the [[Vista: Rustvar Valleys]] Vista.
> - This Biome can be explored within the [[Rustvar Valleys]] Area Map.

The curious stretch of history-laden hinterlands known as the Rustvar Valleys offers a remote glimpse into the Arctus Plateau's storied past. Here, modern Arcturians live among the timeswept outskirts of ancient giant ruins and cultivate a frontier life defined by freewheeling ambition and tenacious grit.

Other than the famed settlement of [[Skybrush]] and a few scattered points of interest hosting the hermetic and the lost, the northern reaches of the Rustvar Valleys are home to relatively few citizens of the Plateau. By comparison, the tributaries of [[Lake Mithra]]to the south provide ample water for travel, fishing, and irrigation; as a result, a small number of households, camps, and farmsteads dot the landscapes near its banks.

### Environment

The Rustvar Valleys are known for a transitional, somewhat arid climate, lying between the surrounding alpine highlands and the drier inland plains, with hot summers and mild winters. The Valleys are markedly less desolate than the Wedgeland mountains that encircle them, and certain aspects of the biome are known to reflect Ember's inherent arcane nature — from the enchanted vestiges of archaic civilizations to the peculiarity of its errant peaks and waterways.

### Ecology

Forests of the Rustvar Valleys are marked by the russet hues of their trees and shrubbery, and a surprising diversity of vegetation — if sparse — can survive here. Although local agricultural potential is hampered, cultivation is possible on a moderate scale. Farmers here are known to grow corn, wheat, barley, sugar beets, peaches, tomatoes, cotton, tobacco, olives, grapes, melons, potatoes, and oranges. Flora unique to the area are known for their resilience and rustic beauty, like the hardy Scragwood Tree and the fruit-bearing Cinderseed Ivy.

The various settlements, farms, and homesteads of the Rustvar Valleys usually cultivate indigenous livestock, but the appearance of outland creatures is not altogether unheard of. A scattering of wild beasts (like bears, wild cats, goats, and more) proliferate the Eastern and Western Valleys, along with a modest array of creatures both mundane and mythical — from the four-horned, oryx-like Nothrin to the venomous, two-headed Vale Monitor. The biome's fauna is often a reflection of (or reaction to) the closest point-of-interest or preternatural phenomenon. And in areas where the Rustvar Valleys border upon the fog-shrouded Barrows, undead creatures like those found in the legendary Varùn burial lands can also be encountered.

### Lore

The Rustvar Valleys are also steeped in the mysterious lore of the ancient [[Shent]] and Varùn giants who once called these lands and the nearby Wedgelands home. Furthermore, the presence of other adjacent biomes have introduced a number of existential threats — including the maddening subterranean fauna of the [[Pathways]] far below and the lingering threat of the Abyssal Horror known as [[Nethehepticas]]. The emergence of these threats has given rise to a disconnected series of tall tales in recent years, originating from the ominous tongues of the most insightful Arcturian soothsayers.
