# Aparon Bay

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This location exists at ⬢ s.3196.2898 on the northern coast of the [[Arctus Plateau Gazetteer]], east of [[Mistwalk]].

Although it lacks any proper ports or docking stations, Aparon Bay has served as an important waterway for seafarers of the Arctus Plateau for centuries. The bay's perpetual fog and mountainous shelter make it a prime candidate for ships that need to lay low while navigating the northern coast of the Plateau. Some regard the area with suspicion, however, daunted by the oppressive nature of its atmospheric events — which often masquerade as supernatural phenomena or folklore made manifest.

### Environment

The barometric pressure of Aparon Bay is constantly above average, caused by the ongoing clash of summery Wedgelands winds with the cold waters of the northern Reaver Ocean. The shores here mostly consist of the same rocky brown stone of the Wedgelands themselves, and the water is often less turbulent than one might expect — stilled by the incessant counterflow of deep ocean channels and the heavy fog-laden air.

### Ecology

Moss growth is rampant among the rocky shores of Aparon Bay, but the relative lack of direct sunlight prevents saltwater flora like algae and kelp from blooming with much abundance. Coastal oceanic fish are plentiful, along with a variety of aqueous predators like sharks and sea snakes.

### Lore

"Apparition Bay" earned its spectral nickname centuries ago when two naval vessels clashed off the northern coast — a pirate galley called The Havoc and an Imperial trireme known as the Leviathan's Tooth. The two ships disappeared in a magical conflagration when The Havoc's warmage let loose a powerful arcane blast, and it is said that any apparitions or figments of the imagination spotted here are the souls of those waylaid pirates and soldiers, who lurk in a metaphysical "otherworld" to this very day.
