### Environment

The environment of the Elenain Delta is varied and largely hazardous. At its safest reaches, small islands, farms and the settlement of [[Saundrine]] swell with vibrant economic activity linked to the moon-reactive Prisma Beetles. Beyond these havens, swamps, mires, peats, and twisting waterways prove an escalating challenge to navigation and survival. The true wild heart of the Delta is the most treacherous realm, where tangled, skeletal trees cast perpetual darkness over uneven, hazardous terrain teeming with all manner of dangers.

### Ecology

The Elenain Delta is a crucible of vibrant plant life and cunning predators, each species having adapted ingeniously to this hostile environment. It is home to numerous carnivorous flora, from Bonebranches that resemble skeletal remains and snare prey with their hardy vines, to Skygrass which swiftly overtakes and consumes anything left sitting too long.

The fauna are equally adapted and dangerous, with wolf-like Bogbiters, poisonous Mudsnakes and horrible Slinkers waiting in the mud and undergrowth. Among the area's more curious inhabitants though, are the moon-reactive Prisma Beetles, which unwittingly create wild magic fields through their vibrant, color-shifting swarms.

### Lore

The Elenain Delta's strange ecology stems from ancient [[Aedir]] aquaculture experiments that were carried out thousands of years prior. These experiments had proceeded without issue until suffering a critical disruption by the cosmic cataclysm of [[The Shattering]]. Surviving remnants of these experiments were warped, imbued with unusual and sometimes deadly characteristics.

However, all of this is ancient and unknown history. There are countless stories from the locals and meandering academic theories about how the land came to be, why it is so dangerous, and why the creatures within are so strange, and few if any of them touch on the true cause. To most, the Elenain Delta is one of two things: home, or best avoided.
