# Golden Flats

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Summary
>
> This Biome is located in the central and northeastern expanse of the [[Arctus Plateau]].
>
> - Travel through this Biome is generally [[Region Exploration]].
> - This Biome is visually depicted by the [[Vista: Golden Flats]] Vista.
> - This Biome can be explored within the [[Golden Flats]] Area Map.

While the sprawling landscape of the Golden Flats seems inviting and serene at first glance, it has recently become rife with turmoil. Chaos and upheaval are now constant threats to those who once enjoyed calm, untroubled lives.

Most settlements in the Golden Flats are [[Arcturian]], built along established trading routes, with smaller towns and farms scattered across the plains. Many of these settlements were founded centuries ago to exploit valuable resources and fertile watercourses. A few settlements, however, were raised in recent years by [[Ordain]] Trading Houses to serve more private ambitions. A steady trickle of merchant caravans — some from as far as Casla-Brava and Toraga — passes through the region, either traveling toward Ordain or circulating locally to supply the villages.

### Environment

Most of the area is safe and quiet, featuring gentle breezes and light rains. However, for nearly two decades, mysterious geological upheavals have increasingly affected several unexpected locations. Residual earthquakes and mild aftershocks from the nearby [[Splinter Canyons]] have altered the landscape, leading many to whisper that the land is now cursed or burdened by some great weight. The appearance of elemental and arcane energy has become common near places imbued with the forces of [[The Cosmos]], growing larger with each new conjunction of the Elemental Moons.

### Ecology

The Golden Flats is known for its rich soil and eponymous golden grasses, which range in color from muddy brown in its furthest reaches to a fiery orange or striking yellow in its centrality. Distinctive plants in the region include Sapgrass, a sweet-tasting yellow grass that can climb any stationary object and is often found trailing down into canyons, and the Waterwell Plant, whose stalks resemble long locks of hair and whose roots can find and hold water even in the driest places.

Animals found throughout the region include the tree-dwelling Docktail Lizard, the carnivorous [[Gore Bird]], the crop-ravaging Tiller Bird, and the hive-dwelling Weaver Bug. Weaver Bugs in particular helped inspire the popular Acturian constructs known as Woven; these ramshackle automatons vary in construction materials, but are generally made of tightly wound materials (from vines to grass to cloth).

The persistent arcane and cataclysmic reshaping of Ember’s crust that occurs throughout the Golden Flats is responsible for the recent reemergence of ancient Arcturian megafauna, such as the six-legged Katherim, the pack-hunting avian Avir, the aberrantly nimble [[Juvenile Garganthus]], and the ferociously brutish Urkon. These rare (and sometimes unique) [[Pathways]] behemoths are creatures thought long extinct, and are viewed by the people of the Golden Flats with a healthy mix of reverence and fear.

### Lore

The Golden Flats has been historically peaceful for thousands of years and was once claimed and tamed by the [[Aedir]], who constructed several outposts and settlements on its shores. Since [[The Shattering]], however, it has been settled by the Arcturians, who reveled in a place with few dangers and mild weather. Unfortunately, twenty years ago, a massive earthquake tore through the Arctus Plateau, sundering the land and releasing an array of primeval creatures from the Pathways into the Golden Flats. The emergence of these megafauna specimens has had various effects on the biome; while some of the creatures live in tranquil cohabitation with the people of the Golden Flats, others have proven calamitous and continue to wreak havoc.
