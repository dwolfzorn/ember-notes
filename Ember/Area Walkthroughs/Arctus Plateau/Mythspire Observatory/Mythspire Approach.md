# Mythspire Approach

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The ruins of Mythspire rise up out of the mountains, in some places looking as though they were carved from the ancient stone directly.
>
> Layers of massive bricks form outer walls that curve around ridges of stone, and meet immense, rune-covered pillars. Everything is oversized here, even the great stone doors which provide entrance to the place.
>
> The ruins reach upward for a hundred feet or more, presenting the shape of a jagged, half-collapsed dome which the mountain winds play with to create a mournful howl that cuts through the silence here.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Crumbling Ruins
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Shent** or making a successful a **Society (DC 14)** check suspect this place is a Shent ruin, given the size and style, though it does break from some of the establish elements one would expect to see.
>
> - **Critical Success** you suspect that these ruins might be early Shent, given the prevalence of large stone bricks versus mostly contiguous stonework found in newer ruins.
