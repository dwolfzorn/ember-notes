# Balcony Perimeter

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A wide balcony extends around the open space here, and along this curve of the upper level are numerous glowing gems suspended over the floor by unseen magic, each anchored in place with heavy bronze chains.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Universal Fittings
>
> The gems are are set in bronze fitting, and held above the ground by unseen forces, likely magic, with heavy chains keeping them in place. The chains themselves are anchored to cog-like tiles set into the stone which are carved with numerous runs of a peculiar make.
>
> A successful **Arcana (DC 14)** check leaves you believing they are Shent in origin.
>
> - **Critical Success** At first glance they appear Shent, but the designs are strange and deviate in places. You suspect these are the runes of a different people, yielding different magic, but related to the Shent magic you're aware of.
>
> #### Cosmic Gems
>
> A successful **Society (DC 14)**recognizes that each of the gems in this upper area coincide with the moons of Ember, and the sun Lantyr. Their specific order along the balcony doesn't seem to be important, but their presence here does.
>
> - **The Gem of Lantyr** is as golden and warm as the sun itself.
> - **The Gem of Akon** gem is nearly black, and featureless with a faint hint of green.
> - **The Gem of Orbis** is a chaotic shifting mess of pinks, blues, and purples.
> - **The Gem of Ragen** is a deep red and radiates warmth even when dim.
> - **The Gem of Mayis** is deep blue, and beneath its facets are dim currents of light.
> - **The Gem of Cora** is a rich green and it exudes a palpable sensation of heaviness.
> - **The Gem of Aura** is an ethereal pink gem with facets that swirl with clouds.
>
> #### Theft Averse
>
> The gems are all magically fixed in their fittings and the chains are reinforced many times over with magic. It should be functionally impossible for the fittings or their chains to be damaged or broken, making theft of these ancient gems beyond the party.
>
> All attempts to do so automatically fail.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Interactable Gems
>
> Each of the gems have an interactable element that when clicked will turn the gem's lighting effect on or off. This is used both for the combat in the [[Central Room]] and the puzzle detailed in [[Ancient Lift]].
