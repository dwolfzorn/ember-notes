# Chamber Overlook

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You step onto a broad, open platform bounded by a chest-high railing of shaped stone. Beyond the railing, the view plunges down into the vast, central void of the Mythspire — a breathtaking drop of hundreds of feet that reveals level upon level of ruined walkways, shattered stairwells, and inaccessible spaces in between.
>
> Overhead, the remnants of a massive dome arch high above, with large gaps in its structure letting the brutal mountain winds in unabated. The constant sigh and howl of the mountain winds threading through ancient gaps can be heard here, echoing off the old stone like a ghostly chorus.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Ruins Overhead
>
> A **Awareness (DC 14)** check reveals that the remains of the massive central dome were once etched with constellations, though much of this has been worn away by wind or crumbled with age.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Machines** or **Knowledge: Shent** note there is an immense amount of bent metal and precariously hanging structures overhead that looks like it was once host to a massive orrery or telescope of some sort.
>
> - A successful **Awareness (DC 14)** while peering up at the remains above leads one to suspect that something grabbed and wrenched a large portion of the building off the top. Not many things could do that on Ember.
>
> #### Familiar Shapes
>
> Characters that completed [[Lunar Awakenings]] or have **Knowledge: Shent** recognize the remains of a planetarium projector similar to the one hanging in the Mayis temple, though this one is heavily damaged and many parts are missing.
>
> Otherwise a successful **Arcana (DC 16)** check reveals the magical runes in the walls and the machinery in the center of the space would have been used to magically project a vision of the cosmos.
>
> #### Loose Change
>
> Characters with **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** check notice an old silver coin on the ground.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Aedir** or making a successful **Society (DC 16)**check recognize it is an [[Ancient Aediri Coin]] that might be worth something to a collector or historian.

Character that spend some time looking up at the dome may also notice a solitary glass orb in the ruins.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Lost amongst the wreckage of the ruins at first, your eyes catch a flash of light and are drawn to a large glass orb swaying with the winds.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### One Lonely Sphere
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Machines** or making a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** note where the orb once connected to the observatory's upper rails. Unfortunately, the damage to the structure as a whole has left the sphere isolated, and impossible to reach.

Characters looking down can also attempt to glimpse insight from the ruins below, though the distance and darkness does limit what they can see.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Ruins Below
>
> A successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check notes that the lower levels are badly damaged, with the various walkways and balconies having collapsed on themselves, or fallen away entirely.
>
> There might be half a dozen other levels to explore in this tower, but there is no clear or remotely safe way to reach them. Even if one could levitate or fly it looks like the entrances are choked with rubble.
