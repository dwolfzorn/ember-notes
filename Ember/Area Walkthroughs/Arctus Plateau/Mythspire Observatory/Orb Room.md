# Orb Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> At the center of this space you see a large, circular frame. Though not too badly damaged itself, the room around it is littered with further pieces of metal, gears, oversized tools, lengths of braided steel cable, and more that seem to have come from some machinery and racks overhead.
>
> Above the large steel cradle, metal rails run into the ceiling. Some stretch northward over a now-collapsed bridge, while the rest continue up into the gloom overhead, supported by cables anchored to high points on the walls.

This chamber holds the mechanisms needed to move large glass orbs in the observatory, however, time and the elements have caused it all to collapse to the point of being useless.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Orb Apparatus
>
> Characters that noticed the suspended orb in the [[Chamber Overlook]] would recognize this apparatus was meant to deal with those orbs specifically.
>
> Otherwise, **Knowledge: Machines** or a successful **Science (DC 14)** check while studying the machinery surmises that the central cradle was used to hold large circular orbs, possibly to work on them, affix mounting hardware, or similar.
>
> #### Off the Rails
>
> The rails overhead would have allowed the Shent here to move orbs back up into the ceiling area, or run them north to another space nearby. However, the rails have become partially detached from the ceiling in places, or are simply missing in others.
>
> #### Beyond Repair
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Machines** recognize that the damage is far too extensive to repair without considerable resources and support.

Characters looking around the room would also note that there are various carved stone plaques fixed to the wall featuring some sort of language. Additionally, among the rubble and wreckage are old tools.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Writing On the Wall
>
> Characters that understand **Language: Pathward** can parse instructions cut into stone plates on the walls stating the followings things:
>
> - Warning: Improper suspension may result in the loss of projection orbs, injury or death.
> - Ensure that all projection orbs are secured in cradle before undergoing movement or etching work. Orbs may become dislodged and could break or fall on you.
> - Please return all etching and cutting tools to their associated racks.
>
> #### Old Equipment
>
> What at first appeared to be pieces of old machinery or rusting debris turns out to be ancient tools. Though rusted and pitted beyond use, they might lend some insight into the purpose of the room.
>
> Characters with `[[/knowledge Shent]]` or making a successful **Arcana (DC 14)** check recognize that the oversized tools feature ancient runs for transmutation and evocation that would have been used to cut and carve materials.
>
> - Characters gaining this insight via their knowledge of the Shent also know that similar tools like this existed for cutting and shaping stone, but were not quite as delicate as these appear to be.
