# Crumbling Walkway

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You find yourself on a broad, curved walkway, partially buried beneath piles of broken stone and ancient debris. The outer edge is protected by a chest-high stone barrier, cracked in several places, which keeps you from a long, fatal fall to the lower levels.
>
> The balcony leads only in one direction; the rest is blocked by collapsed sections. At the far end, a set of broad and unusually tall steps rises up to the level above.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Short Hop
>
> This walkway is 30 feet below the upper, broken walkway that exits the [[Orb Room]]. Characters can jump down to this area without a roll, but it is a one-way trip.
