# Ancient Lift

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A great stone circle thirty feet wide rests in the center of the space, with multiple concentric rings of ornately decorated stone making up its internal area. Numerous magical runes can be seen scattered through the cracked and worn stone, most prominently along the outer lip of the circle.

The passage down into the Pathways is actually an ancient elevator built nearly thirty thousand years ago using magic that predates even the oldest known civilizations.

## Examining the Lift

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Ancient Magic
>
> A successful **Arcana (DC 15)** check while examining the large stone circle in the floor confirms that the runes are of an ancient magical make and contribute to numerous magical effects that not only animate the stone up and done, but project illusory images which can be interacted with, and preserve the stone so that it does not crumble from the predation of time or excessive use.
>
> It must be activated elsewhere, however.
>
> #### Ancient Orders
>
> To open the passage, the cosmos gems must be activated in the correct order: Lantyr -> Akon -> Aura -> Mayis -> Orbis -> Ragen -> Cora. Once this is done the gems will begin to pulse in order and the a glowing set of runes appear on the lift, indicating that it is primed and ready to use.
>
> Interacting with the glowing runes on the lift causes the lift to descend.
>
> To discern the pattern, refer to the [[Central Room]] section found in the [[Central Room]] page.

### Reactivating the Lift

When the party enables the lift down into the pathways, check the **Unlocked Ancient Lift** outcome.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The ancient masonry groans and shifts, unseen mechanisms clicking into place with rapidity. Then, there is a moment of stillness, followed by a deep shuddering in the structure. A moment later a massive glowing rune appears in the air over over the center of the lift, gently glowing.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Elevator Panel
>
> Characters that speak **Language: Pathward** recognize that the rune is an interactable magical projection that will send the lift downward if touched.
>
> Otherwise a successful **Arcana (DC 12)** determines that the projected magical rune is a control mechanism that allows someone to activate the lift. It likely only has on function: descend. Though this may change once the lift is in motion.
>
> A **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** or better notices that the rune is unexpectedly high up, and was likely not meant for humanoids your size.

### Engaging the Lift

Once the party activates the lift, it begins its descent into the Pathways.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> With a deep, resonant hum, the stone platform begins its descent, sinking slowly at first and then picking up speed as ancient magic guides it deeper into the earth. Runes along the lift's edge glow steadily, passing by in a predictably rhythm, their light dancing off the walls as they do.
>
> Minutes pass, yet the lift carries on, moving steadily downward. The air grows warmer, and the color and texture of the stone changes over time, as you descend through more and more layers of Ember's crust.
>
> Finally, the lift slows its descent, and then comes to a smooth halt with a final, magical hum that reverberates through your core. Before you, massive, meandering passages wind into the distance and darkness.
