# Mythspire Entrance

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Crossing the threshold of the Mythspire Observatory, you are greeted by an almost oppressive silence, punctuated only by the whispering wind sneaking through gaps in the ancient stonework. The air within feels heavy, and colder than outside, as if the timeless stone greedily absorbs all the heat it can.
>
> Rubble lies strewn across the floor, the entire structure continuing its slow spiral toward oblivion. Sunlight pierces through the great shattered dome above, casting stark, uneven beams that illuminate the otherwise dark interior.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Opening the Way
>
> The doors are heavy stone and require a successful **Athletics (DC 20)**check to open by a single character. However, if two or more characters attempt to push open the door then only a successful **Athletics (DC 16)** is required.
>
> #### Automatic Doors
>
> Characters making a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check while examining the doors note that there is a large recessed area in the center of the door, situated about six feet up. It has the shape of a hand.
>
> Pressing one's hand to the recessed area causes the doors to open automatically, sliding open smoothly, moved by an invisible force.
>
> #### General Ruins Lore
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Shent** or who make a successful **Society (DC 15)** check spot the hallmarks of Shent construction.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Shent** or **Knowledge: Crafts** or who make a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** **Science (DC 14)**check notice that the construction appears new in comparison to the even more ancient stonework, indicating this structure has been repaired or rebuilt more than once in its life.
