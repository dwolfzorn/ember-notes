# Ancient Lift

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You step into a spacious, square chamber dominated by a broad stone platform at its very center. Measuring twenty feet to each side, the platform is slightly raised from the floor, and its edges are smoothed, as though well traveled.
>
> A raised pedestal stands just next to it and is set with a large crystal plate bearing the shallow imprint of a humanoid hand.
>
> Mounted on opposite walls, large crystals of pale white stone rest within rune-etched metal fixtures. A few of these crystals are cracked straight through, and a few slots in the wall are outright empty.

At the center of the room is the lift, the shape and pattern of the stone clear different from the rest of the floor.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### A Way Down
>
> The crystals set into the wall appear to powered the lift.
>
> - Characters that did [[Lunar Awakenings]] recognize these as carved crystals utilized by the Shent to power magical devices.
>
> The party can descend to the lower level by pressing their hand to the pedestal.

### Activating the Lift

Once the elevator has been reactivated, it can be used to descend to the lower area.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The stone platform shudders as magical energy rushes through it. A moment later the lift begins to descend, smoothly sliding down into the darkness. Though the shaft is enclosed, gaps in the crumbling stone give you fleeting glimpses of the numerous levels in between the upper level and your destination.
>
> The lift slides past each one, as though it is aware the entrances are blocked by piles of rubble, collapsed walkways, and rusting iron. Instead you descend further into the darkness, toward the lowest levels of the Mythspire.

At this point the party will be transported to the Mythspire Observatory's lower area.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Manual Scene Transition
>
> For the time being, you will need to manually load the [[Mythspire Observatory - Lower]] scene and move the party characters to it.
