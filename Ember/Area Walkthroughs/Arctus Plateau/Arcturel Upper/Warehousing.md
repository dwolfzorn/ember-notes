# Warehousing

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This warehouse is lined with rows of towering steel shelves, each reaching to the stone ceiling and laden with stacks and baskets of metal — polished plates, rods, gears, and half-assembled construct frames, and more. The air carries the sharp tang of iron and oil.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Wandering Workers
>
> There is at least one Silver Beam Engineer and one member of Silver Beam Security in this area at all hours of the day. While the engineer is distracted with examining and cataloguing the collection of materials and construct parts here, the guard is actively patrolling and watching for signs of trouble.
>
> The guard's path takes them outside often, and the Engineer takes ample breaks, getting some fresh air out on the [[Walkways]] every five minutes or so. If both the guard and engineer happen to be outside at the same time, they may chat for several minutes.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Work Orders
>
> Near the elevator are parts packed in sturdy crates or loaded into wire baskets. All of them bear bright tags, marking them for transport to distant assembly areas located elsewhere in the Silver Beam headquarters.
>
> Characters with a **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** or making a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check notice that some of the crates are staged near the elevator and are marked for the floor right above them.
>
> #### Stacks and Shelves
>
> Characters that examine the shelves who also visited [[The Scrapyard]] location as part of the [[Junkyard Cogs]] event will recognize the processed pieces of [[Chessman]] constructs. However, these have been properly converted to Servitor pieces, and no longer bear any damning marks.
