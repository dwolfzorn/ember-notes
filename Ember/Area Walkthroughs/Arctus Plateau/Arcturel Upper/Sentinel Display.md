# Sentinel Display

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You see a broad, raised dais of spotless white, at its center rests an imposing statue — a heavy automaton clad in of overlapping plates of brushed steel. One hand bears a massive claw, the other arm is fixed with a long blade. Its back features two projectors of unknown purpose.
>
> Though the statue is motionless, every part of it radiates one purpose alone: war.
>
> At the base of the dais, a polished plaque reads:
>
> > The Silver Beam Sentinel
> >
> > The next generation in combat constructs.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining Automatons
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Machines** or **Knowledge: Crafts** recognize that this massive constructs was probably functional at some point. Some of the construction looks haphazard, with gaps in the armor, and crude, probably underpowered servos. This was likely an early prototype turned into a display.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Aedir** recognize the style of the machine, as with the rest of the Servitors in this place, is more akin to that of Aedir constructs and automatons than classical Arcturian designs.
>
> #### Contents Under Pressure
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Machines** or **Knowledge: Crafts** or making a successful **Science (DC 14)** check on the construct notice a poorly protected tank of fuel just under this the construct's left shoulder. Going by the gauge there, it is still mostly full and pressurized.
>
> This is not only an indication that the construct once was operational, but is a considerable fire hazard. If compromised, that tank would probably fill the surrounding area with a large explosion of fire and shrapnel.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Explosive Hazard
>
> Characters that noticed the fuel tank on the construct can attack that fuel tank to cause it to explode.Those who do at least 10 damage with attacks against the specific target of the fuel tank cause the fuel tank to explode in a 20ft area centered on the construct. Those caught in the blast suffer **Shrapnel Blast (Hazard 9, Fortitude, Health, Piercing)**.
>
> If the tank is destroyed by an attack or spell that deals lightning or fire damage the tank explodes more violently and in a larger area. This creates a 40ft area centered on the construct , anyone caught in this area suffer **Shrapnel… (Hazard 9, Fortitude, Health, Piercing)****…Flaming Sharpnel (Hazard 9, Fortitude, Health, Piercing)**
