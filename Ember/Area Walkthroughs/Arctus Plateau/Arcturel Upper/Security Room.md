# Security Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You find yourself in a low-lit chamber of cold gray stone, here some chairs and a sturdy stone table anchor the space, its surface bare save for a couple half-full tankards and a forgotten dagger in its sheath. The room is stark and functional, free from any decoration or finery, built for purpose rather than comfort.
>
> Along one wall, racks display weapons of varying make—swords, hammers, axes, a handful of shields arranged for quick access. At the back, a narrow, unmarked stairwell descends to some lower level.

The stairwell here descends to the [[Barracks]] found in the [[Arcturel Upper - Underbelly]] area map.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### On Guard
>
> There are 2-4 members of [[Silver Beam Guard]] in this room at all hours. It is their job to respond to alerts, investigate disturbances, and patrol the area.
>
> If an alert is raised from either of the desks in the [[Silver Beam Foyer]] or [[Director's Office]], this is the security team that will respond to it.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Standard Issue
>
> The racks here have a selection of well made but otherwise unremarkable martial weapons including swords, axes, picks, hammers, and maces.
