# Underbelly Alleys

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You find yourself in a secluded quarter of Arcturel situated just under the main plaza and tradeway. Here, sturdy platforms of broad metal plates and latticed grates stretch out over the yawning sinkhole below.
>
> The spaces between these metal walkways are crowded with a wild tangle of unmarked and unremarkable buildings that could be any mixture of homes, private shops, and unseen businesses. Together they form a network of alleys connecting without pattern or plan to other passages that themselves go on to connect again and again.
>
> You get the sensation that only locals know how to get around down here easily.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Abstract Alleyways
>
> This area is full of buildings and places that are used by the locals but have no specific gameplay purpose. You may use this space as a transition point to other parts of the location, such as heading to the [[Arcturel Upper - Tradeway]] or down to [[Arcturel Lower]].
