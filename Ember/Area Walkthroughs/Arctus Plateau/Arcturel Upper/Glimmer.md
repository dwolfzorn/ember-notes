# Glimmer

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Built from broad slabs of golden stone, matching the refined paving of the plaza itself, this squat and unassuming building is accented with wide, clear windows set evenly along the walls. The entrance is a sturdy stone door, faced with the same golden stone as the rest.
>
> Above that door, a sign:
>
> > Glimmer

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Operating Hours
>
> Glimmer is open until sundown, afterward the doors are locked. The doors appear to be made of stone, but they have steel cores and advanced locks. A successful **Stealth (DC 20)** check requiring [[Lockpicks]] is required to open them.
>
> The door, walls and windows are all magically reinforced..

Inside the shop, one finds a mixture of art gallery and gem shop.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Inside, Glimmer opens into a single, spacious chamber paved with fine stone tiles. The light within dances off countless displays lining the walls, where both raw crystals and brilliantly cut gems are set on shelves and nestled in cases for easy browsing.
>
> Statues and busts of polished stone occupy the corners, and are joined by large, richly framed paintings. The busts and art combined with the constant glimmer of precious stones ensures that there’s always something to catch the eye.
>
> Along the wall a stairwell of plain wood descends quietly out of sight, leading to some deeper and unmarked room below.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### All That Glimmers
>
> Glimmer is owned and operated by the flamboyant and gregarious Nodrick Stolas (Arcturian Human, he/him). He operates Glimmer as a mixture of gem store, art gallery, and night club. He is happy to identify and appraise gems on request.
>
> Characters can acquire nearly any kind of gem they might want here at typical market prices, and can even sell any gems or crystals they've acquired for a modest profit.
>
> Nodrick's prices are fair, and fixed, he doesn't haggle.
>
> #### Nightlife
>
> In the evenings Glimmer serves as a club, the lower area serving as a lounge for clients, friends, and other esteemed associates. As Glimmer closes after sundown, the only way into the [[Glimmer Lounge]] after closing time is from under the Tradeway.
