# Lounge Space

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This open-air lounge features chairs and couches arranged for comfort, offering a place for employees to pause and rest. Landscapes of some distant city hang on the wall, each held in a silver frame.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Familiar Places
>
> Characters with **Culture: Railen** or either **Knowledge: Politics** or **Knowledge: Trade** immediately recognize the city of [[Ancalen]], the home city of the [[Silver Beam Consortium]]. Characters without the above traits need to succeed on a**Society (DC 14)** check.
