# Cubicles

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You find a small, utilitarian area tucked beside the lift platform. Four simple cubicles stand together, each walled off by this panels fixed with iron bolts. Within each cramped work space are the signs of constant busyness — thick ledgers, sprawling survey reports, mining maps, and stacks of unfiled work orders, all needing attention.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Wary Workers
>
> During operating hours consortium employees can be found here doing their thankless jobs. They ignore anyone walking past unless they are acting strangely or doing things to draw attention. Characters wearing Silver Beam uniforms won't get a second glance if they leave these clerks alone.
>
> However, if characters do happen to draw attention, the clerks will immediately cry out, trying to draw attention from security in the [[Warehousing]] and [[Barracks]] areas if they can.
>
> They will flee if fighting breaks out.
