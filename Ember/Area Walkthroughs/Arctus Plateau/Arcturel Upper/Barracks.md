# Barracks

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You enter a barracks meant to house off-duty security and other personnel, the floor made of riveted iron plates that echo each step. Five sturdy bunkbeds line the walls, their sturdy iron frames bolted to the walls. Each bed is fitted with a thin mattress, white linens, and dark blue sheets.
>
> In the center of the room stands a table, surrounded by chairs. The place is dimly lit by a single lantern hung from a ceiling bracket, the air cool and heavy with the scent of metal and stone. Everything here is built for function and endurance, leaving little room for comfort.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Distracted Defenders
>
> Almost all hours of the day there are at least two off-duty members of [[Silver Beam Guard]] here, chatting with each other. They aren't expecting trouble, and are not especially alert or wary unless given a reason to be.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Useful Items
>
> Investigating the footlockers here yields two [[Silver Beam Uniform]] which can help characters blend in if they want to utilize stealth.
