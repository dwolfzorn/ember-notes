# Waiting Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You enter a spacious waiting room with four tall, decorative columns in each corner, their surfaces wrapped with delicate silver accents. Along the pristine walls, a collection of grand paintings displays the distant, wild landscapes of Railen — vast plains, wave-swept coasts, and forbidding mountains rendered in vivid detail. Each frame is set in polished silver, drawing your eye from scene to scene.
>
> A deep-cushioned couch and two plush chairs offer a comfortable place to sit.
>
> To one side are double doors and a placard declaring:
>
> > Security — Employees Only

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Standing By
>
> During operating hours, there is always one member of [[Silver Beam Guard]] in this room, standing in front of the security door. They are not hostile, but they are alert and watchful for trouble.
>
> Beyond them, there are several more on standby in the [[Security Room]].
