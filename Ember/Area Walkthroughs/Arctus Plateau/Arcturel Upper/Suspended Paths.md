# Suspended Paths

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Avenues lined with brass railings, diamond-pattered plates, and warm golden stone stretch out before you. Massive metal cables attach at regular intervals along each of them, and extend upward to anchor points all along the mouth of the great sinkhole cliffs.
>
> Ample stairwells and ramps allow for traversal up and down between levels, leading to Brimtown above, or depths below.
>
> Beyond the tradeway you can see numerous homes and other businesses sitting on suspended platforms just like this one, though none of them are especially remarkable from here.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Abstract Avenues
>
> This area has no specific gameplay purpose but can be used as a transition point to other parts of the Arcturel location, such as heading to the [[Arcturel Upper - Underbelly]] or down to [[Arcturel Lower]].
