# Storage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cramped storage room is packed with crates stacked two-high, clusters of kegs and barrels, and burlap sacks filled with flour, dried beans, and other staples bound for the kitchen.
>
> Rising from floor to ceiling opposite the entrance is a shelf unit crafted from rough-cut planks and thick iron brackets fixed to the brick walls.
>
> The shelves hold an assortment of bar necessities: stacks of pewter mugs and sturdy plates, jars of pickled vegetables, dusty bottles of syrup and tonic, spare candles and lantern oil, and more not worthy of mention.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Packed Larder
>
> While this supply room is packed with goods necessary to operate Rallyhome, nothing on these shelves is especially eye-catching or valuable looking. The materials here are mundane at best, worthless at worst.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Employees Only
>
> There's a large amount of fresh food, small amounts of commodities, and a few spare bottles of alcohol here to be taken if desired. However, this area is off-limits to patrons and the bar's staff come through this area often, so windows to steal things will be few and brief.
>
> If caught, guests are ushered back out to the bar after being relieved of any items they have in their hands. If the characters continue to be problematic, they will be removed, with guards being called if necessary.
