# Assembly Area

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You see a long conveyor belt running the length of the marble wall, its surface crowded with a neat procession of mechanical parts — gears, rods, construct arms and heads, and curved metal plates — each piece moving along with the hum of the belt, and meant for some part of this company's new servitor line.
>
> Employees carefully sort the shift parts, fitting smaller pieces together and setting aside incomplete assemblies. Each finished component is placed into a wire basket set beside them.

The workers here are all [[Silver Beam Engineer]].

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Watchful Guards
>
> There is a lone member of the Silver Beam Security team posted up against the wall at all hours, watching over the workers. Any attempts to steal or tamper with the assembly line will get characters thrown out and barred reentry.
>
> If a fight breaks out, the Engineers will join the Security in the fight.
