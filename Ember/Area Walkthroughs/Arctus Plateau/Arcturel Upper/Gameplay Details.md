# Gameplay Details

### Arcturel Verticality

The distance between the Tradeway and Underbelly levels of upper Arcturel is 20 feet in most places, with some rooftops providing shorter 10 foot drops.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### A Long Way Down
>
> Characters that jump or fall off of upper Arcturel and don't land on a lower level will find themselves falling hundreds upon hundreds of feet to the Sinkhole Depths below.
>
> Without some magical means to float or fly, most characters will not survive this fall.
>
> We recommend giving characters one or more **Athletics (DC 14)** checks to catch ledges, suspension cables, walkways, outstretched hands, or hastily thrown ropes to potentially save themselves from an early demise.

### Silver Beam Consortium Offices

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Activating Alerts
>
> There are a few places in the offices where security buttons exist, some are obvious, others a hidden. They all have the same general functionality based on the number of times they are pressed:
>
> - **First Press: Alert.** The [[Silver Beam Guard]] in the nearby [[Security Room]] are alerted, drawing one or two of them to the area to investigate.
> - **Second Press: Alarm.** An alarm bell sounds, bringing all members of the [[Security Room]] to that area. If there are any nearby dormant Servitor constructs, they activate and assist Security in subduing intruders.
> - **Third Press: Lockdown.** Metal security doors seal the offices, and more security are summoned to the level, arriving via any available elevators. It usually takes about 3 minutes for security to arrive once a Lockdown occurs.
>
> #### Disabling Alerts
>
> Holding the button for 10 seconds cancels the alert and unseals the doors, though security will still come to investigate and the constructs will remain active until ordered to stand down by a Silver Beam employee.
>
> #### Idle Servitors
>
> Located all over the Silver Beam offices there are [[Silver Beam Servitor]] that appear to be dormant and meant for display. These servitors are waiting for instructions, or an alert at which point they become active. They only follow orders from Silver Beam personnel, and always listen to the highest-ranking person they are aware of.
>
> The ranking order is: Larissa Toth > Engineers > Guards > Receptionist > All Other Employees

> [!info] Social
> #### Consortium Disguises
>
> While interacting with Silver Beam personnel, visiting their operations, and getting involved in quests that feature them there is a chance for the players to acquire one or more [[Silver Beam Uniform]].
>
> While wearing these uniforms they are able to move through Silver Beam controlled areas with relative ease as long as they aren't caught doing anything suspicious or hostile.
>
> Use of a magical disguise would also suffice.
>
> #### Dubious Hiring Practices
>
> Silver Beam only hires [[Altyra]] and [[Human]] employees, and many of them are imported from other Silver Beam operations from abroad. This means that any other ancestries trying to pass themselves off as employees will stand out sharply and draw attention.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Bypassing Doors
>
> All doors in the Silver Beam offices are of a higher quality and require a **Stealth (DC 15)** with lockpicks to successfully open.
>
> Forcing doors open with tools (such as a prybar) requires a successful **Athletics (DC 14)**check and makes enough noise to alert any Silver Beam personnel within 60 feet.
>
> The doors are not specially reinforced or armored, and can be destroyed with sufficient damage.
>
> #### Heavy Doors
>
> There are heavy metal and glass doors that limit access to the offices via the [[Silver Beam Foyer]] and also can close off the [[Director's Office]].
>
> These doors have no obvious locks, and the recessed mechanisms require a **Stealth (DC 20)** with lockpicks to successfully open by bypassing. Characters with **Knowledge: Machines** have **+2 Boons** on these checks.
>
> Prying them open requires a successful **Athletics (DC 21)** check while using a crow bar. The doors are otherwise magically protected from harm, and highly resistant to physical damage.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Alarmed Doors
>
> Attempting to force open or break down a door, or failing a tool check by 5 or more triggers a security alert. This causes the nearby [[Security Room]] guards to become aware of an intrusion attempt, drawing one or two of them to the area to investigate.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Reinforced Walls
>
> The walls of the Silver Beam offices are reinforced stone and steal and are highly resistant to physical damage. They are designed to resist being breached. Even internal walls are much more resilient than most.
