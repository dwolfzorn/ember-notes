# Main Plaza

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A broad expanse paved with golden stone cut into tidy squares stretches out before you, bands of bright brass trace the edges of walkways and create threads of gleaming gold that weave throughout the area.
>
> At the center of the plaza is a large stone fountain, broad and tiered. Water burbles up from its crown before spilling down each tier, filling the air with a steady, cheerful rush and cooling mist.
>
> Arranged around the fountain are a small number of open-air market stalls, each canopied with materials of bright color and pattern. Vendors call out in friendly chorus, each eager to draw a crowd beneath their shaded awnings.
>
> Beyond the bustle of stalls, the plaza is bordered by shops that see steady foot traffic. Signs proclaim the presence of Arcturian Automatons, Rallyhome, the Silver Beam Consortium, Glimmer, and a restaurant called Precipice.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Tradeway Stalls
>
> The things being offered in this open-air stalls vary often, with merchants coming and going as they choose. In general these stalls will sell fresh produce, hot food, preserved rations, hand-made clothing, dried herbs and spices, and sometimes even spell components.
>
> Prices are usually very reasonable, and when they aren't the merchants are willing to haggle with a **Diplomacy (DC 12)** check being enough to bring them down to average market price for the item being offered.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Good Luck
>
> The fountain contains a bevy of coins tossed into the water for good luck. Characters without a sense of decorum or shame can take a few minutes to fish coins out of the fountain. This yields a fistful of assorted coins worth `[[/roll 1d6]]` silver and can be done once.
>
> The various onlookers and passers by will cast disapproving looks at any characters that do this.
