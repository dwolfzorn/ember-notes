# Kitchen

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Rallyhome’s kitchen is a compact space that is busy and warm. The wood floors are well cared for but stained from years of bustling use. Heat radiates from a squat iron stove nestled against one wall. Beside the stove, a prep table is crowded with sprigs of fresh herbs, half-chopped vegetables, and various utensils.
>
> On the opposite side, occupying nearly the entire wall, are stout shelves sagging under the weight of food. Jars filled with spices, preserved fruits, and pickles stand beside loaves of bread, wheels of cheese, and all manner of other ingredients that the cook might need.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Staff Only
>
> The kitchen is off-limits to patrons and almost always occupied by the bar's cook. Not only this, but the bar's staff come through this area often, so windows to steal things will be few and brief.
>
> If caught, guests are ushered back out to the bar after being relieved of any items they have in their hands. If the characters continue to be problematic, they will be removed, with guards being called if necessary.
