# Walkways

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You stand on an external walkway, its grated floor and pipe rails all that keep you from the long drop into the sinkhole depths. The walkways stretch across the face of the structure, connecting to distant doors and platforms. The wind whistles through the open iron, and there is little here to block the sweep of air or the feeling of height.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Well Traveled
>
> The Silver Beam personnel inside this level have a tendency to come out here to smoke, take in some fresh air, patrol, and take breaks. Every five minutes or so someone will come out here. The general lack of cover means that hiding is next to impossible here.
