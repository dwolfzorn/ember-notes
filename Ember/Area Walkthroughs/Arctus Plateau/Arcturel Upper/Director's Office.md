# Director's Office

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You step into a vast, circular office, its enormous glass window arcing across half the room and offering a breathtaking view of Arcturel. In each corner of the room, a broad stone dais supports a silent gray-metal construct.
>
> Collected to one side are a white couches and some chairs, their pale upholstery blending in with the gray and white of the room's marble. Opposite the seating area a curved desk fashioned from brushed steel commands attention. Its surface is strewn with maps and layers of documents, the placard before it reading:
>
> > Larissa Toth, Director
>
> Furthest from the entrance, against the window, stands a broad, immaculate conference table of silver metal, ringed with deep blue chairs whose cushions mirror the sky just beyond the glass.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Pressing Buttons
>
> The Director's desk features a hidden buttons that can be spotted with a successful **Awareness (DC 16)** check. The button is unlabeled, and only [[Larissa Toth]] knows what it does.
>
> Predictably, it is used to alert security.
>
> See: [[Gameplay Details]] in [[Gameplay Details]] for how this works.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Office Staples
>
> Searching the director's desk yields the same items as the desks in the [[Side Offices]].
