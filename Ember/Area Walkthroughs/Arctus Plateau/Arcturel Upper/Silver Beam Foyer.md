# Silver Beam Foyer

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Built directly into the rich brown stone, this dark structure reaches out across the gap to contact the plaza. There, the dark stone and curved walls are a sharp contrast to the warm, golden angles of the tradeway.
>
> The steps up to the structure bear a sign that declares:
>
> > The Silver Beam Consortium
> >
> > Arcturel Branch

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Not From Round These Parts
>
> Chracters with the **Culture: Arcturian** or **Culture: Ordani** recognize that this structure reads is unmistakably foreign, not bearing any local sensibilities of design or aesthetic.
>
> Characters making a successful **Society (DC 13)** check recognize the building as having Railen sensibilities in places.
>
> #### Operating Hours
>
> The Silver Beam offices are open until sundown, then the front doors are closed and locked.

Inside the double doors is reception, though access to the building beyond this point is limited.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You find yourself in a grand, circular lobby dedicated to the Silver Beam Consortium's offices here in Arcturel. Tall windows allow light to pour in through the glass. This, paired with the gleaming white marble that's been polished to a fine sheen makes the space feel impossibly clean and bright.
>
> At the very center stands a broad desk of ornately worked grey metal, its filigreed surface catching the light with each subtle movement. Here, the receptionist sits, their posture formal and welcoming at once.
>
> On either side of this desk, two slender constructs fashioned of shining silver and painted with blue livery stand perfectly still upon raised stone daises.

The Silver Beam Consortium offices in Arcturel are accessed by appointment only. The Receptionist (an [[Arcturian]]) will not allow anyone inside that isn't an employee or arriving with authorization of someone inside.

> [!info] Social
> #### Do You Know Who I Am?
>
> Attempting to persuade or deceive the receptionist fails as they are infinitely more afraid of upsetting their boss ([[Larissa Toth]]) then letting someone in who isn't on their appointment list.
>
> Attempting to gain access to the offices is intentionally difficult, and not something characters will be able to do outside of certain events. Attempting to intimidate the receptionist will result in her calling security via the security button on her desk.
>
> #### Dress For The Job You Want
>
> If the party manages to [[Gameplay Details]] themselves as Silver Beam employees, they can walk right through the front doors and the receptionist won't spare them a second thought.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### What's That Do?
>
> Characters with a **Awareness (DC 10, Passive)** note there is a moderately sized red button on the desk. It's easily in reach of the receptionist, and is probably a button meant to summon security.
>
> #### Display Only?
>
> A pair of [[Silver Beam Servitor]] stand on the daises on either side of the desk. While they aren't armed, and don't seem to be active, they don't feel like they are just for display purposes.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Pushing Buttons
>
> If the party acts in a hostile, disruptive, or criminal way, the receptionist will ask the party to leave. If it's clear they won't, or the danger they present is too great, the receptionist will hit the red button to alert security.
>
> **See**: [[Gameplay Details]] in [[Gameplay Details]] for how this works.
