# Precipice

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> An unassuming wooden structure stands here, set just at the outer edge of the plaza and bearing a sign declaring this location to be:
>
> > Precipice
>
> A blackboard sign out front displaying the day's offerings and specials. The tantalizing scent of freshly cooked food hangs in the air here.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Operating Hours
>
> Precipice opens around midday and operates until well into the evening. It closes late at night after the dinner rush has ended.

Inside the eatery, a warm, intimate atmosphere awaits.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You are greeted by the quiet elegance of what may be Arcturel's nicest restaurant. Fine art hangs from the wall, as polished wood parquet flooring set against a border of gleaming brass grating spreads out underfoot. The intimate dining room features numerous small, linen-draped tables, each set for two and softly lit by a single burning candle.
>
> Through a broad archway beyond the dining area is a a long communal table set against a wall of tall windows that stretch from floor to ceiling and offering a breathtaking view out over the tradeway’s edge. This is the very view that gives Precipice its name.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Fine Dining
>
> Given the small size, individual tables only seat two guests, but the larger communal table can take larger groups, and for 8 SPcan be reserved for a larger group.
>
> Precipice's meals cost about 2 SP, and are served hot. Appetizes, Sides and Dessert all cost 1 SP. The menu is small, with only a handful of options, but they are varied enough to fit all diets. It takes about twenty minutes to be served, but the wait is always worth it as the quality of the food is always excellent.

Characters that get into the kitchen are greeted by a cramped, hectic space.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Precipice’s kitchen is cramped and busy, lacking all the elegance of the dining area. A messy prep area sits beside a cramped stove, and both are overseen by racks and shelves packed with ingredients destined for a plate.
