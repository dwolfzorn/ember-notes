# Briez Flywell's General Goods

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You see a sturdy structure of heavy wood covered in a patchwork of pale plaster. The exposed wood is dark and worn, betraying the age of the structure. The double doors are fitted with handles worn smooth by countless hands. A sign over the door bears the smiling mask of a Zeph and the name of the establishment:
>
> > Briez Flywell's General Goods

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Operating Hours
>
> Briez Flywell's opens mid-morning and is open into the evening. The doors are strongly secured outside of business hours, and require a successful **Stealth (DC 14)** check requiring [[Lockpicks]] to bypass.

Inside the structure is an eclectic assortment of goods, many imported from all over the region.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Stepping through the doors, you are greeted by immediate warmth and the mingled smells of leather, oiled steel, dried fruit, and parchment. The shop is packed at every turn. Shelves bow beneath books, boots, sacks of goods, helmets, and more. Racks and cases bristle with weapons of all kinds, and the walls are decorated with hanging items, all with prices.
>
> On the wall is a map of the world of Ember, and a portrait of a zeph. The placard on the painted claims this is Briez Flywell, renowned treasure hunter and entrepreneur. The map is marked with numerous places, all locations that Flywell has visited and procured items for sale.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Assorted Inventory
>
> Flywell's provides a little bit of everything, and most every kind of mundane equipment can be procured here without too much issue. In most cases the items are Arcturian in origin, but there are a high number of foreign-made versions of things, and a wide selection of books in different languages.

> [!info] Social
> #### Good Help
>
> Briez Flywell's is run by **Darvin Nolde** (Arcturian, he/him, 40s) who was hired by Flywell years ago. He has near absolute freedom to run this place as he sees fit.
>
> #### Shoot the Briez
>
> According to Darvin, **Briez Flywell** is a veteran member of the adventurer's guild in Ordain, an explorer, treasure hunter, and merchant. He comes and goes almost at random, and often sends back crates of items procured while on the road.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Trade** or **Knowledge: Artifacts** have likely heard the name, but have never met them personally.
