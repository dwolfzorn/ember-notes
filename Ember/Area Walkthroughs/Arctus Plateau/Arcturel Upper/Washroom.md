# Washroom

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You open the door to a small washroom lined with white marble. The space is bright and plain, its walls and floor spotlessly clean. The air carries the faint scent of cleaning chemicals.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Occupied!
>
> The door to this space can be locked from the inside, and can be opened with a successful **Stealth (DC 10)** check requiring [[Lockpicks]] .
