# Elevator

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A small metal plate on the wall reads:
>
> > Lift Platform — Upper Offices
>
> The heavy metal grates of the elevator mask the heavy machinery underneath. A raised control panel with a lever and lights someone to move the lift to other levels.

The Eastern Elevator is slightly different, as it has direct access to the conveyer line found in the [[Assembly Area]].

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Scuffs and scrapes on the grating underfoot betray the heavier traffic this lift has seen. An input sits on the wall here where pieces can be dumped. From there, they are rolled onto the conveyer belt to be sorted and assembled by the workers down the line.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Scale of Operations
>
> The elevators on this area map descend to the level below, contained within [[Arcturel Upper - Underbelly]] as part of the intended gameplay of this area and its related quests.
>
> However, narratively speaking, these elevators can access far more than just the two levels presented, and may hint at a larger Silver Beam operation "off-screen." They can be used to deliver reinforcements in combat, serve as an escape point for fleeing NPCs, and potentially access other levels of Arcturel if needed.
