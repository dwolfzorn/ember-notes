# Rallyhome

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This structure is made of sturdy blocks of golden stone that match the color and cut of the rest of the plaza. The façade is simple — a series of wide stone pieces forming an arch over modest wooden doors. The location's name is carved into that arch:
>
> > Rallyhome

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Operating Hours
>
> Rallyhome opens around midday and operates until well into late hours. It serves food until the evening, after that it's only whatever the bartender is willing to scrounge up.

Through the doors is the upper balcony area which provides a view of the lower bar, and a place to sit and enjoy the music below without being caught in the crowds.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Through the double doors you are met with the raucous din of Rallyhome filtering up through the open space just beyond the entryway's wide balcony.
>
> Several smaller tables and well-cushioned chairs are set about the perimeter of this balcony, providing a commanding view of the stage and tables below.
>
> A narrow stairwell in the far corner descends to the lower area. By the sound of things, all of the bar's life is contained down there.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Party Down Under
>
> The stairs lead down to the [[Lower Bar]] where drinks and food are sold.
