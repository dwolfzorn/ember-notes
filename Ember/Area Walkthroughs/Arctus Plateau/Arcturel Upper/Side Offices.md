# Side Offices

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Here a slender desk of brushed metal sits to one side of the office, opposite a pale, gray wooden bookstand against the wall. A sliding door exits this office, leading out to a private balcony that overlooks the lower reaches of Arcturel spread out below.

**Note:** this description applies to all three offices in this area.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### General Office Supplies
>
> For the curious, the desk and cabinet contain the following items:
>
> - A nice [[Pen]]
> - An [[Ink]]
> - Ample blank paper
> - Several [[Books]] with titles like: "Beg, Borrow & Steal", "Leveling Up Your Character" and "Lawful Ambitious: Systems of Success." Skimming the pages of these books, it's clear the content is not worth anyone's time.
>
> #### Silver Beam Schematics
>
> Characters who examine the westernmost Side Office will find a copy of the [[Silver Beam Schematics]].

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gathering Evidence: Silver Beam Schematics
>
> Important evidence can be gathered here that can be used in support of Hew the Renegade Construct's exoneration in [[Glitter in the Dark]].
>
> If the characters loot the Silver Beam Schematics, record the appropriate Event Outcome. This physical clue plays a major role in the forthcoming [[Presenting the Evidence]] event.
