# Glimmer Lounge

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You find yourself in a modest lounge awash in deep red tones, from the crimson wallpaper to the finely pattered burgundy carpet underfoot, to the richly upholstered seating all around. Even the candles cast a gentle, rosy light into this space.
>
> A small wet bar occupies one corner, its polished wood counter set with a few waiting drinks while its shelves hold a small trove of gleaming decanters, goblets, and jars of dried fruit and everything else necessary to offer luxurious hospitality to those who visit here.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Crimson Club
>
> During evening hours the Glimmer Lounge is open to all who know to come by. Since the upstairs store is often locked up, the only way in is through the door on this level.
>
> Guests are welcome to mingle, explore the space, purchase drinks (3sp each), and even browse the art and gem displays upstairs in case they want to make an evening purchase.
>
> The club is open a few hours after midnight, after that guests are ushered out and the building is locked up.

Characters that explore the side doors will find the balconies.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> At either end of the lounge, sliding doors lead out to balconies. One holds a commanding view of the lower reaches of Arcturel and the sinkhole below, while the other is private, and decorated with vibrant potted plants.
