# The Storage Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A pair of refugees stands in the center of the storage room, arguing in harsh whispers other over several open sacks of grain, dried fruit, and stale bread. One of them clutches a half-filled canvas bag to his chest, while the other stands between him and the door with both arms spread wide.
>
> > Keffie, you idiot, we need to ration these — we can't just take whatever we want, whenever we want. These stores need to last the whole camp!
>
> As the two men notice your presence, their faces go white. The man drops his arms and turns to face you.
>
> > It's not what it looks like! I'm trying to talk sense into my friend here. Please — **please** don't tell Sigil.

The two men are **Merrow** (Neutral Good, Arcturian Human, he/him) and **Keffie** (Neutral Evil, Arcturian Human, he/him).

> [!info] Social
> #### The Squabbling Farmers
>
> Merrow and Keffie have been traveling together since the burning of Helkas. Fearing that the influx of refugees will attract bandits, the two farmers are planning on leaving Oldcraft Lodge at dawn. Keffie wishes to take an "extra share" of food for themselves, while Merrow believes that taking extra supplies will endanger the refugees who remain behind.
>
> The characters can resolve the argument in one of the following ways:
>
> - **Appeal to Fairness.** Any character who appeals to Keffie's sense of fairness and makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 18)** check can talk him out of stealing the supplies.
> - **A Fair Trade.** Keffie isn't interested in gold ("Gold ain't edible"), but the characters can convince Keffie to give up the stolen supplies by trading their own food.
> - **Intimidation Tactics.** Any character who threatens Keffie and makes a successful **Intimidation (DC 18)** check can force Keffie to give up the stolen supplies.
>
> If all else fails, [[Sigil]] or [[Svala Bronwen]] can be alerted to the theft, resulting in the farmers' immediate removal from Oldcraft Lodge.
>
> #### A Strange Reward
>
> If the characters peacefully resolve the dispute without involving Sigil or Svala, Keffie storms out of the lodge, leaving Merrow behind. He thanks the characters with a [[Suspiciously Large Carrot]].
>
> > Don't look at me like that. It just grew that way.
