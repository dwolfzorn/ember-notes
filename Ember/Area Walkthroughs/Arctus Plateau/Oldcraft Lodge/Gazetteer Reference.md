# Gazetteer Reference

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A rustic single-story lodge sits on the banks of Lake Jinro, its grounds marked by the sweet scent of ripe berries and wild irises. While at first glance, its warm wood and lush grounds seem no different than those found at any other Arcturian waterfront home, the iridescent blue of its roof shifts colors ever so slightly each time as the light moves across it, and the faint sound of turning pages whispers on the breeze.

Oldcraft Lodge has long served as the home and library of Sigil, a Shard God committed to the pursuit of knowledge. Though it appears as a modest residence from the outside, it is rumored to contain a vast arcane library whose full extent only Sigil himself is truly aware of. Most visitors to Oldcraft Lodge never come close to seeing its entire interior. They typically work either in the communal spaces provided by Sigil or grounds outside.

Only those who the Shard God Sigil mentors and trusts are allowed beyond the common areas of Oldcraft Lodge — as a result, the true size of its interior is unknown. Even the lodge's public spaces are hard to fully measure; they always appear to have just enough room for whoever is visiting, as if they are expanding and contracting to match the current number of guests.

While no one has been able to observe these changes when they occur, several researchers have confirmed differences in room sizes, orientations, and entrances over time. Some believe that these shifts are the result of the slight draft that runs through the halls, which has no clear origin point and has been known to blow papers across the room, scattering them in patterns that somehow reveal new insights to the scholars working on them.

## Environment

The rooms of Oldcraft Lodge are lit by a soft warm light that resembles candlelight, but comes from no particular direction, as if the air itself was aglow. A slight breeze often drifts through the lodge with the sound of rustled pages and the faint smell of bound leather – some joke that it is the collected air from the sighs that Sigil sometimes lapses into while working, but no one knows its true origins.

## Ecology

It is rumored that there is a greenhouse somewhere within the hidden arcane library, used for the study of herbalism and crafting, but no one has ever found it. Outside of this mythical room, Oldcraft Lodge is kept clear of anything that might threaten the books and knowledge inside of it, including insects and rodents.

## Lore

Oldcraft Lodge was originally built for a trader, Jarith Imeron, as a second home outside of Ordain, but after their death it fell into disrepair. Sigil discovered the abandoned home while on a search for Jarith’s journals, which document some of the early days of Ordain, and remade it into the place it is today, using some of the power he holds as a Shard God to imbue the home with enough magic to hold more space and books than it otherwise would be able to.
