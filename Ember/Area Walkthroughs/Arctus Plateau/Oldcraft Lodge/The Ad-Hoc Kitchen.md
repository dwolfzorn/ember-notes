# The Ad-Hoc Kitchen

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A small kitchen near the front entrance of the lodge looks as if it were recently the scene of a great feast, though a closer look reveals that the food is mostly small items that carry well on the road: pieces of fruit, handheld sandwiches, and bundles of supplies that refugees can take with them on a first-come, first-served basis.

There is little of interest in this room apart from some leftover food on the counters and tables. If the party is lacking rations or supplies, they can gather 3 [[Rations]].
