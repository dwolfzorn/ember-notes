# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The [[Oldcraft Lodge]] Area Map depicts the entirety of the lodge. It is featured in the [[A Friendly Reunion]] Event of the [[An Old Friend]] Main Quest.
>
> - The map depicts a single Level, and its areas are organized into two sections: Exterior and Interior.
> - The party arrives on the [[Entrance Path]].

## Gameplay Details

The areas of Oldcraft Lodge have the following features unless specified otherwise in the text.

### Levels & Elevation

The Oldcraft Lodge Scene features a single Level, and its areas are organized into two sections: Exterior and Interior.

- **Exterior:** The Exterior is set to an elevation of 0.
- **Interior:** The Interior is set to an elevation of 0 and has 40-foot-high ceilings.

### Illumination

The interior of Oldcraft Lodge is illuminated by candle and lantern light, which radiates bright light for 15 feet and dim light for an additional 15 feet. Outside areas are subject to exterior lighting and associated weather conditions.

### Terrain

All areas should be treated as normal terrain.

### Inhabitants

The following characters and creatures inhabit the area:

- [[Svala Bronwen]]
- 12 [[Arcturian]] refugees
- [[Sigil]]

### Enemies

During the [[A Friendly Reunion]] Event, the following enemies are encountered here:

- 6 [[Light Mote]]

### The Mote Chase

During the [[A Friendly Reunion]] Event, the characters will engage in [[The Mote Chase]].
