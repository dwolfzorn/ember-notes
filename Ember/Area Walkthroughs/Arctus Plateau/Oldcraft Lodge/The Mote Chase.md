# The Mote Chase

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you step outside the lodge, you can see several motes of light floating nearby, weaving between tents, hovering over the campfires, and fluttering over the refugees' heads. For the most part, no one seems to be reacting to them, and some of the refugees bat them away with free hands as if they are bothersome flies.

> [!abstract] Light Mote
> **[[Light Mote]]**
>
> Level 0.25 (Minion) · Light Elemental Elemental Mote
>
> ![Light Mote](../../../assets/image094.webp)
>
> This tiny elemental is a tiny coalesced amount of light that floats slowly through the air. They shine with an inner luminescence that gives them an almost ethereal appearance, and they are constantly in motion.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Chasing the Light Motes
>
> The newly spawned Light Motes are not aggressive toward the party, Lyla, or any of the refugees. Before the characters give chase, Lyla will say the following:
>
> > You don't need to hurt them if you don't want to! Just try to soothe them, and they will disappear!
>
> Initiative begins, and Lyla joins the chase to disperse the motes. She will not actively harm them and instead attempts to use the peaceful approach detailed below.
>
> - **The Aggressive Approach:** A character can make a melee or ranged spell attack against the mote. The motes only have 3 health; the characters don't need to roll for damage. Once hit, a mote simply "shatters" into harmless light.
> - **The Peaceful Approach:** A character can attempt an **Arcana (DC 14)** or **Wilderness (DC 14)** check to calm a mote. On a success, they trill with a soft tune and disappear. On a failure, use their [[Unknown]] to reposition to a different location. This can include over the nearby lake. Place them in the most difficult locations the party can reasonably reach.
