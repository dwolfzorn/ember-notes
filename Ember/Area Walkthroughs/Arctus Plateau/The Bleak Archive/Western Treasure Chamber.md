# Western Treasure Chamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Two central pedestals define this small chamber. A beaded necklace sits upon one of them, while the other bears a circlet made of bright silver. Several ancient earthen vases are also stored here, along with an ironclad chest containing a bounty of lustrous coins. The air here is remarkably cold and uncomfortably quiet.

In this area, the characters may discover a handful of relics from the [[Age of the Tower]], which have been stowed away behind arcane lock and key.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Abyssal Adornments
>
> The iron-bound chest here contains **100**worth of archaic Primordial coins.
>
> Two wondrous items of Abyssal origin sit atop the stone pedestals in this chamber:
>
> - [[Darkflame Circlet]], a cursed circlet that allows its wearer to cast a necrotic ray.
> - [[Darkstride Amulet]], a cursed amulet that hides its wearer against divination magic.
