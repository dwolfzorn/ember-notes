# Darkroom

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This junction is adorned with another set of the vault's revolving mirrors. Swaddled in a tattered cloak, the skeletal remains of some long-dead humanoid appear seated upon the ground against the chamber's central column in a pose of ancient abandon.

The Darkroom serves as a junction for the Bleak Archive's complex system of mirrors, and is relatively sparse — save for the presence of a magical Darkness trap installed to ward off trespassers, and a single [[Corrupted Cadrithor]] that rises from the skeletal corpse of a [[Shent]] sage. In this area, the characters may operate several mirrors and doors necessary access the [[Unknown]].

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Magical Darkness Trap
>
> Whenever at least 10 pounds of weight are applied to a pressure plate at the southern end of the Darkroom chamber, a [[Engulfing Darkness]] Spell is cast on the area. The duration for this effect is 1 hour, after which time the trap resets.
>
> - Any character with **Awareness (DC 13, Passive)** and who is close enough to clearly see the pressure plate can notice it.
> - This effect is subdued for 1 hour after becoming the target of the **Counterspell (Oblivion, Pulse, Push, DC 16)**.
>
> Once the trap has been triggered, any [[Corrupted Cadrithor]] within 60 feet sense the magical darkness and are immediately drawn towards it. See [[Unknown]] for information on Cadrithor tactics.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Arcane shadow fills the chamber, but you manage to catch the briefest of glimpses of some strange spectral vapor rising from the skeletal remains. As night surrounds you, you hear the telltale growl of some canine creature in the darkness and the ominous scraping of claws upon stone growing ever closer.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Skeletal Remains
>
> The aged skeleton that sits on the floor near the center of the chamber is a long-dead Shent scholar who presided over this place as a journeyman trapsmith.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Medicine (DC 13)** check recognizes the tattered robes on the skeleton as Shent in origin.
>
> Any character who searches the skeleton finds an ancient set of [[Lockpicks]] and a [[Shent Signet Ring]].

Once they solve the puzzle of the Darkroom's mirrors, the party can travel to the [[Unknown]] to the southwest, completing half of the mirror puzzle required to breach the [[Unknown]].

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Progressing the Beam
>
> In order to correctly advance the beam of light from the mirror puzzle (described in [[Mirrors & Walls]]) through this section of the dungeon, the [[The Bleak Archive]] must be deactivated or disabled, since its magical darkness counteracts the beam of light. Then the beam must be routed through five of the six mirrors in the chamber.
>
> #### Accessing the Inner Antechamber
>
> The [[Unknown]] and the vault beyond can be accessed by engaging a lever on the wall of the hallway to the southwest. This causes a large section of wall to descend that makes travel within the western half of the Archive less restricted.
