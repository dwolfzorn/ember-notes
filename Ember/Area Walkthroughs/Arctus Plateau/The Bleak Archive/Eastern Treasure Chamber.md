# Eastern Treasure Chamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small chamber houses two central pedestals, upon which rest two large and musty tomes bound in dark leather. A variety of ancient earthen vases are also stored here, along with an ironclad chest bearing a wealth of lustrous coins.

In this area, the characters may discover a handful of relics from the [[Age of the Tower]], which have been stowed away behind arcane lock and key.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Literature of the Ancients
>
> The iron-bound chest here contains  **100** worth of archaic [[Shent]] coins.
>
> The two tomes that sit atop the pair of stone pedestals in this chamber are notable treasures in their own right:
>
> - [[Sage's Grimoire]], a collection of various spells used by Shent sages to thwart the Abyss.
> - [[Tome of Eldritch Symbols]], a cursed volume of ancient eldritch lore pertaining to The Abyss.
