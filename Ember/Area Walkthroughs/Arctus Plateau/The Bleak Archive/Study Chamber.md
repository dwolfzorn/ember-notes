# Study Chamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Bookcases jam-packed with dusty scrolls and moldering tomes line the walls of this small two-room chamber, which appears to be an ancient study. Several books are strewn across the floor, and the faint smell of incense accompanies the stale scent of musty parchment and worm-eaten pages.

In this area, the characters can discover a small repository of ancient books and scrolls. They must survive combat with a malevolent Horrendor, whose corpse has lurked here for time immemorial.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Retractable Wall
>
> The Study Chamber is only accessible via a retractable wall. The corresponding lever to this retractable wall is located in the shallow 5-foot alcove just to the west, outside the Study Chamber itself.

A Horrendor lurks here, having risen from the long-dead corpse of a medium-sized [[Shent]] scholar.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### A Ghoulish Surprise
>
> The Horrendor will play dead until one of the characters moves close enough for a melee attack. Any character with **Awareness (DC 13)** notices a subtle clue that the corpse may not be inanimate.
>
> #### Horrendor Tactics
>
> Once in range, the Horrendor will attack gleefully and without remorse using a combination of [[Raking Talons]], [[Necrotic Bite]], and a series of mocking efforts at [[Intimidator]] emphasized through its [[Horrific Critical]].
>
> As a necromantic being, the Horrendor readily fights to the death. However, as one of the [[Restless Dead]], it is difficult to permanently defeat.
>
> #### Corrupted Cadrithor Tactics
>
> If the confrontation with the Horrendor makes audible noise, which is likely as it steadily berates them with [[Diversionist]], it may attract a [[Corrupted Cadrithor]] which joins the fray from the hallway to the southeast. The Corrupted Cadrithor uses similar tactics to those described in [[Corrupted Guardians]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Undead Scholar
>
> Any character who searches the Horrendor remains is able to find a [[Scrivener's Portable Workbench]] and a [[Shent Signet Ring]].
>
> #### The Forgotten Library
>
> The walls here are lined with dusty shelves full of moth-eaten scrolls and moldering books. Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check while examining the shelves is able to locate the following items of interest amongst the detritus:
>
> |  | #### Study Chamber Loot |
> | --- | --- |
> | 1 | #### Arcane Relic  An [[Arcane Orb]] |
> | 2 | #### Ancient Libram  [[Regarding Ebbok Zhùr]], an archaic book written by a Shent artificer named Vadrus Vereth that describes the early history of the Bleak Archive and its mission. |
> | 3 | #### Dusty Tome  [[The Dirgewood Papers]], a collection of Inner Realms lore detailing an eponymous stretch of Primordis that has been notoriously tainted by Abyssal scourge. |
> | 4 | #### Silver Tube  a silver tube perhaps intended to be used as a document case, hiding a collection of loose coins totalling  **6 4 6**, and a small glass phial that appears to be a [[Omniglot Decoction]] |

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Progressing the Beam
>
> In order to progress the light beam from the mirror puzzle (described in [[Mirrors & Walls]]) through this section of the dungeon, it must be routed through all four mirrors of the Study Chamber, ending in the mirror to the southwest of the chamber's entrance.
>
> #### Accessing the Inner Antechamber
>
> The [[Unknown]] and the [[Unknown]] beyond can be acceesed by engaging a lever on the wall of the hallway to the southeast. This causes a large section of wall to descend that makes travel within the eastern half of the Archive less restricted.
