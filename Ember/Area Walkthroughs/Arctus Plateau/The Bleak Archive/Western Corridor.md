# Western Corridor

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A slight current of air flows southward down the brief length of the short corridor here, and you spot a small aperture in the stone of the western wall, situated twenty feet in to the north.

An unassuming hallway wraps around towards the southern [[Unknown]] where the party may advance the light beam puzzle and locate a secret door that provides access to the [[Unknown]] and its network of mirrors.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Spinning Blade Trap
>
> The pressure plate which activates this trap is located at the south end of a short hallway — refer to the **West Blade Trap Trigger** Scene Region.
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 13, Passive)** and who is close enough to clearly see the pressure plate notices both it and the wall seams from which the spinning blades would issue.
>
> The plate can be disarmed with a successful **Stealth (DC 15)** check using [[Lockpicks]] .
>
> The Spinning Blade Trap is triggered when 10 or more pounds of weight are applied to the pressure plate. When the trap is triggered, several whirling blades erupt from the nearby wall and sweep through the hallway.
>
> - Any character who is within the area of the trap immediately or at the start of a subsequent turn faces **Hazard 6 (Fortitude, Health, Slashing)**.

A secret door into the [[Reflector Array]] is located to the west of the Spinning Blade Trap can only be opened and closed using the inconspicuous lever to the southeast.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Reflector Array Door
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 13, Passive)** detects the seams of the secret door itself.
>
> Once discovered, any character who makes a successful **Science (DC 13)** check can determine the mechanical nature of the secret door and confirm the presence of a remote lever.
>
> Finally, any character with **Awareness (DC 12, Passive)** detects the lever while exploring the Western Corridor.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Progressing the Beam
>
> In order to progress the light beam from the mirror puzzle (described in [[Mirrors & Walls]]) through this section of the dungeon, it must be first routed southwest through a small gap in the wall into the[[Unknown]], then channeled around to this hallway where it passes through another gap into the [[Unknown]].
