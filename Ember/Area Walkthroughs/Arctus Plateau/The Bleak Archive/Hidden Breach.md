# Hidden Breach

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A small alcove situated above the narrow ledge here is doused in shadow and overgrown with ash-colored vine, making it difficult to see what, if anything, is inside.

This Hidden Breach provides an alternate entrance to the Bleak Archive via the [[Unknown]] on the other side. The cavernous ravine to the east of the front gate presents a considerable hazard for characters who draw too closely to its edge without care.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Navigating the Ravine
>
> Characters who linger for 5 minutes or more at the ravine's edge must succeed on a **Athletics (DC 12)** to avoid being shoved off the precipice by an intermittent gust of strong mountain wind.
>
> Any character who falls while standing here or attempting to cross the ravine suffers a **Minor Fall (Hazard 6, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)** as they land on another stony ledge in the mist below.
>
> A character who falls here must make a successful **Athletics (DC 13)** check to climb back up to the highest ledge without assistance. A grappling hook attached to a length of rope at least 20 feet long provides **+2 Boons** on this check.

## Into the Breach

The Hidden Breach is concealed by a bramble of ash-colored vine atop a narrow ledge of natural stone, east of the Archive's front doors. If characters can manage to safely reach the Breach from the Mist-Veiled Approach, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Drawing closer to the stony alcove, you can't help but notice a dull, malodorous scent wafting from within; and with it, the somnolent whisper of a soft and steady current of air.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Webbing & Whispers
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 13, Passive)** notices a blanket of cobwebs lining the far end of the small tunnel that comprises the Hidden Breach, and identifies the malodorous scent of carrion rot.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The character also hears a faint scraping sound.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### A Tight Squeeze
>
> Characters who attempt to traverse the Hidden Breach must succeed on either a **Athletics (DC 14)** check or suffer **Scrapes and Abrasions (Hazard 2, Fortitude, Health, Slashing)** caused by protruding roots and rocks.
