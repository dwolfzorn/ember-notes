# Inner Antechamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The mechanical walls descend to reveal a wide room containing two mirrors and little else — save the shadow-ensorcelled forms of a pair of tenebrous dusk hounds. The luminous purple eyes of these creatures regard you with malice as they bare their hideously prodigious fangs.

This oblong room serves as an antechamber for the [[Unknown]] of the Bleak Archive. In this area, the characters must survive an encounter with a duo of Corrupted Cadrithors before they can complete the puzzle and make their way into the True Vault.

> [!abstract] Corrupted Cadrithor
> **[[Corrupted Cadrithor]]**
>

> You behold a quadruped creature made of pure shadow. The curves of its tenebrous form unmistakably resemble the grotesque skeleton of some vaguely canine monstrosity, shrouded in a black and purple cloak of abyssal fire. It's cruel eyes shine with a ghostly radiance, and its otherworldly growl reeks of grave nitre.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### The Watchdogs of Tethra Shùl
>
> A pair of [[Corrupted Cadrithor]] stand watch here, and will engage the characters as soon as they reach the Inner Antechamber or adjacent areas.
>
> The 2 Corrupted Cadrithor follow the tactics described in [[Corrupted Guardians]] and fight to the death.

## Unsealing the True Vault

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Imminent Danger
>
> Direct confrontation with [[Tethra Shùl]], who awaits the party within the True Vault, is especially dangerous for low-level characters.
>
> If the party is only Level 2 at this point or not at full strength and resources, it is strongly suggested to remind them that a source of true evil is likely to be encountered beyond these doors. The party may wish to further prepare themselves or seek help in facing this deadly foe.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Directing the Beams
>
> The True Vault is only accessible via a retractable wall in this room. The key to lowering this retractable wall is the completion of the mirror array puzzle (described in [[Mirrors & Walls]]) that begins in the [[Unknown]]. The two beams of arcane light from that room must be redirected throughout the dungeon to finally arrive in the crystal receptacles located here in the Inner Antechamber, described below.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Taking a closer look at the northern wall, you notice that the central forty-foot section is bookended by two seams that run the vertical length of the wall. The two crystal sconces on each side of this curious threshold all but confirm the presence of another retractable partition here. The northern wall itself feels cold to the touch, and you can almost hear a faint whisper in the back of your mind as you draw near to it.

The final vault door opens only in response to the completion of the puzzle, connecting both beams of light to their respective receptors on either side of the door.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As the two beams of bright light strike the crystals on the northern wall, the entire inner antechamber begins to tremble as the mechanism of one final retractable door shudders to life. The northern wall slowly retreats into the floor, revealing a secret vault on the other side. The large form of some gloom-shrouded creature lurks with intense preoccupation at the center of this darkened chamber.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Locked Mirrors
>
> Once the True Vault door is open, all mirrors throughout the Bleak Archive become locked in position and none can be rotated further unless the entire apparatus is reset using the original levers in the [[Unknown]].

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### "A Brush With Death" Quest Progression: True Vault Opened
>
> When the party unlocks the door to the True Vault, they progress one of their Attunements — the exact Attunement progressed depends on the method by which the party opened the door. See further details in the [[Where Evil Lurks]] section of [[Where Evil Lurks]] inside the [[A Brush With Death]] Quest.
