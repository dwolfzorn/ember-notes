# Eastern Corridor

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A subtle current of air stirs southward down the length of this dusty corridor. A mirror decorates each end of the short hallway, while a third adorns an alcove to the east.

This long hallway east of the [[Unknown]] is trapped with nefarious blades and a sequence of platforms suspended above a deep pit.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Spinning Blade Trap
>
> The pressure plate which activates this trap is at the north end of the hallway — refer to the **East Blade Trap Trigger** Scene Region. Activation of the trap is likely to separate characters on either side of the hazard.
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 13, Passive)** and who is close enough to clearly see the pressure plate notices both it and the wall seams from which the spinning blades would issue.
>
> The plate can be disarmed with a successful **Stealth (DC 15)** check using [[Lockpicks]] .
>
> The Spinning Blade Trap is triggered when 10 or more pounds of weight are applied to the pressure plate. When the trap is triggered, several whirling blades erupt from the nearby wall and sweep through the hallway.
>
> - Any character who is within the area of the trap immediately or at the start of a subsequent turn faces **Hazard 6 (Fortitude, Health, Slashing)**.

After surmounting the hazard of the blade trap, the party must cross a deep pit using small metal platforms in alcoves on either side, circling around to a triangular area with three mirrors just before the entrance to the [[Unknown]].

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Progressing the Beam
>
> In order to progress the light beam from the mirror puzzle (described in [[Mirrors & Walls]]) through this section of the dungeon, the beam must be routed to the southeastern mirror of these three from which it is directed north through a small gap in the stonework.
