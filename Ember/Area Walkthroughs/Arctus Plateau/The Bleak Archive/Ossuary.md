# Ossuary

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Countless humanoid skulls of various sizes adorn the cluttered alcoves of this grim ossuary, and an assortment of bones both large and small lies scattered across the stone floor. If you didn't know better, you'd swear the pitch black sockets of those skulls were staring right back at you …

In this area, the party will encounter the Archive's ossuary, where the bones of countless Abyssal heretics have been collected for safe keeping. The party must manipulate the mirrors to advance the beam of light while braving the psychic hazards of a ghostly miasma that haunts the bones here.

## The Haunted Charnel House

The skeletal remains here consist of the scattered bones of unnamed Abyssal heretics.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Heretical Remains
>
> Any character who disturbs the bones here suffers **Ghostly Miasma (Hazard 8, Willpower, Morale, Void)**.

The remains are horribly decomposed and mostly crumbling into bone dust, however the ossuary compartments do contain some forgotten baubles.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Searching the Remains
>
> There are ample bones here which could be used as Spell Components for arcana which require such.
>
> Each character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check while searching the bones finds one of the following:
>
> |  | #### Ossuary Loot |
> | --- | --- |
> | 1 | #### Collection of Ancient Coins  Assorted ancient coins with a total value of  **2 5 6** |
> | 2 | [[Platinum Tooth]] |
> | 3 | [[Scrimshaw Dagger]] |
> | 4 | [[Heretic's Skull]] |

Spellcasters may endeavor to communicate with the long-deceased remains.

> [!info] Social
> #### Speaking with the Dead
>
> Characters who use [[Unknown]] to communicate with the assorted skeletons can learn the following information:
>
> - Assorted tales of pre-Shattering Abyssal incursions.
> - Misleading hints about what lurks in the Archive's [[Unknown]].
> - Cryptic revelations about the influence of [[Nethehepticas]] and other Abyssal Horrors.

## Out of the Shadows

A [[Corrupted Cadrithor]] lurks unseen within the shadows of the Ossuary's crowded passages, ready to strike once the characters are occupied with the [[The Bleak Archive]].

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A terrible canine growl fills the bone-strewn chamber, and the semi-spectral apparition of a hound made of pure shadow rushes forth from the dusky gloom.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Watchdog of Tethra Shùl
>
> Once the characters are occupied with inspecting the remains in this chamber or manipulating the rotating mirrors, a [[Corrupted Cadrithor]] emerges from the ossuary walls using its [[Amorphous]] feature.
>
> #### Corrupted Cadrithor Tactics
>
> The Corrupted Cadrithor follows the tactics described in [[Corrupted Guardians]], and fights to the death.
