# Reflector Array

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A half-dozen platforms of grated metal here are suspended high above what appears to be a bottomless pit. The air below is cold, and carries with it the hollow sound of fathomless subterranea.

This chamber serves as a crucial hub for the system of rotating mirrors that unlock the Bleak Archive's [[Unknown]]. Characters must navigate the grated metallic platforms as they rotate the mirrors to their proper alignments.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### The Fear-Shrouded Pit
>
> The pit in this room is 40 feet deep, but so shrouded in darkness that it appears to be bottomless.
>
> Any character who falls into the pit suffers a **Great Fall (Hazard 12, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)** and must also face [[/hazard 4 willpower morale void]{Abyssal Whispers}, becoming **Frightened** for `[[/roll 1d4]]` hours.

The northern exit from the reflector array is concealed and controlled by an inconspicuous lever on the east side of the northern wall.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Secret Door
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 13, Passive)** detects the seams of the secret door itself.
>
> Once discovered, any character who makes a successful **Science (DC 13)** check can determine the mechanical nature of the secret door and confirm the presence of a remote lever.
>
> Finally, any character with **Awareness (DC 12, Passive)** detects the lever while exploring the Reflector Array.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Progressing the Beam
>
> In order to progress the light beam from the mirror puzzle (described in [[Mirrors & Walls]]) through this section of the dungeon, it must be routed around the array using all seven mirrors, ending in the top-center mirror that directs the light northwards towards the Study Chamber.
