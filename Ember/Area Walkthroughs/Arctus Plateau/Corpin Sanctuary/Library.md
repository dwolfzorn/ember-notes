# Library

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The earthy smells of parchment and paper assail your senses as you enter this cozy library, which is furnished with multiple shelves stocked with a variety of books and tomes.

This chamber serves as Corpin Sanctuary's public library, although visits from outsiders are rather infrequent. The three shelves on the eastern wall contain an array of books about Arcturian history, while the free-standing shelves to the northwest are lined with a variety of religious texts. Meanwhile, the smaller shelf in the southwest corner of the room contains a more diverse selection of texts, including several works of modern fiction.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Library
>
> Characters who take a moment to inspect the tomes spread out on the chamber's reading table notice:
>
> - The smaller book is the Library Journal, which contains a log of texts that have been checked-out or reserved from the library. The Library Journal is a red herring, and contains no clues related to the [[Corpin Investigation]] event.
> - The larger book is titled "The Druid's Almanac", and is opened to a page in a chapter that provides detailed advice for the cultivation of many of the plants currently found on the Sanctuary grounds. Any character who studies this book during a Recovery or Rest gains **+2 Boons** on their next Wilderness check.
>
> Any character who is familiar with [[Evesso's Note]] who also makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check while searching the room is able to locate a curious tome on the shelf titled [[Nineteen Nights in Haxim]], which contains the passphrase needed to open the Secret Door to the [[Profane Altar]].
>
> - [[Detect Magic]]: Any character who casts the Spell automatically succeeds and is drawn to "Nineteen Nights of Haxim," which radiates a faint aura of Abjuration magic.
> - **Knowledge: Artifacts**: The character gains **+2 Boons**.
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons**.
