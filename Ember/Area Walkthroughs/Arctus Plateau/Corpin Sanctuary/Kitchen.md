# Kitchen

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The embers of the hearthfire in this kitchen burn low, but the inviting scents of sweet and savory foods are as fresh as the loaf of bread that cools on the countertop.

This wide chamber hosts the Sanctuary's kitchen facilities, and is laid out in a rather professional manner despite the rustic, amateur approach to cuisine adopted by the Sages here. However, the lack of culinary ceremony does very little to undermine the deliciousness of the food that is regularly prepared here.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Kitchen
>
> Any character who takes a moment to search the kitchen is able to find:
>
> - Multiple [[Mess Kits]]
> - Several sets of [[Cook's Utensils]].
> - 2 days worth of Rations per character.
