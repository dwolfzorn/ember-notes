# Sanctorum Entrance

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You behold an exalted set of double doors decorated with a bright mosaic that depicts a wizened femme sage clad in the robes of a Cindaric. A great city of white stone stands proud behind this figure, who holds her arms out in dutiful supplication to a throng of needy and hopeful Arcturians in her attendance.
>
> An array of what appear to be holy offerings are scattered on the stone footsteps here — including rare flowers, copper coins, and curios crafted from natural materials.

The doors here at the grand entrance to the Sanctuary's largest chamber depict the first Holy Speaker, Adonia — the founder of Ordain and the Cindaric Sages themselves.

Meant to inspire and embolden those who look upon them, these doors usually stand closed and attended by a Cindaric steward stationed on the other side (in the Sanctorum proper); and the inspiration this place provides is evident in the countless windswept offerings that have been gathered at the footsteps below Adonia's feet.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Entrance
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Society (DC 14)** check recognizes the figure in the mosaic as Adonia, the first Holy Speaker and founder of the Cindaric order.
>
> The character is also convinced that leaving an offering here on the footsteps is a worthy endeavor, based on tales of Cindaric blessings afforded to those who pay proper respects.
>
> - **Path: Cindaric Initiate**: The character gains **+2 Boons**.
>
> Characters who take a moment to leave an offering of their own on the footsteps of the Sanctorum Entrance gain **+2 Boons** on their next roll against any spell or effect that would cause the **Frightened** condition.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Security Measures
>
> In times of trouble, the Cindaric Sage that happens to be on duty as steward within the Sanctorum will barricade the [[Sanctorum Entrance]] with a crossbeam of sturdy oak reinforced with steel. Breaking the door requires a series of 2 successful **Athletics (DC 24)** checks . Once the Entrance has been barricaded, the Sage will alert the other denizens of the Sanctuary using a special [[Horn]] hung on a beam nearby.
