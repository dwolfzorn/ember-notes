# Gazetteer Reference

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A small complex of structures sits nestled in quiet isolation on the lower slopes of Mial Mountain, with a commanding view of the lands below and only the sound of empty skies around it. An aura of serenity blankets this remote location, where the harsh winds of the mountain slopes seemingly cannot reach.

This remote facility is home to an isolated enclave of Cindaric Sages, who — removed from the distraction of a bustling city and the chaos is brings — are able to cultivate their connection to the Heart of Ember in relative peace. Life at Corpin Sanctuary is simple and rustic, lacking most of the creature comforts of Ordain; yet, in these areas where the Sanctuary is lacking, one can instead find a serene and direct connection to the land itself. For some, this is all that's required.

Corpin Sanctuary features a quartet of connected buildings stacked upon the stony mountainside, the largest of which is a central rotunda marked by a massive cupola crowned with a sharp spire. Ruddy tiles cover the roofs of each edifice, with two chambers boasting open-air canopies — including the Entry Courtyard and the Meditation Pavillion. The structures here are all constructed from wood and a creamy white stone similar to the rock quarried in Ordain, although none of it bears the obvious hallmarks of magical reformation.

### Environment

Built on rocky cliffs, access to the sanctuary involves traversing a large number of steps or specially built walkways. The location has a high vantage point with a stunning view of the surrounding land, but is perpetually struck by winds that make the outer areas especially blustery.

### Ecology

The verdant gardens and foliage that decorate the interiors and exteriors of Corpin Sanctuary appear somewhat out of place amidst the stony mountainside terrain here; but those familiar with the Cindaric Sages and their connection to the Heart of Ember are unsurprised by the seemingly effortless proliferation of these otherwise dislocated plants, which include pink-petaled blush blossom tree and ample flowerbeds lined with an array of fruits, vegetables, herbs, and florets. Fauna here is somewhat sparse, with wild birds and rodents comprising the majority of wildlife. Meanwhile, domesticated animals like rask and birbs are always on hand, serving as faithful companions to the Sages who foster them.

### Lore

Corpin Sanctuary was established in 2185 AS by an intrepid group of Ordani sages who sought a simpler place to practice their arts. Shortly after the Shard God Sigil's arrival in Ordain, a small enclave of Cindarics led by a sage named Janas Theed set out to find a new place of worship — one removed from the metropolitan clamor of city life. Their meditations eventually led them to the slopes of Mial Mountain, where careful construction of the Sanctuary took place over the course of two short years. In the decades that followed, Corpin Sanctuary steadily gained a favorable reputation as a safe haven for the Arctus Plateau's most lost and lonely citizens. Contact between Corpin Sanctuary and the Cindarin Temple in Ordain is constant, and sages are known to serve their faith for ample periods of time at both locations (based on the immediate needs of the Arcturian people and of the Order itself).
