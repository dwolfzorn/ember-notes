# Washroom

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This trapezoidal chamber is fitted with wash basins, a wooden tub, and two latrines divided by folding partitions. A mirror in the corner and an array of towels and other textiles all but confirm that this is the Sanctuary's communal washroom.

This washroom provides the Sages and visitors of Corpin Sanctuary with a semi-private place for excretory movements and matters of personal hygiene. While there is no running water, a well nearby and the druidic magic of the Cindarics are the reliable methods used to supply the washroom with fresh water daily (or when otherwise necessary).

If a character idles here long enough, they'll likely encounter at least one [[Cindaric Adherent]] (or another NPC) on their way to or from the [[Dormitories]] nearby.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Keeping it Clean
>
> Custodial duties for the washroom are assigned to each of the Cindaric Sages on a weekly basis, with each sage being responsible for cleaning duties roughly one day a week. Waste is disposed of via a refuse pit concealed beneath one of the floor's central tiles, and is often recycled by the Cindaric Sages for use as compost in the Sanctuary's garden beds.
