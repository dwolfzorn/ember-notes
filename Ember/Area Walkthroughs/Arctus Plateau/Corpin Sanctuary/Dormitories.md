# Dormitories

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A short series of stone steps leads northward to intersect with a narrow perpendicular corridor. Seven doors on the north wall and a trio of doors to the south suggest this collection of rooms serves as the lodging for the Cindaric Sages who live here.

The ten chambers assembled in this northern wing of the Sanctuary comprise the facility's dormitories, where the Cindaric Sages and overnight guests can find comfortable but modest lodging.

Each of the dormitory chambers, excluding the [[Washroom]], features a small wooden balcony; the Sages and their guests are known to utilize these for meditation and study beneath the satellites of Ember's ever-swirling cosmos.

> [!info] Social
> #### Wandering Sages
>
> If a character idles here long enough, they'll likely encounter at least one [[Cindaric Adherent]] (or another NPC) on their way to or from the Washroom nearby.
