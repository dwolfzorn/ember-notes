# Winding Stairs

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This wooden stairway zig-zags its way between the western refectory and the Sanctuary's northern dormitories. The narrow gap between the two buildings catches and channels the mountain wind into a steady and powerful gust that both dries your eyes and sweeps the stairs clean.

The gradual climb of the winding stairs here twists its way between the dorms and the refectory, two of the Sanctuary's most frequented areas. Sages are known to linger here after meals for a quick conversation or a casual pipe smoking.

The stair steps themselves count as difficult terrain — which several of the Sages consider to be a minor inconvenience when their stomachs seek a late night snack.
