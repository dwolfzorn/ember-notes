# Sage's Dormitory

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small square chamber is sparsely decorated with the simple accoutrements of a private bedroom, including a rustic wooden wardrobe on the eastern wall and shelves lined with tools of the Cindaric trade to the west.

This modest chamber serves as a dormitory for one of the unnamed Cindaric Sages that lives and works here in the Sanctuary. A comfortable bed stuffed with down feathers adjoins a footlocker full of personal effects, and the wardrobe on the eastern wall is lined with vestments and clothes for various seasons. Shelves on the western wall are stocked with druidic miscellany and a variety of practical tools and equipment.
