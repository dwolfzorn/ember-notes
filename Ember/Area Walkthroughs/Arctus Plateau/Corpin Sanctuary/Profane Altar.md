# Profane Altar

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The stone wall of the dormitory slides down into the floor, revealing a darkened alcove steeped in the iron smell of semi-fresh blood. At the far end of this secret chamber, an enigmatic altar crafted from black stone looms in the shadows.

Unbeknownst to nearly everyone at Corpin Sanctuary, this hidden chamber contains a Profane Altar that unambiguously identifies [[Evesso]] as the necromancer at the heart of the [[Corpin Investigation]] plot.

The Profane Altar itself is a 3-foot high hexagonal lectern, asymmetrically carved from a solid slab of black jade and embellished with the likeness of screaming undead spirits on each side. Blood stains this unholy relic, which is obviously an instrument of evil.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Properties of the Profane Altar
>
> Those with at least one Rune talent can take the time during a Recovery to attune to the Profane Altar, which grants the [[Profane Blessing]] feature.
>
> Due to the weight of the altar, it is effectively immobile. Attempts to forcefully remove the altar are met with frustration and calamity.
>
> These bonuses remain active until the attuned character finishes a Rest. The necromancer [[Evesso]] is attuned to the Profane Altar unless otherwise stated.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Altar
>
> Any character with **Knowledge: Undeath** or who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 13)** while inspecting the profane altar is able to confirm its magical nature, and accurately identifies its potential to enhance necromancy spells.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons**.
> - @CriticalSuccess[dc]: You suspect that the blood here is the necromancer's own, and that a ceremonial dagger associated with the rites performed here (which is, in fact, Evesso's [[Ceremonial Dagger]]) must be located elsewhere.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> The Altar is imbued with a persistent enchantment from **Extended Aura of Death**.
>
> #### Destroying the Profane Altar
>
> The Profane Altar can be destroyed by using dealing 20 magical damage to it. When the altar is destroyed, all creatures within 10 feet suffer the effects of **Profane Backlash (Hazard 8, Willpower, Morale, Psychic)**.
>
> #### Dispelling the Profane Altar
>
> A character with the capability to **Talent: Counterspell** can neutralize the Profane Altar by succeeding on a **Arcana (DC 17)** check.
>
> - The nature of the enchantment can be recognized with **Talent: Recognize Spellcraft**.
> - Attempts to dispel using the **Life** rune gain +2 Boons.
> - Attempts to dispel using the **Aura** gesture gain +2 Boons.
> - Attempts to dispel using the **Death** rune suffer +2 Banes.
