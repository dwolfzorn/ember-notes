# Avwynn's Chamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small square chamber is sparsely decorated with the simple accoutrements of a private bedroom, including a rustic wooden wardrobe on the western wall and shelves lined with tools of the Cindaric trade to the east.

This room serves as the private chamber of [[Avwynn Taol]], the Sanguinary masquerading as a Cindaric Sage. Like the dormitory's other chambers, this room is equipped with a comfortable bed stuffed with down feathers, a footlocker full of personal effects, a rustic wooden wardrobe, and shelves stocked with druidic miscellany.

Yet, there are subtle signs of Avwynn's more rough-and-tumble attitudes about life and her history as a Sanguinary, if the characters know what to look for.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring Avwynn's Chamber
>
> Characters who take a closer look at the contents of the room find Avwynn's chamber to be relatively unkempt compared to other areas of the Dormitory.
>
> A successful **`[[/skillCheck awarness 14]]`** check uncovers the presence of some interesting items within the chamber, including:
>
> - Akit containing a number of alchemical ingredients typically used in the creation of poisons tucked behind an array of bottled herbs and dried specimens.
> - A [[Black Aspen Wand]] in the footlocker, a talisman crafted from the twisted branch of a black aspen tree.
> - [[Avwynn's Journal]], concealed beneath the bed's down-stuffed mattress.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 16)** check knows the Black Aspen tree to be a native plant of the [[Bloodwoods]] located north of Ordain.
>
> Any character that makes a successful **Society (DC 16)** check is able to identify the wand as a talisman commonly carried by fringe druidic groups like the [[Sanguinaries]]. The party can speculate as to why a Cindaric Sage would collect such a taboo instrument, but the wand offers no direct evidence that would expose Avwynn's true affiliation with the Sanguinaries.
