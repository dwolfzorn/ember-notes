# Cliffside Path

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The large alcove formed by the Sanctuary shields you from the mountain wind as you navigate this narrow cliffside path, which is well worn from the sandaled footsteps of the countless Sages who've lived and worked here throughout the centuries.

The cliffside path here is relatively unremarkable, but offers residents and visitors of Corpin Sanctuary a place to enjoy the rustic features of the mountain without going too far afield of the Sanctuary grounds.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### A Dangerous Precipice
>
> The Cliffside Path is situated at the same "ground level" as the [[Entry Courtyard]], and 60 feet above the floor of the ravine below.
>
> Characters who happen to occupy the Cliffside Path during combat situations will find themselves in a more dangerous situation than the Sanctuary's other areas, due to the precipitous elevation and narrowness of the Path's walkways.
>
> - Any character without a flying speed who is pushed or shoved off the Cliffside Path must succeed on a **Athletics (DC 14)** check to avoid falling off the ledge.
> - Characters who fall from the Cliffside Path ledge suffer `[[/roll 1d6 bludgeoning]]` damage for every 10 feet they fall.
