# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The [[Corpin Sanctuary]] Area Map depicts depicts the entirety of the sanctuary with the exception of some minor outlying buildings, and is encountered during the Events of [[Crumbling Sanctuary]].

## Gameplay Details

The areas of Corpin Sanctuary have the following features unless specified otherwise in the text.

### Levels & Elevation

Corpin Sanctuary is situated several hundred feet above sea level, and the ravines that immediately surround the complex run nearly 60 feet deep in certain areas.

Characters who fall from one of the elevated ledges surrounding the Sanctuary suffer `[[/roll 1d6 bludgeoning]]` damage for every 10 feet they happen to fall.

### Sound

Corpin Sanctuary is a quiet place of reverence, and features little in the way of noise beyond wind and the activity of residents.

### Illumination

Illumination here is largely dependent on the time of day, since the location's exterior is fully open to the sky above and several of the Sanctuary's buildings feature moon roofs with open or glassed ceilings.

Inside the Sanctuary, lighting conditions are dependent on the specific illumination used from chamber to chamber. Most interior chambers feature the warm luminosity of lantern or candle light, which sheds bright light in a 15 foot radius and dim light in a 30 foot radius.

### Terrain

Unless otherwise indicated, the landscape throughout Corpin Sanctuary counts as normal terrain. Several of the exterior mountainside areas are treated as difficult terrain because of the sheer rocky surfaces of their craggy heights.

Doors within the Sanctuary are crafted from sturdy oak, and can be bashed in or destroyed with a successful `[[/check str 18]]` **Athletics (DC 18)** check. Most of the facility's doors are unlocked, however, resulting in a rather rare need for forced entry. Locked doors will be noted where applicable.
