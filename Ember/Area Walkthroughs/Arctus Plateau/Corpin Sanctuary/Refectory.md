# Refectory

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The smell of fresh-baked bread and hearty spices greets you as you enter this large refectory, where four long tables sit decorated with a scattered array of half-empty plates and a buffet of various foods — from fresh fruits to salted meats and plenty in between.

This large chamber serves as the Sanctuary's communal refectory, which hosts daily meals, recreational gatherings, and other events.

Rather than scheduled eating times, meals that are prepared daily in the adjoining [[Kitchen]] are set out for consumption throughout the day at the leisure of the Sages who live and work here; several times a week, however, the Cindarics of Corpin Sanctuary gather here for a communal lunch or dinner.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Refectory
>
> Characters who search the Refectory and wish to plunder its supply of food can find up to 5 days of preserved or dry Rations each.

> [!info] Social
> #### Wandering Sages
>
> If a character idles here long enough, they'll likely encounter at least one [[Cindaric Adherent]] (or another NPC) who visits the refectory for a snack.
