# Mira's Office

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The symmetry of this sparsely-decorated chamber and the presence of a desk cluttered with parchment suggests this room is an office of relative import. A bookcase on the eastern wall, lined with various encyclopedic texts and collections of Kelmezian lore, is flanked by two display cases whose esoteric contents linger timelessly beneath glass lids.

This small chamber serves as an office for the Sanctuary's leader, [[Mira Wavehorn]], and is most often used for private meetings and consultations of a sensitive nature (when it is used at all). Mira is more likely to be found in other parts of the facility, and generally reserves this chamber for the execution of her administrative duties.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring Mira's Office
>
> The books that line the shelf on the eastern wall include various encyclopedic texts and collections of Kelmezian lore, including historical accounts of the Cindaric Sages and the founding of the great city of Ordain.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check locates a rare book among the tomes titled "The Ghosts of Yarinu." Any character who takes the time to read this book during a Recovery or Rest gains **+2 Boons** on their next Society check.
>
> - **Culture: Kelmezian**: The character gains **+2 Boons**.
> - **Knowledge: Souls**: The character gains **+2 Boons**.
> - **Knowledge: Undeath**: The character gains **+2 Boons**.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **`[[/skill society 14]]`** check while exploring the office is able to identify the antique art pieces located in the unlocked display cases here.
>
> - The fossil in the northernmost display case is the ossified shell of a Kelmezian Ammonoid — a "shard-finding" gastropod that was considered to be sacred by the early Cindarics, due to its overt connections to the Heartblood of Ember.
> - The parchment scroll in the southernmost display case bears a copy of the "Chant of Adonia" — a key religious text of the Cindaric Sages, the Chant is known to explain the divine vision that led Adonia to establish the great city of Ordain alongside the refugees that already called the place home.
>
> #### Desktop Evidence
>
> Any character who spends at least 5 minutes searching the desk is able to spot several documents and letters of correspondence, including the following:
>
> - A [[Letter from Sigil]], a document that erroneously makes it look as if Mira is turning away refugees.
> - The [[Letter of Introduction]], a document that describes how Evesso came to join the ranks of Corpin Sanctuary under unusual circumstances.
> - A copy of [[Aberin Lowd's Medical Records]], the medical records of Aberin Lowd. Possessing this item relates directly to the event [[Tormented Threads]], in which the body of Aberin is discovered.
>
> #### Hidden Lockbox
>
> If the party takes a moment to search the office, any character who succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 15)** is able to locate a hidden lockbox in a secret compartment beneath Mira's desk. The lockbox contains the Sanctuary's reserves of petty cash, including  **2 28 80** .
