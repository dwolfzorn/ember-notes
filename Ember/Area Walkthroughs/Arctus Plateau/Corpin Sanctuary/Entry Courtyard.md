# Entry Courtyard

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The sweet smell of blush blossom leaves fill the calm air of this tranquil courtyard, where one of these pink-petaled trees grows from the moist earth of a central pit. The courtyard is lined in benches, where a crimson-robed sage sits in casual attendance. The open roof overhead invites you to gaze at the sky in all its splendor.

The Entry Courtyard offers residents and visitors of Corpin Sanctuary a semi-outdoor haven where they might idle the time in a meaningful or relaxing way. During daylight and evening hours alike, it serves as a commons of sorts for the Sanctuary, and is guaranteed to offer guests a welcoming scene upon arrival.

> [!abstract] Cindaric Adherent
> **[[Cindaric Adherent]]**
>
> Level 3 · Human Cindaric Initiate
>
> ![Cindaric Adherent](../../../assets/image024.webp)
>
> This scholarly healer is clad in garments of bright gold and lush crimson, the colors of the famed Cindaric Sages of the Arctus Plateau. Their shoulders and tunic are marked with the familiar four-leafed diamond that signifies the Cindaric order, and their hands are marked and calloused by the wholesome abrasions of honest labor. The sage regards you with a benevolent smile.

> [!info] Social
> #### Courtyard Conversation
>
> The Entry Courtyard is a favorable spot for local Cindaric Sages and visitors alike, and serves as a welcoming place for those who seek inspired study, tranquil relaxation, or friendly discussion.
>
> At any given moment, someone is present in the Entry Courtyard, including at least one [[Cindaric Adherent]] who serves as a steward of the area. These stewards maintain a watchful eye over the Courtyard, but generally keep to themselves unless approached — providing an air of trust and gentle security. The stewards work in 4 hour shifts, and each of the Corpin Sanctuary Sages who lives here (including major and minor NPCs) is committed to at least one shift a week.
>
> If the characters attempt to start a conversation with one of the Cindaric Sages here, topics of discussion include the following:
>
> - The history of Corpin Sanctuary and the Cindaric Sages who dwell here.
> - Current rumors of the Golden Flats and the Arctus Plateau at large.
> - A genuine interest in the party's journey to the Sanctuary, and what they plan to do next.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Courtyard
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Wilderness (DC 14)** is aware of how relatively irregular it is that a blush blossom tree blooms in the mountainous climate and dry rocky terrain here, and attributes the tree's lush growth to some leyline of Ember's heartblood or the tenacity of the Cindaric Sages themselves.
