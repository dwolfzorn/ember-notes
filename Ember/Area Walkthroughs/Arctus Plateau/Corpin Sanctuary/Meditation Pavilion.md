# Meditation Pavilion

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The balcony overlook leads you to an isolated pavilion that stands high above the gulleys below. At the center of this open-roofed area, a smaller blush blossom tree sways gently in the mountain breeze. The comfortable wooden benches here and the perpetual air of majesty suggest this place would be a perfect spot for meditation or lunar communion.

This indoor-outdoor annex serves as a Meditation Pavilion for the Sages of Corpin Sanctuary, and offers a more secluded area from which to enjoy the majestic splendor of the Sanctuary's mountainside environs.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Pavilion
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Wilderness (DC 14)** is aware of how relatively irregular it is that a blush blossom tree blooms in the mountainous climate and dry rocky terrain here, and attributes the tree's lush growth to some leyline of Ember's heartblood or the tenacity of the Cindaric Sages themselves.
>
> Characters who succeed on a **Awareness (DC 13)** discover a curious trinket that has grown into the twisting trunk of the tree, as if it was placed there long ago, clutched by the knotted bark like a warm embrace.
>
> - A closer look at the embedded trinket reveals it to be a tiny statuette of an otherworldly bird, crafted from some meteoric alloy.
> - If any of the Cindaric Sages here are asked about the trinket in the tree, they reveal its origin story: the trinket was crafted from a meteorite of Aura, and one of the founders of Corpin Sanctuary — a Zeph scholar named Zhulian Rha — planted it with the tree long ago in the hopes that it would bless the Sanctuary with the cosmic inspiration of the Wind Moon.
