# Mira's Chamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The size of this dormitory chamber and the presence of a stately document-strewn desk betray it as the private bedroom of Corpin Sanctuary's most esteemed sage. Several household items decorate the room's shelves, scuffed by perpetual use, alongside years of charitable gifts in the form of simple Arcturian keepsakes.

This room serves as the private chamber of [[Mira Wavehorn]], the Sanctuary's leader. Although the bed is no larger than those found in other dormitory rooms, the rest of the furniture here is somewhat larger, and indicative of the occupant's relative importance among the residents of Corpin Sanctuary.

Mira stores her vestments and day-to-day equipment in the large footlocker west of the bed, and the wardrobe on the northern wall is lined with a variety of femme garments that cater to each of Ember's seasons.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring Mira's Chamber
>
> The relatively cluttered and dust-free nature of this chamber suggests that Mira spends more time here than in her [[Mira's Office]] (a fact that can be easily confirmed by the head Sage herself).
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 13)** check is able to locate one random [[Arcturian Trinkets]] of interest.
>
> #### Desktop Evidence
>
> Any character who spends at least 5 minutes searching the desk is able to spot several documents and letters of correspondence, including the following:
>
> - A [[Letter from Lilla Arien]]. (The 2nd set of evidence found will clarify that Mira is reluctantly following instruction provided to her by a superior in Ordain).
>
> The first character to succeed on either a **Awareness (DC 15)** or **Arcana (DC 13)** check while searching the desk are also able to locate a [[Spell Scroll: Locate Object]] buried beneath the layers of parchment.
