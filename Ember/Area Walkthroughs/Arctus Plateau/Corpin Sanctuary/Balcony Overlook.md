# Balcony Overlook

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This stone balcony presents an impressive view of the mountainside and the terrain below, where the Golden Flats stretch out as far as the eye can see — including the edge of the Arctus Chasm to the east, and the banks of the River Elenain to the west. The decision to construct the sanctuary here was truly an inspired one, if the noble breeze that rustles your garments and stirs your imagination has anything to say about it.

In times of peace, the Balcony Overlook here is an interesting piece of architecture that embraces the majesty of the Sanctuary's mountainside setting; in times of trouble, however, it becomes a dangerous location where gravity can be a liability.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### A Dangerous Precipice
>
> The Balcony Overlook is situated 15 feet above the "ground level" of the [[Cliffside Path]], and 85 feet above the floor of the ravine below.
>
> Characters who happen to occupy the Balcony Overlook during combat situations will find themselves in a more dangerous situation than the Sanctuary's other areas, due to the precipitous elevation and narrowness of the Overlook's walkways.
>
> - Any character without a flying speed who is pushed or shoved off the Balcony Overlook must make a successful **Athletics (DC 14)** check to avoid falling off the ledge.
> - Characters who fall from the Balcony Overlook ledge suffer **falling damage (Hazard 3, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)** for every 10 feet they fall.
