# Empty Dormitory

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A layer of dust has settled on the surfaces of this simple square chamber, which appears to be some sort of guest room for visitors to Corpin Sanctuary. While a scattering of basic accoutrements can be found here, like a wooden wardrobe on the western wall and dusty shelves to the east, they appear relatively unused.

The empty dormitories of Corpin Sanctuary remain vacant and are generally reserved for visitors to the remote Cindaric facility. Albeit dusty, a comfortable bed stuffed with down feathers adjoins an empty footlocker here, and the wardrobe is bare — save for a single sage's robe and two sets of towels. Whatever miscellany lines the shelves here is mostly decorative.
