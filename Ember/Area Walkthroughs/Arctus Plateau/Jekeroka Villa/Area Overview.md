# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The[[Jekeroka Villa]] Area Map depicts the entirety of the [[Anachraenum]] dig site southwest of [[Ordain]], where the ruin of an ancient [[Aedir]] stronghold serves as a modern training facility for the guild. It is featured in the [[Manufactured Trial]] Event of [[The Expedition Challenge]] Main Quest.
>
> - This map only depicts one Scene Level.
> - The villa was the once-proud home laboratory of an Aedir Maejis known as Jekeroka, whose experiments with elemental magic are the stuff of long-forgotten legend.
> - The party arrives at the [[Exterior]] area of the map, where loremistress [[Adelyne Goss]] impatiently awaits them.

## Gameplay Details

The areas of Jekeroka Villa have the following features unless otherwise specified.

### Levels & Elevation

The Jekeroka Villa Scene features one vertical Level of elevation, including:

- **Jekeroka Villa:** The villa interior is set to an elevation of 0 and has 25-foot-high ceilings.

### Illumination

The interiors of Jekeroka Villa are illuminated by three distinct sources: primary lantern light in the [[Main Chamber]], which radiates bright light for 30 feet and dim light for an additional 15 feet; secondary lantern light in the side chambers, which radiates bright light for 2 feet and dim light for an additional 15 feet; and ambient light originating from the Arcane Fire Pits in the Main Chamber, which radiate bright light for 3 feet and dim light for an additional 8 feet. The lanterns here are magical in nature, and are crafted using orbs of semi-translucent crystal. Outside areas are subject to exterior lighting and associated weather conditions.

### Terrain

The terrain here resembles the typical landscape of a well-constructed Aedir edifice, with enough clutter, rubble, and detritus to create intermittent spots of &reference[difficult terrain].

The solitary front door to Jekeroka Villa is usually locked, unless an Anachraenum Guild Member with the [[Jekeroka Villa Key]] is present, in which case the door is unlocked and attended at nearly all times. Powerful magic prohibits the door from being opened by any method other than the key (or other suitably powerful magic).

### Inhabitants

The following characters and creatures inhabit this area:

- [[Adelyne Goss]] is present at the [[Exterior]] section of the villa for the duration of [[Manufactured Trial]], but is otherwise located elsewhere.

### Enemies

During the [[Manufactured Trial]] Event, the following enemies are encountered here:

- 4 [[Anachraenum Aetherial]] await contestants in the [[Main Chamber]].
