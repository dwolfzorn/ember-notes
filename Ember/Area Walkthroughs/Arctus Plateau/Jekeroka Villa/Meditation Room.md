# Meditation Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The small chamber is surrounded by polished stone walls decorated with glowing small Aedir glyphs that illuminate the space with a calming radiance. On the far wall, a raised dais holds a large, intricately woven cushion and several empty candle holders. Beside the cushion is a note. written in common, it appears to have been written by the Anacharanum rather than using an ancient Aedir script, and it reads:
>
> > To gain what was sought by those who before,
> > Speak the truth of what you adore.
> > It’s found in a places, both warm and bright,
> > Where shadows dance in the softest light.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Meditation and Reflection
>
> This is the chamber where Jekeroka meditated rather than sleeping during his time at the villa. The party must solve the riddle to proceed.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Society (DC 13)** check is able to recognize that the glyphs are [[Aedir]] in nature.
>
> - **Knowledge: Aedir**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Alternately, any character who can read **Language: Towyr** can understand what the glyphs say, which is that this was once a room where an Aedir mage spent time meditating on arcane advancements. Nothing else is mentioned, and the graphs seem more meant for reflection and gaining a passive understanding of magic in a peaceful way.
>
> A successful **Arcana (DC 14)** check reveals that the glyphs are also powered by the elemental energy within the main chamber and have been active since [[The Shattering]], a truly astonishing feat of magic.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> #### Finding the Hidden Switch
>
> The riddle on the note is relatively simple: anyone who speaks the word 'Knowledge' hears a small click while in the room, and a hidden compartment opens in the dais, revealing a small push-button switch.
>
> Any character who makes a successful `[[/check investigation 12]]` check is eventually able to figure out the correct answer. Once the switch is pushed, nothing seems to happen immediately, but the challenge with this room is over.
