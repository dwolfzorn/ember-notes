# Main Chamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you step through the entrance into the main chamber of the dig site, you notice the air around you is cool, but an ancient energy seems to hum just below the surface. Your footsteps echo softly against the pale stone floor, and while the chamber is large with tall ceilings, your eyes are drawn to the large pits of elemental energy that bubble gently at the center of the room. The surface of this elemental energy shimmers with deep blues and turns black around the edges. Around the walls, intricate patterns and strange golden lamps pulse softly, casting delicate shadows that dance in time with the light from the magical energy coming from below. The craftsmanship is astonishing, and it’s clear this place has been both preserved and well-maintained by the Anachraenum.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Arcane Fire Pits
>
> Within the main chamber are a number of pits that drop into glowing blue energy, which looks strangely like blue lava. Creatures that fall ten feet into this elemental energy are not submerged, but instead stand on top of it. However, they take `[[/damage 3d10 fire]]` damage. The intense heat and volatile nature of the energy cause immediate and searing pain. The character also takes an additional `[[/damage 1d10 fire]]` damage at the start of their turns if they are in combat, as the energy continues to burn until they climb out.
>
> Anyone who wishes to investigate the elemental energy can learn the following:
>
> Any character who makes a successful `[[/check arcana 14]]` check can recognize that this energy is strange. It is a deeply magical substance and one that seems to be powering the site, but it has not faded over the millennia and curiously continues to draw power from an unknown source, though likely, given the magical signature, that it is tied to several different elemental moons.
>
> - **Knowledge: Elementals**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Aedir**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: the substance fascinating to some degree, as there are notes and myths surrounding this substance being present in other Aedir ruins. Perhaps this might be the original source of the energy.
>
> Additionally, casting the [[Detect Magic]] Spell reveals…

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Polished Aedir Stone
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill prc 12 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check notices that the interior stonework within the chamber is substantially more preserved than the exterior. In fact, the stone still has an element of shine and polish to it, as if it is cleaned regularly. The only marks appear to be from regular travel of muddy boots leading from the door into the site.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Subterranea**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character who makes a successful `[[/check arcana 14]]`, they discover that the stone is slightly magical in nature, at least in terms of preservation.
>
> If a character casts [[Detect Magic]]…

## Ancient Rubble

On the left side of the chamber, when the party enters, are two small heaps of rubble blocking the space into the room on the left, leading to the [[Meditation Room]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Deliberate Challenge
>
> The Anacharsnum has placed these rubble piles to simulate what a blocked room might look like and how a band of adventurers would handle clearing the way.
>
> Any character who makes a successful `[[/check inv 12]]` check can discover that the stone has been stacked together deliberately and seems to be held together by a hard-set glue or binding agent of some kind.
>
> - **Knowledge: Subterranea**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Crafts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Forensics**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character who makes a successful `[[/check str 14]]` check can break apart the rocky rubble, and the glue will crumble, revealing the room ahead.

## Mechanized Trial

Once the party has flipped all three switches in the other rooms and returns to the [[Main Chamber]], read the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> With small magical whines as if they are powering up, strange purple machines appear from nothing within the main chamber. They shudder and twist in the air as the central core turns and focuses directly on you.

> [!abstract] Anachraenum Aetherial
> **[[Anachraenum Aetherial]]**
>
> Level 1 · Unknown Unknown
>
> ![Anachraenum Aetherial](../../../assets/image040.webp)

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### **Anachraenum Aetherial** **Tactics**
>
> The four Anachraenum Aetherials in the main chamber have been programmed to attack only after the three switches in the other rooms have been pressed. Until then, they remain invisible, as all four have had invisibility cast upon them by [[Adelyne Goss]], and cannot be seen. In the event that any character casts [[See Invisibility]], the combat encounter begins immediately, and the party skips finding the switches in the other rooms.
>
> #### **Anachraenum Aetherial** **Tactics**
>
> The Anachraenum Aetherial's are extremely simple creatures that do not have any sentient or cognitive capacity to think. they merely do what they have been programmed to do by members of the Anachranum who have access to them.
>
> In this combat encounter, they have been programmed to attack and use their [[Laser]] action on thier turn to attack members at random.
>
> If they are in melee range with a party members, the Aetherial will move away and try to gain a maximum distance of 40 feet regardless of attacks of opportunity.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Studying the **Aetherial****s**
>
> During or after the encounter, the party may attempt to investigate the small constructs, and they discover the following:
>
> Any character who makes a successful `[[/check perception 13]]` or `[[/check nature 12]]` check can determine that the Aetherials are being powered by a magically infused [[Iolite]] which they can pry away and keep.
>
> Any character who makes a successful `[[/check arc 15]]` check can determine that these strange little constructs have been created by an extremely competent and powerful arcane mage, with a level of technology approaching the Aedir but still somewhat unrefined.
>
> - **Knowledge: Artifacts**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Machines**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

## Claiming the Key

Once the party has defeated all four of the [[Anachraenum Aetherial]], the characters can claim the Trial Challenge Key, which is revealed to them via the magic of Jekeroka.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A glittering key shimmers into existence before your very eyes, conjured out of thin air and hovering idly above the chest.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Completing the Challenge
>
> The [[Trial Challenge Key]] magically appears in front of the party at this time, and the Keystone Challenge is concluded.
