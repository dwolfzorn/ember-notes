# Southern Sitting Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small circular sitting room is decorated with a padded sofa and a hefty stone pot holding a curious plum-colored plant. Much of the floor here is transparent, and seems to be crafted from some kind of durable glass or crystal, permitting one to sit and view the arcane splendor that swirls beneath this sprawling, otherworldly complex.

This unassuming chamber serves a variety of roles — from a sitting room, to an isolated study, or an observation deck. An azure rug detailed with golden line-work adorns the floor at the center of the chamber, and can be removed at leisure.

When the characters arrive, the Energy Doors at the eastern and western edges of the chamber are deactivated and open.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Sitting Room
>
> Characters who take a moment to search the southern sitting room note the following:
>
> - The padded sofa's frame is constructed from fine ashwood, and the textiles used in its padding are exquisite; it is readily apparent that this couch is slightly more posh and preserved than the similar furniture in the [[Tower Antechamber]].
> - The strange potted plant here is similar to those found in the Tower Antechamber. However, this plant appears more vibrant, and emits a soft electrostatic hum.
>
> Any character who succeeds on either a **Arcana (DC 13)** or **Wilderness (DC 13)** check is able to discern that the plant's perpetual proximity to [[Signara]] keeps it infused with latent magic that is otherwise missing from the antechamber flora.
>
> - **Path: Scion of Darkness**: The character automatically succeeds on this check, but must roll with **+2 Boons** to attempt a Critical Success.
> - **Knowledge: Fiends**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Plants**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character is able to identify the plant as a drakehead fern. When the plant is healthy and present in Signara, [[Drakehead Spores]] can be harvested from its otherworldly leaves and used as an alternative for any plant-based material spell component.
>
> The first character who makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 13)** check is able to harvest `[[/roll 1d2+1]]` units of [[Drakehead Spores]] from the fern. After the spores are harvested, it takes 30 days for them to regrow.
>
> - **Knowledge: Plants**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
