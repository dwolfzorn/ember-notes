# Front Gate

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> An artistic relief depicting a cloaked four-armed mage surrounded by embellishments of arcane splendor decorates the tower's main gate. The figure at the center of the relief studies a grimoire with intensity, and a phrase written in an obscure language appears to be etched upon the tome's stony cover.

### Entering the Tower

The first time the party arrives at Vortest Tower during the course of [[The Signborn's Secret]], the Event's preceding narrative is in-progress, and the integrity of the front gate's security measures have been compromised.

Unbeknownst to the characters, the tower has very recently been infiltrated by a gang of fiendish [[Jahud Assassin]], who sundered the locking mechanisms and protective magics used to keep the gate secure. These jahud lurk elsewhere (inside the pocket of Signara), and the broken lock seems to be the only clue they've left behind.

Read the following aloud once the party gets close enough to the front gate to survey the damage:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The front gate stands ever-so-slightly ajar, and the intricate mechanism that once served as the door's handle appears to have been rent in twain by the supremely keen edge of some blade or claw, allowing you to enter the tower remarkably unimpeded.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Door
>
> Characters who examine the front gate door within 5 feet of it are able to note the following:
>
> - The door is effectively unlocked and stands ready to open wider.
> - Upon closer inspection, the mage featured in the bas-relief appears to be a [[Signborn]] woman.
> - Characters who understand Eonic are able to identify the obscure text written on the tome in the relief as **Language: Eonic**. It reads: "House Vortest."
> - The amethyst-hued alloy the door appears to be made from is unlike any metal or ore the characters have seen.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 13)** check notices some additional details about the lock and the damage that has recently occurred to it:
>
> - The metal used in the creation of the lock appears similar to [[Adamantine]] or an alloy of it.
> - The damage to the lock was caused by the blade of a similar substance, if not one more naturally (or supernaturally) durable.
> - Any tracks left by the vandal have been worn away by time and weather. It's hard to tell precisely how long ago this occurred, but it must have been at least a week or two.
>
> Any character who makes either a successful **Arcana (DC 15)** or **Wilderness (DC 18)** check can determine that the curious amethyst-hued alloy the door is fashioned from hails from Signara.
>
> - **Knowledge: Cosmology**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

Once they're ready to enter the tower, the characters can simply open the compromised door and proceed.

### Return Visits

Following the successful conclusion of [[The Signborn's Secret]], Corvana Vortest will take quick steps to repair the locking mechanisms on the front gate. The front gate is sealed with locks both magical and mechanical, and its "normal" conditions from now on are detailed below.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Door
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while examining the door and its locking mechanism is able to tell the following:
>
> - The embellishments surrounding the mage in the relief have been augmented, and appear to feature a peculiar assortment of arcane glyphs.
> - The locking mechanisms and the door handle are made of freshly-forged adamantine.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 16)** check can ascertain that the arcane glyphs mean "secure" and "slumber."
>
> The **Talent: Recognize Spellcraft** talent reveals the presence of powerful magics on the door.
>
> - A powerful Kinesis spell has been cast upon the door by [[Nalgomith]]. The password for this magical lock is the phrase "knowledge unbound" spoken in **Language: Eonic**.
> - The front gate door has also been trapped with a powerful enchantment dubbed the "Sleep of Ages" (see below).
>
> In order to open the door without speaking the password, a successful **Stealth (DC 22)** check with a proficiency is required to pick the lock, and the magic portion can be bypassed with **`[[/counterspell rune=kinesis gesture=influence inflection=negate 18]]`**. However, bypassing the lock in this fashion results in a trap being triggered:

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Sleep of Ages Trap
>
> Any character who attempts to pick the lock of the front gate without speaking the Arcane Lock's password becomes the target of a powerful Sleep spell, and must face **Hazard 10 (Willpower, Morale, Harmless)** to avoid its effects. If affected by this hazard:
>
> - The character is placed into an immediate and unwaking slumber for a duration of 12 hours.
> - Creatures that don't naturally require sleep are still subject to the effects of this Spell.
> - The magical sleep induced by this Spell does not count as a Rest, and does not satisfy the criteria necessary for the recovery of any Resources.
