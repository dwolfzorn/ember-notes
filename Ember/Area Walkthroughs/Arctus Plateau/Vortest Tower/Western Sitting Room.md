# Western Sitting Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A bench and chair adorn this modest sitting room, which faces the Tower Antechamber from a door-sized opening to the east. A lush, plum-colored plant sits next to the chair, which exudes the pleasant scent of ozone from its large lavender leaves.

This sitting room resembles the [[Southern Sitting Room]] in most regards, but features a 15-foot floor extension that also serves as a causeway between the [[Living Quarters]] and the [[Workshop]]. The azure rug that adorns the floor at the center of the chamber features a detail golden line-work, and can be removed at leisure.

When the characters arrive, the energy doors to the north and south of the chamber are activated and closed. A control panel in this chamber opens the westernmost door to the [[Tower Antechamber]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Sitting Room
>
> Characters who take a moment to search the western sitting room note the following:
>
> - The bench and chair are constructed from fine ashwood, and the textiles used in their padding are exquisite.
> - The strange potted plant here is similar to those found elsewhere in the complex. It appears vibrant and emits a soft electrostatic hum, along with a distinct odor of ozone.
>
> Any character who makes either a successful `[[/check arc 14]]` or `[[/check nat 14]]` check is able to discern that the plant's perpetual proximity to Signara keeps it infused with latent enchantment.
>
> Any character who succeeds on either a `[[/check arc 13]]` or `[[/check nat 13]]` check is able to discern that the plant's perpetual proximity to [[Signara]] keeps it infused with latent magic that is otherwise missing from the antechamber flora.
>
> - **Path: Scion of Darkness**: The character automatically succeeds on this check, but must roll with **+2 Boons** to attempt a Critical Success.
> - **Knowledge: Fiends**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Plants**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character is able to identify the plant as a Terra Tree. When the plant is healthy and present in Signara, Terra Trees can purify the air in a 30-foot radius, making it cleaner and more pleasant — and ultimately more reminiscent of the atmosphere on Ember and its own chemical components.
>
> The first character to succeeds on a **Wilderness (DC 13)** check is able to harvest `[[/roll 1d4 + 1]]` matured units of [[Terra Tree Leaves]] from the fern. After the leaves are harvested, it takes 30 days for them to regrow.
>
> - **Knowledge: Plants**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
