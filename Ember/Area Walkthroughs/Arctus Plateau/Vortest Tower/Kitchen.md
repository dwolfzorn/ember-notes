# Kitchen

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cozy circular chamber appears to house a kitchen and dining room area. A small amount of food remains forgotten on the table — including rancid tea in silver receptacles, a brown and rotted sliced pear on a small plate, and a loaf of bread covered in a fuzzy veneer of viridian mold. A solitary teacup sits on the table, suggesting that whoever once dined here was doing it alone.
>
> A sweet smell of fresh decay wafts towards you from the opposite side of the room, where a metal stove is surrounded by crates of spoiled food.

This 30-foot diameter chamber serves as a kitchen and dining room area for Vortest Tower, and is equipped with a stove range, storage cabinets and crates, and an engraved table made of fine petrified ashwood.

The food on the table appears half-eaten and has started to rot. Similarly, other food found in the storage areas near the stove — like produce and bread — has also gone bad. This room currently reflects a state of normalcy that was interrupted when the [[Jahud Assassin]] infiltrated the Tower, and the questionable span of time that has passed in the interim.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Kitchen
>
> Characters who take a moment to search the kitchen note the following:
>
> - The pots and pans scattered on the stove top appear to have been quickly abandoned, and contain the rotted ingredients of a meal that was prepared but never cooked.
> - Some of the rotted foods and ingredients are curious and strange compared to standard [[Arcturian]] cuisine.
>
> Any character who succeeds on either a **Awareness (DC 13)** check is able to determine that some of the foodstuffs and cutlery are in a state of disarray, suggesting that the contents of the room were rifled through during a quick search.
>
> - **Knowledge: Forensics**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Wilderness (DC 15)** check is able to discover evidence of footprints that match those found in the Tower Antechamber, which seem to belong to one or more medium-sized creatures with taloned, three-toed feet. The dust here is less evident than the previous chamber, and the tracks appear to travel from the northwestern door into the chamber, where they circle about before heading westward out of the room.
>
> - **Knowledge: Tracking**: The character automatically succeeds.
> - **Knowledge: Fiends**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check, and can identify the tracks as fiendish in nature.
>
> Although much of the food in the kitchen area has rotted, characters who wish to pilfer the pantry are able to salvage `[[/roll 2d4]]` days worth of [[Rations]] each.
