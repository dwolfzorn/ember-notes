# Northern Sitting Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This glass-floored chamber features a variety of furniture for lounging and relaxation, from an assortment of individual chairs to a lush, pleated lounge sofa. A trio of bookshelves adorn the walls, lined with what appear to be the fanciful titles of various works of fiction, written in a countless variety of languages. The southern threshold of this chamber faces the tower antechamber to the south, nearly close enough for one to reach out and touch it.

This large circular chamber serves as [[Corvana Vortest]]'s most frequented sitting room, and boasts one of the facility's largest transparent floors — allowing those who lounge here to lavishly bask in the majesty of [[Signara]] terrain as they study or sit.

A control panel located in this chamber opens the northernmost door to the [[Tower Antechamber]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Sitting Room
>
> Characters who take a moment to search the western sitting room note the following:
>
> - The furniture constructed from fine ashwood, and the textiles used in their padding are exquisite.
> - The potted plants here, while native to Signara, are relatively mundane.
> - The books that line the shelves are mostly works of fiction. An assortment of interesting titles can be found, written in nearly every language known to the people of [[World of Ember]], but they offer relatively little in terms of gainful knowledge or revelations about the matter at hand.
>
> Any character that succeeds on an **Awareness (DC 17)** check while searching the shelves is able to locate a [[Spell Scroll (Fine)]]] of **Illuminated Sense** folded into the pages of a book labeled "Warlocks at the Gate."
