# Gameplay Details

The remote edifice and otherworldly complex known as Vortest Tower serves as one of the primary locations of [[The Book Of Tales]], in which the storyteller [[Amalthea Stonecraft]] has tasked the party with the transcribing of five curious Arcturian stories — including her own.

Vortest Tower is the transdimensional dwelling of the Signborn Mage known as [[Corvana Vortest]], who is trapped in a state of magical suspension in the Master Bedroom of the area when the characters arrive.

Once the characters reach Vortest Tower, they must:

- Figure out how to travel to [[Signara]] via the Tower Antechamber's secret trigger — a mechanism disguised as a book located on the chamber's northeastern bookshelf.
- Journey room-by-room through the complex, activating the lambent arcane bridges necessary to traverse the area while surviving attacks from fiendish assassins known as [[Jahud Assassin]], interlopers sent here by the malefic Daemos [[Kerastes]].
- Reach the Master Bedroom and save Corvana Vortest, who is caught in a magical state of suspended animation along with a handful of Jahud assassins.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### The Signaran Assassins
>
> The Jahud Assassins who currently occupy various chambers here are otherworldly invaders from the Inner Realm of Signara. Once these alien intruders are slain, Vortest Tower reverts to a safe location for Corvana Vortest and her allies (including the characters, if they aid Vortest in her struggle).

## Illumination

On Ember, illumination here is mostly dependent on the time of day, since the location exterior is fully open to the sky above.

On Signara, the entire area is illuminated with dim light that radiates from the lambent pools of arcane energy that roil in perpetuity below the complex. Additionally, each of the lambent bridges radiates bright light in a 5 foot aura once activated.

## Terrain

On Ember, most of the terrain outside the tower is treated as difficult terrain because of the extreme overgrowth and cluttered macabre lawn decoration. The Tower Antechamber and all other rooms of the Signaran complex are treated as normal terrain, except where otherwise noted. The areas surrounding the Signaran complex chambers on the map are situated below them, and are defined by the presence of a translucent arcane miasma that can be traversed using one's mind.

Doors within the complex are made from a similar manifestation of solid arcane light, and are effectively indestructible. Each of these Energy Doors can be opened or closed with an item known as the [[Jaunt Manipulator]], or via a paired Control Panel. Additionally, each Energy Door can be disabled for `[[/roll 1d3 + 1]]` hours by if they are counterspelled with a **`[[/counterspell rune=illumination gesture=influence inflection=negate 16]]`**.

- Unless otherwise stated, each chamber of the complex features a Control Panel inside next to each of its doors.
- Like regular doors, Energy Doors can be opened or closed as part of a character's Move action.

The various chambers of the Signaran complex are connected by a series of lambent arcane bridges made of solid light, most of which are deactivated when the characters first arrive. These Light Bridges can be activated or deactivated at the same Control Panels that manipulate the Energy Doors.

## Elevation

Vortest Tower is situated at ground level on Ember. Meanwhile, the various chambers and lambent bridges of the Signaran pocket section of the location are situated roughly 60 feet above an alien expanse marked by luminous pools of magical energy that release a strange arcane miasma.

Characters who fall from one of the elevated chambers or bridges must succeed on a **Athletics (DC 15)** check to avoid immediately plummeting into the arcane miasma below.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Navigating the Arcane Miasma
>
> Characters who plummet into the arcane miasma can spend an action on their turn to attempt a **Arcana (DC 13)** check; success causes their movement type to change to Flying until the beginning of their next turn, which can only be used to traverse the area defined by the arcane miasma. Any character who fails this check can repeat it as many times as they'd like, but can only make the attempt once per turn.

## Metaphysics of Signara

The exploration of [[Signara]] can be a disorienting experience for those unaccustomed to its arcane peculiarities. Characters must steel themselves against the potential psychic threats of Signaran entities or enchantments, which present themselves both immediately and over the course of time; and the very fabric of reality is different in Signara, exposing characters to a world both dark and fascinating, full of tenebrous secrets and mind-staggering revelations.

Encounters in Signara are subject to the following metaphysical changes and effects:

- Signborn characters and characters with Attunement: Signara apply **-2 Banes** to any psychic hazards they face here.
- All characters have **-2 Banes** on checks to detect and resist magic associated with the Illusion Rune.
- Arcana checks are made with **+2 Boons**.
- Celestial and fey creatures gain additional weakness to psychic damage.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Otherworldly Hallucinations of Signara
>
> Any mortal humanoid who spends enough time in Signara — including the transdimensional Signaran pocket accessed by Vortest Tower — is likely to encounter the threat of otherworldly hallucinations.
>
> The first time a character sets foot in the otherworldly realm of Signara and every 4 hours after their arrival, they must face **Psychic Drift (Hazard 6, Willpower, Morale, Harmless)**, and if affected by this hazard, they succumb to the effects of Otherworldly Hallucinations.
>
> - Signborn characters and characters with Attunement: Signara cause this hazard to have **-2 Banes**.
> - Characters who are not hit by this hazard are immune to the effects of Otherworldly Hallucinations until the next dusk.
> - The effects of Otherworldly Hallucination last until the character completes a Rest.
>
> Any character who fails this saving throw must roll on the following **Otherworldly Hallucinations** **Table** once every four hours to determine how the metaphysical psychedelia manifests itself.
