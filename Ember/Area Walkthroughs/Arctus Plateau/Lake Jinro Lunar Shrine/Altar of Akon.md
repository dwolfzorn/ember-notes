# Altar of Akon

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Hewn from rough umber, the stone altar at the foot of the switchback path appears to be scribed with runic markings and adorned with a shallow stone bowl.

For details about the properties of the altar stones, consult "Examining the Altars" in the [[Switchback Path]] section of the walkthrough.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Honoring the Shattered Moon
>
> The first altar honors [[Akon]], the Shattered Moon.
>
> - Inscription: "Honor the Sundered"
> - Offering: A broken item or something made of raw bronze.
>
> Characters with **Attunement: Akon** can feel a strong connection to the altar, as if it calls to them. They also feel a slight arcane vibration emanating from any bronze Items in their possession.
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 11, Passive)** while searching the altar is able to notice the tiniest of scratches on the surface of the bottom of the bowl.
>
> - **Knowledge: Forensics**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character with **Science (DC 13, Passive)** is able to associate the phrase "Honor the Sundered" with Akon — the Shattered Moon of order — in the context of the island's history.
>
> - **Knowledge: Cosmology**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Legends**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
