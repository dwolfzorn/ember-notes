# Switchback Path

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> An overgrown path winds its way up and around the forgotten hilltop island. You notice a small rune-marked altar made of rugged stone at the base of the switchback, and a handful of similar altars along the path as it curves upward.

### The Altar Stones

This collection of six stone altars hewn from rough umber serves a mysterious purpose for most visitors of the shrine. However, legends hold true that those who pay proper ritualistic respects at each of these six altars will gain a temporary cosmic blessing.

As the party ascends the slow vertical incline that wraps itself counterclockwise up towards the island's hilly precipice, they may examine and interact with this tertiary collection of monuments to Ember's moons — and possibly receive a metaphysical benefit by enacting all six sacred rites associated with them.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Altars
>
> Characters who solve the mystery of the Altar Stones will be able to grant the party a mechanical enhancement for the rest of their time here.
>
> Any character who properly enacts the ritual by placing the correct offering in all six altar bowls is able to activate a special magical effect. Once the sixth and final offering has been placed in its requisite bowl, each creature within 15 feet becomes the target of an unexpectedly potent **Kinetic Ward** Spell. The duration of this Spell is 4 hours. This effect can only be activated once per week.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 13)** check can decipher the general purpose of the altars: enactment of the complete ritual awards some kind of metaphysical boon, likely channeled through ancient abjuration magic that sits latent in the stone here.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Artifacts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> The inscriptions etched on each of the altars are written in **Language: Arcden**.
