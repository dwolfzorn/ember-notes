# Loot-Lined Hollow

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You notice a sizable hollow in the trunk of the aged olive tree here. The dark opening seems like the perfect lair for some small creature, if only this remote island could sustain such wildlife.

### Inside the Olive Tree

Characters who take a moment to examine the hollow cavity inside this ancient olive tree may be rewarded for their effort.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Hollow
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 15)** check while searching the hollowed-out tree's interior is able to locate a dirt-covered scroll case concealed deep within the mulch-lined insides of the rotting hollow.
>
> The scroll case contains a [[Rustvar Valleys Map]] a [[Healing Tonic]] and a [[Rallying Tonic]].
>
> Once a character reads the Rustvar Valleys Map, it reveals the locations of [[Skybrush]] and [[Storsa's Strand]] on the [[The Arctus Plateau]] Region Map.
