# Forgotten Wreckage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The splintered hull of a small rowboat is washed up on the stony beach here, split in twain alongside a scattered array of water-soaked flotsam.

### The Sundered Rowboat

Overturned during its wreckage, the splintered hull of the damaged rowboat here provides a meager amount of shade for the gulls and crustaceans who intermittently call it home.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Searching the Wreckage
>
> A few Items of interest can be found among the splintered wreckage of the sundered rowboat here.
>
> The eroded remains of a humanoid skeleton are tucked away inside the eastern section of the wreckage. The first character to search these remains can roll twice on the [[Corpse Loot]] table to reveal what they find.
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** while searching the wreckage is able to locate a locked strongboxburied in the sand. The strongbox contains 8 [[Iolite]] gemstones and three [[Bronze Ingot]].
>
> The lock on the chest can be bypassed by any character who makes a successful **Stealth (DC 15)** with a[[Lockpicks]] or forced open with a successful **Athletics (DC 16)** check. The key that unlocks the chest is somewhere else on Ember, lost to time and mystery.
