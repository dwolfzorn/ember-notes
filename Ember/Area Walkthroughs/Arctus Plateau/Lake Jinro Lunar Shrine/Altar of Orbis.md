# Altar of Orbis

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Hewn from rough umber, this stone altar appears to be scribed with runic markings and adorned with a shallow stone bowl.

For details about the properties of the altar stones, consult "Examining the Altars" in the [[Switchback Path]] section of the walkthrough.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Honoring the Spirit Moon
>
> The fifth altar honors [[Orbis]], the Spirit Moon.
>
> - Inscription: "Honor the Maelstrom"
> - Offering: A few drops of your own blood.
>
> Characters with **Attunement: Orbis** can feel a strong connection to the altar, as if it calls to them.
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 11, Passive)** while searching the altar is able to notice weather-worn traces of dark brown bloodstains in the bottom of the bowl.
>
> - **Knowledge: Forensics**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character withcharacter with **Science (DC 13, Passive)** is able to associate the phrase "Honor the Maelstrom" with Orbis — the Spirit Moon of chaos — in the context of the island's history.
>
> - **Knowledge: Cosmology**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Legends**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
