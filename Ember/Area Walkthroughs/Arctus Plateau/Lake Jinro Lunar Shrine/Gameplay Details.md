# Gameplay Details

The island known as Lake Jinro Lunar Shrine serves as the location for the climactic finale of [[The Book Of Tales]], in which the storyteller [[Amalthea Stonecraft]] has tasked the party with the transcribing of five curious Arcturian stories — including her own.

## Objectives

The area must be reached by water or air, and is surrounded on all sides by the aqueous terrain of Lake Jinro. The party will most likely arrive here via boat, along with the Amalthea Stonecraft herself, who they must escort to the precipice for an esoteric ritual of rejuvenation.

Once the characters reach the Lake Jinro Shrine, they must:

- Ascend the switchback path to the island's precipice, where the eponymous Lunar Shrine awaits.
- Help the Aster storyteller Amalthea Stonecraft complete her ritual of rejuvenation.
- Survive a surprise attack from a Juvenile Garganthus, which has made its way to the surface of Ember from the Pathways deep below.

## Illumination

Illumination here is mostly dependent on the time of day, since the location is fully open to the sky above. Additionally, when the six Altar Stones along the switchback path have been lit, each one of them radiates bright light for 10 feet and dim light for an additional 10 feet. And when the Lunar Shrine itself is active, each of its six menhirs radiates dim light for 10 feet.

## Terrain

Most of the terrain here is treated as normal terrain. The waters of Lake Jinro are typically calm, with an extremely slow current that laps the shore with lazy regularity.

## Elevation

Other than the recessed burrow of the Garganthus Tunnel and the scant areas beneath the boughs of the island's handful of trees, the island is open to the sky above. The hilly landmass winds its way upwards a total of 60 feet in elevation at the island's precipice (where the Shrine itself is located), with a 30-foot high halfway mark along the switchback path at the far eastern side.

Characters who wish to climb the steep rocky hillsides must succeed on a **Athletics (DC 15)** check to scale the sheer and craggy earth. Critical failure results in the the character suffering a Serious Fall. Any character who fails this check can repeat it as many times as they'd like.
