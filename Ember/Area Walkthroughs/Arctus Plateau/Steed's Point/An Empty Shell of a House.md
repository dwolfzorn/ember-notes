# An Empty Shell of a House

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> An old damp bed and empty fire pit are all that remain under what was once a home, its roof now ripped and torn away. Deep scratches are embedded in its walls and dried liquids of an unknown substance are scattered across the ground, some smeared by what appears to be footprints.

Characters can search what remains of the house or investigate the scratches and dried liquid on the ground.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Remains
>
> Any character that succeeds on a **Wilderness (DC 15)** check know that the dried liquids on the ground are a mix of blood and saliva. Most of what is here is years old and has mostly faded away, but the smeared blood in the footprints is more recent.
>
> - Relevant Insights: Characters with **Knowledge: Monsters** or **Knowledge: Subterranea** gains **+2 Boons**.
> - **Critical Success**: By the look of them, these footprints belong to a [[Jurtak]] of some type.

Searching the house itself yields some further information and some curious items.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Searching the House
>
> Any character that succeeds on an **Awareness (DC 16)** check after taking the time to search the house finds an intricate [[Woven Map, Steed's Point]], a [[Heart-Shaped Clasp]] under a now-moldy pillow, and a feather on top of the ashes in the fire pit.
>
> With a successful **Wilderness (DC 16)** check, characters know that the feather belongs to a [[Gore Bird]] and that the feather is more recent than the cold ash of the pit and likely fell from above.
