# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The [[Steed's Point]] Area Map depicts the center of the ruined village that is [[Steed's Point]], and is featured in [[A Dying Art]] in the [[Thorny Predicaments]] Quest.
>
> - The Area Map depicts a single level with some minor areas of elevation.
> - Once a lively settlement, monster attacks and encroaching flora, and other dangerous fauna has left the location a ruin.
> - The party arrives in the [[Steed's Point Fields]] area.

## Gameplay Details

The areas of Steed's Point have the following features unless specified otherwise in the text.

### Levels & Elevation

The Steed's Point Scene features a total of 2 vertical Levels of elevation, including:

- **Ground**: The majority of the settlement is set to an elevation of 0 and has 20-foot-high ceilings inside buildings unless stated otherwise.
- **Roofs**: The tops of the buildings in this settlement are set to an elevation of +20 feet.

### Sound

The area around Steed's Point is generally quiet, with only the sounds of nature and local wildlife present.

### Illumination

As most of Steed's Point is open to the sky above, its illumination is dependent on the time of day in the area, and at night there is no additional lighting present in the abandoned town.

### Terrain

The terrain varies throughout Steed's Point, and any specific locations where Difficult Terrain exists will be pointed out specifically.

### Inhabitants

- [[Kali Andrella]] lives in [[Unknown]] and is the last living inhabitant of the settlement.

- [[Rattletrap, the Rickety Man]] can be found in the [[Steed's Point Fields]] working at all hours.

### Enemies

Hostile Fauna

- [[Gore Bird Ravage]] can be found in several locations around the settlement.

Jurtak can be encountered lurking and patrolling through the town at night. The most common variants of them are:

- [[Jurtak Warrior]]
- [[Jurtak Hunter]]
- [[Jurtak Geomancer]]
