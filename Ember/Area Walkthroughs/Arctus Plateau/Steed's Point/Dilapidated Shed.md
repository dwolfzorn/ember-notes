# Dilapidated Shed

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Like most of Steed's Point, this ramshackle shed near the southern edge of town has seen better days. Only the rear third of its roof seems to remain somewhat intact, and you spy a rusty array of farm equipment scattered about the front end, which hangs wide open in the stark absence of a wall or door.

The party can inspect the shed's interior, where weather-worn farm tools might distract them from noticing the two Jurtak Warriors who lurk in a filth pit at the far end.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Shed
>
> The missing roof of the Dilapidated Shed reveals a few pieces of aged farming equipment, including:
>
> - A sun-bleached wooden **Cart**.
> - 2 rusty Pitchforks, the handles of which could serve as viable if Shoddy Quality [[Spear]].
> - A 60 ft. length of [[Rope]].
>
> #### The Filth Pit
>
> This pit of filth located at the eastern end of the shed plunges 5 feet into the earth, and provides Complete Cover for any creatures who attempt to hide there.
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 12, Passive)** while examining the shallow pit is able to find the desiccated remains of an unfortunate Steed's Point citizen among the filth. Roll twice on the [[Corpse Loot]] table and once on the [[Arcturian Trinkets]] table.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Lurking Danger
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** while examining the shed's interior is able to spot the 2 Jurtak Warriors hiding down in the Filth Pit at the rear.

> [!abstract] Jurtak Warrior
> **[[Jurtak Warrior]]**
>
> Level 4 · Jurtak Berserker
>
> ![Jurtak Warrior](../../../assets/image081.webp)
>
> You behold a lean, six-eyed saurian creature, its body clad in fragments of bone and its scales glinting in the dim light. The acrid scent of poison tinges the air, dripping from the bone blade held in its clawed hands. Its long, semi-prehensile tail moves with a predator's anticipation, and a forked tongue flicks across twisted lips as its eyes fix upon you with a predatory malice.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Jurtak Tactics
>
> If possible, the 2 Jurtak Warriors that hide here wait for the party to pass through the Vine Path south of the shed before springing their attack. If discovered by the party, these two Jurtak will immediately engage the characters in combat.
>
> The Jurtak Warriors will utilize their [[Acid Spit]] ability before closing in on the party to fight in melee with their [[Glaive]].
>
> If the Jurtak Warriors are far away from the characters when combat begins, they will most hurl their [[Javelin]] while spending action points on Movement to close the distance as fast as possible.
>
> Both will use their [[Jurtak Poison]] as soon as possible, and reapply the poison during combat when most convenient.
>
> If **Broken** or **Weakened** the Jurtak will break and flee.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Nearby Reinforcements
>
> The Jurtak Warrior and Jurtak Hunter located in the [[Southern Clearing]] do not rush to join combat if it breaks out here. Instead, they wait for the party to potentially spring the trap concealed along the [[Vine Path]].
>
> If they can avoid getting spotted by the characters, the 2 Jurtak Warriors that hide here at the Dilapidated Shed also wait for the party to spring the trap or engage their Jurtak allies before closing in to flank the characters in combat.
