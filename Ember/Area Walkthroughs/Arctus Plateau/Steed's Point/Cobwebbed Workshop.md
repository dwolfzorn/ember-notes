# Cobwebbed Workshop

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The battered door of this cobwebbed workshop has been torn from its hinges and lays flat on the dusty ground. A scattered assortment of tools and equipment is strewn about the one-room shop in complete disarray, and most of the furniture — like Steed's Point itself — is demolished and destroyed.

The characters can search this cobwebbed workshop for evidence of what happened to Steed's Point during its mysterious downfall.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Workshop
>
> Signs of distress adorn this dusty, cobwebbed workshop, which appears to be littered with splintered debris and evidence of a violent Jurtak incursion.
>
> A few pieces of equipment remain scattered about the room, including:
>
> - A cracked and rusted [[Lantern]].
> - A chipped [[Hand Axe]].
> - A sickle so rusted as to be unusable.
>
> A character with **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** notices two distinct sets of blood stains on the wooden floor, including the crimson blood of humanoids and another dark ichor.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Wilderness (DC 13)** check is able to notice or locate the remnants of several clawed footprints from a curious bipedal creature, along with claw and spear marks on the door and interior.
>
> - **Critical Success**:The claws are recognizable as those of a saurian, and the dark ichor that stains the floor (seen now, if it wasn't noticed before) most likely comes from some aberration or subterranean monstrosity.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 14)** check can deduce the rough series of events that occurred here:
>
> - The people that lived and worked here took a stand against some other kind of clawed, bipedal creature — which forced its way in through the front door. There may have been more than one of these creatures.
> - Combat ensued between the two parties, and blood was spilled on both sides. Based on the disarray and the proportions of blood, the humanoids of Steed's Point did not fare well during the assault.
>
> - **Knowledge: Crafts**: The character easily recognizes this building as the workshop of some artisan, and gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
