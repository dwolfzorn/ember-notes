# Old Damaged Bridge

A Drowned Vines trap hides below a wooden bridge.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A battered wooden bridge spans the rocky, half-dry basin below. The planks and beams are old and weathered, and despite the rather extensive damage in places, it still looks sturdy enough to cross without much concern.
>
> Over the sides, the craggy walls of stone descend to a rocky bed where a few pools of stagnant water collect.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Interactable: The Old Bridge
>
> The bridge features an [[Area Maps]], and can be toggled between the solid and collapsed state via the interaction tool in the Scene Controls interface on the left side of the screen.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Dangerous Crossing
>
> Acharacter with **Awareness (DC 16, Passive)** while studying the bridge notices that it has begun to fail at the middle, and can cross the bridge without triggering the collapse.
>
> An unaware character that crosses the middle of the bridge must make a successful **Athletics (DC 16)** to move to safety or fall 30 feet into the ravine as the bridge collapses under them. A character that falls into the ravine suffers a **Sudden Fall (Hazard 9, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)**.
>
> Crossing the bridge on foot after it has collapsed requires a character to get a running start and make a successfull **Athletics (DC 14)** to jump over the gap and land on the other side.
>
> Climbing into or out of the ravine is trivial and does not require anything but the necessary movement to scale the craggy walls. As there are ample handholds and ledges to take advantage of, no climbing equipment is necessary.
