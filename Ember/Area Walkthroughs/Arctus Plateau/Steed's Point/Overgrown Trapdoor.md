# Overgrown Trapdoor

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You spot a sun-bleached and weather-worn trap door beneath the overgrown tangle of wild brambles here, large enough to grant ingress to a cellar or basement.

The wooden trapdoor here reveals a Secret Passage that connects this remote, northwestern corner of Steed's Point to a Secret Passage Entrance south of Kali's Cottage.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Trapdoor
>
> The wooden trapdoor here is made from sturdy, weather-work oak bound with rusted iron hardware. It is locked but not trapped.
>
> Any character who has already visited the [[Steed's Smithy]] who also succeeds on either a**Awareness (DC 12)** check while examining the trapdoor can accurately speculate that the hardware was crafted in the Smithy's forge.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Opening the Trapdoor
>
> The trapdoor can be unlocked by any character that succeeds on a **Stealth (DC 14)** check using a .
>
> Alternately, the trapdoor can be opened with brute force. A successful **Athletics (DC 18)** check is required to pry the iron hardware from the weather-worn wood.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Opening the trapdoor reveals a rickety wooden ladder that stretches down into the darkness below. Remarkably, it lacks the musty odors that linger about the rest of Steed's Point, offering a scent of cool, dry earthiness instead.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### The Passage to Kali's Cottage
>
> Any character that chooses to navigate the length of the Secret Passage can use their entire turn to move from one end to the other. At the beginning of their next turn, the character emerges at either the Secret Passage Entrance south of [[Kali's Cottage]] or the Overgrown Trapdoor here.
