# Southern Clearing

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This rough clearing is marked by the presence of two weathered chicken coops and a dead-end path of loosely-cobbled stone, but is otherwise unremarkable.

A [[Jurtak Warrior]] and a [[Jurtak Hunter]] lay in wait here, ready to ambush any interlopers who might pass through this way. Note that if these creatures were fought after the trap was triggered in the [[Vine Path]], this encounter obviously does not occur.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Lurking Danger
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 12, Passive)** while examining the shed's interior is able to spot a Jurtak Warrior and a Jurtak Hunter hiding on opposite sides of the clearing.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Jurtak Tactics
>
> If possible, the Jurtak Warrior and Jurtak Hunter that hide here wait for the party to get caught in the Vine Path trap before springing their attack. If the party bypasses the agrimage trap, the Jurtak will continue to hide until the party has moved further into the clearing before attacking from behind.
>
> The Jurtak Warriors utilize their [[Acid Spit]] ability before closing in on the party to fight in melee with their [[Glaive]]. If for some reason they cannot reach a target, they will engage from a safe distance by hurling their [[Javelin]].
>
> Meanwhile, the Jurtak Hunter will take aim with its [[Longbow]], targeting prioritizing targets that are caught in the Vine Path trap or who are already engaged in melee combat with one or more Jurtak Warriors.
>
> All Jurtak start combat with their first weapon attack coated in [[Jurtak Poison]]. They reapply it before attacking if they can spare the action points to do so, and aren't being engaged by combatants in close range.
>
> If **Broken** or **Weakened** the Jurtak will break and flee.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Nearby Reinforcements
>
> If they've avoided detection and have not already engaged the party in the [[Vine Path]], the 2 Jurtak Warriors that hide nearby in the [[Dilapidated Shed]] will close in to flank the party from behind when the Jurtak Warrior and Jurtak Hunter here in the Southern Clearing initiate combat.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Clearing
>
> If the characters take time to search the chicken coops along the eastern edge of the clearing, they'll discover a forgotten [[Silver Flask]] of [[Arcturian Liquor]] tucked away in the chicken coop to the north.
