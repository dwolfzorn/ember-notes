# Abandoned Home

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Leaves cover the floor of this unassuming house, which seems relatively unaffected by the ravages of time and weather compared to most of the village. While most of the structure remains unharmed, the dust-covered furniture is splintered and overturned, and a quick glance suggests the occupants that once lived here were normal Arcturian folks with normal Arcturian sensibilities.

The characters can search this abandoned home for signs of what happened to this remote, overgrown village.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Home
>
> Characters who search the house are able to locate a few details that suggest what life was once like here, including:
>
> - A table, still set with empty dishes and cutlery for a family of three.
> - Shelves along the northern wall stocked with kitchenware and cutlery.
> - A splintered bookshelf, toppled and windswept.
>
> Any character who succeeds on an **Awareness (DC 12)** check while searching the house is able to find a [[Moth-Eaten Diary]] among the scattered detritus that once decorated the splintered bookshelf.
