# The Ersatz Bridge

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The splintered wall of some Steed's Point dwelling spans the small ravine here as a rickety ersatz bridge. Although the timbers are weathered and worn, it looks safe enough to cross the expanse without concern of hazard or hindrance.

The characters can cross the splintered bridge with little effort, although a surprise attack from Jurtak raiders awaits them on the other side.

The small ravine beneath the bridge is roughly 15 feet deep, and is lined with the splintered, dust-covered debris of a demolished house.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Bridge
>
> Any character who succeeds on a**Wilderness (DC 13)** check is able to identify evidence that suggests this hastily-erected bridge was placed here following an earthquake or other natural disaster, a testament to the tenacity of the village's citizens.
>
> - **Knowledge**: Characters with **Knowledge: Crafts** gains **+2 Boons**.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Jurtak Attack!
>
> Once most or all of the party members have crossed the bridge to the northern landing, a group of enemies scurries out of the ravine from their unseen hiding places, including: a [[Jurtak Warrior]], a [[Jurtak Hunter]], and a [[Jurtak Geomancer]].
>
> Without the benefit of spells or other special abilities, these Jurtak cannot be detected by the party until they emerge from their hiding places.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Jurtak Tactics
>
> The Jurtak Warrior and Jurtak Hunter will attempt to focus their attacks against the party member with the least amount of hit points.
>
> The Jurtak Geomancer will target another spellcaster, if possible, using its own Innate Spellcasting.
