# Secondary Storage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The room may have held something once, but it has since been stripped down to nothing more than a set of bare walls and smooth, solid ground. The entryways ahead and behind you are ordinary wooden archways, and a similar archway to the south has been filled in with a thick gray metal.

This room never held much while the mine was active, serving as a place for people to rest or store temporary supplies. The Aburyx controlling the mine used the passageway behind the room to access the power source and control switch to the Device, hiding it behind a false metallic wall on the south of the room.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring Secondary Storage
>
> A simple search reveals that the earth here is not furrowed or disturbed. Unlike the rest of the mine, this area appears untouched by the Vorg. Additionally, the metal-filled opening to the south appears to be unique and of very recent construction.
>
> Digging into the earth or otherwise reshaping the earth with magic reveals thick coils of wiring, which prevent the Vorg from burrowing through the area.
>
> Any character who examines the wiring and makes a successful **Science (DC 14)** check can tell that the wiring originates from the south, in the direction of the metal wall.
>
> - **Knowledge: Machines**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
>
> #### Investigating the Wall
>
> Any character with **Knowledge: Crafts** who examines the metallic wall realizes that it is not actually metal, but a thick resin with a metallic sheen.
>
> A character who knocks on the wall and makes a successful **Science (DC 12)** check can tell that there is empty space beyond it.
>
> - **Talent: Smithing Novice**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
>
> The wall can be melted over the course of 5 minutes by applying heat to its surface. [[Metal Crumbler]] has no effect. Alternatively, the resin wall has AC 18, 18 Hit Points, and immunity to Poison and Psychic damage.
>
> When the wall is removed, read or paraphrase the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The wall gives way to reveal a small cluster of gleaming brass and gold mechanisms made of intricately formed segments and sections. The most prominent part of its design is a sliding switch below a glowing glass orb that pulses with yellow light.
>
> There are no labels, no instructions, and no indication of what this controls or influences.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Hidden Switch
>
> This switch controls the flow of power to the Device in [[The Hub]]. Flipping this switch changes the Device's state to **Depowered**, preventing it from activating.
>
> Once the switch is flipped and the Device is **Depowered**, read or paraphrase the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The glass orb over the switch goes dark. You hear a sudden high-pitched whirring hum that rapidly descends in pitch as if diving off a cliff, followed by a loud thumping noise to the east of you and an almost total silence.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Skittering Skithers
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 12, Passive)** hears a slight whirring in the silence like the fluttering of wings. The sound also comes from the east.
