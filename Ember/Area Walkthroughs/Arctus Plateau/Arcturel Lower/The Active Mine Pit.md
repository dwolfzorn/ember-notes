# The Active Mine Pit

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Veins of iron ore run through the cavern walls, but the pickaxes used to extract the ore and the cart used to haul it have been tossed aside and left behind. The pit is otherwise empty, though there are furrows and pockmarks in the ground that seem to hint at some recent activity.

When the mine was active, this was the most recently excavated work area.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### The Device Activates
>
> If the Device in [[The Hub]] is **Powered**, it activates as the characters enter the area. Roll on the [[Lower Arcturel Mine Effects]] table for the action taken by the device.
>
> #### Finding the Source
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check can confirm that they heard no sound before the strange effect came through the mine. If they previously heard any sound in another room, it is absent here.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Vorg Nest
>
> When the characters enter this area or cause a lot of noise, roll `[[/gmroll 1d20]]`. On a result of 10 or higher, all [[Vorg]] on the Area Map move as far as possible toward the characters. Then, if one or more Vorg are within 10 feet of a character, they emerge from the earth and attack.
>
> Refer to [[Area Overview]] for the Vorgs' tactics.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Active Mine Pit
>
> A simple search reveals jagged, uneven furrows in the earth.
>
> Any character who examines these furrows and makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check can tell that they were caused by a burrowing or tunneling creature.
>
> - **Encountered Vorg:** The character automatically succeeds on this check if they already encountered a Vorg.
> - **Knowledge: Subterranea**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 16, Passive)** discovers a fist-sized chunk of unprocessed iron ore worth  **1** on the ground.
