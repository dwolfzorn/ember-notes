# Wrestful Repairs

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> In addition to half-broken machines and tools, various bits and bobs are scattered around the shop. It seems as if many repair orders have piled up in recent days. Notes are scattered around the shop, many with the word "Completed" on them, which has often been amended to read "To Be Completed." Toward the front of the shop, a sign next to a doorway reads "Please put items to be repaired here. Thank you! — Rennerie."

The shop has a number of spare tools, replacement parts, and a set of papers on a bench labeled "Repair Log." Locals come in and out regularly, dropping off and picking up items freely.

During the [[Unwelcome Diversions]] Event, the party can investigate the repair shop and its supplies. Refer to the Event text for more details.

During the [[Scene of the Crime]] Event, the party can speak with a potential witness as he's exiting Wrestful Repairs. Refer to the Event text for more details.
