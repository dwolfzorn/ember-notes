# Level 3 Mine Office

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This office resembles one found in any standard mining concern. Papers sit in the middle of the desk in relatively neat piles, and the books around the office are organized in an orderly fashion on nearby shelves. Only a slight hint of dust in the air gives the sense that this place hasn't been used in some time.

When the mine is active, this room serves as the on-site office for the foreman.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Office
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - The books on the shelves are primarily twine-bound records from previous years about ore quotas and other mine-related business.
> - A note on the desk: [[Troubling Reports]].
