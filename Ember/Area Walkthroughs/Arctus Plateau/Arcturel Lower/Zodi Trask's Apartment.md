# Zodi Trask's Apartment

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The front room of this ramshackle apartment appears quite rough around the edges. Splintered floorboards lie strewn across the floor in various states of repair, and the place is sparsely decorated with timeworn and mismatched furniture. A general feeling of neglect hangs heavy here, as if the resident of this haphazard suite is rarely home to account for cleanliness.
>
> A scattered array of mining tools and dusty work garb betray this place as the residence of one of Lower Arcturel's subterranean laborers. Meanwhile, two closed doors on the rear wall lead to more private chambers, no doubt equally as shabby as the front.

This three-room apartment serves as the living space for [[Zodi Trask]], a gregarious Kiska quarryman who serves as both a trusted head foreman for House Cevher and a local leader for the people of [[Arcturel]].

Outside of Event-specific encounters, Zodi is only likely to be found at home during his restful hours of 12:00am to 7:00am — opting to spend most of his time working on nearby job sites or spending time with his peers in Upper Arcturel's [[Rallyhome]] tavern.

A smug countenance presides upon his mustachioed face, which appears to be marked by a few scars from some violent childhood scuffle or accident.

During the [[Unwelcome Diversions]] Event, the party can speak with Zodi in his apartment after adventuring in the Level 3 Mine. Refer to the Event text for more details.
