# Back Office

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The back office of this mine has seen better days — beneath the stone floor, patches of ground peek through, and clumps of dirt are scattered across the floor. A few sets of tools are scattered between crates and ropes labeled "Level 3," but otherwise there is no real sign of life.

This room is attached to the main mining office and usually contains extra storage and tools.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Back Office
>
> A simple search reveals that the room looks as if it has been damaged and ransacked.
>
> Any character who examines the damage to the floor and makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check can tell that it was caused by something that burrowed up from below.
>
> - **Encountered Vorg:** The character automatically succeeds on this check if they already encountered a Vorg.
> - **Knowledge: Subterranea**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Crates at the back of the office can be opened with a successful **Athletics (DC 14)** check. Most have nothing of interest in them, but one holds a few minor items. Roll twice on [[Arcturian Trinkets]] for the items found.
