# The Hub

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A tall metallic cylinder sits in the middle of the room. It is covered with small metal spheres that appear fixed in place, but as you enter the room, the small metal objects detach themselves with a whir and turn toward you, eyes glowing and metallic claws extended.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### The Device
>
> The Device has AC 20, 30 Hit Points, immunity to Poison, Psychic, and Radiant damage, and a &reference[damage threshold] of 10.
>
> The Device is either **Powered** or **Depowered**. It is **Powered** unless the characters use the hidden switch in [[Secondary Storage]], at which point it becomes **Depowered**.
>
> While **Powered**, it creates a [[Lower Arcturel Mine Effects]] at the end of each round of combat. Once all results of this table have been drawn, reset the table.
>
> If damaged, the closest [[Skither]] in the area will redirect its attention to the Device, spending each of its turns restoring `[[/gmroll 1d6]]` Hit Points to the Device at the expense of all other actions; only one Skither can restore Hit Points to the Device at a time. The Skithers will ignore damage to the Device if the Device is **Depowered**.

> [!abstract] Skither
> **[[Skither]]**
>
> Level 2 · Skither Servitor
>
> ![Skither](../../../assets/image051.webp)
>
> Small and fast-moving, this creature seems at first like something to ignore, little more than a piece of trash with jagged wings crudely shaped to look like a insect. The creature's solitary eye and claw-like appendages seem to surge with fiery radiance as it strikes, leaving streaks of amber light in its wake.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Skither Tactics
>
> The characters are @Condition[Unaware] thanks to the Skithers' [[False Appearance]] talent.
>
> At the start of combat, the 4 [[Skither]] will surround the frailest-looking character.
>
> Over the course of combat, the Skithers will prioritize the following actions and abilities:
>
> - In melee, the Skithers will take advantage of their [[Pack Hunter]] talent to maximize the damage of their [[Talons]].
> - Once **Weakened** or **Broken**, a Skither will position itself to maximize the damage of its [[Radiant Death Burst]], then [[Sacrifice Self]].
>
> The battle ends when the Skithers are slain and the Device is **Depowered** or destroyed.

Once the battle ends, the characters can examine the Device.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Investigating the Device
>
> Any character who examines the Device finds a symbol of an explosion surrounded by patterns of wear on the metal — the Skithers were particularly focused on this section of the Device.
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** discovers a marking scratched onto the underside of a panel: "Wandren."
