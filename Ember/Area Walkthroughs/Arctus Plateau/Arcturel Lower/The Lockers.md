# The Lockers

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Several of the metal lockers in what appears to be a changing and storage area hang open, but a quick glance at the belongings reveals them to be nothing special — mining clothes, gloves, personal goods. While most are either ajar or unlocked, a couple of the lockers have remained firmly closed, their contents a mystery.

This locker area is generally used to store daily clothes and personal belongings during the workday.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Vorg Nest
>
> When the characters enter this area or cause a lot of noise, roll `[[/gmroll 1d20]]`. On a result of 10 or higher, all [[Vorg]] on the Area Map move as far as possible toward the characters. Then, if one or more Vorg are within 10 feet of a character, they emerge from the earth and attack.
>
> Refer to [[Area Overview]] for the Vorgs' tactics.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Lockers
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - A heavily damaged book.
> - Filthy clothing.
>
> Any character who breaks into the closed lockers with a successful **Stealth (DC 14)** check using [[Lockpicks]] or a successful **Athletics (DC 17)** check discovers loose coins totaling  **1**.
>
> - **Using a Tool:** The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check if they use an appropriate tool, such as a crowbar or pickaxe.
