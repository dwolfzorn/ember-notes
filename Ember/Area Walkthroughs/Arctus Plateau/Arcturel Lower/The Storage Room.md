# The Storage Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The remains of some alchemical experiment — shattered test tubes and vials, abandoned papers and beakers — are scattered across two tables and the floor. Whatever happened here, it has left an acrid scent in the air that seems to claw at your senses with each step.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Toxic Air
>
> The air in this room is toxic — any character who enters the room must make a successful **Medicine (DC 14)** or **Wilderness (DC 14)** check to take precautions against the toxins, or they are **Poisoned** until they leave the room.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Vorg Nest
>
> When the characters enter this area or cause a lot of noise, roll `[[/gmroll 1d20]]`. On a result of 10 or higher, all [[Vorg]] on the Area Map move as far as possible toward the characters. Then, if one or more Vorg are within 10 feet of a character, they emerge from the earth and attack.
>
> Refer to [[Area Overview]] for the Vorgs' tactics.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Storage Room
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - A pile of mining ore dust.
> - A rancid-smelling vial coated inside with grayish dust.
> - A thick, viscous substance.
>
> Any character who examines the grayish dust and makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 14)** check identifies it as [[Concentrated Ore Dust]].
>
> - **Talent: Alchemy Novice**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Investigated Waterborne Distillery:** The character automatically succeeds on this check if they investigated the distillery and discovered the [[Suspicious Karal]].
>
> Any character who examines the thick, viscous substance and makes a successful **Science (DC 14)** check can identify it as [[Metal Crumbler]].
>
> - **Talent: Alchemy Novice**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Investigated Wrestful Repairs:** The character automatically succeeds on this check if they already recovered a sample of the [[Metal Crumbler]] from Wrestful Repairs.
>
> The substances can also be identified by [[Arvoda's Elixirs]].
>
> #### The Hidden Passageway
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 16, Passive)** while examining the northwest corner of the room discovers an odd trick of the light — a hallmark of an illusion.
>
> Any character who uses **Illusory Sense**, or who uses some sort of anti-magic item or spell sees the passageway instead of the wall. Additionally, physical interaction with the image reveals it to be an illusion, since things can pass through it.
>
> This passageway leads to a rack of barrels. A small gap allows Tiny creatures to pass through without trouble. Larger creatures can be accommodated by spending 5 minutes pushing the barrels out of the way. Beyond the barrels lies the [[Waterborne Distillery]].
