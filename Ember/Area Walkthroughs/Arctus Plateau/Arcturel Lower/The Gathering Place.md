# The Gathering Place

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Once, this might have been a place where people gathered before heading deeper into the mine to put on equipment, grab a tool, or sit down for a shared meal during a break. Now it has clearly been left in a hurry, with tools lying wherever they were dropped and chairs and benches overturned.

This room is used as a gathering place for those who work in the mines — a place to store tools, eat meals, and socialize.

Once the entire party has entered the area, a wind blows from deeper inside the mine:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Suddenly, a whooshing sound fills the air and a strong wind blows through the room from deeper within the mine, knocking over a cup on one of the tables and toppling one of the remaining standing chairs before battering against you where you stand.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### A Stiff Wind
>
> Characters must make a successful **Athletics (DC 15)** or be pushed backward 20 feet. If this effect pushes a character into a wall or gate, they suffer **Terrain Impact (Hazard 6, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)**.
>
> - **Critical Failure**: The character also falls **Prone** and suffers **A Hard Fall (Hazard 3, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)**.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Locating the Source
>
> The strong wind came from within the mine, but beyond that, its origin is unclear.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 11)** check can tell that the gust of wind was not a natural phenomenon.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The character can tell from the direction that the gust of wind originated from somewhere near the center of the mine.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 14)** check senses that there was a magical component to the gust of wind, and that it was [[Luxarum]] in nature.
>
> - **Path: Lightsworn**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
