# Waterborne Distillery

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster Note
>
> During the [[Unwelcome Diversions]] Event, the entrance to the distillery is locked. See [[Unwelcome Diversions]] for more details.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The smell of earthy liquor fills the air, emanating from the barrels around the room. A sign above a stack of barrels in the corner reads, "Do not disturb until fully aged. That includes sips. Yes, we mean you," while another, scrawled on the divider between the front and back rooms, shows the full process for creating karal, which includes water from the sinkhole and several locally grown plants and grains.

This room holds typical supplies for a distillery — empty and full barrels, distilling pipes, and a cooking fire.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Locked Side Room
>
> The door to the side storage room is locked. It can be unlocked with a successful `[[/skill sleightofhand 15 tool=thief]]`; alternatively, it can be bashed open with a **Athletics (DC 22)** check.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Side Room Hidden Passageway
>
> Any character who searches the side room and makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check discovers air flowing from a small gap between the barrels — a passageway lies beyond.
>
> The gap is large enough for Tiny creatures to pass through without trouble. Larger creatures can be accommodated by spending 5 minutes rearranging the barrels.
>
> #### Illusory Wall
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 16, Passive)** while examining the southeast corner of the passageway discovers an odd trick of the light — a hallmark of an illusion.
>
> Any character who uses **Illusory Sense**, or who uses some sort of anti-magic item or spell sees the passageway instead of the wall. Additionally, physical interaction with the image reveals it to be an illusion, since things can pass through it.
>
> Behind this illusory wall lies [[The Storage Room]].

### [[Unwelcome Diversions]]

During the [[Unwelcome Diversions]] Event, the party can investigate the distillery and its recent batch of karal. Refer to the Event text for more details.
