# Arvoda's Elixirs

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Bottles, basins, and jars packed with a myriad of curious substances crowd the tables of this modest potion shop. Among the clutter, a lithe Ashka woman dressed in Arcturian fineries leans over a handful of herbs, studying the ingredients through a gold-rimmed magnifying glass. Without moving, she glances your way and greets you with a warm, matronly welcome.
>
> > Welcome to Arvoda's Elixirs, home of the finest alchemical solutions this side of the Sinkhole Depths. Tell me, how can I help you in these troubling times?

This edifice serves as the workshop and storefront of the water mage Emelyn Arvoda, and provides the denizens of Arcturel with useful (if overpriced) alchemical resources.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with Emelyn
>
> **Emelyn Arvoda (Neutral Good, Arcturian Ashka, she/her)**
>
> This shrewd-yet-matronly water mage crafts scrolls, potions, and other magic items for travelers of the lightless depths, but always keeps her eye on the bottom line.
>
> Emelyn Arvoda is a pleasant (if somewhat mercenary) proprietor, always willing to help someone, so long as they either buy something or pay her for her expert services. Emelyn is known to occasionally lower prices for those who appeal to her industrious sensibilities, but she doesn't believe in giving something for nothing.
>
> Conversation topics that Emelyn is casually willing to discuss include (but are not limited to) the following:
>
> - Details about her (overpriced) inventory and services.
> - Her history as a mage and potion-maker.
> - Information about Arcturel, including details on The Dives and its colorful residents.
> - General rumors about the Arctus Plateau and current affairs.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 13)** check can readily sense the general air of no-nonsense honesty that Emelyn champions in her daily life, and has no reason to suspect that she has something to hide from them (other than the hard-won secrets of the potion trade).
>
> #### Emelyn's Wares
>
> Arvoda's Elixirs is usually stocked with various alchemical supplies, potions, and other consumables of different rarities — all of which are sold at a 20% markup. These wares include multiple of the following:
>
> - [[Alchemical Silver]]
> - [[Antitoxin]]
> - [[Everbloom]]
> - [[Oil of Slipperiness]]
> - [[Potion of Alertness]]
> - [[Healing Elixir]]
> - [[Potion of Resistance]]
> - [[Potion of Water Breathing]]
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Diplomacy (DC 17)** check is able to convince Emelyn to lower her prices to standard rates.
