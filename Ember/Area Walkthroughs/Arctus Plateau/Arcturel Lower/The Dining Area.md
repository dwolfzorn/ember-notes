# The Dining Area

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> On the two long tables in this impromptu dining area, food has hardened onto plates, cups are filled with nothing more than residue, and cards are scattered on the table from an abandoned game. Beyond them, tools are scattered across the floor — some old and rusty, others sharp enough that they could be used as a weapon.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Vorg Nest
>
> When the characters enter this area or cause a lot of noise, roll `[[/gmroll 1d20]]`. On a result of 10 or higher, all [[Vorg]] on the Area Map move as far as possible toward the characters. Then, if one or more Vorg are within 10 feet of a character, they emerge from the earth and attack.
>
> Refer to [[Area Overview]] for the Vorgs' tactics.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Dining Area
>
> A simple search reveals that the playing cards have been punctured with holes that resemble a sinkhole, and the four suits have been drawn over to represent House Cevher, the Waterborne family, the Silver Beam Consortium, and the Lamplighters. The face cards of each suit showcase local luminaries (including Zodi Trask).
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 16, Passive)** can locate all of the scattered cards and complete a unique set of playing cards.
>
> Any character with **Knowledge: Trade** discovers a [[Sturdy Pickaxe]] among the discarded tools.
>
> Any character with **Knowledge: Warfare** discovers a [[Sharpened Pickaxe]] sticking out of the rock wall.
