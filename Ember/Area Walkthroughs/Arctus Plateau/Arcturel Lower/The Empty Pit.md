# The Empty Pit

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The ground of the cavern is rough and pockmarked, with clumps of dirt and rock spread haphazardly as if flung in all directions. Between them, pieces of metal from carts and other mining equipment lie scattered, separated into collections of pipes, sockets, gears, and wheels.

This cavern was once a mining pit, but has been largely mined out and instead was most recently used to store vehicles and other mining equipment, all of which has been torn apart since the mine was abandoned.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### The Device Activates
>
> If the Device in [[The Hub]] is **Powered**, it activates as the characters enter the area. Roll on the [[Lower Arcturel Mine Effects]] table for the action taken by the device.
>
> #### Finding the Source
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check hears a clicking sound immediately north of them as the Device activates.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Vorg Nest
>
> When the characters enter this area or cause a lot of noise, roll `[[/gmroll 1d20]]`. On a result of 10 or higher, all [[Vorg]] on the Area Map move as far as possible toward the characters. Then, if one or more Vorg are within 10 feet of a character, they emerge from the earth and attack.
>
> Refer to [[Area Overview]] for the Vorgs' tactics.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Empty Pit
>
> A simple search reveals jagged, uneven furrows in the earth. The equipment in this room has also been heavily damaged; the machinery appears to have been deliberately disassembled.
>
> Any character who examines the furrows and makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check can tell that they were caused by a burrowing or tunneling creature.
>
> - **Encountered Vorg:** The character automatically succeeds on this check if they already encountered a Vorg.
> - **Knowledge: Subterranea**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character who examines the damaged equipment and makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 12)** check can tell that the damage was caused by a creature with metal pincers or claws.
>
> - **Encountered Skither:** The character automatically succeeds on this check if they already encountered a Skither.
> - **Knowledge: Subterranea**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
