# Gameplay Details

The following details describe and modify the mechanical traversal of the Bastion Apex area map.

### Illumination

In the center of the Bastion Apex is an enormous orb of light, described in TODO. The Light of Lantyr creates bright light in an 80 foot radius and dim light for an additional 80 feet beyond (160 feet total). Areas of the scene which are not exposed to this light directly are treated as dim light.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Searing Light
>
> Any character which moves within 10 feet of the Light of Lantyr or begins their turn there while in initiative order must make a `[[/save constitution 15]]` saving throw or suffer `[[/damage 1d8 radiant]]` damage or half damage with a successful save.

#### Work In Progress

The mechanics caused by Searing Light will eventually be automated using Scene Regions, but for now must be evaluated manually by the Gamemaster.

### Terrain

The surfaces of the Bastion Apex are treated as normal terrain unless otherwise specified.

### Elevation

The Bastion Apex exists 500 feet above ground level. This spire rises far above the other buildings and fortifications of the Primordial Bastion.

The fortified walls and crennelations surrounding the apex perimeter are 20 feet tall, and the great Light of Lantyr hovers 30 feet in the air above the surface of the Apex.

The stairs on each side of the apex descend 20 feet to platforms which open to the spire interior which is not depicted in this Scene.
