# Void Bridge

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A large stone bridge stretches across this room, with a deep channel carved into the floor in its center. On the far side, a stone bowl can be seen in the gloom. Looking down below the bridge, you initially see only darkness and a deep black void, but as your eyes adjust, you notice that the void itself is glittering with a multitude of small sparkling lights.

Once the party reaches the middle of the bridge, they are attacked by the Abyssal Eyes lurking in the darkness.

>
> Level 1 · Unknown Unknown

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### **The Eyes See You**
>
> Four Abyssal Eyes are waiting in ambush, having hidden themselves in the darkest, most distant corners of the room. The party cannot detect the Abyssal Eyes until the floating monstrosities choose to reveal themselves — which they patiently wait to do until the party reaches the center of the bridge.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 19 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 17)** notices the creatures just as they begin their assault; all others are &Reference[surprise]{Surprised} at the start of combat.
>
> #### **Abyssal Eye Tactics**
>
> During combat, the Abyssal Eyes will:
>
> - Position themselves to use [[Eye Blast]] and attempt to push players off the bridge.
> - Avoid moving closer to the bridge itself, in order to stay out of range of melee attacks.
>
> Abyssal Eyes fight to the death, and if killed, their corpses linger as [[Abyssal Remains]]. If they are killed while floating over the bottomless void, their remains fall and reappear elsewhere by the same rules laid out for party members immediately below.
>
> #### Void Space
>
> If any party member falls or is pushed off the sides of the platforms, they hurtle down into the glittering void of lights at the bottom of the chamber before reappearing in the [[Containment Chamber]]. Upon arrival, they suffer the ill effects specified on that page.[[Gameplay Details]]

## Stone Bowl

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> An unlit stone bowl rests on a polished pedestal, its surface etched with intricate, faded patterns. A narrow channel, carved into the stone floor, snakes away into the shadows, and its path can be followed easily by following the lighter stone trim.

Once the party has defeated the Abyssal Eyes in the chamber, they can approach the Light Bowl and investigate. It is too heavy to lift or move in any way.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Empty Light Bowl
>
> Sitting on the pedestal is an [[Unknown]], which can be investigated and lit by the party using the rules laid out on that page.

## Obvious Door

The door here cannot be opened until the party effectively completes the rest of the area. They will need to solve the puzzle in the [[Mural of Volcanic Glass]] room, which can only be reached once all four Light Channels have been lit. However, they can study the obvious hidden door in front of them in an attempt to understand how to open it.

If anyone walks across the short Light Bridge that appears after they fill the Light Bowl, read or paraphrase the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The Light Bridge ends abruptly against a solid stone wall. There are no mechanisms, keyholes, or visible ways to open what is clearly a route into another passageway.

Attempting to study or investigate the wall reveals nothing more.
