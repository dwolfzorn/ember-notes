# Empty Light Bowl

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Light Bowl Interactable Objects
>
> The Light Bowls featured throughout this Scene are interactable. Once the associated conditions for activating a given Light Bowl are met, the Gamemaster can use the **​** Interact With Objects tool under Token Controls to activate it.
>
> #### Light Bowl Gameplay
>
> Most Light Bowls in the Lightless Halls function identically to each other, and thus their mechanics are centralized here. If players have previously encountered, investigated, or lit a Light Bowl, you are welcome to skip or paraphrase those sections as appropriate.

Massive, empty stone bowls can be found throughout the Lightless Halls.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Investigating the Stone Bowl
>
> Characters who inspect the stone bowl more closely can use the following strategies to gain more information about them:
>
> - Characters who previously encountered a Light Bowl recognize this one as the same item.
> - If the party solved the puzzle of the [[Runekey Altar]], any character can make a successful `[[/check investigation 15]]` to determine that that the inscriptions on the bowl are similar to those on the altar found beneath the [[Sun Star]]. If the party previously translated those markings, they recognize the same marking inscribed on the bowl, translating to "light of sun."
> - `[[/check history 15]]` check to identify one of the ancient markings inscribed on the bowl and translate it as "light of sun."
> - `[[/check perception 15]]` to notice that the large stone bowl appears to be an empty vessel connected to the deep channels that run along the floor. A close look into the shadows reveals that the bowl has an extremely small hole in the bottom center that leads to the channels below.
>
> While making any of the above checks:
>
> - **Knowledge: Artifacts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on the check.
>
> #### Filling the Light Bowl
>
> Any character who carries the [[Sun Star]] and comes within 5 feet of the stone bowl feels a calming glow emanate from it.
>
> > The sun you hold within your hands seems to pulse with energy as you approach the bowl, glowing even brighter than it did before, and seeming to illuminate the bowl's very center. That glow fills the bowl, then flows down along the channels in the floor, lighting up the room with what almost seems like sunlight.
>
> The energy from the Sun Star fills the Light Bowl and flows along its corresponding channel on the floor, opening any doors or obstructions. No checks, rituals, or other mechanisms are required to fill an empty bowl.
