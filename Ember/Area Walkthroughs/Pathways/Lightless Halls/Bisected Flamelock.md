# Bisected Flamelock

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This vast chamber almost entirely drops away into the void below. A stone platform sits in the center, with four spokes connecting to alcoves and platforms that jut out from the edges of the room. Sitting atop a slightly different colored stone pedestal in the middle of the central platform is an inert stone bowl.

#### Work In Progress

The final description and rules for this puzzle are not yet completed as they are waiting for automated elements to be implemented so they may be described accurately.

This challenge will be partially interactive and automated. These interactive elements are not yet implemented in Early Access. They will be added in a later patch, but until then gameplay is dependent upon the Gamemaster to describe the elements of this hazard and enforce its mechanical effects.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Void Space
>
> If any party member falls or is pushed off the sides of the platforms, they hurtle down into the glittering void of lights at the bottom of the chamber before reappearing in the [[Containment Chamber]]. Upon arrival, they suffer the ill effects specified on that page.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Filling the Light Bowl
>
> Filling the Light Bowl in this room is more challenging than in the others, and it requires the party to light all the bowls around the room for the central bowl to remain lit.
>
> Any character who studies the central bowl and makes a successful `[[/check inv 15]]` check discovers that this empty vessel is connected to the deep channels that run along the floor, and that unlike the others, this bowl has no hole in its bottom.
>
> - **Knowledge: Artifacts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Once the party has the [[Sun Star]], they can use its energy to fill the Light Bowl here, which then coalesces into the air and creates a beam of light that travels towards the crystal on the platform edge to the north, lighting the channel on the floor.
