# The Chained Door

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Before you stands a truly massive stone door, made from a single block of stone. Its carved and patterned surface is reinforced with large, heavy chains forged from a dimly glinting dark metal. There is no lock on the chains themselves, which disappear into holes in the ceiling and floor. Their presence suggests that the door is not merely a passage onward but is sealing something within. Behind the chains, the door is adorned with four large crystals embedded deep within its weathered surface that connect to four corresponding channels in the floor.

This utterly colossal stone door is twenty feet across, five feet wide, and nearly fifteen feet tall. As a single slab of stone, it weighs approximately ninety tons. The fact that something like this exists as a door is only possible through mastery of extremely powerful magic.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Chained Door
>
> Any character who examines the chained door and makes a successful `[[/check inv 14]]` check discovers that behind the chains, a detailed depiction of two giants has been carved into the stone of the door. The giants seem to shift slightly as they are viewed, moving their massive arms together as if to bar entry. Not only is this door firmly locked, but its size, weight, and scale suggest very few forces in [[The Cosmos]] of Ember could open this with brute force.
>
> Meanwhile, any character who examines the door and makes a successful `[[/check arc 16]]` check determines that the unlit crystals embedded in the surface of the door emit a faint magical aura from an unknown magical school, and connect to the channels along the floor. They also conclude the chains are made of an unknown metal and are crisscrossed with tiny, almost invisible runes that emit intense abjuration magic.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Light Warding
>
> If any characters attempt to strike the door or break the chains physically or magically by any means, read the following:
>
> > As the seemingly solid and immobile chains twist and writhe like rope, a flash of intense light blasts forth in a wave around the door.
>
> Any characters within 15 feet of the chained door must make a `[[/save dex 17]]` saving throw, taking `[[/damage 2d8 radiant]]` damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. They are also pushed 10 feet away from the door and knocked &Reference[prone].

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Light Lock
>
> This door remains locked until all four Light Channels which connect to it are simultaneously illuminated, via the [[Light Pillars]] in the [[Unknown]]. Once all four are filled, the chains disappear into the ceiling and the door is unlocked.
>
> #### Deadly Encounter Beyond
>
> If the party is wounded or low on resources at this point, it would be prudent to directly or indirectly suggest that they consider resting before continuing further — the battle beyond is designed to challenge a Level 4 party with their full resources available.
