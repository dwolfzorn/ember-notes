# Treasure Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The room before you is a glittering, hidden treasure chamber, embossed and detailed with extravagant golden carvings and relics. In the north and south alcoves on either side, two ornate chests made of stone and hammered gold are positioned between two colossal statues of unknown creatures, roaring with their fanged mouths wide. The chests are far too heavy to move, even with powerful magic, as are the statues, which likely weigh several tons. Scattered around the chamber are several large, heavy gold coins, each weighing a considerable amount on their own.

Anyone attempting to open either of the massive gold-and-stone chests finds them tightly locked, with a large, giant-sized keyhole on the front.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Opening the Chests
>
> Anyone who attempts to open one of the massive chests and makes a successful `[[/skill sleightofhand 15 tool=thief]]` finds the treasures within.
>
> - **Knowledge: Machines**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> #### Northern Golden Chest
>
> Once open, the northern chest contains:
>
> - **80** worth of massive pristine First Giant coins.
> - 5 large gemstones, including 2 [[Citrine]], 1 [[Opalix]], 1 [[Luxony]], and 2 [[Onyx]].
> - 2 [[Potions of Growth]].
> - An [[Elemental Gem of Fire]].
>
> #### Southern Golden Chest
>
> Once open, the southern chest contains:
>
> - **100** worth of massive pristine First Giant coins.
> - 5 large gemstones, including 1 [[Ruby]], 1 [[Opalix]], 1 [[Luxony]], and 2 [[Onyx]].
> - 2 [[Potions of Greater Healing]].
>
> #### Golden Coins
>
> There are a number of massive golden coins scattered across the floor in this chamber. Anyone attempting to pick any up discovers that time has not been kind to the unprotected metal — the coins crumble into worthless flakes.
