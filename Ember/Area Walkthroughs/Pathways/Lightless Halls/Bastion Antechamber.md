# Bastion Antechamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The hallway opens up into a vast chamber. To the north is a massive chained door, while nestled in the corners stand four towering pillars. Each pillar connects to the channels that run along the floor, which snake their way up to large inert crystals. A massive ornate stone bowl rests at the southern end of the room, seemingly unconnected to any other channels in the floor.

## The Four Pillars

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the PIllars
>
> Any character who studies or examines the pillars in the room learn that:
>
> - The top of each pillar holds a large inset crystal connected to the Light Channels on the floor.
> - The pillar is adorned with reliefs depicting the twisting [[Pathways]], currents of energy swirling through them, frozen in the timeless carving of stone. Throughout the artwork, giant figures traverse dangerous bubbling lava, holding back the boiling, fast-flowing waters while making their way through and across the world below.
>
> The first time a given character who inspects one of the pillars makes a successful `[[/check inv 18]]` also discovers a hidden compartment in its base containing a single [[Giants Comfort Stone]]. This check cannot be repeated by the same character upon inspecting another pillar, though other party members may attempt to locate the hidden compartments in those other pillars as well.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Activating the Light Pillars
>
> Each Light Pillar activates once the party fills the Light Bowl that connects to it via the appropriate channel in the floor. Once all four pillars are active, the crystals on the pillars activate, and the [[Light Lock]] on [[Unknown]] opens.
>
> #### Interactable Object
>
> Additionally, once all four Light Pillars are active, the large crystal on the south side of the room may be activated to direct another light beam southward towards the [[Mural of Volcanic Glass]].
