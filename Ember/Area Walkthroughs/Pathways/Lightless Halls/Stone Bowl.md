# Stone Bowl

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> An unlit stone bowl rests on a polished pedestal, its surface etched with intricate, faded patterns. A narrow channel, carved into the stone floor, snakes away into the shadows, and its path can be followed easily by following the lighter stone trim.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Empty Light Bowl
>
> Within this room is an [[Unknown]], which can be investigated and lit by the party using the rules laid out on that page.
