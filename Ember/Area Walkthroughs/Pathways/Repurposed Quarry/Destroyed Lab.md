# Destroyed Lab

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Fire and smoke fills this destroyed lab space, making it hard to examine the contents. Through the heat and haze you can make out a massive vat spilling green ooze out onto the rusted metal floors, and pouring through holes down to the lower levels.
>
> All else in this spare are tables and shelves scattered with the remnants of lab distillers and filters, glassware, vessels and more destroyed by the explosion.
>
> The resulting fire and smoke are laced with chemicals that cause your skin to inch, eyes to water, and lungs to burn.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Caustic Clouds
>
> Characters passing through the destroyed lab are **Blinded** and if they breathe, will begin choking, suffering **Noxious Fumes (Hazard 3, Fortitude, Morale, Poison)** for each round they remain in the gas.
>
> Characters with [[Protective Goggles]] avoid the blinded condition while in the fumes, while characters wearing a [[Protective Mask]] don't suffer the blinded or choking conditions.
>
> #### Alchemical Fire
>
> The flames can also cause **Minor Burns (Hazard 3, Fortitude, Health, Fire)**.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Hazardous Salvage
>
> A successful **Awareness (DC 16)** check while in the lab notices that a dose of [[Drakonbane Poison]] can be claimed here.
>
> Otherwise, nothing in this lab survived the explosion, and there is nothing that can be saved. Additionally, it is impossible to examine this lab while it is on fire and filled with smoke.
>
> #### Fire Management
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Alchemy** or making a successful**Science (DC 14)** using [[Alchemist's Toolkit]] know that this is an alchemical fire, and won't be easily extinguished. In fact, they will likely burn for hours unabated, and water would be completely ineffective.
>
> Instead, to extinguish these flames, soil, sand, clay and dirt must be used. The quarry has ample amounts of this, and scattered tools, but the time necessary to move enough earth would considerable and cause the characters to be exposed to the fumes the entire time.
