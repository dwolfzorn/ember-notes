# Testing Platform

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The testing platform is a massive block of stone twenty feet by twenty feet, standing starkly above the churning water, and only accessible by a bronze ramp.
>
> At its center, a drakeling lies motionless on an examination table, its body connected by a series of tubes snaking from a tank of bubbling chemicals just next to it. Nearby, a complex glass apparatus orchestrates the flow of blood and chemicals within interconnected tubes and vials for some unknown purpose.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Surgical tools
>
> The numerous tools here are well made, and well used, stained with the accumulated blood of numerous creatures. You doubt they were ever cleaned.
>
> #### Blood Distiller
>
> The complex collection of vials, tubes, mixers, and boilers is completely incomprehensible in purpose. Characters with the **Knowledge: Alchemy** knowledge or making a successful **Science (DC 14)** using [[Alchemist's Toolkit]] can determine that the array appears to be processing partially congealed drake blood into a substance for testing.
>
> #### Dead Drakeling
>
> A badly afflicted drakeling is strapped to the steel table here, and is long dead.
>
> A successful **Medicine (DC 15)** check determines that much of its blood having been pumped out of its body. Additionally, numerous incisions have been made across its body to remove tumors, explore seeping wounds, and examine organs.
>
> #### Drum of Ooze
>
> Characters with the **Knowledge: Alchemy** knowledge or making a successful **Science (DC 14)** using [[Alchemist's Toolkit]] notice that this is the same volatile chemical that was being utilize in the lab on the upper level.
>
> The vat is set up to dispense samples into vials, allowing a character with the **Knowledge: Alchemy** knowledge or making a successful **Science (DC 14)** using [[Alchemist's Toolkit]] to acquire a [[Vial of Mutagenic Medium]]
>
> Characters that examine the Examination Notes (below) also know that the purple chemical in the vat is what is a special alchemical medium. It is useless by itself, but if mixed with proper activator compounds it becomes a potent solution.
>
> #### Books and Journals
>
> A lectern holds [[Examination Notes]] relating to this drake.
>
> A rack nearby holds the following other documents:
>
> - A [[Manual of Drake Anatomy]].
> - A journal containing the slab's [[Testing Schedule]].
