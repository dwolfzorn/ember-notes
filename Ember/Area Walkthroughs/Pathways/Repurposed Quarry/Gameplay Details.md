# Gameplay Details

## Illumination

The area here is near pitch black, only very faint lights emanating from the ancient bones and skulls of long dead Giants can be seen through the oppressive gloom.

## Terrain

The area has a number of stepped levels, but these are relatively easy to climb and only require an additional 5 feet of movement to scale. Climbing these is not entirely without risk as the rocks can be sharp and slick from the moisture of the fog.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Dangerous Ground
>
> The entire repurposed quarry is in a state of disarray, and there are numerous dangerous situations unfolding across the entire facility. Parties attempting to go in quietly may have a hard time due to the long sight lines and large numbers of enemies in the area.
>
> To their advantage, the relative chaos incited by scuttling the base means that enemies might not immediately realize they have a band of intruders even if they do spot the party.

## Elevation

The ceiling of the pathways above is far beyond the reach of any player character, likely in the many hundreds of feet. Levels of the quarry are 20ft apart, posing a small risk of injury for characters that fall, and the sheer stone walls of most of the quarry lack hand holds, making climbing between levels difficult without a natural climbing speed.

## Collecting Evidence

Most of the evidence the party needs to acquire for the investigation can be found in [[Chemical Storage]], [[Compound Lab]], and the [[Testing Platform]].

## Enemies

This quarry is filled with multiple members of the Mutagist faction on all levels. The denizens of this lab are actively attempting to destroy evidence in the lab, and are preparing to depart from the site.

- Stopping the Mutagist Bomber in the [[Entry Tunnels]] prevents the Mutagists from destroying any more of the lab.
- Several Mutated Drakes are squared off in combat against Mutagist Vivisectors in the [[Creature Pens]]
- The party can encounter Kalasak the Cutter, overseer of the laboratory in [[Compound Lab]].
- On the lowest level the party will find a challenging opponent in the [[Mutated Ultra Drake]] that has been kept by the Mutagists.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Resting
>
> It is not possible to rest in quarry area until the party has scoured the site of hostiles, driving off or defeating any enemies first.
>
> #### Fleeing
>
> Should the party need to flee the area, they may do so by retreating from the quarry interior toward the entrance tunnel they arrived at, or the cave cliff. Mutagists will not pursue them beyond the immediate area the map covers.
