# Gravel Pit

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The gravel pit is dimly-lit, filled with gravel that crunches noisily underfoot, and stony dust sent swirling through the air with even the smallest movements. Scattered throughout are rotten bones, both animal and humanoid, fouling the air here with their stench. A sturdy gate of rusting iron bars and heavy metal lock controls access to this space.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Remains
>
> With a quick glance, there are the remains of numerous dead creatures, many buried under layers of gravel and dirt, among them are humanoid remains.
>
> A successful **Medicine (DC 14)** check is needed to parse through the remains. Doing so reveals the presents of bones belonging to small drakes, humanoids, dogs, what might be a small yarnac, and several small reptiles. Many are old and rotten, and ripped apart with signs of being gnawed on.

Characters hoping to get into or out of the pit will need to contend with the gate.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Opening the Opening
>
> To bypass the lock on the door a successful **Stealth (DC 14)** using [[Lockpicks]] is necessary. Accessing the lock is easily done from either side.
>
> #### Conspicuous Marks
>
> A **Awareness (DC 12, Passive)**or better notices that the area around the door, and the iron bars themselves bear the marks of claws and teeth, as though something quite large had tried to dig its way into the pit on more than one occasion.
