# Outer Cliff

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The secluded cliff juts out from the cavern wall, offering a precarious vantage point and the remains of a long broken, corroded bridge. From this elevated position, the expanse of a cavern unfurls, the luminescent glow of the pathways strange life visible in the otherwise abundant darkness.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Possible Passages
>
> A successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check notices a shift in the air currents here, and a quality in the air itself. It seems likely that there is an exit at the end of this great cavern, though you'd have to be able to fly to find it.
>
> This might be how some of the drakes got out.
