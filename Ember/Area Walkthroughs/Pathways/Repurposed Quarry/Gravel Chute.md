# Gravel Chute

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The metal plates creak and groan underfoot, sinking slightly as weight is placed upon them as you enter this space. Heavy rusted metal floors and walls surround you, their surface pockmarked and corroded by time.
>
> Dual pullies hang ominously over two large metal plates. At first it looks like a lift, but after a moment you realize they seal off a shaft that descends to the lower level. A net and heavy rope sway slightly with a rhythmic creak overhead.
>
> The air here carries a musty odor of rust and pulverized stone.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Falling Hazard
>
> Characters stepping onto the central platform of the chute will feel the metal give way, dropping them into the chute. The fall is 40 feet, and cause the fallen to suffer a **Perilous Impact (Hazard 12, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)**. This also moves them to the middle level of this scene.
