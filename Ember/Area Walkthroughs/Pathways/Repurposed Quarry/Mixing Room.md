# Mixing Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The mixing room hums and clatters with activity as the waterwheel outside turns steadily, powering the mechanism within.
>
> Two large tanks, each positioned in opposite corners, dominate the room, their contents swirling under the influence of the waterwheel's transferred energy. These vessels rest on substantial, weathered iron tables adorned with an array of lab glassware and chemicals.
>
> Notebooks, their pages open, reveal complex formulae etched in hurried hand, a testament to the ongoing alchemical experimentation.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Curious Compounds
>
> The following items can be found here:
>
> - Stock and supply ledgers which yield a [[List of Chemicals]] utilized in the laboratory.
> - A book containing numerous [[Mutagenic Formulae]].
