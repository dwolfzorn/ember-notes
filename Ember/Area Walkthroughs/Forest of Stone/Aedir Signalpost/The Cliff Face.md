# The Cliff Face

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The narrow passageway from the tower's storage area emerges onto a small cliff. Below, the Forest of Stone stretches out beneath you, its greens and grays and blues inviting, but so far down that a fall would likely kill you. The cliff is blocked by the tower's structure to your right, but appears to continue to your left to some unknown destination for those willing to brave the treacherous heights.

The path is narrow, and must be carefully navigated to avoid a potential fall.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Treacherous Path
>
> For each party member who moves along the cliff, roll a `[[/gmroll 1d6 # Cliff Face Challenge]]` on the Cliff Face Challenges table below.
>
> Any character who moves to the western tip of the tower and makes a successful **Athletics (DC 16)** check climbs down to the level below.
>
> - **Special Equipment:** The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check if they use a grappling hook, rope, or another piece of suitable equipment.
>
> If the check is failed, they face a **Twenty Foot Fall (Hazard 5, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)**.

|  | #### Challenge to the Climb |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | #### Treacherous Footing  Finding solid footholds is difficult and you continually slip as you walk along the cliff. Roll **Athletics (DC 14)** . On a failure, you maintain your grip but are greatly fatigued by the endeavor. You gain@Condition[Exhausted]. |
| 2 | #### Violent Winds  The wind gusts violently, causing you to stagger forward and threatening to knock you off-balance. Make a **Athletics (DC 14)**. On a failure, you catch yourself as you slide down the cliff face but suffer **Rock Abrasions (Hazard 4, Fortitude, Health, Slashing)**. |
| 3 | #### Disappearing Obstructions  A sudden ripple of magical energy flows over the exterior surface of the tower as it temporarily flickers. You are momentarily disoriented as your hand and footholds disappear from underneath you. Make an **Arcana (DC 13)** check. On a failure you fall from the cliff face, and impact an **Invisible Rock (Hazard 4, Fortitude, Health)**. |
| 4 | #### Suddenly, Crevvets!  A [[Crevvet]] scampers by, making you temporarily lose your footing. Roll an **Athletics (DC 13)** check. On a failure, you kick one of them and as your foot comes down carelessly, you suffer a **Crevvet Bite (Hazard 0, Fortitude, Health, Piercing)**. |
| 5 | #### Smooth Climbing  Your passage is uneventful as you avoid any notable obstacles in your path. |
| 6 | #### Glittering Prizes  You notice something glinting against the rock. Attempt a **Athletics (DC 15)** check to grab it safely. On a success, you have retrieved a gem worth  **1**. |

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Scene Transition
>
> Characters can automatically transition to the lower Level by moving into the Scene Region titled **Transition: Descend to Basement**.
