# Western Storage Alcove

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Behind the spider-webbed entrance, this alcove is filled with boxes that have been knocked over and gnawed on. The crevvets you previously spotted are nowhere in sight, having disappeared through some crack or crevice to another part of the tower, but the evidence of their presence is everywhere, from the dusty footprints to the holes gnawed into the stone floor.

The boxes in this alcove contain shelf-stable food items, like flour and sugar, most of which have been eaten by the [[Crevvet]] — which have all escaped to places unknown.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Crevvet Crevices
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 13)** check can confirm that the gnaw marks on the boxes are made by the crevvets. Parties may investigate the room to figure out where the vermin have gone off to.
>
> The pests have been entering and exiting the storage area of the tower through a large crevice in the wall in this section of storage. Any character with **Awareness (DC 15, Passive)** can locate the crevice.
>
> - **Knowledge: Subterranea**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.

The crevice is slightly hidden behind a sheet of fallen slate.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The slab of rock leans against the opening behind it — from a distance, it looks indistinguishable from the rock wall on either side of it, but up close, it is clear that the crevvets have chewed away at enough of it for it to be easily moved, exposing a narrow dark passageway behind it.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Heavy Slab
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Athletics (DC 16)** check can move the rock slab without special equipment.
>
> - **Special Equipment:** The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check if they use a crowbar or another piece of suitable equipment.
>
> If the check is failed, it can be retried, but the party member in question must succeed on a **Athletics (DC 13)** or suffer **The Shifting Rock (Hazard 5, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)**

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Secret Door
>
> A "secret door" on the map is used to represent the rock slab described above. Open the secret door once the party has successfully moved the rock.

Once the party has cleared the blockage, they can go through the passageway, leading to [[The Cliff Face]].
