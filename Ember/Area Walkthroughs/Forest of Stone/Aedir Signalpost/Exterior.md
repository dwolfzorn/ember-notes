# Exterior

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The exterior facade of the tower looms over you, its ancient architecture both impressive and foreboding.
>
> Surveying the flora surrounding the entrance of the tower, you notice a strange desiccation that diminishes the lushness of the environment somewhat. The fringes of usually vibrant heartmoss assume a withered hue and the long reeds that protrude from tiny gaps in the masonry have yellow and wilted fronds.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Inspecting the Exterior
>
> Any character who examines the exterior of the tower and makes a successful **Society (DC 14, Passive)** check realizes that the tower is likely Aedir in origin due to its use of metal and stone, the shape of the tower, and its age. This location, though, is a long way from the bulk of Aedir territory.
>
> - **Knowledge: Aedir**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character also recalls that the Aedir were known to gather information on those around them in signalposts that used mirrors, light, and magic to gather and send information across vast distances.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 14, Passive)** check infers that they may now be within the radius of whatever stealth field was intermittently concealing the tower, and that they should be able to see the structure clearly as long as they remain near or within it.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13, Passive)** check notices that light glints off of specific spots in the ring at the top of the tower. It is likely that there is some sort of mirror or glass there, possibly used to observe the area around the tower.

The plants surrounding the tower have no ill effects if touched, but they do offer some clues about the source of trouble below.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Contamination Clues
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 13, Passive)** check deduces that the plants have likely come into contact with the contaminant.
>
> - **Knowledge: Plants**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character also recognizes that such contamination is likely present in the groundwater and, given the deep roots of plants in this ecosystem, the source of the contamination is probably somewhere below.
