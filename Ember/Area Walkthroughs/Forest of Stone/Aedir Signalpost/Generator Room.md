# Generator Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The light flickers an eerie yellowish orange, bouncing off of piles of books and tools, including several fully functioning metal limbs, which hang from stands as if waiting to be brought to life. The light comes from several large glass tubes in the back of the room, which have a small amount of orange fluid coating their bottoms. They are attached to a contraption that is a combination of metal and stonework and enough magic that it feels like it has a heartbeat. A gentle voice whispers in your ear as you cross the threshold, repetitive and insistent:
>
> > Fluid low. Recharge required. Fluid low. Replenish immediately. Fluid low.

The generator in this room is the source of arcane power for the Signalpost. It is currently empty of the alchemical substance which powers it. Characters who examine the residue of fluid in the canisters and are familiar with the substance already will recognize it as [[Aedir Construct Fluid]].

## Gaining Access

Review the [[Area Overview]] section of the Area Overview page for details on how this secret room may be opened.

## Restoring Power

In order to restore power to the generator, the canisters in the back of the room must be refilled using the [[Recharge Stations]] in the Underforge. By replacing filled canisters in the ports on the north wall of the room, the generator will spring back into life:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Brightness. Everywhere. Lights you could not see before glow from the walls, lighting the entire tower in a warm glow. The gentle voice whispers again:
>
> > Power restored. Our work continues.
>
> If whatever powers the voice is aware that the post has been long-abandoned, it does not mention it.

Restoring power to the generator causes all interior lights throughout the Aedir Signalpost to turn on, suffusing the area with bright illumination. Additionally, the stealth field which conceals the tower from view returns to full operation, no longer flickering intermittently.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Event Outcome
>
> If the party restores power to the generator, mark the **Power Restored** Outcome of the [[Traversing the Tower]] Event as completed.
