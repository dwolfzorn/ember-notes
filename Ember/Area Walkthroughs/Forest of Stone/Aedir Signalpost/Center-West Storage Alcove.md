# Center-West Storage Alcove

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This storage alcove is somewhat more organized than the scattered goods elsewhere. Boxes are well-labeled, and there is a list of items on a piece of paper on top of the largest storage crate, lettered in precise blue handwriting. On the bottom corners of the page are two separate sketches, in red ink.

The paper on top of the storage crate is Bejai's "Final Tower Storage" list, which gives the location and basic description of several items in storage. The storage list can be translated using the [[Area Overview]] rules.

| **Basic Meaning** | **Full Translation** |
| --- | --- |
| metal, piece | Metal construct parts can be found in these boxes. |
| machine, water, bottom | Construct Fluid is stored in the Underforge. |
| water, clean, bottom | Construct Fluid Neutralizer is stored in the Underforge. |
| power, tower, down | Tower can be recharged on Basement Level with Construct Fluid. |

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Remel's Sketches
>
> Additionally, characters may attempt to use Remel's sketches to figure out more about the list. She has drawn the following sketches:
>
> 1. A small golden-brown abstract head with glowing eyes. Any character who makes a successful **`[[/skillCheck social 14]]`** check connects this with the metal constructs that the Aedir Empire was known to use in its structures.
> 2. A battery filled with a liquid of some kind. Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check recalls that the power in the tower is connected to a fluid of some kind.
>
> When making any of the above checks:
>
> - **Knowledge: Aedir**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on the check.
