# Recharge Stations

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Between the crumbling stone, the brass hexagonal platforms on the floor remain almost untouched, each waiting for something that fits between the pipe that juts out from above and the winding plug around the base below. A small sign written in Towyr hangs from the brass wheel in the center of each pipe.
>
> A diagram beneath the label shows a length of hose connecting one large hexagon to smaller hexagons on items, including the construct, a power tube, and a large cylindrical tube that resembles the elevator. A foot can be seen on the large hexagon, and hands hold each of the items with smaller hexagons. Wavy lines around the hands seem to indicate some sort of motion, but there is no written detail.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Station Labels
>
> Each station has an advisory label written in Towyr that can be translated using the rules for [[Area Overview]].
>
> | **Basic Meaning** | **Full Translation** |
> | --- | --- |
> | water, power | Alchemical Recharge for construct and power canister refueling. |
>
> #### Recharging Generator Cells
>
> The Recharge Station can be used to refill the power cells found in the secret [[Unknown]]. In order to be used, the Recharge Station must have a full supply of [[Aedir Construct Fluid]].
>
> - If the party turned off the supply between the vats and the Recharge Stations during their battle with the construct, they must first turn it back on.
> - If the party neutralized the contamination by using the [[Decontamination Station]], it is impossible to refill the canisters with viable fluid.
>
> When placed in the Recharge Station in the Underforge, they refill with fluid:
>
> > The recharge machine hums to life, turning the empty tubes a deep green as it fills them with the alchemical fluid.
