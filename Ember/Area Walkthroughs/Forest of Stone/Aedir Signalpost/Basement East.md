# Basement East

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Between the small tool-covered metal tables and the set of stairs that lead further down into the lower levels of the tower, a half-made metal construct sits in a heap on the floor. Orange liquid has congealed beneath its pile of copper-colored limbs, none of which fully match each other. It is impossible to tell at a glance whether this is a failed project, a pile of scraps, or the remains of some violent experiment. A small note affixed to its chest reads: "Add Shielding." It is not the only one — similar small notes are scattered across the room.

Any character who visited Golarne's room will recognize the notes as being by the same person and serving the same general function — reminders of things to do.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Notable Notes
>
> Golarne has written many, many notes, and it is not immediately clear which, if any, are helpful. To identify notes that may have important information for them, characters must succeed on an **Awareness (DC 15)** check. Up to three notes can be recovered; they are presented below in chronological order.

### The First Note

This note has a large down arrow on it in addition to the words.

| **Basic Meaning** | **Full Translation** |
| --- | --- |
| clean, silver | Clean alchemical spills with silver decontamination powder. |

### The Second Note

The note has only words on it, and matches similar notes about power found elsewhere.

| **Basic Meaning** | **Full Translation** |
| --- | --- |
| power, book | A POWERful read |

### The Third Note

The note has a picture of piping and two small wheels.

| **Basic Meaning** | **Full Translation** |
| --- | --- |
| heat, caution | Beware of steam cleansing, remember cutoff valves. |

## A Hidden Lever

One of the levers to open the [[Generator Room]] is found in this section of the basement and can be activated by pulling any book off a specific bookshelf to the south of the stairs.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### A Powerful Read
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 15)** while examining the room notices the following:
>
> - The books on each shelf are arranged by title, with the exception of one shelf, which appears to have books arranged in a random order.
> - A dusting of a crystalline substance surrounds the bookcase.
>
> The party can activate the lever by rearranging the books in the correct order on the disorganized bookshelf. If the characters are at a loss, the idea to rearrange the books might occur to them by making a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check.
>
> The [[Recognize Spellcraft]] talent may be used to learn that the bookshelf is enchanted.
>
> If a character activates the lever by rearranging the books, refer to [[Area Overview]] for details.
