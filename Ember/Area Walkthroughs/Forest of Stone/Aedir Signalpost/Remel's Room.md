# Remel's Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This is a room in a state of suspended motion — the cover has been dragged off of the bed, clothes are strewn about, and a cup of long-evaporated liquid, now home to a clutch of spider eggs, sits with a spoon still in it on the dresser in the sleeping area. Papers are scattered across the desk, including several sketches. You recognize one as being of the entryway you came through to enter the tower. One word is written on it, which vaguely translates as "dark".

There are a number of sketches on the desk, which include faraway landscapes, portions of the Forest of Stone, and rooms within several towers, all of which have notes on them.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Identifying the Sketches
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)**check can identify three of the sketches as architectural drawings of this tower.

### The First Sketch

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This sketch depicts a half-circle of a room with two desks in its center.

The area depicted by this sketch is the left half of the basement level of the tower. One phrase is written on it in Towyr. Characters who successfully translate the phrase have **+2 Boons** on checks to deactivate the Steam Heat trap in the basement.

| **Basic Meaning** | **Full Translation** |
| --- | --- |
| clean, turn | The cleansing steam will protect, but turning the lever will stop. |

### The Second Sketch

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This sketch depicts a half-circle room with a square-shaped room adjoining it.

The area depicted by this sketch is the right half of the basement level. Two phrases are written in Towyr. Characters who successfully translate the phrases have **+2 Boons** on checks to locate the levers that open the Secret Basement room throughout the tower.

| **Basic Meaning** | **Full Translation** |
| --- | --- |
| power, hidden | Secure the power source in a concealed chamber. |
| four, safe | Four separate switches required for access to ensure secrecy and protection. |

### The Third Sketch

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This sketch depicts a large circular room, filled with large circular vats and square pads.

The area depicted by this sketch is the Underforge. A metallic helmet is drawn in the center of the room. No notes are written on this sketch, but orange lines with arrows trace from the vats to the square pads.
