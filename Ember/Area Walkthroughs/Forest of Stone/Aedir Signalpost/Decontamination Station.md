# Decontamination Station

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Cylindrical tanks colored red, yellow, and green sit on top of a cabinet against the wall. A few Towyr words are etched in large letters on the cabinet, along with a sign that you immediately recognize as the word "Emergency."

The decontamination station is located north of the stairs, on the easternmost wall.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### In Case of Emergency
>
> The station is labeled with the following message, which can be understood using the rules for [[Area Overview]].
>
> | **Basic Meaning** | **Full Translation** |
> | --- | --- |
> | Emergency, Clean | Alchemical Decontamination Station — Use only in case of emergency for immediate neutralization of active reagent. |
>
> If characters open the cabinet, they see a pile of supplies, including several vials of [[Alchemical Silver]] and empty flasks that can be used to collect liquids.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Society (DC 15)** check can identify the powder as Alchemical Silver, a substance that can act as a decontaminant for certain alchemical agents and poisons.
>
> - **Knowledge: Alchemy**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The cabinet opens to reveal a shelf covered with vials, flasks, and spare materials, next to an indentation that matches the size and shape of a hand.

If any character places their hand within the indentation and holds it there for 6 seconds, the decontamination station comes to life.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The tanks begin to pump fluid into the pipes, and before your eyes, the bubbling orange of the broken and rusty vats shifts in color and becomes more still, turning from its previous electric color to a silvery gray.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Irreversible Cleansing
>
> If the alchemical fluid is neutralized in this way, it can no longer be used by the [[Broken Aedir Sentinel]], nor can it be used to refill the canisters required to restore power to the [[Generator Room]].
>
> Any character who makes a successful**Awareness (DC 15, Passive)** check will understand that performing the decontamination procedure will render the alchemical substance inert.
>
> - **Knowledge: Alchemy**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
