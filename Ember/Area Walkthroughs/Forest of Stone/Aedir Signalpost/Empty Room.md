# Empty Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> There is no sign of occupancy in this room — the bed and desk are empty, and there is a layer of dust on the wood that is deeper than you have seen anywhere else in the tower. Only the bed seems slightly less dusty and abandoned.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Golarne's Reminders
>
> Any character who makes a **Awareness (DC 15)** check notices a small piece of paper under one of the legs of the bed, which can be translated using the [[Area Overview]] rules.

| **Basic Meaning** | **Full Translation** |
| --- | --- |
| power, sleep | A POWERFUL Rest |

One of the levers that opens the secret [[Unknown]] is activated by putting weight on the bed in this room, triggering a pressure plate.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### A Powerful Rest
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 15 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while examining the room notices the following:
>
> - The bed isn't fully made — it appears to have been purposefully set so that someone can easily get in and out of it.
> - A dusting of a crystalline substance surrounds the bed.
>
> The party can activate the lever to the hidden basement room by lying down on the bed. If the characters are at a loss, the idea to lie down might occur to them by making a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check.
>
> The [[Recognize Spellcraft]] talent may be used to learn that the bed is enchanted to respond to weight being placed upon it.
>
> If a character activates the lever by lying down on the bed, refer to [[Area Overview]] for details.
