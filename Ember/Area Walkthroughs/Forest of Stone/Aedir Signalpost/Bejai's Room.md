# Bejai's Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The room is sparsely decorated, but the items that can be seen from the door are evenly spaced and precisely placed; the bed is made, and everything smells mildly of antiseptic. A small metal sign on the desk reads “Plan. Honor. Execute. Repeat.” The only thing in the room that does not appear to be in a state of well-organized rest is the small glowing cube that rests on a disc in the center of the desk, which pulses with a soft light.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Message Cube
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 14)**check can identify the cube as a Message Cube that allows for someone to record their voice and have it play back at a later date.

If characters touch the illuminated cube, it glows more strongly and plays a message.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The recording begins with the shuffling of papers and the sound of a voice in the distance that you cannot make out. A voice closer to you says, as if irritated:
>
> > Golarne, if you're that worried that you'll forget how to get into the power room, leave yourself notes or something. I'm recording.
>
> The voice then clears their throat and begins again:
>
> > If you are listening to this, then I am either dead or have abandoned the tower. I hope and trust that you are of the Aedir Empire, but in case you are Shent, or from some other group that does not share our tongue, I have enchanted this message to reach all ears.
> >
> > Please know, the research in this tower is for the Aedir alone. If you are not one of us, it is not meant for you. If you disregard this message, only death awaits you below. Choose your next moves wisely.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Parting Jab
>
> As the recording ends, the cube pulses brightly and sends out a blast of force energy. All characters in the room suffer the effects of a **Forceful Blast (Hazard 5, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)**.
