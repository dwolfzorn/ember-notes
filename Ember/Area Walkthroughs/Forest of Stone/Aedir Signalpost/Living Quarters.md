# Living Quarters

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Metal plaques are affixed to three of the four doors visible from the elevator entrance, each etched with a name in different handwriting — "Remel" is inscribed in block letters on the one to the west, "Bejai" in thin and neat lettering on the one next to it, and "Golarne" in sweeping script with swirls and flourishes on the door farthest east. The remaining door is empty.

Characters who have seen the writing of the three inhabitants of the tower can match them to the writing found elsewhere in the tower. Generally, Remel writes in red, Bejai in blue, and Golarne in green.

From the foyer, characters can investigate the following:

- [[Unknown]] (West)
- [[Unknown]] (Center-West)
- [[Unknown]] (Center-East)
- [[Unknown]] (East)

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Locked Chambers
>
> Both Golarne's and Bejai's rooms are locked with heavy stone doors that cannot be easily demolished or forced open. The doors do appear to have a traditional keyhole, however, on the side of their exterior surface.
>
> The door can be unlocked with a successful**Stealth (DC 15)** using [[Lockpicks]].
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 13, Passive)** notices an elliptical indentation in the same place on each doorframe near where the conventional keyhole is located.
>
> - Golarne's room in particular may be opened by pressing [[Golarne's Signet Ring]] into this indentation.
