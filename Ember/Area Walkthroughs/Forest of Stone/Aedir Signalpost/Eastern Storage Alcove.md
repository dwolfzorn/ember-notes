# Eastern Storage Alcove

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Much like the others, this alcove is filled with boxes, distinguished only by a stand of rusted weapons against the wall.

The boxes in this storage alcove are filled with various supplies: containers, vials, screws, pieces of piping, cleaning fluid, crowbars, and adhesive. The weapons on this rack are practical and not exotic, and are difficult to distinguish in quality due to their extensive rusting.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Ancient Weaponry
>
> Any character with **Awareness (DC 15, Passive)** notices one of the swords in particular appears to be made of an exceptional material. This blade is a [[Construct Carver]] and is obviously a weapon of value notable for its exceptional hardness and sharp edge.
>
> - **Knowledge: Crafts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
