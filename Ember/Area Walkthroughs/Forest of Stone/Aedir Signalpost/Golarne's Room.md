# Golarne's Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Notes in green writing are affixed to nearly every surface in this room — objects, papers, even the door. Many have faded away, but one piece of paper, in the center of the desk, has words large enough to be read from across the room.

Golarne has left notes throughout the room as a reminder of daily tasks. Translation of these notes reveals that most are about mundane things: reminders to eat and sleep, to go to the top of the tower and enjoy the view, and more. The prominent piece of paper in the center of the desk can be translated using the [[Area Overview]] rules.

| **Basic Meaning** | **Full Translation** |
| --- | --- |
| memory, paper | Remember the notes of POWER. |

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Golarne's Reminders
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check can confirm that the handwriting on these notes matches the handwriting found on notes about "power" that are located elsewhere throughout the complex.
>
> - **Knowledge: Forensics**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
