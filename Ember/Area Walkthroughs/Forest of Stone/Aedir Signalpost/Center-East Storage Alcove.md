# Center-East Storage Alcove

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The boxes in this storage alcove are covered in dust and spiderwebs. A crowbar lies against the wall, but it seems unlikely that anyone has been in this area in some time.

This storage area contains mostly clothing items. If any are opened, they reveal everyday clothes, bed linens, and other similar items. Some of these boxes appear to have been repacked with items they did not originally come with.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Contaminating Evidence
>
> Any character who investigates the boxes discovers one that smells vaguely of something alchemical — its source appears to be an orange stain that has soaked into the box's side. The orange stain was produced by [[Aedir Construct Fluid]].
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 20)** check can identify the substance.
>
> - **Encountered Contaminant:** The character automatically succeeds on this check if they already encountered the contaminant during their travels (for example, during the [[Ooze Control]] Side Quest).
> - **Knowledge: Alchemy**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
