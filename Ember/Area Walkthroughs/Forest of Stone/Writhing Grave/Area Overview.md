# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The [[Writhing Grave]] Area Map depicts a small section of the [[Dripstones]] where numerous waterfalls converge and a powerful Abyssal entity is presently trapped. It is featured in the [[An Ancient Battle]] Event of the [[Over The Moon]] Side Quest.
>
> - This map depicts a single Scene Level: the Confluence Falls.
> - The area is home to a dangerous entity. If the party has not yet reached Level 2 or is low on resources, provide clues that foreshadow the danger level, giving the players a chance to turn back if they wish.
> - The party arrives at the northwest corner of the [[Terraced Pools]].

## Gameplay Details

The areas of the Writhing Grave have the following features unless specified otherwise in the text.

### Levels & Elevation

The Writhing Grave Scene features a single Level, and its areas are organized into a single section: Confluence Falls.

- **Confluence Falls:** The Confluence Falls is set to an elevation 0f 0 — the elevation difference between terraces is mechanically negligible.

The cliffs surrounding the perimeter of the Area Map are several hundred feet tall and impossible to climb without substantial effort, training, and equipment.

### Illumination

The Writhing Grave is subject to exterior lighting and weather conditions.

### Terrain

This area is broken up into various stone steps that descend toward the pool where the waterfalls collect. Transitioning between these terraces or moving through the pools of water is difficult terrain.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Tainted Ground
>
> The ground is badly tainted by otherworldly Abyssal energies, making it somewhat hazardous to rest here. While the party is generally safe for the short period they are present here, prolonged exposure to the energies will begin to have an effect on their minds.
>
> Characters that complete a **Recovery or Rest** in this area must withstand the hazard of the **Tainted Ground (Hazard 4, Willpower, Psychic, Harmless)** or or suffer a single effect from the [[Minor Abyssal Madness]] table upon the conclusion of their rest.

### Enemies

This area contains a single formidable adversary, the [[Writhing Whisperer]]. It occupies the [[Waterfall Nexus]].
