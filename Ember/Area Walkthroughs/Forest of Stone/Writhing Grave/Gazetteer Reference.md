# Gazetteer Reference

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The great cliffs that encircle the northern Dripstones valley converge here, creating a space where multiple waterfalls cascade down the rock and culminate in a shallow lake. The terraced shelves of rock and greenery bear the scars of an ancient battle, and are haunted by the pervasive scent of decay. Even the brilliant verdancy of vegetation and the percussive roar of the falls can't fully mask the gnawing sense that something here is amiss.

The gravesite here is a nexus of waterfalls both large and small, whose cascading paths converge into a large pool. The area's wild growth becomes more visibly withered and corrupted the closer one gets to the epicenter of its abyssal taint.

### Environment

This location bears invisible scars of a battle fought long ago, deep gouges in the earth concealed by millennia of natural growth. The area seems normal at first — aside from a subtle sense of unease — but as one nears the waterfall nexus, it becomes readily apparent that a sinister presence has befouled the land.

### Ecology

The flora and fauna of this location resemble the rest of the Dripstones, although the area does feature some heavy decay and rot throughout — particularly where the Abyssal entity known as the [[Writhing Whisperer]] was fought and imprisoned long ago.

### Lore

This ill-fated location was marked by destiny long ago, when an abyssal entity was all but defeated and trapped beneath the righteous blade of an ancient Shent warrior. And the region has not been seen or scouted for thousands of years. Hence, very little is known about this place. The lingering Abyssal presence of the Writhing Whisperer is known only to the Shent seer [[Mioroth]] — whose arcane spirit monitors the malevolent creature from beyond time and space.
