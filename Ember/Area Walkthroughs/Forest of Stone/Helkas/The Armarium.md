# The Armarium

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Wares ranging from dried spices to fresh produce (and everything in between) line the shelves of this general store, alongside stacks of trade goods and bundles of adventuring gear.
>
> Here and there, small art pieces carved from the glittering bones of the mine's eponymous Leviathan decorate the building's wooden walls, hung next to sketched illustrations of the great city of Ordain (and even farther destinations).

This graciously-outfitted general store is owned by the shrewd entrepreneur **Cordayne Kohle** (Ordani Human, he/him), one of Leviathan Mine’s key stakeholders and a skilled broker for [[House Cevher]]. The Armarium is stocked with nearly anything a traveler of the Forest of Stone could need for a safe and successful expedition. Cordayne minds the store most of the day, along with his assistant shopkeep **Thaddeus** (Arcturian Human, he/him), a sleepy-eyed teenage [[Arcturian]] with plenty of bills to pay.

For more information about Cordayne Kohle, refer to the [[Gazetteer Reference]] page.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Items & Equipment for Sale
>
> The Armarium sells common weapons, armor, tools, and adventuring gear. This well-stocked general store also has modest amounts of the following crafting components for sale:
>
> - [[Dreth Sap]]
> - [[Steelmarrow]]
> - [[Inkaro Pearl, White]]
>
> The Armarium offers a 20% discount to members of [[House Cevher]] and their allies.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Game State: Dusktide Rising
>
> While Helkas is under siege during the [[Dusktide Rising]] Event, The Armarium is closed for business and its standard inhabitants are nowhere to be found.
