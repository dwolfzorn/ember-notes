# Boneway Stairs

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This sturdy wooden stairway ascends to a sidewalk lining the Leviathan's ossified remains, where a railway crowns the ancient creature's skeletal form.

The Boneway Stairs connect the eastern edge of [[Helkas Green]] on the Ground Level with [[The Boneway]] on the Upper Level, and must be used if a character wishes to traverse the terrain quickly while changing elevation.
