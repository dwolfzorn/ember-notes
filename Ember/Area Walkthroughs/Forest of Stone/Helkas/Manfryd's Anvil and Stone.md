# Manfryd's Anvil and Stone

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The sound of crackling hearthfire fills this oblong pentagonal building that sits at the southwestern edge of the Green. Multiple anvils and forges decorate this one-room smithy, which appears to see more than its fair share of action.

The forges here at Manfryd's Anvil and Stone are owned and operated by the prodigious blacksmith/stoneworker **Manfryd Grimm** (Arcturian Kivahr, he/him). In tandem with a traditional coal-heated forge, Manfryd utilizes a special Gloamstone forge powered by arcane Duskflame, which serves as a valuable asset during the precarious crafting of Steelmarrow armaments.

For more information about Manfryd Grimm, refer to the [[Gazetteer Reference]] page.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Arms & Armor
>
> Manfryd Grimm sells a wide array of the Weapons and Armor . Manfryd maintains a modest supply of [[Steelmarrow]], but he reserves this rare material for his own crafting rather than wholesale exchanges.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Game State: Dusktide Rising
>
> While Helkas is under siege during the [[Dusktide Rising]] Event, Manfryd's Anvil and Stone is closed for business and its standard inhabitants are nowhere to be found.
