# Helkas Green

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This verdant courtyard is marked by the presence of a spiral arrangement of gray stones, both large and small. Vibrant festival banners are tethered to a pole at the coil's center, and the magenta leaves of nearby aithus trees float by at the behest of a rollicking mountain wind.

Helkas Green is the colloquial name bestowed on the settlement's central courtyard area, which is open to the public at all times. This plaza — defined by the cobblestone paths that wind their way through and around its viridescent expanse — is bordered on all sides by various homes and businesses, and is used by the people of Helkas (and travelers alike) for private and public gatherings.
