# Glinthome

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The rustic features of a modest temple lit by crystal lanterns scintillate with divine radiance. This sanctuary is marked by several distinct furnishings, including four worn wooden benches, a reliquary of sacred tomes and tools, and a shrine that resembles a library desk more than an altar. The smell of juniper tea lingers here along with the faint sound of celestial chimes.

This modest temple is dedicated to [[Spectra]], the Elder Goddess of Magic. Lit by arcane braziers of cold Duskflame, this humble place of worship is decorated with an array of Steelmarrow embellishments, and serves as an inviting sanctuary for the people of Helkas. It also provides its caretaker, the tender-hearted cleric **Briannon Frost** (Arcturian Wirrun, she/her), with a sense of individual purpose.

For more information about Briannon Frost, refer to the [[Gazetteer Reference]] page.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Healing Services
>
> A character can hire Briannon's divine spellcasting services ; she can heal either **5 Wounds** or **5 Madness** at a cost of  **-1**. The kind devotee of Spectra always puts half of her earnings back into the church, and sleeps well at night thanks to this selflessness.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Game State: Dusktide Rising
>
> While Helkas is under siege during the [[Dusktide Rising]] Event, Glinthome's doors are closed and its standard inhabitants are nowhere to be found.
