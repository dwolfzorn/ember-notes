# The Last Satchel

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Situated beneath an overhang of the Leviathan's bones, this small occultist's shop is bedecked with a wide array of components, reagents, concoctions, and curiosities.
>
> An aged wooden sign that hangs above its threshold designates this place as "The Last Satchel," and you can't help but scrutinize the strange and interesting wares that line its cluttered shelves.

The shelves of The Last Satchel are lined with an array of occult wares and arcane materials, including novel spell components developed by proprietor **Lorettia Ben-Wren** (Waerd Human, she/her) from the extracts of Leviathan Mine. Lorettia is a patron of her own alchemical arts, and is known for keeping late hours at the shop while studying her reagents with unfettered focus.

For more information about Lorettia Ben-Wren, refer to the [[Gazetteer Reference]] page.
