# Mine Elevator

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This rickety wooden elevator descends from the ground into the dark bowels of the Leviathan Mine below. Its aged timber, while sturdy and secure, creaks and shudders as the trusty lift moves beneath the rigid tension of steel chains and hempen ropes.

This aged elevator allows characters to move from "Ground Level" to "Below Ground," as described on the [[Overview]] page. It takes the elevator one full turn to move from one level of elevation to another.
