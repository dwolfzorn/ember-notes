# Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The [[Helkas]] Area Map depicts the central section of town, including a few of its most prominent locations. It is featured in the [[Dusktide Rising]] Event of the [[The Winding Trail]] Main Quest.
>
> - The map depicts three Scene Levels, each representing a different area of Helkas.
> - The party can also explore this Area Map during the [[Dusktide Revelry]] and [[Dusktide Destruction]] Events, which are each initially configured for their own dynamic Vistas.

## Gameplay Details

The areas of Helkas have the following features unless specified otherwise in the text.

### Levels & Elevation

The Helkas Scene features a total of three vertical Levels of elevation, including:

- **Green:** The central tier of the town includes most of its featured locations of interest and is set to an elevation of 0. The area is entered from either the southern edge or the western edge of the map.
- **Boneway:** The Boneway curves around the settlement, and is set to an elevation of +50. A 10-foot-wide wooden stairway on the eastern edge of town allows easy access to this area. Characters who arrive in town can arrive directly on the upper level, entering via the northeastern or northwestern corners of the map.
- **Mine:** The Mine lies below Helkas, and is set to an elevation of -40. Descending to the mine entrance is accomplished by using the [[Unknown]]. Characters who arrive in town can enter this area directly via the southwestern corner of the map.

### Illumination

Exterior sections of the Area Map are illuminated by the bright light of the sunlight during the day, and are lit with dim light at night — cast from the moons and a multitude of smaller light sources (braziers, firelight, and small lanterns). Interior sections of the map are lit by these numerous light sources, the majority of which shed bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for another 20 feet.

### Terrain

The interior and exterior locations of the map primarily consist of normal terrain.
