# Council Hall

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A sign above the doorway labels this proud wooden edifice as "Council Hall." Certainly, this multi-chambered meeting place must host the settlement's Town Council when they meet, as evidenced by the historical pomp of its decoration and the relative quietude found within.
>
> The rooms open to the public are kept impressively clean and organized.

Council Hall serves as the meeting place for the settlement's five-person volunteer Town Council, a group of unpaid elected officials that meet semiweekly to discuss matters of local importance. The various chambers here show signs of regular use by the Council and local citizens of Helkas alike, who are allowed to use the northernmost meeting room as a gathering place (if the proper requisition forms are appropriately filled out, of course).

The town magistrate **Sadri Zhalimorne** uses the southernmost chamber here as her office, and can be found here at various times throughout any regular work week.
