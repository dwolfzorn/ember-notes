# Leviathan Mine

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The ground opens up here to reveal a subterranean sprawl conjoined with the skeletal remains of the ancient Leviathan. It's clear that the residents of Helkas use this as one of the primary ways to enter the local mine. Even from here, you can spot signs of the strange, arcane ores that lurk within, identifiable by traces of their remarkable lambent radiation.

The massive earthen cavity here serves as one of Leviathan Mine's primary entrances, and is accessible by way of a steel and timber lift connected to the ground level near [[Manfryd's Anvil and Stone]].

During normal working hours, this area is usually overseen by the mine's gregarious foreman, Hode Cinderhaft (Arcturian Fej, he/him). For more information about Hode Cinderhaft, refer to the [[Gazetteer Reference]] page.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Strange Excavations
>
> Leviathan Mine is the source of several rare arcane components — one of which is unique to Helkas, while another can only be harvested during the lunar conjunction known as Dusktide. This strange, enchanted mine is sometimes referred to as "The Jewel of Helkas," and is responsible for the frontier settlement's enduring prosperity.
>
> The items mined here include:
>
> - [[Dreth Sap]]
> - [[M’abb Salts]]
> - [[Steelmarrow]]
>
> Any character who examines the mine's interior and makes a successful **Arcana (DC 15)** or **Wilderness (DC 15)** check is able to recall most of the properties of these arcane materials, and is aware of their role as rare ingredients in various crafting recipes.
>
> - **Knowledge: Alchemy**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Crafts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Game State: Dusktide Rising
>
> While Helkas is under siege during the [[Dusktide Rising]] Event, Leviathan Mine is abandoned and its standard inhabitants are nowhere to be found.
