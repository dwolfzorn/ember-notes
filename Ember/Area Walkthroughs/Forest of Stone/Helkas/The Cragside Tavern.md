# The Cragside Tavern

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The front room of this charming alehouse is furnished with six tables and two bars for its revolving supply of patrons. Smells of hearty stew and spiced cobbler linger in the air, and the tavern's hearth crackles with a syncopated rhythm that almost sounds like music amidst the din of the town outside.

The aptly-named Cragside Tavern is a charming watering hole that caters first and foremost to travelers and the local working-class. Maintained by the lively barkeep **Eveline Doss** (Arcturian Human, they/them), the Cragside is a place where people from every corner of Aterica can rub elbows with the storied laborers of Leviathan Mine.

For more information about Eveline Doss, refer to the [[Gazetteer Reference]] page.

> [!tip] Exploration

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Game State: Dusktide Rising
>
> While Helkas is under siege during the [[Dusktide Rising]] Event, The Cragside Tavern is closed for business and its standard inhabitants are nowhere to be found.
