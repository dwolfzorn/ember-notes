# The Boneway

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The colossal skeleton of some long-dead, wormlike creature spans the entire settlement, wrapping its way up and around Helkas like a gruesome shawl. When the light hits them just so, these ossified remains seem to glitter with a strange, preternatural sparkling.
>
> A crude but reliable railway has been installed upon the spine of the serpentine skeleton, enabling the transport of heavy ore to and fro with relative ease.

The Boneway is the colloquial name for this lengthy, exposed portion of the Leviathan that encircles the center of town. Equipped with a meager railway line, most of the flesh of this section of the dead Leviathan has been stripped clean — leaving nothing but petrified bone and craggy earth in its stead.
