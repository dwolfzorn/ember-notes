# Ooze Pool

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small niche would be empty if it wasn't for the oozes — large numbers of them have gathered here and appear to be peacefully coexisting. They do not have the glow of the metallic oozes that you've seen, and are seemingly closer in temperament to Tauric's ooze, Squish. They are circled around a long cylindrical object made of metal that appears to have a black-painted handle on one end. A symbol is painted on the wall in silver paint.

Oozes are congregated here, but they appear friendly and do not glow like the oozes that have attacked the party thus far. These [[Oozeling]] can be recruited if the characters have learned how to befriend them from Tauric in [[Stone Life]]. If attacked, the oozes here simply flee, squeezing into crevices in the rock and disappearing into the stone.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Missing Lever
>
> The cylindrical object that the oozelings are congregated around is the [[Yakoshta Blue Track Lever]] that was removed from the switch on the southern end of the [[Blue Track]], which connects the [[Glowing Ore Pit]] to the [[Excavation Pit]].
>
> As long as the characters do not demonstrate any hostility, the oozelings do not attempt to stop them from taking the lever.

Jasper has been conducting experiments here, intentionally exposing the oozelings to small doses of the Alchemical Contaminant.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Alchemical Contaminant
>
> If any of the oozelings are exposed to [[Aedir Construct Fluid]], they begin to glow and slowly transform. After one minute passes, roll a `[[/gmroll 1d6]]` and consult the following results to see what sort of ooze they transform into:
>
> - **Result of 1 or 2:** [[Luminous Iron Ooze]]
> - **Result of 3 or 4**: [[Luminous Copper Ooze]]
> - **Result of 5**: [[Luminous Silver Ooze]]
> - **Result of 6:** [[Luminous Gold Ooze]]
>
> The transformed ooze immediately attacks.

The symbol upon the wall is recognizable and similar to others found elsewhere in the mine.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### An Attractive Symbol
>
> The normal symbol found on the wall in the mine has been painted over with a new symbol, which appears hastily painted. If the party investigates it, they can discover the following:
>
> - The symbol was painted within the last day or two.
> - The symbol matches the one found in the Ooze Pool in the Forest of Stone.
> - The symbol matches the symbol in Jasper's notes in his office.
> - The symbol has been painted with silver paint matching the paint found in the Old Ore Pit.
>
> The party can attempt to decipher the symbol if they have not already. Any character who makes a successful `[[/check investigation 13]]` determines that it is meant to attract oozes to this location.

> [!info] Social
> #### Befriending the Oozes
>
> If the party has learned of the process to befriend an ooze, they can befriend an [[Oozeling]] here by removing it from the pool with a successful **Wilderness (DC 12)** check, then feeding it something in their possession (such as [[Heartmoss]]). Only one oozeling may be acquired per character.
