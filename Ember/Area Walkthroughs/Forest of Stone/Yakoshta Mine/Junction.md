# Junction

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Vertical and horizontal tracks meet at a circular platform with two concentric rings. Between them is an empty slot in the floor.
>
> This junction appears to be a rotating platform that would allow mine carts to change the orientation of their travel. A strange-looking socket stands empty just nearby as if expecting something to be placed within.
>
> Further to the east lies a large elevator cage with something vaguely blue inside.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Missing Wheel
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Science (DC 15, Passive)** check can intuit that the empty socket is designed to accommodate a control rod or wheel of some sort.
>
> - **Knowledge: Machines** : The character automatically succeeds on this check.
>
> The wheel is located in the [[Unknown]].

There are two reachable clusters of [[Kaleidoscope Crystal]] embedded in the walls, which can be removed as described here: [[Kaleidoscope Crystals]].
