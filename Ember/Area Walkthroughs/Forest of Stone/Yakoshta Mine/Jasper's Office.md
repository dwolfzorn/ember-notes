# Jasper's Office

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The simple wood and iron desk at the back of the room is overflowing with papers, including a calendar, a series of ledgers filled with numbers, and page after page of the same set of large hand-drawn symbols.

Other than the papers, a desk, and a chair, the office is somewhat unremarkable.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring Jasper's Office
>
> A simple Search reveals the following:
>
> - Misplaced clutter in a corner of the room.
> - The **silver symbol of a desk** on the back wall. It closely resembles the style and size of the symbol found in the [[Unknown]].
> - An [[Yakoshta Mine Maps]], which provides a general layout of the mine labeled with track switches and rooms.
> - [[Jasper's Key Ring]]
> - [[Jasper's Notebook]]
>
> #### Hidden Valuables
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** check while investigating the cluttered corner discovers a small [[Locked Box]].

## Jasper's Keys

The large ring of keys in Jasper's office includes keys of all shapes and sizes:

- Four large keys, which appear a bit sooty.
- Six medium-sized keys, some more worn down than others.
- One small key, warm to the touch.

The keyring includes keys for all locked doors and objects in the mine, including the Elevator Key and Storage Key (although neither key is labeled).

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Finding the Right Key
>
> If a character must match a key to a lock under duress (during a combat encounter, for example), they can make a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check to examine the lock and identify the correct key.

## Jasper's Notebook

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> It's clear from the entries in Jasper's notebook that he has been trying to decipher some sort of symbol — after several attempts with cryptic notes, one is clearly circled. It is the same as the symbol found at the bottom of the ooze pool in the Forest of Stone.
>
> The notebook also contains notes on the aftermath of the earthquake. Two entries in particular catch your eye. The first reads:
>
> *"Must clear the rubble in the Excavation Pit before we can start repairs. Will order some more Blast Flasks. This will cut into profits. Must tell Sellen."*
>
> A second entry, scribbled in an excited hand, reads:
>
> *"Blast Flasks arriving shortly. Just in time. Ooze attacks are bad enough without exits being blocked. Will lock it up and tell folks to be careful … last thing we need is some kind of chain reaction."*

The characters may attempt to decipher the strange symbol.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Deciphering the Symbol
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 13)** check to examine the symbol, or a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check to examine the notes, determines that the mark attracts oozes to it.
>
> - **Language: Pathward**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Found Symbol in** [[Stone Life]]: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

## The Locked Box

The characters may investigate the locked box.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Unlocking the Box
>
> The [[Locked Box]] is simple and constructed from stone. It has a rudimentary keyhole and a rune inscribed on its top. Any character who makes a successful **Society (DC 12)** check recognizes the symbol as a symbol of warning or danger.
>
> - **Language: Pathward**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
>
> The lock is simple and can be opened by anyone capable of using lockpicks who makes a successful **Stealth (DC 15)** check. Alternatively, the lock can be forced open with a successful `[[/skillcheck athletics 15]]`check.
>
> - If opened with the smallest key on [[Jasper's Key Ring]], the trap (see below) is not triggered.
>
> #### Box Contents
>
> The locked box contains [[Jasper's Diary]], and three jars: one filled with a glowing liquid, one filled with a gold-colored liquid, and one filled with a silver-colored liquid. The gold and silver-colored liquids are easily identified as [[Luminous Pooled Gold]] and [[Luminous Liquid Silver]], respectively.
>
> #### Luminous Evidence
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 13)** check while examining the glowing vial of liquid identifies it as a [[Caustic Phial]].
>
> - **Knowledge: Alchemy**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - Seen Mine Contaminant: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - Encountered [[Flying Predator]]: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
>
> A small symbol on the vial matches a symbol on the [[Yakoshta Mine Maps]], labeled as the [[Glowing Ore Pit]].

The lock on the [[Locked Box]] is trapped.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### **It's A Trap**
>
> The trap activates if the lock is picked or forced open. It can be disarmed with a successful **Arcana (DC 15)** check or by scratching out the runic mark with a chisel or comparable tool.
>
> If the trap is activated, the rune on the box begins to glow hot and hiss as a powerful burst of magical force radiates from its center: all creatures within 10 feet of the box must make a successful **Athletics (DC 14)** check or suffer the **exploding box (Hazard 4, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)**.
