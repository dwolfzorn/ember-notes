# Makeshift Barricade

If the party plans to use the mining cart and explosives to detonate the giant ooze, they will need to send it into the [[Excavation Pit]] from here. This involves two challenges: replacing the track switch and clearing a makeshift barricade.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Missing Lever
>
> The track switch here is stuck sending carts north or east, instead of into the Excavation Pit to the west. Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 15)** check while examining the switch realizes that an important component is missing: the [[Yakoshta Blue Track Lever]].
>
> - **Knowledge: Machines**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> The lever is located in the [[Ooze Pool]], having been taken there by friendly [[Oozeling]] worried about the [[Giant Luminous Ooze]] growth.

A pile of debris sits on the track:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A tangled pile of metal and bits of kaleidoscope crystal sits in the center of the tracks. It is covered with goo that trails into the next room, as if something dragged it this way and then dropped it in its path.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Debris on the Tracks
>
> If the cart is sent between the [[Glowing Ore Pit]] and the [[Excavation Pit]] without the debris being cleared, it crashes to a stop against the barricade.
>
> For the cart to continue, the debris must be cleared. Unless done cautiously, this process may create noise that alerts the [[Giant Luminous Ooze]] in the room beyond.
>
> Any character can clear the debris over the course of 10 minutes. That character must make a successful **Stealth (DC 15)** check to avoid alerting the giant ooze.
