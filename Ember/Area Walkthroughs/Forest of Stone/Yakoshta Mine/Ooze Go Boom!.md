# Ooze Go Boom!

One way for the party to resolve the presence of the [[Giant Luminous Ooze]] in the [[Excavation Pit]] is to use the [[Barrel of Blast Powder]] intended for clearing the collapsed southern passage. The giant ooze is known to devour the contents of mine carts in the area, a predilection which can be used to bait it into a deadly explosion.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Puzzle Clues
>
> There are several clues the party can discover for using the explosives in this way:
>
> 1. The miners at the [[Loading Zone]] are using individual explosive flasks as weapons and mention that there is a larger stash. If the party talks to Jasper from across the bridge, he notes this as well.
> 2. The notes in [[Jasper's Office]] reveal that a shipment of powerful explosives was delivered to be used in clearing the collapsed passageway of the southwest cavern.
> 3. The surviving miners in the [[Old Ore Pit]] can confirm the location of the explosives in the [[Supply Cache]] and provide the control rod needed to operate the [[Junction]].
>
> If the party is suitably inquisitive about the potential use of explosives to combat the Giant Ooze, you are encouraged to provide additional clues which reinforce the steps necessary to accomplish the solution.

## Rigging the Trap

Utilizing this solution requires several specific steps:

1. The blue track cart is needed to transport the explosive powder. The blue track cart is initially locked within the [[Elevator]], the key to which is located on a large ring of keys in [[Jasper's Office]].
2. [[The Junction]]needs to be rotated with the blue cart on top of it so that the cart can reach the other section of track. Rotating the junction requires recovering the [[Yakoshta Junction Wheel]] from the survivors at the [[Old Ore Pit]].
3. The [[Barrel of Blast Powder]]from the [[Supply Cache]] must be loaded onto the blue cart.
4. The Missing Switch is required to switch the final track leading into the [[Glowing Ore Pit]] from the east side. This switch was taken by the friendly oozes in the [[Ooze Pool]].
5. The makeshift barricade blocking the entrance to the [[Excavation Pit]] must be cleared so the cart can proceed into that area, which contains the giant luminous ooze.

## Explosive Results

If the party has successfully retrieved the blue track cart, loaded it with explosives, restored the missing switch lever and junction wheel, and cleared the makeshift barricade, they can send the explosives-laden cart down the track into the [[Excavation Pit]], where the giant luminous ooze will hungrily eat it and cause its own downfall.

This strategy allows a clever party to eliminate the Giant Luminous Ooze without needing to confront it directly in combat.
