# Mine Cart Loading Station

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This stone platform is the last walkable space before the mine tracks that head off into the dim light of the chasm ahead of you. A few older piles of materials that have been run over by the ooze during its attacks are on either side. They are roughed up, but mostly intact. A few Kaleidoscope Crystals embedded in the walls provide a slight shifting glow.

A few old scraps of ore and supplies lie in a disarrayed pile near the tracks. Traces of copper ooze can be seen on the remaining supplies.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Jasper's Hypothesis
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** check while searching the pile finds a scattering of [[Luminous Copper Flakes]] and a note that reads:
>
> > OOZES = PROFIT? Check value of copper flakes in Helkas!
>
> Any party members who have been to [[Jasper's Office]] can recognize the note as being in Jasper's handwriting.

There are four [[Kaleidoscope Crystal]] embedded in the walls, which can be removed as described here: [[Kaleidoscope Crystals]].
