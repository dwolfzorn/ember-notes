# Cavern Bridge

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The long rope bridge spans the chasm below. You dare a glance downward and see the twinkling lights of kaleidoscope crystals descending until the light recedes entirely from view. You surmise that a fall from here would allow you just enough time to contemplate your mistakes before meeting a tragic end.

The cavern bridge is fully operable. It stretches between the back end of the [[Old Ore Pit]] and the upper overlook of the [[Glowing Ore Pit]]. It can be used without incident.
