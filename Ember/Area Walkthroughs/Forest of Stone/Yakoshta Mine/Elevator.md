# Elevator

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You step closer, inspecting the contents of the surface-to-shaft elevator. Inside, you spy a mine cart with blue paint on both sides, separated from you by a locked grate. The grate appears to be secured via a locked panel with a keyhole in the center, and there is a faint aura of magic around it. A note affixed to the grate reads:
>
> > BEWARE: Oozes have been tearing through this grate, so I've added a bit of juice to the lock that will make them think twice before trying it again. If you need to get through, see me for the key or you **will** regret it.
> >
> > Jasper

The elevator has been magically warded to stop the amorphous oozes from entering between the cracks.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Warded Door
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 13)** or **Awareness (DC 13)** check can confirm that the door has been magically warded against entry.
>
> - A character with the [[Recognize Spellcraft]] talent can make a successful **Arcana (DC 15)** check to disable the ward.
> - The elevator can be safely opened withthe Elevator Key from the Ring of Keys found in [[Jasper's Office]].
>
> Any character who makes an attempt to open the door without the key while the ward is active must must make a successful**Athletics (DC 14)** check or face **Deterring Ward (Hazard 7, Fortitude, Health, Bludgeoning)** .
>
> If the ward on the door has been dispelled, the lock can be picked with a successful**Stealth (DC 14)** check using a set of lockpicks. Alternatively, the door can be forced open with a successful `[[/skillCheck athletics 15 check.
>
> If the party has Jasper's Keys, but does not know which one is correct, they can identify the correct key with a successful **Arcana (DC 12)**check to reveal which key has been enchanted to disrupt the magical ward. Alternatively, the[[Recognize Spellcraft]]` talent can also reveal the correct key to use.

Once the door is open, the party may access and use the blue mining cart that was locked inside, and may use the elevator at any time to return to the surface near [[Yakoshta]].
