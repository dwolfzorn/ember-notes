# Waterfall Bridge

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The long wooden bridge is missing half of its planks and the ropes that stretch across the chasm seem frayed. The bridge shifts slowly back and forth, creaking as it does. On the far side of the bridge, rock has crumbled onto a landing, blocking off the sight of anything beyond it.

The entrance bridge has been partially destroyed by the recent earthquake. If characters step onto the bridge, the creaking becomes stronger and is accompanied by the sound of cracking. Jasper, the mine supervisor, is on the other side of the bridge; he immediately calls out in a loud whisper from behind the pile of rocks.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> > Hello? Whoever that is, please don't come any closer. The bridge isn't stable!

Characters can speak to Jasper from their current location in [[Saving Jasper]].

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Calamitous Crossing
>
> If, despite the warnings and Jasper's request that they do not, the party attempts to continue across the bridge, one of the ropes snaps, sending the bridge lurching to the side. Anyone on the bridge when this happens must make a successful **Athletics (DC 14)** check or end up hanging from the rope over the chasm below. They can be pulled up by someone else on the bridge — or get back to the safety of the cavern — with a successful **Athletics (DC 12)** check. If both checks fail, the character may choose to gain the **Exhausted** condition to slowly pull their way back up.
