# Sparring Alcove

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This circular alcove is lit by the flames of a single torch ensconced on the southern wall, and appears to be some sort of modest sparring or training room. A lone Toothbreaker warrior assaults a combat dummy in the center of the chamber with kicks and unarmed blows, shouting loudly with each new strike. Indeed, the clamor of this martial performance is so loud and intense, the thug seems oblivious to your initial approach.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Focused Training
>
> The training thug is focused on his martial exercises. Characters who approach and move into the room can avoid detection with a successful **Stealth (DC 13)** check.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Toothbreakers
>
> There is a solitary [[Toothbreaker Thug]] in this room practicing martial arts.
>
> #### Raising the Alarm
>
> This room contains an alarm bell, which the Toothbreaker Thug will ring if they become aware of any interlopers.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Locked Door
>
> The door to the [[Planning Room]] is locked.
>
> Two corresponding [[Toothbreaker Planning Key]] can be found elsewhere in the hideout: one is in the possession of Vaafo the Scaletamer in [[Scalemaw Training]], while another is currently held by [[Raster Thorn]] himself.
