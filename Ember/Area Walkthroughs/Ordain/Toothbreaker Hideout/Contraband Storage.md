# Contraband Storage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cluttered chamber is packed to the brim with a chaotic assortment of spoiled goods and ramshackle equipment, stacked around the room in a haphazard fashion. A large shelf on the northern wall — which at one point offered some hope of organization — has collapsed, its wood utterly rotted from the pervasive moisture in the local air. It's evident that this room sees regular use, but no effort has been made to restore order here for quite some time.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Searching the Storage
>
> Any character who searches the room and succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 13)** check is able to locate an unlocked lockbox on top of a crate, containing six [[Potions of Healing]] wrapped tightly in burlap.
>
> - **Critical Success**. The character notices a second lockbox among the wrecked shelving, which contains 3 [[Iolite]] gemstones and  **200**.
>
> #### Safe Resting
>
> Although it sees regular use, this room is only visited occasionally by the Toothbreakers, and as-needed. Furthermore, it can be locked from the inside, making it one of the few safe places in the hideout where the characters can benefit from an uninterrupted Recovery.

## Northern Hallway

When exiting this chamber via the southernmost door, the characters will overhear the following muffled dialog coming from behind the closed door of the [[Interrogation Room]] across the hallway:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you make your way into the hall, you notice the muffled sound of a masculine voice behind another door to the south.
>
> > … you should have told us sooner, but now it's too late, dear Fen. Far too late.
>
> A second, high-pitched voice interrupts the first with a panic-stricken tone:
>
> > No! Wait! I can still be …
>
> Panic turns to pain as the second voice screams out with unmistakable agony from some unknown source. This wailing continues unabated for several seconds until a wet gargling overtakes it, and all falls conspicuously silent.

Aside from the obvious ramifications of this exchange, the party is unable to deduce anything further without opening the door to the [[Interrogation Room]] itself.
