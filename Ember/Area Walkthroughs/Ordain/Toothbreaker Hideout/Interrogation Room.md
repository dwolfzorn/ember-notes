# Interrogation Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The chamber before you presents a horrific scene … A rusty cage hangs from a gibbet near the southern wall, suspended by chains. An ichor-stained table near the center of the room is decorated with instruments of torture. And a dead man is bound to a wooden chair, covered in his own blood and vomit.
>
> Behind the chair, a Toothbreaker clad in decorative bone armor leans over this ill-fated prisoner, torture tools firmly in-hand. Furthermore, a large reptilian beasts lurks at the victim's feet, sniffing hungrily at the air as its four crimson eyes survey the blood-soaked corpse.

There are two primary circumstances in which the party will find themselves in this room. Most likely they are trespassing in the hideout, in which case they will be treated as interlopers and attacked. Alternatively, they may have been taken prisoner by the Toothbreakers and sent to Kierelin for the painful extraction of information. In either case, combat here is highly likely.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Fighting the Interrogator
>
> Kierelin is a [[Toothbreaker Scaletamer]], and is accompanied by a highly-trained [[Scalemaw]] companion. They follow the tactics described in [[Gameplay Details]].
>
> #### Kierelin's Key
>
> Kierelin is in possession of the [[Toothbreaker Prison Key]].

This room is sufficiently isolated, and has no alarm bell. It's unlikely that combat here will alert other Toothbreakers in chambers nearby. If the characters take a closer look at the interrogator's recent victim in the chair, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The victim in the chair appears to be a human man whose once-fashionable garb is badly torn and thoroughly sodden with blood. A multitude of wounds confirm the obvious: this man is dead following his torture at the hands of the Toothbreakers.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Tortured Victim
>
> Any character who examines the body with a successful **Medicine (DC 12)** check is able to conclude that the man is very recently deceased; the body is still warm, and the blood in some of his wounds has yet to coagulate.
>
> - **Critical Success** You identify that among the cuts, and lashes the cause of death was a final deep laceration to the side of the neck.
>
> Any character who succeeds on an **Awareness (DC 14)** check is able to spot a necklace tucked beneath the victim's tunic. The necklace features a small silver medallion, upon which is embossed a large key with butterfly wings:
>
> - The symbol is that of the [[Hallows]] which can be recognized by a character with **Culture: Ordani** or a successful **Society (DC 15)** check.

The tortured victim was named Eltien Fen, a representative of the Hallows that was subsequently captured and interrogated by the Toothbreakers. More information about Eltien's identity and purpose is contained in the letter found in the [[Tea Room]].

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Being Interrogated
>
> Should a narrative need arise for the Toothbreakers to interrogate the party, you might involve Kierelin in that gameplay sequence.
>
> Rules are not provided for how such a sequence would transpire, and the themes involved are delicate ones — themes you should only explore using caution with transparent consent from all players at the table.
