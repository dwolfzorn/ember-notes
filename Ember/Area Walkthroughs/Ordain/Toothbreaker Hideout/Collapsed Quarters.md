# Collapsed Quarters

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> There are several rooms off a narrow canalside hallway that have collapsed into from rubble, their ceilings partially caved in. The rubble is slick with moisture and overgrown with vines and mushrooms which thrive in the damp climate of the waterworks. No light or sound comes from within and it seems clear that the Toothbreakers do not frequent this area.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Hidden Stash
>
> The northern collapsed room is only accessible by traversing the rubble through the southern side. If explored, the party discovers a footlocker stashed amidst the rubble. Inside the footlocker is the following treasure:
>
> - **42 17** in loose coin.
> - One set of [[Leatherworker's Tools]]
> - Four yards of [[Silk]]
> - A leather quivver containing 10 [[Arrow +1]]
>
> #### Safe Resting
>
> This area is one of the few in the hideout that affords the opportunity to benefit from an uninterrupted Recovery.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Coming Through!
>
> The weakened wall on the south side of the room can be broken through, with a succesful **Athletics (DC 17)** check, opening a hole and spilling debris out into [[Unknown]].
