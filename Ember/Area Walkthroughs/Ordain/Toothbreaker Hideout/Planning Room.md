# Planning Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The erudition of this large square chamber feels out of place among the relative squalor of the hideout's other rooms. Surrounded by more than a half-dozen rickety chairs, a huge table at the center of the chamber is decorated with an assortment of maps, plans, and other accoutrements; and three smaller tables line the perimeter of the room like desks, each of them covered in some kind of functional clutter.

There are three Toothbreakers Thugs here, led by one of Raster Thorn's ringleaders, a Toothbreaker known as Taamsin the Mastermind.

If the party was escorted here by the guards in the [[Guardroom]], they must speak with Taamsin to convince him of their pretended purpose.

> [!info] Social
> #### Business Propositions
>
> "The Mastermind" is more cunning and insightful than his peers, so convincing Taamsin that the characters are prospective partners or informants for the Toothbreakers is a challenging act of deception.
>
> To persuade the lieutenant of their cause, the party must succeed on a **Deception (DC 20, Group)** check.
>
> - Before making this check, characters may attempt a **Diplomacy (DC 20)** or `[[/skillCheck, performance 20]]` check to charm Taamsin. Success provides **+2 Boons** for the subsequent Deception check.
> - Characters with **Knowledge: Crime**, **Knowledge: Trade**, or **Knowledge: Intrigue** are better suited to crafting a proposition and have **+2 Boons** on the Deception check.
> - Taamsin will accept a bribe of  **-100** or greater which counts as an automatic success on the Deception check for that player. If the party attempts to bribe Taamsin with any lesser amount, he takes the money but it counts as a failure towards the group Deception check.
>
> If Taamsin can be deceived, he will arrange for the party to meet with [[Raster Thorn]] to discuss matters in the [[Tea Room]], where Raster receives visiting dignitaries.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Taamsin's Key
>
> Taamsin is in possession of a [[Toothbreaker Throne Room Key]].
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Stealth (DC 20)** check is able to pick Taamsin's pocket to purloin the key. Failure on this check immediately turns Taamsin hostile.

If diplomacy fails, or if the party is caught trespassing in this area, then combat is inevitable.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Toothbreakers
>
> The Toothbreakers here follow the tactics described in [[Gameplay Details]].
>
> #### Raising the Alarm
>
> There is no alarm bell in this room directly. As long as the party keeps combat contained to this room, it is unlikely to alert other Toothbreakers.
>
> There are two nearby alarms, however: one in the hallway to the north near the [[Barracks]], and another in the small [[Sparring Alcove]] to the west. If opportunity allows during combat, one or more Toothbreakers will move away to attempt raising the alarm.

When the room is cleared of Toothbreakers, the party may loot it's contents.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Planning Room Treasure
>
> In addition to the equipment possessed by the Toothbreakers themselves, this room contains:
>
> - An assortment of 10 simple melee weapons stored in racks around the room.
> - One [[Heavy Crossbow]] and 13 [[Bolts]]
> - 8 [[Javelins]]
> - One set of [[Dice]]
> - One [[Iolite]] gemstone and loose coin including  **8 12**
> - One [[Elemental Gem (Emerald)]]
> - One [[Scroll of Invisibility]]
> - One [[Scroll of Speak with Animals]]
>
> #### Evidence of Criminality
>
> Should the party wish to take legal action against the Toothbreakers for their crimes, an abundance of evidence can be found in this room to satisfy the requirements for prosecution via the Veiled Chain.
>
> For the party to take action here, they would need to gather some of the papers and other elements laid out on the table, then bring them to the [[The Hallows]] and the Veiled Chain. This is beyond the scope of the intent for this questline, but it would enable the Veiled Chain to move openly and divisively against the Toothbreakers gang. However, it's likely that the party will have already solved the problem by defeating Raster Thorn at this point.
