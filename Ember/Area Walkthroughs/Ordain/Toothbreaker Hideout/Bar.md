# Bar

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> On the far western side of the central cistern, a pair of connected circular chambers house a simplistic but cozy bar area. The rooms are illuminated gently by flickering torches and the tables here are covered with mugs, tankards, cards, dice, and other amusements.
>
> You spy a chalkboard that features gambling odds for those seeking to wager on fights taking place in the cistern bloodring.

> [!info] Social
> #### Rest and Relaxation
>
> If the party is in this area without having antagonized the Toothbreakers, they can chat with some of them.
>
> Characters attempting to extract information from a Toothbreaker at the bar can do so by succeeding on a **Diplomacy (DC 14)** check which gains one of the following pieces of information per attempt.
>
> |  | #### Toothbreaker Rumors |
> | --- | --- |
> | 1 | > Sometimes the sound of all this water just drives me insane. I asked if we could shut off the sluices using those old valves up by Vaafo's kennel, but Raster says it's got to stay on. I've no idea why, but it's best not to question the boss. |
> | 2 | > It's great having our own place here, but some parts of this shithole are falling apart. Those old rooms next to Raster's quarters are just rubble now. We're gonna have to move the boss soon. |
> | 3 | > That Hallows scum Eltien is finally getting what's coming to him. He's been picking and choosing which information to pass Raster, probably playing both sides. Well, now he's going to find out that rats are Scalemaw food. |
> | 4 | > This new drink at the bar is wild. You've got to try it while you're here. Humans get the best lift — trust me! But I wouldn't recommend it to Drakons … |
> | 5 | > Turns out one of the Scalemaws went berserk a few days ago. Something caused it to snap, and even Vaafo couldn't manage to calm the thing down. Next thing they knew, the ornery reptile slipped away into the canals. Hell, I even heard it in the water the last time I went to take a shit! |
> | 6 | > A few months back, ol' Raster disappeared for a week or so. Well … you didn't hear this from me, but word has it that he had to stay in one of them Cindaric healing houses for a while because of something that near killed him. No idea what. |
>
> If the party fails this check three times, the Toothbreakers communicate their displeasure to Ukkfal who demands that the party "shut up, or leave".
>
> #### Bloodring Gambling
>
> Characters may gamble on fights occurring in the [[Cistern Bloodring]], wagering up to a maximum of  **10** per fight. All fights have even odds and pay double-or-nothing. One hour of time passes between every fight.
>
> - If a character loses a bet on the Bloodring, they gain **+2 Boons** to their next persuasion attempt to extract information.
> - If a character wins a bet on the Bloodring, they have **-2 Banes** to their next persuasion attempt to extract information.

If the party investigates the southern room of the bar and inspects the countertop table on the southwest side they notice an unusual collection of vials and alchemical materials.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A crate of glass flasks sits next to a suspended carafe of simmering liquid that is kept warm by a burning flame underneath. The viscous surface of the liquid glistens like liquid metal. Next to the vials on the counter are several syringes that seem to be used for injection of the substance.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Mutagenic Serum
>
> The Toothbreakers have a source of [[Gleaming Distillation]], provided to them by [[Kaftor Brenk]] and the Mutagists.
>
> The Toothbreakers here are possessive over the substance, but their greed makes them willing to part with samples in exchange for a fee of  **50** per vial.
>
> The party may attempt to persuade the Toothbreakers for a better deal, a character who succeeds on a **Diplomacy (DC 16)** check is given a free sample of the substance.
>
> - **Critical Success**: Every member of the party is given a sample.
>
> Consumption of the [[Gleaming Distillation]] via intravenous injection has the following effects:
>
> - It produces a momentary exuberance and empowering effect in Humans which is thrilling (and potentially addictive) to experience.
> - It produces deleterious and nauseating effects on Drakon who are repulsed by the experience.
> - Other ancestries experience momentary lightheadedness followed by several hours of languor accompanied by minor hallucinations.
> - If injected into a [[Scalemaw]] it causes them to become enraged, ignoring all commands and attacking whatever creatures are closest by.
>
> Consumption of the serum by drinking makes the imbiber sick and affected by the **Poisoned** condition for 24 hours.
