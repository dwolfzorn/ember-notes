# Filthy Bathroom

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The opulence of this once-proud bathroom chamber has faded, replaced by the squalor of its stained floor and mildew-covered fixtures. If the rank odor here is any indication, this restroom is over-used and under-cleaned. Nevertheless, a grand window on the southern wall overlooks Ordain's endless cityscape with an air of forgotten dignity.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Risky Resting
>
> The party can attempt to rest here, but the endeavor is a risky one. Roll `[[/gmroll 1d4]]`, and on a result of 1, the party's rest is interrupted by a [[Toothbreaker Thug]] entering the chamber to relieve themselves.

> [!info] Social
> #### Toilet Interrogation
>
> If the party happens to catch a Toothbreaker using the commode, they may seize this opportunity to interrogate this particular thug away from the scrutiny of their criminal allies.
>
> The unfortunate gang member can be interrogated while in this vulnerable circumstance with one or more successful **Intimidation (DC 14)** checks. Each successful check results in the party learning one piece of information from the table below:
