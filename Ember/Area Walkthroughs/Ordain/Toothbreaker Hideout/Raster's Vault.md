# Raster's Vault

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The conduits and flooring of this small chamber suggest it was once used as a bathroom, but the copious piles of coin and other eye-catching baubles piled up here suggest that it's been converted into a vault for the storage of prized possessions. Light cascades through a large window on the southern wall, causing the treasured belongings stored within to scintillate with alluring brilliance.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Locked Vault
>
> The door to the east is locked, and the only key is located on [[Raster's Keyring]], in his possession. Alternately, the lock can be picked with a successful **Stealth (DC 25)** check.
>
> #### Raster's Treasure
>
> Plundering the vault yields a substantial amount of treasure, including:
>
> - Loose coin totaling  **1732**
> - One [[Potion of Climbing]]
> - One [[Potion of Growth]]
> - One [[Fine Jeweled Ring]]
> - The [[Bust of Avilla]]
