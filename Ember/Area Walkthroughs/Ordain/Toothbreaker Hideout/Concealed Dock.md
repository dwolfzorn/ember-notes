# Concealed Dock

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The canal water slows to a lazy ebb as it laps against the moss-covered timber of a furtive wooden dock. An over-laden rowboat is tied to the southwestern corner of the dock, where it bobs quietly with the constant sloshing of water.
>
> On the north side of the docks, a small wooden chair leans back against the wall with someone slumped inside its rickety confines. A table next to the chair is decorated with a smoking pipe and a few empty glass vials.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### A Sleeping Sentry
>
> A solitary [[Toothbreaker Thug]] is asleep at his post here. Any loud noises or physical disturbance within the area will awaken him, but he otherwise sleeps soundly due to the sedative effects of a Mutagenic Serum that he recently consumed.
>
> #### Raising the Alarm
>
> If the guard is awoken and alert to intruders, he will immediately ring the alarm bell on the wall in this area to alert the Toothbreakers in the [[Guardroom]] and [[Interrogation Room]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Sneaking Through
>
> The party can move through this area without waking the sentry provided they succeed on a **Stealth (DC 10, Group)** check.
>
> Characters with a **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** notice the gleam of a brass key dangling from the guards belt. This is a [[Toothbreaker Storage Key]], which opens the locked door to [[Contraband Storage]].
>
> - A successful **Stealth (DC 13)** check allows a character to steal the key off the thug's belt without alerting him.
>
> Characters who succeed on an **Awareness (DC 14)** check to inspect the guard's post notice several empty or broken vials. on the round table or wedged between dock planks below. Some are fully dried of whatever contents they previously held. It seems clear that this guard is a habitual consumer of whatever substance is packaged in these vials.
>
> - Characters should be invited to roll a **Society (DC undefined)** check to identify the substance, but success is impossible unless the party has completed [[Hazardous Headwaters]] and discovered the distilled blaze being manufactured there.
>
> #### The Loaded Boat
>
> The [[Rowboat]] that is tied to the dock here is loaded with a mundane assortment of wares: foodstuffs, common adventuring supplies, and a footlocker full of empty glass vials that seem used.
>
> #### Storage Door
>
> The western door to the adjoining storage room is locked, and can be opened with the [[Toothbreaker Storage Key]].
>
> Additionally, the lock can be picked by succeeding on a **Stealth (DC 13)** check, or the door can be forced open with a successful **`[[/skillCheck athlestics 15]]`** check.
