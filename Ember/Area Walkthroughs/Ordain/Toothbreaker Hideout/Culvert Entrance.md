# Culvert Entrance

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You spy something partially submerged in the flowing water of the canal: a semi-circular culvert, 10 feet in diameter. Water flows inward, and you can see the faint flickering of a light somewhere down the tunnel, but it seems impossible to discern details of this light source through the gloom.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Culvert Access
>
> Entering the culvert requires characters to pass underneath the stone walkway of the canal path. To facilitate this transition visually, a Scene Region is placed outside the culvert entrance. When tokens move into this Scene Region they are prompted with the option to teleport through the culvert into the water on the other side.

Characters who enter the water and swim through the large culvert enter an underground area.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You move with the water fifteen feet into the culvert and come face to face with a heavy metal portcullis which is descended, blocking further progress. Holes in the gate are too small to squeeze through.
>
> You peer into the gloom beyond and perceive a small interior dock where a rowboat is moored. Two flickering torches illuminate the chamber.
>
> Leaning backwards in a partially-reclined chair against the north wall of this interior chamber is a motionless figure, their head tilted slightly back and their breathing slow and steady.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Locked Portcullis
>
> The portcullis is controlled using a lever next to the sleeping guard in the [[Concealed Dock]]. Its mechanism cannot be finessed open and it can only be raised using its intended control lever or via the application of brute force.
>
> The portcullis may be lifted with a successful **Athletics (DC 20)** check, and an ally who has a Strength score of at least **5** provides +2 Boons to this roll.
>
> A character who succeeds on an **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** check notices that the portcullis is well-maintained and shows signs of frequent use. It can most likely be lifted quietly.
