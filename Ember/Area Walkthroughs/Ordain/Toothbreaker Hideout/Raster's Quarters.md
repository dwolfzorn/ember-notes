# Raster's Quarters

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This rectangular room is well cared for and furnished more intentionally than the rest of the hideout. Several prominent trophy skulls line the walls, and the decor — while not matching — produces the cozy feeling of a woodsman's cottage.

The door to this chamber remains locked at all times. [[Raster Thorn]] possesses the only key.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Raster's Bedtime
>
> Raster sleeps here from 1am to 9am each day.
>
> If the party seeks to ambush Raster while he sleeps, it is possible for them to break through the adjoining well from [[Collapsed Quarters]], following the rules described there. Raster employs tactics in combat described in [[Unknown]].
>
> #### Risky Resting
>
> The party can risk resting here, if they do so outside of the sleeping hours described above.

The party will doubtless be interested to inspect or plunder Raster's personal belongings.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Raster's Belongings
>
> Upon cursory inspection, the room contains several minor treasures including:
>
> - **12 14** in loose coin
> - Two finely crafted [[Daggers]]
> - One [[Battleaxe]]
> - One six-foot long Varrol tusk in a display case that weighs 40 pounds and is worth  **120** to an interested merchant.
> - One spare [[Toothbreaker Throne Room Key]].
>
> With a thorough and successful**Awareness (DC 13)** check the party can find the following additional items of interest scattered around the room:
>
> - One [[Potion of Water Breathing]]
> - [[Raster's "Gala Invitation"]]
> - [[Distillation Notice]]
> - [[Healing House Script]]
> - [[Bounty Writ]]
