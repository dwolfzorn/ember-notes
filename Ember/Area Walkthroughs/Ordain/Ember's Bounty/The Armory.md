# The Armory

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The smell of boiled linseed oil and full-grain leather rush to greet you as you step into this elaborately-outfitted armory, which appears to feature all manner of weapons and armor on its ample shelves. A mustachioed House Bastilla sailor polishes a broadsword as you enter, nodding your way as if to say "let me know if you need something" without actually making the effort.

The simply-named Armory is the main deck's primary weapon and armor storage facility, and also caters to crew members and visitors alike with the sale of surplus (and often confiscated) goods.

The gruff sailor who usually attends this place is **Hewson Elexios** (Lawful Good, Ordani Human, he/him), a seasoned veteran who simply can't be bothered — by anything. Sarcasm and buffoonery are met with swift and pragmatic condemnation, often at the low cost of someone's pride.

> [!info] Social
> #### The Armory's Wares
>
> A wide array of weapons and armor are available for sale here, including nearly all of those listed in the corresponding [[Unknown]] tables.
>
> Weapons and armor that are confiscated during infractions of the "Local Rules & Regulations" described in [[Gameplay Details]] are eventually brought here for refurbishment and resale. At least one-third of the inventory here hails from confiscated or abandoned equipment.
