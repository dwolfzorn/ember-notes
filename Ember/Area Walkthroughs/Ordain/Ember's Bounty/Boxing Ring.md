# Boxing Ring

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A melange of blood and sweat paints the canvas of this small boxing ring, where two bareknuckle brawlers are engaged in a one-on-one boxing match. As these two pugilists wage their mock battle, a crowd of spectators cheer and jeer with reckless abandon, clutching pouches of coin in eager anticipation of a win.

The boxing ring here is run by **Burl Havoc** (Chaotic Good, Ordani Kiska, he/him), a professional fighter who also serves as an erstwhile boatswain for smaller House Bastilla ships. A formidable opponent in his own right, Burl is known as a Kiska of few words.

The fights here are all unarmed, and contestants brawl until one of them is knocked out. Murder is frowned upon, and offenders are either immediately thrown overboard (per the "Falling Overboard" rules described in [[Gameplay Details]]) or put to the sword by House Bastilla sailors. Gambling is encouraged, and nearly everyone bets.

> [!info] Social
> #### Part of the Action: Betting
>
> Characters can bet on matches between other boxers, with the Gamemaster running a quick nonlethal combat simulation between two NPCs. Chose any combination of two contestants from the following: [[Arcturian Sailor]], [[Ordani]], or [[Ordani Ruffian]].
>
> The rules are simple: the first character to knock out their opponent (by reducing them to 1 Hit Point and giving them the Unconscious condition) wins.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Part of the Action: Boxing
>
> Characters can participate in the boxing themselves if so desired, but must doff all armor and fight using unarmed, nonlethal combat with their bare hands.
>
> The rules are simple: the first character to knock out their opponent (by reducing them to 1 Hit Point and giving them the Unconscious condition) wins.
>
> Winners claim a small percentage of earnings from the house for each match they win, which results in an average of `[[/roll 1d10]]` x 10 sp per match (unless the party engages in even higher bets on the match themselves).
