# Mechanical Lift

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Reinforced with corroded steel hardware, this creaky wooden lift sways ever-so-slightly as it rumbles into position on the upper deck of the ship. Trademark scents of rust and sea-spray mingle with the smells of aromatic smoke and savory foods, which waft throughout the nooks and crannies of the main deck like eager visitors at a party.

This sizable elevator is constructed from timber and steel, and provides general access to the main deck from the cargo hold located 40 feet below.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Using the Lift
>
> The lift can be manipulated by the use of a hardwood wheel located on its westernmost edge, and is powered by an intricate and mostly unseen network of pulleys and hoists.
>
> Any character who wishes to use the lift to journey from the cargo hold to the main deck (or vice versa) must use a standard action to successfully manipulate the device. Unless otherwise stated by encounter conditions, the lift takes a full round to relocate between these two positions.
>
> Additional lower decks of the ship can be accessed via the lift, but are relatively inconsequential to the events of [[Parcels & Pirates]].
