# Perlin's Powders

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The smell of incense greets you as you step into this cozy retail cabin, which is lined with shelves full of various alchemical reagents and spell components. These curious goods — which include everything from rose petals to powdered silver — are packed into countless jars, urns, boxes, and vials, all decorated with handwritten labels.
>
> A young Arcturian woman scrawls the latest tag on a stubby glass vial, barely peeking up above her work to address you with staccato detachment.
>
> > Welcome to Perlin's Powders. Perlin at your service! How can I help you?

**Perlin Prest** (Neutral Good, Arcturian Human, she/her) is the studious proprietor of Perlin's Powders, a shop that sells arcane spell components and alchemical reagents of a rather wide (and widely-sourced) variety.

> [!info] Social
> #### Perlin's Wares
>
> Characters who wish to purchase spell components from Perlin are able to do so with relatively little fanfare. Perlin has a matter-of-fact attitude about her inventory, and has nearly any spell component a character could need worth 500 gp or less. Additional items include the following crafting components:
>
> - [[Cheliceraeth Eye]]
> - [[Crag Raptor Feather]]
> - [[Spider-cap Mushroom]]
> - [[Quickwater]]
>
> If a character is persistent about an item worth more than 500 gp, Perlin is happy to offer a suggestion about where they might find it (given they actually purchase something in the process).
>
> No matter how hard a character tries, Perlin won't budge on her prices (requiring the intervention of magic or similar methods to sway her mind).
