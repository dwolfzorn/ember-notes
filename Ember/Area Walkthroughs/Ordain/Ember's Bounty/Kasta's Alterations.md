# Kasta's Alterations

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small tailor's stall is attended by a young Ordani woman who busies herself mending a cloak. By the looks of it, she also has a small array of clothing for sale, with everything from urban fineries to rural comfort-wear on the modest racks.

This stall is owned and operated by the mindful tailor **Kasta Markos** (Chaotic Good, Ordani Human, she/her), who combines traditional sewing skills with a light touch of transmutation magic to successfully ply her trade. Treat Kasta as a [[Cultist Fanatic]] with the [[Mending]] cantrip on her list of known spells.

Kasta is the younger sister of Grevian Markos of [[Everything & More]], and is no stranger to the machinations of her brother's illicit schemes (which she occasionally attempts to subvert, particularly when it suits the betterment of the community at large).

> [!info] Social
> #### Kasta's Services
>
> The young seamstress provides alteration and mending services, and charges a rate of 5 sp for Common Clothes and a rate of 2 gp for Fine Clothes and similar garb. If spellcasting is required, the cost for arcane [[Mending]] is 30 gp.
>
> Additionally, Kasta has a small selection of clothing available for purchase, including the following:
>
> - [[Fine Clothes]]
> - [[Traveler's Clothes]]
> - [[Robes]]
> - A lone [[Cloak of Protection]]
