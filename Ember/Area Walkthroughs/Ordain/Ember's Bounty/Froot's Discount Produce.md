# Froot's Discount Produce

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This produce stand is bedecked with all manner of fruits and vegetables, including a selection of regional favorites as well as morsels from far-off places like the Lowland Bastions and the Cascilian Republic. A gregarious-looking Thornling with a bushy beard of flower petals busies himself organizing the neatly-stacked produce as you approach.

The proprietor of this welcoming produce stand is **Froot** (Chaotic Neutral, Arcturian Thornling, he/him), an amiable farmer-turned-sailor who is always pushing the glorious benefits of Vitamin C onto his cohorts.

> [!info] Social
> #### Froot's Questionable Wares
>
> Froot's Discount Produce is stocked with a wide selection of regional fruits (sporting an even wider array of freshness, from nearly-rotten to straight-from-the-vine). And because of the questionable origins and freshness of some of Froot's wares, customers without a discerning eye or an iron-clad stomach tend to find themselves regretting a purchase from time to time.
>
> Any produce purchased from this stand has a 50% chance of being tainted with rot. A character who unwittingly eats such rotted produce must succeed on a `[[/save Constitution 13]]` save or gain the [[Poisoned]] condition until they finish a long rest. A successful **Wilderness (DC 13)** check allows a character to ascertain the questionable quality of the rotted produce.
