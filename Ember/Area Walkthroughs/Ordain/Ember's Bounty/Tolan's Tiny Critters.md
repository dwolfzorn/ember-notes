# Tolan's Tiny Critters

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A ramshackle arrangement of tables, crates, and cages decorate this curious corner of the deck, which appears to be some kind of small creature shop. An assortment of tiny beasts reside within the cages here, including crevvets, gore birds, and trickadees. From the look of things, you can't quite tell if these creatures are intended as companions or cuisine.

Owned and operated by the opportunistic **Tolan Rae** (Chaotic Neutral, Ordani Kivahr, she/her), Tolan's Tiny Critters profits from the sale of small and tiny beasts of Ember — including creatures both local and foreign, from all corners of Aterica and beyond. Whatever the reason for a customer's purchase, be it for food or familiar company, Tolan pays no particular heed.

At this time, however, the vendor's supply of "exotic" creatures has been depleted, leaving nothing more than common Arcturian beasts for purchase.

> [!info] Social
> #### Tolan's Wares
>
> Tiny Critters has [[Crevvet]], [[Gore Bird]], and [[Trickadees]] for sale at the price of 5 gp each.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Liberating the Critters
>
> Any character who attempts to steal or liberate one or more of the eponymous critters can do so with a successful `[[/tool thief 14]]` check per cage, but must also succeed on either a **Stealth (DC 20)** or **`[[/skill sleightofhand 20]]`** check to avoid detection.
>
> - Allied characters can use the [[Help]] action to cause a distraction, providing **+2 Boons** on this check in the process.
>
> The character must also succeed on a **`[[/skill animalhandling 16]]`** check to keep the beast docile enough to escape the scene without alerting Tolan or one of her associates nearby.
>
> - Characters with **Knowledge: Beasts** have **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Failure on any of these checks results in detection by Tolan, and will likely end up with the characters being thrown overboard per the details in [[Gameplay Details]].
