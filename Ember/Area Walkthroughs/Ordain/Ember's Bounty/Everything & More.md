# Everything & More

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This sprawling cabin appears to house a well-supplied general store. All manner of baubles, curios, arms, armor, and adventuring gear can be seen lining the crowded shelves, which appear to host more clutter than the proprietors can keep up with.

The aptly-named Everything & More is the main deck's general store and pawn shop, and provides its customers with handy solutions for all kinds of equipment needs — from the mundane to the illicit, and everything in between.

The owner of this bestrewn establishment is a sagacious merchant named **Grevian** **Markos** (Lawful Evil, Ordani Human, he/him), and is rumored to be involved with a black market network that runs all the way from the deck of the Ember's Bounty to the ports of the Cascilian Republic.

Grevian is the older brother of Kasta Markos of [[Kasta's Alterations]], and feigns a certain amount of disregard for his younger sister (despite the love he actually harbors for her as his only relative).

> [!info] Social
> #### Grevian's Wares
>
> The shelves of Everything & More are laden with the fair- and ill-gotten gains of the ship's assorted crew and visitors, with an equal supply of new and used goods that hail from all corners of Aterica.
>
> Per Gamemaster discretion, nearly any and all items listed in the [[Unknown]] section of the game rules are available to customers in good standing with the ship. Additional items include the following:
>
> - [[Blast Flask]]
> - [[Chasm Candle]]
> - [[Sharp Hunting Trap]]
> - [[Steelmarrow]]
> - [[Inkaro Pearl, White]]
>
> Grevian himself seems to know a few details about every item in the store (and he makes up lies about the rest). Used goods are marked down to 80% of their listed price, but there's a 1 in `[[/roll 1d2]]` chance that a used good will eventually be recognized by a previous owner (who is both angry and vengeful) on the Ember's Bounty itself or somewhere on the streets of Ordain.
