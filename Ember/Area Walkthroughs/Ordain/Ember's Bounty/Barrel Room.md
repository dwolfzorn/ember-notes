# Barrel Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This dim and dusty cabin is stocked full of barrels, kegs, and bottles — presumably full of alcohol and food stuffs (if the lingering smell of rum and dried fruits can be trusted).

This cabin serves as the storehouse for the Game Room nearby, and is closed to the public. It's stocked full, with enough barrels full of ale, wine, and liquor to keep the main deck inebriated for a week.

The heavy oaken door is locked (AC 20, 30 HP, DC 18 to open), and can be unlocked using a [[Barrel Room Key]] — found nearby in the [[The Game Room]] scullery.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Barrel Room Inventory
>
> Any character who takes a moment to search the room can readily find a bottle of [[Seawall Brandy]] or [[Arcturian Liquor]] they can easily abscond with. If a character attempts to take more than one bottle, they might raise the ire of crew members who spot their illicit bounty of beverages.
