# Phestra's Boar & Grill

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The delicious odor of savory meats wafts up from this unsuspecting grill, where a Signborn chef turns a wild boar on a spit above an open flame. Despite the relative grime of this wayside establishment, your mouth waters in hungry anticipation.

Phestra's Boar & Grill is owned and operated by the comically acerbic **Phestra Thulios** (Chaotic Good, Ordani Signborn, she/they), a boisterous chef who barks at passers by with bold (and often dubious) claims about her questionably-seasoned meats.

> [!info] Social
> #### Phestra's Sickening Wares
>
> Phestra's Boar & Grill specializes in savory meats cooked from wild game, but her sources are often suspicious (as if carrion and diseased meat make their way onto the menu with some regularity). The squalid cuts of meat that Phestra sells are only 1 cp in cost, but they don't often agree with the consumer.
>
> Any character who eats some of Phestra's overly-seasoned meat must succeed on a `[[/save Constitution 13]]` save or gain the [[Poisoned]] condition until they finish a long rest. A successful **Wilderness (DC 15)** check allows a character to ascertain the questionable quality of the meat.
