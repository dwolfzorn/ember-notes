# The Smoke Hut

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The peculiar, spicy odor of strange smoke wafts through the air around this small tent, which is crowded by an assortment of red-eyed ne'er-do-wells that appear to be quite stoned. Beneath the tent's awning, a large octagonal table is decorated with a variety of water pipes and jars of dustweed. This smoke hut strikes you as a great place to gather some questionable information or lose track of time.
>
> A spry, twenty-something sailor covered in Maziran tattoos waves towards your group, beckoning you to sit at the table.
>
> > Care to join us, friends? Best price on dustweed in Ordain. And the tastiest!
>
> The young man takes a long drag off of a long-handled water pipe before flashing you a silver-toothed grin.

The Smoke Hut is a tiny venue for recreational smoking, owned and operated by the gregarious yet opportunistic **Kyren Volt** (Chaotic Neutral, Maziran Human, he/him), a mirthful young man who is always eager to meet a new customer or acquaintance (for whatever questionable reason). Treat Kyren as an [[Arcturian Sailor]] who can speak Common and Kaziric.

> [!info] Social
> #### Kyren's Wares
>
> Kyren Volt's primary goods and services include the sale of [[Dustweed]] (of which he has ample), [[Maziran Water Pipe]], [[Arcturian Tobacco Pipe]], and various accessories like [[Tinderboxes]] and scented [[Candles]].
>
> Characters who succeed on a **Diplomacy (DC 13)** check are able to convince Kyren to offer them some of his more illicit wares, which include up to 6 doses of rare Maziran [[Rapture]].
>
> - Characters with **Knowledge: Crime** have **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> #### Rumor Has It
>
> The Smoke Hut is always rife with rumors. Characters who dare to strike up a conversation with the establishment's regular customers (including Kyren) and succeed on a **Awareness (DC 13)** check can roll once each on the [[Ember's Bounty Rumors]] table featured below.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Diplomacy (DC 15)** check is able to gather additional information, resulting in one more roll from the rumor table.
>
> - **Path: AnchoriteMarine** and **Path: Nightwatch** characters have **+2 Boons** on this check.
