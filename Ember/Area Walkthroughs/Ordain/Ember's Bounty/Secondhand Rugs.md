# Secondhand Rugs

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You regard a large pile of rugs and carpets, many of which appear rather dusty and worn, with frayed edges and furtive stains. However, few of the textiles here do look remarkably clean, decorated with rare patterns from far-off places.
>
> If anyone actually attends this motley assortment of carpets, it must be one of the spectators surrounding the boxing ring nearby. You have a feeling you'll have to grab their attention if you want to make a purchase.

The quality of the carpets and rugs sold here range from truly exquisite to remarkably terrible. The stall's proprietor, a wizened smuggler named **Anton Straid** (Chaotic Neutral, Cascilian Human, he/him), can usually be found paying more attention to the [[Boxing Ring]] nearby than he does to the sale of his tattered carpets.

This absence is by design, however, since Anton also uses the location as a drop-off and pick-up location for a variety of secret deliveries of illicit goods (such as illegal shipments to and from Grevian Markos of [[Everything & More]], or the smuggling of contraband components to Perlin of [[Perlin's Powders]]).

> [!info] Social
> #### Anton's Wares
>
> If the characters manage to drag Anton away from his gambling at the Boxing Ring, he is willing to sell them one of his rugs or carpets. The textiles range in price from 1 gp for the most tattered among them, to 25 gp for the most artful, imported, or exquisite.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Deception (DC 20)** check can tell that Anton is hiding something among his wares, as indicated by his shifty eyes and shady demeanor.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Hidden Cache
>
> Any character who takes a moment to search the carpet piles here and succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 15)** check is able to locate a hidden lockbox that's been stashed beneath a few of the dirtiest rugs.
>
> Characters who wish to take the lockbox without being detected by Anton or someone else nearby must succeed on either a **Stealth (DC 18)** or **Stealth (DC 18)** check. Failure results in that character potentially being thrown overboard, per the "Local Rules & Regulations" described in [[Gameplay Details]].
>
> The reinforced lockbox (AC 20, 30 HP, DC 20 to open) can be safely opened by speaking the phrase "*Anton sent me*," which is furtively scrawled on the bottom of the box in Cascilian (requiring a successful **Awareness (DC 15)** check to locate), and contains the following:
>
> - **8**
> - 2 doses of [[Rapture]]
> - A treasure map of [[Lake Mithra]] with an "X" marking a spot near the eastern expanse of the lake.
>
> Opening the lockbox using physical force has a 50% chance to shatter the vials of rapture inside. If fire is involved, their is a 100% chance the treasure map is burned and becomes illegible.
