# Varlo's

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Dim lantern light cascades upon the cluttered tables of this small dining hall, where an array of hearty food has been prepared for its patrons. Your mouth waters at the smell of grilled fish and fresh bread as you linger in the doorway, and a bright-eyed server brushes past you with a steamy eel pie on his arm — piping hot and straight from the oven.

The aforementioned bright-eyed server is none other than **Varlo** himself (Lawful Neutral, Ordani Human, he/him). Varlo the Eleventh to be precise — the youngest (and currently last) of the Varlo line.

The food offered in here Varlo's dining hall is of fairly low quality but is reliably abundant. Young Varlo is still mastering his trade, and he has a lot to learn. Quite comically, all complaints can be addressed to his father, Varlo the Tenth (who is notoriously deceased).

A burgeoning entrepreneur, Varlo is known to offer his fellow vendors aboard the Ember's Bounty vouchers for free meals, in the hopes that new customers are around every corner. Of course, the meals provided by these coupons are the cheapest on the menu.

> [!info] Social
> #### Varlo's Wares
>
> The meals here consist of standard Arcturian and Ordani fare, and range from Poor (at 2 cp) to Wealthy (at 3 sp). Additionally, Varlo has a small supply of non-perishable [[Rations]] for sale.
