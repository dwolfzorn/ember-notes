# Anrak Mausoleum

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This L-shaped sepulcher houses three ledger stones marked with the characters of a foreign language. Moss-laden and musky, this monument appears somewhat harried by the ebb and flow of weather conditions here on the Burial Grounds hillside.

This small mausoleum is dedicated to the famed [[Kessian]] pilgrim known as Tula Anrak, who helped establish the Temple of [[Lantyr]] in Ordain's [[Temple Ward]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploration the Mausoleum
>
> The text etched on the ledger stones and in other places here is written in **Language: Solical**, the native tongue of Tula Anrak and her celebrated adventuring party — the Dawnstriders.
>
> - Any character who can read these etchings will learn that this is Tula Anrak's crypt, but know relatively little about the pilgrim herself.
> - The etchings also tell the story of a grand mythical narrative, when [[Lantyr]] gifted his chosen priests various sacred relics to fight a formidable darkness during the time of [[The Shattering]]. Although these relics were lost to the ages, legends say the elder god's priests were permanently blessed with a measure of Lantyr's divine energy.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Society (DC 15)** check is familiar with the legends of Tula Anrak and the Dawnstriders.
>
> - Legends say Tula Anrak was responsible for introducing several essential scriptures of Lantyr's tenets to the Arctus Plateau, in addition to her founding of the temple in Ordain.
>
> - **Culture: Kessian**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Gods**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: Some legends say Tula's body itself was blessed by Lantyr, and that her bones radiate the Sun's divine energy from the depths of her stony tomb.
>
> #### Blessing of the Energy Moon
>
> Characters with a `[[/skill religion 13 passive format=long]]` or who succeed on a **Arcana (DC 15)** check understand the Anrak Mausoleum's ability to channel divine magic via the power of Lantyr, the ever-burning Sun.
>
> - **Knowledge: Artifacts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Gods**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character who spends 5 minutes praying to Lantyr at the Anrak Mausoleum and succeeds on a **`[[/skill wisdom 15]]`** check gains the ability to cast the [[Daylight]] spell once during the next 24 hours, regardless of their class.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
