# Antechamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This wedge-shaped antechamber acts as a modest and unassuming foyer for the facility that lurks beyond. The sconces on the walls here burn with perpetual arcane firelight, which casts its flickering radiance on a large metallic door to the north.

This simple Antechamber is sparsely decorated, and serves as a straightforward junction for ambulation during ceremonies and rituals — when people other than the Cindaric Sages themselves are visiting the facility.

### Funeral Hall Gate

This adamantine gate serves as the forward entrance to the [[Funeral Hall]] section of the Cindaric Crematorium facility, and is sealed with stout mechanical locks.

A [[Burial Grounds Skeleton Key]] is needed to open the Crematorium Gate without the benefit of Thieves' Tools (see below).

During ceremonial events, when the friends and family of the deceased gather to pay their respects, this gate remains shut until the sacred rites are concluded — at which time it is opened by one of the Cindaric Sages for accessible passage.

Meanwhile, smaller steel portcullises are located to the east and west. These grated thresholds are unlocked, controlled with levers to each side, and are used by the Cindaric Sages to guide foot-traffic control during ceremonial gatherings.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Surveying the Funeral Hall Gate
>
> The Funeral Hall Gate is crafted from a unique, magically-reinforced adamantine alloy. It has 200 hp, an AC of 25, and a Damage Threshold of 30.
>
> Any character with a `[[/check prc 13 passive format=long]]` or who succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 11)** check while searching the door is able to spot the concealed locking mechanism.
>
> - **Path: CindaricInitiate**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Machines**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Locked Door
>
> The Funeral Hall Gate lock can only be opened with a [[Burial Grounds Skeleton Key]] or a successful `[[/skill tool=thief 25]]` check.
