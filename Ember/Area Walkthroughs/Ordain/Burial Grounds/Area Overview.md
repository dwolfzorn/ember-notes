# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The area map for the Ordain Burial Grounds depicts the interior chambers of a Cindaric Crematorium as well as an exterior expanse of the overgrown cemetery itself, which will serve as the scenery for the [[Smoldering Cinders]] quest event known as [[Grave Assignments]].
>
> - This map represents one small section of the Burial Grounds, and focuses on the Cindaric Crematorium that comprises most of the map's interior.
> - There is a single method of entry to the Crematorium: the [[Crematorium Gate]] situated outside the facility, near the center of the map.
> - Additional information about the various mausoleums of this section of the Burial Grounds and the locks that contain them are located in the Gameplay Details section below.

## Gameplay Details

The areas of the Burial Grounds have the following features unless specified otherwise in the text.

### Levels & Elevation

The Burial Grounds Scene features a single Level, and its areas are organized into Exterior Locations, and the Cindaric Crematorium.

The ceilings within the interior section of the Cindaric Crematorium are 20 feet high, unless otherwise stated. The bespoke ceilings of the smaller mausoleums outside are detailed in each of their respective sections of the area walkthrough.

The uneven landscape of the Burial Grounds exterior features slight elevation changes, but these slopes and inclines are too minuscule to make a difference.

### Sound

The Ordain Burial Grounds are a quiet and solemn place most hours of the day.

### Illumination

Braziers located throughout the Burial Grounds exterior burn with magical firelight and inkaro pearls, and are regularly tended to by the various lamplighters and Cindarics assigned to work here. These braziers shed bright light in a 20 foot radius and dim light for another 20 feet.

Similar sconces and braziers light the interior Cindaric Crematorium section of the map. Additionally, the magma of [[Funeral Hall]]'s pit sheds light throughout most of the innermost chamber, with a radius of bright light for 30 feet and dim light for another 30 feet.

### Terrain

The exterior sections of the map are littered with gravestones, statues, and foliage. As a result, the general landscape of the Burial Grounds counts as difficult terrain, other than areas such as mausoleum interiors, the Cindaric Crematorium facility, and the cobblestone path that winds its way through the graveyard.

The flagstone floors of the Cindaric Crematorium are crafted from slabs of dark basalt, hewn from the quarries and subterranea nearby. The ground here should be treated as normal terrain unless otherwise noted. The interior walls of the Crematorium are crafted from similar stone.

The [[Crematorium Gate]] and the similar door that leads from the [[Antechamber]] to the Funeral Hall are made of an adamantine alloy, and are locked. The two metal doors on the east and west edges of the Funeral Hall are unlocked and made of solid reinforced steel, as are the two smaller portcullis in the eastern and western Antechamber hallways — which are opened and closed by operating levers located nearby.

The walls and floors of the map's various exterior mausoleums are made of white marble, unless otherwise noted. The mausoleum doors are made of reinforced steel.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Graveyard Tracks
>
> Any character who searches the Burial Grounds exterior for tracks and succeeds on a **Wilderness (DC 13)** check is able to locate the presence of sandaled and booted footprints along the various cobblestone walkways here. These tracks are easily identified as those of the Cindaric Sages and lamplighters who work here.

### Enemies

The following enemies are encountered here:

- [[Corpuleth]]
- [[Skallith]]

These enemies do not populate this area map outside of the [[Grave Assignments]] event featured in [[Smoldering Cinders]].

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Repopulating Minions
>
> If the party leaves the Burial Grounds for any reason before the battle with the Corpuleth and the Skallith is finished (before the [[Grave Assignments]] event has concluded), they'll return to find the location inundated with replacements.
>
> All Skallith will respawn on the map prior to the party's return, along with `[[/roll 1d2+1]]` [[Skallith Warrior]], who take their place throughout the various mausoleums leading up to the Cindaric Crematorium.
