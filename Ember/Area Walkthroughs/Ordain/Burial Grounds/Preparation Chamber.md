# Preparation Chamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The sweet smells of lavender, jasmine, and incense mask the subtle scent of rot in this octagonal chamber lined with mortuary slabs and medical accoutrements. If the wash basin on the southern wall and the reverent arrangement of the corpses here is any indication, this must be some kind of staging room, where the newly deceased are disrobed and cleaned before the embalming process begins.

Cadavers are brought to this Preparation Chamber for the first step of processing here at the Crematorium. Bodies are stripped of their clothing and equipment, which is temporarily stored on shelves against the eastern wall. A wash basin and a nearby utility table adjoin the southern wall, which contain everything the Cindarics need for this step of the funerary rites.

If the characters take a closer look at one of the corpses, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You lean in to examine the corpse, which appears to be an Ordani citizen who met an untimely fate of one kind or another. Based on the relative lack of decomposition, it seems as though some spell or alchemical reagent is keeping the bodies in stasis.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Prep Chamber
>
> If the characters decide to search the room, they'll find the following:
>
> - The corpses of 3 ordinary Ordani citizens, who appear to be unconnected to the party's investigation. The stone slabs they're laid upon are cluttered with incense burners, half-burned sticks of incense, water jugs, cloth sheets, and other miscellaneous debris.
> - The shelves on the eastern wall contain an array of personal effects (recently collected from one or more of the corpses here in the room): one outfit of [[Fine Clothes]], one outfit of [[Traveler's Clothes]], a [[Scholar's Pack]], a [[Shortsword]], and a mundane [[Amulet]] of [[Sockets]].
> - A clean water basin on the southern wall, accompanied by various towels, pitchers, soap, and cleaning salts.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 13)** check is able to notice or locate the following:
>
> - The incense here hasn't been lit for a few hours, but ashes remain settled on the mortuary slabs from an earlier session.
> - The water basin doesn't appear to be attached to a water source of any kind, and the water is remarkably clean.
>
> Any character who examines the corpses for details must succeed on a **Medicine (DC 13)** or **Awareness (DC 13)** check to determine the following:
>
> - 2 of the corpses here appear to have been the result of natural causes (old age and terminal illness), while the third looks like a fatal concussion (likely accidental).
>
> - **Knowledge: Forensics**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> A successful **Arcana (DC 13)** check confirms a few details:
>
> - The corpses are currently benefitting from the effects of a [[Gentle Repose]] spell, one of Ember's "sacred" Necromancy spells.
> - The water in the basin is sourced from permanent versions of spells like [[Create or Destroy Water]] and [[Purify Food and Drink]].
>
> Additionally, the [[Detect Magic]] spell reveals an aura of Transmutation magic on the water basin and an aura of sacred Necromancy on the corpses.
