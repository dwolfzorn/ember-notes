# Tomb of Spectra's Chosen

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This octagonal mausoleum is surrounded by statues of robed humanoid figures tattooed with esoteric sigils. Three adamantine doors seal this tomb away from the outside world, and its spired rooftop stands out as the tallest edifice in this section of the cemetery. However, no placard or sign reveals this curious mausoleum's name or ownership.

This mausoleum is known as the Tomb of Spectra's Chosen, and serves as the final resting place of nine of the elder goddess [[Spectra]]'s most loyal and storied acolytes.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Locked Doors
>
> The three iron doors to the mausoleum are locked, and can be opened with a [[Burial Grounds Skeleton Key]] or a successful `[[/skill tool=thief 18]]` check.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The interior confines of this mysterious crypt are adorned with nine ledger stones encircling a tenth, larger grave at the center of the chamber. The half-melted candles surrounding this central ledger stone appear to burn with perpetual light, as if the wax itself is arcane and unmoving. The soft sound of ethereal chimes lingers here, emitted from some unseen source within the tomb's arched ceiling or shaded eaves.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Mausoleum
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Society (DC 13)** check is able to identify this mausoleum for the monument it is, and is also aware of the following historical context surrounding the edifice.
>
> - The corpses interred here are the remains of ten notable acolytes of the shard goddess Spectra. Half are persons of historical significance in Ordain, while the rest hail from various corners of Ember.
>
> - Spectra is known to bless her chosen acolytes with special boons of arcane prowess that might otherwise be outside their abilities. Some of the dead here weren't simply mages; they include clerics, warlocks, and arcane warriors as well.
>
> - **Knowledge: Gods**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The large ledger stone at the center of the mausoleum belongs to a famed Cor'ak scholar-mage known as **The Rebus**, whose legendary enchantments and illusions within the Anachraenum's [[All-Fable Keep]] endure to this very day.
>
> Characters with a `[[/skill perception passive=long 13]]` or who succeed on a **Awareness (DC 15)** check are able to spot curious arcane symbols hidden with the patterns on each of the nine ledger stones.
>
> - Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 12)** check is able to identify these symbols as the icons of seven arcane traditions — with the taboo school of necromancy the only missing symbol.
>
> - **Critical Success**: Some of the symbols and written characters etched upon the central ledger stone can be identified as arcane writing equal to three spell scrolls. The spells include: [[Silent Image]], [[Arcanist's Magic Aura]], and [[Suggestion]].
>
> Any arcane spellcaster who succeeds on a **`[[/skill sleightofhand 15]]`** is able to transfer the symbols to a parchment via a charcoal grave rubbing or a comparable technique; these rubbings can eventually be transcribed into an arcane spellcaster's grimoire, but they cannot be used as spell scrolls.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Trapped Ledger Stones
>
> Any character who attempts to unearth, damage, or deface the graves here is subject to the effects of a special [[Befuddlement]] spell trap, and must succeed on a `[[/save intelligence 20]]` saving throw to avoid becoming [[Befuddled]], taking `[[/roll 2d12]]` psychic damage on a failure instead of the standard 10d12. All other effects of the Befuddlement spell behave as normal.
