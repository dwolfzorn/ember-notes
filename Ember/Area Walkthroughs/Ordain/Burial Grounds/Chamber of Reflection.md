# Chamber of Reflection

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small round chamber has room for little more than the etched marble slab and two stone benches that occupy it. Perhaps its' just the relatively meager size, but somehow this room feels more solemn and lonely than the rest.

Once the friends and family of the recently deceased have had ample time for observation and remembrance in the [[Chamber of Communion]], the body is brought here to the Chamber of Reflection for temporary staging prior to the actual cremation. Meanwhile, the funeral attendees pass through on their way northward to the [[Funeral Hall]].

A large steel door is located on the western wall at the northern end of the chamber. This threshold is unlocked, and can be controlled with a lever on each side.

> [!tip] Exploration
> Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 13)** or **Society (DC 16)** check is aware of the room's precise purpose and the procedures that accompany it.
>
> - This smaller circular chamber serves as a final staging area for the fully-prepared embalmed corpse, which is brought here from the [[Chamber of Communion]] immediately prior to the cremation ceremony.
> - The benches here are for the comfort of the deceased's partner or next-of-kin, who may attend the body through the rest of the procedure if they choose.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 15)** check while examining the etched slab is able to identify the table's carvings as the litany of a Cindaric prayer written in **Language: Arcden**, meant to protect a physical body from the corruption of undeath.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Undeath**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
