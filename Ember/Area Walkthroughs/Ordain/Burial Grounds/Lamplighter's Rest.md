# Lamplighter's Rest

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The walls of this wide open-air mausoleum are remarkably well-kept compared to a few other structures nearby, as evident by the lack of mildew and mossy overgrowth. A soft breeze stirs through the arches of this proud edifice, labeled honorably by a bronze plaque as "Lamplighter's Rest."

This mausoleum serves as the final resting place for the worldly remains of a handful of venerated lamplighters, noble souls who once served Ordain as caretakers of the Burial Grounds.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploration the Mausoleum
>
> Four ledger stones are set into the marble floor of the mausoleum. They surround a large sarcophagus at the building's center.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Society (DC 15)** check is able to identify this mausoleum for the monument it is, and is aware of the following historical context surrounding the edifice:
>
> - Some of Ordain's first Lamplighters are buried here, including a trio of Cindarics who served as the original Lamplighters of the Burial Grounds.
>
> - **Path: Cindaric Initiate**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character who takes a moment to search the mausoleum will discover the following:
>
> - The ledger stone situated in the northwest corner of the mausoleum appears to be the newest among the group, and is decorated with some recent offerings and mementos from an admirer or loved one. These items include two blue roses, a [[Inkaro Pearl, White]], and a set of edge-worn [[Dice]].
>
> #### Examining the Sarcophagus
>
> The sarcophagus at the center of the mausoleum is unlocked, and contains little more than the dusty bones of an unknown Lamplighter and tattered burial rags.
