# Ruined Mausoleum

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The foundation of this forgotten mausoleum has sunk deep into the earth, and its timeworn walls are streaked with cracks. It seems a massive shift in the terrain here has rendered the crypt's dark interior entirely unreachable.

The walls of this crumbling mausoleum are impenetrable by most means. Even if someone could reach the interior, they'd find it effectively empty or full of rubble and rampant overgrowth.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Ruined Mausoluem
>
> Any character that succeeds on a **Society (DC 13)** or **Wilderness (DC 15)** check can deduce that the notorious earthquakes of the region have been unkind to this structure during some previous seismic upheaval, and are responsible for its ruination.
