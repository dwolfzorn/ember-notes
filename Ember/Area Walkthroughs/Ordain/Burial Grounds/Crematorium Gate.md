# Crematorium Gate

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Dark stone steps ascend to a shallow alcove cut into the craggy hillside, where a large metallic gate marks the entrance of an underground facility. Made of a reinforced adamantine alloy, the massive door is decorated with the diamond-shaped sigil of the Cindaric Sages, and looks nigh impenetrable — with no a handle or keyhole in sight.

This adamantine gate serves as the only entrance to the Cindaric Crematorium facility, and is sealed with both mechanical and magical locks.

A [[Cindaric Crematorium Key]] is needed to open the Crematorium Gate without triggering the stun trap associated with it (see below).

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Surveying the Crematorium Gate
>
> The Crematorium Gate is crafted from a unique, magically-reinforced adamantine alloy. It has 200 hp, an AC of 25, and a Damage Threshold of 30.
>
> Any character with a `[[/check prc 15 passive format=long]]` or who succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the door is able to spot the locking mechanism, which is hidden at the center of the Cindaric sigil.
>
> - **Path: Cindaric Initiate**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Machines**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character is able to notice the telltale signs of a magical trap on and around the lock.
>
> - The door can be opened with successful `[[/skill tool=thief 30]]` check or with a [[Cindaric Crematorium Key]].
> - The [[Knock]] spell has no effect on this lock.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Society (DC 13)** check is able to verify this gate as the entrance to the Cindaric Crematorium, where sacred funerary rites are conducted by the Cindarics for the people of Ordain.
>
> - **Path: Cindaric Initiate** : The character automatically succeeds on this check.
>
> A successful **Wilderness (DC 13)** check reveals the presence of sandaled and booted tracks passing in and out of the gate via the alcove here, which are easily identified as the footwear of the Cindaric Sages who work the Burial Grounds.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The latest tracks are roughly 6 to 12 hours old.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Magical Stun Trap
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 15)** check is able to detect the telltale signs of a magical trap on the door. Alternately, the trap can be found with a successful **Awareness (DC 16)** check.
>
> Additionally, the [[Detect Magic]] spell will reveal the presence of Enchantment magic on the Cindaric sigil that decorates the adamantine gate.
>
> Any character who attempts to open the Crematorium Gate without using the [[Cindaric Crematorium Key]] becomes the target of [[Power Word Stun]] (Spell Save DC 30) with a duration of `[[/roll 1d4+1]]` minutes.
>
> The Spell Save DC for [[Dispel Magic]] when targeting the Crematorium Gate is 30.

When the characters open the door for the first time, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The intricate lines of the Cindaric sigil flash with a blood-orange arcane brilliance just before the heavy adamantine door starts rising up into a recessed area of the rock. A hallway greets you on the other side, decorated with a narrow throw rug that extends directly from your feet to another lustrous door on the opposite end of the antechamber.
