# Chamber of Remembrance

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This sizable circular chamber is lined with marble benches, and features a plinth at its center atop a rectangular dais. Surrounded by half-burned candles and tiny relics, this appears to be an offering space, where mementos have been left behind to honor the dead.

Known as the Chamber of Remembrance, this room serves as a gathering place for friends, family, and associates of the recently deceased, where they're encouraged to leave something behind for the dearly departed. Anyone and everyone who wishes to honor the deceased can sit here in attendance of the sacred ceremony.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Chamber
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 13)** or **Society (DC 16)** check is aware of the room's precise purpose and the procedures that accompany it.
>
> - People gather here to leave a memento or personal effect on the plinth in honor of the recently deceased. These mementos are typically collected after the cremation and relocated to a reliquary elsewhere in the Burial Grounds.
> - An effigy of the recently usually decorates the plinth during the ceremony — from carved busts of wood or stone, to illustrated and illusory likenesses.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> #### Looting the Mementos
>
> Although it would be considered ethically unsavory, any character who attempts to loot the plinth is able to find  **1d6** loose silver pieces and at least one item of interest.
>
> - Roll `[[/roll 1d2]]` times on the [[Arcturian Trinkets]] Table.
> - Characters caught stealing personal effects and mementos from the Cindaric Crematorium are subject to prosecution by the Cindaric Sages themselves and agents of the [[Hallows]].
