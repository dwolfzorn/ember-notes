# Prestyn Mausoleum

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The interior walls of this circular crypt are decorated with an elaborate relief that depicts a fantastic scene: an armored warrior equipped with a mighty glaive lands the killing blow against a terrible draconic creature wreathed in phantasmagorical death. A solitary stone sarcophagus sits at the center of this open-air mausoleum, which exudes a sense of stoic loneliness here by itself.

This mausoleum honors the legendary Kelmezian dragonslayer Rhydel Prestyn, and is accurately rumored to hold a solitary finger of the Shattering-era champion in a reliquary sealed within its impenetrable lone sarcophagus.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Mausoleum
>
> The sarcophagus here is made of an ancient stone reinforced with powerful magic. It cannot be opened by any means known to humanoid kind.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Society (DC 13)** check is somewhat familiar with the legend of Dragonslayer Prestyn, and of the various reliquaries that host his holy remains.
>
> This mausoleum is said to hold Prestyn's left ring finger, which was severed by the Dragonslayer in an attempt to remove a cursed ring. According to the tales, no one alive has ever seen the relic.
>
> - **Critical Success**: Certainly everyone knows that the various crypts and mausoleums that pay tribute to Rhydel Prestyn are ensorcelled with supremely potent magic to protect their sacred contents. Only a fool would attempt to rob or defile them.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 14 passive format=long]]` or who succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 12)** check while searching the mausoleum finds a warning etched in **Language: Arcden** along the steps of the threshold:
>
> > Let whosoever attempts to open this reliquary hold fast to damnation, lest they be descendants of Prestyn himself. Prove yourself elsewhere, pup.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 15)** check is able to notice the powerful traps and wards placed on the mausoleum (see "Phantasmal Killer Trap" below).
>
> Additionally, the [[Detect Magic]] spell reveals the presence of powerful Illusion and Abjuration magic.

The first time a character attempts to open the sarcophagus at the center of the mausoleum, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The marble sarcophagus feels surprisingly hot to the touch, and the smell of ozone fills the air for a brief moment as a bolt of static shock licks at your fingertips. A feeling in your gut compels you to pull away from the stony, rune-marked coffin before something worse happens.

Subsequent attempts to open the sarcophagus are met with folly.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Phantasmal Killer Trap
>
> Those foolish enough to attempt to vandalize or loot the contents of the mausoleum's sarcophagus become the target of a powerful illusion spell, which causes the various figures depicted in the relief on the wall to seemingly come to life and attack the would-be grave robbers.
>
> Any character who ignores the warning and attempts to open or damage the sarcophagus (or the monument at large) must succeed on a **`[[/skill wisdom 20]]`** saving throw or become the target of a [[Phantasmal Killer]] spell (Spell Save DC 20).
>
> - This effect lasts for `[[/roll 1d4+1]]` rounds. For this duration, the character can attempt the **`[[/skill wisdom 20]]`** save at the end of each of its turns.
> - Additionally, an [[Antimagic Field]] appears at the center of the chamber, with a radius of 20 feet and a duration of 1 hour. Other magical effects of the mausoleum itself are immune to this particular antimagic field.
