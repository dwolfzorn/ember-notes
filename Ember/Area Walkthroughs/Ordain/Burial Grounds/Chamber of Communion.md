# Chamber of Communion

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The stone slab at the center of this room is marked with intricate carvings and lavish embellishments, denoting a place of honor or status. The marble benches arranged around this stone table are positioned close to it, suggesting the act of observance and an overt air of intimacy.

The Chamber of Communion is a room where the close friends and family members of a dead person can gather around their fully-prepared corpse for an intimate moment of last goodbyes.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Chamber
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 13)** or **Society (DC 16)** check is aware of the room's precise purpose and the procedures that accompany it.
>
> - Intimate friends and family gather here to say goodbye to the physical remains of the recently deceased.
> - This stage of the funeral can last one to three hours prior to the cremation ceremony (if not longer), but is typically limited to a single hour of observance.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Arcana (DC 15)** check while examining the slab is able to identify the table's carvings as the litany of a Cindaric prayer written in **Language: Arcden**, meant to protect a soul on its metaphysical journey to Mortolis.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Souls**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
