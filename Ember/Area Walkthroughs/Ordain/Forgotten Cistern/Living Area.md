# Living Area

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You step into a long chamber that's been transformed into a makeshift living space. Four cloth tents fill much of the room, with a stockpile of barrels and sacks behind one in a corner. A campfire burns against one wall, casting uneven light and filling the room with a faint haze of acrid smoke and lazily drifts out of a hall toward the east. Pipes hang overhead, fixed to the ceiling and cutting through ancient stone.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Arcane Adversaries
>
> The [[Mutagist Excisor]] here attempt to attack from range, avoiding close combat where possible. However, their [[Arcane Burst]] proves perfectly functional in close range as well.
>
> In terms of spellcasting, they all have mage armor cast (which is automatically applied to their AC) and use [[Ray of Enfeeblement]] to weaken the strongest enemies on the field.
>
> If injured they utilize their [[Vampiric Touch]] spells to recover some lost hit points while harming their foes.
>
> #### Fire Support
>
> The [[Mutagist Bombardier]] in this area attempt to hang back, choosing to [[Throw Alchemical Grenades]] at grouped targets. If engaged in melee combat, they are competent with their [[Axe]], and will defend themselves violently.
>
> #### Melee Specialists
>
> While their allies hang back, the [[Mutagist Vivisector]] choose to get up and personal when fights break out, using their [[Hollow Stiletto]] with their [[Poison Vial]] to harm their enemies.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Unhappy Campers
>
> Examining inside the tents, you find nothing of value, just bedrolls on scattered hay, small bundles of clothing, the odd journal filled with scratchy observations and meandering complaints about being stuck in the lab, and the prevalent smell.
>
> A successful **Awareness (DC 16)** check while examining the clutter concludes that about ten people use this space, sharing tents and sleeping in shifts as they can, and that the people in the lab are rotated out in shifts, indicating they likely have a safehouse elsewhere in the city.
>
> #### Assorted Items
>
> Searching the tents and piles of bags behind them yields few sets of [[Mutagist Clothing]] which can be used by a character to disguise themselves as a [[Mutagist Vivisector]].
>
> Characters can also find [[Personal Effects]] likely stolen from the victims of the lab. These can be taken as evidence or for later sale to a willing and suitably unscrupulous merchant.
>
> #### Narrow Passage
>
> A narrow passage stretches into the darkness here. Small and tiny creatures can move through this space comfortably, but medium sized creatures will need to squeeze through.
>
> While squeezing through a space, a creature must spend 1 extra foot for every foot it moves, and it has **-2 Banes** on any physical rolls and saving throws it makes. Attack rolls against the creature have **+2 Boons** while it’s in the smaller space.
>
> The passage connects the [[Storage Room]] to the [[Living Area]].
