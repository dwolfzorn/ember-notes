# Chemical Outlet

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The black basalt of the old structure ends at a curved stone dock overlooking a cavern river. Here, several thick pipes jut from the wall, gushing forth torrents of luminous green chemical into the water nearby. The chemicals paint the low ceiling and walls with shifting patterns of ghastly green light and fill the air with a choking stench that makes breathing painful.
>
> Rickety wooden walkways, built with little care, hug the cave walls and disappear into the darkness beyond.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Dangerous Dumping
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Alchemy** or making a successful **Medicine (DC 12)** check know that the volume of chemicals being dumped into this water stream would likely end up in city's water supply. This almost certainly would make people incredibly sick if consumed.
>
> #### Shutting it Down
>
> A quick **Awareness (DC 12)** check confirms that there are no valves or other means of shutting down the chemical flow here. Searching elsewhere in the labs may provide a way to stop the flow. Nothing can be done here.
>
> #### Scattered Effects
>
> Discarded in the corner is a pile of clothing and [[Personal Effects]], most of them blood stained and showing signs of damage. These can be collected as evidence or treasure for later sale.
