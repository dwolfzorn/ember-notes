# Drake Den

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Cast in deep, dark shadows from the fading light of wall-mounted gems, a hollowed-out depression is heaped with stones and half-decayed remnants of some long-eaten meal.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Nesting Drakes
>
> 3 x Drakelings can be found sleeping here. A stealthy party will not alert them immediately, but attempting to pry the emblem free of the nearby relief or making too much noise wakes them without fail.

### Ancient Relief

The party may want to examine the relief in greater detail, attracted by the glowing object fixt to it.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Against the wall is a stone relief of an Ashka figure clutching something to their chest. In their hands, fixed into the stone, is a glowing disk that seems to contain an active flame.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Glowing Relief
>
> A successful **Awareness (DC 14)** or **Awareness (DC 12)** concludes that the gem disc is not part of the relief, and should be easy enough to pry free with any sort of flat tool, be it prybar or dagger.
>
> If done, the party gains a [[Sedyri Fire Emblem]] which will be useful elsewhere.
