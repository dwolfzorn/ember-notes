# Extraction Lab

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This room is packed with heavy metal slabs, each perched upon a tilting pedestal and deeply stained by old, darkened blood. On one table, the body of a man lies beneath a coarse burlap sheet. His outline is thin and unmoving.
>
> On the wall behind the body are a row of three large vats filled with shimmering liquids. Each stands connected to the snarl of pipes and hoses which lead to gauges and meters that provide readings without explanation.
>
> Tables of alchemical tubes, sit here and there, each brimming with vials and canisters of fluids, some brown as old blood, others glimmering with suspect green light.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Arcane Adversaries
>
> The [[Mutagist Excisor]] here attempt to attack from range, avoiding close combat where possible. However, their [[Arcane Burst]] proves perfectly functional in close range as well.
>
> In terms of spellcasting, they all have mage armor cast (which is automatically applied to their AC) and use [[Ray of Enfeeblement]] to weaken the strongest enemies on the field.
>
> If injured they utilize their [[Vampiric Touch]] spells to recover some lost hit points while harming their foes.
>
> #### Fire Support
>
> The [[Mutagist Bombardier]] in this area attempt to hang back, choosing to [[Throw Alchemical Grenades]] at grouped targets. If engaged in melee combat, they are competent with their [[Axe]], and will defend themselves violently.
>
> #### Melee Specialists
>
> While their allies hang back, the [[Mutagist Vivisector]] choose to get up and personal when fights break out, using their [[Hollow Stiletto]] with their [[Poison Vial]] to harm their enemies.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Dead Body
>
> The victim is covered in numerous needle marks, many of them old and partially healed. It's hard to tell how long the victim was held here, but since the numerous needle marks appear to show signs of infection, it was likely over the course of several days at least, if not much longer.
>
> A successful **Medicine (DC 14)** check while examining the body reveals information based on the result.
>
> - **Result of 14+** Notes that many of the injection show signs of infection and tearing. The process was almost certainly arduous, and painful, and given the relatively fresh state of the corpse, was only concluded a day or two ago.
> - **Result of 16+** The sites of the injections lead you to believe that they underwent numerous cycles of being injected and drained of their fluids, and as far as you can tell, their death was a result of blood loss.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Alchemical ritual
>
> A mixture of biomagic and alchemy is underway on this corner table, small-scale tests of transforming one fluid to another.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Rituals** note that there are reagents and implements marked with sigils and runes tied to practices of biomagic and transformative rituals meant to manipulate living beings.
>
> Examining the circles of magic with a successful **Arcana (DC 16)** check leads you to believe it's trying to enhance or empower some effect within the fluid. To what end is unclear. Characters with either of the above knowledges have **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> #### A Phantom Pain
>
> Characters making a successful **Arcana (DC 14)** check recognize that the magic at play here is primarily illusory in nature, and mind-affecting, specifically. Without closely analyzing the magic to get specifics, the general function is to create hallucinations or visions and incite feeling and pain and fear in what amounts to magical torture.
>
> - **Result of 16+** The alchemical process at work is attempting to isolate something in the compound, something which apparently only occurs in humans.
>
> Characters with knowledge **Knowledge: Rituals** or **Knowledge: Alchemy** have **+2 Boons** on the above check.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Flow Valve
>
> A large flow valve on the wall here can be used to cut off the chemical supply to this area. Doing so causes all the dials and gauges here to show a drop in flow rate and pressure, and cut off this area from circulating more chemicals through it, but has not stopped the rest of the pipes in the area.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Supply Shelves
>
> Examining the shelves stretched across the far south wall, you find bottles of chemicals, racks of unused restraints, bundles of syringes, and rows of canisters marked by both unreadable sigils and more recent handwriting. Nearly everything here is of plain, merciless purpose: tools for containing, injecting, and extracting, unused and waiting their turn.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Alchemy** recognize many of these chemicals as being used to transmute and manipulate biological matter.
