# Collapsed Ruins

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You find yourself in a tangle of ruin that has partly collapsed, with heaps of black rubble and blocks forming irregular walls and choking old corridors. Deep gouges and ragged burrows slice through the fallen stone, evidence that something immense and neither human nor tool-wise clawed or forced its way through long after the chamber's original builders vanished.
>
> The loosened earth and stone feel unstable in places, and every small sound echoes off both solid basalt and recent scars in the rock.
>
> Here and there glowing blue crystals fixed to the wall or sticking out of panels of stone glimmer, casting light, whatever magic held within them not quite run out yet.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Light Crystals
>
> Fused with the stone, these intricately carved crystals are infused with magical energy that causes them to perpetually glow with blue light. There are a few of these [[Fading Light Gem]] sitting loose on the floor which can be claimed, if desired.
>
> A successful **Arcana (DC 16)** check notices the subtle magical inscriptions hidden among the decorative elements in the gems, indicating the high-level of precision and capacity with magic the builders had.
>
> However, given their age, none of them are glowing as brightly as they once did, and likely won't emit light for much longer.
>
> #### Burrowing Occupants
>
> A successful **Wilderness (DC 15)** identifies the markings of a larger creature with heavy claws not meant for burrowing. However, despite this, the ruins have been extensively hollowed out to allow for easy movement between the collapsed chambers.
