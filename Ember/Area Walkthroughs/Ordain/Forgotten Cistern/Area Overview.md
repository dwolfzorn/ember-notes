# Area Overview

### Area Map Context

The area map for the [[Forgotten Cistern]] depicts the above and under ground area where discovered as part of the [[Spreading Sickness]] quest events.

### Environment

Situated beneath the city of Ordain, this ancient ruin predates the streets above by at least a thousand years. The passageways and chambers are fashioned from heavy blocks of black basalt, some shaped with magic, others by hands long vanished from the world.

Threaded throughout these ruins is a much newer incursion: a sprawling laboratory, its structures awkwardly fixed into and cutting through the ancient halls and rooms without regard for their history.

Alchemical lanterns have been bolted to the old stone, the lighting they provide seldom covers any area well, leaving shadows to cluster thickly at the edges of the harsh and uneven glow.

### Ecology

Very little grows in these ruins, and all the lifeforms found within are invasive, having eaten or driven off whatever used to reside within.

### Lore

Previously studied by the Anachraeunum of Ordain, any secrets it once held have been long discovered and cataloged, the records collecting dust in the All-Fable Keep's many archives.

The location was built by the Sedyri people thousands of years ago, possibly after their exodus from their homeland, possibly to serve as an outpost, or even a private home.

In truth, this structure's original purpose is not known to historians and only a collection of educated guesses and speculations are available.
