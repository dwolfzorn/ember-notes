# Injection Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Five medical chairs dominate the space in this small chamber, their cracked leather surfaces stained dark by old blood and fouler wastes. Two of the chairs still hold occupants, a pair of emaciated corpses, one sitting upright with sightless eyes, the other slumped forward in its restraints.
>
> At the back of the room, the black basalt wall bears a relief of ancient Ashka, the artful carving a jarring contrast to the filth and horror surrounding it.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Medical Investigation
>
> A successful **Medicine (DC 14)** while examining the corpses reveals both have died very recently. Ragged puncture wounds cover their limbs, the marks of numerous injections forced through the skin in haste or disregard.
>
> Their drawn faces and wasted bodies show there was nothing that could have saved them short of arriving days earlier.
>
> #### Discarded Clothing
>
> The stained, soaked clothing underfoot has already been picked of any valuables, but the style is distinctly Arcturian, most of it rugged and clearly hand-made.

### Ashka Relief

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The stone relief is of an Askha figure with their hands cupped at chest level. Their robes have decorative elements that flow like water, and a ball of flame rests in their hands. They stand on cracked, uneven ground that looks like cooled lava, and they are surrounded by billowing clouds.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Empty Slot
>
> Characters with a **Awareness (DC 12, Passive)** or making a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** notice that there is an indent in the palm of the Askha figure, like they were once holding something.
>
> The indent is plain, with no magical or mechanical attributes that any character can find.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Beaten to the Punch
>
> There once was a Sedyri elemental emblem in this statue, but it was taken by the Mutagists some time ago, and is now in the hands of Mutagist Kaftor Brenk, the lab's overseer.
