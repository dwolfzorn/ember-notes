# Caveway

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Haphazard wooden walkways, built from warped planks and crude supports, wind their way above a channel of slow-moving water. The slats lashed and nailed to half-submerged beams sunk into the water, and laid across natural stone jutting up, creating a dubious path through the area.
>
> Below, the water moves silently, carrying streaks of shimmering green fluid along its surface to parts unknown. The echoing mutter of water dampens your ability to hear much else beyond it.
