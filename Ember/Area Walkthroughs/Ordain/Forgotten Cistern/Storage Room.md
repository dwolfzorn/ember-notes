# Storage Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You enter a room cramped with storage containers, its low ceiling and narrow dimensions made tighter still by the ranks of crates, barrels, and bulging sacks that crowd the space. Every available surface has been put to use holding a container or stack of items.
>
> The air is heavy here with the stink of mildew and damp old wood.
>
> Underfoot, the floor has been covered with uneven wooden slats, many of which have softened and warped from the constant damp, leaving patches darkened with rot.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Stolen Effects
>
> The store room contains crates of clothing and items that appear to have belonged to Ordani locals. These can be searched with a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check locates and identifies some [[Personal Effects]]. These items can be gathered up and taken as evidence, or as treasure to be sold later.
>
> #### Packed but Worthless
>
> A quick search reveals nothing of value in the various containers, just bundles of spare vials, cases of needles starting to rust, assorted copper pipe fittings and patches, lengths of buckled leather restraints, and more packed away in no apparent order.
>
> #### Narrow Passage
>
> A narrow passage stretches into the darkness here. Small and tiny creatures can move through this space comfortably, but medium sized creatures will need to squeeze through.
>
> While squeezing through a space, a creature must spend 1 extra foot for every foot it moves, and it has **-2 Banes** on any physical rolls and saving throws it makes. Attack rolls against the creature have **+2 Boons** while it’s in the smaller space.
>
> The passage connects the [[Storage Room]] to the [[Living Area]].
