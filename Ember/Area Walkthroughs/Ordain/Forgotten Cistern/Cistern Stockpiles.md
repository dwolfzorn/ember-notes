# Cistern Stockpiles

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Against the wall pallets crowd the floor, each laden with wooden crates packed tight with vials of emerald-green chemical giving off a faint light.
>
> Alongside the stock, two tables sit, one brimming with a mixture of alchemical and magical implements, while the other two hold materials in support of whatever it is doing.
>
> The air here bites with the tang of distilled chemicals and dust. At the far end, stone stairs rise steeply toward some route out of the depths, possibly to the city above.

### Mutagist Contingent

Several Mutagists occupy this area.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Dangerous Minions
>
> For combat with Kaftor and his minions see [[Glowing Cistern]]on the [[Glowing Cistern]] page.

### Searching the Area

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Searching Ritual Table
>
> A mixture of biomagic and alchemy is underway on this corner table, small-scale tests of transforming one fluid to another.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Rituals** note that there are reagents and implements marked with sigils and runes tied to practices of biomagic and transformative rituals meant to manipulate living beings.
>
> Examining the circles of magic with a successful **Arcana (DC 16)** check leads you to believe it's trying to enhance or empower some effect within the fluid. To what end is unclear. Characters with either of the above knowledges have **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> #### Searching the Alchemy Table
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Alchemy** recognize this setup as a rather advanced alchemical filtering and mixing station. It's taking numerous inputs and creating a new combined compound with specific mix ratios.
>
> Nearby notes and diagrams detail the formulas at play, but it's not clear that the chemicals are or what the final goal is.
>
> #### Searching the Document Table
>
> The table is laden down with a collection of [[Mutagist Alchemical Data]] which details what the Mutagists were trying to do here. Inscrutable to anyone not properly versed in **Knowledge: Alchemy**, the content is so dense that it would take months of study by an expert to make sense of what they were doing.

There are numerous crates here packed with chemicals. The sheer volume of them makes cataloging and searching them an impossibility.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Crates
>
> Dozens of crates large and small hold a countless number of alchemical components, compounds mutagens, samples, and more. There are too many to properly catalog or search.
>
> These will need to be left behind for the Veiled Chain to come pick up later.
>
> #### Imports and Exports
>
> Resting on one crate is a logbook that details the items coming in and out of this lab. Taking a few minutes to examine them, a worrying discovery is made: up to a week ago the lab was only importing materials, but then began exporting small batches of something initialed as "B.R."
>
> The logbook does not list the source or destination for these shipments. This [[Cistern Stockpile Logbook]] can be taken as evidence.

### Leaving the Area

The stairwell to the south of this area leads up into the sewers, and then into Ordain itself.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Meandering Exit
>
> A party taking this exit will find themselves traveling out of the ruins into a disused section of the city sewers. After an hour of walking they then emerge from a city works access point in [[Quarry Run]], above ground.
