# Secluded Grave

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cramped, half-buried chamber is half choked by rubble, the floor and one wall leaning inwards from some long-past collapse.
>
> On the wall, a stone relief can still be made out depicting an Ashka bearing a long blade, turned with menace against an attacker who has been almost entirely destroyed by the collapse. The cross guard of their blade seems to be missing a piece.
>
> Resting by the far wall, a simple funeral cairn of weathered stones marks the final resting place of some being.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Disturbing the Dead
>
> If disturbed, the crumbling armor and bones shelter a circular crystal that contains water in its surface, or appears to.
>
> The party can collect a [[Sedyri Water Emblem]] here.
>
> The body is also wearing a [[Scaled Hood]] which is in pristine condition, despite the considerable age of the remains. Characters with **Knowledge: Legends** or **Knowledge: Ancients** recognize this as a magically infused item made by the ancient Sedyri.
>
> #### Missing Emblem
>
> A successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check while looking over the relief notes that the Ashkan warrior's armor has a fire motif.
>
> If the party places a [[Sedyri Fire Emblem]] into the relief's cross guard, something happens elsewhere in the ruins. Read or paraphrase the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The emblem lots into place and for a brief moment flames leap across the blade of the relief. A moment later you hear a heavy thud and the sound of falling rocks elsewhere in the ruins. The shock of it causes the emblem to shake loose of the cracked relief, and fall to the floor.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Moving Parts
>
> Placing the emblem in the cross guard caused another relief in the [[Crumbling Corner]] to move, revealing more items.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Rudely Awakened
>
> If the party has not alerted or defeated any of the drakes in the ruins yet, they will all be awakened and put on alert. Though they won't know where to look for the party, a fight is almost guaranteed now.
