# Marshy Grounds

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The land flattens out into a sprawling wetland at the foot of a roaring waterfall, its thunder echoing distantly across the basin. Water laps against slick rocks and muddy banks, the rhythmic sound only broken by the constant crash of water far overhead. Mist curls through the reeds, lending the air a chill weight.
>
> As the wind shifts you catch the sudden stench of decaying plants and stinging chemicals. You trace the scene to its source, and find a grim sight: oily ribbons of some sickly green swirling on the surface of the waters, and rotting reeds everywhere the oil collects along the banks.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Finding the Source
>
> Locating the source of the alchemical runoff requires some time spent searching and at least one successful check from the following options: **Awareness (DC 14)**, **Wilderness (DC 12)**, or **Awareness (DC 16)**.
>
> On success the party discovers an old stone culvert.
>
> - A character with **Knowledge: Legends** or who makes a successful **Society (DC 14)** check additionally notes that this stone is much rougher, older, and darker than what's used in Ordain. This may be the remains of some partially submerged ruins.
