# Tank Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Emerging from the water, you find the stone passage rises on a gradual slope into a broad chamber carved from ancient basalt, the walls glossy from long exposure to water.
>
> Dominating the space are a pair of massive brass tanks set to one side, their surfaces mottled and tarnished, and their presence not part of the original construction. A series of pipes stretch out overhead, going in all directions.
>
> The air is thick with the sharp reek of chemicals and rotting flesh.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Stirring Up Trouble
>
> The two large tanks swirl with liquid, both leaking a caustic green chemical from their bases, letting it pool in the group and gaps of the ancient stone underfoot.
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Machines** or **Knowledge: Alchemy** recognizes that these machines are moving liquids between the tanks, separating them in some way, and sending the filtered chemicals into one pipeline, and sending the runoff elsewhere.
>
> The construction is slapdash and crude.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Main Flow Valve
>
> A large flow valve sits attached to a huge overhead pipe above. Characters with **Knowledge: Machines** suspect this is a main flow valve.
>
> Turning it requires a successful `[[/check str 16]]` check, and causes all the pipes to shudder and groan, but doesn't stop the machines.
>
> A successful **Awareness (DC 15)** while looking over the machine notes there is no actual way to full stop the chemical dumping process, the Mutagists built it without such features, seemingly unconcerned about how much chemical they dump into the water supply.
>
> Perhaps that was their goal.
