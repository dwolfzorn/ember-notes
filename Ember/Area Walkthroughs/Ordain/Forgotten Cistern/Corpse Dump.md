# Corpse Dump

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This circular chamber stinks of death and corrosive chemicals. At the center, a broad, stone-rimmed well collects foul water, its surface tinted green by a constant flow from a large overhead pipe that dumps a stream of glowing, acrid fluid into the basin.
>
> Against wall a heap of shrouded bodies lies is piled up, leaking rancid fluids into the already tainted water supply. Opposite the bodies is a beautiful relief standing in contrast to the grisly scene around it.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Awful Runoff
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Alchemy** or making a successful **Medicine (DC 12)** check recognize that combination of rotting bodies and runoff chemicals are immensely toxic and either one of them alone could account for the sickness taking hold in the city. Together, they almost guarantee it.

### Hidden Doorway

On the far wall of the room there is a hidden door behind a stone relief. The party can study it, but won't find a way to open it until later in the dungeon.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The stone relief is of an Askha figure with their hands cupped at chest level, holding an orb. Their robes have decorative elements that flow like water, and a ball of flame rests in their hands. They stand on cracked, uneven ground that looks like cooled lava, and they are surrounded by billowing clouds.
>
> A blue gem fixed and fused with the stone above glows with a dim blue light.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Empty Slot
>
> Characters with a **Awareness (DC 12, Passive)** or making a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** notice that there is an indent in the wall beside the relief, circular, and faceted, like something is meant to slot into it.
>
> A successful **Arcana (DC 14)** notes that there are small divination symbols inside the depression, indicating it might react to something being placed into the slot.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Sealed Door
>
> Any of the Sedyri elemental emblems will work to open this door. They can be acquired elsewhere in the dungeon.

If characters slot any elemental emblem into the space aside the door, the stone wall slides upward, revealing stairs to the [[Glowing Cistern]].
