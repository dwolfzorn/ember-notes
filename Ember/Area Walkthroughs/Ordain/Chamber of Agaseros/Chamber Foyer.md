# Chamber Foyer

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Inside the chamber itself, you begin to feel a buzzing charge infuse you, as if you're walking into some kind of magical nexus. However, the scene before you is entirely ordinary, and the small room seems to be a simple foyer or entrance, with several chairs, carpets, and crates filled with tools and items against the walls. Against the far wall, a large bronze door is embedded in a complex magical mechanism.

The unassuming room serves as both a storage area for simple items and a preparation area where people can return to rest or prepare themselves for the challenges ahead.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Simple Storage
>
> Some members of the Anachraenum and even the Fulgurite Blades (Who are currently within the Chamber in the [[Arcane Engine]] room) carry supplies down into he chamber and leave them in this room. The party may have done the same and wish to leave some of their supplies here to return to, or they may wish to look through what is currently here that might help them.
>
> However, the contents of the room are stuffed into crates at random with some old and broken equipment among the more useful supplies, and the party will have to spend some time here to gain anything valuable. A successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check allows a character to roll on the Chamber Supplies table. Alternatively, if the party wishes to spend an hour rummaging through the supplies here, they can pick what they need from the table instead.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Bronze Door
>
> Anyone who walks up to the Bronze door can attempt to investigate it with a variety of different checks.
>
> - With a **Awareness (DC 12, Passive)** or successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check, it's clear that the surface of the door is smooth, except banding lines of reinforcing bronze. It is also clean and in excellent condition. In the very center is a protruding circular impression of a hand. Anyone who places thier hand upon the door finds that the door slides open.
> - With a successful `[[/check inv 14]]` check, or anyone with **Knowledge: Machines** realizes that the large double door is made of solid bronze, likely weighing several tons. The mechanism is likely complex, and you can see some of the rotating gears that will allow the door to slide into the wall.
> - If a character casts [[Detect Magic]] and or rolls a `[[/check arcana 16]]`, they discover that everything within the room is infused with magic to some degree. The amount of arcane energy in the air is almost overwhelming and can cause a painful reaction if a caster is not careful. Anyone who casts a spell in this room must roll a 1d20, `[[/roll 1d20]]`, including Detect Magic. On a result of 1, they suffer a reduction to their constitution by 1 for 25 hours.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Overwhelming Magic
>
> If a character casts [[Detect Magic]] and or rolls a `[[/check arcana 18]]`, they discover that everything within the room is infused with magic to some degree. The amount of arcane energy in the air is almost overwhelming and can cause arcane magic to amplify spells if a caster is not careful. Anyone who casts a spell in this room must roll a 1d20, `[[/roll 1d20]]`, including anyone who casts Detect Magic to discover this.
>
> - On a result of 1, the spell fails to cast and the caster suffers `[[/damage 1d6 force]]` and suffers a reduction to their constitution by 1 for 24 hours.
> - On a Result of 20, the spell is cast one level higher.
