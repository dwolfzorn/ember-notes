# Matrix of Agaseros

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> With a soft grinding sound, the door slides open, revealing a large chamber hiding one final secret, the heart of the Chamber itself, the Matrix of Agaseros. The dark stone shell, covered in massive runes, emanates a rhythmic, powerful light from its shining core that seems to breathe, expanding and contracting like a living entity. The light swirls in mesmerizing patterns, casting ethereal shadows that dance along the walls. The arcane energy here flows from the Matrix in currents, occasionally sparking and crackling like electricity.

#### Secret Optional Boss

This room is currently inaccessible in Early Access. Its contents will become a secret boss that is unlocked by finding and activating hidden mechanisms, discovered in various rooms throughout the area map.
