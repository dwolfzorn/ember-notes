# Fossil Display

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small room appears to be a display chamber, with a large fossil skeleton of a long-dead hunting creature displayed directly across from the doorway. Several small glass cases line the walls to the left and right, but they seem to be empty.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### An Empty Display
>
> Those who further search the room with a successful `[[/check inv 16]]` notice that one of the cases has a [[Spinel]] lying in the corner.
