# Trap Maze

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Ahead of you is a narrow and dimly lit passage that seems to swallow the light. The smooth stone walls seem to close in around you, and the path ahead twists and turns without end.

The maze within the chamber is fairly simple, with several traps laid throughout to ambush the unwary. The party will have to move carefully around each corner to reach the other side safely.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Interactivity
>
> During the exploration of the maze, the party will encounter two kinds of traps.
>
> - Bronze pressure plates that activate darts set into the walls
> - Thin, taunt wire across corners that activate blade traps in the walls.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Dart Trap
>
> The pressure plates are scattered throughout the maze, mostly in the longer sections of the hallways.
>
> A character who succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 14)** check who is close enough to see the pressure plate clearly can notice it on the floor ahead. The small opening for the darts on the ends of the hallways cannot be seen.
>
> - The plate can be disarmed with a successful use of **`[[/skill dex 13]]`** Thieves' Tools.
>
> The Dart Trap is triggered when 10 or more pounds of weight are applied to the pressure plate. When the trap is triggered, several darts fly across the hallways from the nearby wall.
>
> - Any character who is within the area of the trap immediately or at the start of a subsequent turn must succeed on a `[[/save dexterity 18]]` saving throw or take `[[/damage 1d6 piercing]]` damage or half damage on a successful save. Anyone who fails the check gains the &Reference[poisoned] for `[[/roll 1d4]]` minutes

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Spinning Blades Trap
>
> The wires that activate these traps are scattered throughout the maze, across various corners.
>
> A character with a `[[/check prc 15 passive format=long]]` or who succeeds on a **Awareness (DC 16)** check who is close enough to clearly see the wires can notice them and the thin wall seams from which the spinning blades would issue.
>
> - The wire can be disarmed with a successful use of **`[[/skill dex 16]]`** Thieves' Tools.
>
> The wire is broken when 3 or more pounds of weight are applied across it. When the trap is triggered, several whirling blades erupt from the nearby wall directly on top of the corner section.
>
> - Any character who is within the area of the trap immediately or at the start of a subsequent turn must succeed on a `[[/save dexterity 16]]` saving throw or take `[[/damage 2d6 slashing]]` damage or half damage on a successful save.
