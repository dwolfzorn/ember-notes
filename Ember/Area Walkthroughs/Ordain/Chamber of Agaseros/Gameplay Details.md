# Gameplay Details

## Illumination

The Chamber of Agaseros has a number of light sources throughout its three floors. The primary sources of light are Arcane Lamps on many of the walls. These lights cast 40 feet of dim purple light and 10 feet of bright light.

The chamber also holds floating light sources that wander the larger spaces of the rooms and can be found across all three floors. These floating lights are illusory in nature as they can pass through the Arcane Barriers and doors. The Floating Arcane Lights cast 20 feet of dim light and 10 feet of bright light.

On the first floor, within the [[Skull Spider Lair]], there are many green ceremonial candles. These cast 2 feet of dim light and 0.2 feet of flickering bright green light.

In addition, several areas emit light sources due to various passive effects. These areas include the acid pits, which cast 15 feet of dim light and 10 feet of bright light. Meri's Garden has a few scattered red lights that vary in intensity and brightness. The glowing lava light in the steam room cast 15 feet of dim light and no bright light, and the coals cast 5 feet of dim light and 3.4 of bright light.

## Terrain

The Chamber can repair itself and also move its various rooms around, like the internal mechanics of a great clock. These changes occur over many years, so it will not look any different if the players attempt to access it again. However, for the Expedition Challenge, the Chamber has been specifically configured to challenge new members of the Anacharenum. This means that floors, walls, and any other surface are typically smooth, clean, and repaired, and only a few loose debris items like pebbles, dust, and webs can be found.

Only [[Meri's Garden]] is treated as difficult terrain and even the water within the [[Main Reservoir]] at the bottom of the chamber is usually calm and unmoving.

## Elevation

The ceilings of the Chamber of Agaseroes are quite tall, at nearly 30 feet. This is true across the Chamber except within the Central Column Room, which has a height of 120 feet. This means that between the levels, there is a height of 40 feet.

Across all three floors, there are parts of the room that dip down into lower sections by 10 feet, and each one is filled with various substances or unique floor types. These include:

- [[Skull Spider Lair]]
- [[Meri's Garden]]
- [[Steam Room]]
- [[Acid Challenge]]
- [[Acid Foyer]]

## Additional Gameplay

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Fulgurite Blades
>
> Once they reach the chamber that holds the [[Arcane Engine]], the characters will encounter the [[Fulgurite Blades]], a rival party of adventurers who are also undertaking the Expedition Challenge.
>
> The party may or may not have previously encountered the Fulgurite Blades as part of the [[Curious Party]] event during the [[Over The Moon]] Chapter 1 side quest.
>
> The Fulgurite Blades are currently stuck trying to repair the enchanted mechanism as part of the challenge, and further details on the confrontation between these two parties can be found in the Arcane Engine room description.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Sealed Runic Doors
>
> All the Runic Doors in the Chamber are made of nearly 2 feet of solid bronze and operate through a combination of magic and machinery. None of them can be opened without finding the corresponding color switch, which requires the party to traverse the room's challenges in the correct order.
>
> Forcing the doors open in any way is not possible and would require high-level dispelling magic as they are incredibly resistant to physical force and magical attacks, including spells like [[Heat Metal]] which has no effect on the thick bronze.
