# Disturbed Library

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Stepping through the door, you find a ruined library that seems to have been overturned and attacked by some sort of creature, or perhaps by an explosion or whirlwind that recently tore through the room. The shelves appear to be leaning precariously forward in places, their wood cracked and splintered. The floor is littered with crumbling books and pages, some torn or ripped apart.

## Life finds a way

Once the party enters the room by only a few steps, they are ambushed by the six [[Animated Rune Tome]]'s that lie hidden in the room.

> [!abstract] Animated Rune Tome
> **[[Animated Rune Tome]]**
>
> Level 1 · Unknown Unknown
>
> ![Animated Rune Tome](../../../assets/image038.webp)

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Animated Rune Tome **Tactics**
>
> As combat begins, add only one or two tomes to the combat tracker and reveal them to attack the party. As combat continues, keep adding tomes in between player turns until all six of the tomes have been revealed.
>
> The tomes are mindless, aggressive, and simple, but are able to cast spells from thier pages.
>
> - They will attmept to cast [[Ray of Enfeeblement]] at the start of the combat at each party member.
> - They will also attempt to cast [[Darkness]] or [[Fog Cloud]] around themselves to prevent them from being targeted by the party.
> - Their main attacks will be [[Ray of Frost]] which they can cast as if they are 5th level and the ray deals 2d8 damage instead.
>
> The books have no concept of self-preservation and will not attempt to flee once injured, but if engaged in melee, they will use [[Blink]] to teleport elsewhere in the room. Once a book is defeated, it reverts to an intact book form that is badly damaged, and investigating reveals nothing of value.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### A Lost Library
>
> Within the room are many ruined books and notes, but nothing much that the party can uncover or find useful.
>
> - However, anyone who chooses to rummage through the papers and designs comes across [[Journal of Agaseros: Page 8]].
