# Reservoir Control Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The walls of this small room are covered with large bronze pipes sprawling across dark, ancient stone, slick with moisture and adorned with intricate carvings of flowing water. In the middle of the room is a large square ring of arcane runes embedded into the stone floor, glowing with a vibrant purple hue, and within the center is a large basin of crystal-clear water.

#### Secret Optional Route

This room is currently inaccessible in Early Access. Its contents will become a secret route that is unlocked by finding and activating hidden mechanisms in the chamber, discovered in various rooms throughout the area map.
