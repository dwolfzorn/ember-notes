# Northern Alcove

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small antechamber is bereft of details and is mostly empty, with a single lamp glowing on the northern wall and two thin hallways flanking the lamp that continue onward.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Trap Signaling
>
> Any character that moves through the space and attempts to pass through the two small hallways, either side of the lamp, will encounter pressure plates of bronze inset into the floor. Anyone rushing blindly ahead will step onto the plates and hear a small "click," but nothing happens.
>
> - With a **Awareness (DC 15, Passive)** or successful **Awareness (DC 15)** check within the alcove, a character will notice the plates ahead of time.
> - Investigating the plates with a successful `[[/check inv 14]]` or anyone with **Knowledge: Machines** discovers that the plates are actually not connected to anything.
