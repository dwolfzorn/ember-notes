# Antechamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small antechamber appears to be empty except for a small desk against the far wall. Made of dark wood, it is covered in notes and pieces of loose paper. To the left and right, two thick bronze doors are set into the walls. Each seems to be missing any gems or complicated mechanisms for unlocking them, and they seem to match the entrance door, able to be opened with a simple touch.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Another Lonely Desk
>
> The desk in this room contains several notes that detail further arcane studies and experimentations by Agaseros. Anyone who chooses to search the desk discovers a curious scrap: [[Journal of Agaseros: Page 6]].
>
> Any character who succeeds on a `[[/check arcana 15]]` check while studying the Journal is able to reveal the following:
>
> - Agaseros was intensely interested in creating arcane machines that could outlive him.
> - He attempted various methods to achieve his goals and traveled across the world to investigate strange arcane phenomena.
> - Characters with **Attunement: Signara** or **Knowledge: Rituals** have **+2 Boons**.
