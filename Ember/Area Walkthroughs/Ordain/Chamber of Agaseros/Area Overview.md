# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The area map for the Chamber of Agaseros depicts a three-level subterranean sprawl that serves as the scenery for the [[The Expedition Challenge]] quest event known as [[The Wayfinder's Gauntlet]].
>
> - This map depicts three distinct sections of the area: the [[Chamber of Agaseros - Reservoir]], the [[Chamber of Agaseros - Main Level]], and the [[Chamber of Agaseros - Entrance]].
> - There is a single method of entry: a main gate located south of the [[Chamber Foyer]], off the edge of the map. The party begins the encounter in the foyer.
> - Additional information about the Archive's arcane locks and the rotating mirrors that control them are located in the [[Gameplay Details]] section of this walkthrough.
>
> Since the Chamber of Agaseros is a smaller location within the mile-wide hex that comprises the Ordani district of [[All-Fable Keep]], the Gazetteer Entry below contains information about the neighborhood as a whole.

## Gazetteer Entry

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A massive fortified keep draped in antiquity towers above the district here, standing nearly five stories high above the city's cobblestoned streets. Several smaller structures are built up around this central edifice, whose architecture seems curiously older than Ordain itself. Unlike most of the city's other landmark buildings, there is a relative quietude to this place, suggesting a gentle air of privacy within its furthest reaches.
>
> In spite of this tranquility (or perhaps because of it), scholars, guild members, and visitors alike can be seen reposing among the district's exterior walkways and balconies. Furthermore, you spy a bronze emblem hanging above the tower's large front gate: a sextant fashioned into the likeness of a capital "A."

This fortified stone keep serves as the primary headquarters of the treasure-hunting guild known as the Anachraenum, a tightly-knit organization of explorers, researchers, and historians stationed in Ordain. In addition to grand chambers like museums, libraries, and vaults that cater to the guild's ongoing interests, All-Fable Keep provides comfortable housing for most of the guild's local members, as well as semi-secret training facilities for Anachraenum operations. Several of the Keep's museum galleries are actually open to the public; and the venerated guildhall — despite the clandestine airs of the guild itself — has become a source of cultural pride for many citizens of Ordain, particularly those who prize scholasticism and adventure.

- **District Type:** Institutional with some Commercial
- **Services:** Treasure-hunting guild (historical research, artifacts, antiques, appraisal).
- **Population:** 600
- **Languages:** Common, Arcden, Imperial, Cascilian, Luma
- **Cost of Living:** Comfortable-to-Wealthy

The aged and handsome edifice known as All-Fable Keep serves as the storied base of operations of the [[Anachraenum]] in Ordain, where it occupies most of its local district. The Keep acts as both guildhall and training facility for its motley fellowship, who come and go like the wind while they attend to official guild business throughout the Arctus Plateau. All-Fable Keep also famously plays host to the Amaranthine Archives, an esteemed museum facility that houses a most impressive collection of distinct treasures and notable antiques.

The Anachraenum takes in more artifacts and antiques than they would like to maintain; so, naturally, they turn their surplus into profit when the occasion allows. Gift shops in the museum offer antique trinkets and common magic items for sale, while more sophisticated buyers are allowed back-room access for high-tier purchases and private consignments. And just as the districts of southeastern Ordain look to the Anachraenum for local support in times of trouble, the city's people consider All-Fable Keep to be a major cultural repository, where the most important relics of Aterica's past are kept safe and secure. As they say: "a hint of ev'ry legend ever told lies somewhere in All-Fable's countless holds."

### Landmarks

**Amaranthine Archives**

Also known as the Relic House, this venerated museum serves as the primary repository for the Anachraenum's vast collection of artifacts and antiques. Guild members who maintain the Archives host a quarterly rotation of thoughtfully curated exhibits, offered at the cost of a small donation thanks to the generous sponsorship of the Anachraenum's most elite patrons. To wit, this kind of sponsorship from affluent members of society makes up the lion’s share of the guild’s regular income. Furthermore, rumors persist about a network of secret tunnels beneath the Archives that leads to various points of interest throughout Ordain — and there is indeed some truth to this, but public understanding of this surreptitious architecture is tenuous at best.

**The Orrery**

This large circular chamber is bedecked with a hulking mechanical model of Ember's cosmos, and is outfitted with a variety of scientific and arcane devices used for long-distance observation, including everything from telescopes to crystal balls. The model of the cosmos is an intricate mechanism that is capable of simulating the rotation of Ember's sun and moons, while some of the observatory equipment allows guild members to actually view those heavenly bodies from the confines of the Keep's top floor. The Orrery is maintained by a manic warlock of Volta named **Barnabas Hilheth** (CG, Kithil Kivahr, he/him), who is often consumed by his arcane research for excessively long hours of the day.

**The Chamber of Agaseros**

Powered by magic and esoteric technology, this massive arena in the lower levels of All-Fable Keep was made to train Anachraenum guild members in a variety of combat and exploration techniques. The dynamic features of this self-transforming chamber are designed to challenge the acrobatic and athletic capabilities of anyone who enters, along with their ability to think fast and react to immediate danger or complex puzzles. Occasionally, the Anachraenum will loosen the restrictions on this private room for those eager or impetuous enough to try their own turn at surviving the Risk Chamber's gauntlet (a privilege typically reserved for vetted allies or wealthy patrons).

The Risk Chamber is managed by the retired swashbuckler **Aelen** **Morde** (CG, Arcturian Human, he/him), who oversees the dangerous arena with madcap enthusiasm and sadistic glee. Treat Morde as an [[Assassin]] with a Strength score of 15.

### Locals

**[[Arcos Sarinland]] (NG, Ordani Keth, he/him)**

Arcos Sarinland was once a promising young ranger who joined the Anachraenum forty years ago and became a close friend of Darion Bastilla. He was widely celebrated for his participation in the Battle of the Throat, where a combined fleet led by Darion destroyed the Blood Barons' assembling ships in Golden Bay; however, he retired from active adventuring not long after. For thirty years, he taught and guided new members in Ordain, always maintaining a friendly yet troubled demeanor. Recently thrust into the guild's leadership after Fernis Ossa declined the nomination, he faces a number of increasing challenges as Ordain slips further into unrest.

**[[Fernis Ossa]] (LG, Ordani Ashka, she/her)**

Fernis Ossa is one of the most well-known and admired Anachraenum members living in Ordain. She led a group of adventurers called the Skywarders many years ago before effectively becoming the envoy and deputy to the previous guildmaster, Veron Longspear. She spent many years among the people of Ordain, and her adventurous spirit is matched only by her dedication to her friends and comrades. Fernis often organizes gatherings and events in Ordain and travels to many nearby friendly cities like Luen, Wick, and the Lumek city of Hearth. She has a genuine interest in the stories and lives of the people she meets.

**[[Adelyne Goss]]( NG, Waerd Human, she/her)**

Loremistress Adelyne Goss is a sarcastic and sharp character, and despite her extensive physical injuries sustained after battling a dragon deep in the Duskflare Jungles many years ago, she remains an intensely insightful and productive scholar and librarian. She is also extremely proud of her Waerd heritage and wears a combination of traditional Waerd clothing interwoven with ancient Sedyri trinkets.

### Lore

In addition to the organization's vast collection of antiques, the magical and technological assets of the Anachraenum are numerous. For nearly a hundred years, this storied group has successfully explored some of Ember’s most dangerous landscapes in search of legendary ruins from bygone ages; and the vaults of All-Fable Keep are lined with the most precious fruits of their antiquarian labors. For the hundreds of arcane relics and objets d'art that decorate their public displays, there are hundreds more hidden away from common view.

Some notable relics include: the Amethyst Skeleton of St. Prestyn, the Sword of Arctus, the Silver Raiment of Lost Aed’Orna, the fabled Entropy Stone, and the largest collection of paintings and illustrations from the late clairvoyant fantasist Varan Fel.
