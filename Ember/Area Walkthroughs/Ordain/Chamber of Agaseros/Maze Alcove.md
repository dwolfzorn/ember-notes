# Maze Alcove

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A single light shines ahead within a small square room.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### A Lonely Desk
>
> Within the room is a small desk, with pictures, designs, and diagrams of the traps littered across the maze, in addition to several scraps of notes and papers with thoughts and ideas.
>
> - Anyone who chooses to rummage through the papers and designs comes across [[Journal of Agaseros: Page 3]].
> - Characters who succeed on a `[[/check arcana 15]]` check or **Knowledge: Machines** can identify the bulk of the designs as prototypes and would not be helpful in disarming the traps in the maze.
>   **Result of 16+** You manage to find the correct design for the spinning blade traps and are able to more easily disarm them now that you understand how they work. Anyone attempting to disarm the Spinning Blade Traps gains a +2 to their skill check to disarm them.
> - Characters who succeed on a `[[/check inv 15]]` check while looking through the designs can spot a few scattered notes on the table, likely used as component pieces in magical works, but they are too faded and mysterious to decipher.
