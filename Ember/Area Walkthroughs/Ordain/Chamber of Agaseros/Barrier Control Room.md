# Barrier Control Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Within this small room, more of the large bronze pies line the walls, but the primary focus seems to be a strange central console that dominates the space, covered in dials and levers, each meticulously covered in tiny runes and symbols.

#### Secret Optional Route

This room is currently inaccessible in Early Access. Its contents will become a secret route that is unlocked by finding and activating hidden mechanisms in the chamber, discovered in various rooms throughout the area map.
