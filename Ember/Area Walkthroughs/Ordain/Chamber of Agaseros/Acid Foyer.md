# Acid Foyer

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you step into the small, dimly lit room, your senses are immediately assaulted by a pungent, acrid scent that hangs thick in the air. In the center of the space, a pit of glowing green acid bubbles ominously below a grate that only seems to cover most of the pit. Some worrying gaps on either side are large enough for something or someone to fall into the acid itself. Floating above the grate is a small construct that seems familiar.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Acid Basin
>
> The pit in this room is 15 feet deep, but 5 feet of its depth contains a violent, intensely glowing green acid, so it appears to be 10 feet deep from above.
>
> Any character who falls into the pit suffers `[[/damage 3d10 acid]]` damage. The acid also counts as difficult terrain if anyone tries to swim or move through it. Characters who find themselves within the pit during combat take an additional `[[/damage 3d10 acid]]` damage each turn they stay in the acid.
>
> Characters can attempt to climb out of the pit with a `[[/check acr 13]]` check. If they fail a check outside of combat, they slip back into the acid and take a further `[[/damage 3d10 acid]]` damage for each attempt.

## A strange Introduction

The small single Aetherial in this room is set with a stay command. It does not attack anyone who enters the room. However, when the party enters the room itself, read the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The small construct doesn’t seem to respond to your movements for a few moments, but with a small ping, the device begins emitting a message in a shrill little voice.
>
> > Welcome. Hello!
> > Please continue through the chamber at your own pace. Unlock each door by completing room challenges in order!
> > … bzz.. Thank you!

> **[[Anachraenum Aetherial]]**
>
> Level 1 · Unknown Unknown

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### An Empty Room
>
> The Aetherial will continue to hover in the room, and has little interest in the party. It cannot be talked to and does not react to anything the party attempts.
>
> - A successful `[[/check arc 15]]` or anyone with **Knowledge: Rituals** determines that these strange little constructs have been created by a highly competent and powerful arcane mage, likely the aformented mage Agaseros with a level of technology approaching the Aedir but still somewhat unrefined.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Aetherial Tactics
>
> If the party decides that the little construct has to be destroyed for some reason, it will react to defend itself. Once combat begins, it will not move from its position but will fire its[[Laser]] at whoever attacked it first.
>
> The creature is basically mindless and has no self-preservation instincts. It will stay where it was stationed until it is destroyed.
