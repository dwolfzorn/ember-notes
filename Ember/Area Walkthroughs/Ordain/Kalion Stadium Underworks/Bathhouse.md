# Bathhouse

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The faint smell of petrichor accompanies the sound of lightly running water here, where a large and inviting bath dominates the chamber. Towels and fresh linens lie draped upon wooden pallets to stay dry, and the stonework here is remarkably clean thanks to the immediate presence of the facility's aqueducts.

This chamber is the facility's bathhouse, and it features a small variety of magical and mechanical accoutrements to ensure the good health and hygiene of the residents here.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Bathhouse
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - `[[/roll 1d3+2]]` [[Blankets]], bleached white but variously stained with blood.
> - A vial of [[Perfume]] scented with essence of aithus.
> - A waterlogged [[Book]] of salacious fiction titled "The Executioner's Tales."
>
> The water that flows from the nearby fountains and basins is potable drinking water, cool and clean, piped in via ancient Ordani aqueducts from freshwater sources nearby.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 16 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check is able to notice a button concealed among the bricks that line the bath here.
>
> Any character who examines the button and makes a successful **Arcana (DC 13)** or **Awareness (DC 13)** check determines its precise use without pushing it: the button powers a slab of enchanted stone at the bottom of the bath that heats or cools the water within it.
>
> - **Knowledge: Machines**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
