# Hall of Records

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The three circular chambers here are lined floor to ceiling with bookshelves packed full of tomes, scrolls, and assorted bric-a-brac. The light of two ornate crystal chandeliers casts its glow upon this aged library, where two Undaunted trainees are busy sorting through a large stack of books — setting some on the shelf and some others to the side.
>
> The youngest of them speaks.
>
> > I don't know why we're wasting our time with these moth-eaten textbooks. You know we're not going to find anything.
>
> The other trainee retorts with an air of indignation.
>
> > Look, you know just as well as I do, if we overlook some scrap of paper with a funny sigil or a mention of the Shear on it, she'll tear our fuckin' heads off and drink wine out of 'em during the next match. Just keep lookin' … at least we're not on our knees in the Sanctum right now.
>
> The younger athlete hastily replies.
>
> > You got that right.

These chambers have served as the Underworks library for ages, and already bore an impressive number of books and scrolls upon their shelves when Zira and her team first occupied the area. Since then, Zira's furious efforts to gather lost and eldritch lore have resulted in a recognizable backlog and overflow of new inventory.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Hall of Records
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - Much of the literature here seems to be historical in nature, with many works on the mythology of Ember and legends of the Arctus Plateau.
> - Countless books, tomes, and scrolls of a mundane nature that detail a wide array of esoteric subjects.
> - 12 aged hardcover [[Books]] that pertain to Ember's moons, the Pathways, various deities, and the Abyss.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 15 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the room is able to notice the following:
>
> - A tome on the lectern in the western wing is splayed open, revealing diagrams of martial arts techniques on its pages.
> - A false book that conceals a vial of [[Rapture]] hidden inside.
>
> #### Eavesdropping
>
> If the characters decide to linger in an attempt to hear more, roll `[[/gmroll 1d4]]` on the following table once every 5 minutes:
>
> 1. This latest batch of books was from an estate sale in [[Scholar's Nook]], inherited from an old Nir'ae mage said to have explored the Pathways.
> 2. While there aren't many participants, an underground fight club has popped up in Ordain, and the Undaunted are regular participants of this illicit activity.
> 3. Zira won't let anyone into her [[Arcane Workshop]], and all sorts of rumors circulate about what she keeps in there.
> 4. Zira says the next Solar Games match is going to be one the people of Ordain will never forget. It sounds like she has a trick or two up her sleeve.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Raise the Alarm
>
> Upon detecting an intruder, 1 of the [[Undaunted Trainee]] will attempt to alert The Undaunted in other areas, specifically:
>
> - The Undaunted Trainee in the [[Hall of Champions]].
> - The Undaunted Adept in the [[Hall of Recovery]].
> - The Adept and Trainee sorting equipment in the [[Hall of Preparation]].
>
> #### Undaunted Trainee Tactics
>
> At the start of combat, 1 Undaunted Trainee will use their [[Reckless Movement]] feature and attempt to flee to raise the alarm, as described above. The other Undaunted Trainee will attempt to engage as many characters as possible to allow the first Undaunted Trainee to escape.
>
> Over the course of combat, the Undaunted Trainees will prioritize the following actions and abilities:
>
> - In melee, the Undaunted Trainees will take advantage of their [[Pack Tactics]] feature to maximize damage dealt by their [[Dagger]].
>
> The battle ends when the Undaunted Trainees are slain.
