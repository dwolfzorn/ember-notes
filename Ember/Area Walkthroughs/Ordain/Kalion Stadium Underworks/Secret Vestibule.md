# Secret Vestibule

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you emerge from the so-called Hall of Challenges, you find yourself in a shaded vestibule decorated with a lone bench made of cold stone. A passageway to the south winds westward, dark and uninviting.

This small chamber serves as the unassuming arrival area for characters who successfully traverse the Hall of Challenges.
