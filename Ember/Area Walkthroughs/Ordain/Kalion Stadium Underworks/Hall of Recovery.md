# Hall of Recovery

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The twin rectangular chambers here appear to be furnished for recuperation. To the south, a set of five beds is lit by calm lantern light. To the north, desks and cabinets line the dungeon walls and fountain edges, covered in a wide array of apothecary tools and arcane materials.
>
> An Undaunted Adept lurks here within this makeshift study, replenishing the contents of a healer's kit with a stock of herbs, ointments, and fresh bandages.

The two chambers collectively known as the Hall of Recovery include a barracks to the south and an apothecary's study to the north. While some of The Undaunted use the beds here as permanent lodging, most of them are not present at this time. A lone [[Undaunted Adept]] works here, accompanied by a furtive [[Water Sprite]] — hidden in a jar on a nearby cabinet.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Raise the Alarm
>
> Upon detecting an intruder, the [[Undaunted Adept]] will attempt to alert The Undaunted in other areas, specifically:
>
> - The Undaunted Trainee who cleans the monuments in the [[Hall of Champions]].
> - The 2 Undaunted Trainees in the [[Hall of Records]].
> - The Adept and Trainee in the [[Hall of Preparation]].
>
> #### Undaunted Adept Tactics
>
> At the start of combat, the [[Undaunted Adept]] will use a Bonus Action to shatter a nearby jar, freeing a [[Water Sprite]]. They will then target themselves with the [[Expeditious Retreat]] spell and attempt to escape and raise the alarm, as described above.
>
> Over the course of combat, the Undaunted Adept will prioritize the following actions and abilities:
>
> - In melee, the Undaunted Adept will take advantage of their [[Lethal]] and [[Pack Tactics]] features to maximize damage dealt by their [[Dagger]].
> - From range, the Undaunted Adept will cast [[Eldritch Blast]] to damage enemies, or [[Hold Person]] to hinder enemies.
>
> #### Water Sprite Tactics
>
> At the start of combat, the Water Sprite will use [[Unknown]] as a Bonus Action to engage a character capable of spellcasting.
>
> Over the course of combat, the Water Sprite will prioritize the following actions and abilities:
>
> - In melee, the Water Sprite will use its [[Frigid Swipe]] attack.
>
> The battle ends when the Undaunted Adept and the Water Sprite are slain.

When the Undaunted Adept smashes the jar to release the elemental, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The Undaunted warrior reaches up to a jar on the shelf, which appears to hold some clear liquid within. He drops the receptacle to the ground and shards of glass cascade across the Underworks floor along with the aqueous contents. However, the sheet of water on the stone floor coalesces before your very eyes into a single, living entity — a creature that aims to attack you, if its liquid "body language" is a trustworthy indicator.

> [!abstract] Water Sprite
> **[[Water Sprite]]**
>
> Level 1 · Frost Elemental Elemental Sprite
>
> ![Water Sprite](../../../assets/image055.webp)
>
> This small, energetic elemental is a cohesive globule form with small orbiting teardrops of water that it seems to control. It is surprisingly agile, moving in quick bursts and leaps that are graceful yet unpredictable. They shimmer with an inner luminescence that gives them an almost ethereal appearance, and they are constantly in motion.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Apothecary's Study
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - The fresh [[Healer's Kit]] the Adept was in the process of assembling.
> - A vial of [[Antitoxin]].
> - A [[Potion of Healing]].
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 15 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the room is able to notice the following:
>
> - A vial of [[Serpent Venom]].
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 12)** check while searching the room is able to identify several material components for arcane spells stored in the containers here, and can loot `[[/roll 1d2]]` samples of any mundane material spell component for a spell on their spell list worth 15 gp or less.
>
> If the Undaunted Adept failed to break the jar containing the [[Water Sprite]] during combat, it will be located here now. Fully intact, it will react to the party's presence if the characters linger here longer than 5 minutes, but cannot escape the jar without external assistance.
>
> #### Exploring the Barracks
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - 3 [[Backpacks]] stuffed with [[Ordani Traveler's Clothes]].
> - `[[/roll 1d2]]` doses of [[Dustweed]].
> - `[[/roll 1d2]]` [[Arcturian Trinkets]].
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 15 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the room is able to notice the following:
>
> - A bottle of [[Seawall Brandy]].
