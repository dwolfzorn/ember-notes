# Anterior Chamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The alcove channels you into a small square chamber decorated with little more than a burnished steel turnstile at its center. Peering out through darkened archways to the north and south, you can see that this room seems to be situated on a railway of sorts, as evidenced by the pronounced grooves on the floor below.
>
> To the west, a similar room adjoins this one, illuminated by a solitary brazier. Farther still, you spy the silhouette of a large ballista in a third chamber alight with arcane flame.
>
> Agraband surveys the scene and scratches his chin in contemplation.
>
> > If I didn't know any better, I'd say we must navigate this mechanized maze of malevolent chambers to proceed …

After several moments of adrenaline-fueled decisions and encounters with esoteric hazards, you find yourself face-to-face with an alcove on the opposite side of this inscrutable mechanical sprawl, roughly a dozen chambers and one hundred and thirty feet (as the crow flies) from where you began.
