# Acolyte Quarters

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This sparsely decorated room shows signs of recent habitation: the bed is unkempt, and the crumbs from a recent meal linger on the bedside table.

This small room serves as the private quarters for one of Zira's Undaunted Adepts. Very few possessions can be found here, as the Undaunted maintain lives of relative freedom from the distraction of personal belongings.
