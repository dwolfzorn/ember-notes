# Arcane Workshop

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This darkened area reeks of stale ash and chemical fumes, no doubt emanating from the cauldron in the middle of the chamber, which sits idly above a cold hearth. A small table next to this fireplace is decorated with an array of alchemical apparatuses, while shelves stocked with all manner of strange reagents and spell components line the entire space.

This room is Zira Hestidero's arcane workshop, where she prepares the ritual incense and other esoteric ingredients necessary for the cult's various activities in areas like the [[Chamber of Bargains]], her private [[Ritual Chamber]], the [[Temple Sanctum]], and other locations outside the Underworks. Likewise, this place functions as an ersatz kitchen of sorts, and some of what appear to be alchemical supplies are actually jarred foodstuffs.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Workshop
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - 32 pints (4 gallons) of [[Water]].
> - `[[/roll 1d2]]` vials of [[Acid]].
> - `[[/roll 3d4]]` jars of preserved fruits (2 lbs. of [[Rations]] each).
> - `[[/roll 1d2]]` [[Tinderboxes]].
> - [[Alchemist's Supplies]].
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 15 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the room is able to notice the following:
>
> - [[Alchemist's Fire]].
> - [[Component Pouch]].
> - [[Improved Alchemist's Supplies]].
>
> - **Critical Success**: [[Potion of Necrotic Resistance]].
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 12)** check while searching the room is able to identify several arcane spell components stored in the containers here, and can loot `[[/roll 1d3+1]]` samples of any mundane material spell component for a spell on their spell list worth 15 gp or less.
