# Chamber of Bargains

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This atmospheric chamber reeks of the sickly sweet smell of some potent incense, and large runic markings on the floor pulse with intermittent arcane light. In an alcove to the north, the fragments of a shattered statue lie in ruin; to the south, a small collection of occult tomes and a ritual dagger sit atop a bleak stone altar littered with half-burned candles.

This curious room is a special shrine to Ku'arta, where would-be adherents can perform a blasphemous Ritual of Bargains to receive a divine reward: a double-edged blessing that bestows a magical impediment upon the willing recipient as well as an arcane benefit. Furthermore, Ku'arta speaks to those who would beseech his bargain, and this room serves as an arcane conduit for this mystical palaver.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Chamber
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - An altar on the southern wall, upon which rests a copy of the Manual of Pain — one of the most notable religious texts regarding the shard god [[Ku'arta]].
> - An obsidian [[Dagger]].
> - `[[/roll 1d2+1]]` unused [[Candles]].
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 15 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the room is able to notice the following:
>
> - To do.
> - **Result of 19+**: To do.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 15)** or **Arcana (DC 18)** check can use the obsidian dagger and other accoutrements here to initiate the Ritual of Bargains, which bestows a temporary curse on the character known as the Curse of Bargains.
>
> - **Study Manual of Pain:** The character automatically succeeds on this check if they study the Manual of Pain for at least 30 minutes.
> - [[Commune]]: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Gods**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Attunement: Luxarum**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Characters who successfully initiate the Ritual of Bargains also have an opportunity to commune with Ku'arta himself (see below).

If a character manages to successfully initiate the Ritual of Bargains, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The runes on the floor seethe with uncanny brilliance as the air swirls about you. A raspy masculine voice echoes through the chamber, emanating from nowhere, metallic and clanging with guttural malice.
>
> > Avow to me, willful seeker: who art thou to bargain with the Tormentor, and what is thine ambition?

> [!info] Social
> #### Ku'arta's Bargain
>
> The character must state their ambition for the ritual to continue. Once they do, Ku'arta responds from his hidden sanctum on the other side of the world with one simple phrase:
>
> > Carry this blessing, and don your suffering as unholy armor, painful and resolute.
>
> The character immediately becomes afflicted with the Curse of Bargains, which has a duration of 24 hours.
>
> If a character responds to Ku'arta's query with an honest request to strike an arcane pact with him, the Shard God will most likely oblige them. See [[Chamber of Bargains]] below for more information about what this eventually entails. For now, the Shard God will answer three questions for them, per the [[Commune]] spell.
>
> Meanwhile, a successful **Deception (DC 13)** check offers a character all the assurances they need to believe this paranormal encounter was an authentic interaction with the so-called God of Torment himself.
>
> - **Attunement: Luxarum**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.

Once a character has completed the Ritual of Bargains, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You feel a strange sensation take hold of your body, like a flash of cold sweat and mortal panic. But somewhere behind this animal fear, you sense a distant presence, guiding your will with the ferocity of a thousand forgotten vengeances.
>
> The Curse of Bargains is upon you.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### A Pact with Ku'arta
>
> This encounter provides characters who wish to take Ku'arta as their arcane patron with an optimal opportunity to do so. However, the ultimate fulfillment of this bargain requires the character to invest at least one level in the [[Character Classes]] class when they next advance in Level.
>
> Characters who make a Warlock's arcane pact with Ku'arta this way receive the [[Thaumaturgy]] cantrip as a bonus to their list of known spells.
