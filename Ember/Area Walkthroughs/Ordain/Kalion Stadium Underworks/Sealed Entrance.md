# Sealed Entrance

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Long ago, this dead end was the site of a massive entrance to the temple from another section of the city Underworks. Now, all that remains of the once-proud threshold is a pile of stone rubble.

This alcove was once the main entrance to the temple, but was sealed centuries ago by the authorities of Ordain in an effort to quarantine this unholy location from the rest of the city and prevent the old acolytes of Ku'arta from rising to prominence.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Sealed Entrance
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the area determines that this was once the main entrance to the temple, but it must have been sealed long ago by authorities of Ordain — the collapse is manmade rather than the result of an earthquake or natural disaster.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The character is able to pinpoint the age of this rubble (and the fortified stonework around it) as being from the earliest days of Ordain.
>
> Alternatively, a character who makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 15)** check can determine that the collapse here was not due to natural causes.
