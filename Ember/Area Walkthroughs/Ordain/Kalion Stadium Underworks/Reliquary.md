# Reliquary

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The sconces that illuminate this narrow chamber appear to gleam with perpetual arcane firelight, casting their warm glow on an array of glass display cases and other containers situated about the room.

This chamber serves as the temple's Reliquary and contains a small collection of relics and antiques that are considered sacred to adherents of [[Ku'arta]].

One noteworthy relic of the original inhabitants remains: a magical crystalline glaive, which [[Zira Hestidero]] and her cohorts regard as something of a holy item, given its history with previous acolytes of Ku'arta — including [[Regus Halamattrix]], the first champion of Ordain's Solar Games.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Reliquary
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - Three urns on a stone table at the northern end of the room, which contain the ashes of [[Regus Halamattrix]] and two other champions of Ku'arta.
> - Six broken relics, which can be harvested for their rare crafting components.
> - A chest at the center of the room, marked with lambent arcane runes.
>
> Any character who examines the runes and makes a successful **Arcana (DC 18)** check identifies them as a [[Glyph of Warding]].
>
> - The chest itself is unlocked but trapped (see [[Reliquary]] below), and contains the [[Pellucid Glaive]] — a relic that Zira and her allies discovered here when they moved in.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Magical Chest Trap
>
> The chest containing the Pellucid Glaive at the center of the room is unlocked, but trapped with a [[Glyph of Warding]] spell. To avoid triggering the trap, a character must speak the password to disarm it:
>
> > Tormentor, guide my hands.
>
> The passphrase renders the glyph inert for 10 minutes. If a character triggers the trap, they become the target of a [[Bestow Curse]] spell (using the **Curse Actions** activity for a duration of 24 hours).
>
> The password can be discovered via two primary methods: searching the shelves of the [[Ritual Chamber]], or successfully interrogating an [[Undaunted Adept]] or [[Zira Hestidero]] herself.
