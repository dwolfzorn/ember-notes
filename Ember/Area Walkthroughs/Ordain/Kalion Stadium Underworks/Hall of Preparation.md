# Hall of Preparation

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Two Undaunted warriors occupy this chamber, which appears to be stuffed full of assorted weapons, armor, and adventuring gear. Based on this motley collection of equipment, you'd wager these goods were gathered from a variety of different sources.
>
> The two athletes appear to be caught up in their task of sorting and cataloguing the assorted gear, and exchange a few words between each other without bothering to look up.
>
> > If you ask me, we're wastin' our time with this scuffed-up junk. Who needs to know how to use a blasted polearm, anyway? These two fists of mine have been good enough so far.
>
> The older warrior chimes in with a quick rebuttal:
>
> > Sure they have — good enough to get you stuck on inventory duty here with me. If your strikes were half as fast as that mouth of yours, you'd be wearing the captain's uniform by now.
>
> They continue to go about their business, and the steady noise of clanging metal and padded leather keeps pace.

The Hall of Preparation was historically a chamber for the arming and outfitting of gladiators before combat in the arena above (or amidst other parts of the Underworks at large). These days, it serves as a staging area for loot, plunder, and additional rewards The Undaunted bring home from their various triumphs in the city (illicit and otherwise).

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Advantageous Stealth Conditions
>
> Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to sneak here, in the adjoining [[Entrance Hall]], and in the adjoining [[Secret Passage]] gain **+2 Boons**, thanks to the loud noise from the sorting work. This effect ends if the Undaunted here are slain.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Hall of Preparation
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - Much of the equipment here seems well used and lightly damaged by battle, as if these items were culled from various encounters with an assortment of armed warriors.
> - The two Undaunted acolytes here are sorting the items and recording associated details in a couple of small notebooks.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 20 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 18)** check while searching the room is able to notice the following:
>
> - A secret door on the western wall. Crafted by expert Ordani stonesmiths using arcane techniques, this door can only be opened using a [[Temple of Ku'arta Key]] or magic like the [[Knock]] spell (which will alert all Undaunted enemies in the Underworks).
>
> #### Eavesdropping
>
> If the characters decide to linger in an attempt to hear more, roll `[[/gmroll 1d4]]` on the following table once every 5 minutes:
>
> 1. Half of the goods here were the property of Undaunted hopefuls before they joined the cult, while the other half have been culled from private matches with enemy teams outside of the arena.
> 2. While there aren't many participants, an underground fight club has popped up in Ordain, and The Undaunted are regular participants of this illicit activity.
> 3. The quality of equipment here pales in comparison to some of the goods stored in the Underworks [[Reliquary]].
> 4. Zira says the next Solar Games match is going to be one the people of Ordain will never forget. It sounds like she has a trick or two up her sleeve.
>
> #### Looting the Room
>
> If the characters want to sort through the equipment here, they can find at least one of every Simple and Martial Melee Weapon listed on the [[Weapon Table]], along with most Simple Ranged Weapons.
>
> Additionally, there are several pieces of Light and Medium Armor here, with no single item exceeding a value of 200 gp, as depicted on the [[Unknown]].

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Raise the Alarm
>
> Upon detecting an intruder, the [[Undaunted Trainee]] will attempt to alert The Undaunted in other areas, specifically:
>
> - The Undaunted Trainee who cleans the monuments in the [[Hall of Champions]].
> - The 2 Undaunted Trainees in the [[Hall of Records]].
> - The Undaunted Adept in the [[Hall of Recovery]].
>
> #### Undaunted Adept Tactics
>
> At the start of combat, the [[Undaunted Adept]] will target the Undaunted Trainee with the [[Expeditious Retreat]] spell to aid them in escaping to raise the alarm.
>
> Over the course of combat, the Undaunted Adept will prioritize the following actions and abilities:
>
> - In melee, the Undaunted Adept will take advantage of their [[Lethal]] and [[Pack Tactics]] features to maximize damage dealt by their [[Dagger]].
> - From range, the Undaunted Adept will cast [[Eldritch Blast]] to damage enemies, or [[Hold Person]] to hinder enemies.
>
> #### Undaunted Trainee Tactics
>
> At the start of combat, the Undaunted Trainee will use their [[Reckless Movement]] feature and attempt to flee to raise the alarm, as described above.
>
> Over the course of combat, the Undaunted Trainee will prioritize the following actions and abilities:
>
> - In melee, the Undaunted Trainee will take advantage of their [[Pack Tactics]] feature to maximize damage dealt by their [[Dagger]].
>
> The battle ends when the Undaunted are slain.
