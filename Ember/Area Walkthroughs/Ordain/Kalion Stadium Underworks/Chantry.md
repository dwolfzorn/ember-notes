# Chantry

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Five wooden pews face a short stone dais at the northern end of this modest chamber, where a podium overlooks the temple's dust-strewn chantry. Scattered arrangements of half-used candles decorate the dais and the bookshelf on the southern end of the room, where puddles of wax keep company with moldering texts.

This dusty chamber serves as the temple's chantry, where recitations, prayer sessions, sermons, and ceremonies are traditionally carried out with less pomp and circumstance than the majestic [[Temple Sanctum]] nearby.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Chantry
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - The bookshelf to the south is stocked with a variety of workbooks filled with handwritten accounts of the lectures here.
> - A solitary tome sits atop the podium on the dais, which appears to be a chronicle of Ku'artan champions called "Fists of the Tormentor."
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 15 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the room is able to notice the following:
>
> - The sheer amount of dust on the various surfaces and accoutrements here suggests that the chantry goes largely unused compared to other chambers in the temple complex.
> - The character spots a particularly curious tome on the bookshelf titled "The Triumphs of Regus Halamattrix." Any character who spends at least 15 minutes studying this tome gains **+2 Boons** on Charisma checks made to influence [[Regus Halamattrix]] in the [[Hall of Champions]] and elsewhere.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 12)** check ascertains the purpose of this chamber, along with its role in the temple's ongoing practices: while the Temple Sanctum is reserved for important ceremonies, the Chantry here is a place of learning, proselytizing, and demonstration.
>
> - **Knowledge: Gods**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
