# Ritual Chamber

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The lingering smell of incense and grave nitre assaults your senses as you step into this macabre chamber, which appears to be decorated with a variety of occult trophies and arcane accoutrements.

This room serves as a chamber for Zira's clandestine arcane rituals, as well as her private quarters when she so desires. A collection of bleached skulls, scented ritual candles, bloodied trinkets, and profane texts is scattered about this unholy place.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Opening the Locked Doors
>
> The doors to this chamber are locked, and can be opened with either a successful `[[/skill sleightofhand 18 tool=thief]]` or a [[Temple of Ku'arta Key]]. The doors can be broken open with a successful **Athletics (DC 22)** check.
>
> Alternatively, the doors can be opened with magic like the [[Knock]] spell, which will alert all Undaunted enemies in the Underworks.
>
> #### Exploring the Ritual Chamber
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - A collection of humanoid skulls, which appear aged and decrepit.
> - A strange array of components and esoteric materials is gathered here.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 15 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the room is able to notice the following:
>
> - Zira's Diary. It reveals her newfound allegiance to an entity she refers to as "The Red Ruin," which promises to offer her otherworldly power in return for her betrayal of Ku'arta.
> - A [[Spell Scroll]] of [[Speak with Dead]] is tucked into the rear fold of the diary.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The character also discovers the password to disarm the [[Glyph of Warding]] on the chest in the [[Reliquary]], which is written alongside a sketch and brief description of the [[Pellucid Glaive]]:
>   > To access the champion's relic, you must speak the mantra "Tormentor, guide my hands," lest a curse befall you.
>
> Any character who examines the room and makes a successful **Arcana (DC 15)** check recognizes its purpose as a ritual chamber, and allows the character to retry the Wisdom (Perception) check detailed above.
>
> - The character also recognizes that the material spell components here include incense traditionally associated with the [[Commune]] spell.
>
> - **Knowledge: Rituals**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
