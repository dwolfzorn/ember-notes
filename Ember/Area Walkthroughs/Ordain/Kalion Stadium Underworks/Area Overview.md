# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The [[Kalion Stadium Underworks]] Area Map depicts the entirety of the stadium's subterranean sprawl. It is featured in the [[Running the Gauntlet]] Event of the [[Unfinished Business]] Side Quest.
>
> - This map depicts a single Level, and its areas are organized into three sections: the Hall of Contest, the Hall of Challenges, and the Temple of Ku'arta.
> - The party arrives in the [[Entrance Hall]], located at the northeastern corner of the map.

#### Early Access Under Construction

The Kalion Stadium Underworks Area Map is a work in progress, and some chambers and features have yet to be fully implemented (most notably the central "Hall of Challenges" section).

## Gameplay Details

The areas of the Kalion Stadium Underworks have the following features unless specified otherwise in the text.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Avoiding Detection
>
> If the party wishes to navigate the Kalion Stadium Underworks without being detected, they'll need to move stealthily from chamber to chamber.
>
> Whenever the characters enter a room that is adjacent to a room containing one or more enemies, the characters must make a **Stealth (DC 15)** group check. If at least half of the characters succeed, they avoid detection. If fewer than half of the characters succeed, they are detected by the enemies in the adjacent room(s), who respond to the disturbance after `[[/roll 1d4]]` rounds.

### Levels & Elevation

The Kalion Stadium Underworks Scene features a single Level, and its areas are organized into three sections: the Hall of Contest, the Hall of Challenges, and the Temple of Ku'arta.

- **All Sections:** All sections of the Kalion Stadium Underworks are set to an elevation of 0 and have 20-foot-high ceilings.

### Illumination

Braziers and sconces located throughout the Kalion Stadium Underworks interior burn with magical firelight, and are tended to by the various Undaunted acolytes when necessary. The braziers shed bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for another 30 feet, while the smaller sconces shed bright light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for another 15 feet.

### Terrain

The flagstone floors of the Stadium Underworks are crafted from slabs of gray slate, hewn from the ancient quarries of old Ordain nearby. The ground here should be treated as normal terrain. The interior walls of the area are crafted from similar stone.
