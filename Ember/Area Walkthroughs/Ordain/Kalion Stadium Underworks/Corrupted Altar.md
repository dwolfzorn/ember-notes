# Corrupted Altar

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The surface of this wide stone altar appears to have been discolored by some foul, purple-black ichor. Tenebrous rivulets of murky stain run down the sides of the blemished rock, and you swear you can almost hear the rasp of an otherworldly whisper as you stare at the stygian grime.

The stone altar has been corrupted by Zira Hestidero's attempt to commune with abyssal forces beyond the veil of reality, and bears signs of her loathsome experimentation.
