# Gazetteer Reference

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you stroll down the bustling central street, vendors hawk their wares from all sides, enticing you with the scent of delicious local street food. The aroma of spices, grilled meats, and sweet treats wafts through the air, and small groups of people stand and eat while chatting enthusiastically with one another. In the distance, you can see the backdrop of a huge circular structure, covered in flags and banners.

The district known as Arena Ridge emerged as a bustling hub of activity after the construction of the Grand Kalion Stadium of Ordain, a massive stadium that serves as the heart of sports and entertainment in the city. Once an open field used for local competitions, the area has now transformed into a vibrant community that thrives on the excitement of events held within the stadium, while also offering a wide array of local food, shops and entertainment options along its bustling streets.

- **District Type:** Entertainment, Retail, and Residential
- **Population:** 13,460 (Moderately dense population of folks who love the central walkable location)
- **Lifestyle:** High Energy (Fans of sporting teams, shoppers, and entertainment-focused locals)
- **Services:** Sporting (Arena) and Retail (Mix of Food, Apparel, and Lodging)

Arena Ridge is dominated by the Grand Kalion Stadium, with many residents working within the bustling and lively entertainment industry that surrounds the various games, shows, and sports that are hosted there. There is also a huge demand for food, lodging, and space from visitors traveling from other districts for special events or games. Additionally, a significant number of residents are food stall owners, shopkeepers, or traders who serve the steady flow of Ordain's population.

The most popular game in Kalion Stadium is a more modern version of an ancient handball once played in the Kelmezian city-states known in Ordain as Thronefire. Nobles and royalty originally played it before spreading to the masses, who transformed it into a more contact-driven physical sport that requires several athletic qualities. The game is played with a ball enchanted with various fiery and energy-based elements, and the goal is to score by throwing the ball while standing still into an opening in a tall tower called the Throne. Of course, other players will attempt to push, grab, shove, or run into opponents at full speed, creating some dramatic moments that are talked about for weeks.

Many teams within Ordain compete each year in the sports season called the Crown's Cup, and the winning team receives a golden crown trophy that they keep until the next season, when it is passed to the new winners. The Crown's Cup only lasts for roughly four months during the [[Steading]] and the [[Gleaning]] seasons, but throughout the rest of the year, many other events are held in the stadium, including Relayrun, Champions Duel, and smaller-scale sporting tournaments.

### Culture

Arena Ridge is often lively with an electric atmosphere, especially during game days or major events, when the streets fill with fans wearing their teams' colors and chanting slogans. Street performers, artists, and vendors line the roads, creating a vibrant market where local food and crafts are celebrated. Sports play a central role in the community, and friendly rivalries are common, fostering camaraderie among residents. When events are not scheduled in the Stadium, the district becomes dramatically less busy, and residents tend to spend more time relaxing and slowly preparing their local businesses for the next surge of traffic.

### Landmarks

**Grand Kalion Stadium**

The Grand Kalion Stadium, named after the legendary Crowns Cup eight-time champion Arcus Kalion, stands as the pride of the city of Ordain. It is an architectural marvel designed to host a variety of events and entertainment spectacles. With a seating capacity that rivals the largest arenas in the known world, it features state-of-the-art magic and technology, including a dynamic scoreboard and sound systems that enhance the viewing experience. The stadium features a versatile field that can accommodate a range of sports and events, and the floor of the stadium can be easily converted within a few hours.

**Champions Square**

Located right outside the Grand Kalion Stadium, Champions Square is a bustling hub of celebration and camaraderie. This vibrant plaza is adorned with sculptures, flags, and banners representing various local teams. It is a favored gathering spot for fans to share their excitement before and after events.

**Lost Wod**

Lost Wod is a hidden tavern tucked away in the heart of Arena Ridge, often shrouded in an air of mystery. It is a place that is almost exclusively reserved for Oaken who travel to Ordain with the express purpose of participating in the games and events held in the stadium nearby. It has an infamous reputation for being one of the most violent places in the city, with fights, tests of strength, and bruised egos causing fights and clashes on a near-constant basis. Luckily for the Ordani residents, the tavern is maintained by an Oaken elder called Wod, who erected wards, charms, and magic around the area to both hide the location from prying eyes and prevent noise or disruptions from escaping out into the city.

**Saltrack Gymnasium**

Saltrack Gymnasium is an elite training facility at the forefront of physical fitness and athletic development in Ordain, primarily funded and maintained by House Salva. Equipped with weight training areas, spacious courts for various sports, and training facilities, the gymnasium attracts both aspiring athletes and fitness enthusiasts who can afford the high prices alike.

### Locals

**Donavan "Doc" Reed (Lawful Good, Ordani Human, he/him)**

Donovan, affectionately known as "Doc," is the charismatic commentator for many of the major events held at the Grand Kalion Stadium, including the Crown's Cup, which he has hosted for nearly fifteen years. With a booming voice and an infectious enthusiasm, he brings an electrifying energy to every event he covers. A former athlete himself, Donovan possesses an intricate understanding of Thronefire, allowing him to provide insightful commentary and engaging anecdotes that captivate both live audiences and those watching from home. His trademark catchphrases and playful banter have made him a local celebrity, adored by fans and participants alike.

**Rowan "Greenball" (Chaotic Good, Ordani Thornling, they/them)**

Greenball is the champion of the Crown's Cup four years in a row and is an endlessly energetic character who delights in teasing the crowd with last-minute plays during games of Thronefire. Known for her agility and endurance, she excels at anchoring herself firmly and making excellent shots on the towertop as well.

**Wod (Lawful Neutral, Oaken Vrjnhar, he/him)**

Wod is a revered Oaken elder almost seven hundred years old, who commands respect and admiration throughout the city, especially among visiting Oakens. He resembles a gnarled tree, with a trunk-like body, and his fur looks patterned like hardwood or tree bark. He is an extremely friendly and easygoing Oaken who runs the tavern known as Lost Wod, which is well known among all Oakens who visit the city.

### Lore

Initially, the area was a large open field slightly raised above the floodplain of the nearby river, used to host local competitions and informal games of Thronefire in the early days of the city. As the years went by, the city's population grew, attracting a diverse mix of artisans, traders, and fans eager to watch the games. With this growth, the need for a bigger venue to handle the increasing size and frequency of events became clear. This led to the ambitious plans for the Grand Kalion Stadium. The massive stadium was then built in  AS2197, becoming the centerpiece of entertainment in Ordain. Its opening marked a new chapter for Arena Ridge, turning it into a bustling hub.
