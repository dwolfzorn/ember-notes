# Secret Passage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A dark and narrow corridor extends into the darkness ahead, as still and silent as the grave.

This secret passage connects the temple [[Armory]] with the [[Hall of Preparation]] to the east, and is frequently used by Zira and her most experienced Undaunted allies to traverse the Stadium Underworks while conveniently avoiding the Hall of Challenges.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Advantageous Stealth Conditions
>
> Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to sneak here gain **+2 Boons**, thanks to the loud noise from the sorting work in the [[Hall of Preparation]]. This effect ends if the Undaunted there are slain.
