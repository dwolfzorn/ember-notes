# Armory

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This darkened chamber is furnished with an archaic forge, its embers cold and latent for time uncounted. Piles of damaged arms and armor are stacked around the room in various states of disarray, with little rhyme or reason to the accumulated mess. A layer of dust has settled on this forgotten smithy, which is little more than an antechamber now.

This chamber is the ancient forge of the once-proud Temple of Ku'arta, but it has grown largely dormant in the days since Zira and The Undaunted have lived here.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Armory
>
> A simple search reveals the following:
>
> - 12 broken [[Shields]].
> - 6 broken suits of [[Scale Mail]].
> - 8 broken suits of [[Leather Armor]].
> - 1 unbroken [[Breastplate]].
> - 1 unbroken suit of [[Studded Leather Armor]].
> - 1 [[Shortsword]].
>
> The broken shields and suits of armor provide no bonuses to AC but can be sold as scrap for 10% of their listed price (rounded down).
>
> #### Detecting the Secret Passage
>
> Any character who searches the room and makes a successful **Awareness (DC 18)** check spots a trail of footprints leading to the secret door on the eastern wall.
>
> Crafted by expert Ordani stonesmiths, this door can only be opened using a [[Temple of Ku'arta Key]] or magic like the [[Knock]] spell (which will alert all Undaunted enemies in the Underworks).
