# Entrance Hall

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This octagonal chamber holds the first light you've seen for nearly a mile while navigating the subterranean reaches beneath Grand Kalion Stadium. Despite the archaic architecture and clandestine qualities of this shadow-haunted place, you get the distinct feeling that someone has recently been here.

This large antechamber serves as the Entrance Hall for the Kalion Stadium Underworks, and is typically trafficked only by The Undaunted who call this place home. In times long gone, there might have been more inhabitants here.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Advantageous Stealth Conditions
>
> Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to sneak here gain **+2 Boons**, thanks to the loud noise from the sorting work in the [[Hall of Preparation]]. This effect ends if the Undaunted there are slain.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Entrance Hall
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 15)** or **Wilderness (DC 13)** check notices the presence of sandaled footprints coming to and from the area, with the steadiest flow of traffic leading toward the [[Bathhouse]] to the east.
