# Lavatories

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This small junction chamber holds two plain doors, each marked with signs denoting one for visitors and one for inmates.
>
> The visitors’ washroom is lined with wooden toilet stalls, their partitions offering partial cover for occupants, while the inmates’ facility features rows of bare stone toilets cut into the rock and simple bathing materials stacked close at hand.

The space leading to the washroom always has at least one guard in it if there are visitors and inmates on this level, and the rooms are checked often if someone is inside.
