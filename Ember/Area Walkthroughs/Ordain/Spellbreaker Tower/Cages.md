# Cages

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> On this raised brick platform are iron cages sized to hold a person, but not much else. Their thick bars are set close together, but their doors hang slightly open on crooked hinges. Rust grows where rain and moisture have been allowed to gather, causing streaks of orange to paint the sides of the cages.
>
> This collection sits fully exposed to cutting wind and open weather, offering no shelter, no warmth, and nowhere to lie down, an arrangement that would make confinement here a long, raw misery.

Each show signs that they once held occupants, though it doesn't seem like they have been used in a long time. The cages are made of light iron bars with swinging doors, all of them are unlocked.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### False Prisoners
>
> If during the [[Diplomatic Impunity]] event [[Breaking the Tower]] the party manages to reach the roof of the tower [[Breaking the Tower]] then theses cages will each contain a [[Veiled Chain Agent]] posing as cage occupants and ready to jump out and join the ensuing fight with [[Loris Tezran]] when he arrives.
