# Stairwell Checkpoint 5

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Two wide stairwells meet here, one rising up to the landing, and the other descending. Each ends at a wide landing sealed off by heavy iron gates and marked with red lines. Barred tight with the heaviest iron locks, armored guards stand by them, ready for trouble.
>
> Beyond the gates, the flooring changes to well-worn hardwood planks and the space it cluttered with stacks of crates, barrels, and chests. A few long wooden benches provide seating, though it doesn't seem like many linger here.

This space is occupied by riot guards at all hours. It uses an intercom to communicate with central security who work the [[Gameplay Details]] remotely. The [[Gameplay Details]] and walls are heavy here, providing maximum security.
