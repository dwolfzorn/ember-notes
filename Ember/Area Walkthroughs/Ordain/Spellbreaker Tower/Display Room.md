# Display Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This room features a collection of artifacts including historic armors, weapons, books, along with alchemical and medical curios. Most of the items appear old, some of them not fit for use, but all are lovingly displayed, with many behind glass.

The vast majority of items in this space are of historical or academic value and aren't useful in the hands of the party. However, a particularly larcenous party might want to help themselves to the items anyway. The cases are locked (DC 15 to open) and sturdy enough (AC 15, 15 HP).

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Museum Pieces
>
> The arms and armor displayed here are old, and in many cases have been maintained for display, not use. The armor's leather straps and fittings are old and weak, unfit for the rigors of combat, while the blades are dull, and more useful as bludgeons.
>
> Characters with **Culture: Tayan** or **Path: Imperial Conscript** recognize some of the rifles as being early models of Tayan firearms. They are unlikely to be functional, and even if they were, there isn't any ammunition present.
>
> Of the medical curios present, many are bottles with contents long since removed, consumed, or dried up, leaving them as nothing more than display pieces.
