# Warden's Office

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This large room is lined with four huge bookcases laden with tomes. Each one climbs to the ceiling ands towers over the rest of the room which contains a half-circle desk of ornate wood and a small seating area with chairs and a sofa. The furnishings here are simple, but of high quality.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Hidden Latches
>
> Characters that search the room and make a successful **Awareness (DC 18)** check note that there is a hidden lever here that opens the treasure room to the south.

### The Prison Warden

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Warden Availability
>
> During daytime hours the Warden of the prison can be found here, handling paperwork, and administering the prison. During evening hours there is a chance he is in the [[Warden's Quarters]], but it is also possible that he's left the prison for the night. His presence is up to your discretion.

**Warden Cyran Holst (Lawful Neutral, Ordani Human, he/him)**

A gaunt, quiet leader, Holst has spent nearly three decades balancing the expectations of the Ordinate, the Cascillian Republics' demands, and the needs of the Tower's inmates. His commitment to routine and fairness underlies every decision, rendering him respected by Ordinate, Cindaric, Cascillians and Veiled Chain alike. There is no aspect of operation within the tower prison levels that he doesn't know about.

Cyran Holst has been married for over twenty years to his wife Surna. He has two grown sons, one of which works in the Hallows, and the other in House Lilifeld.

He is well respected by his guard team and does his best to make sure they are well taken care of and safe, alongside the people the prison inters.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Guarded Warden
>
> Cyran Holst should use the [[Spellbreaker Guard]] actor, and has the same equipment and training as one. If attacked, he will defend himself while trying to raise the alarm with the rest of his guard team.

It is possible to interact with the Warden when he is present in his office, and inmates in the prison may even request meetings, which have a good chance of being granted.

> [!info] Social
> #### Meeting The Warden
>
> A meeting with the Warden of the Spellbreaker is not especially difficult to arrange for inmates within the prison. To do so, one simply needs to put in a request with any guard, and assuming that the guard forwards the request, a meeting may be granted in the next few days.
>
> Visits are always supervised by a small team of guards, and for inmates in yellow or red block, the warden is more likely to come visit their cell directly to talk.
>
> #### Not Unreasonable
>
> The Warden is willing to consider requests that improve the quality of life and comfort of inmates without sacrificing the security of the prison. They are also willing to listen to complaints and will do their best to solve them, if possible.
>
> However, Warden Holst doesn't respond well to being threatened and doesn't accept bribes. He is a lawful individual who has high ethical standards when it comes to operating the prison.
