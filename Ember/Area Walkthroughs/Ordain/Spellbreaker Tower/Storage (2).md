# Storage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This storage room is crowded with stacked crates, rough boxes, and tied burlap bags stack atop each other. Simple shelves are bowed slightly under the weight of general goods of the same type. The air is dry and dusty, carrying the mixed scents of wood, hemp, and old canvas.

This space mostly holds general goods used by the prison level, such as spare barrels of water, wine, cooking oil, lantern oil and similar. At your discretion there may be other useful items here beyond what's listed.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Useful Items
>
> Searching the boxes here yields basic hand tools such as a hammer, saw, chisel, an axe, and a pick. There are also other useful items here like gloves, lanterns and and three 50' coils of rope.

The back wall is also damaged, which keen eyed characters might notice.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Curious Damage
>
> Characters with a **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** or making a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check notice a boarded up corner where the stone appears to have been damaged.
>
> Prying the boards off requires tools (such as a prybar) or a successful `[[/check str 16]]` check, and both actions are noisy enough to attract attention from the adjacent room.
>
> Success grants access to the [[Wall Tunnel]] beyond it.
