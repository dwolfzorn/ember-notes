# Library

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This quiet library and lounge is a split level space, with the upper area featuring a sitting area with plush chairs and hardwood floors. The lower level features a bubbling fountain surrounded by benches and the whole room is lined with shelves that are packed with volumes of every thickness.

Off duty guards can relax and read here, and often has one to two guards reading and chatting quietly.
