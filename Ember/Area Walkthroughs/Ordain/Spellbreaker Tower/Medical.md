# Medical

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This medical room is a clean, spare chamber of dark stone with a few beds set out in the open, each fitted with thin mattresses, light blankets, and anchor points for shackles. The air here stings with the scent of medical salves, herbs, and spirits.
>
> A simple desk stands nearby for an attendant, its surface kept for notes and cabinets holding basic supplies, and beside the desk two doors connect to a small treatment room with a bed and storage, and a cramped break room for the staff.

Attended all-hours by at least one [[Cindaric Adherent]] trained in medicine and a guard. Any healing provided here is mundane due to lack of magic, but medical supplies are ample.

The cabinets here hold healing supplies and tools typically found in a healer's kit, allowing for a trained character to treat wounds on themselves or another.

The amount of supplies here are sufficient for several rounds of treatment if necessary, and the treatment room is even outfitted for simple surgery if the situation calls for it.
