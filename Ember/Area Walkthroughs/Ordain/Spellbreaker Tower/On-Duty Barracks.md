# On-Duty Barracks

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> These barracks are a cluster of connected common rooms featuring rows of wood-framed beds and accompanied by chests packed with personal belongings and clothes. The air carries the mixed smells of oiled leather, sweat, and lantern smoke.

Guards on duty are housed here. This space is more occupied during the evening hours than during daytime, but always has a few guards in it, often sleeping.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Personal Effects
>
> Searching through the chests here will yield several outfits of common clothes, and more crucially, clean guard uniforms including belts, boots, and padded coats.
>
> There are enough uniforms for two people, though a successful **Awareness (DC 15)** check will yield two more. Wearing these uniforms allows one to pass for an off-duty guard on this level without drawing attention as the wearer is not acting suspiciously.
>
> - **Result of 20+** You also find a set of guard keys that will open any wooden doors encountered on any level, and any cells on Level 2 or Level 3.
