# Exercise Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A sizeable open space has been dedicated to exercise here, with painted lines, a few metal baskets, and leather balls available for play. In one corner stacks of weights sit ready to be lifted, while on the opposite end of the space a shelf of books is accompanied by chairs for inmates less athletically inclined.
>
> A pair of single doors with signs denote lavatories for inmates should they need them. Above you the ceiling is grated, allowing for guards on the upper level to keep an eye on the inmates here.

This area is open to inmates during daytime hours, but is cleared out and locked up in the late evening. Nothing in this space is allowed to leave it if a guard is watching.
