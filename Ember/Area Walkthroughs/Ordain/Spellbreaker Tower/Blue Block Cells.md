# Blue Block Cells

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This block holds a row of single-occupant cells, each one broad enough to pace a few steps before meeting cold basalt. The entrances are a grid of light iron bars paired with sliding jail doors, their metal pale and worn smooth at the handle points.
>
> Within each cell a stone bed is built into the wall, fitted with a proper mattress and ample bedding. They also feature stone desks with a stout stone stool tucked beneath, all made of a lighter gray stone not matching the tower's.
>
> Near the back, a semi-private toilet space is set behind a short wood partition, just high enough to break the line of sight from the door without truly sealing anything off.

Cells are unlocked during daytime hours, allowing inmates to roam somewhat freely.
