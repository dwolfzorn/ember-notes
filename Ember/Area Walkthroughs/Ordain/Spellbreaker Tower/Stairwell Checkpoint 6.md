# Stairwell Checkpoint 6

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A singular stairwell connects to this space, its landing hemmed in by an iron gate and watched by a pair of wary guards.
>
> Beyond the gate the room is filled with long benches like a waiting room, and a half-circle desk manned by a guard acts as a receptionist.
>
> A single, narrow door is the only exit available into the rest of this level.

This space is occupied by guards at all hours, and all guards present have keys to open the doors here, as do the rest of the guards on this level. The [[Gameplay Details]] and walls are medium here.
