# Red Block Gate

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This checkpoint consists of two heavy iron gates and the inner space has seating for two guards, and some furniture for them while on shift. A glowing button is fixed on a copper pipe going into the floor here and is used by the guards to communicate with the central security room.
