# Stairwell Checkpoint 4

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Two wide stairwells meet here, both ending at wide landings sealed off by iron gates and marked with yellow lines. Guards posted here keep keen watch.
>
> Beyond the gates a large room sits with a stone fountain bubbles softly in the center of it. To one side of the room are wooden benches, and in the opposing corner a large desk where records and reports are processed for the level.

This space is occupied by guards at all hours. It uses an intercom to communicate with central security who work the [[Gameplay Details]] remotely. The [[Gameplay Details]] and walls are medium here.
