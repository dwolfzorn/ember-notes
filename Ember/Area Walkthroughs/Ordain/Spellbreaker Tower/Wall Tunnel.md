# Wall Tunnel

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A long, pitch black tunnel winding through the basalt stone in the walls of the tower. Its shape and length guarantee that all visible light is gone before you even reach its first bend.

The origin of this tunnel is unknown, and was covered up some time ago. It is large enough for a small creature to move through at normal walking speed, and a medium sized creature to move through at half speed. It connects [[Storage]] to the stairwell of the [[Stairwell Checkpoint 2]] area.
