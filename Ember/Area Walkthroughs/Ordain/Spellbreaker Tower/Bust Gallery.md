# Bust Gallery

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Five stone busts sit on large stone plinths in this hallway, their gray countenances lit by the dancing flames of the braziers nearby. The stone plaques at their bases are each engraved with the names of the figures represented.

The busts are representations of the wardens that previously served the tower, and include the dates that they served. Each warden seems to hold the position for about twenty years before being replaced.
