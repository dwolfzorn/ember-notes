# Kitchen

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cramped kitchen is packed wall to wall with baskets and crates of fresh produce, bundled herbs, and stacked cuts of meat laid out for quick use.
>
> A few narrow worktables are crowded with boards, knives, and bowls, while pots simmer and kettles steam over squat stoves, filling the air with heat and sharp, savory scents.
>
> The stone floor is damp and mottled with spills, and everything here is arranged for speed, with ingredients and tools kept within arm’s reach.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Cooks of the Line
>
> Two [[Cindaric Adherent]] trained as cooks work here to prepare food for the other levels during the day. They use the dumbwaiter to move meals between floors when they are ready.
>
> #### Ups and Downs
>
> The dumbwaiter is strong enough to move up to three Medium sized creatures at once, though not comfortably or swiftly. The elevator takes two minutes to move to a new level and makes enough noise to alert anyone within 15 feet of its arrival or departure point.
