# Dark Cells

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This corner of the prison is dark, the lights all snuffed out and the cells left open save for one where a single glowing cube hovers silently, emitting a strange light. The cell it resides in seems to have suffered damage, with a collection of dark stones piled in the corner behind it.
>
> A few of the other cells feature cages large enough for a person, albeit without an ounce of comfort. The air here has a heavy, unsettling quality, and lingering too long feels unsafe.

The cells are unoccupied save for [[The Cube]]. Most of the cells have smaller iron cages in them. Guards don't come into this area unless they have to.
