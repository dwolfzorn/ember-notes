# Red Block Cells

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The cells here are sparse, mattresses lie directly on the basalt floor with bedding piled atop them. The air near the bars tastes of smoke and the shadows cast through the bars feels harsher against the ample bare stone.
>
> The fronts of these cells are built for endurance: thick, heavy iron bars and matching heavy iron jail doors seated on armored rails keep the occupants in permanently, and each looks to take effort to even open.

Cells are locked all hours and inmates are not let out except under full guard.
