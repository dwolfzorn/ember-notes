# Fountain Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A huge, three-tiered fountain dominates the north wall here, a large rask's head spewing water into the basins. Lanterns fixed to the wall on either side cast warm light across the flowing water.
>
> Doors sit on either side of the fountain, while a pair of heavy double doors sit on the other side of the room. The rest of this space is occupied with assorted barrels and storage containers.

This space mostly holds general goods used by the prison level, but at your discretion there may be more useful items hidden here, such as a coil of rope or a weapon, but a Perception or Investigation check might be necessary to find them in the piles of containers.

#### Work in Progress

Hidden door assets and interactivity are planned for this secret door, and will be added in a future update.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Hidden Passage
>
> Characters with a **Awareness (DC passive)** notice seams on the eastern wall that denote what might be a hidden doorway. However there is no clear way to open it.
>
> #### Remote Entry
>
> Searching the wall and the space around the hidden doorway yields nothing in the way of a mechanism to open the hidden doorway, indicating that it might be opened elsewhere.
