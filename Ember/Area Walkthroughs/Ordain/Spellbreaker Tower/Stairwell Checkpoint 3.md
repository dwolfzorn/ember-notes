# Stairwell Checkpoint 3

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Shallow steps of well worn stone meet here at wide landing sealed off by iron gates and marked with blue lines. The gates are barred tight and held with heavy locks, with guards posted at them.
>
> Beyond the gates a large room waits, with badly scuffed wooden flooring, a shelf of reading materials, and a few couches sit against the walls in the corners for guards to use as a resting space.

This space is occupied by guards at all hours. It uses an intercom to communicate with central security who work the [[Gameplay Details]] remotely. The [[Gameplay Details]] and walls are light here.

#### Work in Progress

Hidden passage assets and interactivity are planned for this secret passage, and will be added in a future update.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Cracks in the Wall
>
> A character with a **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** or making either a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check while passing through the stairwell notices that a section of the wall has suffered damage of some sort and might be exploitable with the right tools.
>
> A chisel and hammer, or mining pick would be necessary to break open this section of wall, and there's almost no way to do it without being seen, but if done, it would create a passage through the stone.
>
> Characters that do this would create passage to a [[Rickety Platform]] outside. This passage is narrow, but easily traversed by a small creature. Medium creatures would need to squeeze through.
