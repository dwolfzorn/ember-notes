# Blue Block Gate

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This checkpoint consists of two light iron gates and the inner space has seating for two guards, and some furniture for them while on shift. An glowing button is fixed on a copper pipe going into the floor here and is used by the guards to communicate with the central security room.

This space is occupied by guards at all hours. It uses an intercom to communicate with central security who work the [[Gameplay Details]] remotely. The [[Gameplay Details]] and walls are light here.
