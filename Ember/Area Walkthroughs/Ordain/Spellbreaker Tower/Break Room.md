# Break Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This break room is plain but comfortable, a few rough tables and benches set here provide seating, and a collection of snacks await hungry occupants. On nearby racks some spare pieces of equipment sit, and one whole side of the room is piled with crates and sacks holding unknown contents.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Useful Items
>
> A few shields and clubs can be claimed from the racks, similar to what the guards use.
>
> #### Unattended Uniforms
>
> Characters choosing to search through the sacks will find they are stuffed with dirty prison uniforms and bedding destined for the laundry. However, a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check yields three dirty guard uniforms.
>
> While these uniforms lack belts, boots, gloves, and padded armor, they might be enough to masquerade as a guard from a distance. Wearing these uniforms provides **+2 Boons** on stealth checks against other guards as long as they aren't within 20 feet of the wearer.
>
> A more complete uniform would be far more effective, but it won't be found here.

At your discretion there may be more useful items hidden here, such as rope or tools, but a Perception or Investigation check might be necessary to find them in the piles of containers.
