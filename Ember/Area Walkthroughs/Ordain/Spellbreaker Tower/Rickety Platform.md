# Rickety Platform

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Old, half-rotten wood hangs from corroded metal struts, the slats lashed together with frayed rope long past its prime. Possibly the remnant of some older construction effort, this platform seems as though it was forgotten when world concluded and was left to the mercy of the elements.

This rickety platform connects to the [[Damaged Cell]] and the [[Stairwell Checkpoint 3]] and accessing either space through the damaged wall requires tools and considerable effort to widen the gaps on either side.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Falling Hazard
>
> A character that falls or jumps from this platform without some way to land safely would suffer the maximum `[[/damage 20d6 bludgeoning]]` damage after falling about 250 feet.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Escape Options
>
> This location provides a viable jumping off point for wingsuits.
>
> Characters might consider this a viable location to tie off rope to climb down, and a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check will come to the conclusion that the rickety platform likely would work, but it wouldn't be the most stable or reliable point.
