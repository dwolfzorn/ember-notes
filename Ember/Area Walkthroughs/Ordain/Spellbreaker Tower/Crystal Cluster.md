# Crystal Cluster

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A huge cluster of crystals rises from a basalt mound protruding from the roof. Each crystal is a little different, and each emits a pale lavender light, though the qualities of that light, from the richness of the color to the frequency that it pulses is unique.
>
> A loud, modulating hum rolls out from the cluster of resonating crystals, swelling and dipping like a steady, resonant chord that you can feel in your bones.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Part of the Sum
>
> A successful **Arcana (DC 15)** surmises that this is the source of the antimagic field that permeates the city district. The lack of any spell runes, symbols, seals, or wards makes the operation of this cluster an absolute mystery.
>
> - **Result of 20+**. While this huge clump of crystals is certainly generating a powerful antimagical field, given the size of the tower, the other nearby crystals, and the way energy is moving through the space, this is likely only one part of a mechanism that might be spread out through the entire district around the tower.
>
> #### A Sliver of Crystal
>
> The crystals are unbreakable in any practical sense, and cannot be destroyed or disabled. However, a character with proper tools (such as a prybar or pick) could separate a single [[Spellbreaker Shard]] from the cluster.
>
> Attempts to get more than one automatically fail, even if attempted by different characters.
