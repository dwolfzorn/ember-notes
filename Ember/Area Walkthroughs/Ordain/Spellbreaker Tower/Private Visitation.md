# Private Visitation

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cluster of visiting rooms is divided into small, plain chambers separated by a barred window narrow enough to make passing anything larger than a finger's width impossible. Benches and simple stools sit opposite the bars, with stone tables fixed to the wall.

Visitors heading into these rooms and inmates coming out of these rooms are searched by an attending guard before they are allowed to move on.
