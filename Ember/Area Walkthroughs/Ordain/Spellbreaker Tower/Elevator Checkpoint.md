# Elevator Checkpoint

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This checkpoint consists of two iron gates and the inner space has seating for two guards, and some furniture for them while on shift. The gates connect the central elevator space and a carpeted lobby.

This space is occupied by a pair of guards at all hours, both guards have keys to the gate, as do all other guards on this level. This area uses [[Gameplay Details]] and walls.
