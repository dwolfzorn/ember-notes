# Stairwell Checkpoint 1

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You enter a broad, square chamber where two wide stairwells meet, one rising up to the landing, and the other descending. The steps are shallow and worn smooth at the edges, as though traversed by many sets of feet over the years.
>
> Each stairwell ends at a wide landing sealed off by iron gates set into thick frames that are themselves set deeply into the stone above and below it. The gates are barred tight and held with heavy locks, with guards posted at them.
>
> Adjoining rooms connect to this space, one filled with chests and storage crates, and the other a small space for personal searches.

New prisoners are brought in here to be searched and relieved of their belongings. Any items they entered the prison with are claimed here and stored in the chests nearby. Once their possessions have been claimed a guard checks their records and assigns them a cell block on a higher level.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Smuggled Items
>
> Sneaking an item past the guards here may require one or more successful **`[[/skill sleightofhand 14]]`** checks. A character can only hide one item at a time, and that item must be small enough to fit in their hand.
