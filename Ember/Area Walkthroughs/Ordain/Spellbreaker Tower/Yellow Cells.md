# Yellow Cells

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This block holds a row of single-occupant cells, each one broad enough to a fixed stone bed and mattress with bedding provided. The cells also feature a broad stone desk against the wall with a squad stone stool to accompany it.
>
> All these fixtures are cut from a paler stone that doesn't match the tower's own. The toilet space is half-screened by a wood partition that provides modesty but little privacy.
>
> The entrance to the cells are comprised of sturdy steel bars and moderately reinforced jail doors sitting in reinforced metal tracks.

Cells are locked most hours and inmates are not let out without at least one attending guard to supervise.

The halls can be secured with light metal double gates, limiting movement between individual groups of cells.
