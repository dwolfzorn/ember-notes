# Armory

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This armory is lined with racks and stands each crowded with ready gear. Shields are stacked in orderly rows, crossbows hang by their stocks, and quivers of bolts sit bundled in crates within quick reach.
>
> Suits of sturdy armor stand along the walls on simple frames, each ready to be put on, while weighted clubs rest in thick clusters below.
>
> The air is rich with the sharp smell of weapon oil, leather, and oxidized iron.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Armed and Dangerous
>
> The racks here hold a large assortment of armors and weapons, enough to outfit upwards of two dozen characters in [[Riot Armor]] with [[clubs]], [[Heavy Crossbows]], and [[ammunition]] for all of them.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gearing Up
>
> During a crisis, standard [[Spellbreaker Guard]] can come here and gear up, taking up a crossbow, ammunition, armor, helmet, and shield, becoming a [[Spellbreaker Riot Guard]] when done.
>
> These more heavily armored guards then move to the central elevator and head to whatever level needs help.
