# Blue Block Canteen

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Behind light iron bars sits a wide canteen dedicated to feeding the inmates of this level. Half a dozen stone tables and accompanying stools provide space for two dozen people to eat at once and a central dumbwaiter seems to be the source of the meals that are served here.
>
> Side doors lead to a pantry with serving dishes, and some lighter consumables fit for between meal snacks.

Access is free most hours from side doors connecting to the cell blocks while access via the central hall access is limited via a security gate.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Three Square
>
> Meals for guards and inmates arrive via a dumbwaiter three times a day, but a small cupboard is available and tended by a pair of [[Cindaric Adherent]] to provide snacks throughout daytime hours.
>
> Given the lower security of this level, the pantry workers and inmates are able interact fairly freely as long as no issues are created.
>
> #### Ups and Downs
>
> The dumbwaiter is strong enough to move up to three Medium sized creatures at once, though not comfortably or swiftly. The elevator takes two minutes to move to a new level and makes enough noise to alert anyone within 15 feet of its arrival or departure point.
