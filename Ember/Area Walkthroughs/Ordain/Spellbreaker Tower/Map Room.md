# Map Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The walls of this room are lined with shelves, each one comprised of dozens of cubby holes and they in turn cradle rolled up parchment and vellum tied with leather stringers. In the center of the space are four tables with old, beaten maps on display.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Cartographer's Cache
>
> Looking through the shelves you quickly realize that this room houses a large collection of old maps. Most are decades or centuries old and depict regions of the world as they were understood to exist back then.
>
> Most of these maps are likely wildly out of date and inaccurate, giving them more value as collector's items than anything else.
