# Shooting Range

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A collection of weather beaten bows rest on tables alongside quivers stuffed with arrows. All around them are barrels, bales of hay, and dummies serving as targets.
>
> Everything about this space is geared toward recreation, not training, and likely is used by the guards to entertain themselves when not on shift.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Worthless Weapons
>
> Examining the bows and arrows on the tables here for a moment reveals that these are cast-off weapons, low quality, and not meant to see actual combat unless one had no other options.
>
> There are six [[Battered Shortbow]] available to be claimed here, along with several hundred low quality arrows. Together, these weapons deal only 1d4 piercing damage and have half the range of a normal bow due to their low quality.
