# Yellow Block Mess

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The small dining area here is furnished with a few wooden tables, some broad and communal, others smaller, both with half-eaten meals and trays of snack foods sitting unattended.
>
> Built into one wall is a dumbwaiter framed in stone and fitted with a light iron grate to limit access without a key. A small panel allows it to be sent to different levels.

The dumbwaiter is strong enough to move up to three Medium sized creatures at once, though not comfortably or swiftly. The elevator takes two minutes to move to a new level and makes enough noise to alert anyone within 15 feet of its arrival or departure point.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Guards Only
>
> A place for guards to eat. Since Yellow Block feeds inmates in their cells, and has a higher on-duty guard roster (also on call for Red Block above) this space is where guards gather and relax while on duty.
>
> #### Three Square
>
> Meals for guards and inmates arrive via a dumbwaiter three times a day, but a small cupboard is available and tended by a pair of [[Cindaric Adherent]] to provide snacks throughout daytime hours.
>
> #### Ups and Downs
>
