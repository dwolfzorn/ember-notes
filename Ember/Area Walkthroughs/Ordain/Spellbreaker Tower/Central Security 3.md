# Central Security 3

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Braziers spit smoke toward the ceiling where tendrils of it disappear into pinhole vents in the corners. The unstable light dances over carved floors and numerous barred windows.
>
> A collection of clutter comprised of sacks and crates sits piled in one corner next to a lon work desk scattered with papers and half-written log reports. Fixed to the wall above it are a series of color coded buttons on copper pipes.
>
> Set against either side of the central elevator walls are banks of levers, six levers total, each labeled with the gate they control access to.
>
> A pair of weapon racks are bolted to the north and south faces of the central elevator column here, their frames holding a collection of heavy clubs just like those the guards carry.

Access to this room is controlled by two [[Gameplay Details]].
