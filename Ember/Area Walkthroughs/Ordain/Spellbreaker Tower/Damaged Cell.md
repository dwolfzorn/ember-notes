# Damaged Cell

This cell appears as any other, but keen eyes will notice that the back wall has suffered some damage that could be exploited.

#### Work in Progress

Hidden passage assets and interactivity are planned for this secret passage, and will be added in a future update.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Cracks in the Wall
>
> A character with a **Awareness (DC 16, Passive)** or making either a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** or **Awareness (DC 12)** check notices that the damage and cracks in the corner of this cell are more extensive than they might initially appear.
>
> With proper tools, like a chisel and hammer, or mining pick it's possible for a character to break away the wall and possibly create a passage through the stone.
>
> Doing so might take hours of conspicuous work, and hiding it would be all but impossible, but if done it would create a passage to a [[Rickety Platform]] outside.
