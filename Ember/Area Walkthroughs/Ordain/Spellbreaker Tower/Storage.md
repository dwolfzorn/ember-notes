# Storage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This storage room is crowded with stacked crates, rough boxes, and tied burlap bags stack atop each other. Simple shelves are bowed slightly under the weight of general goods of the same type. The air is dry and dusty, carrying the mixed scents of wood, hemp, and old canvas.

This space mostly holds general goods used by the prison level, which generally consists of sheets, pillowcases, spare prisoner uniforms, and the like. At your discretion there may be more useful items hidden here, such as rope or tools, but a Perception or Investigation check might be necessary to find them in the piles of containers.
