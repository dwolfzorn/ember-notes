# Sky Cells

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The cells here are sparse, mattresses lie directly on the basalt floor with bedding piled atop them and each one is missing a wall, instead they open to the sky beyond. The air here is crisp, bordering on cold, and a strong wind sweeps through the cells periodically.
>
> The gates to these cells are ironically heavy, with thick iron bars and matching sliding doors that roll over armored rails, as if the occupant couldn't simply leap from the tower at will. Though, such a decision might surely be their last.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Falling Hazard
>
> A character that falls or jumps from this platform without some way to land safely would suffer the maximum `[[/damage 20d6 bludgeoning]]` damage after falling about 250 feet.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Escape Options
>
> This location provides a viable jumping off point for wingsuits.
>
> This location provides a viable tie-off for rope to climb down the tower.
