# Stairwell Checkpoint 2

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You enter a broad, square chamber where two wide stairwells meet, one rising up to the landing, and the other descending. The steps are shallow and worn smooth at the edges, as though traversed by many sets of feet over the years.
>
> Each stairwell ends at a wide landing sealed off by iron gates set into thick frames that are themselves set deeply into the stone above and below it. The gates are barred tight and held with heavy locks, with guards posted at them.
>
> Several wooden shelves and open racks have been set along one side of the central chamber, crowded with folded prison uniforms in dull, serviceable cloth.

Here, incoming inmates are given a checkup by a waiting [[Cindaric Adherent]] before receiving uniforms here based on the cell block they are heading to. Privacy is a luxury not afforded here, sadly.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Curious Damage
>
> Characters with a **Awareness (DC 14, Passive)** or making a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check notice a boarded up corner where the stone appears to have been damaged.
>
> Prying the boards off requires tools (such as a prybar) or a successful `[[/check str 16]]` check, and both actions are noisy enough to attract attention from the adjacent room.
>
> Success grants access to the [[Wall Tunnel]] beyond it.
