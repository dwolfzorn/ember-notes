# Staff Corner

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Secluded from the rest of the prison is a quite space with a few doors, one leads to a private lavatory for guards where they can wash and do their business, while another leads to small but comfortable break room with a couch and shelf of books.

Beyond the break room is another door, and a storage room.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This room is packed high with crates, sacks and barrels goods. It seems there is no organization or method to the madness here, and each container holds an assortment of goods, making finding anything specific here a chore.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Useful Items
>
> Without spending much time searching this space a character can find a few spare sheets, sacks of spare inmate uniforms (Yellow Block), a few bottles of wine, a lantern, and little else of use.
>
> #### Digging Deeper
>
> A character that spends 10 minutes actively searching through the mess will find more useful items, including a 50 foot coil of rope, and a set of [[Thieves' Tools]].
