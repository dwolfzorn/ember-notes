# Warden's Quarters

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> These private quarters are cozy and clean, the floor is spotless, and the shelves neatly organized. A collection of bottles and packages of food cover the flat surfaces, while a double bed sits in the corner.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Useful Items
>
> The footlocker and cabinet both contain a pair of guard uniforms in good condition. They are tailored for a human, and might not fit all ancestries, but they would allow one to easily pretend to be a guard provided the wearer doesn't act suspicious.
>
> #### Sundry Items
>
> The rest of this room contains packaged foods such as candies, dried fruits, pastries, and the like. Some of them appear to be gifts, and a few have cards indicating they were given by someone named "Surna" who is quite fond of the warden and hopes to see them soon.
>
> #### Clothing for Two
>
> Searching through the cabinet, a character would notice that some of the clothing is different in style and size, likely meant for someone with a more petite frame. It is likely the warden doesn't always sleep alone here.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Guest List
>
> The items found here are meant to indicate that the Warden has a partner in the city that sometimes stays over in the tower. If the party wants to make use of this insight, they can.
