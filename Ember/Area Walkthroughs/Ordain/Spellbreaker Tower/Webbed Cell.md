# Webbed Cell

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cell is coated with glittering, iridescent webbing wrapped tightly over the furnishings meant for more humanoid inmates. At the center of this arachnid's nest is a large spider made of faceted crystal. It watches you from the dark with glowing eyes.

This cell is home to a unique, and deadly creature:

**The Bleak Weaver**

A strange, intelligent [[Young Cheliceraeth]] spider found hunting citizens in [[Quarry Run]]. Seemingly invincible, it was fought to a standstill at which point it became dormant. Without any way to dispatch it permanently, the Hallows ordered it stored in Red Block indefinitely. The tower has not suppressed whatever force makes it immortal.
