# Entry Checkpoint

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You enter a broad, square chamber at the top of the stairs. A metal gate clatters open loudly as you're ushered through into a broad space with wood floors and numerous benches. A guard manning a desk here looks up at you as you arrive.

Everyone coming into the tower from lower levels are checked in here, from new inmates to guests to guards. Visitors and inmates receive a brief, cursory search for contraband before being sent onward.

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Nothing Dangerous Allowed
>
> Characters entering the tower from this point will have relieved of any weapons and dangerous items already, all of which end up in storage crates in the [[Stairwell Checkpoint 1]] storage room.
>
> This search is just to make sure no other dangerous items or contraband items (such as narcotics, lock picks, poisons, etc.) made it this far.
>
> Note that inmates will be subject to another search before moving up to the next level.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Smuggled Items
>
> Sneaking an item past the guards here may require one or more successful **`[[/skill sleightofhand 14]]`** checks. A character can only hide one item at a time, and that item must be small enough to fit in their hand.
