# Ballistae

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A raised platform of weathered planks supports a pair of large wooden ballista, their frames thick and splintered at the edges where the wood has dried and checked. The bow-arms sit slack, cords dulled and frayed, and the winching gears are crusted with rust, seized stiff in their housings.
>
> The ladder up is rickety and suspect, in truth the whole emplacement looks neglected and long unused.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Unused Arsenal
>
> Characters with **Knowledge: Warfare** of succeeding on a **Awareness (DC 14)** check know that these weapons have not been maintained whatsoever. Attempting to use this weapon would pose a greater hazard to the operators than their intended target.
