# Entrance

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Just past the double doors, you can feel and hear the thrum of the Pit Trap grow louder, the intensity of it now just a pair of doors away. In this space, the club staff relieve people of coats and cloaks, tucking them away in a coat room lined with racks and shelves. On the other side of the room a door leads to a cramped office where club staff talk loudly amongst themselves.
>
> Periodically, the main doors open as people come and go, each time they do the noise and chaos of the club spills out in ringing bursts.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Posted Rules
>
> Per a large painted sign by the doors, these things will get you thrown out of the Pit Trap for the night:
>
> - Fighting, whether you start it or finish it.
> - Harassing the staff, band, or patrons.
> - Casting spells or using magic on the unwilling.
> - Hanging from the mezzanines or trying to get into the dancer cages.
>
> A further warning at the bottom states:
>
> > Repeat offenders will be banned.
> >
> > Ignorance is not an excuse.
