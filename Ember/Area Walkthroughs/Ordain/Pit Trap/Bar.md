# Bar

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The long counter of the bar is a rough, dark wood burnished smooth by countless hands and drinks traveling over it. Rings of old spills stain the wood, and leave it sticky in places despite frequent wipe-downs.
>
> A four-headed metal tap juts out of the bar's center where drinks are drawn and served as rapidly as orders come in.
>
> Behind the counter, the Pit Trap's bartenders move to a chaotic tempo set by the bodies that crowd the wood and call out orders over each other. Each demand is met by a flurry of motion and practiced precision as bottles come off shelves, drinkware is laid out, and libations are mixed.

### Ordering Drinks

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A chalkboard listing drinks and their prices hangs over the bar, each one overpriced by a huge margin, but still selling at a rapid pace. Prices are paid up front, each handful of bright coin glittering against dark wood swiftly replaced by a drink of unidentifiable parts and undeniable strength.

> [!info] Social
> #### High Quality Spirits
>
> The Pit Trap's drinks are all strong and delicious, each one expertly mixed for the patron by a bartender who tries to look like they don't care about your order. A successful **Deception (DC 14)** check notices that they take their job seriously, despite appearances.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Drink Responsibly
>
> The Pit Trap serves strong drinks. Characters can consume a number of drinks equal to their Constitution modifier without ill effect. Drinking more than that requires a successful `[[/save con 14]]` saving throw to avoid the &reference[poisoned] condition for the next hour.
>
> #### Bottle Service
>
> In the event that a fight breaks out, there are several drinks and bottles of alcohol on the bar which can be used as improvised weapons. Characters can take an action to use either of these improvised moves, provided they are within reach of the bar and there's at least one use available:
>
> - **Throw Drink (2d4 uses)**. The character grabs a random drink from the bar and splashes it in a target's face. The intended target must make a successful `[[/save dex 14]]` or suffer the &reference[blinded] condition until the end of their next turn.
> - **Swing Bottle (1d4+1 uses)**. The character grabs a bottle and swings it at a foe. The intended target must make a successful `[[/save dex 14]]` saving throw. On failure, the target is struck by the bottle, dealing `[[/damage 1d10 bludgeoning]]` damage and shattering the bottle.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### House Offerings
>
> The following drinks are available to order:
>
> **Snake Bite, 5 gp**
>
> Bright green and threaded with gold, the taste is crisp, a mix of lime and sour apple with a peppery finish that numbs the throat and lips.
>
> **Rusty Snare, 5 gp**
>
> A coppery drink with orange and green flecks swirling through it. It's powerfully sweet at first, but has a metallic snap that makes your teeth hurt.
>
> **Shot of Venom, 10 gp**
>
> A single shot of pearlescent black liquid that tastes like violets and roses, with a sweet blackberry tail.
>
> **Snapped Ankle, 10 gp**
>
> A cloudy bone-white drink with a thin red swirl of syrup at its center that tastes like sweetened milk and raspberry that hides the high-proof alcohol within.
