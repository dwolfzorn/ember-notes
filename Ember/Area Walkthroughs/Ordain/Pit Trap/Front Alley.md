# Front Alley

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The wider alley you arrived by gives way to smaller passages between buildings, each one choked with discarded barrels. You can see people in the shade and shadow of these alleys, all of them appearing to be club patrons taking a moment to get some fresh air before heading back into the building.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### No Easy Way In
>
> While the alleys run the perimeter of the Pit Trap's building, there are no alternate entrances for the party to find here. They must go through the front door to get in.

### Gaining Access

If the characters want to explore the interior of The Pit Trap, they'll have to gain privileged access — by any means necessary. Sneaking into the facility is nigh impossible, so the front door is the party's only option.

> [!info] Social
> #### What's the Password?
>
> The party's first order of business is gaining access to the exclusive nightclub by properly entreating with the doorman, who is not easily swayed.
>
> **Marth Holbrook** (Chaotic Neutral, Ordani Kiska, he/him) is a dismissive and easily offended fellow who takes his job very seriously, and he has the muscular physique to prove it. Marth will only permit club members and their guests to proceed past the front door, and requires the establishment's weekly password to enter.
>
> - The password this week is: *"Auntie says smoke 'em if you got 'em."*
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 18)**, **Intimidation (DC 22)**, or **Diplomacy (DC 18)** check is able to convince Marth to let the party enter without too much hassle.
>
> - **Knowledge: Crime**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Alternately, Marth is willing to accept a sizable bribe of 100 gp in exchange for the party's access, but they must also make a successful **Diplomacy (DC 13)** or **Deception (DC 13)** check to seal the deal. Agraband has an extra 20 gp to spare for the cause, but is otherwise cashed out for now.
>
> #### Alternate Methods
>
> If the characters fail to gain entrance to The Pit Trap via one of the methods mentioned above, they can choose instead to eavesdrop on other club patrons in an attempt to learn the coveted password.
>
> The first character to make both a successful **Stealth (DC 18)** and **Awareness (DC 15)** check while hiding at least 20 feet away from the entrance is able to discern the password as another patron speaks it aloud to Marth.
>
> - In the event of a failure, the party can repeat this check once every 20 minutes. During this time, other patrons come and go, showing varying amounts of comical disdain for the characters if nearby.
>
> Alternatively, a character who casts the [[Detect Thoughts]] spell on Marth or one of the other patrons is able to learn the password after probing deeper into the target's mind.
