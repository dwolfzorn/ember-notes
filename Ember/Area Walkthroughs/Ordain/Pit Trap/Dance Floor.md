# Dance Floor

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The Pit Trap's central space is dim, the people here little more than silhouettes outlined by the glow of pink lamps on the walls, dancing through a thin haze of smoke. With the crush of thirsty patrons at the bar on one side and the throng of dancers crowding the stage on the other, the central space is dotted with dancers and mingling guests who have yet to be consumed by either group.

> [!info] Social
> #### Dance the Night Away
>
> Characters who choose to dance with the crowd must make a successful **Performance (DC 14)** or `[[/check dex 14]]` check to avoid bumping into anyone or getting jostled by fellow dancers.
>
> Failing by 5 or more results in the character suffering `[[/damage 1 bludgeoning]]` damage from a wayward elbow. Due to the hectic nature of the venue, they can't tell where it came from.
