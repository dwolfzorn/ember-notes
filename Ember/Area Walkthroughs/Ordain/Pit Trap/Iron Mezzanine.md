# Iron Mezzanine

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you move past the entrance doors, the full force of the Pit Trap is sprung on you at once. The confined space of the entrance is replaced by the sprawl of a warehouse, the clatter of heavy grating underfoot, the rattle and creak of metal cages swinging from great iron rafters, and the roar of a crowd enraptured by a live band below.
>
> It takes you a moment to acclimate to the sudden change, as your senses attune to the darkness of the Pit below, barely lit by the unsteady light of dozens of pink lamps affixed to the walls, not a one of them strong enough to do anything but faintly illuminate the edges of the crowds that gather in the center.
>
> Down the stairs, you can see yet more patrons crowding around a bar, while across the space you can see an entire mezzanine dedicated to private seating, and the private parties with enough clout to have access to them.
