# Stage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A broad wooden dais rises a step above the floor, its boards dark as the rest of the wood here, stained black by polish and countless spilled cups. A broad embroidered rug swallows most of the platform, the intricate patterns of gold and black weaving through the rich red cloth. Great pillows serve as seats for band members who attend a collection of instruments.
>
> At the edge of the stage, bodies press close, moving in time with the band's music, the roar of their collective voices rising and falling as the performance does. The air here is thick with the smell of sweat, alcohol, and smoke, and the energy is intoxicating.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Band Members Only
>
> Access to the stage is not physically restricted, but any characters attempting to take the stage will find themselves being physically removed by a pair of nearby [[Ordani Bouncer]] who are keeping an eye on the stage.

> [!info] Social
> #### Special Guest Appearance
>
> Characters who approach the stage while openly carrying musical instruments might be invited to the stage to perform. If the characters accept the invitation, they need to make a successful **Performance (DC 16)** check to impress the band and crowd.
>
> If successful, they are given a random drink from [[Bar]]. On failure, they are booed off the stage, being physically removed if they refuse to leave on their own.
