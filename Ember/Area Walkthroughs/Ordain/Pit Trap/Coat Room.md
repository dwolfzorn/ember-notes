# Coat Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cramped space is lined from floor to ceiling with shelves and iron rails burdened by a confusion of cloaks, capes, coats, and traveling bags. A dozen different perfumes cling to the air in thin threads, mingled with the tang of leather, and dried sweat.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Hidden Treasure
>
> A character than spends 5 minutes searching the shelves and makes a successful **Awareness (DC 18)** check will find the [[Mantle of Shadows]].
