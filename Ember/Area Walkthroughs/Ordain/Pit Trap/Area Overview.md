# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> [[The Pit Trap]] Area Map depicts the entirety of the club. It is featured in the [[Status Effects]] Event of the [[Unfinished Business]] Side Quest.
>
> - The map depicts two Scene Levels: the Mezzanine and The Pit.
> - The party begins in the exterior [[Front Alley]].

## Gameplay Details

The areas of The Pit Trap have the following features unless specified otherwise in the text.

### Levels & Elevation

The Pit Trap Scene features a total of two vertical Levels of elevation, including:

- **The Mezzanine:** The mezzanine is set to an elevation of 0. Guardrails on the mezzanine help prevent accidental falls.
- **The Pit:** The pit is set to an elevation of -20 and can be accessed by metal stairwells.

Dancer cages are set to an elevation of -10. They are suspended from the rafters above by long iron chains; the rafters are inaccessible.

### Sound

The area around the club is quiet, but the sound of the crowd and music within leaks out, and are audible as you approach. Within the club, however, the noise levels rise sharply, and between the people talking and cheering, and the energetic performance of the band, the club is intensely loud.

As a consequence, hearing in this environment is more challenging. Staff and clubgoers alike talk loudly to each other, and it's only between songs that the din quiets down at all.

- Characters in this area have **+2 Boons** to any **Stealth (DC undefined)** skill check to avoid being heard.
- The verbal components of spells can be enunciated quietly enough such that they do not attract attention.

### Illumination

Outside the Pit Trap, the alleys and streets are lit by lanterns at night, leaving the areas brightly lit in most cases.

The mezzanine areas are bathed in a pale but bright pink light from wall-mounted sconces. The lower pit area, which includes the bar, stage, and dance floor, is shrouded in dim pink light that borders on darkness.

- Characters in the lower dance floor area have **+2 Boons** to any **Stealth (DC undefined)** skill check to avoid being seen.

### Terrain

The Pit Trap's terrain is normal in all areas.

- The mezzanine's floor is metal grating that allows Tiny objects and liquid to pass through.
- The pit areas are a mixture of stone and dark wood flooring.

### Inhabitants

Though they aren't hostile to begin with, the club is protected by a team of 8 [[Ordani Bouncer]] that are on-hand to resolve any issues as they arise.

- If forced to deal with unruly patrons, the bouncers' go-to correction is to escort the offender outside the club, and not let them back in.
- The bouncer team attempts to break up fights if they occur, but is not willing to risk their lives in combat. They withdraw if any combat more dangerous than a drunken brawl breaks out.

The club is also filled with a large number of [[Ordani]] that serve as the location's dancers, performers, bartenders, and the clubgoers visiting the location. These humanoids aren't hostile, and flee from combat if anything more than a barehanded brawl breaks out.

### Enemies

During the [[Status Effects]] Event, the following enemies are encountered here:

- 4 [[Undaunted Adept]]
