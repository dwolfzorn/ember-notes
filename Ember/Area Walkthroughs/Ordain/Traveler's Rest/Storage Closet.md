# Storage Closet

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This closet is crammed full of all sorts of supplies, including the telltale tools and equipment of the healers, surgeons, and apothecaries who work here. From wall to wall, well-used instruments and apparatuses sit beside or upon half-forgotten containers surrounded by clutter, suggesting a sense of haphazard convenience and utility.

This chamber serves as a storage closet, and contains a variety of supplies, tools, and equipment used by the Cindarics of Traveler's Rest.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Closet
>
> A simple search reveals the following: the closet is full of practical supplies used throughout Traveler's Rest, including folded linens, empty chamber pots, spare tools, lamp oil, and other common necessities. Nothing stored here is especially valuable in isolation.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 12 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 10)** check notices that the shelves have been allowed to fall into mild disarray, with several items stacked out of place and a few smaller supplies in need of sorting.
>
> Any character who spends 10 minutes reorganizing the storage closet discovers a single silver piece that had rolled beneath a shelf.

> [!info] Social
> #### Light Work
>
> Once the work is finished, the head sage Jessa Aritou takes notice of the unusual tidiness and conducts a personal inventory of the closet's contents, which takes `[[/roll 1d4]]` hours. Though slightly irritated by the unsolicited help, she offers the party a **`[[/award party 5sp]]`** for their labor — accompanied by a gentle admonishment not to touch the lodge's supplies.
