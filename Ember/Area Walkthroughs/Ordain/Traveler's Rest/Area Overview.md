# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The [[Traveler's Rest Healing House]] Area Map depicts the entirety of the healing house. It is featured in the [[Swift Healing]] Event of the [[Unfinished Business]] Side Quest and the [[The Secret Autopsy]] Event in the [[Smoldering Cinders]] Main Quest.
>
> - This map depicts two Scene Levels: the Healing House and the Basement.
> - The healing house is a place of refuge, rest, and relaxation for [[Ordain]] citizens and visitors alike.
> - The party arrives through the doors to the [[Main Office]], where the Cindaric Adherent on duty can set them on the proper course.

## Gameplay Details

The areas of Traveler's Rest have the following features unless otherwise specified.

### Levels & Elevation

The Traveler's Rest Scene features a total of two vertical Levels of elevation, including:

- **Healing House:** The healing house is set to an elevation of 0 and has 15-foot-high ceilings.
- **Basement:** The basement is set to an elevation of -15 and has 15-foot-high ceilings.

### Illumination

The interiors of Traveler's Rest are illuminated by torch and lantern light, which radiates bright light for 15 feet and dim light for an additional 15 feet. Outside areas are subject to exterior lighting and associated weather conditions.

### Terrain

The terrain here resembles a typical urban landscape, with enough clutter and furniture to create intermittent spots of &reference[difficult terrain].

Most doors within Traveler's Rest are unlocked. Of the doors that are locked, a [[Traveler's Rest Key]] is necessary to open them without forced entry. The Basement Entrance has an additional corresponding [[Traveler's Rest Basement Key]] that must be used to open it. All of the Cindarics at Traveler's Rest are equipped with the Traveler's Rest Key, while only a select few (such as Jessa Aritou) have full-time access to the Basement Key.

Other points of entry include smaller doors attached to the [[Library]], [[Main Office]], [[Jessa's Private Quarters]], [[Guest Quarters]], and [[Rear Storage Vestibule]], plus multiple points of access via the exterior [[Courtyard]].

### Inhabitants

The following characters and creatures inhabit this area:

- Traveler's Rest is inhabited by several [[Cindaric Adherent]] and [[Cindaric Sage]], including the head sage, Jessa Aritou. While they work at various positions throughout the facility, many of these Adherents can often be found in the [[Cindaric Lodging]] chamber during evening and off-duty hours.
- One [[Burnished Hand Protector]] is regularly stationed here on security detail, and spends a lot of time in the [[Recreation]] room on the eastern side of the facility.
- The [[Infirmary]] is currently occupied by four [[Ordani]] and two [[Arcturian]] — patients in need of Traveler's Rest's storied healing.
- Throughout the day, other Ordani and Arcturians tend to drop in for a variety of short consultations or healing services from the Cindarics, but the frequency of these visits is as unpredictable as the weather. Interestingly, more and more people seem to be in need of the Cindaric Order's help of late (a phenomenon that hasn't gone entirely unnoticed by the Cindarics themselves).
