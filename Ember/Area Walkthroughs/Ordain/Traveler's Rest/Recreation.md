# Recreation

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cluttered room appears to have once been a guest bedroom or the like, but now bears the signs of a somewhat communal space. A narrow bed on the northern wall sits half-made. Nearby, a game board and several wooden pawns lie scattered atop a small round table, as though the players abandoned it only moments ago. A bored-looking Burnished Hand Protector greets you with a nod.

This chamber provides the residents and visitors of Traveler's Rest with a place for rest and relaxation, whether of a communal or solitary nature. More often than not, a Burnished Hand Protector from [[Lower Ashvale]] can be found here, having been stationed at Traveler's Rest to provide martial support for the local Cindarics.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Chamber
>
> A simple search reveals the following: this room is supplied with a handful of simple comforts for idle hours, including spare blankets, dog-eared books, a deck of cards, and a well-used Kingslayer set with mismatched wooden pieces.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 13 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 11)** check notices several neat rows of tally marks carved into the table's sides.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The character also finds a folded scrap of paper tucked beneath a table leg: it lists a few favored Kingslayer opening plays in a messy scrawl. Any character who spends 10 minutes studying these notes gains **+2 Boons** on one Intelligence check made to play Kingslayer in this room.

> [!info] Social
> #### The Bored Guard
>
> **Lucel Darrigon** (Neutral Good, Arcturian Human, he/him), the Burnished Hand Protector stationed here, is starved for company, and readily engages the party in light conversation. He will not disclose sensitive information, but may reveal the following:
>
> - The Burnished Hand keeps a protector at Traveler's Rest to discourage trouble, assist the Cindarics in emergencies, and provide a visible reminder that patients and staff are under protection.
> - Visitors are welcome in the healing house, but certain areas — such as the [[Private Surgery]] room and the [[Basement Entrance]] — are off-limits.
>
> A successful **Deception (DC 13)** check confirms that the guard is speaking honestly within the limits of his station, and is more bored than suspicious.
>
> #### Water Water Everywhere
>
> Lucel may ask the characters to retrieve the bottle of [[Waterborne Whiskey]] from the Refectory and return it to him. He will also accept [[Cindaric Mint Tea]] from the [[Infirmary]] or the [[Alchemy]] workshop.
>
> In exchange for one of these substances, he palms them a **`[[/award party 5gp]]`**, consumes the drink, and promptly settles in for a sound nap lasting `[[/roll 1d4]]` hours.
>
> #### Care For a Game?
>
> Lucel may ask the characters for a friendly game of Kingslayer. The game takes 1 hour. Any character who participates must make a **`[[/skill intelligence]]`** check against the Burnished Hand Protector's Intelligence score (10).
>
> - **Skilled With Game Set:** The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check if they're experienced with any game set.
>
> Regardless of the game's outcome, the guard grows more talkative: the next **Diplomacy (DC undefined)** check made to solicit information from him is made with **+2 Boons**.
