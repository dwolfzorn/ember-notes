# Alchemy

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cramped alchemical workshop is crowded with blackened glassware and dried herbs strung from a timber rack. A clouded alembic rests on the workbench, surrounded by sparse but meticulously arranged supplies.

This area is used as an alchemical workshop and storage space. The Cindarics prepare medicines, tinctures, and curative draughts here before distributing them throughout the infirmary.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Locked & Off Limits
>
> The doors to this chamber remain locked unless a surgical procedure is taking place. Each door can be unlocked with a [[Traveler's Rest Key]] or a successful `[[/skill sleightofhand 15 tool=thief]]`; alternatively, it can be bashed open with a **Athletics (DC 22)** check.
>
> Characters found in this area without a good reason will be asked to leave Traveler's Rest and be barred from returning.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Alchemical Lab
>
> This area contains an alchemical lab and enough alchemical components to craft 1 of each of the following:
>
> - [[Antifungal Draught]]
> - [[Anticoagulation Tonic]]
> - [[Dissolution Tonic]]
> - [[Heartward Elixir]]
> - [[Cindaric Mint Tea]]
> - [[Potion of Healing]]
>
> Alchemical components are restocked on the first day of each month.

### [[Spreading Sickness]]

If the characters return during the [[Spreading Sickness]] Side Quest, the alchemical supplies are completely exhausted.
