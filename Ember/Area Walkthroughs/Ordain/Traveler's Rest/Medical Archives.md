# Medical Archives

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This neatly cluttered chamber is lined with shelves, replete with numerous books, scrolls, and tomes that appear to contain the facility's archive of medical records and reference documents. The remaining wall space is decorated with assorted medical diagrams, and a handful of dusty skeletal specimens surround the room. Meanwhile, a small desk covered in half-forgotten research occupies a space along the northern wall.

This chamber hosts the medical archives of Traveler's Rest, including current and historical records of patients, handy reference documents, timeworn Cindaric manuals, and more.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Archives
>
> Characters who take a moment to search the archives are able to immediately spot the following:
>
> - Several volumes of patients' medical records. Many of these leather-bound tomes are sealed with permanent [[Arcane Lock]] spells, and require the password "*Adonia's grace*" to open, spoken in Adonia's native **Language: Arcden**. The contents of these volumes are mostly inconsequential to anyone other than the relatives of the patients or deceased people documented therein.
> - The labeled skeletal reference models that stand here in the room are the cleaned and reconstituted bones of four distinct specimens of humanoids: a human, an Ashka, a Keth, and a Vrjnhar. A fifth, somewhat larger humanoid skeleton occupies the stone table at the center of the chamber, in a state of slight disarray.
> - The desk here sees relatively little use, if the layer of dust on it is any indication. A [[Book]] labeled "An Encyclopedia of Aterican Osteology" is splayed open on the desk, revealing a page that details the skeletal system of a Kivahr male.
> - A hook on the wall near the Basement Entrance bears a key ring containing both a [[Traveler's Rest Key]] and a [[Traveler's Rest Basement Key]].
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the archives is able to locate the following:
>
> - The desk drawers contain a loose set of [[Calligrapher's Supplies]] and `[[/roll 1d2]]` random [[Arcturian Trinkets]].
> - Additional [[Books]] pertaining to various subjects like Bodily Systems, Alchemical Preservation, and Taxonomy.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The character is also able to find a [[Flask]], half full of [[Arcturian Liquor]], hidden above the desk inside a hollowed-out book.
