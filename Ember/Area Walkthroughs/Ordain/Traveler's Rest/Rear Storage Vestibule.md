# Rear Storage Vestibule

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Half of this vestibule is crammed full of foodstuffs — including sacks of grains, bushels of dried fruits and nuts, and barrels of water and wine — while the other half contains an assortment of other items. A clear path cuts through the middle of the room, and the scuff marks on the floor near the northernmost door betray the function of this chamber as the delivery depot of Traveler's Rest.

Due to the exterior door positioned on the northern wall, this antechamber is used as a reception area for deliveries, and also serves as pantry storage for the [[Refectory]].

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Off Limits
>
> The doors to this chamber remain unlocked, but the area is off limits: characters who are found in this area without permission (or a good reason) will be asked to leave Traveler's Rest, and may be barred from returning.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Vestibule
>
> A simple search reveals the following: in addition to the foodstuffs stored here for the [[Refectory]], the vestibule contains empty crates, wrapping twine, burlap sacks, and other mundane delivery supplies used to receive and sort incoming goods.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 13 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 11)** check notices a folded delivery manifest tucked onto a crate near the northern door. It lists a number of routine recent shipments; at your discretion, it may confirm where certain common supplies are usually stored within Traveler's Rest, as well as the contents of the locked medicine chest in the [[Private Surgery]] room.
