# Private Surgery

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A single operating table sits in the center of this room, illuminated by a bright surgical lamp. Counters nearby hold carefully arranged tools, bottles, and folded cloths. A brass basin for washing instruments stands beside the table, and the sharp smell of antiseptic solutions pierces the air.

This chamber serves as the primary facility for patients in need of surgery, and is off-limits to visitors during all hours of the day unless they have express permission.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Locked & Off Limits
>
> The doors to this chamber remain locked unless a surgical procedure is taking place. Each door can be unlocked with a [[Traveler's Rest Key]] or a successful `[[/skill sleightofhand 15 tool=thief]]`; alternatively, it can be bashed open with a **Athletics (DC 22)** check.
>
> Characters who are found in this area without permission (or a good reason) will be asked to leave Traveler's Rest, and may be barred from returning.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring Private Surgery
>
> A simple search reveals the following: in addition to the room's surgical implements, the counters here are stocked with clean bandages, splints, brass needles, hemp sutures, and several stoppered vials of antiseptics.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 13 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 11)** check notices that one of the southwestern tables has been draped with a cloth, low enough to conceal something beneath it; a closer look reveals the corner of a small, locked medicine chest.
>
> - **Path: Cindaric Initiate**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> The chest requires either a [[Traveler's Rest Key]] or a `[[/skill sleightofhand 15 tool=thief]]` to open. It contains the following items:
>
> - [[Drakonbane Poison]]
> - [[Thieves' Lament]]
> - [[Heartward Elixir]]
> - [[Potion of Greater Healing]]
> - A [[Spell Scroll]] based on the party's level:
>   - Level 2: [[Unknown]] of [[Unknown]]
>   - Level 3: [[Unknown]] of [[Unknown]]
>   - Level 4: [[Unknown]] of [[Unknown]]
>
> #### Surgery in Progress
>
> At your discretion, a surgery might be occurring here. Roll on (or choose from) the [[Surgery]] table to determine which particular procedure is currently in progress.

### [[Spreading Sickness]]

If the characters return during the [[Spreading Sickness]] Side Quest, the private surgery theater is unoccupied and emptied of supplies; all Cindarics are busy fighting the mysterious wasting illness here and elsewhere in Ordain.
