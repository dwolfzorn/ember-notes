# Refectory

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This large refectory is organized around a broad oak table at its center, the wood stained dark in places by years of careless dining. Along the walls stand preparation counters and shelves of brass cookware. A stone hearth smolders in the northwestern corner, and the smell of cooked meat and charred herbs drifts from the embers.

This area functions as both a food preparation area and a shared dining hall. Meals are typically taken whenever they are most convenient for the Cindarics on duty and their visiting guests.

> This scholarly healer is clad in garments of bright gold and lush crimson, the colors of the famed Cindaric Sages of the Arctus Plateau. Their shoulders and tunic are marked with the familiar four-leafed diamond that signifies the Cindaric order, and their hands are marked and calloused by the wholesome abrasions of honest labor. The sage regards you with a benevolent smile.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Refectory
>
> A simple search reveals the following: the shelves and larders here are kept in orderly condition, with simple ingredients and cookware kept close at hand so meals can be prepared quickly.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 14 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check notices a bottle of [[Waterborne Whiskey]] partially hidden behind a number of other bottles.
>
> If stolen, the whiskey can be given to the Burnished Hand Protector in the [[Recreation]] room for a small reward.

> [!info] Social
> #### A Hot Meal
>
> A Cindaric is busy at work preparing meals for the whole lodge. They welcome the characters to sit and enjoy a hot meal if they're hungry, but are generally unwilling to engage in long conversation. However, any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 12)** or **Diplomacy (DC 12)** check is able to solicit some additional information, including:
>
> - Meals here are prepared for both Cindaric staff and any guests or patients well enough to leave their rooms. Those too weak to walk are typically served directly in the [[Infirmary]].
> - The Cindarics keep simple staples on hand at all times: broths, roasted meats, coarse breads, herbal teas, and other foods meant to be easy on the stomach.
>
> - **Path: Cindaric Initiate**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The Cindaric decides to "share a little something extra," and retrieves the bottle of [[Waterborne Whiskey]]; they leave the bottle on the central table.
>
> A successful **Deception (DC 13)** check confirms that the Cindaric is speaking honestly, along with a genuine desire to be warm and inviting to guests.

### [[Spreading Sickness]]

If the characters return during the [[Spreading Sickness]] Side Quest, the refectory is emptied of supplies.
