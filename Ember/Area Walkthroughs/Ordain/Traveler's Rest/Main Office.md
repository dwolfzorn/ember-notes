# Main Office

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The cluttered front desk of this main office is covered in parchment notes and dog-eared ledgers, and is flanked by a modest bookshelf on the north wall. An array of low wooden benches fills the front and sides of the room, and the amber glow of lantern light keeps a warm and welcoming vigil here.

This room serves as a reception and waiting area for healing house patrons. A sturdy desk sits near the entrance. Next to it, a large woven basket holds folded wool blankets for patients who must wait overnight.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Main Office
>
> A simple search reveals the following: a prominent ledger rests upon the edge of the front desk. Any character who takes a moment to look at this visitor's log sees the two most recent entries, aligned beneath categories labeled "Name," "Reason for Visit," and "Notes."
>
> Entry 1 features the current occupant of the [[Study]], as determined by the [[Traveler's Rest Itinerant]] table.
>
> > [NAME]. Research.
>
> Entry 2 features the current occupant of the [[Guest Quarters]], as determined by the [[Traveler's Rest Itinerant]] table.
>
> > [NAME]. Temporary housing.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 14 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check while searching the room is able to notice the following:
>
> - A logbook of remote Cindaric assignments for various locations throughout Ordain, including the [[Lower Ashvale]], [[Spellbreaker Tower]], and the [[Burial Grounds]].
>
> - **Path: Cindaric Initiate**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character discovers a [[Traveler's Rest Basement Key]] inside the desk.
