# Cindaric Lodging

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> If the crimson robes hanging around the room are any indication, this modest dormitory houses the resident Cindarics of Traveler's Rest. Seven slender beds line the chamber, each paired with a small footlocker and an iron hook for garments. This orderly space is one of unostentatious comfort, rife with the soothing scent of clean linens and aromatic teas.

This room serves as the primary bedchamber for Cindarics assigned to work at Traveler's Rest, where they're expected to tend to the ill and the infirm at nearly all hours of the day. While many sages, adherents, and initiates who work the healing house do actually live elsewhere, several of the Cindarics are provided with beds here.

> This scholarly healer is clad in garments of bright gold and lush crimson, the colors of the famed Cindaric Sages of the Arctus Plateau. Their shoulders and tunic are marked with the familiar four-leafed diamond that signifies the Cindaric order, and their hands are marked and calloused by the wholesome abrasions of honest labor. The sage regards you with a benevolent smile.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Off Limits
>
> The doors to this chamber remain unlocked, but the area is off limits: characters who are found in this area without permission (or a good reason) will be asked to leave Traveler's Rest, and may be barred from returning.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Cindaric Lodging
>
> A simple search reveals the following: several of the beds are made with nearly identical precision, while others show small signs of recent use, such as a half-finished cup of herbal tea, a pair of slippers tucked beneath a bedframe, or a freshly washed robe left hanging from an iron hook to air dry.
>
> The room's footlockers contain little of material value, consisting mostly of spare robes, linens, writing materials, Cindaric texts, and small personal effects. However, any character with a `[[/skill perception 15 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the room discovers a Kingslayer travel set with carved bone pieces.
