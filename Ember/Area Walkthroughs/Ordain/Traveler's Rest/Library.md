# Library

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The air here is infused with the earthen smell of aged parchment and the congenial aroma of lingering incense. Numerous books, tomes, scrolls, and folios are stacked neatly on the shelves that line this welcoming library.

Cindarics are known for their studious application of knowledge, and this library chamber contains all the standard scholastic resources that have come to be associated with the Sages, including time-honored volumes on subjects like medicine, alchemy, botany, geology, Cindaric purification rituals, and the metaphysics of Ember.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Library
>
> A simple search reveals the following: the library's collection focuses heavily on Cindaric medicine, anatomy, and the natural world, but a small shelf dedicated to Ember metaphysics stands apart from the rest.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 13 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 11)** check while searching the area also notices the following:
>
> - Every book bears a small brass clasp engraved with Cindaric sigils, though none appear to be locked.
> - Most volumes show almost no sign of wear, as if they have been repeatedly restored.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The character discovers an ornate feather-shaped bookmark of silvered metal tucked inside a book on Ember metaphysics. The base is embossed with a cat's head symbol.
>
> Any character who examines the brass clasps and makes a successful **Arcana (DC 18)** check is able to detect that the brass clasps are anchoring a powerful magical effect; [[Detect Magic]] reveals a pervasive aura of Conjuration magic surrounding each clasp.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Society (DC 18)** or **Arcana (DC 20)** check is aware of the reason: many Cindaric libraries secure their collections with enchanted clasps that teleport misplaced or stolen books back to their proper shelf `[[/roll 1d4]]` hours following due date expiration. The same enchantment also periodically repairs wear and tear. If the clasp is separated from the book, its enchantment is broken.
>
> - **Path: Cindaric Initiate**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
>
> Known by all Cindarics, the secret phrase "Knowledge in the margins" suppresses the clasp's enchantment for 36 hours.
>
> #### Using the Library
>
> A character can perform a Study Action in this area over the course of a Short Rest, gaining **+2 Boons** on their next check related to a chosen area of knowledge. At the GM's discretion, they may also learn new pieces of information about the subject.
