# Morgue

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The cool air of this large chamber is thick with macabre suspense. Six stone slabs decorate this room, four of which are adorned with humanoid corpses. On top of the abstruse alchemical practices of the Cindarics, you suspect these cadavers are magically preserved in some kind of suspended animation, based on the lack of both visible rot and the putrid stench of the grave.
>
> Just around a corner to the northwest, a narrow set of stairs wraps upward to the ground level of the facility, where the soft thrum of activity can be heard in the nearby distance.

This large room is the morgue of Traveler's Rest. Once a cadaver has been properly cleaned and examined, it becomes the target of a [[Gentle Repose]] spell and is brought here, where it awaits final transport to the [[Burial Grounds]] or another location. There are currently 5 cadavers in the morgue.

The morgue is attended by at least one [[Cindaric Adherent]] at all times. A solitary Cindaric works the night shift here, and 1-2 Cindarics can often be found working in this location during daytime hours.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Morgue
>
> The morgue is supplied with various tools of the trade, which can be readily spotted by the characters as they survey the area. Notable elements of the room include:
>
> - A desk on the northern wall, stacked with various notes and documents relating to the cadavers that have recently been processed here.
> - A shelf crammed with official documents like coroner's reports and death certificates.
> - A few piles of random equipment, loose tools, and sundry other items.
> - 4 rolled canvas body bags on a steel cart in the hallway to the east.
> - The skeletal specimen of a deceased humanoid, stored behind glass in a horizontal display case.
>
> The first character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the morgue is able to locate the following:
>
> - A [[Book]] on modern embalming techniques of the [[Cindaric Sages]], labeled "The Vascar Papers: A Treatise on Cindaric Funerary Rites".
> - A [[Book]] on traditional [[Kelmezian]] embalming techniques, labeled "Consecrations of Old Carinth".
> - A [[Spell Scroll]] of [[Mending]]. Alternately, this scroll can be instantly located by a character using the [[Detect Magic]] spell.
>
> - **Critical Success**: A Holy Symbol of [[Thayloc]] is hidden within a concealed drawer inside the desk, a [[Reliquary]] containing TBD.
>
> #### Inspecting the Cadavers
>
> A series of successful **Awareness (DC 14)** checks can confirm some basic information about the cadavers currently stored here, including their respective causes of death.
>
> - These corpses are random Ordani citizens, and the details of their lives and deaths are relatively inconsequential to the party's adventures. You are encouraged to come up with your own details about the ancestry, gender, and cause of death of these deceased individuals.
>
> - **Knowledge: Forensics**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> The cadavers stored here effectively change every three days. If the characters leave Traveler's Rest and return at least three days later, the initial cadavers are no longer here, and have been replaced with different ones.

> [!info] Social
> #### Cindaric Small Talk
>
> The Cindarics assigned to work the morgue either love it or hate it, and conversation in this part of Traveler's Rest tends to be more droll and morbid compared to the chatter upstairs.
>
> If the characters find themselves here in the morgue without Cindaric supervision, the Cindaric Adherents on duty will attempt to escort them upstairs and into the welcoming attention of a fellow sage.
>
> Otherwise, if the characters have permission to be here, the Cindarics on duty in the morgue are willing to discuss the following topics of conversation:
>
> - Day-to-day duties of Cindarics assigned to the downstairs areas of Traveler's Rest.
> - The general layout of the upstairs locations of Traveler's Rest.
> - Jessa Aritou's role as the head sage, and the roles of the various Cindarics who work here under her inspired guidance.
> - Some of the recent cadavers that have made their way through the morgue.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 17)** or **Diplomacy (DC 14)** check is able to convince the Cindaric on duty to share a bit more information.
>
> - The Cindaric mentions the presence of resources like the Holy Water in the [[Examination Room]] and the barreled chemicals in [[Basement Storage]].
> - The Cindaric will also absentmindedly or accidentally reveal the presence of the "lost & found" chest in the [[Downstairs Vestibule]] area.
>
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Path: Cindaric Initiate**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
