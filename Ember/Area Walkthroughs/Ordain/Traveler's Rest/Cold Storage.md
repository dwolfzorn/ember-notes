# Cold Storage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The air of this small, dimly-lit chamber is unnaturally cold. Four recessed appliances like tall steel vaults line the southern wall; one of them stands open, revealing a humanoid body atop a metallic cart half-extended from within. While overtly morbid, this place is nevertheless highly clinical and remarkably clean.

This chamber serves as a cold storage facility here at Traveler's Rest, where the cadavers of the recently deceased can be contained without fear of spoilage.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Chamber
>
> The room here is sparsely decorated. Its only notable contents are the four refrigerator vaults that line the southern wall, and a matching set of size-appropriate carts made from a light steel alloy. One of these carts bears a humanoid cadaver, with a parchment tag inconspicuously attached to the right foot (see "Investigating the Cadaver" below for more information about the corpse itself).
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check while searching the room is able to notice the following:
>
> - Most of the vaults here are currently empty.
> - The room itself and the steel carts appear to be cleaned with regularity, to an exceptional standard.
>
> - **Critical Success**: A surgical knife is covertly attached to the bottom of one of the steel carts here via a simple tension clamp. Treat this knife as a [[Dagger]]. Any character who uses this hidden knife to begin combat gains a +1 bonus to initiative if using a weapon attack with it for their first action.
>
> A successful **Arcana (DC 13)** check is able to confirm the use of magical enchantments as the primary source of refrigeration here.
>
> - **Critical Success**: The character is able to surmise that rare [[Duskflame Powder]] is likely a source component of the refrigeration methods used here.
>
> #### Inspecting the Cadaver
>
> A successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check is able to confirm some basic information about the cadaver currently stored here, including the cause of death.
>
> - This corpse is a random Ordani citizen, and the details of their life and death are relatively inconsequential to the party's adventures. You are encouraged to come up with your own details about this deceased individual's ancestry, gender, and cause of death.
>
> - **Knowledge: Forensics**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> The cadaver stored here effectively changes every three days. If the characters leave Traveler's Rest and return at least three days later, the initial cadaver is no longer here, and has been replaced with a different one.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Freezing Cold Temperatures
>
> Any character who spends more than an entire hour in this chamber must make a successful `[[/save constitution 15]]` saving throw to avoid gaining 1 level of Exhaustion. This saving throw must be repeated every three hours after the first.
>
> - A character cannot gain more than 3 levels of Exhaustion this way.
>
> - **Ancestry: Wirrun**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
