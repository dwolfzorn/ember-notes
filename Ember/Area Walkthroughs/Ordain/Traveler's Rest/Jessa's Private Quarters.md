# Jessa's Private Quarters

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This cozy chamber is a curious amalgam of bedroom and study lounge, with more than enough space for a tiny gathering — a notion supported by the small couch at the foot of the bed. Ample tools, trinkets, and curios are stashed around the room, and an impressive model of Ember's cosmos serves as an eye-catching centerpiece.

This tool- and trinket-cluttered chamber serves as the private quarters of the Traveler's Rest head sage Jessa Aritou. For more information on Jessa, consult the [[Area Overview]] or the [[Westgate]] gazetteer entry in the Ordain Gazetteer.

The kindhearted Jessa Aritou is a [[Cindaric Sage]] equipped with a [[Traveler's Rest Key]] and a [[Traveler's Rest Basement Key]].

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Locked & Off Limits
>
> The doors to this chamber remain locked unless Jessa is present. Each door can be unlocked with a [[Traveler's Rest Key]] or a successful `[[/skill sleightofhand 15 tool=thief]]`; alternatively, it can be bashed open with a **Athletics (DC 22)** check.
>
> Characters who are found in this area without permission (or a good reason) will be asked to leave Traveler's Rest, and may be barred from returning.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring Jessa's Quarters
>
> A simple search reveals the following: the room's shelves, side tables, and cabinets are crowded with reference books, rolled charts, writing materials, tea tins, spare garments, and a careful assortment of tools and curios. Though eclectic, the chamber is orderly, and nothing here appears particularly strange.
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 13 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 11)** check notices that the Ember cosmological model is not merely decorative, but articulated: the Elemental Moons have been manually aligned into a conjunction. [[Unknown]] reveals an aura of Illusion magic surrounding the model.
>
> A character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 20)** check while attempting to realign the cosmological model causes it to reconfigure itself into a small chest.
>
> - **Knowledge: Cosmology**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - `[[/knowledge stars]]`: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Inside the chest is a plain silver band worth 25 gp and an ornate, leatherbound journal. Its pages contain appointment reminders, inventory notes (including items found in [[Basement Storage]] and the [[Downstairs Vestibule]]), a few patient histories pulled from the [[Medical Archives]], and brief personal observations about patients, staff, and guests.
>
> The chest automatically reconfigures itself into a cosmological model after 1 hour.
>
> #### Basement Access
>
> A staircase in the northwest corner of the room leads down to the [[Downstairs Vestibule]].
