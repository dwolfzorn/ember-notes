# Basement Storage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This shadow-cloaked chamber appears to be reserved for basement storage. Packed full of various crates, chests, and barrels, it reeks of some kind of pungent chemical.

This basement chamber is utilized for the storage of assorted tools and equipment, along with various chemicals and compounds used by the Cindarics during their mortuary services.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Storage Chamber
>
> This darkened room is plainly packed full of tools, equipment, and consumable resources. Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while searching the room is able to spot the following:
>
> - 2 barrels full of a curious chemical (formaldehyde).
> - 2 barrels full of a diluted isopropyl alcohol solution (used for cleaning), and 1 barrel full of undiluted isopropyl alcohol.
> - The contents of a [[Scholar's Pack]] and `[[/roll 1d2+1]]` random results on the [[Corpse Loot]] table.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Hazardous Chemicals
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check is able to identify the pungent chemical smell as that of an aldehyde used for the preservation of organic tissue. The substance is fatally poisonous if ingested.
>
> - **Knowledge: Forensics**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Characters with a `[[/skill nature passive 12 format=long]]` or who make a successful **Wilderness (DC 14)** check are immediately wary of the alcohol stored here and its flammable properties. The alcohol is used for industrial cleaning rather than consumption, and is severely poisonous if ingested.
>
> - **Knowledge: Alchemy**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character surmises that the undiluted barrel of isopropyl alcohol would make a powerful improvised explosive, dealing `[[/roll 4d6]]` points of fire damage to any creature within 30 feet of a detonation caused by an attack that does fire damage to the barrel itself.
