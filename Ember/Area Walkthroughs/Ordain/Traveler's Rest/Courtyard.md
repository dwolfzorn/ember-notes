# Courtyard

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A magnificent stone sundial sits near the center of this recessed courtyard, where nearly a half-dozen doors lead into various chambers of the healing house. A breeze sweeps through this area with regularity, carrying the ceaseless sounds of the city with it.

This exterior area serves as a quiet retreat where [[Cindaric Adherent]] can rest and contemplate amidst the open air of western Ordain.

### Time and Time Again

If the characters explore this area during daylight hours, they may discover a curious aspect of the courtyard's centerpiece: the large stone sundial doesn't tell accurate time.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Courtyard
>
> During daylight hours, any character with a `[[/skill perception 14 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check while searching the area notices that the shadow of the sundial doesn't align quite evenly with the hour markers.
>
> A character that examines the faulty sundial and makes a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check can determine the cause: the gnomon (the raised pointer that casts a shadow upon the dial) was likely designed for a different latitude.
>
> - **Path: Academy Dropout**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Artifacts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Cosmology**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Culture: Rhivan**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character identifies the precise reason for this error: based on its design, this particular sundial was imported from the [[Rhivan]] [[Star Academy]], and seems to have never been recalibrated for orientation in Ordain (although it's obviously "close enough" by Cindaric estimates).
>
> Once the problem is identified, any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 13)** or **Wilderness (DC 13)** check while spending 1 hour working on the sundial using the appropriate tools is able to repair the broken device — aligning the gnomon within its bracket until its shadow falls accurately along the hour mark.
>
> - **Knowledge: Crafts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Machines**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The job is finished in half the time.

As soon as the sundial is properly aligned, read the following aloud:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you nudge the final component into place, the sundial shudders for a brief moment, vibrating with arcane energy. Just then, a dozen tiny blue orbs of magical light illuminate the numerals that line the circumference of the dial. The current hour appears to glow more intensely than the others, and an artful scrawl of lambent text appears on the side of the sundial's stony plinth. It reads:
>
> > A gift of the Star Mages. May time always be on your side.

> [!info] Social
> #### Just Rewards
>
> If the party manages to repair the sundial, the head sage Jessa Aritou is astonished by the unsolicited refurbishment, and offers the characters a **`[[/award party 25gp]]`** for their timely service.
