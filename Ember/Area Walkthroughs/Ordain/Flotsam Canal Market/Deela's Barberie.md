# Deela's Barberie

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The crisp smell of disinfectants lingers in the air of this quaint little barber shop, vaguely masking the copper scent of freshly exsanguinated blood. A mural on the eastern wall showcases a wide range of hairstyles for potential customers, and a ponytailed Kiska barber greets you with a half-cocked smile, her hands wet from the daily cleaning.
>
> > Hi! I'm Deela. Have a seat. I'll be right with you.
>
> She motions to two benches along the west wall as she finishes up the task before her, placing an array of dirty tools and accoutrements in a blood- or dye-stained sink (you're not entirely sure which).

Owned and operated by the acclaimed coiffeur Deela Danryn (with help from two junior cosmetologists), this Stoneside barberie offers haircuts, shaves, and dental work (along with occasional hedge-medicine procedures like bloodletting).

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with the Barbers
>
> **Deela Danryn (Chaotic Good, Ordani Kiska, she/her)**
>
> A testament to modern irreverence, Deela Danryn is a talented barber-surgeon who carries a lot of sway with locals of the Flotsam Canal Market — whether due to her infectious charisma, her skill with a razor blade, or both.
>
> Conversation topics that Deela is willing to discuss include (but are not limited to):
>
> - The exceptional quality of the services offered here at the barberie.
> - Various details about the Flotsam Canal Market at large.
> - Observations on [[Varinna]], with a mention of the mural she painted here at the shop (see "Exploring the Shop" for details).
> - Observations on [[Falar]], with a focus on his poor moods.
>
> A few specific dialogue options for Deela are provided below.
>
> #### Barberie Services
>
> The various services provided by Deela's Barberie (and their associated costs) are as follows:
>
> - Haircut (1 sp)
> - Dye Job (1 gp)
> - Permanent (5 gp)
> - Shave (5 sp)
> - Dental Work (1-100 gp)
> - Bloodletting (10 gp)
> - "Life-Changing Advice" (1 gp)
>
> Any character who purchases one or more of these services gains a temporary +1 bonus to their Charisma skill checks for a duration of `[[/roll 1d2]]` days. A character can only benefit from this bonus once a month.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About the Canal Market?
>
> **A:**
>
> > The Flotsam Canal Market has been good to us over the years. New faces come and go, and familiar favorites have a way of sticking around.
> >
> > You know, quite a few folks who work or shop in the market actually get their hair done **here**. In fact, if you see someone with a bad do or a lousy shave, please send 'em my way!

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About Varinna?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Varinna is lovely. She's been a good neighbor, and she actually painted the style wall over there for me a few years ago. She even touches it up every once in a while, thanks to a haircut or two on the house.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About Falar?
>
> **A:**
>
> > That gray-haired paint-pusher is pretty much always in poor spirits. If you ask me, a proper cut and a shave would do the old boy some good. But he don't seem too interested.

In addition to speaking with Deela, the characters may look around the shop.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Shop
>
> A mural on the eastern wall depicts a wide range of Ordani citizens with a varied assortment of haircuts and styles, painted by Varinna as a commission a few years ago.
>
> Any character who takes a moment to examine the art posted throughout the barber shop is able to notice [[Varinna's]] signature on the mural, which is overtly abstract in style.
