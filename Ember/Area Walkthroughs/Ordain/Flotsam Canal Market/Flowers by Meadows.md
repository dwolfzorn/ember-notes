# Flowers by Meadows

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This structure is surrounded by numerous earthenware pots filled with bright and blooming flowers in vibrant reds, golds, and yellows. Wooden crates line the low wall dividing the promenade from the lower paths, and each one is crammed with flower pots, each one priced for sale.
>
> Leaning halfway out of a half-door is a young human woman sipping liquid from a large mug. Bags under her eyes and a slightly unfocused look betray her lack of sleep, but as soon as you drift close enough to catch her eye she sets the mug aside and opens the door fully.
>
> > Hello, hi, welcome to Flowers by Meadows, I'm Merygold! Can I help you find anything today? We've got potted flowers here ready to go, and inside I've got plenty more cut and ready to be arranged!

This building is one of the older ones in the area, and like many of the businesses here, has changed hands multiple times. Flowers by Meadows is actually a transplant from another district, having moved here to avoid rising rent.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with the Shopkeep
>
> **Merygold Meadows (Lawful Neutral, Ordani Human, she/her)**
>
> A friendly if perpetually tired-looking florist who is never less than halfway through a mug of strong tea. What she lacks in sleep each night she makes up for in caffeine. She has owned this business for over a decade, but only recently relocated it to Flotsam a year and a half ago.
>
> Conversation topics with Merygold include (but are not limited to) the following:
>
> - Her favorite flowers, which will always be snakevine. She loves the shape of the flowers, but they are terrible in bouquets.
> - The key to a good flower arrangement being the proper layering of textures and shapes to create something with depth and visual variation.
> - Where she gets her flowers, which is mostly from local suppliers, but she sometimes has to buy from [[House Lilifeld]], which is fine. The quality isn't always great, but it's cheaper and the flowers are abundant.
> - Her tea is a custom blend she came up with years ago, and is steeped for ten minutes at least. It's bitter enough to wake the dead, which is great, because she's half dead most days on account of her insomnia.
> - If the party asks about [[Falar]], Merygold states that she doesn't know the paintmonger personally, but her friend Tula has complained about someone of the same name. She works nights at [[Unknown]] and probably knows more, or has a good story at least.
> - If the party asks about Varinna, she says that she's a fairly regular customer, coming in every few days to buy arrangements to make her studio look nice. Or she was, but Merygold hasn't seen her in about two weeks.

### Inside the Shop

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Inside the building you can see rows of buckets filled with fresh flowers and other decorative plants, shelves burned by glass vases, and displays of pre-arranged flowers ready to be taken home.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Shop
>
> Any character who examines the flowers or plants and makes a successful **Wilderness (DC 14)** or **Wilderness (DC 16)** check will observe that they are all healthy, fresh, and well cared for.
>
> Merygold offers an extensive collection of flowers from all over the region, grown by local farmers and brought in fresh every day for sale. Her shop offers the following:
>
> - Numerous types of [[Potted Plant]] for 10 gp apiece.
> - A custom made [[Fine Bouquet]] made by hand from the finest flowers available for 25 gp.
>
> A character with **Knowledge: Arts** notes that the arrangements on display are well crafted with a keen eye for design.
