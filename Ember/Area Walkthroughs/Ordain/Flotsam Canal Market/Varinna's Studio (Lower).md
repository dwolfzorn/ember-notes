# Varinna's Studio (Lower)

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A spirited Ordani woman answers the door, no doubt an artist by the look of her paint-dappled clothing and pigment-stained hands. She appears somewhat fatigued from labor, but an active air of inspiration and enthusiasm hangs about her. She blows a tassel of graying hair from her eyes before addressing you:
>
> > You folks don't exactly look like my normal patrons, but I never turn away a good commission, and I could use a break anyway. So, how can I help you?
>
> Behind her, the bottom floor of this two-story dwelling appears to host a spacious art studio. Several tables stand about the room, adorned with paints, palettes, brushes, and more — anything a professional painter might need to ply their trade.
>
> Meanwhile, two large canvases dominate the room, which appear to feature in-progress works of abstract art. The larger piece on the eastern wall depicts a mythical clash of two heroes on Signara, while the smaller piece to the north bears the emerging scene of a pair of intertwined, passion-wrought lovers.

Along with [[Varinna's Studio (Upper)]], this Stoneside-level area serves as the home studio and private residence of the accomplished Ordani artist known as Varinna — who plays an important role in the [[Mixed Media]] Event of the [[Local Color]] Side Quest.

The first floor of this two-story edifice is the primary studio space of Varinna's humble abode, and has multiple works on display that showcase her noteworthy talent as a mural artist, including a palimpsest of sketches and paintings on the very walls themselves.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with Varinna
>
> **Varinna (Neutral Good, Ordani Human, she/her)**
>
> Grew up middle-class, was once more disillusioned about some of Ordain's shortcomings (the Houses can strangle independent traders/makers out of the markets, forcing them to languish in smaller markets, like Flotsam's) but has changed her tune a bit now that she's succeeded well enough to land commissioned work with Salva.
>
> Varinna is happy to discuss her art style and history as an artist, but is initially unwilling to share more intimate details of her work and private affairs with strangers. However, she can be swayed with effort. Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 13)**, **Intimidation (DC 16)**, or **Diplomacy (DC 13)** check is able to convince Varinna to share some noteworthy details.
>
> - [[Outside the Lines]]: The party can invoke the name of [[Mistress Caberi]] to automatically succeed on this check, relaying that she has expressed a personal interest in this attack on House Salva's image. This elicits Varinna's immediate cooperation.
> - **Attunement: Heart of Ember**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Path: Lunar Empath**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Conversation topics with noteworthy details that Varinna is willing to discuss include (but are not limited to):
>
> - The [[House Salva]] mural from the [[Drawing Attention]] Event, and her relationship with the famed Trading House.
> - Her alibi, and potential witnesses who can corroborate the details.
> - The source of the paints and materials she uses for her work, and the inspirations for her abstract style.
> - Other local artists and painters of note.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 13)** check is able to tell that Varinna has nothing to hide, and is being totally forthcoming with her explanation of the matters at hand.
>
> A few specific dialogue options for Varinna are provided below.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What do you know about the mural?
>
> **A:**
>
> > I don’t, not firsthand. I haven’t seen it at all, and I certainly didn't paint it.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Why would it look like your style if you didn’t paint it?
>
> **A:**
>
> > I don't know! Perhaps a copycat?

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Where were you when it went up?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Home. I’ve been under the weather these past few days, some sort of head cold that's left me feeling terrible. I barely left my bed, let alone climbed a scaffold with pots of paint to work on a mural.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Can anyone confirm your alibi?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Yes, actually. Go speak to Seylu Koine, she runs the produce stand down on the docks, and is a friend of mine. She came by several times with soups and fruit and stayed to make sure I ate. She’ll vouch for me. Neighbors saw her coming and going too, I'm sure.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** What’s your relationship with House Salva right now?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Currently professional. I’m contracted to do some interior murals for them in exchange for pay and possible inclusion into the house later. I won’t jeopardize that by going after them through a mural.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** You used to be more critical of the trading houses.
>
> **A:**
>
> > I grew up watching friends get squeezed out of the big markets. I said plenty about it. I still think the system can grind down independents. But I’ve got a contract now, and I’m not foolish enough to bite the hand that’s feeding me, or pretend it isn’t feeding me.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Where do you buy your paints?
>
> **A:**
>
> > From Falar, mostly. He’s nearby, carries good stock, and it's all very consistent. If you look around my studio, you’ll see his labels on nearly everything.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Do you use magical or “infused” pigments?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Not yet, but I did buy some. My last order is sitting by the door, and the jars are still sealed with his wax stamp. You’re welcome to check the dates and break them open if you need to.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Would you object to us examining your studio and supplies?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Go ahead. If someone’s trying to steal my style and ruin my name, I want them identified as much as you do. Just don’t smear the charcoal thumbnails pinned on the east wall — I need those for a Salva mock-up.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Who might know your style well enough to imitate it?
>
> **A:**
>
> > There's plenty of painters in the local community, some of them far better than me, and I have my studio open all day for anyone that wants to come buy something or commission me.
> >
> > Look, I’m not accusing anyone, just … if you’re after material study, my work’s out in the open most days.

In addition to speaking with Varinna, the characters may examine her studio.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Studio
>
> Most of the pieces are for sale, and a few are already sold; they are going for modest prices, and the art is all well done. A sign on the wall notes she is not accepting commissions for the time being.
>
> If the party has previously completed the [[Drawing Attention]] Outcome, they can study Varinna's work by making a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check, concluding that while the styles are similar, they have some key differences. For example, Varinna's work has less bold lines, and her figures are portrayed slightly differently.
>
> - **Knowledge: Arts**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Attunement: Primordis**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
