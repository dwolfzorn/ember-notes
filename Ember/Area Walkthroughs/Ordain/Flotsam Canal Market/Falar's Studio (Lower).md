# Falar's Studio (Lower)

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Decorated for comfort, this cozy dockside chamber appears to be an artist's public-facing office or client sitting room. Two armchairs flank a coffee table near the room's center. Close by, an artist's desk props up a canvas carefully splattered with paint, and an array of other tables, desks, and shelves line the room's perimeter. Meanwhile, a large painting hung upon the northern wall depicts an image of Ordain's waterfront, masterfully rendered using oils of the deepest hues.

Along with [[Falar's Studio (Upper)]], this Dockside lounge is owned and operated by the retired adventurer [[Falar]], a wizard-turned-shopkeep who plays a central role in the [[Mixed Media]] and [[A Sketchy Situation]] Events of the [[Local Color]] Side Quest.

The sitting room here serves Falar as a parlor and showroom for potential customers, and is quite obviously set up for entertaining others. An illusory painting on the northern wall conceals a passage to [[Falar's Studio (Upper)]] via the staircase beyond.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Studio
>
> A brief examination of the room reveals the following:
>
> - The art here is stylish, full of distinction and skill. However, the newest piece is dated several years ago. It seems the artist hasn't done any new artwork in years.
> - The desk on the western wall contains the [[House Salva Art Missives]].
> - The Ordain Waterfront painting on the northern wall depicts a scene of immigrants from all over Aterica arriving to Ordain's shores, the city's docks brimming with community and hope.
> - The table in the northwest corner of the room bears both [[Painter's Supplies]] and [[Alchemist's Supplies]], along with [[Falar's Studio Notes]].
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 15)** check while examining the Painter's and Alchemist's Supplies is able to identify some curious qualities in them, including the presence of rare lunar elements that suggest an association with [[Cora]], the earth moon.
>
> - **Attunement: Cora**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Alchemy**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Cosmology**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> #### Illusory Painting
>
> The Ordain Waterfront painting on the northern wall is actually a permanent [[Major Image]] spell that conceals the stairway to [[Falar's Studio (Upper)]].
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Arcana (DC 15)** check while examining the painting is able to identify the telltale signs of illusion magic.
>
> - [[Detect Magic]]: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Artifacts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Crafts**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Physical interaction with the painting reveals it to be an illusion, as things can pass through it. Any creature that takes a turn to examine the image can determine that it is an illusion with a successful **Awareness (DC 16)** check. If a creature discerns the illusion for what it is, the creature can see through the image, and its other sensory qualities become faint to the creature.
>
> Alternately, any character who casts the [[Dispel Magic]] spell and succeeds on a spellcasting ability check (DC 16) can suppress the effects of the painting for a duration of 1 hour.
