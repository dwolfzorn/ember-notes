# Dockwood Books

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This canopy-covered landing appears to be some kind of used bookstore or community swap center. The dog-eared tomes and paperbacks here are all well-worn; indeed, some have missing covers, pages, or both. A quaint sign above the long wooden table to the west reads: "Take a book, leave a book!"
>
> Similar placards can be found above several crates here, each one labeled "Donations" in the same unsophisticated scrawl. All of these receptacles are laughably full, with books stacked high on more than one, and others strewn about in half-forgotten disarray.
>
> A lone shopkeep here takes inventory of what appears to be a new delivery of used books — a studious Ashka teenager with ink stains on his fingers. He offers you a respectful nod and glances at the various signs before getting back to his task.

Operated by the young shopkeep Sorian Harsa, this ramshackle stall is a used bookstore where locals of the Flotsam Canal Market can donate or trade pre-owned books. Most of the hardcovers and paperbacks here are in rough shape, and the low prices match the relative quality as a result.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with the Shopkeep
>
> **Sorian Harsa (Chaotic Good, Ordani Ashka, he/him)**
>
> This bookish young shopkeep is an erudite 17-year-old who seeks to make his mark on the world — one salacious paperback recommendation at a time.
>
> Conversation topics include (but are not limited to) the following:
>
> - Sorian's ambitions as a storyteller, which include writing his own novels one day. He's hard at work on his first title, a novella-length piece of speculative fiction called *The Conjurer's Apprentice*.
> - The inventory here includes donations from nearby [[Scholar's Nook]] and other neighborhood bookstores, along with used books contributed by locals.
> - He doesn't know Falar or Varinna personally, but is aware of their respective places of business.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 12)** check can immediately perceive the honesty behind Sorian's conversation and intentions, and has no reason to mistrust the wide-eyed young bookseller.

In addition to speaking with Sorian, the characters may also peruse the shelves. Most of the [[Tattered Book]] here are worth 5 gp each, and only provide a +1 bonus to any nonfiction topic contained within.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Shop
>
> Any character with a `[[/skill perception 14 passive format=long]]` or who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 12)** check while searching the stall is able to locate a few rarer [[Books]] scattered amongst the inventory here, including titles like *Mythic Wyrms of Old Akon*, *Cascilian Nights*, and *Tales of the Lonely Conscript*.
>
> - **Attunement: Signara**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Legends**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Critical Success**: The character finds a folded [[Map]] of the [[Sinkhole Depths]] inconspicuously concealed within a mold-stained [[Book]] called *Plumbing the Depths: Atro's Guide to Subterranea*. This map can be purchased along with the book at no extra cost.
