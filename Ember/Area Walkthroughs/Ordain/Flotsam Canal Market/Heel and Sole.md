# Heel and Sole

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The Signborn shoemaker here makes ample use of their four hands while working this small stall, which appears to be overstuffed with quality footwear from all four corners of Ordain.

This market stall is the business place of the Signborn cobbler Narro Pinz, who provides quality repairs on top of a modest inventory of locally-designed footwear for sale.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with the Shopkeep
>
> **Narro Pinz (Lawful Good, Ordani Signborn, They/Them)**
>
> This industrious shoemaker never sits still while working the four corners of their stall, all four Signborn hands working as fast as they possibly can. Narro talks almost as fast as they work, and believes that "good enough" is the enemy of perfect.
>
> Conversation topics include (but are not limited to) the following:
>
> - If asked about [[Falar]] they notes that the old paint maker stops by occasionally to get his shoes fixed or shined. He never has anything nice to say about anyone, but Falar always tips.
> - If asked about [[Varinna]], they say that Varinna's art is a bit too chaotic for them. They prefer classic fantasy styles.
> - If asked about Seylu in the quay, they maintain she's very nice, and always shares her leftover fruit. She's close friends with Varinna, but their friendship hasn't always been smooth sailing, so to speak.

In addition to speaking with Narro, the characters may explore the shop.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Shop
>
> Multiple sets of [[Cobbler's Tools]] can be found on the various surfaces here, along with at least one set of [[Leatherworker's Tools]]. These belong to Narro, and are not for sale.
>
> Narro offers shoe repair, cleaning, customization, and simple shining services. Prices range between 1 sp and 10 gp, depending on the desired service. They also sell resoled and repaired shoes for discount prices; the workmanship is of an expert quality, but the items are all mundane.
