# Hadwyn's Hats & More

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You come across a makeshift stall bedecked with all sorts of clothing accessories — hats, gloves, scarves, bags, and everything in between. The gregarious Nir'ae merchant in attendence here seems excited to see you.
>
> > Well, well, well … your head looks a little lonely there, stranger! How about a fancy new headpiece from Servio, eh? Perhaps a scarf to keep your neck warm? Or a new belt for your britches? I can see you have the style of a true Ordani!
> >
> > Whatever accessories you seek, Servio Hadwyn is at your service!

This provisional market stall plays host to the goods and services of Servio Hadwyn, a purveyor of hats and other clothing accessories.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with the Shopkeep
>
> **Servio Hadwyn (Chaotic Good, Ordani Nir'ae, he/him)**
>
> This industrious thirty-something tailor was blacklisted by House Salva for speaking out against some of local their fashion market designs. Now, he's resigned to selling his own wares on the street — a concession that causes Servio a great deal of consternation to this day. He conceals this frustration with flamboyant salesmanship (but this facade can slip when no one's looking).
>
> Conversation topics with that Servio is willing to discuss include (but are not limited to):
>
> - The high quality of his wares, which include all manner of clothing accessories.
> - His negative opinions of (and history with) House Salva.
> - Scant observations on Varinna.
> - Scant observations on Falar.
>
> A few specific dialogue options for Servio are provided below.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About House Salva?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Contemptible rogues, the lot of them. I had the audacity to challenge their preposterous new designs last season, and it seems the revocation of my license was the punishment. In perpetuity, no less!
> >
> > If you ask me, that street mural everyone's talking about is spot on. We've very little to gain from the Trading Houses, and everything to lose in return.
> >
> > Speaking of which, I **am** losing precious time (and potential sales) with this conversation. Perhaps a new hat will lighten your mood?

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About Varinna?
>
> **A:**
>
> > I've never met this Varinna, but her artwork is popular with the locals here. She has a Stoneside studio just around the bend, if I'm not mistaken.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About Falar?
>
> **A:**
>
> > The name doesn't ring a bell.
> >
> > A paintmonger, you say? I understand there's a paint shop southwest of here on the docks; but fine art is not my area of purview. Can I interest you in some clothing accessories instead?
