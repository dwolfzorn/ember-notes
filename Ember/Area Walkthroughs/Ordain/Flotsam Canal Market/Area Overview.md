# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> The [[Flotsam Canal Market]] Area Map depicts a notable subsection of the [[Flotsam]] district's famed marketplace. It is featured in the [[Mixed Media]] and [[A Sketchy Situation]] Events of the [[Local Color]] Side Quest.
>
> - This map depicts three Scene Levels, each representing a different architectural tier of the market.
> - The area is a popular destination for working-class patrons in search of food, leisure, art supplies, and more.
> - The party arrives near [[Dockwood Books]].

## Gameplay Details

The areas of Flotsam Canal Market have the following features unless specified otherwise in the text.

### Levels & Elevation

The Flotsam Canal Market Scene features a total of three vertical Levels of elevation, including:

- **Dockside:** The main waterfront of the area (which includes the wooden docks themselves, the first floor interiors of buildings on the docks, and the westernmost section of the connected stone walkway), is set to an elevation of 0.
- **Stoneside:** This central series of winding ramps and terraces is 20 feet high.
- **Promenade:** The easternmost section of the map, which depicts a stony promenade lined with typical Ordani edifices, is 40 feet high.

The water surface of the area's eponymous canal is set to an elevation of -6, and is approximately 40 feet deep.

### Sound

Sound in the area is full of white noise, including the constant lapping of water in the canal, and the chatter of idle patrons and market barkers during business hours.

Wisdom (Perception) checks made to hear something have **-2 Banes** throughout the market exteriors.

### Illumination

Most of the interiors and exteriors of the Flotsam Canal Market are illuminated by lanterns, which radiate bright light for 15 feet and dim light for an additional 15 feet. Alternately, throughout the first-floor interior section of [[The Bread Barge]], most light sources radiate dim light for a total of 15 feet.

Additionally, the exterior sections of the map are illuminated by the light of the sun during the day, and by the light of the moons at night (with visibility dependent on current weather conditions).

### Terrain

Aside from the waterway of the canal, the terrain here is manmade and considered normal terrain unless otherwise indicated (such as the difficult terrain sections of [[Merchant's Quay]] or the [[Cluttered Dock]]).

The wooden timbers of Dockside are aged and weather-worn, but the people of Ordain keep them sanded with regular maintenance. The cobble- and flagstone walkways of the Stoneside area and the Promenade include the same white stone typical to the rest of Ordain, built upon the tiered earthen foundations common to the area.

### Inhabitants

The following characters and creatures inhabit this area:

- The Flotsam Canal Market is usually occupied by an array of various [[Ordani]] and [[Arcturian]], with a higher concentration of visitors from nearby Flotsam and other districts during daytime hours.
- Important characters here include [[Falar]], [[Varinna]], [[Merchant's Quay]], and a variety of shopkeepers.

### Enemies

During the [[A Sketchy Situation]] Event of the [[Local Color]] Side Quest, [[Falar's Studio]] is occupied by a group of antagonistic [[Paint Globlin]].
