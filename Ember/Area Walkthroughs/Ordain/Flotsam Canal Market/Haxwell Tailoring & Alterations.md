# Haxwell Tailoring & Alterations

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A tiny brass bell above the doorway ring-a-ding-dings as you step across the threshold into this tidy little tailor shop, where a sharp-eyed seamstress busies herself close to the floor, making acute adjustments upon a garment-draped sewing mannequin. She stands from her crouched position to welcome you with a professional smile and a tilt of her head.
>
> > Welcome to Haxwell Tailoring. I'm Phoebe. How can I help you?

This Dockside tailor shop is operated by the skilled seamstress Phoebe Haxwell, who inherited the century-old business from her great aunt Vernita Haxwell, and is considered to be the best couturier in Flotsam.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with the Shopkeep
>
> Phoebe sells all manner of garments and accessories, and is always eager to take a custom order for clothing. Her alteration services cost a total of 2 gp per hour. For a full list of the shop's current inventory, see "Exploring the Shop" below.
>
> **Phoebe Haxwell (Chaotic Good, Ordani Human, she/her)**
>
> This pragmatic seamstress is the proprietor of Haxwell Tailoring & Alterations, and is extremely proud of the critically-acclaimed history of her family's multigenerational business. Phoebe makes her own clothes by hand, and always has a compliment in store for a new acquaintance.
>
> Although she's a law-abiding citizen, Phoebe isn't in the habit of providing sensitive information to those she doesn't seem worthy. Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 15)** or **Diplomacy (DC 15)** check is able to convince Phoebe to share some insights with the party.
>
> - **An "investment" of 50 gp**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Attunement: Heart of Ember**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Crime**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Path: Lunar Empath**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Conversation topics with Phoebe include (but are not limited to) the following:
>
> - Errant small-talk and gossip she's picked up from customers.
> - What she knows of the abstract artist [[Varinna]] and her associates.
> - Her relatively positive opinions of [[House Salva]].
> - What little she knows of the paintmonger [[Falar]] and his [[Paints]].
>
> A few specific dialogue options for Phoebe are provided below.
>
> If the party has completed the [[Outside the Lines]] Outcome, Phoebe will buy the [[Clothes, Exquisite]] they received as a reward for full price.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About Varinna?
>
> **A:**
>
> > She does good work. It's a bit abstract for my own tastes, but it's bold — which would explain how she caught the eye of House Salva. I hear they recently added her to their roster of artists. She deserves the recognition, and the Trading House could certainly use a little shakeup when it comes to style.
> >
> > Speaking of shakeups, Varinna had a falling-out with her best friend Seylu a while back, but they seem to have patched things up; which is good, because Varinna barely leaves the house. I'll never understand the mind of a tortured artist myself.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Who's Seylu?
>
> **A:**
>
> > Seylu Koine is a produce vendor with a stall on the Merchant's Quay nearby. She runs her stall most days, with lunch breaks here and there, and an early quitting time. Once in a while we'll all grab a mug of ale and a bowl of stale olives at The Parched Lark on the Promenade, but it's been a few moons.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Regarding House Salva?
>
> **A:**
>
> > As far as Trading Houses go, it's nice to have one that caters to the family business. Some of my finest work has come about thanks to the enchanted textiles of Nix Salvar's studios. That said, it would be nice to have a few more options when it comes to my choice of suppliers. Salva knows best, right?
> >
> > On that note, is anything in the shop rousing your interest? A cloak modeled after the esoteric styles of Signara, perhaps?

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About Falar?
>
> **A:**
>
> > The paintmonger? I don't know him very well, aside from his sour disposition. You can see his scowl from across the canal. His paint shop has been open for years, but there was this nice little tea house there when I was young. I miss that tea house …

In addition to speaking with Phoebe, the characters may peruse the shop's inventory.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Shop
>
> Any character who spends a few moments searching the shop is able to locate the following without much effort:
>
> - [[Weaver's Tools]]
> - [[Leatherworker's Tools]]
> - `[[/roll 1d3]]` sets of [[Fine Clothes]]
> - `[[/roll 1d6]]` sets of [[Traveler's Clothes]]
> - `[[/roll 2d4]]` elegant [[Costumes]] for a gala or ball.
>
> These items are also currently for sale. Removing this inventory from the shop without consent, however, will eventually lead to criminal prosecution if the characters are caught.
