# The Parched Lark

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A sun-bleached wooden sign hangs above the front door of this small edifice. It proudly reads "The Parched Lark" atop the caricature of a fowl in bard's clothing swilling a bottle of ale. The clinking of glass mugs and the ambling din of idle chatter can be heard beyond the establishment's frosted windows, and the earthy smells of delicious alcoholic brews waft upon the salty canal air.

This dingy little alehouse is a favorite among Flotsam locals, who are known to soak up the modest tavern's shade and libations while escaping the waterway sun.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with the Barkeep
>
> **Sigurd Halfspear (Chaotic Good, Ordani Vrjnhar, he/him)**
>
> Known for his Oaken tattoos and a tendency to excitedly (and humorously) lose his place during conversation, this verbose barkeep has an ale for every mood.
>
> Conversation topics include (but are not limited to) the following:
>
> - Aside from the alehouse, [[Merchant's Quay]] is a fine place for gathering information about locals of the Flotsam Canal Market.
> - [[Varinna]] is a fine neighbor, nice enough, and has even put some of her old sketches up for sale. The party can ask to see these abstract studies, gleaning the same insight as when examining the art in her studio.
> - Observations on Falar, who is considered to be a bit of a lonely figure who's fallen out of favor with whatever audience he once had.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 13)** check is able to tell that Sigurd is an honest fellow, with nothing to hide other than the sob stories told to him in confidence by other patrons.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 13)** check while gathering information here is able to collect one of the following details from Sigurd or the alehouse patrons (roll `[[/gmroll 1d4]]` to determine):
>
> 1. [[The Bread Barge]] makes a mean cold cut sandwich and soup combo.
> 2. [[The Last Component]] has some curiously esoteric wares on its shelves.
> 3. [[Falar's Paints]] is gaining a reputation for their novel illusory paints.
> 4. [[Merchant's Quay]] is a terrific source of gossip and local rumors.

In addition to speaking with Sigurd, the characters may examine the alehouse.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Alehouse
>
> Any character who takes a moment to examine the art posted throughout the alehouse is able to notice [[Varinna's]] signature on most of the paintings, which are overtly abstract in style.
