# Aed's Threads

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Racks of clothing sit outside along the outer walls of this building, each one bearing a paper tag with a price and rough size. The front doors of Aed's Threads hang open, and beyond its threshold you can see an even larger collection of racks, each one as packed with second-hand clothing as the ones in front of you.
>
> Nearby, a Signaran fellow offers a wave as his ephemeral hands sort clothing onto a rack. He says:
>
> > Welcome, feel free to browse. If you're looking for anything specific, want to sell, or have questions, let me know.

Run by Aed, this second-hand clothing shop has a lot of good deals for anyone willing to dig through the racks. Everything is in decent shape, but there is nothing new or truly high-end on offer.

The party can sell the [[Clothes, Exquisite]] they received from Cherish as part of [[Outside the Lines]], but Aed will only offer coin equal to half their value.

> [!info] Social
> #### Conversation with the Shopkeep
>
> **Aed Weaver (Neutral Good, Ordani Signborn, he/him)**
>
> Aed is a life-long purveyor of fashion and has done his absolute best to clothe the fine people of Ordain for as long as he has lived. He is opinionated, loves to chat, and believes that everybody should do their best to help everyone else, so that no one suffers alone. He is a devout follower of the Cindaric tenets of unity, cooperation, and charity.
>
> Conversation topics with Aed include (but are not limited to) the following:
>
> - Aed is not a member of House Salva, and quite frankly never wants to be. He considers them to be antithetical to the good taste and creative spirit of the Ordani people. It is his very strong opinion that the way they hoard textiles, and gatekeep fashion is downright criminal.
> - He was training to become a fully robed Cindaric, but accidentally discovered his love of fashion and decided that he had a higher calling in Ordain: making sure everybody had access to fine clothing.
> - If asked about Varinna, he says that he knows her well enough, since she used to be a regular in his shop before she became a sellout. She and her friend Seylu used to come thrift here every week, but that stopped as soon as she got her vaunted art commission with House Salva.
> - If asked about [[Falar]], he says that he's had a couple run-ins with the man, and none of them have been pleasant. Though he is a snappy dresser, he's more sour than a sack of lemons. Aed also thinks he heard something about Falar being a retired member of the Anachraenum, but he's not sure where he heard that.

In addition to speaking with Aed, the characters may explore the shop.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Shop
>
> As long as a character is not worried about the quality or state of them, [[Traveler's Clothes]], [[Fine Clothes]], or a functional [[Unknown]] can be acquired here for half their normal price.
>
> Aed also sells a few sets of worn and stained [[Padded Armor]] and [[Leather Armor]] at half price. It's clear they've seen better days, but the deal can't be beaten.
