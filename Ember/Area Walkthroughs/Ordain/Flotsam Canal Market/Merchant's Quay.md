# Merchant's Quay

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The stone platform here is furnished with a handful of makeshift market stalls, which appear to offer everything from fresh produce to assorted gift-shop trinkets. And the ambitious vendors who operate these ramshackle booths go about their business with the gregarious demeanor of people who not only have a profit to turn, but a story to tell along with it.

This small dockside quay plays host to an assortment of vendors who lack a more permanent place to ply their wares. Despite a few fabric canopies and makeshift awnings, this modest jetty is mostly open to the elements, which communicates a more inviting and lively atmosphere as a result. If there's something a potential customer can't find elsewhere in the Flotsam Canal Market, Merchant's Quay is a great place to start.

If the characters are here for the [[Mixed Media]] or [[A Sketchy Situation]] Events of the [[Local Color]] Side Quest, one of the merchants has more information regarding the artist Varinna's alibi — her best friend, the produce vendor Seylu Koine.

### Merchants of the Quay

A handful of vendors use the quay as their regular place of business, and can usually be found here during normal shopping hours (and well into the evening). These merchants, their typical hours of operation, and their wares are detailed below — along with any information they might be able to share with the party.

> [!info] Social
> #### Seylu's Produce
>
> Business Hours: 8am to 5pm.
>
> **Seylu Koine (Neutral Good, Ordani Human, she/her)**
>
> This warm-hearted produce vendor sells fresh fruits and vegetables sourced from all corners of the region, and stoically operates her stall independently of House Lilifeld's influence. Seylu's crops are all sold at exceptionally reasonable prices, and she's considered to be a burgeoning community leader by her peers.
>
> If the characters engage Seylu in conversation, she'll readily share the following information:
>
> - Her produce is sourced from local growers who operate their businesses independently of [[House Lilifeld]].
> - She offers discounts for certain produce at the end of the work day. What she can't sell, she gives away to local vagrants.
>
> Other details are sparse, however, as her independence from House Lilifeld comes at the cost of constant precaution. Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 13)** or **Diplomacy (DC 13)** check is able to convince Seylu to share a few personal or noteworthy details.
>
> - **Culture: Ordani**: The character automatically succeeds on this check.
> - **Attunement: Heart of Ember**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Crime**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Intrigue**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Knowledge: Trade**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Path: Lunar Empath**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Conversation topics with personal or noteworthy details that Seylu is willing to discuss include (but are not limited to):
>
> - Her negative opinions of the Trading Houses.
> - What little she knows of [[Falar]], who runs a [[Paint Shop]] just east of here.
> - Her opinion of her old friend [[Varinna]], and their idealogical differences regarding Ordain's Trading Houses.
> - Varinna's alibi: she was having lunch with Seylu at the time.
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Deception (DC 13)** check can readily detect the honesty that is an essential part of Seylu's character, and has no reason to doubt her various anecdotes and accounts.
>
> A few specific dialogue options for Seylu are provided below.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About the Trading Houses?
>
> **A:**
>
> > I'm not a fan of their expansionist agendas and the control they seem to exert over nearly every aspect of life here. But I do what I can to make a difference in my own way, even if it's a simple matter of making sure some Arcturian farmers can get their crops to market.
> >
> > Speaking of which, have you tried these moonshine melons?

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About Falar?
>
> **A:**
>
> > The old curmudgeon doesn't bother with niceties when he comes and goes. His paint shop behind us is open most days, and he spends a lot of evenings in his studio space across the way. But he isn't much of a conversationalist unless you're buying something.
> >
> > From what I understand, he also doesn't paint much these days. Capitalism can crush your soul, man.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** About Varinna?
>
> **A:**
>
> Seylu stifles a bit of a knowing smile.
>
> > We've known each other since we were kids. She's a lot to handle, but she means the world to me. I was pretty disappointed when I found out she's hooked up with House Salva, but I suppose a starving artist has to actually eat every once in a while.
> >
> > Her friendship means more to me than politics. But we can have our moments.

> [!question] Q&A
> **Q:** Varinna's Alibi?
>
> **A:**
>
> Seylu's brow furrows with incredulousness.
>
> > There's no chance Varinna is responsible for those murals. I was with her in her studio that day. She gets so obsessed with her work, she can't even be trusted to take care of herself. So I took her some potato and leak soup to make sure she didn't wither away into some oblong shape, like one of those feisty ladies in her paintings.
>
> She smiles at the thought of the erotic abstract paintings in Varinna's studio.
>
> > And it's a damn good soup recipe, if I do say so.

> [!info] Social
> #### Scooter Geary's Discount Tools
>
> Business Hours: 8am to 7pm.
>
> **Scooter Geary (Chaotic Neutral, Ordani Hulg'run, he/him)**
>
> An intrepid adventurer on the weekends, Scooter Geary is one of Flotsam's most beloved scoundrels. His Merchant's Quay stall caters to rogues and dungeoneers, and has nearly anything an explorer might need to replenish their inventory before their next jaunt.
>
> Scooter's wares on any given day include at least one of each item from the [[Unknown]] and [[Unknown]] equipment lists, along with a wide array of standard [[Unknown]].
>
> Conversation topics with Scooter include (but are not limited to) the following:
>
> - Errant small-talk and rumors he's picked up from customers.
> - His somewhat neutral opinions about Ordain's Trading Houses.
> - He has a bit of a crush on Seylu, but this adoration is unrequited (and he's fine with that).
> - Observations on Falar.
> - Observations on Varinna.
>
> Scooter is an open book, with nothing to hide (other than the details of his next dungeon crawl). Any character who succeeds on a **Deception (DC 12)** check can assess his honesty with ease.

> [!info] Social
> #### Yorm's Freshwater Grill
>
> Business Hours: 7am to 9pm.
>
> **Narius Yorm (Lawful Neutral, Arcturian Kivahr, he/him)**
>
> This chronically-relaxed fisherman runs a humble grill at the center of the quay, which he dutifully stokes throughout the day while offering a delicious selection of local freshwater fish. A relative expert on the local waterways and a man of few words, Yorm has an absurdly brief tale to spin about every fish he's ever caught.
>
> Yorm's wares on any given day include a modest selection of seasoned freshwater fish, caught that day by the chef himself, piping hot from the grill (count as Poor or Modest &Reference[food]{Meals}, depending on the quality of fish).
>
> Conversation topics with Yorm include (but are not limited to) the following:
>
> - Errant small-talk and rumors he's picked up from customers.
> - His neutral opinions about Ordain's Trading Houses.
> - Observations on Falar.
> - Observations on Varinna.
>
> Any character who succeeds on a **Deception (DC 13)** check can tell that Yorm is being forthcoming and honest with his responses.

Characters who explore the quay may find something mysterious:

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Quay
>
> Any character who makes a successful **Awareness (DC 15)** check while searching the area finds a forgotten  **1** lodged beneath one of the planks atop the quay, stamped with the seal of Naloria Vale — the domain of the [[Casia]] known as [[Vesper]].
>
> - **Ancestry: Kiska**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
> - **Attunement: Primordis**: The character gains **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> Mysteriously, this coin (or another just like it), reappears in the exact same spot on the quay whenever one of Ember's moons reaches its zenith. Only one of these curious [[Primordis]] coins is present at any given time, and the cause of this phenomenon remains an undiscovered mystery (much like the coin itself).
