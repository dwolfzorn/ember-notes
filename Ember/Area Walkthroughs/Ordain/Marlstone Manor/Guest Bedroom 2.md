# Guest Bedroom 2

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Guest Bedroom 2
>
> Access to Guest Bedroom 2 is **restricted** at all times. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details. If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A fairly generic guest bedroom, which is much less lush that Hephiss' and appears a bit claustrophobic.

### Items In Storage

If the party has been detained in Guest Bedroom 1, all of their inventory has been placed in Guest Bedroom 2.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Retrieving Seized Items
>
> The room can be entered from the makeshift stairway on the West Wraparound Balcony, or via the door. If entered via the door, characters must either succeed on a `[[/check stealth 17]]` check to enter without any of the party guests knowing or on a `[[/check deception 17]]` check to convince the strangers that they have good reason to go into the off-limites room.
