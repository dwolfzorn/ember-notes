# Art Studio

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Art Studio
>
> Access to Art Studio is **restricted** at all times. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details. If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Easels have been haphazardly scattered around the room, and between them, flecks of paint look as if they have been thrown from one side of the room to the other. The paintings themselves all seem to be of the same three subjects over and over again - a metallic monster with waving tentacles, Hephiss herself, and an oddly coherent beam of light.
