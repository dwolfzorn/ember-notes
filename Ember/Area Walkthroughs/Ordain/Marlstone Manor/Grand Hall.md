# Grand Hall

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Grand Hall
>
> The Grand Hall is open to all at all times. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details. If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

### Pre-Gala

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As the crowning achievement of Marlstone Manor, the decorative stone pillars, large sloping staircase, and intricate wall panels will be the centerpiece of the Marlstone Gala, held every year beneath its high arched ceiling. Now, though, the rooom is urrently very much a work in progress, with flower clippings, bits of ribbon, and other decorative pieces spread across the floor between the hard-working decorators. Well, mostly hard working. One woman appears to be mostly watching while pretending to get work done.

The woman in question is the decorator Erisa Wandren, who the party can speak to in [[Casing the Joint]]

### During The Gala

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The soft tones of the music, balls of lights dangling from a lattice high above, and flowers, which almost overflow the boxes they sit in along the landing above in a an explosion of color, combine to create a sense of magic in the grand hall - somehow it seems even larger than it did before the gala, and yet there is a sense of welcome and camaraderie that floods the room.
