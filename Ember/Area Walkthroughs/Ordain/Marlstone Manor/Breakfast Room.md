# Breakfast Room

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Breakfast Room
>
> Access to the Breakfast Room varies. See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.
>
> - Before the Gala: **Open** to Kitchen Staff, Decorators, **Restricted** to Gardeners
> - During the Gala: **Restricted** to Guests
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details.
>
> - Funar Cevher is **nearby.**

### Pre-Gala

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Light pours into the room from its bay window, highlighting the fine furnishings and plush rug, but most of the staff in the area are using it as little more than a throughway between the kitchen and the rest of the manor.

### At the Gala

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> In the evening light, the Breakfast Room are dim and still, its large window providing little in the way of illumination. From the dimly lit tables and benches, muffled sounds of activity and vague shouts about food and service can be heard through the thick wooden door.
