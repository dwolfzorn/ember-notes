# Dining Room

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Dining Room
>
> Access to the Dining Room is **open to all** at all times (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details. If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **adjacent to this room** (in a room directly above, below, or next to it)**.**
>
> - With an additional charge of the rod, the party can determine that Funar is **below** the dining room.

### Pre-Gala

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Though the largely empty wooden dining table that dominaates the room looks far from ready to host any kind of formal meal, staff have clearly been assigned to make the room presentable, whether they're the gardeners adding floral displays to the side tables, kitchen staff polishing glasses from a side cabinet, or decorators ensuring that all sconces and candelabras are lit.

### At The Gala

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Soft light from the room's candles flickers across a mostly-empty room, dancing against the polished surface of the empty dining table and suffusing the leaves of the flowers on the room's side tables.
