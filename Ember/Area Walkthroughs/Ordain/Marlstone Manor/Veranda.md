# Veranda

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to the Veranda
>
> The Veranda is **open to all** at all times. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor. See [[Casing the Joint]] for details.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The long white stone veranda overlooks the Manor's well-manicured gardens and provides one of the best views of Ordain in the city. Wherever you stand on the walkway, Ordain's spires and white stone buildings draw the eye, in part due to the soft glow of the tiles, which have been designed to gently mimic the lighting of the sky above.
