# West Wraparound Balcony

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to West Wraparound Balcony
>
> Access to West Wraparound Balcony is **restricted** at all times. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details. If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This balcony extends around the western half of Marlstone Manor, giving those who live here the opportunity to stroll out and see a breathtaking view of Ordain.

This balcony can be used to reach the Greenhouse and Hephiss' Chambers, as well as the windows of Guest Bedroom 1 and Guest Bedroom 2.
