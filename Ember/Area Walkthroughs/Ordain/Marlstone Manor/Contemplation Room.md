# Contemplation Room

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Contemplation Room
>
> Access to the Contemplation Room is **restricted** at all times. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details. If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> While this may have once been a bedroom, it now seems designed for rest and contemplation.
