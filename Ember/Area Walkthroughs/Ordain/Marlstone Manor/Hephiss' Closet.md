# Hephiss' Closet

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Hephiss' Closet
>
> Access to Hephiss' Closet is **forbidden** at all times. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details. If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This closet holds clothes similar to those that you have seen Hephiss Wandren wearing in the past, but appears to have nothing else of interest.
