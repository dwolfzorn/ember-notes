# Guardhouse

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to the Guardhouse
>
> Access to the Guardhouse is **forbidden to all** at all times. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor. See [[Casing the Joint]] for details.

The guardhouse connects directly to Lantern Road through the[[Marlstone Manor Entrance]]. It is not significantly different before or during the gala.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Two guards sit inside a simple guardhouse, surrounded by nothing other than a simple table and chairs. They stare idly into the distance until the ring of a small chime from outside, when both rise from their seats and head into the gardens beyond the guard station, heading in opposite directions.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Fooling the Guards
>
> The party can convince the guards to go out of the guardhouse and into the gardens by duplicating the tone that signals them to go on duty.
>
> - With a successful `[[/check perception 16]]` check, characters hear the chime well enough to be able to duplicate it.
> - Characters will need a way to make the noise of the chime - with a successful `[[/check performance 16]]` check, characters can throw their voice and mimic the sound. They gain **+2 Boons** on this check if they use a musical instrument with which they are proficient. They may also use a spell that creates sound, such as [[Minor Illusion]] or [[Prestidigitation]].

> [!info] Social
> #### Persuading the Guards
>
> With a successful `[[/check deception 19]]` check, the party can claim that the guards have been called away to patrol elsewhere, or that the party is also made up of guards and will replace them. The guards will then leave, allowing characters to move through.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Fighting the Guards
>
> The guards in the guardhouse are [[Wandren Patroller]] dedicated to preventing the party from passing between Lantern Roads and Marlstone Manor - one guard will block the entrance opposite the one that the party entered through, using their [[Paralyzing Poison]]and [[Slowing Serum]] to stop the party from exiting quickly. If they are nearing death, they will throw [[Choking Fog Dust]] in the room, even though they will be affected as well.
>
> The other guard will go for help, though they will move at a normal pace so as not to alert the guests or staff about the problem. If they reach another guard before being subdued, the party is either kicked out (if scouting the manor) or detained in an upstairs room (if at the gala).
