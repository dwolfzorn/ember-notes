# Hephiss' Chambers

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Hephiss' Chambers
>
> Access to Hephiss' Chambers is **forbidden** at all times. See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details.
>
> - Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result within Marlstone Manor.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Delicate gray silks arch over a the bed in the center of the room - it, like the rug in the middle of the floor, the large mirror at the side of the room, and the dressing desk by the rear curtained window, is decorated with the patterns and colors that Hephiss Wandren is known for, and carries the faintest hint of the smoky perfume she's long been known to wear.

### Pre-Gala

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Lady of the House
>
> Hephiss is in her chambers during the events of [[Casing the Joint]]. If the party enters the room, she immediately demands to know why there are there.
>
> > What in the name of all that is — this room is strictly forbidden to staff. Who told you that you could come up here? And did you force your way in? I demand an explanation.
>
> The party (or a single character, if they have separated) must immediately succeed on a `[[/check persuasion 19]]` group check - if half or more of the group succeeds on the check, the party succeeds as a whole.
>
> - If the party succeeds on the check, Hephiss is not happy, but allows characters to return to their duties. Their suspicion level is raised by 1.
> - If the party fails the check, Hephiss has them escorted off the grounds immediately, ending this event.

### At The Gala

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### A Magical Panel
>
> The panel that must be pressed to reveal the door on the floor below is part of Hephiss' dressing table.
>
> - Characters who succeed on a `[[/check 15 perception]]` check or who have `[[/check 17 perception passive]]` note the large mirror on one side of the room, which has a note affixed to it - a copy of Hephiss' [[Gala Security To-Dos]]. On it, she has added a note - press after you dress! - and a few doodled curlicues.
>   - Characters who succeed on a `[[/check investigation 15]]` check know that the note indicates a panel in the bedroom that reveals the door.
>   - Characters who search the room and succeed on a `[[/check perception 17]]` check see that the panel on the desk has the same curlicue panel as was used in the note.
> - The panel is magically attuned and can be located using [[Detect Magic]] or similar spells.

If characters push the panel, it clicks loudly.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> With a resounding click, the panel depresses, and an image appears on its surface as if it were being carved into the patterns in the wood before your eyes - an ornate door in a small room, leading to parts unknown.
