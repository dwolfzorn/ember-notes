# Gameplay Details

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Gamemaster's Guide
>
> This area map is used ast least twice within the Disgraced House quest, once when the party scouts it in , and once during the Manor. As needed, rooms in the manor's area walkthrough may have a **Pre-Gala** section (used in [[Casing the Joint]]) and **At the Gala** section (used in [[The Marlstone Gala]]). If these are not indicated, the room remains the same for both events.

## Illumination

Lights in most rooms of Marlstone Manor are bright, though there are unoccupied rooms that may have dim lighting as noted.

## Terrain

All terrain within Marlstone Manor is normal terrain, unless otherwise noted.

## Elevation

Ceilings in Marlstone Manor are generally 40 feet high, unless otherwise noted.

## Manor Access

As the party moves through Marlstone Manor, they may have **Open**, **Restricted**, or **Forbidden** access to a room. Open rooms can be accessed without raising suspicion or requiring checks, unless the party is doing something that would independently raise suspicion, such as breaking into a locked safe.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### **Exploring Restricted Manor Rooms**
>
> **Restricted Rooms** are rooms that characters have no reason to be in. During scouting, this is dependent on the characters' role, while during the gala, it is generally static unless there is a special circumstance noted.
>
> Characters whose role is restricted from the room must succeed on a `[[/check stealth 17]]` check to sneak into the room.
>
> - If they succeed, they enter the room with no consequences.
> - If they fail, guards are summoned. They must succeed on either a `[[/check persuasion 17]]` check or a check related to the room (as noted on the area walkthrough) to explain themselves.
>   - If they succeed on the check, they are able to leave without consequence.
>   - If they fail the check, they gain a level of suspicion.
>   - If they fail the check by 3 or more, they gain an additional level of suspicion.
> - After failing a stealth check on a restricted room, the stealth DC for that room increases by 1. This penalty stacks.
>
> #### Exploring Forbidden Manor Rooms
>
> **Forbidden Rooms** are rooms that no characters have cause to be in, no matter what their role is. They operate similarly to restricted room, with the following differences:
>
> - Characters must succeed on a `[[/check stealth 19]]` check to sneak into the room.
> - A failure increases the stealth DC by 2.
>
> Characters also have a limited amount of time within the room. After 2 checks have been made within the room, a member of staff will enter to check on the room. Characters must immediately exit and make a `[[/save charisma 18]]` saving throw.
>
> - If the save is successful, they can leave without consequence.
> - If they fail the save, they gain a level of suspicion.
