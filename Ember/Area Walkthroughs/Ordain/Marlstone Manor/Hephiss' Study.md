# Hephiss' Study

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Hephiss' Study
>
> Access to Hephiss' Study is **forbidden** at all times.See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details.
>
> - Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** Note: this is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

There is no significant difference between Hephiss' Study before the gala and during the gala.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A guard stands at the edge of the well-appointed study, leaning against the wall in a stance of feigned relaxation that looks much less like a casual member of the staff and more like they are determined to keep any nosy visitor out.
>
> Beyond them, a large desk sits in the middle of the room. Hephiss' desk, most likely. There is enough of her signature black and gold decor and the smell of the smoky perfume she favors to make you nearly certain that this is her dedicated office.

### Entering The Study

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Evading the Guard
>
> This room is forbidden to outsiders. Characters that wish to cross the guard's line of sight must either succeed on a `[[/check stealth 19]]` check or persuade the guard that they are in the room for a reason with a successful `[[/check persuasion 18]]` check.
>
> Characters may increase their chances of stealth by distracting the guard - if any of the below succeed, the DC of the stealth check decreases by 2:
>
> - Using a cantrip or spell that creates noise or generates a light (such as [[Dancing Lights]], [[Minor Illusion]], or [[Prestidigitation]])
> - Throwing an item far enough away from them to draw the guard's attention with a successful `[[/check athletics 17]]` check.
> - Using an item that creates provides cover, shields their appearance, or clears the room (like [[Choking Fog Dust]] or [[Dust of Disappearance]]).
> - If an item or spell is chosen that does damage to the guard, they alert the rest of the staff and the guards become more watchful, raising the suspicion level for the entire party.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Confrontation with the Guard
>
> Guards in Marlstone Manor will avoid direct combat, fleeing to alert others and detain the party. At most, they will use their [[Choking Fog Dust]] to stop the party's movements while they leave in search of help.
>
> If the guard flees the room before being subdued, the party has been caught. If they are scouting the manor (in Casing the Joint), they are escorted from the manor and the event ends. If they are at the gala (in Marlstone Gala) they are brought to Guest Bedroom 2 and locked away.

### Searching the Study

Characters who have avoided or subdued the guards can explore the study, using the rules for [[Gameplay Details]]. They notice the large wall safe without a check.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Wall Safe
>
> If characters wish to open the wall safe:
>
> - The safe is secured with [[Arcane Lock]] - it cannot be opened unless the spell is suppressed (via [[Knock]]), disrupted (via [[Disintegrate]],[[Dispel Magic]], or similar), or bypassed using the password "Indigo," the name of Hephiss Wandren's favorite artist.
> - Once the Arcane Lock spell is suppressed or inactive, the safe can be opened with a successful `[[/check dexterity 19]]` check with [[Thieves' Tools]] or with [[Hephiss' Keys]].
>
> The wall safe's contents vary:
>
> - Before the Marlstone Gala, it contains the following:
>   - A coin box containing  **60**
>   - A vial of [[Withering Flesh Poison]]
>   - An[[Unlit Kynryth Torch]] (in truth, a torch belonging to the celestial creatures the Kynryth)
>   - A draft copy of the article found in [[Overheard In Ordain: A Stitch In Crime]] with additional details about the murder
> - During the Marlstone Gala, it contains all of the above **and** [[Juro Wandren's Ledger]]

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Hephiss' Desk
>
> Characters can look through the papers on Hephiss' Desk for anything of interest without a check. Most of the papers are daily ledgers and plans for the gala, with two exceptions:
>
> - A list of members of House Wandren. Several have been crossed out. Juro Wandren's name is on the list with a question mark next to it. Vitt Wandren's name is underlined.
> - [[Gala Security To-Dos]]. If the party sees this list before the party, only the first item has been checked off. If they see it at the party, all but the last item have been checked off.
