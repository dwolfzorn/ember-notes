# Staff Bathroom

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to the Staff Bathroom
>
> Access to the Staff Bathroom varies. See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.
>
> - Before the Gala: **Open** to Staff (Kitchen Staff, Decorators, and Gardeners)
> - During the Gala: **Restricted** to Guests, **Open** to Staff
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details.
>
> - Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result within Marlstone Manor.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Function dominates over form in the staff bathroom - the tiles are plain, the fixtures are basic, and the lighting feels somewhat harshly bright, as if someone is watching to make sure no one is taking anything they shouldn't be.

A search of the staff bathroom finds the cupboards bare. If there was anything to take here, it has long since been stolen.
