# Gallery

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access
>
> See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.
>
> - Before the Gala: **Open** to Decorators, Kitchen Staff; **Restricted** to Gardeners
> - During the Gala: **Open** to Guests
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details.
>
> - Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** Note: this is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

### Pre-Gala

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The benches in the center of the room have been set less for conversation than observation, centering around the large sculpture in the center of the room and a set of smaller paintings that line the walls.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Small Door
>
> There is a small door in the back of the room - characters who have access to the room can enter it without a check, finding the [[Gallery Storage Closet]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Paintings
>
> Hephiss has a number of paintings, most of Ordani landscapes, each with a note about her. Characters who spend 10 minutes examining the paintings and have either **Knowledge: Arts** or succeed on either a `[[/check insight 16]]` or `[[/check investigation 16]]` check to see their commonalities and themes have advantage on persuasion checks made while speaking with Hephiss.
>
> The paintings include the following:
>
> - **Streets of Virtue and Vice:** A series of three paintings depicting crowds walking through the streets of Ordain from the same vantage point - one done at sunrise, one at midday, and one at sunset. The note reads "Those who claim to be virtuous in the light are often something else when Lantyr looks away."
> - **On Plateaus:** A pair of paintings, one of a river in the Arctus Plateau and the other of a ship sailing the Destine River. The note reads "In the words of the brilliant artist Indigo Ray, pllateaus have their beauty, but they are always best left behind."
> - **Wanderings:** Portraits of various Ordani individuals, all in the middle of an action, from juggling balls to building Marlstone Manor. The note reads "Always in motion, always towards the top." Any character who succeeds on a `[[/check history 16]]` check, or who has **Knowledge: Trade** knows that these are past members of House Wandren.

### During The Gala

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Lights on the wall spotlight the paintings on easels beneath them, drawing the eye towards them and away from the large sculpture in the center of the room. Under each painting, a small sign reads "Art by Hephiss Wandren." In the back corner of the room, a rope has been set up, calling attention and blocking access to the door that sits behind it.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Small Door
>
> There is a small door in the back of the room, blocked by a rope meant to dissuade guests from reaching it.
>
> - Characters can open the door without being seen with a successful `[[/check stealth 17]]` check, leading them into the [[Gallery Storage Closet]].
>   - On a failure, they are shooed away from the door. They can repeat the check, but the DC increases by 2 per check.
>   - After the third failure, they are escorted from the room and gain 1 point of suspicion. They cannot retry the check.
