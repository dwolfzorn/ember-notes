# Gallery Storage Closet

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> While there is nothing out of the area among the cleaning tools found in the closet, there is a thickness to the air here that seems out of place, as if something is humming on a frequency you can't quite hear.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Examining the Closet
>
> Characters can make the following checks while in the [[Gallery]] closet:
>
> - With a successful `[[/check 18 perception]]` check, characters have the sense that this feeling comes from the wall at the back of the closet, but there is no discernible reason for it to exist.
> - A [[Detect Magic]] spell finds nothing within the closet, but is blocked by the wall at the back of the closet and cannot penetrate beyond it.
> - With a successful `[[/check arcana 18]]` check or **Knowledge: Cosmology**, party members know that this feeling of unease can come from the use of powerful magic from the Inner Realms around Ember.
