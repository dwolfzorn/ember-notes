# Kitchen

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Kitchen
>
> Access to the Kitchen varies. See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.
>
> - Before the Gala: **Open** to Kitchen Staff, Decorators, **Restricted** to Gardeners
> - During the Gala: **Restricted** to Guests
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details.
>
> - Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result within Marlstone Manor.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The air is full of the smells of a good meal, already wafting up from the many prep tables filled with vegetables, meats, and herbs. Cooks move between these prep stations, the cook fire, and the many dishes that will serve Marlstone Manor's guests tonight, focused on their tasks as they move from folding one thing to stirring another. Between the cooks, barrels filled with varied liquids are being added to cookpots on one side of the kitchen and poured into glasses to be served to guests on the other.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Entrances and Exits
>
> With a successful `[[/check perception 17]]` check, characters notice the top edge of a door to the gardens in the back of the kitchen. If they choose to go in the direction of the door, they must succeed on a `[[/check stealth 18]]` check to avoid catching the chef's eyes. Characters without an Apron of Many Pockets have **-2 Banes** on this check.
>
> - With a success, characters make their way to the door, which is unlocked by jammed shut. Characters can open it with a successful `[[/check athletics 16]]` check to force it open by using strength or a successful `[[/check sleightofhand 16]]` check to jimmy it open. Once opened in this way, it remains ajar, providing a direct route between the gardens and the kitchen.
> - With a failure, the chef stops the character and asks them to quickly chop some vegetables at the back corner before "going on their break." Characters can attempt to talk their way out of the task, succeeding on a `[[/check persuasion 16]]` check. If they fail to convince the chef, they must either complete the task or leave the kitchen.
>   - To chop the vegetables, characters need to succeed on a `[[/check sleightofhand 15]]` check. On a failure, they cut themselves, taking `[[/check 1d4 slashing damage]]`.
