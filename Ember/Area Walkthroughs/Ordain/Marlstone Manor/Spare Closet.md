# Spare Closet

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Spare Closet
>
> Access to West Wraparound Balcony varies. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> - **Pre-Gala:** Open to All
> - **At the Gala:** Open to Guests, Restricted to Staff
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details. If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A closet that appears to be little used, though it has been cleared to hold overflow from the night's gala.
