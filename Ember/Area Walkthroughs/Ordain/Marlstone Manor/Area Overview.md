# Area Overview

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Area Map Context
>
> This area map showcases the grounds and interior of the two-level Marlstone Manor, both before and during the annual Marlstone Gala.
>
> The map appears in two events:
>
> - [[Casing the Joint]], which gives the party the opportunity to get the lay of the land and determine the missing Funar Cevher's location while posing as staff planning for the gala.
> - [[The Marlstone Gala]], where they attempt to pull off a rescue of Funar Cevher in the middle of the gala by using their access as guests.
>
> Physical differences in the map between the two events are minor and generally restricted to decor and the number and type of guests present, but gameplay may differ based on what is present and available to the party.
>
> This map is adjacent to the Lantern Roads map, which depicts the nearby Lantern Roads neighborhood and the secret dungeon underneath the manor.

## Gazetteer Entry

The most prominent mansion in Marlstone due to its impressive height, Marlstone Manor has had other names over the years as it passed between different owners, but most eventually fell on hard times. Local superstition says that naming it anything other than Marlstone Manor brings bad luck to the owners. The manor is famous for its glass panels, which avoid fully blocking the views of any other building while catching the eye with their impressive water features. The building also plays host, every year, to the Marlstone Gala, an event where the powerful and influential mix and mingle while showing off the latest fashions.
