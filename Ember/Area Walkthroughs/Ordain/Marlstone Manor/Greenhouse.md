# Greenhouse

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Greenhouse
>
> Access to the Greenhouse varies. See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.
>
> - Before the Gala: **Restricted** to Gardeners and Decorators, **Forbidden** to Kitchen Staff
> - During the Gala: **Restricted** to Guests
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details.
>
> - Funar Cevher is **nearby.**

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The plants here are of a wider variety than those showcased elsewhere in the manor from their rainbow of vibrant colors to the differing shapes of their leaves and petals - some feather-like, some resembling large bells, and others like stars bursting forth from the ground. Though the air is still and quiet, a bag and tools on a bench in the center of the garden look as if they have only been resting there for a moment.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Gardens
>
> Characters can open the gardening bag and explore the garden without a check
>
> - If they look in the bag, they find a book of flowers, titled **Beauty in Bloom: Floral Notes from Near and Far.** While the book is readable, some of its pages appear more well-read than others. With a successful `[[/check perception 17]]` check, characters can identify a page on Lanterelles as the ones that are the most worn.
> - If they explore the garden, they can note which plants are most dominant and getting the most love with a successful `[[/check nature 17]]` check or **Knowledge: Plants**, identifying the most well-loved plant as the Lanterelle.
> - If the party has seen Lanterelles elsewhere on the grounds, they can find the name of the flower in the Beauty in Bloom book or on a label on the flower bush, without needing a check.
