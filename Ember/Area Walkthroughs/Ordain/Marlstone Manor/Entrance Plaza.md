# Entrance Plaza

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Entrance
>
> The Entrance Plaza is **open to all** at all times. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details. If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

### Pre-Gala

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A mosaic of tiles at the edge of the road marks the entrance of the plaza that leads into Marlstone Manor. A couple of gardeners tend to the abundant red grasses and flowers nearby, evening some out in preparation for the upcoming event and clipping others to be displayed inside, while a pair of decorators work on straightening out a decorative fabric awning.

The workers in the front are intent on their work - there is nothing else to find here, and characters will be urged to go inside to speak to Jalla Difae if they need anything. In addition, the barriers around the sides of Marlstone Manor are impassable.

### At The Gala

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Now lit for a party, the entrance plaza seems to glow and the faint sounds of music and conversation float through the air from inside. Guests occasionally come and go, but remain intent on their own thoughts and pay you no mind.

There is nothing of particular interest to be found in the front entrance plaza. The barriers around the sides of Marlstone Manor remain impassable.
