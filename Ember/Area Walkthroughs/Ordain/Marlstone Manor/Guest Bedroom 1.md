# Guest Bedroom 1

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Access to Guest Bedroom 1
>
> Access to Guest Bedroom 1 is **restricted** at all times. (See [[Gameplay Details]] for more on access levels.)
>
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> See [[Casing the Joint]] for details. If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **in the vicinity.** This is the baseline result when using the Locator Rod in and around Marlstone Manor.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A generic-looking bedroom, though oddly it has only high and narrow windows, as if it was designed to hold things in instead of giving guests a way to look out.

### Makeshift Holding Cell

If the party gains too much suspicion during the Marlstone Gala (as noted in [[The Marlstone Gala]]), they will be brought to this room to be held until after the gala ends, with their equipment brought separately to [[Guest Bedroom 2]].

This clears their suspicion level, bringing them back to no suspicion.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Getting Out of the Guest Bedroom: The Windows
>
> The Guest Bedroom is subject to an anti-magic field - spells do not work within its boundaries.
>
> Though the windows are towards the ceiling. characters can attempt to reach the windows and climb out. Each window is located 25 feet off of the ground.
>
> - Characters who succeed on a `[[/check investigation 16]]` check while looking around the room can see that the desks or table can be used to get further up and out of the window.
> - Characters who are able to get high enough, via the ability to fly, or clumbcan climb out of the window successfully by succeeding on a `[[/check acrobatics 17]]` check. Only characters of size Small or smaller are small enough to fit through and can make the attempt.
>   - If the check is failed, the character tumbles through the window and lands outside on the balcony, taking `[[/damage 1d6 bludgeoning]]` and `[[/damage 1d6 slashing]]` damage.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Getting Out of the Guest Bedroom: The Door
>
> The room in which the party is restrained is not far from where guests at the party are enjoying a quiet conversation.
>
> - With a successful `[[/check perception 17]]` check, characters can clearly hear the sounds of talking on the other side of the door.
> - With a successful `[[/check strength 17]]` check, characters can bang on the door loudly enough for the guests outside to hear them.
>   - With a success of 3 or more, characters break through the door lock and the door swings open into the hallway.
> - Once characters have made it through the door, the guests around them will question their presence. Characters can convince the guests that they are there for good reason with a `[[/check persuasion 16]]` check. If they fail the check, one of the guests informs the guards that they are snooping around, and their newly cleared suspicion level raises to low.
