# Terrace Gardens: Nature's Glory

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The plants on this rooftop smell less floral and more herbal, and their colors while beautiful, are somewhat muted. Though they don't grow particularly tall, any attempts to see Lantern Roads from here are blocked either by the rock to the side or the taller and brighter flowers on the adjacent rooftop.
>
> A sign on the garden reads:
>
> > Not all beauty is worn on the outside. These herbs are favorites not just because of the way they look, but also what they can do.

Characters wishing to take the rooftop route to the Northern section of Lantern Roads may continue along the walkway towards [[Lookout Point]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Fresh Herbs
>
> Characters can learn the following about the plants in this section of Terrace Gardens. Any character with **Knowledge: Plants** has an **+2 Boons** on all checks.
>
> - The herbs in this section of the garden can be used for healing or for poison. With a successful `[[/check nature 17]]` check, characters can identify which is which. If they fail on the check, any attempt to take herbs from the garden results in them grabbing a poisonous plant and taking `[[/damage 1d6 poison]]` damage from the contact poison.
> - Characters who can identify the herbs can remove them safely with a successful `[[/check 16 sleightofhand]]` check. Any character with proficiency with an Herbalism Kit can use the herbs to create 1 Healing Potion or dose of antitoxin.
