# Weapons Cache

### Outside of the building

The building is locked.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Entering the Building
>
> Characters can enter the front door by either picking the lock, breaking the door down, or using the [[Brigade Key]].
>
> - To pick the lock, they must succeed on a `[[/check dexterity 18]]` check with Thieves' Tools.
> - To break down the door, they must succeed on a `[[/check strength 18]]` check. If they use a tool, like an axe or pickaxe, this check is made with **+2 Boons**.
>   - The sound of breaking down the door will catch the attention of all Beacon Brigade members within 100 feet.
> - The Brigade Key can be used without a check.

### Inside of the building

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The building is small, but packed to the brim with weapons. Most are poorly forged pieces of steel, but there are plenty of them.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Searching the Weapons Cache
>
> If searched, the party can find the following without a check:
>
> - Several [[Low-Quality Daggers]]
> - Several [[Low-Quality Shields]]
> - Several [[Empty Vials]]
>
> If the party searches more, they can find:
>
> - A single [[Withering Dagger]]. The poison and dagger size match the weapon that killed Darius Cevher, and the handle is engraved with the letters BB.
>
> This can be used as evidence that the Beacon Brigade killed Darius Cevher. Mark the appropriate outcome in [[Revealed by Lantern]].
