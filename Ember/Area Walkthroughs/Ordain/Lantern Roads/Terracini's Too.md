# Terracini's Too

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A single couple sits in the small rooftop cafe, having a hushed but intense conversation, while occasionally glancing across to the sentries on a nearby roof. The sentries stare off into the distance, seemingly paying the customers — and you — no mind.

The sentries on [[Vitt's Balcony]] will not react to the party unless they take a hostile action against the Terracini customers or the sentries themselves.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Listening In
>
> Characters can eavesdrop on the nearby conversation with a combined stealth and perception check.
>
> - With a successful `[[/check insight check]]` or **Knowledge: Intrigue**, the party has a general sense that their conversation is about trying to sneak into Beacon Brigade territory.
> - With a successful `[[/check stealth 15]]` check, parties can get close enough to the couple to hear a small amount of their conversation. They will need a `[[/check perception 18]]` check to listen in clearly.
>   - If the party succeeds on their stealth check by 3 or more, they only need a `[[/check perception 16]]` check to hear clearly.
>
> The couple is having a conversation about whether they should try to find out more about the Beacon Brigade by sneaking across to the other side of the neighborhood.
>
> > They claim they just want to use our store to hold a few things, but I'm not sure it's quite so innocent. I say we go over there and learn more about them. I'm pretty sure all we have to do is bring a lamp to that secondhand store and they'll let us through.
