# Storage Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Boxes lay scattered across the floor of the room. Dust and spiderwebs give the sense that they haven't been touched in quite some time.

If the party encounters this room while exploring Lantern Roads, read or summarize the following:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you make your way through the boxes, you can feel a slight hum in the air that you can't quite place the origin or direction of.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Investigating the Room
>
> Characters who have **Knowledge: Machines** or who succeed on a `[[/check perception 17]]` check to listen more closely can tell that there is a machine or gears working in the distance.
>
> Characters who succeed on a `[[/check investigation 17]]` check can tell that the boxes have been specifically arranged to allow for a direct route to the back of the room.
>
> If characters make it to the back of the room, they find the door to the [[Training Room]]. This door cannot be opened.

If the party encounters this room while leaving Wandren HQ, they can open the door and move through it without a check.
