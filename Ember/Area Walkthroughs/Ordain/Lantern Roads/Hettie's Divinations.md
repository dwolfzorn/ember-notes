# Hettie's Divinations

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Getting to the North of Lantern Roads
>
> - **Shadowbox Alleys:** Characters can pursue the Shadowbox Alleys path to Beacon Brigade territory by searching the vicinity of the shop. An entrance to the Beacon Brigade side of town can be found by going past or through Hettie's Divinations.
>
> There is no information about the rooftop route or bluffing in this area.

### Outside Of The Shop

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The shadows in the window of the shop are constantly in motion, creating one constellation and then another, before spelling out the words Hettie's Divinations.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Trapped Alleyway
>
> The narrow alleyway between Hettie's Divinations and Ordani Imports contains a spike trap coated with Paralyzing Poison and rigged to alarm the Beacon Brigade.
>
> - Characters who succeed on a `[[/check perception 17]]` check can detect the trap beneath the wooden floorboards.
> - The trap can be disarmed by succeeding on a `[[/check 18 sleightofhand]]` check.
> - If the trap cannot be disarmed, or a party member steps on it without disarming it, spikes shoot up from the ground.
>   - Anyone who has stepped on the trap must make a `[[/save dexterity 17]]` saving throw.
>   - On a failure, they take `[[/damage 1d10 piercing]]` damage and are inflicted with Paralyzing Poison.
>   - On a success, they take half damage. In addition, the trap triggers an alarm bell that alerts any members of the Beacon Brigade within 60 feet.

### Inside Of The shop

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The smell of incense wafts towards you as you make your way inside the shop. It is neat and tidy and appears to be in fairly regular use, lit by various small candles and a glowing crystal ball in the center of a ceremonial table, but at the moment the chairs around it are empty.

If this is the party's first time entering the shop:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> No sooner have you stepped inside the front door than a voice calls out from around a corner.
>
> > Vitt, I'm a diviner, not a miracle worker. You'll have your money next week, same as —
>
> The voice cuts off as a Kiska woman appears from around the corner.
>
> > Oh, I'm sorry. You're not…forget all of that. Please. How may I help you look into your future today?

> [!info] Social
> #### A Reading From Hettie
>
> Hettie (N female kiska) will speak with the party, but only if a member of the party sis down for a reading with her.
>
> - She prefers to be paid in "interesting items" than cash - an item is considered "interesting" if it is worth 2 GP or more, or if it is an item of uncommon rarity or rarer.
> - She will accept 20 GP  **-20** but anyone who pays in cash has **-2 Banes** on persuasion checks.
> - With a successful `[[/check persuasion 26]]` check, she will give the reading for free.
>
> |  | #### Hettie's Divinations |
> | --- | --- |
> | 1 | #### Take Care - Bad Luck Waits Around The Corner  The character has disadvantage on their next skill check. |
> | 2 | #### You have inner strength beyond what you know.  The character gains an additional +1 to their next damage roll. |
> | 3 | #### You understand more than you think you do.  The character has advantage on their next 2 Insight checks. |
> | 4 | #### You will get through a tough situation by the skin of your teeth.  The next time the character rolls a 12 or below, they can re-roll the check, but must take the resulting roll regardless of result. |
> | 5 | #### Though few see you, all know your impact.  The character gains advantage on their next 3 stealth checks. |
> | 6 | #### Unexpected Fortune Awaits You  Hettie refunds the money she took from the character and gives them a tip: she's heard that the Beacon Brigade keeps their money in a supply room with blue-framed windows. |

> [!info] Social
> #### A Conversation with Hettie
>
> Once the reading concludes, Hettie will reveal any of the following:
>
> - Vitt Wandren is the leader of the Beacon Brigade.
> - Vitt has been extorting her and other shopkeepers around the city for months.
> - Vitt has always been annoying, but in recent years, they've become somewhat powerful thanks to making new friends.
>   - With a successful `[[/check persuasion 16]]` check, she will reveal that the new friend in question is Hephiss Wandren.
> - The entrance to Marlstone near Vitt's candy shop can only be opened from within Marlstone — to get to Marlstone, the party will have to go through the Brigade's territory.
> - Hettie feels uncomfortable letting the party use the entrance to Brigade territory near her shop for fear of retribution.
>   - With a successful `[[/check persuasion 16]]` check, she can be persuaded to look the other way, but the party will still have to sneak by ([[Hettie's Divinations]]).

### Returning To The Shop

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you step into the shop again, Hettie is nowhere to be seen, though you can hear a faint humming from behind the closed door in the middle of the shop.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### "Sneaking" Past Hettie
>
> If the party **came to an agreement with Hettie**, they can pass by her shop and into Brigade territory as long as they do not come within 10 feet of her, take anything from her shop, or critically fail the Hide actions. All Hide actions to sneak by automatically succeed unless the party member rolls a critical failure.
>
> - If the party comes within 10 feet of Hettie, takes her property, or critically fails the Hide action, she calls out:
>   > You're not very good thieves, are you? I'm going to call for help, but I'll take my time about it. You should be able to be to the other side by then.
>   While she makes no immediate moves, Hettie does alert the Beacon Brigade (see [[Hettie's Divinations]]).
>
> #### Actually Sneaking Past Hettie
>
> If the party **could not convince Hettie to help them**, they can attempt to sneak by her by using the Hide action with a successful `[[/check stealth 17]]` check. If the check is failed, she alerts the Beacon Brigade (see [[Hettie's Divinations]]) and calls for the nearest Patroller. Any Patroller with 100 feet of Hettie immediately begins moving toward her to intercept the intruders.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### On Alert
>
> If Hettie alerts the Beacon Brigade that there has been a breach, the party has **-2 Banes** on all persuasion and Hide actions while interacting with the members of the Beacon Brigade going forward.
