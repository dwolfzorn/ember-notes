# The Cell with Empty Chains

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The chains on the wall of this cell seem to be placed awkwardly, crossing over each other in an X that seems like it should have collapsed by now instead of laying flat against the wall.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Mysterious X
>
> The X in the chains is being held there by a thin set of thread that's difficult to see from a distance, but can be noticed if:
>
> - Characters succeed on a `[[/check perception 15]]` check.
> - Characters attempt to touch or move the chains in any way.
>
> The X is laying against the wall on top of one brick in particular. With a successful `[[/check perception 16]]` check, characters can notice that there is a small gap around the brick. If they touch the brick, they also find that it moves, though it only moves sightly, as if stuck.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Marked Brick
>
> The brick marked by the X can be removed using one of the methods below. If characters use a tool (such as a pickaxe) or substance (such as grease, or a substance that dissolves brick and concrete) to aid them, they make the check with **+2 Boons**.
>
> - Characters succeed on a `[[/check strength 16]]` check to push it out.
> - Characters succeed on a `[[/check sleightofhand 16]]` check to wiggle it out effectively.
>
> Removing the brick reveals a small hollow - it contains 2 GP  **2** and Diary of a Prisoner (this can also be used as evidence against Hephiss Wandren).

One of several [[Holding Cells]] in the Wandren HQ facilities.
