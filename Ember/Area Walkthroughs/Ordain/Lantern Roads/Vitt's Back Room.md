# Vitt's Back Room

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you enter their bedroom, Vitt Wandren seems unsurprised, and a little amused.
>
> > It takes a lot of guts to break in here. I wonder what they'll look like splashed all over my walls.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Vitt Wandren Tactics
>
> Vitt Wandren is an aggressive combatant who uses a [[Gem of Shielding]] worn around their neck to protect themselves from [[Choking Fog Dust]] and [[Slowing Dust]], which they throw to affect the party before attacking with their [[Withering Dagger]]. The destruction to their own home seems unimportant to them.
>
> If the [[Gem of Shielding]] is removed from Vitt, they lose their protection from the effects of their own weapons and will resort to traditional combat using their knife and bow.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Vitt's Key
>
> After Vitt is defeated, the party can search their belongings - with a successful `[[/check perception 16]]` check, they find a key in one of Vitt's pockets. It is similar to the [[Brigade Key]], but slightly more ornate.
>
> Vitt's Key will work as a substitute for the [[Brigade Key]], and opens the locked chest in their back room. If parties do not have they key, they can pick the lock of the chest with a successful `[[/check dexterity 17]]` check with [[Thieves' Tools]]. If the party has a [[Brigade Key]], they have **+2 Boons** on this check.
>
> The safe contains:
>
> - 100GP  **100**
> - A list of names, with initials next to them in parentheses. Several have been crossed off, including Darius Cevher. The initials next to Darius' name read HW. This can be used as a piece of evidence to clear Funar's name. Mark the appropriate outcome in [[Revealed by Lantern]].

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Searching Vitt's House
>
> With a successful `[[/check perception 17]]` check while looking through Vitt's House, characters notice the outline of a doorway along the back wall.
>
> > Other than the faint line in the shape of a doorway that cuts through the wall, there are no other indications of a door - no handle or hinges or obvious way to open it.
>
> In truth, the wall in front of the door is a visual illusion - characters can see through the illusion with a successful `[[/check 16 investgation]]` check while examining the door more closely, or by physically touching where the door will be, as they can feel the true door beneath the illusion.
