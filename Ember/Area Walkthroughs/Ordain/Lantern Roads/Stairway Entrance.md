# Stairway Entrance

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Locating Funar Cevher
>
> If the party uses the[[Locator Rod]] in this area, they find that Funar Cevher is **adjacent to this room** (in a room directly above, below, or next to it)**.**
>
> - With an additional charge of the rod, the party can determine that Funar is in a room **next to this room.**

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The dark stone of the spiral staircase exits into a narrow hallway whose smooth glowing floors and hum of activity promise something completely different than the elegant manor that sits above.

This stairway connects Marlstone Manor and Wandren HQ. Once used by characters, it remains unlocked until Hephiss appears in the facility in [[Not All Who Wandren]], at which point it is locked and cannot be used as an exit.
