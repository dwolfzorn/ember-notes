# Vitt's Balcony

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Though the table and chairs on this rooftop almost look as if they could belong to another elevated dining establishment, the two well-armed sentries at each corner give it a sense of casual foreboding.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### The Beacon Brigade
>
> Two [[Wandren Watcher]] are stationed on the balcony, though their focus is on the ground in front of them. These Watchers can be dealt with similarly to any other member of the [[Area Overview]], with the following additional conditions:
>
> - Characters cannot persuade the Watchers that they are on the Rooftop accidentally.
> - Characters who carry the Courier Bag will not be attacked, but are asked to turn over their letter and leave immediately. If they do not, they are attacked.
> - Watchers attacked from behind are surprised.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Entering the Locked House
>
> While most of the doors to Vitt's House are locked, characters who take a close look at each can find an unlocked door on the southern side of the locked house. With a successful `[[/check stealth 20]]` check, or if characters are already invisible, they can enter the home without alerting either of the Wandren Watchers on the nearby roof.
