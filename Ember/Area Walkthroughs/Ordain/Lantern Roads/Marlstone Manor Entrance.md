# Marlstone Manor Entrance

This set of stairs leads directly up to the [[Guardhouse]] at the back of Marlstone Manor.

### Ascending to Marlstone Manor

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> As you ascend the winding set of stairs, Marlstone Manor grows larger and larger in your vision, until it is just ahead of you, with only a small squat building between you and the building's back entrance.

### Descending from Marlstone Manor

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Marlstone Manor fades into the background behind you as you descend the stairs towards the warm steady light of Lantern Roads.
