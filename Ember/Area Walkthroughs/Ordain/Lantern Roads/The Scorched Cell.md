# The Scorched Cell

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A pattern of singe and scorch marks covers the floor and walls, and the bars on the cells show signs of having been bent from the inside.

The damage to these cells was created when the Tyraphem prisoner Yllis attempted to escape.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### A Meaningful Pattern
>
> Characters who succeed on a `[[/check investigation 17]]` check can see a clear pattern within the scorch marks. It appears to be a message, but is written in a language other than Common.
>
> - Any character who succeeds on a `[[/check history 16]]` check can identify the language as Harmos, a language used on Luxarum. Any character who knows **Language: Harmos** can identify it without a check and translate it as "They Will Pay."

One of several [[Holding Cells]] in the Wandren HQ facilities.
