# Locked Gate

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> A large metal gate cuts through the center of the Lantern Roads neighborhood. On the south side, pedestrians and locals walk between establishments, lit by the many lanterns that help to give the neighborhood its name. To the north, the streets are much more empty, and those who walk behind it appear armed.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Direct Assault
>
> If the party attempts to directly break through or unlock the door, they are swarmed by members of the Beacon Brigade - two [[Wandren Watcher]] and three [[Wandren Patroller]] converge.
