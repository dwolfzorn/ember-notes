# Local Treasures

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Getting to the North of Lantern Roads
>
> - **Shadowbox Alleys:** Characters can pursue the Shadowbox Alleys path to Beacon Brigade territory by searching the vicinity of the shop. An entrance to the Beacon Brigade side of town can be found by going past or through Hettie's Divinations.
>
> There is no information about the rooftop route or bluffing in this area.

### Outside of The Shop

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Beneath a sign that reads "Local Treasures," a large shadowbox windows displays a large silhouette of the Ordani skyline that almost seems to move, as if the city is alive with energy. The movements are constant and rhythmic, and follow a pattern, as if they reflect something in the room behind the shadowbox that you can't quite see.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Observing the Shadowbox
>
> Inside the shop, a pair of Beacon Brigade members is putting money into bags and loading them into a nearly-full chest.
>
> - With a successful `[[/check perception 17]]` check, characters can hear the sounds of coins clinking and heavy objects being placed down inside the shop.
>   - If the check succeeds by 3 or more, characters can also hear a faint murmuring between two different individuals and make out the following:
>     > When we're done counting this up, I'm getting a nice long drink back at the Barracks.
> - With a successful `[[/check investigation 17]]` check, characters can take a look at the pattern of movement and tell that there are likely two individuals inside making repetitive motions.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Seeing Behind The Shadowbox
>
> If characters wish to see what is behind the shadowbox window, they must cast light on the shadowbox from outside. This can be done using any spell that casts light, including [[Light]], [[Dancing Lights]], or similar, or by lighting a conventional torch or candle.
>
> Once the source of light is within 10 feet of the window, the party can clearly see what is happening inside, but are visible to any members of the Beacon Brigade who are inside the shop or within 30 feet of the light source.

If characters see behind the shadowbox, they observe two members of the [[Area Overview]] counting money:

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Where the image of the Ordani streets once stood, you can clearly see a pair of locals counting up coins and putting it in bags. The bags are being loaded into a chest, which looks as if it is almost full.

### Inside the Shop

Two locals are counting money, which they put into bags in a large crate.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### The Beacon Brigade
>
> The two locals counting money are both [[Wandren Patroller]] in the [[Area Overview]]. They will attack the party on sight.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Searching the Shop
>
> When empty, characters can search the shop without a check.
>
> - The books and other small trinkets scattered on the countertops have no particular value.
> - The chest on the floor is filled with 15 [[Bag of Bronze Coins]].
