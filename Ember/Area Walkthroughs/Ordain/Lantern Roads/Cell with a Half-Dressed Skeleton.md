# Cell with a Half-Dressed Skeleton

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The skeleton in the center of this cell is wearing a set of clothing that has not yet been destroyed by the teeth of rodents or the wear and tear of time.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### The Skeleton's Clothing
>
> Characters who succeed on a `[[/check history 17]]` check, have `[[/knowledge fashion]]` or **Knowledge: Trade**, or are **Culture: ordani** can easily recognize the colors and fabrics associated with House Wandren. The skeleton likely belongs to a House Wandren member.

One of several [[Holding Cells]] in the Wandren HQ facilities.
