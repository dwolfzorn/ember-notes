# Cell with a Scratched Wall

> Tally marks have been etched into the wall. Though they are even and deep in the uppermost corner, the marks get fainter and shakier as they go, until the final mark trails off before it has even been fully chiseled.

One of several [[Holding Cells]] in the Wandren HQ facilities.
