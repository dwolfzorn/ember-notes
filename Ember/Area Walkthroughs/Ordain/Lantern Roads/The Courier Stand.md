# The Courier Stand

> [!warning] Gamemaster
> #### Getting to the North of Lantern Roads
>
> - **Shadowbox Alleys:** Characters can pursue the Shadowbox Alleys path to Beacon Brigade territory by searching the vicinity of the shop. An entrance to the Beacon Brigade side of town can be found by going past or through Hettie's Divinations.
> - **Bluffing:** The courier bag found here can be used to convince the Brigade that the party is on official business.
>
> There is no information about the rooftop route.

### Outside of the Shop

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The shadowbox window at the front of this stand clearly shows the silhouette of piles of letters and packages, underneath a sign that reads Courier Stand. Attached to the sign, on a piece of paper that has been nailed to the sign and is slightly askew, are the words "Back in 20."

No one is currently inside the Courier Stand and the door is locked.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Seeing Behind The Shadowbox
>
> If characters wish to see what is behind the shadowbox window, they must cast light on the shadowbox from outside. This can be done using any spell that casts light, including [[Light]], [[Dancing Lights]], or similar, or by lighting a conventional torch or candle.
>
> Once the source of light is within 10 feet of the window, the party can clearly see what is happening inside, but are visible to any members of the Beacon Brigade who are inside the shop or within 30 feet of the light source.

If characters see behind the shadowbox, they can tell that the courier stand is empty, though there are supplies - letters, packages, etc - scattered around.

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Letters, packages, and stamps litter a simple counter inside the shop, which is otherwise empty, but the pile of letters, half opened and half still sealed, next to an open courier bag, gives the sense that whoever was here will be back shortly.

### Inside The Shop

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Exploring the Courier Stand
>
> The door to the Courier Stand is locked, but can be opened with a successful `[[/check dexterity 17]]` check using Thieves' Tools. The [[Courier Bag]] and letters can be taken without a check. The letters include a letter addressed to Lyla Cevher. If characters open the letter, it contains [[Choking Fog Dust]].
