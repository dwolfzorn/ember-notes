# Wardrobe

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Along the far wall, long racks hold dozens of costumes outfits, from ornate dresses to archaic armors, and every accessory one might need in between. A nearby tailoring station allows for quick fixes and alterations.

Characters may want to dig through the racks and chests here to find items, though there is very little of value to be found.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Off the Rack
>
> There is a broad selection of outfits, dresses, uniforms, and stage armors hanging from these racks. While all of them exhibit considerable style and care in their design, none of them appear to especially valuable or unique.
>
> #### Hidden in the Chest
>
> A character searching the various chests in the wardrobe area finds container of jewelry. A successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check finds a real piece among all the prop pieces, yielding a [[Simple Jeweled Ring]] that might be worth some money if sold.

Characters will want to explore the collapsed section of wall and see where it leads.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### A Hole in the Wall
>
> The old stone of the wall has been torn out and smashed, revealing the raw basalt stone behind it.
>
> A character with **Knowledge: Subterranea**, **Knowledge: Crafts**, **Knowledge: Warfare** or succeeding on a **Awareness (DC 14)** check recognizes that the wall was smashed fast and with no regard for rebuilding the wall later.
