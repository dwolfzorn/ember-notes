# Storage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Just off of stage left, this space holds numerous large wooden crates likely packed with unneeded costumes and props waiting for their time to shine. Set along the wall is a row of ropes connected to the overhead pulley system that allows stagehands to control suspended props and decorations above.

Not easily visible from the main floor, a mutagist agent is hidden behind the curtains here.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Waiting Enemy
>
> A single [[Mutagist Grenadier]] is waiting here for a signal to head onto the stage. They are bored and not paying attention to their surroundings, and could be avoided by a stealthy party.
>
> When the time comes, they head out onto the stage to help subdue the crowd collected in the [[Main Floor]] area.
>
> #### Prop Drop
>
> The ropes tied off here can be cut or untied with an action to drop hanging props on creatures in this area. This can be done three times, and the targeted creature must make a successful `[[/save dexterity 14]]` or suffer `[[/damage 3d6 bludgeoning]]` damage as the prop lands on them.

The party can also search the area if they want.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Prop Hunt
>
> The boxes are filled with all sorts of items, including costumes, stage weapons, prop jewelry, decorative items and set dressing elements. These items are all dusty and haven't seen use in quite a while.
>
> None of the items appear to hold any particular value, and there are no notable items to be found within.
