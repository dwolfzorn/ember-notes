# Stage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The broad wooden stage stands in full view of both levels of the theater, with not even a curtain to provide cover. To either side of the stage props and decorations sit in haphazard piles, as though the venue was never fully reset after the last performance concluded.

There is not much to be found on the stage aside from some discarded props and tools.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Pointless Props
>
> The various props and decorations on the stage are flimsy and cheap, worth nothing in terms of coin, and all but worthless if used in combat. At best they could serve as basic improvised bludgeons but wouldn't survive one or two uses before breaking.

Characters may notice a large trap door in the stage floor while on the stage. This trap door starts closed, but if opened, becomes a combat hazard.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Stage Fall
>
> There is a large trap door in the stage. Spotting this trap requires a successful **Awareness (DC 14)** and there is no obvious mechanism for triggering or opening it on the stage.
>
> This trap door links to a passage that leads directly to the iron cage found in the [[Backstage]] area.
>
> Climbing out of the passage back up to the stage requires a successful **Athletics (DC 16)** or **Athletics (DC 16)** check. This is impossible if the trapdoor is closed.
