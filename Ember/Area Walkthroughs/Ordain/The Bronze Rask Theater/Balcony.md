# Balcony

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Seats line the balcony level which overlooks the whole of the theater. While not as close as the front rows, the view of the full stage is commanding, and in some ways superior. The seats are worn but well maintained here, and the slightly warm air is thick with the scent of polishing wax and oils.

#### No Balcony Access

The balcony area of the theater will not be available until the release of Foundry VTT V14 and the inclusion of scene level technology. Until this time, consider the balcony closed for renovation and physically inaccessible.

A later encounter states that there are enemies on the balcony, they are instead found on the main stage for the time being.

There is nothing of note to find in this upper area, however there are some archers present

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Overwatch
>
> There are 2 x [[Wandren Tracer]] on this balcony that are sitting together and talking. Before combat breaks out, they are not especially aware of their surroundings and easy enough to avoid.
>
> If alerted, or when combat breaks out below, they separate and begin selecting targets to fire at.
>
> #### Long Fall
>
> The distance from the balcony to the theater floor below is 40ft, and deals `[[/damage 4d6 bludgeoning]]` damage to any character that jumps or falls off the balcony.
