# Forgotten Passage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> This broad passage is built of the same white stone as the rest of Ordain, but it all seems older and worn by comparison. The floors are lined with piles of rubble, broken tiles fallen from the ceiling, and wrapped in cobwebs.
>
> The hall is empty otherwise, with only a trio of lanterns to light the way.

Though there's nothing of value to find in the passage aside from rubble, bricks, and stone.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Old Stone
>
> Characters that examine the passage and make a successful **Society (DC 16)** check know that Ordain is a city that was built up over centuries and features layers of passages, old streets, forgotten aqueducts and more that were built over as the city expanded.
>
> Characters with the **Culture: Ordani** have **+2 Boons** on this check.
