# Backstage

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The expansive backstage area encompasses the whole back quarter of the theater, and is packed with items needed to run the various performances held here. Overhead props and decorations hang from ropes and hooks, ready to be lowered when needed.
>
> Two things are immediately strange about this space:
>
> First, a section of stone wall has fallen inward. Pale dust veils and rubble lies on the floor and just beyond the cracked masonry gapes a dark tunnel and freshly pulverized stone.
>
> Second, at the center of this area stands a huge iron cage. Its bars are thick and riveted to a square frame bolted straight into the wooden floor which is covered in scrape marks indicating it was dragged into place recently. A heavy lock hangs on the double door’s hasp.

This note provides an overview of the space. For detailed information on specific areas, see the [[Wardrobe]] and [[Makeup]] pages.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Cagey Addition
>
> A successful **Awareness (DC 14)** check notes fresh scrapes and gouges on the floor. This would indicate that the cage was recently brought in and installed, and not a normal part of the backstage area.
>
> The heavy lock on the cage can be picked from either side of the cage, but is easier if done from the outside. A single successful **`[[/skill thief 16]]`** check is necessary to pick the lock. Attempting this from inside the cage is done with **-2 Banes**.
