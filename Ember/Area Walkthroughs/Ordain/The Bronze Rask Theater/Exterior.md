# Exterior

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> You stand beneath a canopy of bright banners in red, gold, blue, and purple. Their patterned cloth stretches from building to building over white cobblestone streets worn smooth by countless steps.
>
> The street flows around a tall, white-bricked building. Above the double doors hangs a plain wooden placard, its letters simple and firm:
>
> > The Bronze Rask Theater
>
> The theater’s facade rises at least two stories, each course of large white bricks stacked with careful order, and capped with a roof of weather worn violet tiles. The building appears to be one of the older structures in the area, but has been well cared for over its lifetime.

The front doors are unlocked, and the party can enter at will.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### A Historic Theater
>
> Characters with **Culture: Ordani** and **Knowledge: Arts** are aware that this theater has been known by several names over the years, and is one of the oldest theaters in the city, having stood for over four centuries now. It has passed through many hands, and has been home to countless acting troupes, and was renovated several times over the years. Once it was known as the City Center Theater, when Ordain was much smaller.
>
> Characters without the above culture and knowledge combo must succeed on a **Society (DC 17)** skill check to know this information.
