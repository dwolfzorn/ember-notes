# Side Entrance

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The long alley here terminates at a double door entrance at the side of the theater. A brightly painted sign declares:
>
> > Private entrance.
> >
> > Cast and staff only.
> >
> > Not for theater guests.

This entrance is locked, but would allow the party to enter the backstage area directly.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Locked Up
>
> The side door features a hefty and fairly modern lock. A successful `[[/tool thief 18]]` check is necessary to bypass the lock. Each attempt takes several minutes.
