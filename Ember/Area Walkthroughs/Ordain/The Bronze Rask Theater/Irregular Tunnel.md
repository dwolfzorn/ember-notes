# Irregular Tunnel

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> The stone has been hastily chipped and broken away, the iron-hard basalt having been removed on a meandering path likely determined by least resistance rather than engineered plan.
>
> The tunnel breaks through stone and brick into a new area up ahead.

This tunnel stands in for both of the dug-out tunnels located in this scene. Their qualities are identical, and characters studying one tunnel will note the same traits in the other tunnel without needing to roll.

> [!tip] Exploration
> #### Fast and Messy
>
> A character with **Knowledge: Subterranea**, **Knowledge: Crafts**, **Knowledge: Warfare** or succeeding on a **Awareness (DC 14)** check recognizes that the digging was done fast and messy, with little regard for typical mining processes or good tunnel engineering.
>
> Whoever dug this was taking the path of least resistance while still trying to aim in a specific direction. The work to excavate the tunnel that lies ahead likely took days if not weeks.
>
> A successful **Wilderness (DC 14)** check notes that the stone around Ordain is basalt, a notoriously resilient stone that can be harder than iron. Digging through it could not have been easy.
