# Breached Waterway

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Water flows rapidly though this narrow, forgotten channel, the sound of it reverberating off the wet brick, and filling the space with noise. Though the water is clean and clear, the accumulated grime and mildew on the damp stone is impossible to ignore and hints that this waterway has sat forgotten and unmaintained for quite some time.
>
> Up ahead light spills out on the stained white stone from another hole in the wall.

There is nothing of note to find in this space, but the slick, noisy environment does present its own issues characters need to be wary of.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Slippery When Wet
>
> Moving through this tunnel faster than a walk speed requires a successful `[[/save dexterity 14]]` or else the character slips and falls prone.
>
> #### White Noise
>
> The flowing water is especially loud. Characters attempting to make perception checks related to hearing while inside this tunnel have **-2 Banes**.
