# Gameplay Details

The following gameplay features apply to the Bronze Rask Theater area unless specifically stated otherwise in a specific room description.

## Enemies

This area is currently occupied by a collection of House Wandren agents and some Mutagists on loan from the local contingent. They are gathered here for the upcoming meeting. The following enemies are encountered here in the provided approximate number:

- [[Brynna Verocorrt]]
- 4 [[Wandren Patroller]]
- 2 [[Wandren Watcher]]
- 2 [[Wandren Tracer]]{Wandren Tracer] (Variable)
- 2 [[Mutagist Grenadier]]
- 6 [[Ordani Ruffian]]

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Nonlethal Methods
>
> The combatants in this area are atempting to capture future prisoners, and are not striking with lethal intent. All characters and creatures reduced to 0 HP are incapacitated, but not killed or put into death saving throws.
>
> #### Resting
>
> It is not possible to take a Long Rest or Short Rest within this area due to the events unfolding.
>
> #### Fleeing
>
> Should the party need to flee the area, they may do so by retreating out of the theater and into the city streets beyond.
>
> #### Captured
>
> If the party is subdued in combat, they will be placed in a cage in the backstage area. Details for this outcome are presented in the encounter text.

## Illumination

The theater is well lit with high mounted lamps and lanterns throughout. The backstage area features unique lanterns that glow with a pure white light, improving visibility in the area.

The tunnels and old passages behind the backstage area are lit unevenly by a handful of lanterns.

## Terrain

Rubble and collapsed sections of the ruins in this scene serve as &reference[difficult terrain] for characters moving through such spaces.

## Elevation

The theater has 20ft ceilings on the ground floor, and in the main theater room the vaulted ceiling reach up to 80ft at their highest.

In the dug out tunnels the space is a cramped 5ft in all directions, this includes the old water. The old passages and door chamber have 20ft ceilings.
