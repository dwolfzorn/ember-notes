# Flyspace

> [!quote] Read Aloud
> Just off of stage right, this space is sparse, featuring nothing more than a row of ropes connected to the overhead pulley system that allows stagehands to control suspended props and decorations above. The space is otherwise empty, possibly left open so that the nearby side entrance can be used to bring things in and out of the theater easily.

Characters entering the theater from the [[Side Entrance]] will find themselves here, and might have a chance to sneak up on an unaware enemy.

> [!danger] Hazard
> #### Waiting Enemy
>
> A single [[Mutagist Grenadier]] is waiting here for a signal to head onto the stage. They are bored and not paying attention to their surroundings, and could be avoided by a stealthy party.
>
> When the time comes, they head out onto the stage to help subdue the crowd collected in the [[Main Floor]] area.
>
> #### Prop Drop
>
> The ropes tied off here can be cut or untied with an action to drop hanging props on creatures in this area. This can be done three times, and the targeted creature must make a successful `[[/save dexterity 14]]` or suffer `[[/damage 3d6 bludgeoning]]` damage as the prop lands on them.
